[legatura_la_teme id_capitol="4" id_subcapitol="2"]... The priests do not know that it is here where the new Jerusalem is being built. Now it is hidden; I do not want to reveal it. It is like starting to build a house, and the ones who started it got sick, and I found other workers and I finish it, though. [/legatura_la_teme]You did not see then that the enemy was among you and clapped his hands when I took My trumpet.

… The relics of Saint John the Baptist were discovered through My work and the rulers were asked if they receive him in the country, but the answer was not given. It is the body without the head, the way they buried him when they cut his head off. My Mother cries to be accepted in Romania. The one who will accept it, will be saved. It is like a golden globe. I do not tell you where it is. Pray to be accepted in Romania. Pray that Romania will accept the relics of Christ’s godfather.

… The time comes for God to separate you from this world. My sons, the time will come for Me to scent you with heavenly perfumes, to smell from the distance, and I shall make miracles with you, but stand by Me.
