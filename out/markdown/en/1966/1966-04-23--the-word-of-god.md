[legatura_la_teme id_capitol="5" id_subcapitol="2"]Weave the crown brother with brother and do not submit yourself to death, do not give yourself to this time, as there will come something that you have not seen since you were born; there will come the creation of earth and heaven and the New Jerusalem; that is, they are made but they are not on earth yet. [/legatura_la_teme]Come to your senses, as there is none in the world to stop the thing which comes from Me, but the Lord is coming on other side and print another book and you are not devoid of happiness. Heaven and earth will pass away, but My word shall not pass, that at the appearing of a new work I will fire up the earth and all that is sinful will burn. And, as a house is made of stone and wood, the wood will burn and the stone will remain.
