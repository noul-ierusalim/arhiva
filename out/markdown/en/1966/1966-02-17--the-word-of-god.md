Oh, how many will come and try to destroy this work! However, they will not be able to do that for there will come creatures from the east and the west, from the south and the north, who will look for Me then.

Oh, happy you will be, Christian, who will reach the end of the age with Me! You are My work from the beginning and from the end of the age. Son, there will no longer be any place or land that will not be yours if you listen, for the Lord will call all His creatures and will pass what is good on His right an what is evil on His left, and the good will inherit the earth.

Sons, not this flesh is God but this is God’s trumpet. The Lord has come from His holy heaven and bowed to you to the ground.
