Oh, Lazarus, come out for it is not the time to die and it is the time to wake up. Come to Me, Christian, for those in the tomb will come out too and will ask for salvation.

As much as I would say two words out of ten, and you will understand them with one word, Christian. If you understand them, it will be well. If I gave you My words largely, I would not finish whole days and nights with you.

[legatura_la_teme id_capitol="7" id_subcapitol="1"]… Come, dear brother and sister, come for I am about to call the dead after you, as there is no other wonder than this: the dead will come out of the tomb.[/legatura_la_teme]
