I am the Lord God, the Master of the heaven and the earth, for I am their Creator. I am the Son of God Sabaoth and the Father is in Me, and I am in Him. I am the Alpha and the Omega, the First and the Last; I am the beginning and the end, the Lord, Jesus Christ. I become word over the earth and I speak by it to the faithful and unfaithful man. The faithful one strengthens his faith, its power and its work, and the unfaithful one hides from Me so that he may be God, and thus he draws upon himself the fruit of his unbelief, for he suddenly is called to give an answer to those that come from God face to face with him and to see the One Whom he shakes off by his pride of man against God.

I am the Lord, Jesus Christ, the Word and the voice of the Father, and I have had long endurance with the unfaithful ones, big and small, face to face with the sons of the holy faith, to prove more powerfully God’s fruit through those who are faithful, and it was so, and it is so, and from now on it will be likewise, because God proves Himself against all lying gods of man who cannot do anything.

Let the people stop from their weak walking for their salvation and let them understand the true God, looking at My works and at My fulfilling words and to discover in them the work of the godly Trinity, the Father, the Son and the Holy Spirit, the God and Creator of the heaven and the earth, of their visible and invisible things, the God of the Christian people, a nation who has known its sinfulness and then found God, loved Him and followed Him, for the Lord reveals Himself as the giver of grace to those who humble in their spirit, soul and body before Him.

I become word of watchfulness over those who are faithful in a day established by the saints and fathers to be dedicated to the Lord for a feast with faith, the Sunday of the right faith, a memorial of the faith, the spirit of the revelation. You, those who stay ordained as servants over people in My name to show them their way to God and to the truth, bow on this day and learn from God, for the way with God is the spirit of revelation, and My way with the man is written on earth by its revelation. Bow with holy decency at My voice upon you on this day, for My speaking of today on the earth and the book, in which I write Myself in order to remain written in it, is no other kind of work but the spirit of testimony, the spirit of revelation, and you strengthen your faith in God and you strengthen it a great deal, for satan and his angels try to pull out all the good seedling and so that only the cockle may remain, and those who eat the fruit of the cockle become like it and they want to wipe out My trace and My name upon you and upon the earth and to establish themselves as God, for it is written about this fight between light and darkness, and the battle has its front on the earth.

I, the Lord, Jesus Christ, am coming to you and speaking to you from the Father, Sabaoth. Come to the holy faith to My word upon you during these days, because the Father has sent Me to you to speak; I am not doing this from Myself. I do the work of a Son, because I listen to the Father and then I work everything He gives Me to do, and this is how you also need to do, the work of sons, because those who are sons are not otherwise. Look at those who do not work like that and do not have faith like that, look at the faith they have and what they do with it and how they want to overthrow God and to rule in His place. However, you should seek after the work of sons, because I come to you from the Father in hard times and I show you from Him how to work so that you may take after Me and after the Father and not after the sons of this sinful and passing age that has nothing in Me. Oh, learn from the saints and from the parents to whom, I, the Lord, spoke by the spirit of revelation and strengthened in them the faith and God among the people on earth. Look at My sustaining works through the saints and bow to take after them, and do not be conformed to the face of this age, for I, the Lord, work much word in your days and through it I prepare the life of this age, for it must be prepared that which is to be, and it is written into the Scripture to work in the last days and to do God’s work on earth and to exhort all people to repentance, for all the things of the man’s darkness come from his sins and the darkness is so big on the earth, and the people cannot get healed from it and from all kinds of sufferings until I, the Lord, lift up their sins, for I am the One Who has power to do this after I put in man faith and the power of faith in the One Who forgives the men’s sins so that He may heal them from their weakness and unbelief, and then I work all things by the spirit of revelation. Amen.

I am bowing and speaking to you, servants of the churches of the Romanian people, and I am reminding you of the salt. If the salt goes bad it has nothing to salt anymore. However, you have to be good salt, and if you chose this salt, that is, to be salt on earth, then see if you do this work and examine yourselves for it, as behold, I am coming and visiting you and I am exhorting you now to measure your stature and its fruit. I am not asking you for anything, I am not taking anything from you, but the Christian people needs priests and holy servants, and the heaven needs this on the earth, for the harvest comes near and there are no wise harvesters through whom I, the Lord, may be able to protect My wheat and to separate it from the weeds and then to put it on My table, and My wheat can hardly, hardly be seen into the midst of the weeds, and I speaking with you about My pain.

Oh, ask yourselves, sons, what kind of work and fruit you have. If those you shepherd see well the holy way and walk in it after My will, according to My expectation, then you are good salt, but if they do not see the way and do not see the light to follow it, then listen to My voice and take light from it to be illuminated by it and the people to see the light into the darkness and to come to it; let the people come to Me because it is a hard time. Oh, do not give away My voice of today from your way, the spirit of revelation, which is coming from the Father to you through Me, His Son, Jesus Christ, the Word! I am coming from the Father as word to you to give you grace and to give it to you from abundance, in such a way that the grace may be known when I give it to you. Hold your vessel so that I may pour out in it, sons, for you to have and that it may be seen that you have and that you may not work from you but to receive from God, to have and to give further, and in this way you will be blessed. Oh, let the Lord be at your helm, for the waves are troubled and big and you cannot do it alone. Put your faith in the Lord, Who is coming and speaking to you, and do not rely on you. You do not have to run to the prophets to ask about the hard time. I, the Lord, come towards you, only that you may receive Me the way I come, sons, for I come with protection, only for you to understand this and to believe like that. The ship sheers on big waves, and the helm is threatened by the storm, and I stand and wait for you to call Me so that I may protect and save you.

Strengthen over the people on earth; strengthen over the Romanian people the spirit of the Orthodoxy and ask the saints and the fathers to come and help your little boat on the waves, so that I may strengthen a living church on earth for Me, which is to have the saints as its fathers! Oh, seek to work according to their customs and do not come out of the book, for the devil and the antichrist want to devour the whole flock and he expedites this work. Wake up to watch! It is time to believe that there are not fairy tales those that the saints and the Scriptures foretold that would come to test the people. You have to listen now more than anytime, for I, the Lord and those who are Mine have to overcome, as it is written to overcome with them and for them.

I am coming to you and sitting in counsel with you. It is a time for fast and prayer. Come with days of fasting and calling to God with humble heart in order to destroy the development of the man of lawlessness, the antichrist, which tries to bring into submission the whole creature. God will ask you if you have watched and if you have overcome against the antichrist. Open the map, open the Scriptures for this time and let us fulfill them on behalf of God, for it is written that Christ will overcome with His faithful ones. Tell the evil to stop! Learn what holiness is and share in its exhortation! Watch, for you have established yourselves as watchmen, and if you have chosen this, and then see if you work according to this duty!

Oh, see that there are many shepherds among you, who sleep, and because of this, satan has a way to drive its stake. Come, sons, to work together closely, come! It is My voice; it is the voice of the Lord on the way with you. I have mercy on you, I have mercy on the flock and that is why I come down among you full of word, and I speak mysteriously, as I was speaking with man in paradise when he heard My voice walking, speaking, exhorting and asking and working, as well.

Oh, My coming as word to you is not a lie. Do not be afraid! Do not try to say that this word is not I, the Lord. I do not come to ask from you or to punish you, but I rather come to forgive you and I come to teach you with God, for you have come out of being with Me, sons, and you try to work by yourselves, only you; however, I can work with man otherwise, because I need faith from man, and by it I want to give him the spirit of revelation. Oh, do not leave off the spirit of revelation! The saints and the fathers were not doing this way! Seek to learn the work of the son, the work of obedience to God, sons! I come on the earth as word. The Lord comes to you as the word and the bread of word. Listen to Him and eat from Him; be nourished with God’s word and listen to Him! Amen.

I, the Lord, stand into the midst of a tiny people, which has been prepared by Me during these days on the hearth of the Romanian people, of which I have come out to build the new Israel in order to replace the one which crucified Me and has been throwing Me away from it up to this day. Into the midst of the Romanian people, I laid up the manger of the birth of My word, and I had put faith in the sons born of this word and I had fulfilled over fifty years of word on the hearth of the Romanian people and I have also come out as word over the earth like the bridegroom who comes out of his chambers to preparer his bride.

Oh, peace to you, servants of the churches, but receive God’s word in you, sons, to be able to do and to receive My peace, for I give you not as the world and its spirit gives to you, and I will look at you and measure now your wisdom and then your faith, sons! I would teach you much and many things, and I will do this as soon as I see that you take My word in you to put it into practice. Stop the work of the antichrist because I give you power to be able to do this and to shake off his trap laid in secret for the Christian people, for the man of lawlessness is against the strong ones, against those who believe in God and through whom I will overcome, and they will also overcome in My name, and their lot will be with the faithful ones; it will be that of the faithful ones. Amen.

Oh, get behind Me satan, get behind Me antichrist! Go into a desolate place and leave the Christian people, because it is the people of God, the One eternally living! Be good, for I, the Lord, Jesus Christ, command you to do this, and you have to listen accordingly. Amen.

Oh, peace to you, people of My word, into whose midst I have been speaking to you and to those who have gone to it for work. Amen.

Peace to you, church of the Romanian people, for I have been freely given you holy faith to your victory with Me during these days and to My glory, that is God’s glory into your midst. Amen, amen, amen.
