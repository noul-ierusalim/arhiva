… There will be a cleansing in Romania as it is done in everyone’s house. How was Ro-mania in the past ages that all lived after God’s will? Romania was a house with pleasant hearts to God, but look, now Romania is the same as you would give a house to some tenants *(During the communist dictatorship, r.n.)*, and they would defile it. Let all little eyes and all little hearts take heed that all who work against God will no longer step on this land. Romania was after God’s will in the past and now I want to bring it again after My will. The other little coun-tries which join Romania and help her, they will receive help from God as well. If some of the countries show hate towards Romania, they too will come into blood and cleansing.

God wants to come to light with His leadership. God wants that His work may be known on all earth. God wants a single heart and a single thought, a single road and a single light on this earth. Who wants God, that one shall live. Who stands against Him, will no longer be able to live. All are struggling in eager rivalry to be at leadership, *(After the removal of the dictatorship at the Revolution in December 1989, r.n.)*, but they will not be able to do anything until that one to lead to the light will come.

[legatura_la_teme id_capitol="4" id_subcapitol="5"]… Now God has a little work to prepare His own ones in order to conquer the enemy, and this is being done with pains. I want to come to light with a divine leadership so that the work of God may be known on the whole earth. Until the coming of the leader, who is to lead in order to please God, the country will have no rest.

None of the priests who serve the little churches for their belly and for money will remain.[/legatura_la_teme]
