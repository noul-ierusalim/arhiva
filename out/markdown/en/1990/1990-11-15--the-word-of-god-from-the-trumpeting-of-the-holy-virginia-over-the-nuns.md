[legatura_la_teme id_capitol="6" id_subcapitol="2"]I have come to tell you now: *„Get up in order to understand what happens with you, My people, for you will soon be a new miracle and you will come into that condition of happiness and will never die”*. Oh, why was it so hard for you to understand the mysteries of God? It is nothing left without God telling you. Behold, now you will have to rise quickly and to enter into wisdom, for God will go with you to the peoples, to breathe over this world, so that it may come to life. Behold, the days of resurrection that are written that will come, for these dead people will soon hear the voice of these words, and these dead people will come to life and will celebrate the days of resurrection of this age.[/legatura_la_teme]
