It is a day of Sunday, and I, the Lord, serve My Father, I serve Him and His house in heaven and on earth, for My Father works for His house, and I work likewise, too, for everything the Father speaks and does the same does the Son, and the Son does everything only from the Father.

It is a day of Sunday, I am working by the word, and My word is turning into action, as at the making of the heaven and the earth when I spoke and I turned into action everything I spoke.

Peace to you, people of My word! Oh, how beautiful, how beautiful is to have a people and to speak upon it, and it to turn My word into action and to take after Me in word and in action, My people, for behold, it is much and it is always to work for God’s rest.

[legatura_la_teme id_capitol="5" id_subcapitol="6"]It is a day of Sunday, oh, sons, and My exhortation is upon you. Oh, get up well and do My work, as now I am declaring upon you a great blessing for more than ten years of the word of My book, the blessing We have to raise high on our hands to the Father, and then to be able to share it, sons. Forty years after the laying down of the foundation of My word on the hearth of the Romanian people I raised up the first book through My trumpet Verginica, in which I had put much word of My mouth, selected in time to remain written and then to be confessed over time that the Lord spoke and speaks over the earth and that He has built a fold, a people to which His comes with His word when He speaks from heaven on earth, and thus in the year of 1995, after forty years of word on the Romanian land, I raised up to the Father the first book, much word from My great speaking for forty years. Then ten more years have passed and again, sons, I have drawn with you more word from this living well and I have built with it a great book and I have raised it up to the Father, as I have taken much of My river of word and I have spread it all over the earth, and now, if ten years have passed again, we are raising again a book and we are writing in it starting there where we stopped with our writing and up to this day, sons, and the books of My word work on earth because the word goes, it goes on the way on which man walks and it meets man, *(The Internet, r.n.)* and no one will be able to say that he did not know or that he did not know about the Lord; the Lord Who speaks from heaven on earth in the midst of the Romanian people, and the prophet Daniel said even from his time about this book of My word that many will thoroughly search it and the knowledge will grow in the man’s mind and heart, and many of those who are asleep in the dust of the earth will get up at the hearing of the book of My word and will hear the voice of the Son of God, as it is written, and the faithful people will be saved, and especially it will be saved anyone who will be written into this book.[/legatura_la_teme]

Behold, it is written into the prophets about this river of word from this time, in the end of the time and the book of My word comes out; it always come out as the spring of water comes out from the rock and flows eddyingly and then it appears flowing and confessing, and many will bow and wash in this river and will be cleaned, whitened and clarified, but the lawless ones will behave like those who are lawless before this ripple of word, for all those without the Lord, without the law, all these will not understand and only those who are wise will humble themselves and will understand God, as it is written.

I, the Lord, am blessing now your work for the preparation of the issuing of the book, sons, another book of word, starting with the place where we remained with the book ten years ago, and now this will be added and we well be up to date with the work of the confession and we will write sixty years of word now on earth with the book of My word.

Oh, get together in a bundle and work, sons, for I, the Lord, will work from above and from below and I will help you for the being of the book and we will lift it up in our hands then and will make the Father rejoice and we will thank Him, Who loves us, well sons, for He is the One Who sent Me on earth two thousand years ago to give Myself for the life of the world, and He is the One Who has sent Me now as well; He has sent Me now for the life of the world too, as the Father is only love, and I am His fruit, and I work like Him; however, I have also had you as fruit, and you have to work like Me, sons, and give our whole love to the Father, all the work which we do in His name on earth and the fruit of this work will come out, which will bring much rest, much joy, much heaven, well sons, and many will get up to this joy, as did the paralytic in the Gospel, remembered today by the churchly order, who got up at My voice on him while I passed by him, after that for so many years he had been going to that pool which was healing people through an angel, believing that perhaps someone would be to help him go down into the water at the coming of the angel and to be healed, only that every time there had been another one that got into the water, and he always remained on the bank waiting. Thirty-eight years this paralytic had been waiting and on that day I told him: *«Get up, take up your mat, and go up to your house!».* **(Matt: 9/6)**

Oh, how much agitation was stirred up among the Jews on that day! It was a day of Sabbath and the Jews said that the one who was healed was not allowed to take up his mat on the day of Sabbath and when he was questioned, he answered them: *«He Who made me well, the same said to me, ‘Take up your mat, and walk.’»* **(John: 5/11)** Then they asked him, *«Who is the man who said to you, ‘Take up your mat and walk.’?»* **(John: 5/12)** Then I found the one whom I made well in the Temple and I told him no longer to commit sin so that nothing worse might happen to him. However, he told the Jews about Me that I was the One Who healed him and therefore the Jews were angry with Me blaming Me that I did what was not lawful on the day of the Sabbath.

Oh, behold sons, those who are not faithful are many today too, and they are those who do not believe from God. Nevertheless, who are those who do not believe? They are those who work lawlessness, sons. When you see unbelieving people, you should know that by the unfaith which they show they cover their trespasses, they cover their disobedience to God when they say that they do not believe, but they also cover their lack of wisdom too, when they do not know anything else but only say that they do not believe those that I have made come down today from heaven to their fulfillment on earth.

Oh, it is not the same with those who are wise, and they take My word and search it well and see in it God’s hand and they understand the Lord and understand this time and clean themselves from all the evil in them and around them, and the Lord comes to them and dines with them, as it is written, and this is how the words of My Scriptures are fulfilled when the time of their fulfillment comes.

Oh, sons, I am leaving blessing upon you for the work of the book, which is added now to My river of word, a river that flows from heaven and comes to you to be put into its book. Be full of joy to the work for the Lord on earth, sons! Each of you should work what you can at this building, at My work with you, which is the being of My word among you and then shared to be useful.

Be full of peace and diligence, be full of good conduct and be of a humble spirit and wisdom from above in your work with Me, sons! As little as you are, as few as you are, I, the Lord, make My work with you, and the whole nature, all of it, sons, from heaven to earth, from earth to heaven, all of it waits for the revelation of God’s sons, the revelation of those who will have worked with Me for the redemption of the creature, as it is written, oh, sons.

Now, sit down and give priority to this work and then go with it to the printing house and lift up into your hands its being to the Father, and the Father will make you rejoice, as the feast of the Pentecost is coming near and we are going to stay with this book on the table on that day, and the Father will thank us, well sons, and we are going to write between earth and heaven sixty years of word on the hearth of the Romanian people, and we will have a Christian feast, those from heaven and those from the earth, and we will come with days of Holy Spirit before the heavenly hosts and on that day will be love, great love, and the Holy Spirit will hover above and will cover us, and how beautiful will be then, oh, how beautiful, how beautiful, sons. Amen, amen, amen.
