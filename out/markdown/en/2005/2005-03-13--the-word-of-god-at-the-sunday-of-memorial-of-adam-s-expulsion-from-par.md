The Father Sabaoth is in Me when I speak, for I am His Son and Word, as I was in the beginning when I built the man saying: *«Let Us make man in Our image and after Our likeness»*; He spoke with Me through Me and from Me. Amen.

Let us come down into My book in these days, children who receive Me as word from the Father. You and all the people of My word should know well and with much living that your God is called the Father, the Son and the Holy Spirit, for the people who pretend to be Christians on earth do not know the name of God, because only the Holy Spirit knows in man, for the Holy Spirit is the being of the Father and of the Son, and when God’s being does no longer has work in man, the man does not know God, even if he tries to speak about God. When My Father and I spoke we fulfilled, for this is how the Holy Spirit, God’s being, works, after He speaks out His word. Amen.

Oh, how much We, the Father, the Son and the Holy Spirit loved the word of man’s creation so that We might fulfill Ourselves as house and to have rest in it, and to confess Ourselves in it and through it! Oh, children, speaking of God! Two thousand years ago, My disciples confessed God’s mystery and the faith for it and they said that the man has to believe in the Father, in the Son and in the Holy Spirit and in the church, and by being baptized within these and believing, to be forgiven from all his sins and to become divine, and by confession to share God to the people, being afterwards the son of resurrection and of the eternal life, which is to be in the new heaven and on the new earth, which God promised as an inheritance to those who love Him. Amen.

Oh, My people, all God’s mystery is through the church, and God’s church is nothing else by the man. If the Holy Spirit, the Lord Creator of life, is not glorified in the man and from the man, he is not God’s man in the church and does not know God in him and that is why he cannot show Him to others, for the Holy Spirit, the Father’s Being and of the Son, confesses and glorifies Himself on the sides when He is in man, and He is fulfilled by the man confessing Himself. Amen.

Oh, how much it hurts Me the fact that I do no longer have a resting and confessing place on earth! Oh, children sons, how beautiful is the man who is a church of God’s Being, a place of the Holy Spirit, a true worshiper, as I told the Samaritan woman about the true worshipers, who worship the Father in spirit and truth, as Father wants! [legatura_la_teme id_capitol="1" id_subcapitol="3"]I built the man as beautiful as I am, for I wanted to be in him with My image and after My likeness and to be a dwelling place of My glory. My glory is My work, and the man had to be only that, and God to be his Master, because He made him. However, the man let himself to be deceived by disobedience, and disobedience is just as severe as his disbelief. It makes the man self-confident if God lives secretly to help the man to always have the wages of obedience by submission and fear of God, the armor, which does not let the man lose God’s being from His dwelling place. I have been enduring a long pain for seven thousand years, since the man spoiled My love in him, which would have kept him as the dwelling place of My glory, submitted to God’s glory within him. When the man crushes My love in him, he grows within himself and becomes sin against the glory of the Holy Spirit, Who makes the man as beautiful as God is and My joy and rest in him, for it is for this that I created him, and I built him by love.

Oh, My people, when the man makes a beautiful doll for himself, he makes it by love, and then he enjoys it, because its master can do nothing else for it or against it. This is how I worked out My doll kneaded on earth, and I built My building looking at My image, which My Father loved. However, if then I breathed over it in order to put life in it, My doll did not listen to Me as a doll would listen and which can do nothing but only what its master can do with it.

Oh, how much I have been longing for the man to be a good doll to My joy, to My comfort, especially that I breathed life in her to be living doll and to put My love in it, loving her master from within it. I cried much on earth two thousand years ago, and I told everybody that unless they became like little children, they would not enter My kingdom, within My love, for My love in man keeps the man clean and obedient and loving and holy, just as I am, and it keeps him a sweet doll to the joy of the One Who made him, as I am the doll of My Father Sabaoth, because I listen to Him and I love him to His joy and comfort. Amen.[/legatura_la_teme]

Oh, children who build God in man! How much I want you to have dolls, children and sons, doll sons! I want to make you glad because you are children, and the children know to rejoice if they have joys. I am the doll of My Father God, and I dwell with My being in you for you to settle Me down in you and to fulfill My kingdom in those who have become little children by obedience to the Father, for such worshipers Father wants, with the Holy Spirit in them and with the truth, which makes them children, new born, sons, making of Holy Spirit in man. I long for the beautiful man, I long for the living man through the Holy Spirit in him, because I lost him from Me and the man, built by My hand, lost Me from him too, for his disobedience made him not listen to Me, and he expelled the one in Me who did not take after Me, as I resemble My Father by My obedience to Him, by His love towards Me and which keeps Me within Him and Him within Me.

Let us cry after man’s happiness! Let us cry before the Father, My people! Oh, sons, become My dolls so that God may have you as His dwelling places. Oh, sons, oh, a doll made by man to the man’s joy and comfort does not ask for anything. It cannot but give her beauty to the one who made it beautiful. Oh, My people, take a living example of what I wanted to make you understand and to be able to do. I invited you to My love in you, and which makes you divine to become a dwelling place of My glory, son and My people.

Let Us stand in front of the mirror now to make Ourselves beautiful, more and more beautiful with every passing day. You should look in Me and I in you in order to become dolls, sons. I strengthen you with blessing for the time of abstinence from food to be able to prepare your little bodies brightly for the dwelling of God’s being in them, because to the churches that are sanctified to God there are, are written seven weeks of fasting in the body and for the feeding of your hearts within the being of the Holy Spirit, for woe to those who do not know what fasting within the body is, and it brings to the joy of the glory of the Holy Spirit in the man who becomes beautiful to God! Let My thought be your thought, working children with God for this time, so that we may be able to work with the Holy Spirit for the being of the book, which will preach on the earth the feast of the Pentecost of the word of My coming, My word, which gives birth to the man in him to the joy of the fulfillment of the life of the age, which will be eternal for those who believe all the things that I have promised that will come on the earth. I give you heavenly progress for you are small and the book of My word is just as great as God is. And I will give you all those necessary for its being by which we will hold a feast of Holy Spirit, fifty years of resurrection word, and then a little bit of time and patience with grace for the new birth of the world. Amen.

Oh, children who guide My people to the grace of the Holy Spirit! Those who have not known to love you and to believe in you for My glory, which I share through you, will soon sigh, when they look at you and will see that I, the Lord, have believed in you and had My love in you. I have told you several times that My love in you has its own husbands, and blessed will be those who will have loved My love in you and used its wisdom in you. Turn these people, who stay at their making, into My dolls and rejoice over their obedience, and comfort yourselves over their holy fruit. And be dolls, sons, so that I may have you as models for those who know and love My life within them with wisdom for them and in them. Amen, amen, amen.
