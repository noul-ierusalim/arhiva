Open My book with you, children who watch in the gates, to glorify Myself within word upon you and upon the earth. Amen, amen, amen.

I have always spoken My word over the earth, for I, children sons, have no one with whom I may carry My burden from man’s unbelief and his disregard of Me, and I carry it with you, and then we set it down on earth over those who make it for Me, for that which the man works before Me, that is what it comes back to man, and that is why I have to speak out all My crying from man on earth.

The man lives on earth with the evil in him, with his great distance from Me and the man on earth is heavy, since even the earth can no longer keep him, and the earth struggles with pain, children sons. With whom shall I cry over the pain of the earth, which struggles within the pain of new birth? I cry with you and with the people that cries together with us. We also cry with those who do not let themselves to be heavy on earth, and I speak out My word into the midst of My people of today in order to fulfill it afterwards. Amen.

[legatura_la_teme id_capitol="3" id_subcapitol="4"]Oh, My people of today, the saints and the angels hear your name when I call you: *„My people!”.* Oh, My people, I do not have on earth another people, another church, in which to become truly body and word. Those who call themselves church on earth, say this from themselves, not from Me, for I did not choose them. If I had been to choose, I would not chose those who hid and hide under the garment known as holy, only to go into immorality at ease and in such a way that the world may not know them. However, I have spoken that everything the man works before Me, this comes upon him before the people, and every man will receive his reward according to his own works, for I said: *«I come quickly, and I will give each one according to his work».* ) Amen.

Oh, My people, the immoral man has not wanted to hear the parable of the prodigal’s son from Me, for he who hears has ears to hear. I speak into your midst My long sufferance from those who have squandered their life in sexual immoralities, but not like the son prodigal of life, and who hid far away from his father and who, coming to his senses, repented with grief and came back begging his father to receive him under his shelter only like a hired man, like one who was forgiven and shown mercy, because he came back. My affliction from those who squander their lives in outrageous debaucheries, is that which even at the table known as God’s on earth, even under the shelter called church by those who stay under it, these people prodigal of life, these deceivers who stay over the people who look after God, have come together and gathered the reward for their sins, and which will come over those with a great and shameful noise. They also do not longer come back to God as some hired men, for the time is different now, and it is nothing else but only what is written into the Scriptures, for it is written: *«He who is holy let him be holy still, and he who is filthy, let him be filthy still for the time is near and it is right at the door»*, but these people do not know if they have not watched.

However, now, with great mercy, within full sufferance, I cry with a word of mourning and tell those who are faithful and holy, among those who are liars and say that are My church, and I also tell those who come to repentance: come out from the midst of those who are immoral, who have hidden under a garment of holy man and separate yourselves! Do not stay, do no longer stay touched together with those who stay unclean before the glory of My word, which comes and gives each one according to one’s work! Come out and be separated and I will receive you and I will feed you and water you and I will show you My will to be able to walk in it, for My word comes on earth and it will burn everything that is dry and will reveal everything that is holy and the dimly burning wick he will not quench, and I will blow into it to kindle it and to give light, and the bruised reed he will not break. Amen.

I, the Son of the Father Sabaoth, revealed Myself as God Who came from the Father, Man born of the Virgin, and I have come again under My name in the end of the time and I am called the Word of God, and I speak the language of the Romanian people, where I have been glorifying Myself by the word for fifty years, by the mystery of My second coming after the man, for there have passed seven ages since the creation of the world, and within the mid century after this one, I wrote seven thousands five hundred years and I rose My church again on earth, and I set it on its foundation by the word, and I am its foundation and this is how I have been speaking from the midst of My church of New Jerusalem to those who falsely call themselves My church:

Do no longer lie to the nations of the earth that you are God’s servants, you, those who have turned the places known as holy places into houses of immorality for your empty glory; you, those who have defiled the altars from the fathers; you, those who live in your blood and which has risen nine fathoms above your heads! I am the Son of the Father Sabaoth and I have come down on earth to write upon you the end of the lie, for the devil is the father of falsehood and you have the devil as your father. Do no longer put people off with fine words for you belong to God and the path to Me is towards you! Do no longer lie to the sons of the people, who walk like blind people after you, if you serve the outrageous sins! My word has been crying for fifty years on earth over the earth for the man to hear it, and you, those from the altars, have been defiling the church, you have been ruining it, you have been spoiling its holy name. The church is holy by those who are part of it, but if these are the sons of the devil by their immoral works, by all means they are not a holy church, but rather they are an immoral church, but God’s church is not like that. Carry your sins under whatever name you may want it, but not under My name, not under the garment known to be a church garment.

And as for you, those who fear God among these people of falsehood, who called and call themselves a church of saints, I speak to you with this determinative word before the wages of sins may fall over the bodies, which have not repented in themselves from their wicked works: come out of their midst, out of the midst of those immoral people from the altars, so that the wrath for the works that are worthy of wrath may not fall upon you, so that you may not drink of the wine of God’s wrath. Come out and separate yourselves and then come to My mountain of word and learn from Me the law of holiness to be able to walk on it, for the eternity is coming on earth and it has to be brought, it has to be built by holy bodies and full of the Holy Spirit! Come back to My love, for I built the man from the beginning for My love, not for his love, and I built him to be My church and not his. I call you with mercy and tears before I pour out My wrath, for the outrageous sins of those, who hide under the name of a holy church, slap My hand, and I have into My hand the cup of the wrath. Come to repentance and to salvation and become a feast of Holy Spirit and give yourselves freely to God, now when I, the Son of the Father Sabaoth, have written down on earth fifty years of word of My mouth, spoken into the midst of the Romanian nation, and when I give the book of the judgment of the creature to all those who will see it now. My word of fifty years has been crying deeply within Me and I have been speaking it over the faithful ones and I have made it into a book, which is called the Book of the Lamb’s Life. The word is the life and the life is the light of the people, and the light shines in the darkness. Reach out your hands to Me if you want to receive salvation, and receive and feed on the word of life, for I want to destroy death, the last enemy. I want to destroy the sin, but I cannot do this by the people of falsehood, who call themselves antichrists by their sins, by their life with lie in it and who have brought the man to ignorance of his soul, since I do no longer see on earth a man who fears God, by which the man to be able to put an end to his wandering away and to come back so that I may teach him the way and salvation.

Oh, Romanian people, shake off the Pharisees who make lame your waking to your salvation from under the man of lawlessness. Come, so that I, the Lord, may shepherd you, for you do no longer find any shepherds for you on earth. Come to the way of the Lord, your God, for there is no one to save you besides Me. Come to God and be faithful and learn the mystery of your being from God, My mystery in you, for by My word into your midst I make the world again, I make the new heaven and the new earth, for I have to fulfill this Scripture on earth, but I want to protect you from perishing into the midst of the nations of the earth, for you are My land, chosen by the Father from the creation of the world for Me, after seven ages of people. Amen.[/legatura_la_teme]

And as for you, people of My word, strengthen yourself in the mystery of My new beginning, and may your humility of spirit be your path to everything that I have to be into your midst, to everything that you have to be into the Romanian nations, for I want to sound My trumpet with My Gospel upon you over the entire world, the book of the new kingdom, for My kingdom is just as endless as I am. Amen. You can do nothing without My teaching and without any teacher upon you, for it is not for the body but for My Spirit that I have separated you from among the sons of the people who walk without any way between earth and heaven, My people.

[legatura_la_teme id_capitol="8" id_subcapitol="3"]May be blessed the work of the being of the book of My word, spoken and written into the midst of the Romanian people for fifty years, be a book by which I, the Lord, by My gates may be able to mark on earth the feast of the Pentecost of God’s word from the end of the ages, the word of My coming, as in the beginning so in the end; and the word of the new making of the world, as two thousand years ago, I prophesied that it would be. Amen.

Lift up your prayer to Me for the being of My book with perseverance and with one heart, My people. Those who have laughed at Me and at you after they heard of My voice into your midst, will be put to shame by their own outrageous trespasses, and I will draw many true worshipers to the Father, and I will not make the new world until first I make a new church by those who will have heard this voice of Mine and will have pulled themselves out from among those who will be the fire, which they themselves will consumed by it, for they did not fear God. Amen.[/legatura_la_teme]

You should wait for Me within the gates when I tell you that I come as word, watchful children for My coming. I come down in you with a heavenly power, so that you may be able to do the work of the being of My book, the Book of the Lamb, children sons. Oh, do not forget that I have asked you to prepare My people heavenly, within its body and soul, to be able to be My path to the man. Amen.

You should give Me health, My people. [legatura_la_teme id_capitol="8" id_subcapitol="3"]My word has been crying between heaven and earth. The earth has also been crying and struggling because of the sufferance from the sinful man, from the man of falsehood, who himself calls My church. And I want to make the new heaven and the new earth as in the end, for in the beginning, when I made the man, the man worked the land and the earth worked out the man, and the man did not prepare his heaven, and now, in the end, I prepare the man to make the heaven on earth together with him, for My will to be on earth as in heaven, children sons. Amen.

And I will speak out the word over the earth until I fulfill it, because although the man is unfaithful, I am Faithful and True, for I am the Word of God. Amen, amen, amen.[/legatura_la_teme]
