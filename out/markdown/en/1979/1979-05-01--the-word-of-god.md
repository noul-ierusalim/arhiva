… What a beautiful city Bucharest is! What a beautiful city Târgoviște is! What a beautiful town Pucioasa is! What a beautiful city Brașov is! However, you should know that they will no longer be and they will fall down.

[legatura_la_teme id_capitol="2" id_subcapitol="3"]… Look, you will be called openly and be told this way: *“Do you renounce God?”* And he says this: *“Yes.”* Why does he say, *“Yes?”* Because of fear of what that one is keeping into his hand. The one who says no, that one is suffering. This is the deepest matter, and if you escape this trouble you will shake your hand with heaven. The fear is great but put the fear away. But I say: the world and its ruler are dying; the antichrist and all his army are dying too.[/legatura_la_teme]

… Woe to you, antichrist, because you have deceived the world! Woe to you, man from the world, who has clung to this king! Woe to you! Woe to you! Woe to you, earth and sea, for you will greatly suffer, because your corpses lie on all the roads and in all your trenches. Be faithful, for everyone will see what the Lord has spoken.
