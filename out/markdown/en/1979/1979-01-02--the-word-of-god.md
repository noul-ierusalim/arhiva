… Be careful, as you will have the opportunity to see satan in the flesh and even to make war with him face to face, but know that the Lord Jesus will not leave you to be overcome. But it should not come into your mind that the power is yours, but rather only with the sign of the cross will you no longer see satan; but if you fight with haughtiness or with oth-er kind of work besides Me, you should know that satan will bring you under his feet.
