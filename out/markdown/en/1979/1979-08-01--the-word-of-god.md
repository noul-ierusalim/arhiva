[legatura_la_teme id_capitol="1" id_subcapitol="6"]… As the Joel’s prophecy was fulfilled, of Habakkuk, of Elijah, all the prophecies in the book will be fulfilled. And here that Lord Jesus went down in Spirit and prophesied all that was remained. [/legatura_la_teme]Be prepared to be one flock and one Shepherd, not in the earthly wealth, but in the spiritual wealth.

Oh, My people, I will come to you to show you My wound in the hand for if you had believed, you would have not joked and smiled and you would have not gone talking things of the world.

… Strengthen yourselves by fasting and praying and overcome your enemies that attack you. This is the weapon of the Christian, as nothing overcomes satan like these, for he goes away from you. But you should not fast today and then no longer fast until the next year. You should not pray today and then no longer pray until next year.

[legatura_la_teme id_capitol="2" id_subcapitol="4"]… Once more God tells you: do not be stubborn in belief, as you will all see today’s work in the middle of the sky, from the genesis till the end. All the trumpets will be revealed and shown in front of you.[/legatura_la_teme]
