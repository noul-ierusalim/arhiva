... I brought you to this place not that you may get rich, nor that only you to make use of it, but to look, son, for someone else’s benefit. This is the land of the promise. I brought you close to Me. Be as the Father, the Son and the Holy Spirit ask, as the Lord did not bring you here in order to get mad. Be clean, for today I say to you to do My will not for fear as I let the will of man free. Arm yourselves well with faith and be good literate, for the Adventists passed before you. David was a child when Goliath came with the thought to destroy the faith of the Lord’s people and he mocked God. And I brought David; I took him from his father’s sheep and brought him in front of the pagan’s armies and in front of Goliath; however David did not kill Goliath with a little stone; rather by the word and power of faith.

… Well, here is the woman *(The church, r.n.)* My people who was sitting on the beast. The beast got on the woman *(The communist dictatorship, r.n.)*; and now their time has come to be judged, and the woman and the beast to be destroyed, the beast and those who nourished it. In the twinkling of an eye I will do this and everything will change, even the sea. *(The work was accomplished at the Revolution in 1989, r.n.)*. My child, you will wonder and say: *„Is this really so? Or, is it only an illusion?”* The time has come, My little children, for God to bring the liberty; He will bring justice and there will be no one to oppress you anymore. You will leave your thing on the road and it will be hard for a thief to live among the believers, for the Spirit of God will uncover Him; and it will be hard for the wicked man, for He will be punished by the law.

You will dig a well in your garden, like Jacob’s well, and we will meet at the well. Because of the great joy that will be, the baby will no longer suck his mother’s breast and the bridegroom will no longer come closer to his bride. I give you power to tread on serpents and dragons and you will not be harmed of anything that is coming upon you. Do not be frightened. I am with you. Wherever you go I am with you.

Did you see son how sick My trumpet *(Through which the Lord speaks, r.n.)* was that it could not breathe? Well, look how well I speak through her speech. I will bring the time of wonders again and anything bad that may happen will be repaired; the deaf will hear and the lame will walk.

Do not wear your garment inside out. Do not be ashamed to wear you garment openly because I was not ashamed either to carry the cross for you; I was not ashamed to be dressed in the mocking robe for you; I was not ashamed when they were crying: *„The King of the Jews!”* I could destroy them in one breath, but I let it to be fulfilled, so that I may take all human kind out of the heavy curse. I suffered to take Adam and Eve out of hell. Do not take the side of the dead; do not do what this world does, as the Lord prepares you just for the dead to receive life and resurrection through your hand.

[legatura_la_teme id_capitol="2" id_subcapitol="2"]Observe the feasts of the saints for they are of great benefit for you. There are many saints every day who sacrificed themselves for the holy faith and for truth and decorated the arch of the church. There is no sand in the sea; there is no leaf in the trees; there are not stars in the sky as how many saints God has, and I counted in this multitude those in heaven and those on earth as well.[/legatura_la_teme]
