The Lord is coming down with His saints. The Lord is coming down on the earth in a feast of new heaven and new earth, the place of the Lord’s righteousness. Amen, amen, amen.

In the name of the Father and of the Son and of the Holy Spirit, this is the name of My coming down over the garden of My word, to become word on the earth. [legatura_la_teme id_capitol="2" id_subcapitol="4"]I am the voice of the seventh angel’s trumpet, as it is written about Me in the Scriptures. There is no other mystery to be worked out. I am the One, Who accomplishes the mystery for which I was born of the Father and of the Virgin, a Man, so that God may be seen by people, and for the people to believe in Me and to come to life and truth.[/legatura_la_teme]

… Zachariah, the Lord’s bishop, the Lord’s prophet, fulfilled the Scripture of the mystery hidden from ages, the mystery about the Virgin who was to give birth to God. He was faithful and fulfilled God’s work and took a holy care of the Virgin who was about to give birth to the Lord, the Son of the Father, the Lord on the earth, the true Lord. And I have also had faithful people during this time for the work of the birth of My word from the Father, as the Father worked and gave birth to Verginica, and He put near her those that were faithful, and from these were born disciples from disciples, the sons of God, and the Lord, the Word is being born on the earth as word and gathers through the voice of the seventh trumpet those that are His elected from the four winds as it is written. I am a holy tabernacle for you, and for those who are unfaithful I am a stumbling block and many stumble and fall; they are crushed and stay caught in the trap of slavery.
