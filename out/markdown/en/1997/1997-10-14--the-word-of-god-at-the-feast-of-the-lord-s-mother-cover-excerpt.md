[legatura_la_teme id_capitol="3" id_subcapitol="4"]… I have come down from heaven for the salvation of the human people. I have come down now after two thousand years and I have become the Son of Man, flesh among people, God from heaven, born of the Virgin on earth. And now, after two thousand years, I have come down again from heaven as word coming down from the Father over the nation of the Romanians to make God’s nation out of Romanians, not having hypocrite faith, and by the love of God. I have not come to destroy, as My father has not sent Me to divide, but He has rather sent Me as Master of those that are seen and over those that are not seen, which the Father has in His control. I have come on the earth to announce Myself as the Master of the heaven and the earth, and you, those who are stiff-necked, who eternally stand against the Holy Spirit, Who speaks from heaven, listen: I am the One Who speaks from heaven over the earth from everlasting to everlasting. With fear of God, with sincere faith, with holy love, with wisdom and in a spirit of justice, open your closed hearing and listen to what My Spirit is speaking to you:

I am He Who is, but you, servants from the churches, who are you, since you do not know Me in this word? You are the teachers who teach My law to the people, but My law is holiness. Oh, how comes that you do not know Me by My speaking of today? There have passed forty years since I have been dwelling as word from heaven in the people of Romanians, and after forty years of Holy Spirit, coming down as word, I have been knocking to the doors and the doors have been closed and I have passed through the closed doors and got in among you as word. How comes that you do not know Me by this word? Who are you, servants of the church, since you do not know the One to Whom you say that you serve Him? Who can be This Word Who is speaking to you now, if He is not God, the Word, the One Who has spoken by the prophets? I have come again to you as word and asked you about this word. Is this word the devil? Is it nothing this word that knocks at the door so that it may be opened and the Word received, that is God, the Word, Who is the door? I am the door, and you are the servant of the churches and you stand before Me and you stand before the people in My name, but why do you not know Me when I come and when I knock? Do you not really believe in the Scriptures? They speak about this word, which will bring the renewal of the world, just as I, the Lord, have prophesied. They speak about the new heaven and about the new earth, which I promised for the time of the age of My justice. The heaven and the earth will pass away, but My Scriptures will be fulfilled for I come and fulfill them. Amen.

Oh, servants of the church, do you want to receive the reward of the unbelievers? What do you want to do with yourselves and with your flock? Do you not really have any God to listen to Him, since you do not want to believe in this word, which My Scriptures prophesy to be in these days before My second appearance? However, if you stand against the word of today, at least do believe in the word of the Scripture, which speaks about My coming. I come soon within a visible glory. What shall I do with you in My day of glory with you, those who pretend that are unfaithful to My word of today, which is knocking today at the doors? What shall I do with you? Oh, the reward of those who are unfaithful is severe, those who know and do not fulfill God’s wisdom and power and the law of holiness that I have asked from man in all the ages. It is heavy and eternal the reward of the unbelievers who make use of My name in order to receive glory before the people and not with the purpose to become My heavenly servants on earth. I stay with the whole of My heaven of saints, angels and invisible glory above Romania to clothe it within My glory, and you stand against Me. Why have you become servants for My name if you are not able to know Me, if you do not wait for Me as the Master Who comes, as I told you that I would come? Oh, what shall I do with you? Do you really want Me to do with you what you do with Me? You do not know Me when I come as word to you. You do not receive Me. Should I also not receive you? Should I also not know you when I come? You do not want Me to come on earth as I told you that I would come. However, I was not the One Who told you that I would come? Was I the Son of falsehood when I spoke about My coming even at that time? Answer Me! I will definitely hear you, for My ear hears everything you speak in your chambers and even at your ear.

I looked over your trouble when you saw that I told the sons of My word about your plan well hidden from them, when You spoke to each other to come and strike against the gates of the Lord’s citadel, for I am the Lord of the citadel of My word, and this citadel is called: *«There the Lord is»*, as you know that it is written in the prophets. You were confused and said: *“Who told them about our plan against them?”* Oh, you of little faith, why do you pretend that you do not know God, the Almighty, Who works His plans speaking about them in due time?

… When the Syrians arose against My people Israel, I spoke to Elisha, My prophet in Israel, to send My word to his king not to pass through the place where the enemy was hiding and who wanted to knock it down, and the king of Syria was troubled and spoke to his servants: *«Won’t you show me which of us is for the king of Israel?»* One of his servants said: *«No, my lord, oh, king; but Elisha, the prophet who is in Israel, tells the king of Israel the words that you speak in your bedchamber».*

My Spirit speaks to the ruler of the church *(Teoctist, the patriarch, r.n.).*

Amen, amen I say to you: do not stagger from your place where you sit, for I strengthen you to sit. Open My book and see in it how the true shepherd is over the true sheep. Remind the shepherds who shepherd the flock to take in them the law of holiness and let them serve Me in this way. See what it is written: *«The hired shepherd flees when he sees the wolf coming».* **(See John: 10/13)** If I took away from the shepherds their reward suck from the sheep for their work of shepherd, no one of them would stand to shepherd, because he does not shepherd but he rather sucks the blood of the sheep and no one serves Me, and no one serves as I served, for I am the Good Shepherd, Who laid down My life for the sheep, and the hired shepherd flees when he sees the wolf coming to the sheep.

Amen, amen, I speak to you, to the one who stands in front of the church: you have two wolves around you, and even three that want to remove you from your place. However, you should not run away as the hired of shepherd does and stand carefully for the life of your sheep, for your sheep are the people chosen by Me in the end, for it is the time from the end and from the beginning and for the new birth of the world, for the spirit of the world has come to an end, and My Spirit, in the beginning, and the end joins the beginning, and I work again by the word so that all that have remained may come. Amen.

Oh, do not let the flock to mix itself with other flocks and beliefs, which have no holy fathers. Christ’s church is not a mixture. I am the One Who will make one flock and one shepherd, and the one who makes before Me only what I can do, that one is a thief and a robber for the sheep, and he mates the sheep to spoil their seed. The church is that which is Mine, and My sign stays over it, the cross, the sign of the Son of Man, as I said.

Amen, amen, I speak to you: get up again, as you got up when I told you to anoint for Me the one who is from Me among the shepherds of the church. *(Irineu, r.n.).* Get up and comfort the crushed spirit of the least one among you, of the one who was faithful to My word, which comes on the earth with the renewal of the world. Stand always with your hand above his head, for I will ask his life from your hand. I strengthen you as his shelter until I come with heavenly fulfillment over him to make heavenly work on earth, for his humility will give forth fruit. Amen. I strengthen you as his shelter, for he listens to you and obedience means love. No one among those that are under your staff listens to you, but he listened to you even when you asked him to get up against My word, for disbelief is great in the church, and the one who is big is afraid of those who are near him.

Amen, amen, I speak to you, the one who stay over the Christian flock of the Romanians: the bishops and the servants of the church are without holy life before Me, and they stand before Me with sexually immorality, with drunkenness, with hypocrisy, with love for silver and with empty glory before Me. However, this one, who is wounded among you because he is a witness of the citadel of My word, is a pleasant shepherd before Me and loves holiness and keeps My commandments in his heart fulfilling them, and I loved him and I chose him and I love him, and this is how you should also love him, to comfort him and to keep him from the stones of those who strike in him for My name. Amen. I sit into the midst of the church, I am the mysterious Shepherd and I say: he, who is without sin, let him throw the stone at him first. I am the One without sin and I do not throw, but these who throw, how comes that they do this? Do not let them throw at him, for I am the One without sin and I do not throw, but rather I have always blessed everything he did before Me in the time of his sincere and holy faith for the salvation of the human kind. Amen.

The Spirit of God has spoken to the church in Romania. Amen, amen, amen.[/legatura_la_teme]
