[legatura_la_teme id_capitol="2" id_subcapitol="8"]My Spirit sighs bitterly because of the unfaithful and for those with false faith, for those who stay over the people into My name not doing My will and not working the works of the holy faith. However, the confusion of languages will come over the church of the world and then it will fall down. The church is not allowed to be one with the pagans. A mixed flock is not from God. I have nothing to do with the sheep mixed with goats. I look down on earth and read from heaven the word of the man’s church. It is not possible to be a worldly church. Oh, what do the people think that the church means. Over what kind of Babylon do the people want to write Christ’s church? The church is only one. It cannot have other kinds of living but only Mine. I am the head of the church, but there where I am not the head; I am not the foot either. And I am going to give the word to the ruler of the church in Romania so that he may not allow the true church to become one with Babylon, one flock with those who are without holy ancestors. I will give fresh word over the church in Romania, and if it does not listen to My word to be only with the Lord, and not with those who are separated from the ancestors, *(The Catholic Church and the rest of the Christian cults, r.n.)* I will give it to the Babylon and I will make it into confusion of languages, and only those who are Mine will come out of this confusion, and they will be set to the right, for My church has never been overcome. Amen.[/legatura_la_teme]
