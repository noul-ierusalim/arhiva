I am, sons. I come to you to turn your sadness into joy and to strengthen My power in you, for you have to be able to work into My name everything I want, all I have to work in you. Amen.

I have changed the sadness of My apostles into joy. I have changed their sight to see Me in glory and to have joy, and I told them not to tell anyone about their good sight until I it is glorified by My Father through My resurrection. I ate then the Passover with them and said to them: *«I have earnestly desired to eat this Passover with you before My passion and resurrection, for I will no longer by any means eat of this until it is fulfilled in the kingdom of God.»*

Oh, weak and little children, I want to fulfill with you this Scripture so that it may be fulfilled in the kingdom of God, and so that you may be the kingdom of God in which the Passover may be fulfilled, sons. I have always wanted Myself to be fulfilled in you, and to help you also be fulfilled in Me, and both of us to want, from both sides, that My new Passover to be fulfilled in you, before and in the hearts of many nations, sons. But be cautious, and in your weaknesses seek to be strong into the Mystery of My kingdom in you, so that My Scripture may be fulfilled, sons; to have someone to fulfill them with, as I fulfilled them through My prophets. [legatura_la_teme id_capitol="3" id_subcapitol="4"]I come to you to clothe you with the strength of the Holy Spirit and that to stand well for My name against anyone who calls you to account for your faith, for look, the priests want to come to you to deceive you with their empty words, as because of this the wrath of God is coming upon the sons of disobedience. How comes that, sons? Be careful for I told the faithful ones: *«Watch out so that no one may deceive you, for many will come into My name saying: “I am Christ”, and shall deceive many. Do not follow them».* I have also said this by the Spirit: *«Let no one deceive you by any means, for I will not come to those who were supposed to believe until the cup of disbelief is filled; I will not come unless the denial of faith comes first and the man of lawlessness is revealed, the adversary of My Christians, exalting himself against all that is called God or that which is made to be worshipped; so that he sits as God in the church of God, setting himself up as God, and to put Me to silence».*

My adversary is in the church, sons, and he is bigger than I am in the church, and he does not ask Me about his works and gives My sheep and turns them into a sect. These call the worldly ones, Christians, and the Christians they call sect. These fill the place made for worship with people who do not do My will, and the faithful Christians by their hearts and deed, they call wandered away and sect, the same as My adversary did in My time; who exalted himself against all that is called God or against all that is made for worship, who exalted himself so high until he crucified, by unbelief, the Son of God, the Son of the vineyard’s Master. But I took from them the vineyard of My Father and I gave it to others who worked upon it with My word, not with their word. And the same I have done it today too with the adversary, the man of lawlessness, who is sitting over the church; and the vineyard is Mine, and I shepherd it, and I prevent him from grazing My flock any longer, for My word is a shepherd and is an iron staff, and I shepherd the nations of the earth with it, and the adversary who hid in the church wriggles and seeks to put My word under a bushel. When they too were persecuted, they were believed by the Christians, but now they escaped, they said this, and now they persecute the Christians as well, for fear that their vineyard may not be taken by someone else; but the vineyard is Mine, not theirs.

Sons, sons, the priests have risen against you to deceive you with their empty words and they fill their cup of unbelief by their words, but you should watch and be careful to those who think that they can steal your minds with philosophy and vain deceitfulness of the human tradition after the ghosts and not after Christ. Take heed to those who are written in the Scriptures about Me, as though the entire fullness of the godhead dwells in Me, and I have the head over the entire reign and dominion; I and not the ghosts on the earth; I and not those who set themselves up as God by not receiving Me. Take heed, sons, and as you received Me so should you walk in Me, rooted and built within Me, being strengthened in faith as I have exhorted you, and prospering in faith with the whole of your hearts’ discontentment, children sons. Watch all the time praying so that you may become worthy of all these which will come and stand before Me at My coming. Amen.[/legatura_la_teme] Watch all the time in prayer, for too many false prophets have risen and sat into My name in the church and deceive many with the philosophy of their empty words because of which God’s wrath comes over the sons of disobedience, over the sons of unbelief, well, well, My faithful little children. False prophets do not have names of Christs but only in the church, sons, for they have borrowed My name and put over the empty philosophy of their words, and it is written: *«Let no one deceive you in any way, as for these God’s wrath comes over the sons of disobedience».* And when God’s kingdom comes, I will be the true Christ of My Father and it will be fulfilled the Passover, which I ate with longing into the midst of My faithful disciples and together with them before My passion and My resurrection, for it is written that that Passover will be fulfilled in God’s kingdom when it comes. Amen. Then the false prophets will see what the true Christ is and what the fulfilled Passover is in God’s kingdom, which comes, but I say that it has already come, and those who are great hide from God’s wrath, for they have not watched to know and that their house may not be torn down, and they fell into the edge of the sword of My word, for they have cast Me away with their empty words and with their philosophy of the deceit from the man’s tradition and according to the ghosts of the world and not after Christ.

… Christ, the Great Bishop, blesses you, oh little garden, and He will soon change your face so that the people may see My glory from you, o little garden, sanctified by My word which runs from Me upon you. Amen, amen, amen.
