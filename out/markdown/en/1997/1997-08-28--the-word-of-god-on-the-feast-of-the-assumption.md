I come with the clouds and I become feast of new heaven and new earth over you, Jerusalem, My people. It is a feast and I come to you to make your spirit glad, and to have you as the fruit of the joy before Me. Let there be peace, and let there be blue sky! Let the heaven and the earth serve you, the visible and invisible ones, at My meetings with you, for I love you, My loved people!

I am with the kingdom of My Father in you, Jerusalem. You are the kingdom of God, as the Lord reigns in you and becomes word over the earth. Amen. May the kingdom of the Father and of the Son and the Holy Spirit be blessed! Amen, amen, amen.

I am My way to you, and I am your way to Me, and you are My way to the people, you people, My path. We are a path for each other and we meet, and My love is being made perfect over you and becomes the word of love, so that you may take My love and to give it to the people, and that the people may take from it and to know My path, which becomes word to the people, and that the people may walk on My path, which leads to the kingdom of God, and to receive from it a spirit of comfort, a spirit of salvation and of resurrection.

You are the kingdom of God, Jerusalem, My loved people. You have the Lord as King over you, and the King lays a heavenly table into your midst, and the angels and the saints and all the heavenly hosts come together at His table with you, and eat from those that are from heaven, which are laid on the table, and the heaven rejoices in you, Jerusalem, My kingdom. The heaven comes down to you with a council of feast for My mother Virgin, and blessed are the heavenly feasts in you. Every man waits for the days of celebrations and joy, and those from heaven wait all the more, but the heavenly joy is a true joy; it is a true comfort. I comfort you, My people, with the comfort, which comes from heaven. The Spirit, the Comforter, has His dwelling in the Father and in the Son, and becomes the Comforter over His sons, and He becomes the King Comforter.

Oh, Jerusalem, oh, loved people by God! The Holy Spirit, the Comforter is King over you; He is king over you and comforts you, as what is sweeter than the word of the heavenly comfort? I also need comfort, My people, and I comfort you to make you My comfort, to make you comfort for Me, comforted child. The child is comforted with his mother, and his mother is comforted with her child, for great is the mystery of the comforting, and the affection of the Holy Spirit is the perfect love for the sons of the Holy Spirit. Amen.

Oh, little children, that I called Jerusalem and sons of the Holy Spirit, I gave you power to become sons of the Father, as it is written: *«To those who believe in Me, I will give power to become sons of God and to be born of God, to be the sons of the Father».* I gave you power, sons, to be born from above, from heaven, to be born of the Father, to be born of the Father through Me, Who was born of the Father before the ages, and the Holy Spirit of the Father made Me come down in the body of the Virgin to show Myself from her, a Man among people, God came from the Holy Spirit and from the Virgin, daughter of My Father, daughter of the perfect faith and love for man.

Oh, little children, I gathered some of you to mark down a word of joy of the Holy Spirit over you and in you, for the kingdom of the Father and of the Son and of the Holy Spirit is in you, and becomes word. Oh, sons, the kingdom of God is near to those who believe in the works of God over the earth. My mother Virgin was a kingdom of God. I dwelt in her as King from heaven to come out and to shepherd the nations of the holy faith.

Oh, sons, there is no one on the earth to understand where the place of the kingdom of heaven is, and behold, the mystery of the holy faith is great; the man with a holy faith has a great gift from God. He, who has no holy faith, does not understand the mystery of the kingdom of heavens and its place and work. But who is the one who has the holy faith? Sons, sons, only a holy man has a holy faith. The holy faith does not dwell in the mind and heart of the man who lives in sins and in fleshly pleasures. I speak to you about the holy faith to understand why the people of the church do not believe in the kingdom of My word, which flows over you and from you to the people. The kingdom of God from you becomes the interpreter for you, so that you may know its mysteries well, as there is no one on the earth to understand those that are true. I want to prepare a place in the people with the kingdom of My word, which is the Spirit Comforter, the Spirit Teacher, Who draws to the Father all who My Father gives to Me to include them in the mystery of the kingdom of the heavens on the earth. I want to prepare a place in the people, but the antichrist, the man of lawlessness stands against Me. Oh, little children, those who have no holy faith stand against Me, as the antichrist does not have his place and name anywhere but only near Me and in those things that are established by Me.

[legatura_la_teme id_capitol="2" id_subcapitol="6"]Oh, sons, the people were afraid, the Christians also were afraid of the communist man, as they said that he stood against God and against those who were faithful among the people, but this communist man, *(The red apocalyptic beast, r.n.)*, did not know those things of Mine, but only from the antichrist man who was near Me, near those holy of Mine, near those that were established for Me and for those with a holy faith. And behold, the communist man was the slave of the antichrist man, who was standing near Me to take My kingdom and to reign through those that are My holy things. The communist man could not be greater and worse with God than the antichrist man. The first God’s enemy is the antichrist man, and only after that comes the communist man. And behold, the communist one bows to God’s mysteries, which come from heaven on the earth, but the antichrist man does not bow, and he hardly lets himself be thrown down from his place near Me, as he does not take care of My things, and if he does, he does it only that My wealth may become his wealth; his and not Mine.

Oh, little children of the holy faith, I want to win over My vineyard through you from under the antichrist’s slavery, as My vine is Mine, and I am God. Antichrist does not want Me to be a free God. He believes that I will stay forever under his dominion to work as he wants, but the mystery of My kingdom is an unfathomable and incomprehensible mystery, and it is with its dwelling in the sons of the holy faith. Of what use is a dyed kingdom for Me, on which to stay written: the kingdom of God? Oh, the kingdom of God is inside of its sons; it is not within the boundaries of the antichrist man. *(In stone churches, r.n.)* What does the antichrist man do? He woke up and dressed with My garment and set himself in My house and turned it into a hiding for him and for the deeds of his iniquities and for his disciples who keep the world in slavery, for the antichrist man cannot bring the man to the kingdom of God. I told to My disciples when I saw the antichrist that he was setting down over those that are established by Me; I told them: *«Do not be afraid of those who kill the body, but be afraid of those who kill the soul to throw it into Gehenna, to throw it in unbelief and into a state of not watchfulness and carelessness of soul».* The same is today; the man should not be afraid of the communist man, but rather of the antichrist man, who took My little house and garment, and My path to the people.

Behold, sons, My path, which was leading the man to Me, became a human path, so that the people might know the path to those, who took My vineyard and house and table and garment and My path to the people, and the people do no longer know the path to the kingdom of God. No one teaches the people the way to the kingdom of God anymore. There is not another way to be crossed by foot and by walking of the body, for the kingdom of God is in man, and the man looks for it outside of him, as this is how the antichrist, who fights to keep his sons in slavery, taught him, and he fights to take the sons of My kingdom into slavery, who do not know from Me the place of My kingdom in man. No one can find the way to the kingdom of heavens, as it cannot be found on the earth. It is written: *«The heaven is My throne, and the earth is My footstool»*, and this is the kingdom and My rest. And what did I make the heaven and the earth for? Oh, sons, I made them for the man, so that I may be the God of the man, and for him to be a dwelling of My kingdom, to be a throne and a footstool to Me. But the antichrist man teaches his sons otherwise; he does not teach them as I teach, since his sons look otherwise for the kingdom of God, which is given as a gift and not on money. But today, the churches are ruled by those who stand against Me through the people of the antichrist, and they reached to a means of dishonest gain for the man of the antichrist. Salvation is not bought. It is from God, Who gives Himself to the people to save them and to make them His sons, and the Lord is not a king, who takes tribute from His sons. The buildings of the churches, which do not have Me in them, reached to be a goods store where Chris and His forgiveness and the services dedicated to Christ are sold, and in the place called the Holy of Holies, there where My Body and My word should live, the money slip in, the measure of Caesar, sons. Behold what it is on the earth.

Oh, little children of the holy faith, the communist man is not an enemy to Me as much as he is the antichrist man, and My church, My true church is the man of the holy faith, and many of them coming together into My name, as where they are I may also be, and I am with them, for the man of the holy faith is not swallowed *(supported, accepted, r.n.)* by the man of the worldly church, who stands against Me through this deeds and through his unbelief. [/legatura_la_teme]

The man who has no holy faith is unfaithful. Let no one deceive you with empty words, as he, who does not have a holy faith to be a holy man, speaks in vain by his words, if he speaks into My name, even if he uses My teaching in his words. Leave them for they are blind guides, sons, and they do not have the view of the holy faith, and keep Me hidden and put Me in their treasuries to sell Me and to take money and to spend them in the deeds of their iniquities. But you, sons, sons of the holy faith, be holy, as it is written: *«He, who is holy, let him be holy still until the coming of the Most Holy»*. Amen. Nourish yourselves with holiness. Let this be your food. Make yourselves worthy of those holy of Mine, as the pain of the heaven is without limits for the holy things, which are thrown down. I look into the churches from the world when the priests come out with the holy things and say: *„Let us be careful, for those that are holy are given to the saints”. **(At the Divine Liturgy, r.n.)*** This is what they say and then they eat the holy things. This is what they were taught to say, but they do not do what they say, since they are not holy, as the holy people are humble and say: *„Only one is holy”.* But you should know, sons, that if those, which are holy, do not sanctify you to make you holy afterwards, then they do not bring the man into God’s kingdom, for the kingdom of God becomes glory in the man who has it, and that man is known for what he has in him. And behold, the kingdom of God disagrees with the man, who loves lawlessness, and gives you a disgraceful name, you who have the kingdom of God in you.

The holy faith makes the man holy with his life and mind and heart and love, dear sons. I have come to you to make you faithful through the holiness, sons, as the man is not faithful otherwise. I have come with My mother Virgin, as We celebrate a holy feast in a council of saints. We celebrate the feast of My mother’s ascension into heaven, sons, for I have come and I made her body stand up and I told it to come into those from heaven, which are not seen by the earthly eyes. She is with her body in heaven and on earth. She comforts you all with her little comforting hands. She is My mother, the mother of the world Comforter, and she is the mother of comforting, and I am the Firstborn of My brothers, as My mother Virgin made sons of her comforting all those that are adopted by the Father through Me. Amen.

Oh, mother, oh My mother, the earth is without comfort, and its being cries and does not know why it cries. The being of the earth is the man, but the man forgot about the road of comforting. We become the way with comfort and we come to the people with the Holy Spirit, the Comforter, as Our mercy is great and has no boundary in comforting those created by God and the man who lost the house of comforting, the house of heavenly guests on the earth.

Oh, My loved people, I speak with My mother Virgin, so that you may hear My speaking with her and to be comforted, son of today, son of the holy faith. My mother Virgin comforts you, My people, and My holy fruit. My mother is your mother, and this is what she tells you:

*— Oh, little child people, you are like a single child, and my Son keeps you as a little child before the Father, so that the Father may give you all the milk of His comforts, all the joy, all eternity, little child people. I also comfort you, as I have no more patience until I see my Son coming perfectly for His kingdom. I protect the Romanian land with great wake and love, as it is the wedding country of my Son, the country given to Him for His coming in word until the number of the nations is fulfilled. But as for you, people of my Son, which receives my Son as word over the land, I protect you with a love that has never been spoken of in heaven and on earth, as all the saints have been waiting for you to come out; they have been waiting for you as the little one, as the little heir, who gives to all of his brothers before him; as the youngest child that the Father appoints to administer His kingdom for all his brothers, for all the sons of the Father. My son Jesus Christ, will soon let Himself be seen with everything that He has as His glory and with all the sons and daughters of His glory and with all the angels, little child people, for my Son loved you and put His hands over you and blessed you with a great blessing, because you are small and you are a little child, and the little children are pleasant, as this example of love is learned from those of the nature. But the great ones, who pose as servants and watches near my Son, as they say, try to depart you from my Son, but my Son cries out and tells them:* «Allow the little children to come to Me, and do not hinder them, for the Kingdom of God belongs to such as these». Amen, amen, amen.

The little ones know to believe; they know to value; they know to be pleasant; they know to love. The little ones are love and stand close to love as the baby stays near his mother. The big ones are big and are not small, and it is bad of the big one, and every man seeks to be big, as he looks for greatness, and looks to come out from under his Father, to be big too. The big one does not listen anymore. The big one is no longer protected by his parents, for he leaves and becomes big, but the small one stays near his protector and is blessed and watched.

Oh, little child people, I comfort you and I bless you from my Son, Who is the dwelling of all from heaven. Oh, little child people, you should believe in those that will be upon you with my Son, Who comes as word to you. You should believe with great love in my Son, the Word; you should believe until the number of the nations will be fulfilled, as my Son wants to bring salvation to many at your faith, little child people. I take the entire love of the heavenly hosts into my hand and I put it over you and I become my feast in you, together with the Father and the Son within the joy of the Holy Spirit, the Comforter. The mother’s love from heaven covers and protects you in the spirit of the holy faith, to be a dwelling of the kingdom of my Son, little child people. My Son has no other welcome but in you, and you welcomed Him to dine with you and to spread the table for the multitudes.

May your holy faith be blessed, little child people of my Son and mine! Amen, amen, amen.

— Oh, mother, here I am with My people on the earth. I have made a kingdom for Myself on the earth and I have been perfecting it before the nations on the earth, as there is still a little time and I will appear in My glory with My holy people. Amen.
