… Here is what comes: as the godfather renounces his son from satan, so he will call you and say: *„Have you renounced God?”* However, if you are with Me, you will not even get your feet wet, just as Moses escaped through the Red Sea. He who has strong faith escapes. He who has weak faith, that one attacks himself. The denial of God is coming. *(Of those who pass through the Red Sea of the communist epoch, r.n.)*
