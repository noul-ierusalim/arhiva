Open your eyes, sons, for I have reached to an end and you do not know it. It is painful for the one who has not come to learn the words of the Lord, Jesus Christ. The flight of the sinners is coming, to hide in the caves and in the crevices of the mountains. It is painful that you have not taken into account My teachings. You live and say that you die, but what did I shed My blood for? You will answer if you die!

We have a point, sons, but no one knows it. *“Why has the Holy Spirit no longer come to give us more exhortation?”* some say. *“How good is it, that I now do what I want!”*, others say. Oh, what a heavy pain this point will show us! We are in an unknown place, and it should be known. If you knew what point is in front of you, you would stop drinking and eating. Sons, we are meeting the horses of antichrist, the horses of the Apocalypse. We are meeting the dragon that swallows everything and it swallows you too, if you are not faithful to Me.

[legatura_la_teme id_capitol="6" id_subcapitol="2"]… Truly I say to you that many of you will not receive death, but with your body you will receive God.[/legatura_la_teme]

… How beautiful the day will be when heaven meets the earth and the reception to one another will be made! And how deep the sorrow when the disobedients will separate themselves from you and from Me and they will let their heads down!

… Daniel, look in the Scripture and see what point we have in front of us. This point is a symbol, the point which was in front of the pregnant woman, who was sitting with her legs on the moon about to give birth and someone was seeking to know when she was about to give birth to devour the baby, so that it may not be worked as it had been worked through the Lord, Jesus Christ. This point is above and someone is looking now so that the prophecy may be stopped too. After this the prophets will increase in number. You will hear that God works there; do not go. Over there you will hear that the dead will be raised; do not go, for they are not My prophets, and they will deceive you too. Keep in mind that it is not work any longer. It was at Vladimireşti, it was at Maglavit, it was and it is here and it was also at Arsenie Boca, *(Where the first apocalyptic trumpets worked among the prophets from there, r.n.)*, but what was done there? Take good heed for many have strayed from the right prophecy and do not tell the truth any more. For this prophecy, has not been and it will not be. Be faithful and workers until the time of the last.

… There are many good things too and you need to know them. A Christian played a host to a demon, who pretended, and another Christian played a host to God and did not know Him. Therefore, know well the one and the other, so that you may not be deceived.

… Sons, such as this prophesy there has not been and there shall not be.
