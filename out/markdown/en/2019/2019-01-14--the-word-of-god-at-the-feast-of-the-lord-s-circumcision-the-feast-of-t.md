I am with you at work, sons, for you are at work with Me, and I am accompany you with the same measure and we draw ourselves to each other and work for each other, too, and Father always blesses our work and He always looks here, where My work is, for I have always worked here with you.

Peace to you, sons! Whoever hears of you and comes to look and see can understand clearly what king of fruit the faith of the real Christian man has got, and now I am bringing into the book the word that I spoke two thousand years ago, for then I said: *«If a man believes in Me, I will come to him, and make My home with him, I will dine with him and I will reveal Myself to him»*, **(See John: 14/21, 23).** And I also said, *«Nevertheless, when the Son of Man comes, will He find faith on earth?»* **(Luke: 18/8)**

[legatura_la_teme id_capitol="6" id_subcapitol="3"]Sons, sons, would it be possible for Me not to find faith when I want to come? Oh, it would have been like that too, if I had not put faith in you for My coming of today. However, I have given you a great gift: faith, which has prompted Me to fulfill what I spoke at that time: that I would come to the faithful one and will make My home with him, that I would dine with him and reveal Myself to him, oh, and this is what I have already fulfilled with you, and only what is written in the Scriptures have I fulfilled by the faith that I have put in you, oh, and there is no longer any faith on earth in the people to believe like you and to receive Me like you too, for faith is not any kind of faith but only if it brings the Lord on earth, sons, and this means Jesus Christ’s church, a church according to the truth, and otherwise the church is a mere institution under the name of church. And the time has come so near for every man to distinguish My true church from the church, which only has on it the name of a church. Oh, My church has into its midst the spirit of prophecy, as it started two thousand years ago, when My apostle spoke over My church: *«Desire earnestly to spiritual gifts, but especially prophesy, to built Christ’s church on its rock!»* **(See 1 Cor: 14/39)**[/legatura_la_teme]

Oh, sons, I have come out as news and story with you for all the nations to know far and wide that I am with you on earth and that I work upon you with the glory of the word of My mouth and I share Myself from margins to margins, because it is written to fulfill, oh, and the Scriptures cannot be abolished for they have to be fulfilled, not abolished, and behold, I have been speaking over the earth for more than sixty years on the Romanian land, and I now I have you as those who preach My word, following those that were My preachers before you in all these years of My trumpet sounding over the earth.

Oh, sons, I cannot request those who have set themselves upon each other as big and strong on the seats of authority over the Christian flock, I cannot ask them to have faith, for they, like those two thousand years ago, who did not know Me, but only put Me out from among them and crucified Me like a bad man, they, those from today repeat the unbelief of those at that time, but they cannot show Me that they are My true church, but only with My name written on it. However, with the works of its life it is not My church, and that is why in all these years, more than sixty years since I established this word here, I have been calling out to them, and they gave My trumpet to the butchers and did not want to hear what God spoke by this word. Oh, only God alone knows those who are His, but He also knows those that are not His and who cannot believe that the Lord works all the time and that the Lord has fruit in His work with the faithful man.

Oh, sons, it is a holy feast for Me, for two thousand years ago, after I was born of My mother Virgin, I was circumcised according to the law given by the Father to the father Abraham. Oh, I have to work only mysteriously today too, for the faithful people are not in the church today, not even today, sons. However, I do not let the Scriptures unfulfilled, and behold, I have put faith in you and I have fulfilled with you that what I have to work during these days, and when I come to work, I take the saints with Me, as I do not go alone, and I have today in My coming to you the host of the hierarchs from heaven and I am staying before the day of the feast with the bishop Basil and I am giving My joy from you to him, My mystery with you on the hearth of the Romanian people, oh, and great comfort receive My saints from your work with Me, from your faith in My coming to you, oh, and they think that your faith is great, that faith which makes the Lord come down on earth, sons!

Oh, what kind of mind can remain for God and for the self of the one who comes, who believes and fulfills, and then suddenly changes and leaves, and he does no longer has any mind to help him stay with the Lord so that he may not lose the fear of God? Oh, do you know why, those who lose the fear of God, do you know why do they lose their fear of God? Do you know why they lose it? They lose it because of fear. They lose it because they are so fearful and it is written that these people are those who fall from the presence of God. What is the Lord supposed to do with those who are fearful? These are not good for anything for God, as with the Lord you have to fight so that you may not lose Him, because you do not remain otherwise, because the devil divests you of the grace. That is why I urge you, sons of the people of My word, to crush the devil! Do not listen to the fearful men who are afraid of you because you are Mine! Oh, listen to Me and from Me, sons! It is not you who initiated My coming to you, but I did it, starting even with 1955, when you were not here, near the spring. I am the One Who has to tell you what you need to do, and those who want to tell you do something else, then let them stay far from My will with you, this is what I am telling them now. They do not feel God with them, for they are not afraid of the Lord as you are, in order to have the wisdom and understanding that you have from the spring of the fear of God, as it is written in the Scriptures about this kind of fruit of the holy fear. Oh, who has got the Lord in his heart knows that you are with the Lord and from the Lord before them, and those, who do not have the Lord with them, cannot see you, because they do not have the sense of seeing, sons, and this sense of seeing is in accordance with the clean heart in man, without pride in it with respect to one’s neighbors.

Now, sons, I am bestowing upon you great work of blessing and holy powers, that are in the body, in the soul and in the spirit, to be able to prepare the great feast of Epiphany, which is coming in the threshold at once, and where there come together year by year, that is, those who are older than you as far as the mystery of My coming of word on earth concerns, and again, also those whom My Spirit has led as He has done it with you, too, to the spring from here, after that. And as for you, in a few days, you are going to stand up and open the gates of the citadel because the Christians are coming to the spring, sons, and we are going to sing and serve at the feast of Epiphany and we are going to share cleaning water with the Holy Spirit, too, with power of word upon it, and we will be feeding them from above and we will also be giving holy comfort to those who have already set on the way and waited for the starting to the spring. Travelling angels are going to be with them to strengthen their steps and the protection from above when they come and when they go back to their things, and let their faith be known by those who are not faithful, for I am working by this spring of word for the revelation of God’s sons, as it is written for this to be fulfilled.

Oh, sons, prepare a table of holy feast, too! I am relying on you on earth to do My entire work given by the Father to Me to accomplish, and the saints and angels help you, and they protect you on My behalf, too, and all want to serve you when they see Me at work with you, and the hierarch, celebrated with his people from heaven, joins his voice with My voice as a sign that I am coming with the saints, as it is written, sons. **(1Tess: 3/13)**

— Oh, who, Lord, who is on earth to see how You stand above with Your saints and angels and how You give voice to Your mouth to be set into Your today’s book on the hearth of the Romanian people? Oh, You do not need any high buildings and churches with walls to come and to be with the people on earth. Those have much space for the people of the world and are very narrow for You, for Your saints, for You are with the people who are poor in spirit, You are with those who are devoid of the spirit of the world, which stopped for sin and did not give themselves over to You for its ministry, oh, Lord. Those who made buildings of church for themselves and ordained themselves with high authority in them, full of pride, they do not have any share at the table of Your coming with the saints, for You are the One Who are gentle and humble, and You cannot stay with those who are so tired and crushed under the spirit of hypocrisy and human haughtiness, and let them stay aside, let them be discontent within their high thrones, and those who are little and love You with longing and obedience, oh, let them comfort You and know Your coming, Your love and the song of the Spirit, Your sweet word, with which You feed a little handful of people set aside for You to do mysteriously with it Your mysterious works, but well-known by them, oh, and let the world see this beauty and take it in its nature the beautiful spirit of God’s sons, for those who only stay under the name of church, they do not do Your will, they do not love You, Lord, but they rather use the name of Your church. With all this they are far away from You within their faith and life, and You have those who love You, who follow You and who welcome You to come to them, to make Your home with them, to sit at the table with them and to reveal Yourself to them, as You promised then to those who would love You with faith, Lord.

Oh, but it is far from the bishops and priests to serve You; those who pretend that are Your church, for with their interests they only serve people, and in no way do they tend Your flock; on the contrary, they only suck it of its vigor, of money and of everything it has got, oh, and You have great compassion on it, Lord! It was the same in our time on earth too, and we came to You in heaven full of mourning for the flock, for Your flock was nothing but robbed all the time and it was always sucked of powers by those who have stayed over it by the name of shepherds. Oh, no, they had and have something else to do on earth, and here they are today; they serve the politicians, they serve the money and they are the servants of those who give them power to have.

Oh, there is great mourning in heaven, and Your patience speaks to them to seek with faith the right way of their walking with You, for You, Lord, are the One Who will weigh according to every one’s works, You, Lord, You, but they do not want to know this.

— I, the Lord, have got a great host coming from the earth to heaven, and it serves Me from heaven on earth and it helps Me for those who are righteous on earth, and with it I help you, those who are today and those who are carrying the Lord in His coming after man, sons.

Oh, sons, how big the earth is and how big the world is! Oh, well sons, and how few are those who know God in their heart and work! Oh, there is no seeking after God and there is no faith either, and I need faith in order to be able to come as I promised to come, and I have relied on you, after I set you with faith before Me. Oh, learn from Me; learn only from Me, always, always, lest you may take after the world that teaches always only from the world and from the devil, only like this, sons.

And now, sons, we are going to set into the citadel a great feast of Epiphany, and My speaking with you is being strengthened by the Holy Spirit, Who works with the power of lightning of the heavenly light from margins to margins, *(On the Internet, r.n.)* **(See Luke: 17/22-24, Matt: 24/25-27)**, and each one is and will be found within the things he has got to do.

However I have been calling for a long time: Oh, sons of men, seek after Me! Knock so that I may open to you, to answer you, if you knock! I am Who I am and I am the One Who comes. Oh, seek after Me, you, people! The Lord is coming to us, and He is coming with mercy! Hear this! Oh, do you hear the voice of His word? Amen, amen, amen.
