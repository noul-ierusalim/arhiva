I am looking down and bringing My love. It is the feast of the Holy Spirit, the Comforter, on this day, and the hosts of saints are singing here, above, in the garden of My word, and the angels lift up their bugles to announce the feast.

You have opened the gates of the garden, you have been working hard days and nights and you have opened now for the guests to come in at the feast, sons from the spring, and now we are giving the news for the beginning of the celebration, those from above and those from below, from the earth, gathered here for the feast with joy. I am looking down and bring My love and I thank you with respect for My work with you, for your obedience whenever I ask you to bring a sacrifice, and now, peace to you, sons! We are opening the celebration and welcoming the guests from above and from below and we are laying down the spirit of joy between you and Us, and the most unhappy ones are those who do not believe the miracle and My coming to you with the saints and angels at it is written for Me to come.

[legatura_la_teme id_capitol="5" id_subcapitol="6"]First, I want you to know, sons, that from you and up to Us there has been rising and growing a fruit tree with fruit in it during these days while you have been setting the bedding of the celebration of the Holy Spirit. I have put the seed of the fruit tree in My garden with you, and you have been taking care of the life and growth of the tree, you have been working with much peace, with much patience and love, sons, with sacrifice and readiness, and all together are only one fruit tree bearing fruit, and now the angels are sounding their bugles and the beginning of the feast of the Holy Spirit is getting ready and everything is only glory here.[/legatura_la_teme]

Oh, little angels, sound, sound your trumpets, for the joy on this day is great both with Us and with them!

And now, I am giving you joy, to those who have come to the spring of My word. Rejoice, oh, sons! I am giving you the Holy Spirit to be your joy. Oh, welcome to the spring of the descent for the Lord!

Oh, sons, what are your joys? Do you have any joys, sons? Do you make any joys? Do you know how to make your joys? Do you share any joys around to be joy on earth? I told those who were aggrieved at that time: *«Rejoice!»*, and this is what you are to say now to each other, too, oh, sons.

Sound your trumpets, angels of the glory from above, you those who make God’s commandments come true! Sound, for the people to know that the good will is peace, it is glory! Let the people hear the Lord and let them learn what joy means and what the sign of peace on earth means as well.

[legatura_la_teme id_capitol="5" id_subcapitol="1"]Two thousand years ago, I used to walk from place to place and the multitudes were following Me to bring comfort and rest to their grieving spirits, I was setting the table of word and food for their body, I was blessing it and giving it a good taste and I was making them happy with My mercy upon them; I was making Myself known as God with power for miracles, and I was also bestowing gifts upon My disciples saying to them: *«Do the same to those on earth!»* **(See Luke: 6/31)** Oh, and I could hardly wait for the mystery of My coming from the Father to come upon them, the Son of the Father coming in the flesh to be able to bring to man salvation, joy and man to learn to do these, too. I was praying to the Father with petition for man and I was lifting up those who were suffering. I was teaching those who were close to Me the prayer to the Father, oh, and behold what a great blessing is from Me to have a place day and night in the midst of this little village from where prayers, watching, spirit of humility and love to come up for those in heaven. This little mound has the Lord on guard over the town here; it has hands lifted up to heaven and it also has a calling to the Lord for the watching from above, for support, for peace and for longing after heaven, for the watch and for the glory of the country, oh, and that is why it is order here, set from heaven to come up to the Lord; and, therefore, we are organizing feasts of joy, of good will, oh, and all these heal, all build God in man, and that is why I have been raising among people sons useful in heaven so that God may work with them for those that do not look for those in heaven for them on earth, and behold, the work of the Holy Spirit is growing, watching and it is bringing joy and holy work upon people; it brings as spring brings with its coming wonderful flowers and all kinds of fruit, and all grow like man, they grow little by little and offer themselves.[/legatura_la_teme] However, when spring comes with bad weather, which spoils the flower even in its bud, and then its fruit does no longer come out, oh, the same is with man, too; if he has good weather, if the bud of the life blossoms in him, he receives comfort and grows from flower to flower and the fruit passes through many things before it comes to complete ripening, and then it gives itself to God and man as fruit of love and commitment. And on the other hand, if bad weather comes ahead of time and spoils the man’s course and growth, oh, then man does no longer bring forth any fruit and he is no longer to the Lord’s joy.

Oh, the lack of fruit is not good, sons. Let us invite all to a sweet table with those in heaven in days of holy feast, for behold, I am trying to teach you how to be with the heaven, My sons, and this is how We are to teach many at the hearing of My voice upon you here. The holy prayer comes out from the holy man and makes the connection of the heaven with the earth, oh, and then people would be to bear a great burden if the holy prayers and holy saints had not been heard giving to God with a clean heart and with clean hands so that their fruit may come back to people.

Oh, sons, sons, I thank you for your coming at the water of the spring of My word, and My joy is great as many times as I have you gathered here. I always have ordained holy fulfillments to do good with them to those on earth. Oh, I am teaching you to take care of you by your resemblance to Me as much as possible for you, sons. The human body has to be a vessel in which God dwells, and care must be taken not to let anything unclean enter it, for the human body is the house of God.

[legatura_la_teme id_capitol="6" id_subcapitol="3"]I have been teaching the people, tended by My word, and telling it not to take from man, and rather to prepare everything by himself, with his hands washed, everything that comes into his body, of God’s son’s among people, because people do not wash, they do not wash their hands and their heart, and neither do they wash their mind or spirit, or the house where they live on earth, and if you, man, are not and do not stay clean, oh, God does not dwell within you, and it is known that God does not dwell with His face in you.[/legatura_la_teme]

Oh, come to teach you, come to the spring and take advice for your life from the Lord, sons! Rejoice that I have a healthy people with its fulfillment here, and for which I have waited for such a long time to come out on earth through its obedience to God.

Yes, sons, I lift up more and more, higher and higher above the evil, those who are the people of My coming, and you, those who receive from them My word springing here upon them from My mouth, oh, you have got now a strain in your heart, and I, the Lord, understand it, and here is what I am telling you now: [legatura_la_teme id_capitol="6" id_subcapitol="3"]I have told those who are My people, ready and more and more prepared, I have told them not to eat any bodies but to eat only those that the Lord ordained in paradise for man to use as his food. Oh, neither do I offer, nor push anyone to take more and more of his steps to fulfill more and more completely the prayer with fasting, so that victory may be through this. However, I am still saying, sons, that he is overcome by satan, and that the time has come to have victory against him, oh, and he and his devils cannot be overcome but only by fasting and praying, these two powers helping each other against the enemy devil, who was born even from paradise to become the man’s enemy by his disobedience to God’s will upon him.

When I built man, I did not built him to eat bodies with a living soul, nor to eat what comes out of bodies, for I wanted man to be immortal, and I advised him what to eat in order to live for God, for the One Who had created him.

Oh, man did not receive the obedience to the Lord in paradise, but now I am coming on earth as word and My way of coming is built by the people who are obedient to the law of heaven and life. And I am telling you now, to those who are confused and questioning, that you may choose to be on the right or on the left of the obedience to God, and I am telling you, sons, do not be confused. Yes, I have come and told the people of My word not to eat the fruit of the little bees, but I did not force anyone to do what I asked the people of My word to do, the people I need to work the fasting and praying in a wholesome way; however, anyone who does not choose to do so, that one is not among the sons of My people, for he alone chooses not to fulfill the Lord’s will, and if he chooses so, then so it remains and he remains on the left side.

Oh, sons, more and more difficult days are coming, and they are coming because of the non-fulfillment of God’s love for the people on earth. People cannot be guarded from evil unless they dwell in God’s will, oh, and there can be no bargain for the man’s protection from the Lord. Now, each one chooses, and I have the duty to say, for I am the One Who foresees and I announce man to avoid the way of evil, for evil comes through the man’s disobedience of seven thousand years ago, oh, sons, because man wanted to be like him even from the beginning, and he did not want to be like God.

Oh, and this kind of advice I have over the people of My word and for its good conduct before God, which keeps the Christian far from fire, from sins, from big damages. And the same is with the holy conduct too, the same is with the shunning of evil, which man does on behalf of the devil, who pushes him to sin.[/legatura_la_teme]

Oh, sons, two thousand years ago, I went to be with the Father, and I told those who were following Me that the Holy Spirit would come and take from God and tell them everything that were to be told. Oh, and this is what I am doing now through the Holy Spirit, and I am bringing advice for those who follow Me with their fulfillment and conduct. And I am giving you these dear gifts, too, if you want to have them, but I do not press anyone, but each one chooses to be on the right or on the left, as he wants, as he chooses. However, I, the Lord, want you all to be My joy, My rest, but satan also wants you to be his joy, his gain, oh, and how shall I not tell you this, to know that the Lord had created man to be His house?

I like it very much to gather you here once in a while to spend time with you and to teach you the mysteries of life and the beauty of the holy man. I miss very much to have as many sons of the law of the heaven as possible, and I miss to remind you of it, sons. Oh, you know how much I suffer that God cannot be with man? I am holding you tight at My bosom and I am asking you this with longing, that I long very much, and My longing prompts Me to come on the way and teach you.

Two thousand years ago, I spoke about a very deep mystery; I said that I was going to set the table and invite to it the sons of the kingdom of the heavens on earth; however, they did not come, and then I sent My servants to invite and gather from everywhere the armless, the crippled, the paralytics and all those forgotten by everyone, and to bring and have them sit at My table and fill the table, to which those who were invited did not want to come.

Oh, here is the table, here is the parable I spoke about at that time, and those who call themselves My church do not come, they do not come closer, they do not like to do God’s will, and hide from it behind their unfaithfulness in the word that has been springing out of My mouth for such a long time on the hearth of this people, chosen now, in the end of the time, so that I may come on earth to fulfill the work of My coming, and if My coming in the word had not been true, oh, its trace would have been lost, it would have been lost a long time ago; however, I go and not a jota of the word of My today’s coming will pass without its fulfillment.

Oh, so it was, that I called and call those who understand and believe in their heart and know Me after My song of Shepherd, and those called did not want to come, and they are afraid of their rulers and of those who are their little ones, and do not have power to come. However, I have a people, I have a field, I have found faith on earth, oh, and the Lord’s will does not perish from the midst of the people, because I have found an obedient people and protected by its obedience, and My glory is with it.

Oh, to this glory you gather when you come at My voice, which is calling you at My table, at the table empty of guests and which is waiting to be filled and to bring joy.

Oh, sons, gathered at My table, rejoice as I, the Lord, rejoice when you come. Oh, learn from Me to share true joys, the party with God, sons. It is a great honor that I give you, and you remain indebted for it, sons.

I am looking down and I am coming with My love. I am gathering you under My look and love. I am visiting you with mercy. I am giving you powers from above for My will in you. I miss you to be at My table with those coming from heaven here at the feasts. Oh, be wise. Look at the fruit in a tree to see how it grows day by day until it becomes tasty, sweet and giving of health. Oh, in the same way you should want to be for Me, to grow like the fruit that grows bigger day by day, and which it grows like man, little by little until the fulfillment of the ripe fruit. The Holy Spirit, the Comforter, is teaching you all these things. You are the sheep that do not have a fold, for there is no sheepfold on earth, nor shepherds with mercy on My sheep.

I want to make sheep of those who hear My voice and I want to be their Shepherd and to guard them from the wolves and from enemies. Oh, listen to Me that I may be your Shepherd, for you are not tended, sons, and that is why you do not know how to do My will. Behold, I am coming and teaching you. You know My song with which I shepherd you, oh, sons! I want to feed you with sweetness, with joy, with the peace from above, sons. There is a feast of word of the Holy Spirit. Take and eat from heaven, for everything is poisoned on earth. I come on another way and I do not let you die, only for you to know My voice and mercy for you.

Now I am setting your steps on the way back and I am giving you angels on your way, sons. Oh, do not forget about My place and table here. My voice is milk and honey from heaven upon you. Oh, peace to you! I wrap you up in My longing for you. I am asking your heart to dwell in it, to put in it, so that it may be able to do. Peace to you! Remain My people! I remain with you, sons, only for you to want this.

And as for you, people from the spring, oh, behold, you have succeeded. You have prepared My bedding, and I will give you joy for your love. I am your rest. You are My rest. I thank you that you are Mine. I thank you now and forever because you carry Me.

Oh, peace be upon you, son, My people, son and fruit of My coming as word on earth!

We remain embraced together, sons. Peace to you, peace to you, peace to you! Amen, amen, amen.
