From earth and heaven it was set and it is set here now a day of feast and guests at the heavenly table, on the table My word and My love in it and with it over you, those in heaven and on earth gathered together at the feast of My angels, and peace to you! And peace over the citadel of My word and over My work over it, as well!

Oh, peace to you, sons! And peace to those who are sitting at My table with you! The heavenly hosts are matching their state of celebration, but also their look and spirit, their longing and all their joy near Me with you here, and the love of you is embracing them all with every passing moment.

Behold My little house of longing, My story of longing, My citadel with watchmen in it for the work of My coming as word on earth now!

We are sitting down, those from heaven, those who are invisible and work from God’s invisible things, and the spirit is set on fire with longing and thrill.

Oh, each one of you set your spirit in My love, for you have made your way to the spring, taking to the road early in the morning, and I, the Lord, come out on your doorsteps, for I have waited for you and opened for you with a feast. Oh, come under My love full of celebration here today, and then let your spirit bear My truth in you, My love in you and over you, that My hope for you may not be in vain, for I want you to remain with Me forever, but it is not enough for Me alone to want for you, but you also should want more.

I am setting you before Me, and here it is how: Oh, do not bow down to the spirit of doubt concerning My word, concerning My spring of word and concerning the place between Me and you, concerning those who give Me to you when I come to give you from heaven; stand aside from those that might wound your faith, the miracle I have prepared for you as a gift, and also stay awake for it and pray to Me, sons; oh, pray so that your faith in My coming to you may not be weakened; pray with longing and for longing, for without My longing in you, you may wake up with another longing, which is able to steal you from My way.

And behold, I and those in heaven have welcomed you and sat you at the table, and We have showed you the way you are supposed to stand before Me as today, always, always, for you should stay only with God, because if only for a moment you take off your longing and your faith from Me, the temptations that lie in wait to find you weak and without a cross come to enchant you with those that are not from God in the man’s way with Me, and I teach you My way.

Therefore, we are setting them at the table and giving them the entire comfort that is here today, and their joy is as great as their comfort.

Oh, many are in tears and are wounded by longing, and there are so many when they come, and their evil works come into their way and there is not much in their life worked on earth into the name of the Lord, something by which I may be able to help them somehow face to face with the winning devil. Oh, and I am asking you, those whom I have set at the work of the resurrection of all things, make a descent for them and tell to the Lord: Lord, have mercy on those who have departed from life unprepared, have mercy for those who have not known You by their works, and may Your will be done according to the measure of Your mercy for all the creatures which have been living on earth.

Oh, praying sons for those who have passed away from the earth, oh, My sons, pray, and pray for those who were in their earthly life, but also who are still alive and rich, for they, among those who are gone, are waiting for mercy now, sons. However, I am telling those who are not taken out of their flesh yet, and I am telling them why there are riches on earth and why they are rich.

Behold, you are tested by God to see if you have faith in your service to the One Who is the Treasurer of the man’s goodness, life, health and wealth. Know that all the rich should come to understand why they are rich, for the Lord has given them to have and He gives them to be stewards, and He has entrusted to them pantries with goods so that they may share wisely and without partiality with those who do not have a pantry. The rich should not consider themselves rich, but that God have only set them in the service for their fellows, and they should have nothing more than the others, who do not have this stewardship, for God have entrusted to them the pantry for those who receive food from it, clothes, shelter, seeds of instruction, watch and the entire care on My behalf through those who are entrusted the work of stewards, and they are not supposed to have more, nothing more than others, so that they may have in heaven gathering and reward for their sacrifice within service. A horse serves a man without asking something from the master it serves who gives it shelter and care. [/legatura_la_teme]I am blessing now and I am receiving the table of remembrance for them, and they are taking with thankfulness from the today’s table and are bowing with respect, and are happy to take from God.

Oh, but it is an angelic rustle and the little house above is full of glory, for the angelic hosts have no boundary, and they are in their celebration now and everything is beautiful, a beautiful view, a beauty as in heaven, the heavenly powers row by row: The thrones, the Dominions, the Beginnings, the Principalities, the Powers, the Cherubim and Seraphim, the Archangels and Angels, **(See also Col: 1/16)**, and all bow before the throne of the Lord’s glory, and all the heavenly armies are coming together here and leaving their powers for the power of the Lord’s citadel and its entire work during these days on earth here.

I, the Lord, am blessing the feast, the sitting at the table, the spirit, the speech and the exhortation on the table and the hearts that are taking from My table, and we are giving voice to the day of feast, together with My servants that are serving here.

Oh, My people, let each one of you take from God. You do not have to take from people. Do not take alone but with Me, sons; I am giving you! Oh, do not share with one another, rather share with Me everything you have to share! At the Last Supper, I gave to My disciples at that time and they did not take alone, and after that I empowered them and told them this: *«What I have done to you, do the same but with Me; do the same with a seal!».*

I am telling all those who have taken their steps to come closer to the work of My word, and I am telling them this:

If you are not able to stay with God to the end, if you cannot, son, walk all the way to the end, then you sell your brother on the way, for this means not to stay until the end. Behold, Judas walked on the way with Christ’s brothers and did his job, but after a while he stepped aside, fell from near the brothers and died in his mistake on the last day of his walking. And there is a question here: Why did he become a traitor on the Lord’s way and then for the Lord and brothers? It was because he walked sideways and worked likewise, too. He worked on the sides but in the name of the ministry for My walking on earth with My work at that time.[/legatura_la_teme]

Oh, sons, the one who works from Me, that one works everything knowingly, only at sight, those that are known by all with whom he lives, oh, and only a little self-walking can cost the one who uses his staying near Me for the unknown work, but in the name of the community. Judas, dealt guiltily with the community possession and he went wrong by not faithfully fulfilling the mission, which he had and loved, because if he had not loved it, he would have done greater evil.

Oh, son, you should not follow those who say: let us hide with the things we are going to work! Oh, you should not consider good the good works with which you hide, for the devil teaches you to hide and to turn good into evil, and to demolish the one you say that you do him good. Judas went in the name of the community and did what he did, good or bad, but then he separated from the community of My apostles because of his walking and because of his working alone.

Oh, sons, this work of Mine cannot be a pledge for you when you work indirectly as on behalf of My table of work with you. Many can stumble and you do not know how, those that you do without knowledge, fall. Let the people learn well not to take anything as they may have come from Me, but rather let him examine well what it comes to him as on My behalf and from My work worked with a helm.

I waited for Judas to return to reconcile him with Me before My resurrection, which was to prove that I was true God. However, he never came back, for the devil did not allow him to come. Oh, and that is why you must learn to do all of your works only knowingly and the devil will flee.

Those who fear the Lord are those who walk in His ways, as it is written. Oh, learn to be with God. You have to spend more time with God. Nevertheless, how does this come? Oh, it is the same with the time for other works of the mind, but the best time of the mind is your staying with God, sons, and man is no longer taught to spend with the Lord, oh, and that is why he suffers much.

Oh, sons, those who stand watch for Me in My citadel do not have to be drawn to the way of living of those beyond the borders of the citadel because a state of heart and mind is needed here. These must not be used anyhow and for anything, but rather they must be kept for the Lord and for His holy work, far from the world and from its spirit, and the world with its abominations of all kinds must stay away from them. Oh, and you have the duty to know this, especially that they are under My burden, for which self-denial is needed that I may be able to work with My plan in them for man, and their work with Me is great, and, by staying under this glory, they will rejoice then over how much they will have understood what God means in the life of a man who lives with the Lord on earth and who lives for the Lord.
