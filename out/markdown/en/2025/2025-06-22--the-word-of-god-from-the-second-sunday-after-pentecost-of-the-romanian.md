I walk with the clouds between heaven and earth, **(Apoc: 1/7)** between earth and heaven, and I have hosts of saints and angels accompanying Me and I leave the word of My mouth upon the earth, for I am a Shepherd and I do the work given to Me by the Father in order to fulfill it and to draw to Him those who take the voice and the word of My voice and carry it in their hearts, if they know with their spirit the voice of the Shepherd from heaven who came to call and shepherd the one who draws to this sweet pasture. I have a wonderful name like My voice and I call Myself the Word of God, **(Apoc: 19/13)** as in the beginning, when I spoke and heaven and earth and man, when all the visible creation appeared, and from the invisible things of God all came to be seen and are, and have got God, as their Maker, from then until today and until tomorrow, and therefore, I am the Alpha and the Omega, the beginning and the end of the works of God, and I am coming now with the timeless eternity, for time has passed and passes away completely and God remains with His, remains with the fruit of His seven thousand years of work and better still, the Lord and His love remain and He says to those who love Him: *“Remain in My love!”*

Oh, I cannot, I cannot stop speaking to you, My country of today. You are the land chosen by God at the beginning and at the end for His glory, for His divine work in the midst of the visible things of His creation. I cannot remain silent, I will no longer remain silent, since My glory and yours are approaching upon the earth, especially since those to whom I keep speaking do not heed My voice, which comes with the clouds, for this is how I am coming, I am coming with the clouds, for thus it is written that I am coming.

Oh, arise, Romanian nation! Pay attention to the One Who speaks to you from heaven among the saints, oh, and I will raise you up and all those dressed in shepherds' garments of My flock will take note, will be called to account for the fate and for the faith or unbelief of those who are shepherded or not shepherded, for here I am, the Shepherd Who came unexpectedly to those on earth, and who does not watch, as I left a written word, two thousand years ago, for I said: *«Watch, for you do not know when the Shepherd is coming to visit His flock!»* **(See also: Luke: 2/8; Matthew: 24/42-44)**

Oh, those who are not shepherded are leaving, leaving the flock, they are leaving towards the spirit and poison of the world, and the world is My enemy to Me and to them, for I said that friendship with the world is enmity with God, oh, and they do not pay attention to My word from two thousand years ago, so that those who read it and know it, may be able to read it later and keep it fulfilled, starting with the shepherds, whose job is to guard My flock from wolves.

Oh, My Romanian country, I have My Virgin mother protecting you, and she does not leave you from under her hand and loves you like Her Son, Jesus Christ, for you are My rest after My long wait, and I am preparing you for My wedding with you in the sight of all peoples, because I am marrying in you with a table of words, and I have been writing My word on your hearth for so long, and I am writing in My book today, seventy years of words, **(Apoc: 14/6)** from which the saints and angels have been nourished sitting at My table here, on your sweet land for Me and their hosts of saints, oh, therefore, I cannot leave you from under My sight, so that any enemy may harm you, and if he were to do this to you, he cannot, because over My Virgin mother there is no passing, and all evil is shattered and returns from where it would strike, and here is and remains the Lord and the walking of His word over the earth, because this word is the fear of every enemy, and God is not silent, and the enemies are silent and will remain silent, for My light in your midst is great, and with the power of the word from My mouth issued upon every enemy wind, and all the evil that may arise gives way to Me and moves away, and the fire turns back wherever it might approach.

And I have said this now so that you may know, Romanian people, that the source of My word is the fire of the Holy Spirit that cuts and removes every evil thought, every enemy hand and weapon, and behold how wonderful you are, My country, My dear, My beautiful one from the end of time, land of your saints from all ages, for behold how they stand at the border with their protective hands, with their Romanian spirit, with their election from God, oh, that there is no more glorious company among the saints than the company of the Romanian saints, and this because of your election from God, My country, My Romanian daughter!

Oh, I cannot forget you, I cannot let you out of My hand, out of My love. The Father has great glory for Me in your land, My country. I will lead you to the love of God and I will open windows to heaven for you, so that you may see and understand your choice, for after I rejected Jerusalem, which had rejected Me and cast Me out of its hearth, I, the Lord, bound Myself with you with a holy covenant made by those in heaven, and the Father chose you for the mystery of the new Jerusalem in your midst, **(Zacarias/Zechariah: 2/10-13.)** and now I am coming, fulfilling and proclaiming you Mine and I am showing Myself with you over the nations, and from glory to glory I am ascending above on the clouds, shaking My word of My love to you, oh, My country, My flower of forget-me-nots.

Oh, do you hear the Lord speaking over this chosen land, at the end as at the beginning? Are you hearing, you who are struggling to stay and continue to lead the country, after you have sucked its power, as much as you could, for as long and always? Here you are, you still need this country for yourselves! You keep confessing your discontent and longing for power, rule on high seats. You are without God in your hearts and that is why you have been so dishonest with the body and spirit of this nation. You are behaving like gods with rights over this land and always saying that you deserve the biggest slice to sit at the helm.

Oh, until when will you keep being arrogant and believing yourself to be the most compared to the others who are fewer in number? You are among the remaining ones, who were before you for decades, sucking the blood of the impoverished in the country and you took control by killing your leader, *(Those remaining from the Communist Party during the dictatorship of the communist red beast, overthrown from power by "revolution" in December 1989, when the head of this beast, the dictator Nicolae Ceaușescu, was arrested, summarily tried and executed, on December 25, 1989, by those who made this plot against him and seized power further, calling themselves social democrats, r.n.)*, and since then, you have been calling yourselves the saviors of the nation, but your deeds are completely evil, they are theft, debauchery, high-ranking lies, justice chained by unjust judges, and which plays to your liking and covers up your small and large transgressions, oh, and for too long you have been oppressing this people calling you the most, with the greatest rights, the greatest party. Oh, well, how could you not be the most if you had the country's money on hand and bought those who would elect you, and that is why I, the Lord, am telling you that you elected yourselves, because you paid your voters and are sick with the desire for power and are not liked by people, on the contrary, you are looked down upon, because you oppress them and those who try to straighten out the things you have distorted, and I, the Lord, have great patience with you, so that you do not fall under your iniquities, nourished for so many years with the spirit of pride in you and with great skill to put on a show and pretend that you are righteous. However, you only sit in sinful parties and in oppression over the little ones, who find it increasingly difficult under your enemy's hand, oh, and I have told you this day the truth about you and about the work of lies with which you have been sitting for so long on the chest of this people, so that they will not rise up and gain their freedom from under you, the oppressors over them. Oh, enough with you! Bow down, for you are many! Your oppression over this nation is great, and you will have to give an answer soon, soon.

Here, I am now telling you the words of a saint of the Romanian nation, who said and wrote thus: *“The soul of a country abandoned by the living is left in the care of the dead, the great dead, and is restored through them.”* So, listen to My word over you! All the saints of the Romanian nation are rising up and entering into holy battle against those who are remaining in iniquities, **(Apoc: 2/26)** and are trying to lead this holy country, which also includes those who were and have gone among the ranks of the saints, for there is only one country, Romania, the one on earth and the one in heaven. Oh, how could you dare to believe that these saints are dead, when Christians are crying out to them to intercede with God for their needs on earth? Here they are, rising up now and rebuilding their country and yours from the foundations, if you have had such a lack of faith that you wanted to keep standing at the helm for the country as you are!

May the hosts of Romanian saints and their holy struggle for the cleansing of lawlessness from the Romanian country and land be blessed, for the glory of the Lord is coming and will encompass this land and nation from all over, and that is why I am placing the seal of blessing and heavenly victory for the fate from God of the Romanian Christian nation, for heaven is still waiting for this nation to clothe itself in the indescribable beauty of the Christian life, of the likeness with the Lord and the saints, for My glory is waiting at the border to illuminate the world on earth from God and to heal the blindness of those who do not see the Lord anywhere below or above, but only know and see themselves. Oh, no! My time is coming and it has come. The saints are coming with Me, **(1Thess: 3/13; Jude: 1/14; Apoc: 19/14.)** for the time has come to come, when I am coming.

Oh, arise, My Romania, arise! Come, arise, My love! The Lord is clothing you in the celebration of His wedding with you. You will shine more and more over the earth and over the peoples because of the glory of My word that has its throne and source on your land. The glory of the New Jerusalem is coming to your hearth and you will be the light of the world as it is written. **(Apoc: 21/1-3)** You will be light and word on your land, on which I have been writing Myself with seventy years of word and with this feast proclaimed from edge to edge, and all the peoples will rejoice in My love over you.

Peace to you, much peace to you, to you and to those who have now been lifted up by My powerful hand and placed with difficulty at your helm, so that you may learn the justice of things, the beauty of behavior between Romanian and Romanian, between brother and brother, oh, and I will always draw near and give you My advice, so that you may benefit from it, oh, My country of today.

Peace to you, My country, My Romania! I will open Myself more and more towards you. I am the Lord your God, I am the light in your midst and I will be with you a heavenly light in the midst of the peoples of the earth, and the gift of faith will come to their aid, and the world will be enlightened, will change in face and love and will pass from glory to glory towards a more beauty, towards a greater destiny, greater than has ever been in ages and will never be again, when everything will change in an instant and everything will become new **(Apoc: 21/5)** because eternity is coming, spring is coming with its gifts, the Lord is coming, and this holy news is no lie!

The new heaven and the new earth are coming, for so it is written, and all will be clothed in glory that will never pass away or end, and I, the Lord, walk with the clouds between heaven and earth, between earth and heaven, and I have the hosts of saints and angels as My companions, and I am the Shepherd on earth, and My language is sweet and it is the language from the beginning with which I spoke when I created heaven and earth and man, and I am still speaking in this language at the end, when I am leaving My word upon the earth in the language of the Romanian people. Amen, amen, amen.
