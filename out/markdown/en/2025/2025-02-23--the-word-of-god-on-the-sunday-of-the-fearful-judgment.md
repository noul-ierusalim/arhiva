On clouds of heavenly glory I go above the citadel of My word on the land of the Romanian nation and I let My voice be heard, so that My word may be placed in the book and may it go to present Me to those to whom I speak, and then may it be fulfilled and so that I may work through the word everything that I bring to light, everything that the Father gives Me to work.

Peace to you, My country today, peace to you, Romanian nation! I am the Lord your God, Jesus Christ, and I stand above with the heavenly hosts and watch over you so that you do not waver in moments of trial. I no longer wait for you to believe or fulfill My word, because for this you need the Holy Spirit in your spirit, and that is why I sound the trumpet My word for you and it has the power to be fulfilled, oh, that there is great haste for the laying down of the enemies within and from the sides, for your glory from God awaits you at the border, My country. You are the country of My coming from the Father, the word on earth, now, at the end of time. My new name is written by angels over you: the Alpha and the Omega, the Word of God. **(Apoc: 19/13)** You are the land of the Sun, for God the Word has a citadel of stone on your land and the Lord comes out of the citadel word upon the earth, He comes out like a Bridegroom from His wedding bedroom and He becomes the light of the world, speaks over peoples and languages and rules them with a rod of iron, as it is written about My coming with the glory of the word, and therefore I raise My voice and speak peace upon the earth, and behold the work of God upon you, Romanian country, and you have in the coming days, you have to place at the helm at the top, *(At the presidential elections that will soon follow, r.n.)*, a man with knowledge for this work, and so that he can carry this sweet cross with help from heaven, and behold, I am watching and have been watching for some time to remove the enemies, to heal wounds and traces of wounds, to forgive those done wrongly, which have brought trials upon the country, but also enemies, because behold how many give to take part in this competition! Oh, however, let there be great and ever-greater care, and in no way let there be work according to human desires, for the weather is very foggy, and therefore, it must be well considered by those in the chairs, lest they make mistakes that will hurt later and continue to hurt.

A man with clean hands is needed to lead the country, with a quick mind, with high training, with love for his people and country, with comprehensive eyes, with knowledge of the languages of the peoples, and this is why the Lord must work, and not man, not those who have made mistakes in every way possible until now, and who must no longer make mistakes, since enemies of all kinds lie in wait, so that they can conquer this country and climb to the helm; however, I, the Lord, stand watch and make My path with the word and sort to the right and to the left and bless the high seat of the country and the one suitable for this service, oh, that I want the peoples to see and hear that God works and that He can do what is impossible for men, for it is written: *«What is impossible with men is possible with God».* **(Luke: 18/27)**

Oh, Romanian people, I bless your spirit and love and share with you, knowledge from above from God, for in these days you will know how to work for your destiny today and tomorrow, because the Lord has a work of great glory on your hearth. Oh, do not seek to make mistakes again and again. Enough! Take from the Lord, and work like this. You have the Son of God as Shepherd above you. Here is My word over you! We will soon celebrate seventy years of word on your hearth and this day will be heavenly on your land, where a great crowd will gather for this confessional feast and I want you to prepare yourself beautifully for this holy mystery, for I await you, I await your sons prepared for the feast in Romanian clothes and I will speak to them the final word, the voice of the Lord over the Romanian land.

I promised you that I will discipline all the enemies, evildoers and troublemakers, all the liars and the arrogant, and I will prepare for you a little shirt of glory, My country, and you will celebrate with those in heaven, we will stand above on the clouds and I will shake the Holy Spirit and peace over the earth, and for this greatness the spirit of peace is needed everywhere, and here I am saying:

[/legatura_la_teme][legatura_la_teme id_capitol="3" id_subcapitol="5"]Peace to you, peoples and leaders over the peoples! Oh, be good, be good, be good! Be humble, be gentle, be peacemakers! I, the Lord God, send you this word from the land of the sun, where My word dwells.

Peace to you on Sunday! Be sons of Sunday, be sons of the resurrection! Peace to you! Cherish yourselves as brothers! Let love work in you, let heaven embrace you in its beauty! Make peace on earth! Bring your hearts to the Lord!

Let the year of the Lord's favor be written on earth, the year of peace! The Lord is coming as word on earth and sharing Himself with you. I am the Alpha and the Omega. The beginning and the end are united for a new beginning, a new heaven and a new earth. Lies work from the earth, and from heaven, the renewal of the world. Be faithful to this word!

I am the Lord, Jesus Christ, and I have written Myself on earth with seventy years of word on the land of the Romanian nation. My enemy is called antichrist and he melts before the glory of My word. The time of the sons of God on earth is coming, for antichrist falls under the breath of My mouth.

Be reconciled to God, you nations, peoples and leaders of the peoples! Bow down to His love, be full of love! The heavenly Father is sending Me to you, and this day I have spoken My word to you in the name of the Father, of the Son and of the Holy Spirit, and I have embraced you in My love.

I will always and always speak to you, for the work with which I come from heaven to earth is the word, and I now leaving My name on My letter to you:

Jesus Christ, the Word of God. Amen, amen, amen.[/legatura_la_teme]
