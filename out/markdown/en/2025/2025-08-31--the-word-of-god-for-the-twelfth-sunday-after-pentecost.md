Again, and again My embrace as your Shepherd is embracing you in My word, country Romania, My country of longing, My country of today. I am in your heaven and on your earth with My third coming, oh, and do not ask yourself why so, for with you it is different than with other lands of the earth, My love.

I had stood on your earth in the beginning, when after the creation of heaven and earth, I had took with My hand from your clay and created man in My image and likeness, and when for a moment I had rejoiced incomparably, until My joy changed into pain, for I lost man through his disobedience to his Maker, and then through the loss of his love and fear of God and the wisdom given to him by grace, oh, and after all this, man received the heavy punishment, fell into conceit, and from this pain of losing man the Father sent Me again to earth to take him out of the waters of the flood, and I took him up on it with the word, and you are the head of the earth and again, I stood in work with you on earth, My dear land to Me, and behold, I have now come to you for the new creation, for a new heaven and a new earth, the land of My country, and I am kissing your land and the place where I stood with My feet and blessed you with the touch of My divine body, where you have this mysterious sign on your hearth, oh, and I cannot let you get lost from My work with you, and I am coming to you with My new and a lot of work, and I am writing My book with you and I am protecting you with My word at most, so that satan may hear that you have the seal of the Son of God on your body and let himself be crushed here by the sword of My word, and so that then eternity may begin, and so that I may prepare everything and take back My joy from the beginning, and for which I have longed sighing for seven thousand years and better, oh, and I have awaited this moment, My country.

I am Alpha and Omega, and so are you, for with you I begin and with you I end to begin again, country Romania, My country of longing.

And now, I am reminding everyone of My speech that was given two thousand years ago from the moment of My ascension to the Father on the clouds, after I conquered death on the cross and gave resurrection to those on that day, when I rose from the dead and raised many with Me to the Father, **(Matt: 27/50-53)** oh, and I told My disciples at that time, that I would be with them until the end of time, and if time is ending, the Lord is coming to earth as word, as it is written to fulfill My coming again to people, and here I am clothed in word above the Romanian land, as it is written about My coming with armies from heaven, for thus it is written: *«He bears a written name, which no one knows but He alone, and His name is the Word of God, **(Apoc: 19/11-13)** and out of His mouth is coming a sharp sword that with it He may strike the nations and rule them with a rod of iron, and He has this name written on His thigh: King of kings and Lord of Lords»*, and behold the coming of Christ, My coming to earth, the word of new creation, and all these words are true! Amen.

I am asking you, My country, to take care of the Lord your God, to take care to believe in Him and receive Him with your heart and faith, so that I may protect you, so that I may always wrap you in My word and nothing and no one may stand against you. Oh, stay under My guard, under My little umbrella, stay in My Shepherd's arm, for time is pressing as at the end, and I have to make with you a new beginning, a new creation, and behold, I am marking on your land places, chosen for My glory, so that I may establish on them the mystery of eternity, life without death, without sin, My country, as in heaven so on earth, My bride, and you may stay under My shield, under My word, under which I keep you sheltered from bad weather, oh, and power from above will come upon you, so that you may do My will, so that you may be able to work for God and for you, Christian land, to be filled with longing, because My longing for you is growing among those in heaven, who are now weaving your garment of glory to clothe you in it, to prepare you for the wedding table, and behold, this is why the Lord is coming to you with a word of longing, a word of vigil, a word of love and comfort, for where is My word spoken by My Shepherd's voice, sweet grass grows and a choice table is set and you will be My beauty, My joy, My comfort you will be, because I long for comfort in pain and I miss it.

So, your being is born again, it is born from heaven, from My word, and you will give buds and you will be My field of immortal flowers, you will be My garden of paradise, because I am beginning the paradise in you and I will prepare you with a holy dress, so that you can be for paradise, My Romania, and you will be the land of the Lord's flowers, oh, and I am speaking great words for you before I am fulfilling them in you, and so that you may be Mine through your love, My country.

Every people and land has a guardian angel assigned to them by the Lord, as it is written, but you have over you not only the angel of the Lord, for you have My Virgin mother, who has carried you in her arms for two thousand years, and that is why the mystery and being of this land and nation is sweet in heaven.

Oh, do not worry, My country, do not worry like the world that worries about tomorrow. Do not let yourself be overwhelmed by fear and by the many lies of the arrogant that they know, that they say. You should stand in the Spirit of the Lord even if you go through temptations for your washing to be beautiful later, for all things are written, as they are and as they will come, and all are under the Lords’s eyes, and the arrogant man will bow down, will humble himself, will be ashamed.

The time is coming to separate the tares from the wheat and then My wheat will grow on your land; however, seek to make your life wheat, My land, because only the wheat will remain for God, only the full grain, My beloved, and nothing else is to be expected except your preparation according to My will on earth for your salvation, because your name is written in the Scriptures that you are the Lord's land, do you hear?

And again, on Sunday I am embracing you and caring you in My word, so that you would be the blessed one, the protected one and the loved one, oh, and I am awaiting the sweet moment when I will appear with you on earth to all nations and languages, and every breath will find out who God is, His people and the mountain of the glory of the Lord are, from which His word flows like a river over the earth.

I am the Alpha and the Omega on the Romanian land. I had stood on this land in the beginning, and again after the flood, and behold, again I descended My body and marked My third coming on the Romanian land and I began in 1955 the sounding of the trumpet of My word through the trumpet from Maluri village, and now a garden of paradise has been built there, on the place where My body rested to mark My blessing and the proclamation of My word trumpeted from edge to edge, as it is written to be before the day of the Lord's appearing, and behold My steps across the land, the word of God, My new name and My proclamation from edge to edge, and nowhere, oh, nowhere is it more beautiful than here where I, the Lord, now have My coming on the earth, on the Romanian land. Amen, amen, amen.
