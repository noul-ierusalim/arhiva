Everything is a heavenly glory above the citadel of My word. Everything is a holy commotion in the air here, for the heavenly powers have gathered together in celebration, singing and praising the Lord, and the one celebrated in heaven and on earth on this day, Elijah, the prophet, the one so great with his faith and with his closeness to God in his life on earth, as much as the Lord's closeness to him was great for his great love, for his longing through which he proved his longing, faithfulness and the restlessness that grew in him because of the multitude of idols, other gods of the people of Israel, who had forgotten God and had lost their fear of judgment for the sin of separation from the Lord, oh, and behold, a celebration and the company of prophets come to the table of My word today here, in the citadel, and I am finding you standing in My presence, sons of the watchmen, and I am coming to you with My saints, oh, sons.

Peace upon the citadel of My word and peace upon you and upon those who are coming near you to the feast table today, and I am leaving a rain of word upon you, and I am clothing you in the glory of My word whenever I come with the saints on feasts in the citadel to you, sons!

I have no more place to bow My head on earth, I have no more, and that is why I have put you to holy work to raise My citadel with you, to enter you and then travel with the glory of My word over the earth, oh, that I need to shout far and wide everywhere before the day of My glory full of My victory over sin on earth, for sin reigns everywhere on earth, sons, and there is no place for the Lord anywhere, and My day is near, and it is impossible not to prepare My way, open it with the word and let it go to all the nations and languages of the earth, because I have never let go of the punishment for sins until I have proclaimed through the spirit of prophecy the cry for resurrection, for rising from sin, to the fear of God over those who have abandoned the wisdom of not seeking the God-separating sins of people who are careless about their lives today and tomorrow, of their eternity for good or for pain afterwards, and behold, I cry and go on crying, and with the company of saints I work and walk sighing with pity, because I have pity on all those who sleep, and they no longer wake up to know what is with them, what will happen to them, what comes upon those who do not watch all the time, and the Lord cries and keeps crying, and there is no one on earth to open to My cry and to understand and believe by waking up and hearing the Lord crying.

Behold the voice of the Lord, who breaks in the way thundering over many waters, with a storm breaking the cedars, laying them low, the voice of the Lord pouring out flames of fire upon the earth, shaking the wilderness and the settlements of men and reigning over the flood, sitting as King of creation, as it is written, oh, and I cannot break sin as I break the mountains, the valleys and the groves with storms that fill all creation with fear, with earthquakes that bring the waters out of the womb, with the breaking of clouds as at the flood, only, only will the world of men understand that the Lord is coming preparing and coming with glory to the earth.

Oh, but on earth the antichrist works, performing his miracles before the eyes of people, who forget that everything on earth perishes, everything perishes, and through those deceived by satan perishes, and perishes like the antichrist who is doomed to perish, and people do not know that the king they worship is passing like them, is temporary and falls under eternal punishment together with the coming of the Lord, with glory for those who wait for Him to come to establish His kingdom on earth, His eternal kingdom, not passing, oh, and, therefore, [legatura_la_teme id_capitol="3" id_subcapitol="3"]you, people of the earth, wake up to hear the voice of the Lord, Who calls you to Him! Stop the daily running after miracles and temporary entertainment and ask yourself what will happen to you today and tomorrow, and who is the one who promises you good, so that you will linger to look towards heaven, from where the Lord is coming to you on earth to awaken you, so that fear may not seize you unexpectedly, for the Lord is coming for His obedient ones to give the reward to the lovers of God, oh, and the Lord is coming for the servants of satan, the liar, and is coming with fear for those who are not watching for His coming, oh, and do not cry in vain, because in vain you cry when you lose what you accumulate on earth under the kingdom of satan, the red antichrist everywhere, and with many venomous heads and promising lies to the deceived day after day, to keep those separated from God under the spirit of perdition through deeds and through carelessness of soul.

Oh, people who do not wake up to seek the Lord in times of trouble, storms and great hailstones, *(Of ice, r.n.)*, which destroy all your savings, behold, the king of time takes you to the block of flats, puts you in a box, in his pleasant shelter, and laughs when he sees the fruit of his enmity on you, for this temporal king separates you from the Lord, from the eternal One, and Who has His dwelling in heaven, in great glory, and will come with it to the earth after the washing of the earth and will place on it those who watch for His coming, prophesied to come, for the Lord is the eternal King, and satan is the deceiver who keeps man in temporality and gives him nothing, except the lie that he gives everything to everyone, and behold, the Lord is coming and calling upon men to seek for the gift of faith, of love for God and of holiness for the Lord, Who is coming to establish and preparing His eternal kingdom on earth, oh, and it is still time for people to separate themselves from satan, the liar, and to look to heaven, for behold, heaven reigns over the earth, heaven shakes the earth and people, heaven cleanses the sin from everywhere, heaven prepares a new heaven and a new earth, a new heaven for a new earth, oh, it cannot be otherwise, because heaven means holiness, eternal and incorruptible beauty, light and truth, eternity and glory, and that is why the Lord is coming to earth as word and announcing Himself coming, and that is why you, people everywhere, wake up and look to heaven, to the Lord, to the things with which the Lord is coming to you on earth now.

Oh, the prophet Elijah stands impatiently next to Me, next to My speaking mouth, so that he too may cry out from Me to the people, for his zeal splits the mountains and the springs and their streams and the forests and plains, and here he is splitting the hardened necks of the people alienated from God and says to them:

— Oh, who is a great God like our God, who does wonders, oh, inhabitants of the earth?

My spirit is all a burning fire in the invisible homeland of the Lord's saints. We have our homeland between heaven and earth, between the visible and the invisible of God's creation, and that is the homeland where no one perishes, or cries or suffers and loses peace, and you, people, have lost the fear of God and have fallen into grave and constant sins. I am His servant and I am calling you to heaven and I am calling you to Him, before His glorious day arrives and I am telling you: Strengthen your search for the Lord, turn your hearts to Him! He is the One Who is and Who remains, and I said this from the earth through revelations from Him and I did not seek another god, but I sighed for the mercy of the One Who had been waiting in vain for man to come to Him, for the mercy of the One Who created man. He has been waiting for man to come and to passionately love Him, the Lord the Creator.

I suffered on earth because of the idols chosen by people instead of God for them, for their daily life on earth. I suffered and was pained by the separation of people from God, and I rose up to prove the Lord the true God, and I proved Him through the great miracle of the glory of the Lord and the destruction of the idolatrous priests, the servants of the lie of that time, and then I went up to my beloved Lord, Who sent fiery angels to meet me, chariots of fire between earth and heaven, and I went to my sweet homeland; however, I still sigh, I am sighing with the Lord for the great error of the human race, and I am doing the work that I have to do to return many to the Lord, to faith in Him, to holiness, oh, because all sinners perish and will perish, all of them, and that is why all people are called to holiness after seven thousand years of satan, who separates man from God for sin, for death, for sin is death.

Come to life, you people on earth, come to salvation, come to the sweet Lord, Who is coming after you, and behold, He is coming to earth speaking the word to you and asking you to take life in you from His life, as all people should have lived on earth; nevertheless, satan came with deceit and crushed man to his will! But now, it is time for satan to fall and for the Lord to establish His kingdom, heaven on earth, so that it may be on earth as it is in heaven, do you hear?

Oh, hear, Lord, hear me, and sound the alarm over the people, and they will hear! Amen.

— I call and I keep calling, the Lord is calling across the earth and is calling people from everywhere to Him.

Come to the water! I have living water, come! I will wash away the dirt from your sins with it and I will give you white linen shirts like those of My saints, so that I may find you like them, clothed, and not naked. Amen. [/legatura_la_teme]

It is a feast day and I am leaving the word of My mouth upon the earth and I am taking it over the mountains and over the waters, over the plains, over the people. I come with the word, and with it I shepherd, with this iron rod I shepherd.

I grant peace to those who submit to My voice as Shepherd, Who sanctifies with His voice as He walks over the earth!

Oh, peace to you, those in the citadel of My word on the land of the Romanian nation, for with you I, the Lord, walk My step and word across the earth!

Peace also upon the Romanian land and nation, towards which the Lord looks, watches, works, cleanses and shepherds, and then bestows upon it His peace! Amen, amen, amen.
