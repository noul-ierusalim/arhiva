My voice is heard from above and I am entering the citadel of My word and I am placing My speech in the book to share comfort and blessing over the country, over you, Romanian people, to protect you and to record over you My speech with you, so that the evil spirits may fear and avoid you and the heavenly hosts may see Me on watch, for I, the Lord, have stopped with those in heaven above you to share My word over the earth and to keep saying peace over the earth and over people, for people are without peace and seek comfort, oh, and there is no comfort on earth; it is only in My word which I promised would come with its comfort, with My counsel, My country, oh, and I am urging you to learn to love God more and more and not to despise My word, for behold, it hurts Me, with all those in heaven, it hurts Me, that you are written to God, a Christian country, oh; however, I do not see in your sons and daughters love of God, fear of God, the accomplishments left by ancestors for a Christian walk, not just any walk, and on earth the rod of My wrath stings people from place to place and unknowingly takes away all their breath from edge to edge, oh, because the earth cries and struggles under the pressure of the lawlessness committed without stopping by all peoples, by all kings as in the time of Herod, when I had come to earth to draw Jerusalem and all Judea and all the nations to the Father, but there was not possible, because sin was a consolation for all as now, and no one had not wanted to be with God, rather with sin and its fruits, which brought wrath and death, hell and sorrow everywhere.

Oh, My country, it is written for this time of fasting and preparation for the feast of resurrection, but you are forgetting God. It hurts Me and it hurts Me because of you. Oh, come to your senses! Your holy ancestors are waiting for you at the fountain of heavenly teaching, so that you may not trample on the will of God like the pagans, oh, country with the Christian name on your forehead!

There is mourning everywhere on earth, for the pagan life of the sons of men attracts curse and death, despair and a painful end. There is mourning in you, too, and much confusion of tongues. The will of God is required to be kept, oh, but the Romanian no longer has My will in nature to work it upon him. I have the source of My word on your hearth, Romanian people, to divide Myself over the earth and to announce My coming, to accomplish the victory written in the Scriptures and to establish on earth the heavenly kingdom renewed by the word. It is written to fast for seven weeks now, and you kill to eat meat and the rest of the meat. It hurts Me. The evil spirits judge Me because I love you too much, oh, and I cannot leave you for punishment, because I have in you My work from the end of time. I have made the people of My word to stand day and night with a Christian life before Me for them and for you, My country, because I have to accomplish the renewal of all things on earth. Oh, submit yourself to My will, for heaven is working above you, the holy watch and your destiny from God at the end of time. Come in the spirit of obedience and tell Me truly: *“As it is in heaven, so on earth, Lord, let it be.”*

Oh, I am asking you, tearfully, come to obey God and your parents, My country of today! Wash yourself in the river of My word from your midst and give up the longing and taste of sin and take the longing for God and paradise within you, for it is written for you that paradise will be on your earth, for the mystery of God is hidden in you, the creation of man from your earth at the beginning of creation, oh, and I will wash you, weeping I will stand with you and wash you from sin and the wages of sin.

Man is holy if he eats holy food, clean from sin and from hands with sin on them, because food for the body is made with clean hands; however, there are no longer clean hand on earth, and no one washes their hands anymore to work cleanly with them afterwards. Therefore, I advised those who follow Me to the word of My teaching and told them to do everything with their hands for food, if they want to be My saints, and I, their Saint. For those who are pure it is My teaching, and for those who defile themselves day and night then it is My call to obedience for holiness.

I, the Lord, must also have a pure people on the Romanian hearth and that is why I leave My advice on this earth for those who want to be a people of My word and to whom I say: Do not touch what is unclean, and I will receive you and you will be able with the holiness that the Lord needs in people, oh, because it is the holy time at the border, like My coming to a people prepared for the glory with which I come.

Food bought and found ready-made is not clean from sin in man, because the hands of those who cook this food are not washed from sin, but even deliberately touch them with sin, so that man is filled everywhere with the spirit of debauchery that comes from the body, and man does not know what he eats and why the food he buys is touched with the name of fasting food, but if the one who cooks the food does not fast from sin, the food is not clean, and behold, what great distress on earth, how many bodily and spiritual illnesses are spread through sin, and how much fallen mind is in people!

Oh, who would tell the earthlings what I, the Lord, see being done from hell on earth? Oh, that is why I come! My right has been taken away in the heart and in the body of man, and even in the Christian, because even the Christian does not love the Lord and does not make a holy place for God in him, and woe to the one without God, without the protection of the Lord inside and outside of him on earth among the sons of men filled with the spirit of debauchery day and night! Why should people mourn their sons when they burn and die in a time of debauchery and sinful lusts? Sin attracts death, torment, tears and despair, oh, and there is no longer any comfort to come from anywhere. I come and comfort with exhortation, but if My exhortation is despised, there is no longer other comfort, oh, there is none, for comfort is one, that of God, and it is for those who love as God. Amen.

I have had a great and numerous people near the spring, but as they came, they also went away, because they did not listen to My teachings for holiness. Only holiness protects the Christian, and holiness is worked with clean hands, oh, and who still has the nature to wash their hands from sin inside and outside of him?

I would like to have holy children, Christians ready for a holy meal with Me, and therefore, those who receive communion every day must feed only on paradise food **(Gen: 1/29.)** and clean hands washed from sin, for man on earth has learned to keep committing sin, loving sin and longing for it, and behold, this is how I have lost many who came, knocked and entered, and if they did not persist in their dwelling in Me, they have lost their place near the Lord and went to their longings.

Oh, this is where holiness in people comes from! It comes from obedience to God, from holy love, and especially from clean and holy food, with hands washed all the time, as was the law in Israel for washing hands and feet, for washing those touched by sin and unclean until evening, for the law against sin makes known that sin exists and must be washed, and that sin is as dirty as the man who does it.

I have a handful of people taught from heaven, and through it I hold the land and the Romanian nation in My palm, to reach the day when the Holy Spirit will distribute power and the spirit of holiness over the sons of this nation, and from them over the earth from place to place, little by little, for the Spirit of God is beautiful, it is abundant, it is great over this nation, which was once beautiful through the holy ancestors, who kept the law of God over this earth.

I would like to multiply My sanctified people, to show Myself with many Godloving people. The sweetest day is coming, not long from now. Let the kindergarten at Maluri village be ready, and I, the Lord, am immediately going to announce the day of the feast for those who are gathering at the spring, at the feast of testimony, and I am going to advise everyone in advance to prepare their royal garments and to come dressed royally, for I will be looking down from the throne to see them gathering as sons of the heavenly King, for that day is going to be the wedding day of the Son of God, and those gathered must resemble the wedding table, the heavenly decor, oh, I have always told Christians to make themselves holy clothes like those in heaven, like princes, like princesses who await their suitor.

I am now coming down towards the city of the seat of the country (Bucharest) and we are going with witnessing news, *(The “Seventh Trumpet” Exhibition at the Romanian Peasant Museum)*, and we are descending from there with the great proclamation shared across the country and we are coming descending to the garden at Maluri village, for the day of celebration, oh, and other proclamations are going to come from Us, for everything is going to be heavenly, and those from heaven will fill the air from bottom to top, up to the ninth heaven, and sweet news will fill the whole earth about My source of word upon the earth, and therefore, we must work in preparation as in heaven, and My little people with whom I, the Lord, am proclaiming Myself across the earth and bringing My visible glory ever closer, for time is running out.

Therefore, peace to you, those who stand watch and work with Me in the citadel of My word, peace, sons! I am sharing courage with you, and I always prepare holy powers for you. I will always stand by you and I will place on your table the word of advice and watch, sons, and you will be hopeful because you have the Lord with you.

I am embracing you in comfort, I share with you love, strength and help as much as you need, and we will come out with the beauty of the garden at Maluri, and which the heavenly hosts await, and especially the heavenly host of the Romanian nation, and from which all the hosts in heaven take this name, for My country today is the first, it is the one from the beginning and the one from the end, and I am its Shepherd, oh, sons. Amen, amen, amen.
