A voice from heaven approaches again and again and speaks above the Romanian land.

Again and again, I speak from the cloud, again and again, the Lord settles His word over you, My wedding land, My Romania, for I am tender for you and I am a Shepherd full of words above you, I take care of you and I envelop you in My tender word to always distance the work of the evil spirit that keeps lurking for you with cunning to find you in weakness and to keep squeezing out all your strength, oh, that My and your enemy cannot stand still when he knows and sees your fate from God and the glory that I, the Lord, am preparing for Me and you, so that peoples and kings, nations and languages of the earth will bow down under this glory, as it is written about the holy mountain, from which many will take My teaching and will find the happy and so happy way and walk, oh, that for no other reason does the Lord come and prepare His glory on your land, and to set a royal table on your land and come with the hosts of saints and with eternal wedding days, with the days of eternity, with heaven and earth together, in a very sweet party, even if now those on earth know no other sweetness for them than the life with fleeting pleasures and so sinful before the Lord, Who comes to prepare His rest on your hearth, oh, and why, perhaps, in you, why in your gardens, My country?

I am the One Who answers to make Myself heard to the ends and to make all creation tremble at the voice of My word, which again creates the world with the word as in the beginning, when I created the heaven and the earth and when I said, let there be light, so that I could work in the light all creation in all its beauty from God, and then I began speaking for the emergence of those who appeared in the light, and I said, let the waters be separated from the waters and let the firmament be established between the upper waters and the lower waters, and from the lower ones I said, let the dry land appear and I called it earth, oh, and you were the first patch of dry land to come out from the waters at the creation of the world, My country today, and after the creation of all, I took with My hand from the top of the dry land and made from your clay the body of man, created by the hand of God, I breathed into him a living soul and I gave him all creation, all the earth to rule it with everything on it and to fear him everything, and he should obey God and the law of heaven, for I made him a dwelling, I made him a garden of paradise and I placed man in it; however, his disobedience to My word appeared, and then the enemy of man and God, the opponent of God's will in heaven, and then on earth next to man, from then until today.

Oh, what does the adversary mean? The spirit of pride, a spirit established by man within himself, this spirit wanted to be greater than God over earth and heaven. Man wanted to be great, after being created by God between the earth and the sky, and it was not enough for him what God had subjected to him, as he wanted everything, he wanted to be on the throne above God, above the clouds that were carrying the Lord between the sky and the earth, oh, and all creation shook at this thought of man's arrogance and a great earthquake was stirred up among the angels and angels fell at that moment of arrogance of God's creation; however, the archangel Michael commanded before the angels and the fall of the angelic hosts stopped, and only the host serving the thought of man's arrogance, only that one did not remain in its place and was broken by the fall, and these fallen angels remained between heaven and earth from place to place, for man wanted to be greater than God and wanted everything.

Oh, My land, what a great mystery God is placing upon your land! Do you hear the Lord, do you hear, kings and nations and languages of the earth? Oh, arise and stand before the voice of the Lord, Who is speaking upon the earth His mysteries, the mysteries of creation, all that will appear and remain that will be the glory of the Lord, and will adorn My wedding land, you, My Romania, for you are the first handful of earth, and I took from it with My hand for the creation of man, created by God the Creator, and before Whom the peoples and the great ones over them will kneel, take from God, walk with the Lord on a smooth and glorious path, as it is written, oh, and there will be no more unbelief, but only glory will be and only Romania will shine under the glory of the Lord and it will share joy and light far and wide, after the earth will be cleansed and prepared by the celebration of heaven on earth with people, starting from you, My country of today, and you will be My new Jerusalem, the new choice of Jerusalem, **(Zacarias / Zechariah: 2/10-13.)** you will be the Lord's rest, for you have received Me now, when the Father has again sent Me and is sending Me to you to make your wedding preparations, to prepare His people for Him, for Me and for My glory with them before the nations of the earth, for your light will no longer go out and you will be light as I am and remain.

Oh, I have fully clarified the answer for your choice from the beginning, My country today, and now I am going into your body and being, for in your people there is the spirit of human arrogance, where lies, My and your enemy, there is the evil spirit, who knows your choice, hates you and weaves temptation for you all the time, and I, the Lord, have always destroyed its hidden web, all the poisonous cobwebs and taken your life out from under the enemies, oh, and I have done and am doing this from creation until today, and I will still do it, for now you have rodents that the angels hold under power from above, so that they cannot spoil the time of My glory and the news with which I began to trumpet and announce over the nations My coming to you with the glory of word, and with this word I have always prevented and crushed the poisonous belly of the spiders that are still trying to suck your strength, My country, Romanian nation, people chosen for My glory at the end of time.

Oh, what a great name you have, Romanian nation! You are chosen and destined to be the host of the glory of the Lord, for it has now been revealed, and the name of Romanian will be great over the earth, and more and more will give to bear this great name, and behold, everyone who will from now on put on the little shirt of a Christian will be called Romanian, the new name of the sons of God, as once the first name was the people of Israel, who lost their primacy with their rejection of Christ two thousand years ago, oh, and how beautiful was the beginning of that time, when Romania received the name of the country of the Lord, and only I knew and recognized her gift from the Father for Me with her, now, at the end of time. And just as then I chose some humble and lowly fishermen on earth and placed them before Me as new leaven to prepare a new people for Me, and to completely overthrow the primacy of the great temple of Caiaphas with his servants dyed red for their transgressions, oh, so now the transition has been made, and I have chosen some humble children, gave them the gift of faith and fed them from heaven with the word to prepare them for My glory of this time, and here I am with the small and lowly or wealthy, and here they are with Me working for My glory and sharing over the earth My word sent from the clouds upon them, so that they may share light over the peoples and nations, and those dressed in passing glory and with royal crowns on their foreheads when they are not worthy of My glory, here they are, I have left them to luxuriate in the seats of servants over the world and to think themselves greater than others, while I am going with the glory of the word and with the small and beautiful ones of Mine, as two thousand years ago, with the disciples gathered from the seashore to make them worthy to prepare My kingdom over the earth and over time until My coming, when here I am coming and shepherding, examining the flock and sounding over it the call to the table, to the sweet pasture, to the wedding days of the Son of God on the Romanian land, the Shepherd with His sheep, who listen to His voice and follow Him, not after a stranger without mercy for the sheep.

Oh, Romanian people, open My book towards you and read in it, for behold how beautiful is My word as your Shepherd! **(Apoc.14:6)** Oh, wake up to the voice of the Shepherd and come and learn the apostolate that is announcing My coming to you, and with you then to the nations of the earth. And I am asking you, Romanian people, reject those who are still sucking your strength and grip, and make them understand that you are waking up and that you understand the steps that I, the Lord, am placing under your feet, so that you may go working towards the joy that coming with Me to you, and then with you to the nations, that you are a missionary people, whether you know it or not, whether you understand it or not, but this will be your fate and that is why I am coming and preparing for you a great and lot of work.

Be good, oh, be ever more good, you, who have done such a hostile disservice to the people of God in your time on the seats of the country's council, *(Social Democratic Party, r.n.)*, oh, that you have not been good, you have not loved, you have not been able to be just, you have not been pure in heart for the country, but have only sucked all the good of the country to the full and still want more. Oh, no! Come to your senses and be good from now on, for the people must wake up to vigilance and work with justice in all things.

And now embrace the two sons, *(President Nicușor Dan and Prime Minister Ilie Bolojan, r.n.)*, who are sacrificing their good power and wisdom in order to work with them beauty for the country and justice for the entire nation, oh, and come now and redeem with beautiful behavior and clean hands all that you have done ugly and bad until now, for I am full of mercy, but I am also working to clean up the dryness of My garden in these days, and those who are under the lying name under which they stand and who have hidden themselves in the womb of the country to suck its strength and they stand under the name of gold, *(GOLD – Alliance for the Unification of Romanians, - in Romanian – AUR - Alianța pentru Unirea Românilor, r.n.)*, but there is only rot and lies under this name with which they are deceiving the sons of Romania to name themselves the first for election, oh; however, these dryness will be given to disintegration and those guilty and cunning for so long under this name will be brought to the fore, willing or not, oh, and happy will be those who will come out in time from among these cunning, impudent and filled to the brim with the spirit of arrogance, hidden under the work of socalled justice for the people, but they are the most rotten of all and the most traitors to the nation and brothers, and they are enemies, and enemies have a special fate, according to their deeds, as it is written.

Now, peace, unity and good understanding with perseverance for the spirit of justice above all, may this be established as the work of those who are now establishing the straightening of those who have been so crooked for a long time, oh, and may the heavenly powers of vigilance and help from heaven stand for the lifting of the Romanian nation to unity, to faith, to Christ the Shepherd, Who becomes a word over the earth on the land of the Romanian nation, where He is now shepherding His flock.

Peace to you, My beautiful country, My kingdom of today and tomorrow on earth! I am carrying you in My arms like My sweet child. Oh, peace to you all the time, all the time! I will always watch over you and I will speak to you, shepherd you tenderly, tenderly, and I will caress you with My comfort, oh, how sweet you are to Me, and that is why I am holding your hand and I am leading your steps on the path, on My path with you to My victory and yours, always the Lord with you on the path, oh, My country of today, always Mine, always and forever. Amen, amen, amen.
