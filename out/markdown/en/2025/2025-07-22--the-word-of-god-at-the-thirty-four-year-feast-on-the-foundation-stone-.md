I am here, sons who are receptive to God, I am above the citadel of My word and I have companions from heaven, I have heaven as My companion, I have with Me the heavenly and angelic powers, **(Matt: 25/31; 1 Thess: 3/13)** the Romanian saints and all the saints on earth, oh, and We are looking with amazement from above and We are going back in time to the day celebrated today, when with all the heavenly hosts We rejoiced and consoled ourselves, by planting the cross as the beginning of the building of the citadel of My word on the land of the Romanian nation in the year 1991, oh, and what joy among those in heaven could be greater than that, after so much expectation was for that day to come and the power for it to begin on earth here the fulfillment of My word spoken from time through My trumpet Virginia, in which I had walked and spoken for twenty-five years of prophecy for the mystery and the gradual building up of the mystery of New Jerusalem in work on earth, with Me and with a people chosen for their faith and for their work with God going forward, **(Apoc: 21/2-3)** a prepared people, just as it was with the beginning two thousand years ago, with disciples gathered from the world.

[legatura_la_teme id_capitol="7" id_subcapitol="3"]However, the mystery of this prepared people is now, after seventy years of word, for I, the Lord, am now working My glory of My second coming from the Father to men, glory seen, sons, glory brought out from under the mystery, and My path from heaven to earth is the word of My mouth and its collection in its book of this time, My great labor, My heavy cross of today, carried with Virginia and her people, *(Saint Virginia, the sixth apocalyptic trumpet, r.n.)* and now with the least of the people of the seventy years of word, since I, the Lord have been speaking, fulfilling and hastening My walk with the word over the peoples to the ends, so that then the renewal may come, the rebirth of the world, fulfillments that I had promised two thousand years ago that they would be fulfilled on earth, a new heaven, a new earth and a new people, **(Apoc: 21/5)** a people prepared for My glory from the end of time, for My fight with the antichrist, about whom it is written that he will be destroyed by the breath of My mouth, **(See Isaiah: 11/4 and 2 Thess: 2/8)** that is, by the word that comes out of My mouth, with which I am fighting against him to overcome him and to scatter him from the way of My coming and all My work on earth, now, when I am coming proclaiming and sharing faith and the power of faith among the people, as it is written to be fulfilled and to come to the glory of God, those redeemed by the Father for My glory at the end of time.

I am welcoming you to the awakening and I am placing you at the work of My confessing word, the sons who are standing watch for Me in the citadel. Oh, peace to you, those who have remained steadfast, sons! I am preparing joys for you to reward you for remaining steadfast in serving the coming of My word on earth, and behold, on this day we are celebrating My beginning with you, the beginning of the building of the citadel of My word over you, sons.

[/legatura_la_teme][legatura_la_teme id_capitol="5" id_subcapitol="3"]Oh, it was a sweet day that July, when you and I, together with the witness bishop from the church of the Romanian people, when united in love and holy joy, planted the cross on the earth that now bears the white stone, the little garden of My Holy of Holies in these days of the end of time, sons.

I keep looking longingly at you, at those with whom I had stopped in the garden of the word and then pronounced the blessing of the laying of the foundation stone, upon which the body of the mysterious shrine was then raised through its construction. It was a beautiful day as in heaven, and your faith was comforting the Father, the Son and the Holy Spirit and all the guests of heaven, witnesses of that day of holy mystery, sons.

From age to age, thus have My mysteries been worked between earth and heaven, for testimony and for their manifestation hereafter. I had you then, My dear ones, My listeners, for you submitted with faith, even though you had nothing to fulfill and establish on earth those things that came from heaven, and to work both secretly and openly in that time, in this time.

Oh, I have then remained with you and continued the work, from then until today, because after that initial fulfillment, My faithful witness to My word was taken from Me and from you, taken by those who have not believed in the things that came from God for their settlement on earth. He withdrew from all that and promised obedience to the elders over the flock of the Romanian church, and you also withdrew for this obedience, because I, the Lord, had strengthened you, given you grace and walked with you step by step, word by word through the word with which I have remained at the helm of the people, and behold, I went and are still going with you and we have arrived together at the celebration of the seventy years of My word on earth, and divided through you to the ends, because what is impossible through men, God can, so it is written, sons.

[/legatura_la_teme][legatura_la_teme id_capitol="5" id_subcapitol="6"]And we are today under the memory of the beginning of the citadel of My word, and thirty-four years have passed since then, and you were and have remained the fruit of My trumpet Virginia, and you are her and My planting, and the Lord is coming out victorious through you with the work of His word over the earth, and in a short time, we are going to write in the book the year 2025, the year pleasing to the Lord, seventy years of the word on the land of the Romanian nation, and many guests will gather on the day of celebrating this year of fulfillment and we will stand before them with heavenly joy and comfort, sons, and therefore, another little sacrifice, another step of labor with you, and we will speak of the day of celebration and the meeting at Maluri village, of all those who are waiting so long to come and see the prophecy fulfilled, for I said, more than once I said at the time of this prophecy, I said that the place, where I descended from heaven on the threshold of the little house of Virginia's family, will not remain an unseen, unknown, obscure or just a beautiful unprepared place then, after My body stood on this earth and worked the trumpeting of My word, oh, and this earth was impossible not to enter under its heavenly glory, whose body and form is a prophecy, a resemblance to the body of a heavenly wing, this patch of earth, and many will rejoice under this heavenly sight and mystery, will take from My glory from here and carry it over the earth for testimony, oh, how great are the prophecies spoken over this holy place, sons.[/legatura_la_teme]

Now, we are beautifully preparing everything that is left to prepare in this little garden, and then we will open its gates, share comfort and heavenly fulfillment, peace, faith and everything from the Lord that this heavenly little garden will bear on its new birthday, its renewal and confession, for I, the Lord, lived here with Virginia and I prepared through prophecy everything that has been fulfilled and will continue to be fulfilled and shared, and many will look on in amazement, and still in amazement they will bear the holy feeling in their hearts upon touching the place of the beginning of My word in 1955 at Maluri village, on the hearth of the Romanian nation, oh, and Romania will be sought and loved because of this heavenly mystery, with which I, the Lord, made My abode in this corner of the earth, until Virginia was persecuted, oh, what a great wave of unbelief the work of My word suffered from the villagers, when I took My trumpet and I chose another place and built another covering for My work, oh, and then I built the beginning of the citadel, seen for the mystery of New Jerusalem in Romania, which I chose as My country, **(Zechariah: 2/12-13.)** after the people of Israel rejected Me **(See Matthew: 21/4.)** and crucified Me.

I am sending through angels secret comforts to the witness archpriest, with whom I, the Lord, supported Myself thirty-four years ago, when I wrote on earth the time of the creation of the white stone, prophesied by the Scriptures to appear, and on this day of remembrance, I am placing the spirit of comfort over the witness, with whom I wrote Myself then under the foundation upon which the citadel of My word was then built, little by little, and the pearls around it until today, next to which the garden of paradise from Maluri village, will now be written, and I will tie the beginning of the confessing word from then until today, and this pearl stone will stand next to all the fulfillments and we will go forward with glory, sons, and I, the Lord, am in front and I am the Shepherd.

We, now, are leaving aside the pain of memories and writing a day of joy on this day, and I am leaving you with much peace, much strength, much vigilance, for My care for you is great, it is great, sons, oh, and let us work together with My power in you, let us be able to work and you be able to work, too.

And peace to you, sons, oh, and do not forget the comfort that will be felt in full as a reward for you having been subject to My longings and having carried My walk further on earth. And all the heavenly comfort on that day, which We are greatly preparing, will be to bestow upon all those who will come from all over to the Maluri, and will taste the joy of witnessing the beginning of the work of My word, the little garden of paradise at the Maluri village, My labor with you for the comfort of many, oh, sons. Amen, amen, amen.
