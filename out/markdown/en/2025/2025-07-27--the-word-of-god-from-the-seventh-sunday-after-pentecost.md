I am taking you in My speech, I am taking you in My word above you between heaven and earth, between earth and heaven, My country today, My Romania, oh, and how much I would like you to be all Mine, so that your sons, the Romanian sons, may have Me as their life, as their love, oh, how much I would like and how long I have been waiting for this longing to be fulfilled on your hearth! You are My new people, My new Israel, after I was rejected by the people born from My work with Abraham, Isaac and Jacob, the one with the name Israel then, **(See Galatians: 3/6-7, and Romans: 9/8.)** oh, and I have had you under this choice in great mystery and now this mystery is revealed under which I have kept you for two thousand years, oh, My country that has been Mine from then and until today, **(See Matthew: 21/43.)** and now, at the end, here is what choice you had, and which I am revealing to the world now, that here I am with seventy years of word, from heaven that came and was written on your earth through secret disciples, the word of My mouth over you, My country dear to Me, and with it I feed you, as well as you do not know how as the little baby does not know the food, with which its mother raises it, not only with milk, and after that she gives it solid food, so that it grows vigorously and quickly, and the solid food chews it in her mouth and then gives it to the baby who still has no teeth, and here is what a mystery growth is, what a holy story it has and what fruit it bears afterwards!

I have been carrying you in My speech for seventy years and secretly feeding you with food already chewed in My mouth, oh, I have been feeding you and waiting for you to grow and know the spirit that feeds you as the first, as the firstborn among the peoples on earth, and for two thousand years you have been Mine from the Father, and your land is the first land to emerge from the waters at the creation of heaven and earth, and on this land you now are living, Romanian Israel, and you are the first, son carried in the mysteries of God for the time of His victory over sin on earth, upon death over men, for sin is death, it is nothing else, it is nothing else the sin with which satan tempted and made man fall from paradise, the one who did not obey God the Creator, and when satan appeared, the adversary of life, the enemy of God and man, oh, but who would have been to tell this to man, if I had not come two thousand years ago to fight death, to free man from eternal punishment and to forgive his sin by crucifying him on the cross through My crucifixion for man, oh, and since then I have been working and keep on working on the new creation of the world, as it is written in the Scriptures, the word of My mouth, when I said that the renewal of the world is being worked on, and those who destroy God in man will be held accountable, and many will be cleansed and made white, as it is written, **(Dan: 12/9,10)** and the wicked will remain as they are because of their disobedience, unbelief and sin, on whose side, those, who will not choose the life of heaven with God on earth, will remain.

Oh, Romanian nation, I am saying great words to you, for you are at a crossroads and do not fully understand to side with Christ, with the side of victory for you. I am the Samaritan Who is going before you on your way to rescue you from eternal perdition, to bandage your wounds and to take care of you as a father. I am Christ, I am your Bridegroom, I have My bride in you and I would want you entirely to be Mine, Romanian country, My new Israel. The Romanian Christian will be of the first rank and this will be known all over the earth, oh, and what will the Romanian do who will not love to belong to Christ, a Christian living in holiness, of the glory that is coming from heaven upon this nation chosen for My glory among the peoples? Those who love like God will come here and will ask to bear the name Romanian to escape the plagues of the pagan world with its life and customs that come from the darkness of satan, in the path of people who flee from God for a life of sin.

Oh, Romanian people, oh, new Israel, do not let yourself lose Christ as satan wants your fate, for satan is the secret enemy who tempts you to separate yourself from Christ, from the One crucified and resurrected, two thousand years ago, for the judgment of this enemy of the world.

I want to clothe you in glory alongside My glory, Romanian people. Whether you know your destiny or not, whether you understand it or not, I, the Lord, am coming out with the word in your path, announcing it to you from heaven, coming above your land speaking, announcing and fulfilling, oh, and I am true to the word to fulfill it with you and for you, only do not close it to Me when I am coming to give you from heaven and to know your destiny and your work as a people chosen by God for the coming of His word on earth and for His glory shared with you before all peoples.

A great time is coming, great and unexpected fulfillments are coming; however, they are coming, just as in all times God has worked between earth and heaven for the fulfillments written to come, and you, the greedy and hypocrites through words and through your work, so evil in the midst of this lied and deceived people for so many years, sorrowful and crowned with the fruits of your lies, you, those who have sucked with hunger and thirst the wealth and strength of the Romanian land and nation, I am now announcing to you that you will suffer as in the story of the parable of the rooster who carried in his beak the little bag with two pennies found on the way in his wanderings, *(See The Rooster and the Two Coins, r.n.)* after his poor and foodless master had driven him away and sent him to look for food, only the boyars of the time found him on the way and took the little bag with two pennies in it and tried to get rid of the rooster and kill him. However, the rooster always defeated them, always escaped punishment, and was crying out for the boyars to give him the bag with two pennies, so that he could take it home to his master and escape poverty.

However, there was no way for the boyars to escape the mischievous rooster, it seems. They were then damaged by everything they had gathered once with the bag they had gotten back and all the wealth of the boyars, all the hoard of the country's thieves were held after the rooster and all those remained stuck to the ground as thieves deserve, oh, and this is how I am crying to you, I am crying like the rooster crows, I am crying until you remain poor and until everything you have gathered returns for the country, so that you may have it and not care about the abyss into which you have pushed the country, as if you are not also Romanians and sons of this nation, for you have behaved like invaders and here you are now, being revealed, with everything you have done badly and all the work that was woven, so that everything that could be sucked up, will be sucked up by you and yours, as if it were never to be found out about the iniquities you have worked on the Romanian nation for many years.

Oh, be ashamed now and lower your heads, for your deeds are too shameful, discovered one after another, and to which many more are added one after another.

Oh, do no longer try to speak out of your mouths anymore, do no longer add unjust words, do not raise pretensions anymore, but rather humble yourselves now, sons. Let justice take the helm and be good, for you do not have the love of justice and truth in your nature, and you long for wealth, for wicked parties, and you have not been afraid that you will have to give an answer for everything once and for all. I am the One Who always speaks to you and urges you, those who have been so much wrong, to bow down, to humility, to repentance, because here these will come and are standing in line to come now. Therefore, take the side of justice in all things, and I will take care of you if you listen, so that you may not hinder the cleanliness that has begun across the country, through those who are now fighting to put the Romanian nation on the right path.

And you, those who have worked to save and free this nation from its burden, I am urging you to look carefully, so that you may have near you sons who are pure in heart and deed in all your labor for the salvation and establishment of the path of the country, and I, the Lord, with all the heavenly powers remain your support and I am going with the mystery of God's Romania, I am going towards the glory of this nation next to the glory of My word, with which I stand and work on earth a sweet kingdom for those who will be sweet to it, oh, how great will be the glory of the land and the Romanian nation, great, My sons, and ever greater, and all under My godly eye and all make way for the endless spring and there will be a beginning, as it is written.

Oh, peace to you, Romanian nation, peace, new Israel! Let yourself be worked by the word that is coming with Me to you, for you are a blessed people in this time for all the nations who will seek your name and My glory from your midst, and the promises are not taken back, but remain and are announced, being fulfilled with glory, as it is written.

And I have spoken on Sunday over the Romanian land and people, and My name is called the Word of God **(Apoc: 19/13)**. Amen, amen, amen.
