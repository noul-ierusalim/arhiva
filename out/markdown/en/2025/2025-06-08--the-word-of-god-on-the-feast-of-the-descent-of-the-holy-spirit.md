The cloud of the glory of My coming is standing above the Romanian land and I am leaving My word in its book today, on the day of the feast of Pentecost, and I have the nine heavens with all the layers of glory as companions, and the heavenly and angelic powers stand at My service, and I come and work My coming and announce it over the earth Myself when I come on holidays to shepherd, for I am a Shepherd and I have a wonderful name, the Word of God is this wonderful name of Mine. **(Apoc: 19/13)**

I am Who I am. I am the Son and the Word of the Father, and the Father gives Me work on earth among men with the word as at the beginning of God's creation, and behold, before dawn, peace to you, to those in the citadel who welcome Me when I come!

On this great day, I have in My coming the people of those who have fallen asleep from all times since the work of the creation of man and all that was done then, and I have placed this people without number in layers, layers, for they have a memorial table for them in the citadel here and they stand ready to receive their gift and comfort, and everything is still an infinity, the sight of My glory on the day of the feast of Pentecost here.

With the spirit of comfort I am opening Myself for My country today, on the day of the holy feast, and I am embracing and enveloping it in the word of the glory of My coming, and it is the Father's chosen one for My coming to earth, as word now, at the end of the age, and I am preparing with it My appearance and victory, and heaven and earth together passing over this land, for I am a comforting Shepherd for it and for this nation, and I am speaking to it gently and saying:

Peace to you from God the Father, the Son and the Holy Spirit, My country today, My wedding country, the table of the feast of the Holy Spirit in this time, oh, and I do not know how else to call you more beautiful, more holy, more loving, for you are under the cross, you are struck with words of reproach upon you, from your midst these coming out and striking and staining your face and your sons, just as only the evil spirit knows how to work the most evil and vengeful when it sees itself defeated and ashamed and with the name exposed, the antichrist and his hidden plan.

But here I am, you enemy lurking behind your dark words, and with the spirit of revenge brewed to deceive and divide the spirit of lies and to make the sons of this nation dizzy. And as you take out your head covered by the spirit of lies and enmity, here you are face to face with My voice over you to watch over My flock and nation today, the Romanian country and people, My dowry from the Father for the work of My coming word on earth at the end of time!

I am putting you on the run with the heavenly and angelic powers, I am following you, I am calling you antichrist and I am exposing all the lies with which you keep hiding from foreigners, walking around and defaming the Romanian nation, only My victory over you will be great on this land, because here I am, catching you working to ruin My coming and its path and My Spirit Which is blowing to destroy your work, your stupidity and your enmity towards this land and nation, and for which you pay your lying apostles well with the money you receive from everywhere to please those who use you a lot to stand in My way, so that they can maintain their glory and power over the earth and over the nations on it, oh, and you are disgusting a lot, and I, Jesus Christ, the Lamb of the Father, will put you to great shame and I will reveal all your evil work, all your plans that came from the spirit of lies and all your evil workers like you, and I will show you as an enemy and I will then tell who you are and yours, and Who I am and who are those who are Mine, and I will say: whoever is unjust and defiled, let him be so from now on, and the righteous and holy to God, let him be so from now on, for the time is near, and I am coming with a reward and I will give to each one according to his work, as it is written, and again I will say that blessed are those who wash their robes that they may have the right to the tree of life and enter through the gates into the citadel, oh, and then I will speak against the enemies and I will bring them out where the dogs are, the sorcerers, the fornicators, the murderers, the idolaters and everyone who loves and lives in a lie, and My word will sort and separate the light from the darkness and the truth will be victorious, as it is written. Amen. **(Apoc: 22/11)**

The gift of the Holy Spirit is coming, His work is coming upon the earth, it is coming and is still coming, and those who are attached to My word, should not depart from this protective voice, but should stay and wait for the promise of the Father to receive power to testify of God, for great signs will be worked through the work of the Holy Spirit, and these great times of My glory upon the earth are prophesied through the Scriptures, and therefore, My word no longer sets and I am still preparing and working for the glory that is approaching to blind the antichrist, the one who conquered the world of men with his temporal lures, and he would now dare to walk to My country from the end of time, to the body and spirit of God's Romania, from the midst of which I am sharing Myself over the earth word, so that the enemy may know that he cannot overcome God and the things of God, and that he will be ashamed and overcome face to face with the nations, intoxicated by him all over the earth, to leave the Lord and go with his work destroying souls and holy powers, oh, but he still does not expect to be able to stay good and stop looking for what is not his, and what he would try to penetrate and destroy, just as he sought to destroy in paradise; however, he was taken out of there along with the man who took advice from him, and I, the Lord, have sheltered My garden and the secret of paradise and he no longer sees it, and so he will be taken out from everywhere and will be thrown into the eternal fire of himself and his workers.

And I am saying all this now, so that you can hear Me, antichrist, you and all your hired hands, you and those you have lured from the Romanian nation, and who are well paid to denigrate in mass and walk the whole earth and play dirty games for those who sit in the darkness and strike souls and bodies all the time; however, here is My word over the Romanian land and I am telling you, antichrist, that it is not yours and there is no place for you on this blessed land, and I will work here and from here, and you should leave, spinning in place, so that you will not get anywhere, and stop your work and your hell that kills people, and with which you came to the surface to laugh in the face of God.

Oh, back off! You do not have much time, because the time of paradise is coming, the repair is coming everywhere, the Holy Spirit is coming and blowing on the earth and reigning everywhere and all man are becoming small, and God will be great and heaven will descend on earth with the eternal spring, and work is done according to what is written in the Scriptures, according to My Gospel of two thousand years ago and today, and I will be the One Who will overcome all the enemies of the Father and Mine, and the song of victory of the Lamb will be sung everywhere, the light overcoming darkness, the Lord overcoming the antichrist, the red beast, the seven-headed dragon and the false prophet, as it is written, and at these words of the breath of My mouth, oh, listen, antichrist man, and take your tools and go and get out of the way of My coming and take your hand from the people, for My Spirit will blow the word on the earth and many will take and be healed of foolishness, of lie, of satan of every kind and they will seek heaven, and I will come with all My heavenly glory, with the nine heavens I will stop above the Romanian land and this country will be dear to all for My radiance in it and they will take God from it and the sweet life, of which they have not tasted because of the adversary antichrist, who has been hostile to Me all the time, even though he understood well that he was deceived and that he cannot catch Me to submit to Me, and now he will eat his bitter fruits and will see his black work inside and out, in secret and in plain sight, just as anyone who is an enemy will lose his work, and his reward will be bitter, as it is written.

I have shaken Myself with the gift of the Holy Spirit over the Romanian land and left a written word in the citadel, and now I have a word of comfort to speak for those who have passed away, and who are called to remembrance for them here, at the table of the feast of Pentecost, when the sons of the righteous church of Orthodoxy remember their ancestors and saints and their loved ones, and behold a blessed table and tables set from below and up, for the sky and its air are many, it is high, so high that it is unknown where is up, but the confession of My speech discerns the greatness of the unseen creation and builds the spirit of hope and faith for the above of those above, and therefore, I am saying: may the table of the feast of Pentecost be blessed, and peace upon those seated at the table on earth and above the earth to endlessness, and My word is difficult to understand for many who are not penetrated by God and the mysteries of the heavenly, if they do not listen closely to the words of Scripture and to My word of these days, for behold, seventy years of My word on the land of the Romanian nation, and many explanations can be found in it for those who have faith as a heavenly gift in this time of My coming to earth as a word.

This day is sweet in the garden of the citadel of My word. The view from which the inhabitants of heaven are taking comfort on this day is warm. I said long ago, that a bird from heaven comes to be king on earth and that the thrones of kings are taken and this heavenly bird will sit on them and chirp through the word of the Holy Spirit, through My word, the word of God, the scepter with which I am Shepherd over the earth.

Bow down, you who think and wish to be great over your peoples! I am the One Who rules all, and all is according to each people, for heaven pays attention to deeds and gives each one according to his deeds, as it is written.

Let no one be surprised why in some places there is war or there are great waters, storms, fire or wrath of all kinds. All come for deeds, for where God and the commandments of life are not followed, there is no helping and protective angel from God over the lands. Therefore, the reward is according to the deed, as it is written, and I, the Lord, am taking care to cleanse Romania from life without God over her being and to awaken her for love, so that she may shine over the earth, oh, and I will set a sign soon, soon and it will appear far and will speak over many, and then My voice will come and clarify the mystery of this sign, so that it may be understood by those who have wisdom and the law of God over them. Amen.

And now I am bowing and saying:

Peace from heaven upon those at the helm of the country, so that they may renew their work for the progress of this people, so bewildered now, following the excessive waste, left by those who have stood so far and keep on staying without the fear of God to count the wealth of the country, and which they have drawn for themselves, and here they are, still wanting to stay and continue to suck the power and bread of the Romanian nation. Everything is coming towards renewal, this is how it must be worked on and this is how it will be, through the power of the Holy Spirit it will be so. Amen.

Peace to you! Peace to the Romanians everywhere! Oh, Romanian sons, take from Me for yourselves. Do no longer take any advice from the enemies among you. Rise up and raise your voice and distinguish yourselves and multiply the number of those who are healthy in mind and spirit, for you are constrained from the midst and by those who have chosen to strike their people and nation.

Come forward, you who govern the Christian Romanian country cleanly and tell everyone clearly that the blows are blows, they are not truths, but the meaning must be explained much more thoroughly for the sons of this nation, so that they can then stay vigilant for the country and for themselves. Therefore, come out to the gates of the country and help the sons of this nation to understand the evil work, which keeps hitting and threatening the weak people, now divided in two, and behold, this people need much and ever more clarification, so that they can regain their love for their country and for their brothers. Oh, do not let the enemies paid to discredit, to strike the soul of this nation through the spirit of lies.

Oh, peace to you and multiply peace, Romanian sons! Strike the darkness, sons, so that it does not multiply! Do you hear?

Peace to the Romanian people! Amen, amen, amen.
