The heavenly hosts, the angels and the saints seated in a beauty of a great celebration of heaven on earth are with Me here, above the citadel of My word, and the joy of those in heaven today cannot be contained in words, but only with the spirit, only with the heart and with faith it penetrates those in heaven and those on earth who have been close to Me for many years on the Romanian land, and those who now stand under the glory of My word, which I have placed on this land for seventy years, starting with the Sunday of My resurrection in 1955 when I began to trumpet My word over the earth, and behold, I have fulfilled the Scripture of My coming in the word of preparation of a people drawn by My Father Sabaoth to this spring of living life, of living water, oh, and now it is being sung among those in heaven "Christ is risen!" as among those on earth, and I remember that seventy years ago these were My first words: *“Peace be with you! Christ is risen! I am not in the flesh, but in the Spirit, and I have come to gather a people for Myself and to speak to them the mysteries for this time, and I am the Lord, Jesus Christ, the Word of God.”*

Oh, how much, how much word have I spoken in this time, and how much has been written by those who came near to hear from God, oh, and how much I speak even now, and the word is contained in his book!

It is the feast of My word on the hearth of the Romanian nation and I am setting a table of word on your hearth, My country today. You are the country of My second coming from the Father on earth, and in you I have set up My room, My Romania, and I am the Sun Which rises on earth, and you are My dwelling place and I am coming out of you as a Bridegroom and I am speaking the word of God over the peoples. Oh, peace to you on the day of the feast of the resurrection and the anniversary of Christ, the Word, on your land! I have the heaven of saints and angels with Me in your air, but what faith do you need for Me to share with you and for you to have and receive your Lord! The earth and the nations on it have been filled with false christs, who dream as Adam to be above God and that they will be accepted in My name and that the world will bow down to them, but I tell them that I, the Lord, when I created man, made a counsel between My Father and I, to make man in Our image and likeness, and not just to be the one who works and speaks in the name of God, and those who do not resemble God are false christs. And why christs if they are liars? Because they speak sharing the word of God, but their heart and life are from the world, and those who come from Me go out of the world with life and face, so that they can have from Me what I give to share in My name among those who are poor in God and in faith for God.

Amen, amen, I say to all who come to this source of word: whoever does not have a cross is not sent anywhere by God and goes with his lie and many fall into the trap and escape with difficulty, if they are to escape, if they come to their senses, oh, and that is why I have come to earth as word once with the multiplication of false christs who lay the stars of heaven to the the spirit of holy faith and renew the world through faith, not through words without power in them like those who share them without touching them with a finger.

Oh, I have set to work for the spirit of holy teaching over those who find the path of My word, but on this great day, I have to pronounce God's blessing and the crowning of the seventy years of the word of Christ on the land of the Romanian nation, and this great blessing will be crowned with the renewal of the garden at Maluri village, on the stone of which I, the Lord, Jesus Christ, descended and spoke My initial word through My trumpet, the vessel prepared by Me since its infancy to carry Me with My coming and to write on earth My word, its book and all those who I had at table with Me and the saints, and through which the word and its work and the witness people of this time were gathered, each in his time, in his company, in his work.

I bless and crown this day as a holy feast of My resurrection and of My word and My coming land, the first among the peoples, the first as at the beginning of creation when I brought the earth out of the waters and it was the head of the earth and has a name written in the Scriptures, the land of brightness, **(Dan: 11/41)**, for behold, I shine in it through the word and through My grace over the people of My word with which I carry My steps on the earth to the ends, so that then I may finish My work, as it is written, and start a new beginning, oh, that there is nothing in you like in heaven, My land in which I stood in the beginning and created man, the garden of paradise and the beautiful dress on your body, My grace over you and all My love for you and now, so that I may walk Myself with the voice of My word through you, and then from you over the earth, oh, and I like that you are Mine from the Father and many in you will weep, because they did not know My time on your land, nor understood My call and the school of heavenly wisdom for your people; however, a faithful people will be born from My word who will no longer fall from grace in the midst of this time with so much wandering from God of the people on earth, because behold, everything has been changed, everything is going nowhere on earth, everything, and great will be those after this time who have thrown away from themselves the corruption of man of this time full of disguises so that you will not know them to be men, oh, and I will have a remnant chosen by grace who will carry God, body and word, because I went to the Father after the resurrection, but I did not go until I left My body and blood as food for the saints, for those who sanctify themselves day by day, so that they may be with Me, and I with them on earth and that God may remain with a handful of sons as Moses remained with the two of Israel at the time of the destruction of the people who wandered from God, He perished through the corruption of nature, just as everyone dies today and loses the path to life.

Oh, My country, oh, Romanian sons, come and see the works of God! I will show you the unseen and hidden things of My wisdom. Come to the Romanian celebration at Maluri village, *(Maluri village, the village and the house where Saint Virginia was born and lived, r.n.)* from where I, the Lord, began to trumpet My word seventy years ago over the earth! Flee from resemblance with the pagan world, flee as much as you can, if you can still flee! Flee quickly and hide in the spirit of My word, in your resemblance with Me and the saints so that may know you as Mine and I may protect you with the heavenly powers by your side all the time! Oh, listen to Me as I listen to the Father, so that we may be brothers and I may have boldness before the Father for you, for the painted weather will lay many of you to the ground if you do not watch, if you waver from one foot to the other. Watch with Me, work with Me when you see storms on earth. May the work of My word with you be dear and sweet, and yours with Me afterwards!

The song of My resurrection is now being sung on earth and it is also being sung in heaven, it is being sung: *“Christ is risen!”* Hell is shaking and trembling, for two thousand years the spirit of hell has been shaking and wailing, threatened by this song. Sing with faith, you Christians, sing, like the birds of heaven sing to God, not to the spirit of hell, for the entertainment of the spirit of satan ends with heavy pain brought by the spirit of lies, which has clothed everything in false promises and in temporary and futureless imaginations, but the love from God does not perish, it never falls from its eternal work, for love is eternal, it is like God, and I am speaking of the love that does not fall, and through which no one falls, but, on the contrary, it shows the way to paradise, to what God has prepared for the children of His love, for the true children of God on earth among men, and happy are those who proclaim the Lord and His works and the promises of His happiness for those who remain with Him and in His love, which is not found in the world and with the children of the world, for the world is full of idols and murderous pleasures, and its poison goes to the heart of man and pulls it towards lies and the realm of death, where the woman became a man, and the man a woman, and all these have come out of hell, and no one will remain on the side of life, except those who remain in My love in this time of the end of the pagan world, disfigured in face and deed, until there is nothing left to gather from this fire that consumes everything and that ends the world that perishes, just as the people of the Lord of old perished through the wilderness full of longing for sin and death from sin, but God and His beloved ones will overcome and open to the heavenly grace and blessing that will establish on earth the renewed kingdom, promised by Me that it would come face to face with the world that was willing to change everything into a curse and a wilderness.

It is a day of resurrection songs and the spirit of hell is trembling. The locks of hell are shaking when satan hears My great news, the Romanian holiday from Maluri village, *(Maluri = also margins, r.n.)* and which has workers from heaven for its preparation, alongside My workers from the citadel of My word and from those who love as God, on earth as in heaven, and this fulfillment must be called to come.

I am taking you into My embrace, oh, My coming land. Peace to you! Christ is risen! I am speaking to you from the midst of the people of My word. I am comprising you in the word more than ever, and for seventy years the word of God has been written on your land and a time will come when many, many on earth will seek this consolation to soothe their wounds and will proclaim the Lord, the One Who is coming to earth as a word in the name of the Father, of the Son and of the Holy Spirit, and this consoling spring is endless, My word over the earth on the hearth of the Romanian nation, seventy years of Jesus Christ the Word with the people on earth. Amen, amen, amen.
