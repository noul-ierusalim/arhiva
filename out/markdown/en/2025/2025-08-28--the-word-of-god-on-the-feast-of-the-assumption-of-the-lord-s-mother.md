I am entering the book with a word of wakefulness and comfort, with a word of creation and light, the Lord is entering with a table of words and a holy feast.

I am coming to you, My country of today, Romanian country. Oh, peace to you, My country! The Lord is becoming in you a light over the whole earth, beloved. My Word is the light, and I have within you, My youngest people, who share My light over the earth, and on this day I am walking with a retinue of saints and angels, and I am entering with a heavenly feast for My Virgin mother, I am entering the citadel with a table of words, for two thousand years ago, on this day, I had fulfilled her longing for My mother and I took her up to Me and to the Father, and I gave her a beautiful gift, which the Father gave Me, after I had finished My suffering on the cross, and the Father gave you to Me as the country of My coming at the end of time; however, I gave My mother to watch over you and to carry you towards God and to mark with guard from above the borders and your vineyard, My country, and I told her that you are the country of My coming with the saints at the end of time and with the new creation of the world, oh, and there must first be light, and with it I work the new creation, as in the beginning, when My word was the light and everything appeared through word, through light, and behold, the word is light, and My word makes its way over the earth and illuminates the light of My word in the darkness, and the darkness cannot overcome it, the darkness cannot face the light, oh, and that is why the Lord comes to earth as word, the light and the new creation come and can light over the darkness, it can, the Lord can work. Amen.

Oh, peace to you, citadel of My word on the land of the Romanian nation! Peace to you, guardians of the citadel! I comfort your labor, sons. I have made you light bearers over the earth, for you make way for My word to go and shine everywhere, oh, sons.

Oh, what a great miracle those on earth today experience, and they do not know, poor things, to open their eyes and see the light and feel the fresh air of creation through the word as at the beginning of time, when all things appeared at My spoken word, and the first word was to be light, and the light appeared, oh, My word appeared, My sons.

All people, behold, are like those of two thousand years ago, they are strangers to the wonders that descend upon the earth in the work of heaven. All the heavenly powers are in work with Me and with My youngest ones, with whom I distribute the gifts to all those who have been waiting for Me for seven thousand years for their redemption from the adversary satan.

Every father leaves the house, where he raised his children, to his youngest, and so do I, so does God. He gives it to the youngest, to the one at the end of His work, He gives him the light to share it over the earth for all those who are, who have been, and are awaiting their raising and the gifts according to their merit. To the youngest, I am giving it, just as I gave the kingdom of Israel to David, and he was the least among his brothers.

Oh, My sweet country, here is what the Lord has in your land! I have worked on your hearth as I did two thousand years ago, when I came and was not received by the arrogant ones of the darkness of that time, and I went to the seashore and chose some fishermen and made them My witnesses and disciples of My working word, and I succeeded in letting them then proclaim My heavenly wealth and share it far and wide throughout the earth, and they fulfilled this and prevailed against those who have tried to erase My name from the earth, from then until today.

Oh, so I have worked today, and I have a young people and I have given them My word to sound the trumpet over the earth and over the nations, and I have a royal table in their midst, and I leave Myself on the table with them, with My body and blood, and when the words of the transformation of the bread and wine are spoken, so that My Being becomes their food, behold here is a miracle that is not seen anywhere else, for on this table I, the Lord, also become a word for their food, and this word is the light from the beginning of time, oh, and this is the mystery misunderstood by the arrogant, who wonder what is with this word that goes and is shared.

Oh, behold, the table of My kingdom with them on earth is complete now and it carries on it the chalice with My body and blood in remembrance of Me with them at the table, and it carries My word, the source of eternal life, the light from the beginning, with which God created heaven and earth and all creation, and then man, and so My word is working today, and all things are done and appear through the word.

I am the One in the chalice, and I give Myself to the saints to keep them within Me, oh, and so I now have a young people and I am sharing My light with them over the earth, and in a short time I will establish a feast on this land with the power of announcing heavenly glory, and this glory will sound the trumpet far and wide, and I will gather under the glory of My word a great multitude, dressed in royal robes in Christian feast, and in no way worldly attired, for the one dressed in worldly attire is naked before Me, and the one dressed in Christian attire is distinguished from those alien to God, oh, and great joy will be in heaven and on earth on that day of glory, and I will open to view the garden of paradise from Maluri village, the place of the beginning of My word in Romania, when I, the Lord, came down and opened the mouth of My trumpet and spoke My first words, when My word was written by those who heard Me speaking through My trumpet, through the instrument of My breath, and behold, now I am sharing Myself far and wide and I am no longer lying in wait, oh, and this spring of word never ends, and it is working and still flowing with wealth and shedding light on many and much comfort, oh, and the seventy-year celebration of the Word of Christ on the hearth of the Romanian nation is coming, and I have to speak a word of watching for the spirit of the celebration and I am saying now: If any enemy of this word will try to put on a sheep's coat in order to come and dare to gather and take to those enemies like him, and who have sent him, I, the Lord, Jesus Christ, the Word of the Father, am telling him even from now on and tell him not to dare! And to those who will come closer, let them come to this meeting with Me and with heaven, and with faithfulness and lovers of My Shepherd's voice, let them seek with their hearts, and with pure steps let them set out on the path to the source of My word, and I, the Lord, am blessing the steps of the pure in heart and give them angels to accompany them on the path, and they will be embraced in My Shepherd's word. Amen.

Oh, sons of the citadel, you are toiling for the preparation of My wedding day here, in this city, for in this city is God the word over the earth, and I have in it the citadel and My little and young people, and behold, I have prepared with you the garden of paradise at Maluri village, the place where I, the Lord, descended in the year 1955, after I proposed, and then I prepared My trumpet for My work through her, and now this place is clothed in glory, for it was poor and without any brilliance, but you have toiled, and toil a little more and the gates of the garden will open for the celebration of My word, seventy years of Jesus Christ, the Word of God over the Romanian land.

I am still strengthening the wings of your heart, I am strengthening your steps and little hands, so that you can finish preparing the garden of paradise and receive guests at the table with heaven here, My sons. A little more effort and I will give you joy and peace, after much care for the Lord, and you will be able to do it all the time, because Father gives wings to the youngest, My sons.

I would stay with you longer, I would stay for My longing with you, but now My Virgin mother is giving Me a sign that you are tired and that tomorrow you are going to work again and be able to do it to the end, and she is wrapping you in her protective garment of My little ones and empowering you with her blessing as My mother and yours, and she is looking at you from My side with great love and tenderness, all for your sacrifice in the service of My glory with you, My sons.

Now, the blessing of today's feast is shared with you, those gathered at My table of words, and you are to take from it and learn to be leaders for the fulfillment of the teaching upon you, sons, because many hear and know; however, they remain behind, remain unwise towards the light of this word.

May the Romanian country be glorified among the nations, My little fish, whose fins were torn from her body, but she has remained My little fish, My love, My beauty, My sign, My longing for her after seven thousand years, since I, the Lord, stood on her land and spoke all the words for the creation of heaven and earth, and all that came into being through the word, and then the creation of man from the clay of this land, and whose secret I, the Lord, have discovered upon the earth now at the end of time.

Peace to you, Romanian people, My country! With you I am beginning My new sunrise, and soon, soon, the night will no longer descend upon you, and you will be My light upon the earth, for I am light upon you, I am the word of the creation of the world anew, I am your Shepherd, and you are My land on earth, My home, My coming, you are My love from the end of time, you are comfort, you are and will always be a comfort everywhere and over everything soon, soon, oh, My country of today. Amen, amen, amen.
