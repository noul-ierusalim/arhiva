I would like you to hear My voice and to encompass you in My heavenly word, in the spirit of My tenderness, in My peace as your Shepherd, My country of today, because I am coming to you with a word with comfort in it and I am stirring up your scents when the breeze of the wind that carries Me passes over your homeland, so that your skin may feel Me, so that you may comfort Me by feeling your heartbeat, oh, Romanian land, and behold, I am dressing you in a shirt of woven word and keeping you shrouded in heavenly mystery, because I have on your hearth My coming word upon the earth, and I desire you to be beautiful, and ever more beautiful to prepare you and to give you powers of victory over satan, My enemy from the beginning, and who has been troubled since then until today and wants to be victorious, but I am the Maker of heaven and earth and I am standing with My second coming on your hearth and I am telling you your fate of the future from God, and here, on Sunday I am embracing you in My word and saying to you: Peace to you, My Romania! The peace that I wish you has always disturbing by all kinds of enemies and temptations, for the spirit that is opposed to you and Me is mixed in your dough, oh, and it is not leaving you in peace to shape your path and the victory that all heaven awaits you, and I, the Lord, am standing above you with the heavenly hosts ready to sound over the whole earth the wakefulness for the coming of the Bridegroom and His wedding with you in the midst of the peoples and languages, to show My light and the garment of light from above and to sound the gong of eternity, and then the separation between the sheep and the goats and the peace, long awaited by the Father, the Son and the Holy Spirit, between heaven and earth, and behold, I am sitting with you at the table of the word, My country, because it is a day of rest, and I want you to be My rest, and that is why I am preparing garments of light for you, so that the peoples may see you all, that you are the land of My wedding and because I had made you a wedding dress, My land.

Behold, it is a pain for Me and for you, for you lack peace and quiet, and your steps are very heavy towards victory over the enemy antichrist, and all kinds of your enemies, and who do not let you be able to work, My country, and therefore, I am leaving a spoken word that I am rising up to investigate in detail and to lay down the thought and restlessness of every kind of enemies in your midst, who are slanderers and evildoers, for I, the Lord, have to walk, not to stay, and I must free you from obstacles, even if this cleaning will be done with pain, but I cannot let you stand still with the walking, My country. Therefore, I am raising the heavenly powers and we do cleaning to clear the way and make way for the walking forward, for My judgment is in your hearth, oh, and it will not be like man, and it will be like God the work of justice, and I am putting the seal and say Amen.

Oh, there is no peace at your helm, My country, Romanian people, for in your dough there are discontented enemies, and who want you on your knees to stand before them again. The Lord is waking up for you, for the enemies are enveloped in the spirit of lies and want to be even greater, and they will not want you freed from the enemies. I am now rising with the powers from above and we are investigating, correcting and working, oh, and I am asking those who are now at the helm, I am asking them to stay united in the work, for I, the Lord, have overcome hardship to place them at the helm, and they must take care of the country, not themselves, oh, because there is no peace in the country and there is no sitting on the sidelines when everything starts to shake again and again.

May the spirit of justice of things, the spirit of truth, fulfill the works and their progress everywhere and the country on the right path, for I, the Lord, expect a lot from those at the helm, so that they can weaken the enemy power and the greedy one from the wealth of the country, and then may the country go to victory, not to shame, not to go back, and may the enemies weaken immediately and hear what God is saying now, and they will hear and weaken. Amen.

And peace to you, My country, for with you, My Romania, with you I have to walk, and in you I am proclaiming My glory and word over the earth, over the chiefs over the nations, oh, and the glory of the Lord is coming little by little, as it is written, and the word of My mouth has all power in heaven and on earth, and I am with Romania and I am proclaiming Myself with her over the earth, I always proclaim Myself and I always fulfill, I always carry you in My word and I always work for My glory from your midst, My country of today. Amen, amen, amen.

Now, a little word of encouragement over the citadel of My word, that a holy feast must now be set in the citadel for My Virgin mother, and I am leaving a blessing for the feast table and for the heavenly work over the people at the table who will gather.

Then, I am leaving a word of command upon you, those who are still adjusting the garden at Maluri village. All the work there is a holy work, and so let it be worked like this, holy let it be worked, and all the peace from heaven is coming to earth here for all the work of this garden, of the beginning of My word upon earth in the year 1955.

Therefore, to the sons who are giving your faith, power and sacrifice for the day of My glory over this little garden of heaven, I am asking you from the midst of the heavenly powers, do not forget even for a moment the Lord, of His holiness, of His greatness with you, the work of heaven with you, and therefore, the spirit of peace in you and among you may shine just as peace shines with Us, those in heaven, and peace must stay with you at work at all times, for the work is with haste and with ever greater haste and sacrifice, oh, and I will announce Myself with the day of this garden and celebration, and with everything that will be in the mystery of that day awaited by many, by many, sons, and you must not forget this for a moment. Therefore, let yourselves be guided from heaven and get used yourselves only in this way, for you have the Lord among you, and you remain small, so that the Lord may be great, for the Lord has all power, My sons.

Oh, be good, be true, be true, sons! Make room for prayer with a cry for the country and for yourselves! Make room for moments of prayer to heaven, sons! Be full of humility before one another! Hold on with all your power to peace among you, to love among you through the spirit of brotherly peace, through My Spirit among you.

And again, peace to you, peace to you, peace in all and for all, and may it always be seen from below and above whose sons you are, oh sons. Amen, amen, amen.
