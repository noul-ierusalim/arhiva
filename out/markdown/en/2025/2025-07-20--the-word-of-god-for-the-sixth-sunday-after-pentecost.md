I am carrying you in My word, I am carrying you in My Spirit, in My love for you, My country of today, Romanian people. I am holding your hand and giving you words of encouragement, and spiritual gifts I am giving to place upon your sons, Romanian people.

Oh, you need love, My love! Peace to you from God, peace and love! May your love be blessed, and love means God!

I am Who I am, and I am eternal, I am without beginning and without end. I am Who I am, because of the love out of which I am created; therefore, I am. Woe to those who are not, to those who do not have the love of their creation, for they are nothing, they are not, as it is written!

I am coming to you with your word of creation, Romanian people. Oh, arise and come out and meet Me, for I want to adorn you with wonderful gifts and with their love, for if you do not have love, you are nothing, nothing benefits you, so it is written. Love teaches you to be, to remain and not to perish like the unbelievers. It chooses you from the sinners of the earth, from all the nations and adorns you with its gifts and with their glory, which shines and illuminates the earth and all that is in it, nations, peoples and languages.

Oh, this is how your fate from God is written for you and this is how it will be with you, and that is why I come and keep coming to you with My voice, carried in the clouds and brought down in its book, which is going to you from My side to shape your fate now, at the end of time, and for the nations on earth to know you, My chosen one, My love, the place of My coming again from the Father to men, for this time has come, and I, the Lord, am placing it over you and working you beautifully for Me and for you, and I am comforting your name and choice, body, and spirit, mountains and plains, waters and forests, and I am announcing Me with these over the earth, for all these know My language, recognize Me, receive Me and speak to Me through their love for God and through the steadfastness, with which the man separated from God, who wastes his life in all its vanities, endures like Me, and I call man to the truth from heaven to give him the gift of wisdom of life, to give him strength and holy behavior, for you are made by God, man, oh, Romanian man, and that is why I keep seeking to speak to you and for you to know My language and for you to open up to Me and sit at table with the Lord on your hearth.

Oh, do not forget God, do not forget your Master, do not despise your Maker and love Him who gives your life, love and patience, with you when you forget Him. Oh, make yourself beautiful, My country, My beautiful flower! Learn to receive Me and behave before Me, as those in heaven on earth, so that it may be as in heaven on your earth, for you are the place of My coming among men, now, when the time has come for Me to come, to come again.

Oh, do not forget love, adjust your behavior to love, not otherwise, My beloved, not otherwise, My chosen one. Love is the one that teaches you long-suffering and kindness at all times, it drives away the danger of envy, guards you from self-praise and does not fatten on destructive anger, rather, on the contrary, preserves its propriety and does not seek its own benefit for it. It does not have in its work arrogance or thought of evil or injustice, for it loves the truth for all and believes everything hoping and suffering for all, and therefore, love does not fall, never perishes, and has in its work faith and hope, and above these is love, as it is written.

Oh, Romanian people, oh, sons desired by God, oh, how ugly everything is on earth where there is not love and its working goods among people! Look and see with wide eyes everywhere and understand how desolate it is due to the lack of love and its good.

You are nothing, man, nothing if you do not have love as your work. It is the being of God in man, oh, and I want to cover the whole earth with this spiritual gift and to teach man not to waste the years of his life without redeeming them in eternal life, and eternity means God and with God, and it means satan defeated by God and man, for satan delights man starting from heaven with his fleeting lures; however, he who has love in his being overcomes hell and loves what remains and follows them from here until the eternity of the New Jerusalem, promised by God for those steadfast in love, and who prepare to be eternal, and are always preparing.

Oh, how sweet, how tender, how beautiful, how warm I am teaching what life and its love are, Romanian son! I am bestowing upon you heavenly gifts, and that is why I am coming to you with word, oh, and I am restless and I have always come, for I, the Lord, see that the time demands it and ask the angels who fight for your life with God, and ask the saints with their prayers not to leave you from under My word, My country, Romanian people.

Oh, here is the great saying of the elders that says: *“Whoever does not know love, may God not give it to him.”* Oh, there are so many souls deprived of love and its goods, so many people wander without this light, which is not given to those who do not have love and do not have this gift from God, because love means God. Those without decency do not share in the gifts of love, as do all the proud, all the boastful, all those who lie every second, that is, all the time in a false life, all the greedy, all the angry, all those who are many and devoid of spiritual gifts. Oh, those who are impatient, the envious, the wicked, those who walk badly do not share in these gifts.

Here are those who are devoid of the love of heaven, those who are devoid of God and who have deceived many and have risen to the helm of the country without deserving it, oh, and these are seeking their own! Oh, who makes you do this iniquity and want to be over the cities at the helm, when you are not honest, when you are greedy and lie all the time and live in iniquities, which you choose as your life? Oh, vanity of vanities, who fill the earth with deceit, oh, how great will be your fall, shame and judgment!

Get away from the high places above men, get away, you who work iniquity and injustice all the time! These heights are not for you, but they are for the righteous and true for them and for their fellows. I, the Lord, will break you down, for the justice of all is coming, and if it comes, oh, where will you be able to hide, you who are greedy and indecent in everything and jealous with work and behavior?

The Lord is coming! This is the time now, and the wicked world is not receiving Me, is not opening to Me, does not believe, for there is no way for the one to believe to whom the gift of faith is not distributed from heaven.

I will always shepherd you on the days of rest, Romanian people, and My word over you will work for you in secret, and then the curtain will be drawn and you will learn the work and its fulfillment and you will stand in amazement and ask yourself: *“When were all these things done?”*

I am embracing you again and again in My comforting wish for Mine: Peace to you, My country, My Romania! You are the riverbed through which My word flows and is written on the earth for My testimony, and I am your Shepherd all the time. Peace to you! You are My gift from the Father for this time of Mine with you. I am embracing you in My love and kissing your face and soul and comforting Me by embracing you in My word. Oh, remain in My love, remain Mine, remain under My blessing, you, the blessed one among the peoples with the name of Romania, the mysterious name of My choice now, of the earth chosen at the beginning and at the end for My glory and of My chosen people to prepare it for Me, for God, for My coming word on earth, now, at the end of time, as it is written.

I am walking with you across the earth with My new name and yours, My country, the Word of God **(Apoc: 19/13)** and His people, the Romanian people now shepherded by this word. Amen, amen, amen.
