My coming with the glory of word carries Me on the cloud above the Romanian land and nation, so that the evil spirit may know Me, hear and see Me that I am your Shepherd, My country of today, and that I have you under My protective hand from the beginning to the end of time, oh, and that is why today, on the first day of the week, again and again I am surrounding you in My word of love for you and I am sealing you with what protects you from God, and behold, peace to you, oh, peace to you, My beautiful garden! I give you even greater beauty when I encompass you in My word of a tender and gentle Shepherd, oh, and I strengthen heavenly guard over you when I envelop you in the word of My mouth above you.

The cloud of My glory from this time always stands in your sky and carries My word on the wind, so that I may place it on your table of goodness from heaven over your earth and spirit, Romanian people. The opposing spirit is humbled unwillingly when My Shepherd's voice gives you blessing and holy protection from heaven, until the mystery that keeps you between earth and heaven will open like a flower, petal by petal, and will announce your beauty and fragrance and will open a wide way to the whisper of My word to the nations on earth, so that they may seek your heavenly wealth, your beauty from God placed upon you at the creation of heaven and earth, hidden until now, when I am coming to earth as a Shepherd's word above you and rule through the word and distribute Myself everywhere, because I guard the course of time and all that is and happens under the sun, and you are My doll and you have a being from God, Who keeps preparing you with many beauties to show Himself with you in His glory from the end of time, and much prepared to be and to be seen, oh, and much impatience is borne by those in heaven and they always keep asking Me: *“Lord, until when?”*

God is speaking with your spirit, My country from the end of time, Romanian nation. All men are liars in every way and form, so it is written, but the truth of My word does not pass without being fulfilled, oh, and therefore, your fate lies in My word above you, Romanian people, and behold, I had to come above you with the heaven of My glory to prepare you for My coming at the end of time, to choose from you a people prepared for My reception and to place them in My wills, to carry My coming and word over the earth, so that the way may appear and be opened for the angels who will sound their trumpets and announce that the Lord is coming with tens of thousands of His saints, oh, and therefore arise, Romanian people, and hear the voice of the Bridegroom and open to Him, so that He may enter you and prepare you for the eternal wedding feast, as is your fate from God, oh, and you will believe and you will be, for I, the Lord, am the Truth and I fulfill My word. Amen.

I am strengthening with the heavenly powers the guard and the course of those at the helm of the Romanian nation, but also the wisdom with which they may work the path and enlightenment of this nation, because without support from above it is difficult, because of the adversaries who do not know how to deal with one another, after so many years they have sucked the strength, the wealth, the power and the honor of the Romanian nation, chosen by the Lord at the end of time, as a people blessed through time until today, when they have a Shepherd from heaven and guide their steps and protection under the guard of angels, because the time is difficult, and that is why the Lord is coming to earth in word, He is coming on a cloud above the Romanian land.

Oh, My country, we are remaining embraced. Amen.

Fear God, you evil spirits and evil people and enemies in every way and form! Fear, and plot no more, for the time is on the Lord's side, Who comes to earth with a word! Fear and humble yourselves if you can, for I am with mercy in My arms for those who humble themselves, abandoning pride and the longing for power, oh, for there is no power except the Lord God, Who is making the world again, as it is written!

I am preparing Romania for a holy holiday. I am placing the holiday of the beginning of My word in 1955, on the hearth of this nation, and I will show My beautiful story put on film, seventy years of the word of Christ on the Romanian land, and peace to you, Romanian nation, on the eve of the heavenly holiday on your land!

Let the celebration be as in heaven, not as on earth, for on earth all man has given himself over to ugliness, both in spirit and body, in dress and gait. Let it be as in heaven on the Romanian land on the day of this celebration, for I have a citadel of word on this earth and I am calling to the celebration those who are faithful to My word, and who are changing in face and nature and preparing for the holy celebration, for I, the Lord, am setting a heavenly wedding table on the Romanian land, and we will sit at the table of word in the garden of heaven at Maluri village, and heaven will be the first guest at My table with those who will gather, with those who believe and with those who will believe in My coming on the clouds with the glory of word above the Romanian land. Amen, amen, amen.
