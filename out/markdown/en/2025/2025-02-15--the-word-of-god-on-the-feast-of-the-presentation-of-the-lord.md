I am welcomed in the citadel of My word on a holy holiday day, and I am entering and leaving My word in My book today and setting it on My path, for I, the Lord, Jesus Christ, walk with the word, I walk and have been walking for seventy years across the earth, and the book of My word is on the table of the Romanian people and I take from it and send it over the earth and over people, over the good and over the bad, over those who believe and over those who do not believe, and My word works and is fulfilled, oh, and those in heaven have waited a long time for this time to come, for Me to come from the Father with the word over the earth and for God to be able with the beauty of heaven among people, for all people, nations and even the ruler of the nations have their plans and thoughts, and no one works in the name of God and as God, and everyone forgets that the earth is the Lord's; therefore, behold, I must watch over the earth with all the heavenly powers so that people may not ruin it according to their own minds, so that they may not work wickedness, so that they may not drive away peace on earth, as long as there is, oh, and that is why I have commanded the angelic hosts to strengthen the heavenly guard and to take care of life on earth, all creation and the world of people, birds and animals of all kinds, and here I am working and watching with power, and My work is the word, and this is how I am going.

I am Who I am. I am the Alpha and the Omega. This time has come because it had to come and that I may be able to say while working: I am the Alpha and the Omega, the beginning and the end, and again the beginning, and I am working as in the beginning, and I am working as in the end and I am calling man to holiness, I am calling the world to God, for behold, the Lord is coming, Christ is coming upon the earth as word.

Oh, I am taking care of you with the glory of My word on your land, Romanian nation. Peace to you, people loved by God! Peace to you, My country from the end of time! I am coming to your land with the voice of My word and ruling over the peoples and leaving My peace from place to place, for people know only to work war and only to give each other grief and much unrest. The spirit of greed, the spirit of pride, spirits alien to God haunt the earth and the world of men, oh, and these spirits spoil the good vigilance, and I am passing everywhere with the vigilance from heaven, watching, bandaging wounds and sharing hope and salvation, for I carried the cross and crucifixion, so that I could then take from the Father all power in heaven and on earth and be among the peoples and speaking to them.

First, I am sharing with you My coming from the Father, My country today. Peace to you! I am embracing you in My embrace and I am giving you from heaven. You are God's gift from the Father for My return to men. You have great favor with the Father, because you are the earth from which I took with My hand and created man. I miss you; I have missed speaking a fresh word with you. I have loved you with the love of the Father and the Son and the Holy Spirit and I have carried Myself over the earth with My glory from your midst, with the word of My mouth, which I have placed on your land for seventy years, just as My book today is carrying in it My word from all these years over the earth.

I let you know that I am sitting down to discipline the rebellious ones among this nation and who have hoped and still hope that they will exchange Me for them over you, My country. I am speaking a word of fire. I am going to discipline all the arrogant and lying and disturbing ones, because I have mercy on them. *(The presidents of the parties that form the governing coalition in Romania, r.n.)* I will take one by one all those who have brought unrest and the spirit of lies and betrayal, all those who are eager for ranks and power. I will look over them if they humble themselves, if they discipline themselves, and I will work to change them for the better and in pure work for them and for the country, and those who will not discipline themselves will stand face to face with My word for the rebellious and arrogant ones and will stop their evil intentions. I will build brotherly love over those who have stretched out their hands and steps to the top of the watchtowers, oh, because it is impossible for a people of one country and name to fight against each other in such a dangerous and bad way, for God is love, not division, and I will work over you, Romanian people, and I will place the spirit of humility in those who are disturbing and I will prevail with peace over your land, and then from the sides for you.

[/legatura_la_teme][legatura_la_teme id_capitol="3" id_subcapitol="5"]And now, I am sharing Myself over the surrounding nations, from near and far, and I am speaking a heavenly word: Peace to you, to the nations and to the great ones over the nations! Peace to you, not war, not conquests, not greed, and instead of these great evils, peace to you! I am announcing to you that the plans that have kept confusing your minds and peace among you will not come true, for My plans are not like your plans. No longer seek to do things for yourselves in God's place. Be content with what you are and have, and then work peace and share only peace, as I, the Lord, work and share.

Oh, there is a lot from saying to doing. God stands against the plans of the leaders of the nations, and therefore let peace and the joy that comes from this great work be set to work, for I said so, while I was with the visible body on earth and I said: *"Peace be with you!"* and so I taught the disciples to work wherever they would go and share God on earth.

Again and again, I send word and say: Peace to you, peoples! Do not forget My greetings shared with you from the citadel of My word on the land of the Romanian nation! Be good, be good, be good-minded! With a spirit of peace, learn to work, to resemble God, for satan, My enemy and yours, this arrogant spirit since the creation of the world, has been inciting you to war, to unlove; however, you must have mercy on one another with pure hearts and be like God, for behold, I am coming to you, I am coming from heaven to earth with a word and I am teaching you heavenly and gathering you in My peace, so that I can work with you for peace on earth, for the glory of the Lord is coming, and this time has come and it will be with people on earth, and no beauty will surpass it, oh, no, for the kingdom of God is what will take the place of temporality, and therefore you, the leaders over the nations, rise up and work with God at this eternal construction, the kingdom of God with people, for this time has come and it is written in the Scriptures that I am the Alpha and the Omega.

Again and again, I will send word over the earth, for there is much to be done and settled, and in the citadel of My word let there be watchfulness and let Me be welcomed, and let Me be shared.

[/legatura_la_teme][legatura_la_teme id_capitol="5" id_subcapitol="6"]Peace to you, My citadel of coming, when I come word upon the earth! Oh, children of the citadel, My glory is now working with you in preparing the garden of paradise at Maluri village. Those in heaven hear this all the time from My mouth about you and are preparing for the great celebration, because we have come to establish on earth the year pleasing to the Lord, and we will commemorate on that day the beginning of My word upon the hearth of the Romanian nation in the year 1955, on the feast of My Resurrection of that year.

We will prepare ourselves for guests, We and you, sons. It will be beautiful as at the creation of the world. We will unfold on that day the sweet story of the work of My word, from then until today, and great joy will float between heaven and earth, and behold the land of the sun, in which I, the Lord, have set My dwelling place and I am coming out over the earth like the Bridegroom from His bedroom and I am embracing the earth and the peoples upon it and warming them in the bosom of My glory.

Peace to you, land of the sun, My Romania, My beautiful girl! The Lord is in you, as word. I am the Lord your God, with open arms towards all those who will come to My glory in you, to My word that makes the world again, a new heaven, a new earth and a new man, as it is written to work at the end of time. Amen, amen, amen.[/legatura_la_teme]
