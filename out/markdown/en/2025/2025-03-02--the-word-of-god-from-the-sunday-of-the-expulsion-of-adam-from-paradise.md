[legatura_la_teme id_capitol="3" id_subcapitol="5"]The heavens open and My voice, the voice of God is sent upon the earth. I am the Alpha and the Omega, I am. The Father Sabaoth sends Me. I do not come nor speak of Myself. I am the Word of the Father. The Father is in Me, and I am in Him.

The earth cries out to God and says: *“I can't take it anymore, Lord!”* Many sorrowful and frightened voices cry out and say to Me: *“Come, Lord, come!”*

Oh, behold, therefore, I come. The sorrow and the anxiety of the oppressed of the earth call to Me, and their cry opens the heavens, oh, that they have no place to hide from the terror. Oh, why is it so? Oh, there is no peace on earth, and the sighing from below meets the sighing from above, and fear increases everywhere and the cry from the earth to the heavens, and I, the Lord, how can I not raise My voice and not speak over the earth and not answer?

Amen, amen I say to you, the great ones over men, oh, you are not alone. There is also God. I, the Lord, am saying this to you, leaving My word upon you from above, for I am standing on the cloud and speaking to you and saying to you: You are not alone. Do not forget God! You did not make the heavens and the earth. I am their Maker, and therefore, fear God, not only each other.

I am crying to you from above: Peace be with you! Again and again, I cry, and again and again you do not listen to God, and that is why fear grows. The voice of the church is telling and reminding you of the beginning of the fasting season, for the coming of the feast day of My resurrection, two thousand years ago, after the leaders of the time took My days by death on the cross. Oh, but how is the fasting and blessing season to begin on earth over this time, for blood is shed on earth, terror and death are multiplying over the helpless under bullets, under war machines on earth and in the air? The spirit of hatred, fear and revenge is covering the earth in every way and form, and I, the Lord, am carried by the angelic hosts and the heavenly powers, and passing through the moments of terror on earth and in the air, and comprising with the angels, those who are dying crushed by the scythe of death.

I am crying out to you, to the strong and great above men: Stop crossing God!

Oh, how great you are! You are the terror of those who die murdered without a trace. I am coming to you and crying with My divine voice far and wide and I am saying to you with a bow like a God full of mercy: Stop! Let the peace from God come and comprise you in it, but stop being greedy and cruel. Make peace among yourselves as brothers and then make peace with God. My voice is settling over the earth and a book is written on the land of the Romanian nation, to prepare the kingdom of heaven and its glory with men and the renewal of the world, that this Scripture and this time have arrived, and what are you doing, and until when? Oh, ask yourselves, too, but answer yourselves, too.

Oh, why so much war and always war? Know that I will stop you. I am the Alpha and the Omega, I will discipline you with mercy or with pain, if you do not settle in love, if you do not forgive, if you do not sheathe your sword.

I am still asking you, waiting for you and urging you towards the spirit of tranquility and peace, and therefore, thus says God: Peace be with you!

Peace to you again and again, for you have caused great pain to the earth beneath you and you do not find a good opportunity to stop, to put an end to the destructive spirit!

I am leaving upon you the spirit of wisdom from above, that you may reckon with it your thoughts, your life and your human plans, for behold, you have a number of years, and you do not know how many you will have more, but you keep working as if you had no end of days on earth, and that you will inherit eternally what you give to have and keep having. Oh, why do you not look up to God, so that you may not remain greater than Him on earth?

Oh, humble yourselves, you leaders over the peoples, humble yourselves, no matter how great and strong you feel, for I, Jesus Christ, the Son of the Father Sabaoth, was true God and I humbled Myself very, very much, because out of compassion for man I came down from heaven to be born a man on earth and to humble Myself until death on the cross, so that all man may then understand how small he is, and how great he thinks he can also lose God before him.

God created the heavens and the earth, and then took the dust with his hand and formed man and breathed the breath of life into him and made him a living being, and I took from his body and I made them mates and they bore children. However, the spirit of arrogance destroyed the love between these children and they harmed each other, oh, that God did not need their sacrifice, but their humility. However, they worked on their own and have established death over people, from that time until today, and I am saying this to you, the leaders over people, that the spirit of arrogance also works in you and urges you to rise above God, above each other and above people.

I look at you from heaven moment by moment and I am merciful. I have compassion over you and those who die under your play, for greed and conceit are evil spirits, which bring hell out of the depths and show it working on earth, and God weeps, overcome by you, but until when?

I would like you to bow down and receive My word for you, and I would like that it may work within you and place you before Me with the humility that you need so much for yourselves and for those over whom you rule on earth. Therefore, be reconciled to God first, and then peace will come among you.

Oh, do not forget Me, do not forget Me, remember Me, receive Me! I am Who I am. Feel yourself for a moment in My place to understand My pain and mercy, tenderness and patience, and then feel yourself small, helpless people. Only in this way, will you understand why I descend My voice to you.

I lost man from paradise because of his disobedience and the arrogance with which he felt, believed and desired himself to be greater than God. There is life after the earthly life of the body. Look towards these chambers of eternal life and gather in them rest for eternity, not the sleepless worm of the opposition between you and God from your time on earth.

I am only love. I give you of Myself, so that you too, may be love. I call out to you tenderly: make peace on earth and establish it among yourselves! Ask God for what you do not have apart from the things on earth, which you keep gathering around you. Ask for yourselves what does not perish, ask, so that God may give you, for you have only what perishes. Oh, and do not forget, do not forget the work of the church, the voice of the church, the seven-week fast for the preparation of your life with God, and then the joy of the feast of the resurrection and the peace among you.

Again and again, I embrace and advise you with the spirit of peace, and for which you need the Spirit from heaven. Bow down as I, the Lord, bowed down and spoke to you on the day of painful remembrance, when God lost man from paradise, for his lack of obedience and humility expelled him from the sweet glory of the garden of paradise.

I am placing a blessing from heaven upon all the sons of God on earth for holy power during the seven weeks of humility and sanctification to welcome the feast of My resurrection, and I am leaving this blessing also upon the sons of men, oh, that many of them will have the gift of knowing the Lord and receiving power to become sons of God on earth.

I have sweetly blessed this year, the year in which I, the Lord, have fulfilled Myself on earth with seventy years of word on the land of the Romanian nation, and a great and famous celebration will be established from end to end, so that I may then reveal My glory that has remained secret for seventy years, so that the great day of the celebration of My word can be prepared for Me, the great Romanian celebration, the celebration of renewal, and which I have been waiting for so long, so that I may sit with it and announce it and that the peoples and languages of the earth may know My love from the end of time, My word, the word of God that makes the world again, for the new birth of the world has been My longing that I have showed in all My word, in all this time of My coming as word upon the earth, and with which I came and am coming from the Father. Amen, amen, amen.[/legatura_la_teme]
