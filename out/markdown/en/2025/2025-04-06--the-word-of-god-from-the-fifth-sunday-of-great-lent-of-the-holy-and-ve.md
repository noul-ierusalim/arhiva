When I come word on earth to sit down with him in My book today, I take with Me the heavenly retinues with which I carry Myself between heaven and earth and I write My word both on earth and in heaven and I share with it, with My work given to Me by the Father to work, and behold, on holy holidays and on Sundays I have a work of word on earth, oh, that there is much to work and to write down below and above, and I have workers from above and below and God does not stop from His work written and fulfilled all the time, from the first day of the creation of the visible and the invisible and until the age of eternity, until then I keep speaking and writing, and all are placed as in a film in the heavenly places for eternity, and behold, I work in this time the work at the end of My visible work, and I have a little glove of obedient people and I rely on it with My coming as word on earth, as in all times I have prepared someone to have with to come down on earth, when I come to do My work among people.

Oh, not only man works. I, the Lord, work much of everything, even those that man works for good or for evil, as he happens to gather his reward through deeds, for I see before man's sight and place them all according to his life with God or without God, and behold, on earth there is a lot of work without God on the part of man possessed by the spirit of pride, the spirit that separated man from God in heaven, for the man who does not love God is proud and does not have any love, for how could the proud man love?

Oh, the people on earth do not know why they do not love God, why they do not bow down, why they do not give God the right to work through them and from them according to His will from above.

Oh, peace to you from the Lord your God, My country of today, My Romania from the beginning of creation and today! I love your place on earth very much. You are the earth on which I stood with My feet and took clay from your body, I made the body of man and then breathed into him the spirit of life, and man was then a living being like God, oh, and man then has behaved with Me as if I had not made him to be him, and he was filled with pride when he saw how and how much he was, and from then until today man has loved himself and has not loved God, his Maker, from Whom he came into being, and that is why I said, two thousand years ago, when I came and stood again on earth and I said to every man who wanted to follow Me closely, I said to him: Whoever wants to come after Me and follow Me, let him deny himself and take up his cross and follow Me, leaving everything he had and gaining God on his side.

Oh, My country today, My word springs from you over the earth, as at the creation of the world. The work of the new man, after My image and likeness, this I have to work in these days through My word placed by My mouth on your hearth, on your table. I love you with great love and I would like to rejoice in you as I rejoiced when I created heaven and earth and man then, the man created by God from your body and from My Spirit.

Oh, what a great mystery! There is no one to shepherd this pasture that fell from heaven like the manna that came to Israel when I brought him out of the world to be placed on the blessed land. I gather with the heavenly hosts above you and comprise you in My Spirit and in My speech above you and I give you from heaven, so that you may learn where to choose your food to make honey and that your labor with God may be beneficial. I have over you your holy ancestors, who watch over your body and spirit, oh, and I have over you My mother, the Virgin, to protect you and Me to guard what is Mine, to raise in you a handful of people clothed in the gift and grace of faith of My coming as word on earth now, at the end of time, and let them divide Me from edge to edge with the news of My coming again from the Father, to prepare My appearance and victory, as it is written that My work from the Father may be fulfilled in all its completeness, for My Father has sent His servants over the earth since creation until today to turn man back, after man took possession of the earth and the heavens, and the self-absorbed man killed all the Father's messengers, and then the Father sent Me, His only Son; however, the man’s pride also gave Me to perishing, and now I am clothed in the Spirit, in the word, oh, and man can no longer overcome Me, and I am coming with the Father, and the Father has made an account for man, for what man has done against Him, and now is coming the victory of God and of the Son of God, and behold, I am the resurrected One, I am the One Who is returning to bring victory and justice to My chosen and righteous ones from all times sent by the Father after man, and I am speaking all this on the eve of a holy feast, the feast of the divine Good News, when My Virgin mother was wooed by an angel two thousand years ago to receive Me to come and be born as a Child through the body of a Virgin, as it was written in the Scriptures about the Virgin, who would bear God, the Son, in her womb, oh, and I am at the table of the word with her here and We are comforting each other and taking from her and sharing paternally for whom wants to take from Us and be a distributor of comfort as We are from one to another among people and to rejoice that the heavenly message and all its work established by the word at the end of time goes forth, so that people may know that God is preparing Himself with great glory on the Romanian land and bringing forth above those things worked in secret for the new face of the world, for this renewal is coming, it is coming, that this time has come, and the Lord begins His work again. Amen.

[/legatura_la_teme][legatura_la_teme id_capitol="8" id_subcapitol="5"]Behold, faith and fulfillments through faith are needed, for all things were fulfilled through faith in the times under heaven, oh, that the time of the unseen things of God's creation is coming, and blessed are and will be those who are touched by the mystery of the Holy Spirit, for this is how they are seen, and My heaven means the unseen things of God between earth and heaven. And how so? Behold, the unseen things are those that were with God from eternity, that is, before the creation of those things made by God visible to the eyes of man, oh, and the time of the unseen things of God is coming to be seen from earth, and therefore, I am crying out everywhere and saying: Blessed are you who see the works of God, so that you too can say like the prophet and king David, who spoke to the Lord and said with great humility: *«Lord, the unseen things and the hidden things of Your wisdom You have shown me!»* Amen. Behold, I will show these things, when at their appearance the pride of man will end and man will see the dwellings of My Father and of His Son and of those who reign with Him forever, and the creation of man will no longer be then, for all things pass away, as it is written that they pass away, so that God and His love and those who love may remain, for those who love are holy, all are holy through love.

[/legatura_la_teme][legatura_la_teme id_capitol="5" id_subcapitol="6"]Now, I am enlivening with fresh words the preparation and work at Maluri village, the place where I, the Lord, descended and began the trumpeting of My word in 1955, oh, and I am waiting for the last watered flower, the last hand of preparation and the wide-open gates and the heavenly joy of that day, when My seventy-year word will flow from above and ennoble the hearts of those who will gather for the celebration, at My cry of proclamation, let those who long to come to Me, come and tell them that I am there with the unseen of creation, with all the heavenly powers, with the angels and the saints in thousands and with all the holy ancestors, so that we can open the book and My seventy-year story on the land of the Romanian nation, when I have worked and spoken so mysteriously and everything came into being, all in the unseen of God and am ready to be shown, oh, and what faith do you need, oh man, for this sight over your spirit that may say within you: *“Lord, you have shown me the hidden and unseen things of Your wisdom.”*

I am leaving now a blessing in the book for the beginning of the proclamation of the feast at Maluri village, and you will go, My sons, to the royal city *(Bucharest)* of My Romania and you will place there *(The “Seventh Trumpet” Exhibition at the Romanian Peasant Museum)*, for display the symbols with which I, the Lord, work and fulfill My word over the earth, the word of all My time with you, and then you will come home for the progress of the preparation of the garden of paradise at Maluri village, and this feast day is awaited with the most unbridled longing by all the heavenly hosts, and many on earth will gather and partake of My glory with you, and until then I, the Lord, will keep speaking to you, oh sons. Amen, amen, amen.[/legatura_la_teme]
