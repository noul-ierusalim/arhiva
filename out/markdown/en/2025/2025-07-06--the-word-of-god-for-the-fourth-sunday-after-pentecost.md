My Word works upon the earth with its utterance, and this is how it testifies to its heavenly origin, power and its fulfillment for which it is spoken.

I am Who I am, I am this word, I am the source from which it flows and works its fulfillment. I am the Lord, Jesus Christ, the Word of God, and My work is the word and its fulfillment. I have Romania, as the land of My coming word upon the earth now, at the end of time, and if I have it as Mine upon it, oh, how can I stop My word upon it and its fulfilling work?

The Lord has you as His country, My Romania. You are My joy and My wealth on earth, you are My sweet and dear treasure, and if you are My treasure, My heart is always with you, as is the word of the heart, and I am preparing for you with it great glory, as great as My glory, for I am your glory and I have been in your midst for seventy years since I wrapped you in My word, which speaks over you always, always and always from now on, for your radiance is approaching and the peoples will look at you like the sun, together with My victory over the work and servants of the antichrist, oh, that I have to cut off the heads of this dragon one by one, who has been mocking God on earth since the beginning, since the creation of man, and now, at the end, satan has made Sodom and Gomorrah everywhere and is shamelessly parading the powers of hell that have come out above everything, and this shame done to God by this defiled and red enemy everywhere, oh, this is what is hasting Me the power of victory and behold, I am wielding the sword of the word face to face with satan over the world of men, so that he may make all of them devils, but no, satan, no. I will stop you with a righteous fight **(See Apoc: 17/7-14)** and I will roll you into the abyss of fire, in which your place is reserved by the word set in the Scriptures to be fulfilled upon you, **(Apoc: 20/1-3)** for behold how far you are trying to go! Oh, you are setting upon Me, and you do not know what you are doing through all the corruption that you bring out of hell to place upon those on earth. I am coming before you with the heavenly hosts (Apoc: 20/4) and you will be cast into the fire, as it is written by the one who accuses all to God, day and night, **(Apoc: 12/10)** and testifies to his own, but here is what I am telling you now:

Man is created by God in His image and likeness, and there is no way for you to escape from the hand of the One Who created man as His rest, and here I am face to face with you, and you are caught in the act with all your dark plan on the souls of men to make everyone devils like you. However, I am coming, examining My creation and finding you walking in My inheritance, and I am writing you the eternal condemnation, freeing people from the trap you have set for them throughout the earth where you have placed them to perform miracles for you with their hands and to charm them towards them, so that they lose the power of their soul and blindly serve you, day and night, through the sin they have wrought; therefore, I, Jesus Christ, am coming in your way, speaking of your devilish deeds and sitting down to speak over them and say: *“It is finished!”*, and it will end at My word, and I will redeem from sin the creation of My hands and of My life-giving Spirit. And just as I created man from pure earth and baptized him in the waters of the creation of heaven and earth, so will I work now and raise man from the swamp of black sin, into which you have pushed him and completely bogged him down, and your shame will not be measured, but will be condemned to fire together with you, for fire is sin, and death is as well, but I am the One Who is resurrecting all the small and great dead who are to be resurrected, and you will pass to the place prepared by God for His enemy who appeared in ambush in heaven to confuse the walk with God of man created by My hand in the beginning and to shake the rest of God. Do you hear?

[/legatura_la_teme][legatura_la_teme id_capitol="3" id_subcapitol="4"]Everyone sleeps as they make their bed, everyone receives according to their deeds, that is how it is at the end of their journey, but I take care of you, My Romania, and here I am, on your land, judging Myself with the antichrist, with satan, the old man in evil, and I write My book of My word on your hearth and it is called the book of judgment of My enemies, of the thieves, of the deceivers, of those who have been ashamed of God, with this word of Mine from the end of time, oh, and what will the servants of the flock of My church do, and where will they find to hide, who did not open a word to My coming upon the earth? However, I have passed over and placed them before Me, speaking to them of their cowardice, carelessness, disbelief and the leisure in which they live without caring about the life of the flock, for behold, great shame will fall upon their faces soon, soon, for My seventy-year word will illuminate the earth far and wide, kings and lords, great and small, and peoples everywhere will give each other the sweet news of the joy that comes to fill the world with the glory of God, with the beauty that does not reach the mind of man, with the spirit that will be divided without measure so that there will be enough, as it is written, and the shepherds of Romania will be asked, who have been silent for so long with My light hidden under the bushel, oh, that they have not given an answer to those who asked and will keep asking about My place and word on the Romanian land.

The pope came from Rome, he came to Romania many years ago and sent his servants to bring him My word and his history here in Romania, and he would have liked to be able to see, to be able to learn more, but no path was opened for him, no word with which he could be answered from the pastors of the church in Romania.

The patriarch of the Russians came to learn the history of My word, which reaches everywhere like the blowing wind and carries the news far and wide, oh, and he, too, received no answer to his search, and he left bewildered, disappointed, wondering *"Why so?"*

Many and more will come and will keep coming, oh, and the shepherds of Romania cannot put a lid on the source of My seventy-year-old word, placed in its book, when it comes from heaven to be gathered and placed on the way to all the edges. **(Apoc.14:6)**

Oh, how will the leaders of the Romanian church bear the shame of their indifference, through which they remained silent for so long towards My source of word, which flows from the throne of God upon the earth, as it is written? **(Apoc: 14/6)**

The black stain on the face of the church, after the gathering at the synod in Crete around the patriarch with the great name and who sold his soul and joined the adversary of Christ and plays according to the song of the antichrist, who works through the people of the church, so that the ignorant crowds of My mysteries may believe in him through the church, and so that this enemy will be established as the god of heaven and earth, oh, how can this stain be erased? *(Reference to “The Pan Orthodox Synod”, which took place on 16th – 27th June 2016 on the Crete Island in Greece, r.n.)*

This is what this enemy, called the antichrist, the enemy who came from the lands without God, did! Behold, he has been waging war in the neighboring country of Romania, *(Ukraine, r.n.)*, daring to overthrow our watchful shepherds of the faith of our ancestors and to have them removed from the altars, so that they could be placed as servants of the antichrist in the clothes of church shepherds, and they changed the celebration of Christmas and the feasts of the saints from the ancestral calendar *(The political leadership of Ukraine forced the Orthodox Church of Ukraine, under pressure from the Ecumenical Patriarch of Constantinople, Patriarch Bartholomew, to switch the vast majority of church holidays, mainly the Feast of the Nativity of Jesus Christ, from the old style - the Julian Calendar, to the new style - the Gregorian Calendar, Catholic, this leading to strong conflicts between pastors and among believers in Ukraine, r.n.)* and joined forces with the pagans without saints and without faith, and behold, how much suffering and how much war with painful pay this country, fully attacked by the rejection of the faith of our ancestors, is now enduring, for the saints of heaven are taking revenge on the disorder that struck the ancestral church law, and behold the war for the faith, and those on earth do not know the battle between darkness and light, the angels of God with the angels of satan, oh, and who can now say: *"Enough!"*

Oh, My country today, Mine as at the beginning of My creation, I have you from the Father to be the path of My coming word on earth and to proclaim Me to the ends, illuminating the world with the glory of My word. The people of the church, as well as those who have hung on chairs with high positions across the country to suck the money, oh, so do the shepherds who must shepherd the flock, to comfort, to show the path of salvation, they do the same, they demand from the lowly, from those who have little and take everything from them, and the elders and rulers of the church live in luxury, and the flock is no longer made up of sons, but only of laborers, because sons should be exempted from the taxes, as it is written, but how can shepherds do such a thing, how, when they get dirty on their honey fingers and still want to suck the sweetness on fingers, and the shepherds are ashamed to confirm those who ask about My word and its origin?

The poor give alms to the rich. They take from those who have little and collect, they give alms, bonuses and large pensions to those who spend nothing from their work, but only from the collection from the poor, from which they collect for the rich. That is how it is with those in the council of the country and that is how it is with those in front of the Christian flock, oh, and this injustice reaches heaven, and I would ask these greedy people to wake up a moment earlier before their just reward catches up with them, and I would tell them to look to heaven and try with God, too, because the Lord is not pleased with how they are, separated so much, so much from God with their lives.

Take also from heaven, you who have reached the forefront on thrones of power over the country, or on chairs of church pastors! Take also from heaven advice and work for yourselves, not only from the earth from each other, not only in the pleasure of the antichrist to keep working with knowledge, or without knowledge, what you are working. Oh, the Christian path and life are far too beautiful, but you do not want to know and feel its taste and its holy perfume. Put Me to the test to see how sweet it is with God on earth, and how bitter your life has been far from the will of the Lord until now, and with so many mistakes, day after day. Adam liked it in paradise, but not with God, because of his steps. Oh, that is how you are, too, you like to be among good things and ranks, but without God in your walk, because you can't have both God and satan, you can't have two masters, you can't, you sons who have strayed from God and wanderers for your fellow men.

I am admonishing you with great love, My elder and younger sons in positions over the Christian flock and over the country. Oh, stop sucking the strength of the poor! Stop this sin that cries out to heaven! You have reached out for loans from foreigners to buy your supporters, who may keep you in positions at the top, but these supporters of yours did not know, and do not know what troubles you are preparing for them.

Oh, how can the Lord now save you from the payment of your deeds? Behold how deceptive mammon is, this devil, money! Oh, come to your senses and repent! Support your country and nation now, in the eleventh hour, so that the Romanian people may not fall into helplessness, so that they are not despised by those around them, sons! Put your shoulder to repair the standing of the Romanian nation! I, the Lord, beg you and ask you this, and may My word show its power over you. Amen.

I have spoken with the shepherds of the Christian flock and with the elders of the Christian and Romanian people, that not all Romanians lead Christian lives, oh, but Romania will be Christian, not only through baptism, moreover, also through Christian life, and that will be her protection and glory from heaven descended upon her for her election by God now, at the end of time.

And, I am now descending a word of blessing and peace from heaven upon My country today, oh, and I will keep you always under the glory of My word, so that it may work for you moment by moment, My country. Do you hear?

And to those who watch over her fate and recovery, I always send the heavenly powers of support for them and by their side, for the waters are troubled and we must watch from heaven over the country and over those who watch over it. Amen.

Oh, sons from the citadel of My word, peace to you, sons, and I will speak with you and for you in the day that follows this to revive you, only that you may take from God for yourselves, because I have work with you, and I have called you My hands and feet, oh, and I will give you enlivening counsel and I will breathe it upon you, so that you may know that everything that you cannot do, I can, God can do through you, and you will be able to do through Him, you will be able to do for God, you will always be able to work, oh, sons. Amen, amen, amen.
