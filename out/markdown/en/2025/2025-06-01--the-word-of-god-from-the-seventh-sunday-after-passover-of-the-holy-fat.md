I am wrapped in the word and I am leaving My voice from the cloud **(Apoc: 1/6-7)** over the citadel of My word on the land of the Romanian nation and I shepherd with the word the fate of this nation, which the Father has destined to make My coming to Him at the end of time and to announce to the peoples and languages that the Lord word is coming to earth, He is coming to shepherd and to announce Himself coming, and My name is called the Word of God. **(Apoc: 19/13)**

At every descent with which I shepherd speaking from the cloud, I, the Lord, say who I am, why I come and what I do. Oh, the Father is sending Me and that is why I am coming, and the Father is with Me, for I am inseparable from Him, and that is why I said that I am in the Father, and the Father is in Me and We are One, oh, and so God makes Himself known by His name and by His work through His Son, Jesus Christ, the Word of the Father.

Oh, it is no longer time for My voice and word to be silent. The battle written in the Scriptures is being fought between earth and heaven, the beast with the Lamb, the manyheaded dragon and Christ, the Word of God, Who blows with the breath of His mouth to overthrow and completely destroy the power opposed to Him, to overcome the antichrist, the man of lawlessness, and all his servants hidden under the lie with which they keep covering themselves, so that their hostile work to God and man may not be revealed to them, but no, for the victorious rod is the word of My mouth, **(Isaiah: 11/4)** the breath under which I have kept this old enemy for over seven thousand years, and with which breath I am preparing the glory of My coming, My glory from the end of time, as it is written.

My voice is no longer silent, and I stand on clouds of words, and the word covers My divine being and conceals Me, so that I can speak and leave on earth among men the spirit of testimony, My Spirit, My work as Shepherd from the Father, coming with the renewal of all things, with the creation of all things again, **(Apoc: 21/5)** for all things have been corrupted, and they need a new creation to show itself after the enemy antichrist will no longer have his work, for I, the Lord, therefore, am coming and speaking and breathing word upon this red dragon, which sucks blood, as it is written. **(Apoc. 19/19-21.)**

Oh, My country today, oh, Romanian nation, land chosen and blessed by the heavenly Father for My coming at the end of time, oh, My sweet garden of mystery, behold, I cannot leave and not speak to you again and again, for in your land satan keeps intensifying his fight against Me, against Christ, Who defeated him and shamed him and wrote him the name of enemy two thousand years ago through My victory on the cross, and through which he was discovered and judged as an enemy lurking in darkness, and whom the light of My resurrection exposed and gave him the name of antichrist.

Oh, My country, I keep you under My word, under My blessing, to protect you. The fight for your protection from God is hard, because the red dragon sees himself deceived by everything he has worked for a long time and is still trying to guard, to hide his heads, so that they will not be cut off by the sword of My word. I, the Lord, keep him under this threat and that is why I always keep My guard and the river of My word over you and for you, so that this enemy may see and hear My word, with which I wage war against him.

Oh, Romanian people, rise up to see what struggle the powers of darkness are giving so that you may not escape the threat of the antichrist man, and who here he is wielding his forces through all kinds of servants, hidden under the guise of good, and over whom My light comes and keeps coming and brings them out with their hostile and in no way saving work. Oh, sit down to examine Me and understand My word with which I shepherd you from the cloud with a tender and gentle voice, for you are My love from the end of time, as you were at the beginning of creation, when I was so happy to make heaven and earth and then to stand on your earth and to work from it the body and being of the first man created, and who then crushed My joy by letting himself be lured by a foreign voice and abandoning the love for his Maker.

Behold, time has taken its course and a new beginning prophesied by the Scriptures is written, and it is coming and settling as a new creation of the world, oh, and with you the Lord is beginning this renewal and you will appear over the nations with your anointing from God and again you will be, and again you are the first and the Lord will illuminate with you far and wide and you will be announced over the earth and over the nations with the work of My word, with which creation is settled again, the new heaven, the new earth and the new Jerusalem, as it is written, **(Zacarias / Zechariah: 2/10-13.)** and you are the land of brightness, the land of the word of God, Which illuminates the whole world soon, soon, oh, and the comfort of God over the earth will be announced from place to place, My comfort over the nations who will find out and know what comfort is and its coming from heaven to earth and its heirs afterwards, for many will take the Lord from this river of word and they will share with each other the new creation, and the Holy Spirit will fulfill and sanctify this mysterious work upon the earth.

And now, I am leaving upon you the seal of My mouth: Peace to you, peace to you, Romanian sons! Open yourselves to My voice! We have to overcome the antichrist together in the battle, for here, in the land of brightness, he is judged by his own evil work, which is called the outermost darkness, and with which he has been struggling to encompass the world under him. But now he is meeting Me on your land, oh, My country, and here he is being judged and shamed, and there is only a moment left and that is why I, the Lord your God, am clothing you in My word as in a holy garment and leaving upon you a holy word: Peace to you! Do you hear My voice? I am shepherding you with great love, My country, and therefore, peace to you, peace from the Lord your God! Amen.

I am leaving My word over the citadel to establish here, now, the work for the preparation of the feast of Pentecost, with which I will stop accompanied by My heaven of saints, just as today I have at the table of words the fathers of the Christian people of all times, those who work in My vineyard after My ascension to the Father, and who have great names of holy fathers, through whom I, the Lord, have shepherded and am shepherding My Orthodox flock for two thousand years and I have preserved My spirit of the church in a bridal state, so that I may now choose My workers who will stand watch for My word to come on earth and for My word and its spirit of resurrection to be shared with those who will believe and be baptized in this word with a great name: the word of the Father and of the Son and of the Holy Spirit. Amen.

And behold, may the Holy Spirit be on My part the guide, the worker and the fulfiller of the garden at Maluri village, and of My glory and beauty with which I am going to stand before those who will gather for the great day and the famous Romanian celebration across the earth soon, soon.

May the Holy Spirit be able to work. Amen.

May the Father, the Son and the Holy Spirit bless the hasty progress of the work at Maluri village, *(Maluri village, the village and the house where Saint Virginia, the sixth apocalyptic trumpet, was born and lived, r.n.)* which still remains to be done, and may the spirit of obedience and diligence be at great work, so that God may be able to establish and proclaim the expected day, the day of great celebration of My sealing and glory over this garden of paradise, and on that day the heavenly hosts will stay awake and work with Me all the movement, all the work, all the heavenly joy, and everything will be shared with the people who will gather and who will have fellowship with Me and with My heaven on earth in the celebration, and the whole air of the village of Maluri, will carry the angelic hosts in it and will be host to the heavenly hosts and all the saints, and I, the Lord, with the word of My mouth will comfort, I will be a Comforter, I will be a Shepherd.

May it be blessed now the work of preparing for the feast of the Holy Spirit in the citadel of My word, in which the spirit of obedience may always work! May there be much vigilance for the full and complete fulfillment of My word, so that it may show its power. Amen.

And you, the sons who are watchmen over the citadel, watch on My behalf for the fulfillment of My word. I bow to you for your bowing when you receive Me to come with a table of words in the citadel and when you share Me, sons. Oh, peace to you, for we have accomplishments to accomplish! Peace, sons! Peace and prayers for the country, for the fight with the red dragon is in full swing and God must be able to work! The prayer full of the spirit is a seven-edged weapon and accomplishes heavenly victory over the power of the enemy darkness. Amen.

And now, let the increase and the work multiply with great swing and with another leap with much and great dedication and union, so that we may place before the Father the sealing of the little garden at Maluri village, My longing for now, oh, that I miss this day, I miss it very much, and the longing for it carries Me, oh, sons. Amen, amen, amen.
