The heavens have been open **(Apoc: 19/11)** for seventy years above the land of the Romanian nation, so that the word of God may come to earth, so that Christ may come against the false christs, so that the Lord may come with His victory and with tens of thousands of His saints, to come with those from heaven in armies dressed in fine linen, clean and white, all on white horses like their leader, like Me, the faithful and true One, **(1Thess: 3/13; Jude: 1/14; Apoc: 19/14.)** and with many royal crowns on My head, as it is written, and from My mouth comes My word like a sharp sword **(See Isaiah: 11/4 and 2 Thess: 2/8)** with which to strike, to awaken and to shepherd the nations of the earth and to rule them with this rod of iron, and I have a name written on the pure linen garment and on My thighs: King of kings and Lord of lords, oh, and it is a sight of heavenly glory My coming with the countless armies of saints, as it is written to come and judge with satan, with the beast, with the seven-headed dragon, with the false prophet and with all these servants of the antichrist, My adversary for two thousand years, since he found Me and awaited My coming to overcome Me and be alone afterwards, but here I am now catching him here to bind him in a bellows and throw him into fire and brimstone, as it is written to do to him and his servants from all his times upon Christ, and My wonderful name is the Word of God. **(Apoc: 19/13)**

It is the feast of My coming word on earth, it is a great feast this table of word with which I have sat on the Romanian land for seventy years, and on this day of the first Sunday after Pentecost, I am writing Myself in the book of My word, I am writing Myself with the saints, with the Sunday of all saints celebrated by the church according to the calendar on this day and here I am ruling the nations of the earth with this iron rod, which is called the Word of God, and I am telling them that a voice is heard in heaven coming from the earth and saying: *“Come, Lord!”*, and at this long cry, here I am coming and finding the thief everywhere, so that they can keep piling up and keep stealing and that is why those who are thieves keep looking and keep giving positions, stealing and sucking the blood of the oppressed and weakening the people, so that they can have thieves for their parties all the time, and so that they can buy power over the weak, as did those in the time of My body, who sold Me to the executioners then to the cross, and they paid the soldiers on guard with cursed money and bought them to lie and said that the disciples stole Me and that I did not rise and so that they could keep themselves in high positions, oh, but it did not work out for them much, because the wrath came for each one for the deeds, and I, the Lord, am alive forever and ever and behold, I am the word over the earth now, at the end of time, and ruling the world with this rod, oh, and those on earth do not know this secret, the blind do not know; however, a day is coming when they will want to give like Zacchaeus not only half, but all their wealth taken by theft, from the heap of the country gathered, from the poor below, oh, but they will then have nothing to do with it, because they will not use it for sins and vain parties anymore, oh, and what will you, the thieves, do, after how long have you been far from heaven, from God, for a life of sin, stealing from those who are dear to you, so that you can live and buy your seats of power from time to time?

Behold, the Lord is coming to earth as word, he is coming with the saints in armies in tens of thousands, oh, and I have nowhere to find to bow My head, because not even the servants at the holy altar are fit to receive Me, to open to Me, because I have lived poor, tearful, driven out and crucified by those who opposed Me at that time, and even today I have no brothers among them like Me in behavior, in mercy, in love, even though I am a great and powerful God in heaven and on earth, and they do not receive Me, they do not want to lose their primacy and power over the Christian flock between earth and heaven; however, I come and keep coming and I give to make My flock, oh, because I no longer have and I must make Myself and be its Shepherd and go out with it to the pasture and show Myself with the accomplished work before the Father, as it is written, and then to sound the wedding gong and set the table for the saints and the hosts of heaven and that the Scripture of a new heaven, a new earth and a new Jerusalem may be fulfilled, **(Apoc: 21/1-3)** and if it is written thus, it must be fulfilled, and therefore, I am coming.

[/legatura_la_teme][legatura_la_teme id_capitol="2" id_subcapitol="4"]This coming of Mine is settling and working on the Romanian land, uniting heaven with earth here in the land of My coming, word on earth, and this land is dear to Me and it is My dear, My beautiful, guest house for those who have come from heaven through My coming with the saints, and I am writing Myself on this day on earth here, on the Sunday of all saints, with the Sunday of this feast of the year 1923, the day when Virginia was born among men, the trumpet from which I, the Lord, had sounded twenty-five years over the earth and placed My word of My coming and it was written in her book.

Then Virginia grew under My heavenly care over her and I then prepared her with those in heaven, in order to make her the trumpet of God, to bring My plan of coming and all its speech and fulfillment to light through her. I had linked all three times of My word, beginning through her in the year 1955 and until the year 1980, when I descended with those in heaven and took her among the saints. Then, I raised the little sister from a root with her and I further tied the thread of My word, until I was to stand on the clouds above, to release My word further, to be placed in My book by those who have been watching over My coming to them, the word of My mouth and its sending over the earth, oh, and the seventy-year celebration of My word over the Romanian land is coming, and therefore, I am preparing sweet sights for the eyes of all who will come closer to see and know the place, where I spoke on earth, in 1955, My first word through My trumpet, then throughout the twenty-five years that she had been standing under the fire of My coming on earth as word, when her suffering was great and her tears were many, because many people had come and passed and much suffering have always been left behind, from then until today, for so it is with God, it is with the cross like the Shepherd Who shepherds the those under the cross of His coming now to rule all nations with His word as it is written.

The Lord is coming and working in your midst the work of His coming on earth, Romanian people. Oh, seek to meet Me at this well. I have much to tell you and to bear, for this time has come and its fruits have ripened, the heavens are working on your land, and all those in heaven are working on your hearth, and I, the Lord, sit on the throne and am saying: I am making all things new and writing My word in its book today, because these words are trustworthy and true, and I am still saying: I am the Alpha and the Omega, the beginning and the end, and to him who is thirsty I will give to drink freely from this fountain of life **(John: 4/14)** and I will be his God, and he will be My son, as it is written for this time of My coming word on earth to build a bride people for Me, **(Apoc: 22/1-3)** to come to them to shepherd them and to give them work to share this word for Me on earth, so that its fulfillment may come true.

I am carrying you in My arms, My country, so that I may protect you, to dress you beautifully, with ornaments from above before the peoples of the earth. Peace, I am granting you now and always! I am embracing you from all sides, from the earth and from heaven I am embracing you. There is something else farther from you, and you are the miracle of the Father, the Son and the Holy Spirit, from the beginning to the end, and I am writing on your land My word, and I am the Alpha and the Omega on your land, and here I am catching the man antichrist and judging with him, as it is written in the Scriptures to fulfill My victory over him, over the seven-headed dragon and the false christ, the one who baptized the whole world with his enchanting poison to take it away from God, only that I am coming as word on earth and drawing to the Father those who hear, come and mark over their lives My name today: the Word of God, the staff with which I shepherd and announce the time of great miracles and the land of light, on you, My Romanian land, oh, and there will not be a corner on earth that does not know that God is coming to you, speaking over the land and calling to the life of heaven those who are crushed near also by the roller of satan; however, I, the Lord, am preparing a place where there is no pain or sadness or tears, but only God is and only those who love Him, only Him and His from place to place on earth, and satan will be thrown into the fire that burns with brimstone as it is written.

Peace to you, My Romanian country, My house from the end of time, the bed from which I, the Lord, come out over the earth and over the peoples to say to them far and wide: Peace to you, the divided peoples! What do you think among yourselves? Which of you wants to be greater, more powerful? Oh, it is written about the greatest to serve all and to share his good watch. So, put your swords back in their sheaths! Do not forget the word of Christ: *«He who draws the sword will perish by the sword»*, so I said. **(Matthew: 26/52)**

[/legatura_la_teme][legatura_la_teme id_capitol="3" id_subcapitol="5"]Oh, peace to you, peoples and leaders over peoples! People cannot be great. Come to your senses to understand your measure and stop dreaming of greatness, for sacrifices are needed, only sacrifices of brothers for brothers. No one can be great among people except the one whose greatness comes from God, and you have taken and given each other greatness and have worked with the help and power of mammon and that is why you are its sons, servants and masters of money, and you think that money is the power over all, and that is why you have learned a lot through this power. Oh, no! Woe to you! Do not deceive yourselves, and wake up while time permits.

So, take heavenly exhortation, take it from God, that I left on earth the parable of Zacchaeus the tax collector who said upon his meeting with Me: *«Half of the wealth that I have collected, I give to the poor, Lord, and if I have wronged anyone while I have collected, I return fourfold».* **(Luke: 19/8)** Oh, what do you mean fourfold? It means everything. So, you are not in need either, My Romanian country, you are not if those who stole from you would put back into the country's barn what was stolen unjustly. Do you hear?

Listen now to what I am telling you and then believe: Oh, you do not need wealth. You gather one bread, one clothing and one shelter from bad weather through work, so that you may be righteous and not be sinners through the sins that come from the spirit of greed and pride.

The reward for the deeds of life is coming. Make peace with God and with yourselves, for this is the only wisdom of the wise, and the rest is vanity and chasing after the wind as it is written. Amen.

Oh, here is how much God loves! Flee from wealth! God teaches you to be like Him, and not greater than Him. Ask the poor to reconcile you with God and with eternity where there are no pain, sadness or sighs with tears, for the Lord is coming to earth with tens of thousands of saints, so that all those on earth may learn about heaven and its wealth.

Free yourselves from the bonds with which you have bound yourselves, earthlings! Take your heavenly citizenship on earth! Make room with you for Christ and the heavenly guests, who walk with Him in heaven and on earth and write through angels the deeds of men, the deeds are chosen on the right and on the left for the day of judgment of deeds, as it is written.

Oh, vanity of vanities, enough! Eternity is coming, and He Who sits on the throne is saying: *«I am making all things new»,* **(Apoc: 21/5)** and let this word be written by the angels, and let all who are chosen for Christ have their names written in the Lamb's book of life, now and forever.

[/legatura_la_teme][legatura_la_teme id_capitol="5" id_subcapitol="6"]Oh, I would no longer want, I would no longer want to end My speech, and I am blessing, again and again, the work of preparing the garden of paradise at Maluri village, the first place where I had descended seventy years ago with My body and word, and which I am preparing now to show Myself with it and with the blessing of this time over those who will gather around My word here, for on that day I will show the view and the story of My word on the land of the Romanian nation and it will be a sweet day of holy celebration at Maluri, *(Maluri = Margins, r.n.)* in the village from which the new branch, My trumpet Virginia, had sprung, oh, that the time had come for Me to come, as I needed this path to stay with My story and hers in the sweet celebration at Maluri, where it will be a day of heavenly and Romanian celebration and much comfort will be everywhere, for it will be divided and will comfort it.

Peace, sons of the citadel, peace to you! Hurry, make haste still, so that the feast may come, sons! Hurry the work that has still remained. I take care of you all the time. So, may you also take care of Me, and those in heaven, who are waiting overwhelmed with longing, sons.[/legatura_la_teme]

Peace and peace again over all the earth, peace from heaven over the earth!

Peace and much more peace over the Romanian land, peace from heaven over it!

And peace over the citadel of My word and over you, those who receive the Lord with His coming to you, oh, sons! Amen, amen, amen.
