[/legatura_la_teme][legatura_la_teme id_capitol="8" id_subcapitol="3"]The earthly angel, John the Baptist, he is the one who stood at the border between heaven and earth and heaven and earth were united at that time and that is why it is written in the Scriptures that I am Alpha and Omega, the beginning and the end. And if in the beginning I was the word of the Father, through Whom all things were made, behold I am the word at the end too, and that is why I said through the Spirit that I am Alpha and Omega, and you, the people of My word, place Me as the word before the people on earth and you are My forerunner like John, the Baptist, and I unite with you the beginning with the end, the end with the beginning, and all are renewed, all are born again, all, when no one expects God to come, and I, the Lord, work in secret, and all are settled, and then they will appear in the light and will shine through the darkness and this light will not be overcome, for it will overcome as it is written.[/legatura_la_teme]

We are setting a table of words for the Epiphany, My children. I am with you at the table in the citadel and I am speaking with John, the Baptist, as at the Jordan, after he was tempted by the Pharisees who asked him: *«Why do you baptize if you are not the Christ, nor Elijah, nor the Prophet?»*, **(John: 1/25)**, and he answered them: *«I baptize with water, but among you stands the One Who comes after me and Who is before me, for He was before me»*, **(John: 1/15)**, and then I came to him and he baptized Me in the Jordan and said in the sight and hearing of those gathered: *«Behold the Lamb of God!»***. (John: 1/29).**

— Oh, Lord Lamb, so I said then, and the beginning and the end were united on that day, oh, and only You and I understood this at that time, only we, from then until today, Lord, and now You are confessed by the little ones to whom You come and speak, so that Your word may be written and the beginning and the end may be united, for Your word is what does this, Lord.

And now, I am bowing down and humbly asking You, let Yourself be in the people with Your Spirit, so that they may understand what this time is and Your word coming to earth!

Oh, it is not possible for people to understand You, because they are so small, poor things, but with You all things are possible, and make people understand You, feel Your breath, so that they can receive God and understand more and more that You are the Life they encounter, and that satan will be cast aside, so that You may make Yourself known, and so that the world may know that satan has covered You before them, so that people will not follow You and follow him; however, his time is ending because You are coming.

Oh, come, Lord, word on earth, come always, for heaven is still waiting for You to remove the dividing wall, even if humanity is not ready to see this time and understand the light of Your word, which can, Lord.

We speak to one another again on earth, for all heaven is full of longing, only longing, Lord Lamb. You are the light of the world. This is Your work and coming today, and we are awaiting the year pleasing to the Lord, oh, Lord Lamb.

[legatura_la_teme id_capitol="8" id_subcapitol="3"]— Oh, My Baptist, the wait is oppressive for those above. There is one more hour. You were working before Me to prepare My way and God was able through your love and sacrifice. You were a model of repentance and purity, of fasting and prayer, and so you were working for Me so that I could come to show Myself, and to testify then, oh, and the Spirit came, and the Father came and these were written and they proclaimed God. Now, I am coming with the power of the word Myself, and My word is working as in the beginning when heaven and earth and man and the world were made.

Oh, there is still one hour, and all things will be renewed, as it is written, and therefore you, the sons of the citadel of My word, stand strong by My power, so that we may prepare and proclaim the acceptable year of the Lord, as it is written, and therefore, I am renewing a great blessing upon you for the preparation and adornment of the garden at the Margins *(Maluri Village, the village and the house where Saint Virginia was born and lived, r.n.)*, and with which we will stand before the Father with the beginning of the work of My word and with all this testimony and we will arrive with it until the day, sons. Therefore, the heavenly powers are standing by you, so that we may be ready with the work at the Margins, and with all the preparation of that great day, which will be announced, far and wide, that it is coming, for on that day a great multitude will be gathered and this day will be testified to the ends of the earth, and step by step, My light will overcome the thick darkness, and many will be whitened and enlightened, as it is written, and the wicked will remain the same and will pass to the left, and only the light will go forward, only God, He, and those faithful to Him. Amen.[/legatura_la_teme]

Oh, come with new strength, sons, come, for I will not leave you anymore! I leave the heavens open above you, so that those in heaven may work with you, for you are few under My cross today, but God can do, sons, you too will be able to work, for I will not leave you anymore.

Peace to you! Keep Me near you all the time, for you are little, and you will be those through whom God can work, as I, the Lord, could work through John, My Baptist, only to be My obedient children, and that is why I am not leaving, I remain with you, I remain with you, oh, children. Amen, amen, amen.
