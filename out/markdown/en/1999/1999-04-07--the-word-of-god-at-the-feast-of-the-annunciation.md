I am God, the Father, the Son and the Holy Spirit, speaking from heaven on earth. Amen. I come from heaven speaking, and I have come on the earth. I announce Myself well with a feast of annunciation over My church. I announce Myself well that I come from heaven, and that I have come on the earth to reveal My coming, as there is no one to prepare it for Me, and this preparation for My coming has to be. The man prepares it for Me as on earth, eating, drinking and going to weddings as on earth, but I need to have a preparation before My coming as in heaven on earth, for this is what I said: *«As in heaven, so on earth»*. Amen.

Peace to you, sons from the garden, and peace from you to Israel, My people of today, the people that fulfill My coming, My earning among the people. Peace to you, for you are those who announce My coming well, as Gabriel, My archangel gave the news that I was about to come a baby through the Virgin two thousand years ago. I submitted to the Law in Israel at that time and I could not give My gifts to those under the law, for they killed Me after the law. But I am coming now with gifts, and Michael, the archangel, My angel and yours, is imparting heavenly gifts on you from Me.

The law of the body is one, and the law of the gift is another. I enshrouded you into the mystery of My heavenly gifts and I have sent Michael, My angel, before Me, who works out My word, to testify to you about those that are to come after the annunciation of My coming, and My coming will be, for it is written: *«I am the First and the Last».* Amen, amen, amen. Gabriel, the archangel, served to the fulfillment of the law and announced My coming in the flesh. Michael, the archangel from the beginning, serves to the fulfillment of the gifts in the end, for it is My angel and yours, the angel of My word.

I taught My disciples to say that My will be done in heaven as on earth; I taught them to ask from Father and the Father’s will to be done on earth as in heaven, but the man taught himself only to say and that is all. The man says what I taught him to say to My Father, and then he goes and does his own will on the earth, and the Father’s will remains in heaven. Day and night the man does his own will saying: *«Your will be done, Father, as in haven, so on earth».* Behold, there are not true worshippers on earth, for every man says one thing and does another, violating the law from heaven and the one on earth. How is he not supposed to violate the heavenly law, if he also violates the one on the earth, which he, the man, made it to be and to be observed? The confusion of languages is the reward of the man’s false life, and the man is a murderer of every good thing. Nothing that is enduring remains behind man, nothing but a human name and pride from earth to heaven. But I, the Lord, tell you and those who are faithful to Me and to you, and I let you know about a great joy, that I come soon, soon. This is what I announce you: My coming from heaven on earth, to make a new heaven and a new earth on the earth, so that the word that you declared may be fulfilled; that is, the Father’s will to be done on earth as in heaven.

Preach well too! Be the angels of the Annunciation for the end of the man’s time. Be the people which bears good news; be the work of My angels who works through your bodies, who makes My gift come down upon you, so that you may be the angel of the Annunciation, of My last coming. I go with you to the people, and I do not go without you. If I speak from heaven with you, this means that I go with you to the people. And a sweet time will come, a heavenly time on earth for you and with you, for it will love you and it will seek every man who will still be on the earth, for I will glorify Myself with a great glory through you upon Romania, upon My last country. I come through it for the people of Israel, which killed Me in its midst. [legatura_la_teme id_capitol="1" id_subcapitol="5"]I come through Romania and for the Jerusalem of My passions, for I will gather together many haughty people, people who trespass the commandments and I will judge Myself with them there; there where I came among them in the flesh two thousand years ago. I will declare the word of My pain and justice here, and there I will fulfill it, and I will judge Myself with the people for which I willingly gave Myself to be crucified.

I had to die for the rest of the peoples on the earth to redeem them from under the ignorance, but it was not so, and I died for Israel, to redeem it from under the law, for it is written: *«He who does not fulfill the law is guilty»*. He knew the law from Me and was guilty because he did not believe in Me when I came from heaven from the Father to be in its midst. Behold, I tell you a revealed mystery: the people of Israel knew that I am the Christ of the Father and that I had to be crucified for its sins. It was not Judas who was guilty for selling Me and My death. He was the tool by which Israel killed its God, and it is written: *«Woe to the one by whom the Son of God will be sold and killed!»*. If Israel did not seek to kill Me, it would have not killed Me when Judas sold My mystery, for Judas knew that I am the Christ. The rulers of Israel were guilty of My death, and with them all the people of Israel, all, except a remnant chosen by grace to be without guilt so that afterwards to be able to testify about the truth of My coming from heaven in the midst of Israel.[/legatura_la_teme]

In all time I have had people on earth, a remnant chosen by grace, so that My truth may have been witnessed in each time, as I have you in this time, sons near My coming. Blessed are you, who believe in My coming as word on the earth. Those of that time, who believed in My coming then, were also blessed. As then, those of today, who call themselves the rulers of the church, believe as those of that time; they believe that I am the Christ of the Father and that I have come to you to prepare you to be the sons of My coming, the good sons of this time, a remnant chosen by grace. But I overcame for you and against them, as the time of now and My coming of nowadays have been worked with power and much glory, as it is written in the Scriptures: *«They will see the Son of Man coming on the clouds of heaven with power and much glory, and He will send His angels with a loud trumpet call, My word, which gathers His elect from the four winds, from one end of the heavens to the other».* Amen.

I have come on earth and prepared you to be My little angels for the gospel of Me coming as Savior upon you, and Judge over those who crucified Me then and now through haughtiness. Those without love and without mind have no fear and are haughty in their spirit, but you should be the angel of annunciation for My last coming, and stand well in fear and careful to My words, for they are My coming with judgment over the earth.

Sons, the dead come to life at the voice of My word. Only those that are haughty do not want to come to life, but they will stand before Me whether they like it or not, and they will melt like wax, as I am the First and the Last, and I come with much power and with much greatness, as it is written into the Scriptures of My second coming from heaven on the earth, for the earth is My footstool. Amen. The man says in vain that he took My earth from under My feet. Soon, soon, every man will see that he is weak and will receive Me whether he wants it or not, because I come and I am clothed in the garment of justice. Amen. [legatura_la_teme id_capitol="6" id_subcapitol="3"]But you, sons of My greatness, I have always taught you to keep away from My judgment, doing My will before Me. Do many merciful deeds, as much as you could, as mercy is a candle with oil, a burning candle. Give Me over as a gift to every man who reaches out his hand for heavenly food. Also give Me to the one who is ashamed with Me and who does not reach out his hand but only stealthily. Give God as mercy to all who do not want the heavenly food, as I, the Lord, give them the bitter bread of anxiety to those who are careless of the soul. I make them all anxious to the hearing of My word, and a fire will be kindled all around and it will threaten the earth with fire and judgment, but My country with My manger in it will be the throne of My word, out of which a word of consuming and cleansing fire will flow over the man and over the earth.

Give God as mercy, messenger sons of My coming. I am the Word of God for the coming of Christ, the Son of the Father, and I share Myself through you as My coming upon every man.[/legatura_la_teme] I call out the trumpet. The word is the trumpet, and Michael, My angel, is the angel of My gift upon you. Let My word come to you from one margin of the heaven to another and let the iniquity on the earth burn, as I make a new haven and a new earth, and a new and just man between heaven and earth, and the haughtiness will fall into the abyss. Amen.

I come with the good and eternal time for you; I come with a glorious time upon you, sons, and emperors and kings will follow you, as My great power and high glory will be with you and on you, and those, who did not love you because of Me, will lament, and the rich people, who did not do merciful deeds for God, will lament, as My coming had called out to them, but they had hidden themselves under their richness and left the right way. Behold, it is written: *«They will build houses and will not dwell in them; they will buy fallow lands and will not have them; they will plant vineyards and will not eat of their fruit».*

Oh, sons, wake up the spirit of prayer in you with great power and greatness, true worship, and say: *«Father, Your will be done, as in heaven so on earth»*. Let your prayer be fulfilled by Father. Amen, amen, amen. And to the Father I say: I thank You Father that You listened to Me through the sons of My coming from heaven on earth. I came to do Your will in them and on them, as in heaven, so on earth. I made them My angel who announces My coming, lest I come unexpectedly or like a thief. But the man sleeps; he sleeps deeply, Father; he sleeps in the time of My coming and the one who sleeps does not see. But Father, protect these who are My coming. Wake them up for My coming is with them; give them power, for they do not have to sleep in the time of My coming, but rather they need to be wakeful and with their candle full of burning oil for Me. Amen.

*— I am the heavenly Father, Son that came down from heaven on earth, and I am in You with power and much glory, for I am One with you, both in heaven and on earth. I, Myself, announce You that You are My beloved son, in Whom I am well pleased in heaven and on earth, from beginning to end, for I am in You, and I announce Myself in You and You in Me, God, the Father, the Son and the Holy Spirit. Amen, amen, amen.*

— And I, Father, thank you on earth and in heaven, in heaven and on earth, and I fulfill the word that I have from You to be fulfilled, for a new heaven and a new earth, and for the new age, for which We have been longing for seven thousand years, Father.

Oh, children of My coming, I am with the time from heaven over you, an endless age, and not with a time as on earth. Give this grace to those who want to become sons of the Father, for to those who believe in the word of My coming now, I give power to become sons of the Father, sons born of God, of the Word of God, as you are, for it is written: *«At My trumpet call, the dead will come to live»*, at My word, which is in you, calling the nations to a life of a new age and forever new. Amen, amen, amen.

***

And now, sons from the garden, I announce you that I enter into My garden with Verginica and with her little sister that I took in heaven a year ago. However, you should work out her remembrance before Me, and I, the Lord, meet you with the one that you welcomed.

Verginica, come into the book! I am your linking bridge to My people and yours. You are great in heaven, as My work through you was great, My last work. We go to the memorial service into the garden. Take your little sister to see her feast on the earth, for she was My linking bridge between you and the working people of today, so that I may speak today from above, and so that the people may have Me as word for food and for life and for the resurrection of the dead at the call of My trumpeting. The angels write a new step, the ninth pass for your little sister, the work accomplished in heaven, a heavenly citizen with full favor before Me. I announce you and your little sister in the garden. Announce it as well and give the people the voice of your word. I in you and you in Me with a feast of annunciation over today’s Israel, over the Lord’s people, the one taken out of the Romanians. Amen.

*— Lord, Your name and Your word is glory and power over the last ones who have from heaven the gospel of Your coming with the saints, the day of the Lord, to be imparted. You are the First and the Last. You are the Father, the Son and the Holy Spirit, the consuming fire, who had been scorching me for as long as twenty five years into the middle of a people, with which You, Yourself, spoke from heaven in my time, the last time. I was the sacrifice place for fire. Your Word was the sacrifice on it. You had fed the new Israel on fire. I was one body with You. I with my body, and You with the Spirit of Your word, and after twenty-five years, You took my body out of flames and gave the heaven to me. With great care for the people left without the food of Your word, I prayed to You to cleanse my little sister, and to ask her in marriage for me, and I was to make your passing on to the people, Lord. You fulfilled my prayer for my people and prepared My bridge. I set my heavenly work on my little sister, and I worked from heaven on her, and entrusted to my people that she was my word through her. I made great signs, testifying for me in her, and then I also asked You, Lord, to pass with me in her, and then to make Your passing between me and my people, so that the work of Your word may not die, the work started through me in the year of 1955. You worked wonderfully, Lord. You gave many days to my little sister and made Your passing to the new people that You feed today with the last mysteries, with the fulfillments that were announced even in the time of my body, to be accomplished on the earth.*

*May You be blessed for the gift of the holy faith that you put over the last ones, over those in Your garden in the end, over those who give the news of Your word to the people on the earth!* [legatura_la_teme id_capitol="5" id_subcapitol="1"]*It is great this little patch of land that you worked and work over with the heaven in the beginning and in the end, the place where the heaven and the earth open up, a mystery of new heaven and new earth, the core of the fulfillments of Your prophecies made out seven thousand years to come true now, and which You have been proclaiming through all of Your prophets,*[/legatura_la_teme] *and in the end through Me on the earth, for I was Your sacrifice place of fire on the earth.*

*I had nourished Your people with faith and hope, Lord, for You can work whatever You want with the faithful one. I gave my sister to the people as a passing bridge to Your word. One year has passed by since I came on Your name and I took her into heaven and led her with the angels through the twenty four straits between heaven and earth,* (The twenty four customs of the air, aerial toll-houses, r.n.)*, between man and God, after the soul leaves the dwelling of its body. She passed easily from place to place to the throne of Your glory, to the ninth heaven, Lord, as our forefathers were speaking about, for today one does not speak of the ways of the heaven on the earth, for the man got up and taught that everything our forefathers had from You as knowledge was a lie. Behold, the humble spirit of my sister was the sacrifice brought by her to You, and You made for me and for You a passing bridge through her, so that Your word may go on and on as work over the earth. Now you stay in the air and work over the garden, for this is the time, and this is how You work for Your coming.*

*I come today with My little sister for her memorial in the garden.*

*Oh, little sister, here is the mystery of the heavens, and the throne of the Father and the Son and the Holy Spirit in heaven and on earth is hiding in it. This is the root of the work that was through me and then through you, a linking bridge to those who work today with the Lord. The throne of the Lord’s glory is the root of which this word came and is coming over the earth. Amen.*

*— Oh, Verginica, little sister, tell the people below, that I have the Lord’s glory before me, and that I want where I am they also may be. Love is heaven, Verginica, my little sister.*

*I tell you too, children of the Lord on the earth, that love is heaven, the heaven in man, and the heavenly man on earth and in heaven. The Lord’s glory is under the eyes of my spirit. I look under it and see the little garden below, and I see you. From it on to the sides is otherwise; it is as on earth. Pray to the Lord so that where I am to be all who believe into the word, which is with you from here, from above the garden at your place. The river of the Lord’s glory waters from above, and the Lord’s glory grows under it. The seal of the living God shines on your foreheads. The hand of the Lord is on your head and draws the curtain aside, which hides the Lord’s seal on your foreheads. Soon, soon, the mark on you will be able to be read. Glory in the highest to God and may the peace of the Lord’s glory be on you! Amen, amen, amen.*

*— Oh, little sister, I brought you with the Lord and with me and with the Lord’s glory to the feast of your memorial in the garden. The love is heaven, but it is otherwise on the earth. Only those from heaven see what love is, and they testify it as it is.*

*Lord, the humble spirit of my little sister was the sacrifice that she brought to You, but You made her a passing bridge for You and for me to the small and new people, Lord. Amen.*

— Verginica, you are great in heaven. Soon, soon, your heavenly greatness will be known on the earth in a visible glory. Soon, soon, I will glorify Myself for you and for Our children of today, who work for the fulfillment of the glory of My word over the earth. Soon, soon, you will be My testimony vessel into My hand, My cup, the Lord’s cup, pure wine, the new wine, a cup that I poured out the new wine into. Your glory will be like Mine, for I will come with you when I come, for this work means My coming, and I set you as the foundation for My coming, for the building of the people of My coming. Kings and emperors will look and see the glory of My word, the river of the everlasting life.

And you, children from the garden, be the angel of the Annunciation over the earth. Announce My coming, for after this annunciation My glory is coming, the morning shining star. You are My witnesses, My testimony before the people. Keep what you have from Me until I come, for it is written into the Scriptures: *«To the one who overcomes and keeps My works to the end, to him I will give authority over the nations, and he will rule them with an iron scepter, shattering them like clay pots»*, as I also received power from My Father, and I will give him the morning star. Amen, amen, amen.
