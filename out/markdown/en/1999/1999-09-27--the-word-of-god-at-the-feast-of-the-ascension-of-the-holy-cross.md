You sons that bring Me on earth as word so that I may prepare My coming with the word, listen and write My word into My book of today. Amen.

In the name of the Father and of the Son and of the Holy Spirit, I embrace you with My hands, pierced by the nails of crucifixion, for I sealed into My palms all those for whom I died on the cross, as I spoke before through the prophet Isaiah, saying to Zion: *«Behold, I marked you down on My palms and I will not forget you, and you will be forever before Me, and your sons will be your ornament garment, and you will gird on with them like a bride».* Amen, amen, amen.

Sons, I have always come as word to you, and you should give Me to all those who seek to find Me and see My way with you, My coming down from heaven on earth as word on you. In the name of the Father and of the Son and of the Holy Spirit, worship God all the time and pray stirring up the spirit and increasing the time of praying, for My coming is near, and it will be to come visibly and to find watching for My coming. Amen.

Oh, sons, I share My pain with you, and here is what I tell you: the man that receives My word from you does not set down; he does not set down to turn it into deed and love that are accomplished on him. Many a people take and receive this word and rejoice over it and are comforted by it and feed on it with hope, but they do not make any living work out of it and the man, who receives My word from you, does not keep a good watch, even if he receives it with longing. However, I remain weak in them, for they still mind their own affairs and do not give Me any room with life in them, and this is how I will find them when I come to search the Zion out.

I told you to set My word before the people to help them come to repentance and humility and to a holy life for My coming. However, the man has his own life, and I have Mine. Why does the man not really receive what I gave him? Why does he not receive? I gave My life for the man to take his life from My life, so that the man may also give his live for Me. I marked him into My palms to recognize Me as God on him, for I came from the Father in heaven to save the man from the payment of his sins, if the turns his eyes to the cross of My crucifixion. But the man was not moved. The man does not live My pain on the cross then when he makes the signs of My pains on his body, the sign of the cross, as I have it marked on My own body. Many crowds forsook the cross and do no longer put the sign of My crucifixion on their bodies, the sign by which I overcame the death, as a soldier prevails in war with his weapon against his enemies.

Oh, sons, sons, the more the time of My coming in flesh on earth has come near, the more the crowds have forsaken the sign of My cross, and since then the devil has completely gone to sleep, and he has no longer been afraid of any man but of the man that is true in his love and living and his watch for My coming. Behold, the devil is not afraid of any man, but the man who does the devil’s will is frightened to death of the Christian who loves Me doing My will in his spirit, soul and body.

Sons, all the heads of the people, all who have subjects and multitudes of subjects, are afraid of you more than they are afraid of Me, for you are on earth, and you are My strength from above the people’s heads, because those who are born of Me are above everybody else, for they are born of heaven and do those from above on the earth, and they make the way of My coming with the reward over every man. I embrace you into My arms reaching out My pierced hands for you. I have you in My hand as a weapon against My enemy, the devil. I pour Myself out into you abundantly so that you may be full, like the loaded weapon ready for fight into the hand of the fighting soldier. I made you into a working tool and I work with it over the earth and sow the ground with a seed of new age. I have turned you into a seed of new age, and the man on earth is afraid of you that you might take over his ground, but the earth is Mine, not man’s. If the man wants to rule the earth forever, I do no longer want it, because the earth is Mine and it is I that I made it out of waters, not the man. The man knows nothing but to stand up against Me and to put Me out of My own kingdom and out of My own vineyard. However, the Father is the Master and He comes with Me and takes away the vineyard from the hands of the robbers and gives it to those who love Me as the Son of the heavenly Father, as the Master of the vineyard, as the Father’s Heir. Amen. I share My pain with you and here is what I tell you, sons dedicated to God: the Father will come and lose these workers and He Himself will take care of His vineyard, for I am His Son, because the man has taken away My inheritance from Father. I will come from Father and lose all the people who have had time to hear the gospel of My kingdom, with which I come from My Father on earth, but did not want to believe. I come from Father with the entire heavenly glory and I will show Myself as a Master over the earth, and further I will destroy everything the man has done on earth; the man who has lived without God all his days, for everything the man has done on earth he had done only for himself, and he has taken My right to be.

Oh, from birth to death, the man does not want to perceive that I am the Master of the earth and not him or his descendants. I will break down all that the man made, for the man made it only for him. The man says that the world gathers together in them and the man does not know that a church means the sons of God together for a holy and humbled life before Me. The man erects walls and gives them to the world, not to Me, for I do not enjoy Myself with the lawless ones. The man erects walls and sets himself as a master upon the souls of the people, upon the lives of the people; and the priests establish themselves from their own reading; they put one another in positions, but they do not do My will. And I will come from the Father, and I have already come, to lose every man who had time to hear My cry which calls every man to the kingdom of heavens on the earth with God, but the people did not want to believe. Here, I come and I come with those that are written to come for the time before My coming with a heavenly glory and with the resurrection of the dead, as it is written in the Scriptures of My coming.

Sons, sons, what do the people do with the Scriptures of My coming? How do they read in them? How do they see in them? Oh, the people hide from them like vipers which see their threat, but My wrath for the unbelievers comes and comes again; it comes to confirm the Scriptures of My coming, for the heaven and the earth will not pass unless they are fulfilled word by word, as I spoke by the Scriptures. You, sons from the garden of My word upon the earth, call Me to come with those written for this time and do not be afraid that I come, for I make you victorious upon this time and I keep you in My arms with My hands thrust by the nails of crucifixion, and I have you in My hand like a weapon against My enemy, the devil.

The evil spirit made the people busy so that they may not make the sign of the cross on their body, the sign of My crucifixion that I overcame the old serpent by. Not even they, who were baptized Christianly into My name, make the sign of the cross on their body, and if they do, alas of this sign that pleases only the devil. The man, who still makes the sign of the cross on his body, makes his cross according to his own life. The man does not know any longer the place where he should put the fingers of his hands when making the cross. Not even the priest makes the sign of the cross as required by the Christians, and there is no one to teach the man the cross and its signs, and the sign on the man’s body. The man does no longer raise his cross to God, and rather he goes down into the deep, and I complain to you of My pain from the man. But you should embrace Me by the sign of the cross, by the sign of my body’s crucifixion, for when I made the man, I made him to be a cross, a holy cross, a holy body, a crucified body to the sin, a life put into God’s service. But you should embrace me when you make the sign of My cross on your body, the sign of the One Who embraced you by crucifixion, for blessed is the man who used My death on the cross, for this one is arisen, and is alive forever and ever.

Sons, the wounds of My crucifixion hurt Me, for the man was bad with Me and with those who followed Me. The rulers of the people were always afraid of those who followed Me and killed them, and put a bad name among the people because of My name. All who want to be rulers upon the people and upon the earth are very much afraid of those that serve Me with the spirit and the truth. However, the people of the earth are afraid of those who are a living church to Me, but no one is afraid of a worldly church, and neither the devil nor his angels are afraid. But you should embrace Me by the sign of the cross of My crucifixion upon your body, for I made you to be feared for the sons of the people who do not want Me to come. The people are afraid of you worse than of the death, but they should better love you and listen to My exhortation which is upon you. The people should better embrace you but they cannot do such a thing, for the empty glory seized them in the darkness, and they are afraid of their sins, for you are the Scripture of My coming and that is why I come to you, and that is why I am with you. I am with you for the fulfilling of the Scriptures of My coming. Amen, amen, amen.

Be careful, sons, and give before the people My word, for a great deception is growing on the earth for the Scriptures of My coming. Be cautious, for I told you that once with My coming I bring the resurrection of My righteous ones and an imperishable body upon those who wait for Me to come. Please Me, for I come as it is written in the Scriptures that I come, and I will protect with My godly arm those that celebrate for the feast of My coming with their spirit, soul and body. Be heavenly on the earth, sons. Amen, amen, amen. Be on the earth as in heaven, to make My will perfect in you. Be an upright cross among the people, for the true cross is the cross of the crucifixion, and other kind of cross is not cross. The one who is not crucified has not got a cross; he has nothing to present himself with before Me at My coming. You should celebrate the feast of My cross in your members and ascend by the crucifixion of the man who struggles in you for My life in him, so that I may have you as a seed of life without death, as I said that you should be. I said that My church will never be overcome and that is why I made out of you an invincible church, a holy Zion, as I said to Zion: *«Behold, I marked you down on My hands and you will be forever before Me».*

Sons, sons, be from heaven, be on the earth as in heaven, so that I may make My will perfect in you, and to make out of you My garment of adornment, and I gird Myself with you as a bridegroom to be My power at My coming, My sign before My coming, the victors of the old serpent, as I was. Amen, amen, amen.
