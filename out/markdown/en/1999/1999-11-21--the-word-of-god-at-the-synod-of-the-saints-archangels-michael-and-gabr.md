… The man who goes to church does no longer know what the real fasting and praying are, the man’s work with God; the man’s work against the devil and against the flesh, which lusts against the spirit. However, I have always taught you this so that man may receive instruction; again and again I teach you so that My advice for the Christian to be heard. Pray and fast, as I taught My old disciples. Pray as I have also taught you for My works to be perfected through you. Do not sit down to eat in the days when you eat, do not eat until 10 o’clock, for you are Christians and you need to live as those in heaven. Amen. The healing of the bodies is done by the love of God, that is by fasting, by praying, by humility and repentance, for the body asks from you as you teach it. Do not be afraid to fast. Be afraid of food, and eat with humility, for you are Christians. Amen. On the days that are written for fasting, fast as I have taught you to be healthy in your spirit, in your soul and in your body, and so that My things may be perfected over the earth, for you are Christians. Look into My teaching with which I have been staying for more than forty years in the midst of those who believe. Look into My word, for it is the word of love over those who choose the eternal life with Me, because it is not sin when you do not fast, but rather it is sin not to fulfill God’s word in which many other things are comprised besides fasting. However, some say: *“What am I fasting for?”* Behold why one should fast: one fasts for health, for goodness, for humility, for the love of God and for the perfection of God’s things, which come to appear by fasting, by the preparation of the soul and body, because the body is for the Lord, as it is written into the Scriptures. When man loves his own body, he cannot love God, for man cannot serve two masters at the same time. This is why I said: *«No one can serve two masters, for either he will hate the one and love the other; or else he will be devoted to one and despise the other».* **(Matt: 6/24)** Man was not made for Sabbath; rather Sabbath was made for man. Man was not made for food or for fasting, but food and fasting were made for man. And let him who hears understand, too. Amen.

… Behold, I am blessing those who have listened at the right time to My word, preparing of new age, of new body, of new spirit, for those who will be are those who take from Me in them the new law in paradise which I put over man; the law of eternity, sons, for I have made everything well, but man has spoiled them against his life with Me. However, I have come again to set all things back to their place, and if the Gospel of My coming is still covered, it is covered for the sons of perdition, in which the god of this age have blinded the minds of the unbelievers so that it may not shine the light of the Gospel of Christ, the One Who is God’s image. However, you submit fully to the word of life, for I am with you and I teach you, and I teach many through you, only for man to live, only for man to love God. Behold, I give you wisdom from heaven so that those who want may take life and I will look and see that if they have taken it, if they have fully submitted, for whoever submits fully, has with him the work of the angels for the eternal life. He who fully submits is the one who is humble before Me, and the humble one is the one who fully submits. The archangel Michael was fully submitted and did not come out of God’s fear and he obeyed God only. The same is to you; have fear and be careful to My entire word for it is God’s word. Amen, amen, amen. [legatura_la_teme id_capitol="6" id_subcapitol="3"]Welcome one another with My word that prepares the new age, but also love one another so that you may receive one another. Work among you as among angels, for I have given you an angelic life to bring before Me. Let all those who want to be with you know this measure and this is how they have to come to you and take from you, for you do not need anything else than to give angelic life upon man by the teaching with which I cover you. This is how My word is written in the Scriptures: *«Those who are worthy to receive that age and the resurrection from the dead do no longer die for they are like angels and are God’s sons, being sons of resurrection».* Amen. This is the seal with which I am sealing those who are born at the voice of My word, which comes to you for the new birth of the world. This is the fishing net which I, the Lord, throw into the world and when I draw it to the shore, I choose out of it all that is big and full, and I throw back all that is small. [/legatura_la_teme]
