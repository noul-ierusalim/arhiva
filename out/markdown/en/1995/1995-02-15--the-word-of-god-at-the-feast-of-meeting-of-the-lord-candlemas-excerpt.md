Israel, Israel, My dear people, the moments spent with you are sweet, and My life in you is sweet when I embrace you with My speaking. Oh, children and girls, who have the Lord Jesus Christ as your Shepherd! Here I am with you at the pasture; sweet pasture and not pasture from the seed, which passes like the seed from the ground of this time; pasture of life without death, pasture from heaven among you. Amen.

I sit in counsel with you with peace, Jerusalem, My child of today. I give you peace, son! Peace to you! Peace to Me as well, son!

In the name of the Father and of the Son and of the Holy Spirit, I have come down to you, My people, for you are My field. I have come down to spread seed upon you. Oh, the seed is a great mystery, son, and you do not know its entire mystery, and you do not know why I have said to you in the beginning to eat seed. I come to you, My people, and I give you to learn the book from God, and there is nowhere else what I give you. Sons, many saints have written words about heaven and about Me so that you may have what to learn. There have been many, who have written books about saints. There have been some others still, who have interpreted the words written by saints, so that the man may have where to read and where to start from. However, I have come to you, My people, and I am neither a saint, nor a prophet, but rather I am your Lord, Jesus Christ, and I speak to you the words of the Spirit of the Father and of the Son and of the Holy Spirit, an incorruptible seed, son, My people. Oh, the seed is a great mystery and I have given it to the man whom I created and whom I made into a living soul, and I have given him to eat seed. Son, I speak with you and I tell you that I have given to the man to eat seed. If the man had done so, that is to eat seed and its fruit, then I would have no longer been crying for the man now. However, the man had eaten something else after that. The man smelled blood and forgot the smell of seed and then I dropped him out of My hand. Son, seed means blessing and anointing, and it is My sign of anointing. The seed is the hope of what it has to be after that. The seed has oil in its body and it carries in it the chrism of My anointing, the chrism of hope and the chrism is not erased and it is not defiled, and it is My sign upon man. Seed means the fruit hoped for, of which the fruit and seed in the fruit come out. He who eats the seed, that one receives anointing in his body, for it carries oil in its body. Any seed carries oil in its body. The seed is its body and the oil is its spirit. The man who eats seed and oil from seed, that one has the Spirit of God; he has the anointing of the Holy Spirit in his body, for the seed carries this mystery in it. Behold, son of today, what it means to learn book. Who, sons, shall speak on the earth anymore for the son of the earth to know? Behold, I speak upon you, and you speak upon the son of the earth and you tell him about the mystery that is borne by the seed. I have told the man from the beginning to eat seed and fruit of the seed and with seed in the fruit. Behold, this is what I told the man in the end too.

Oh, Israel, My man from the end! Eat, son, eat much seed, and eat it too for the man from the beginning, who gave the seed for blood. Eat seed, for the man will thank you from everlasting to everlasting, for the man of eternity has not wanted to love the seed and to remain in this love. The seed grows in the soil and in its fruit, and it is without sin, like Me, son loved by God, for I love you much, and I gave you great teaching for you to learn and that I may also give you a crown of flowers to the teaching. The heaven sighs, sons, when it looks down on earth and sees children rewarded with a little crown for the teaching that is without Me. And I also sigh, child, to see that you are diligent at My heavenly school and to see that the little crowns multiply for you. Son, there is no one to give you what I give you. You will not lose what I give you, and you will remain forever crowned and will not get used as in the wilderness, if you love My school and My book. My book is known by the Holy Spirit, by the Holy Spirit in man.

… Oh, My beloved people, like a bird, which clucks having food for the chicks, such was My work around you, Jerusalem, and I stayed like a bird with its food in its beak to give it to you, so that you may not eat anything else, and I told you this way: let the seed that you eat not be sin, son, for the sin is a strange chrism and an ugly anointing, son. The man does not eat only with his mouth. The man eats both with his mind and with his heart. What does he eat with his mind, with his heart and with his feelings? He either eats God or satan; either holiness or sin. Son, you should seek in your thought after the holy things, for those who are holy make you into a heavenly flame on earth and in the holy heaven, but those that are sinful take your courage for heaven away and make you invisible to the heaven. Son, what do you want to be? Do you want to be Adam or do you want to be Christ? Take care, for Adam died and Christ was resurrected, son. If you go out after another kind of food and the bad time catches you outside, then the rain makes your little wings wet, and the storm breaks them down and you remain without wings. No one will be able to fly above the evil, which is coming to clean the earth from the evil that is on it.

… Behold, man will come to you and tell you that it is not from God this book, which I have taken out now and which I am giving as living food at My forty-year feast with you, My people. Man will tell you that it is written into the Scripture that one should not longer add or take from that book. However, I, the Lord, am coming and telling you what that word means. That word is for the book of John, for the book of Apocalypse, not for God’s word, which will never end. There it is about John’s prophecy, about the prophecy of the end of the world and about My coming; it is not about the Holy Scriptures that they will not end and that they will be with the Lord to the end of the time. It is about the word of the Apocalypse and about the number written in it, [legatura_la_teme id_capitol="3" id_subcapitol="4"]and see to what I am going to tell you now. Take out that book, sons, and read in it with Me and see that this taking out and adding to it has been fulfilled. The people of the worldly church have violated this word and have taken out of it and added to it; they have changed the word and the prophesied number, and it will happen to them as it happened to the righteous Simeon, who did not die until he saw those he had wiped put in the prophecy with the birth of the Virgin. Why, sons, oh, why has the man of the today’s church done something like this? He has done this to kill you and to make room with his righteousness so that you may no longer be afraid that I am coming now, and also that you may no longer prepare yourself and to take you from before My word. The spirit of this age has come into the church, and that is why the church belongs to this age and not to the heaven. However, you are My heavenly church, Jerusalem, and be careful, because the man of the church in the world has changed the Scriptures about My coming and about the number prophesied by the Holy Spirit. I wrote into the book one thousand and one more thousand until My coming, but the man in the worldly church has written a long way, whose end is not known, and he changed it and wrote thousands of years. Behold, this will fall on the man who lengthened the way. He added to the number and took out My coming, and if those who took and cut My number and way, if they are reckless, then the same is their flock. However, I am coming to you and I do not keep silent, and I tell you to be ready, for I am ready to appear and I will send prophet Elijah before Me, who will clean My path and pick up from My way the bodies and he will set them as a heavenly feast before Me. [/legatura_la_teme]

Oh, son, the man in the world knows better than you about My coming, for he has read about it and is not a fool, and he tries to lengthen his staying and his age. However, I am coming and telling you to love God with all your heart, with all your watch, sons, and with all your mind, with the watch of your mind, and you should not say that the Lord does no longer come, but rather be well prepared, for if you are ready, I will come, son. You prevent Me from coming; you with your slow tardiness. I do not want, sons, to send you into the world when I come. I do not want to go there where I do not like, because I do not go there; I do not go into the world. Elijah is in the world with the cleanness of the earth, for he has much zeal for God and that is why he is in the flesh. Elijah did not want to die in his flesh, and because if he did not want that, I fulfilled his wish, and behold what a great saint is he. Enoch did not want to die in his body, too, and because he did not want, he did not die. The same is with you, Israel, My child from My coming. I suffer to see that you have not become ready yet to have the fruit of life from Me, for the fruit of life is clean, clean, sons, and you are not clean yet, and I cannot sit at the table with you as in heaven, and I sit at it as on earth. My heart is broken because you have not become worthy to have from Me the gift, which I have brought down into My garden. [legatura_la_teme id_capitol="2" id_subcapitol="4"]I have great anointing in this little garden, sons. I sent the three holy hierarchs to Verginica after her fast of forty days and forty nights, and they gave Me to her as a heavenly cup; they gave her My Body, worked by the heaven, through the three hierarchs who have been at the foundation from the beginning, together with My first apostles. This was the gift that I gave to Virginia after her forty days of fasting. The gift was Jesus Christ, brought from heaven, by the hierarchs from heaven. They are the hierarchs of this work and they have their anointing upon the sons of My today’s little garden. They are from the beginning with Virginia, *(Saint Virginia, the sixth apocalyptic trumpet, r.n.)* starting with 1955, and no one is able to walk at this work established by the heaven.

… I tell you Israel to prepare well with your body and with your soul and to go to the seat of repentance and to go without sins, as the evil spirit has been stitching Me into the hurt and blooding rib for a long time, when he hears you that you would still have sins. I wish you had no more sins, Christians, and I gave from Me a man in order to feed you with My Body and my Blood, so that you may not be mixed with those who crucify Me, for the priests of the world, all are crucifying Me, they crucify Me harshly, for the have their hands full of sins, and have their bodies full of the pleasures and flesh, for they do not eat seeds and the mash of the seeds as you do. They eat dead oil, *(Double refined oil and not cold pressed oil, r.n.)*, not living oil; they eat a dead anointing, and for Me all have life, son. The seed has life into it, and its unction is anointing the life, soul and heart, but when this chrism is put into the fire to do with it as it is done with the alcohol, (Refining, r.n.), that chrism is killing the soul and body and no one knows any longer that the antichrist has been touching the oil and vine for a long time. I brought you a servant who is staying clean with the body and I told him to stay with you and to feed you for holiness and for goodness so that I may snatch you from the arms of your strangers, who are not from this work. The man who takes Jesus Christ must afterwards no longer sin so that he may not always put the Lord into an unsound and dead member. You have seen son, the seed is given once as a sacrifice and afterwards is making for itself a new body and it is given to you for food. The same is with the body of the sinful man; let him leave off his sins and take the Lord, and afterwards let him sin no more in his body and with his member, as he is turning himself into a member of Jesus Christ.

[/legatura_la_teme][legatura_la_teme id_capitol="4" id_subcapitol="5"]I have been patiently for two thousand years and since then I have stayed only into the grave and I have worked only in the grave, for even if I have had priests with a clean life in body and spirit, they have given Me over to each man who have always sinned, and I have always entered the grave, son, in the man who was into the grave for Me. Or, only if the man who was coming closer was clean, as many of your sons were, Israel, but you had an unclean priest son, and had a foreign priest to your people and to your life, and you did not have what you needed. I took a witness from the church, *(Ioan-Irineu, r.n..)*, and I gave him the word to be bishop, and My word made him bishop, and I kept him in My work and carried him through the word and I wrote him into this book as a witness on earth, from the church on earth to witness this time and My work with you, My people, the work of My word over you. And I have put through the word an anointing over the place of My garden of today, and I have raised the stone of the clean covenant, and I have put at its foundation My image and My holy law, and I have built on this foundation sons with a heavenly anointing through My heavenly word. And at this new establishment I had as a witness a bishop from the earth, appointed by Me through My work, for he was with My long while ago work, long ago son, and he was with a clean body, and I added him then to My people and made him My witness and here, he is testifying from My new foundation. He is feeble and with bound steps as the fleshly bishops hated him and stopped his step and his voice, but I have worked through him and with him what I needed to work for today and what was to come into time, into the history of the time, and here, the opposing spirit is screaming, is screaming in pain and does not know how to make his room any more, but he is doing it in vain, for I have worked My work and it is testifying and this witness of Mine is great in Me.[/legatura_la_teme]

… My love is with you, Jerusalem, My bridegroom; it is the love between Bridegroom and bride. A bridegroom and a bride are clean as long as they are spouses. And this kind of love should be between Me and you, Jerusalem, My bride of today, and you should not want any marriage, sons, that is lusts, that is idols, that is a will according to your will. Remain bride, Jerusalem, for the bride is clean and not dirty. Sons, the world comes and asks Me about you; it is coming soon and it will be for you to show your garment, your stature and your work. The man, deceived by those of this age, is coming; he is coming to look at you and to do what you do, and to do what I do; he is coming to learn the lesson of life without death. It is My love with you and it is My book with you, the book of My road with you, and you will be to say what the apostle Paul said after he woke up from his own self, after he persecuted Me and the sons of My church.

Priests and bishops will come to you to give them what you have as well and to stay under your glory, but you should be a clean bride, My last people. I dressed you beautifully, sons. See that you do not go dirty, see that you not get caught and see that you should not lose your garment. The storm is great and the weather is foggy. See so that you may not be carried by the wind, son. The wind, sons, listens to Me if you are with Me. If you are with yourself, the wind is not afraid of the body.

I embrace you with My love, into My mantle, and you should no longer hear this song of love anywhere. It is My love with you, Jerusalem, and I want you to have your face into My image and after My likeness.

Peace to you, children and child of My people! How good it would be for My people to be a child, to be a child as I prophesied. However, the one that is not My child is seen that he is not child. Love the spirit of child within you. The children do not know what fear is. Be children of the Father and of the Son and of the Holy Spirit and you will hover above!
