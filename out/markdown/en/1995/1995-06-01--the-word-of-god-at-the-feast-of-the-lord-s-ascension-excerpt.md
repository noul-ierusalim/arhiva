… Oh, Israel, you do not know, son, to understand what I am speaking with you, for I have been speaking truly and I have been asking you no longer to look into the world, and I have been saying this to you: *“Woe to you, My people, when you do not listen to Me in such a way that you may no longer look in order to know what the world gets what is coming to it, too!”* The world, sons, will undergo all kinds of evil, bigger and bigger, growing more and more in pains, as the saint prophet Elijah went into the world, and he went to avenge Me, Who was mocked and denigrated by the world and trampled under feet by the world and by the ministers from the world, who sit into My name before the man from the world. They sit, and they sit in vain because the world becomes corrupted from bad to worse, and the fish gets his head spoiled more and more too. Everything that is still holy has become filthy and there is no longer any clean spot, and the prophet Elijah has been filled with zeal and pain because the man who says that is a Christian has become much more evil; he has become more wicked than the Israel in that time when Elijah was crying at Me because of the lack of God in man.

Oh, My people Israel, I have told you, that you are more than Moses and more than Elijah, and this is what I told you in the time of Virginia. Walk, sons, walk into the book because you have to learn from it now, and see that I have told you this. I have told you to be more like them for Me and you have to be stronger than they were within their work son, for I have given you, son, I have given you more than I gave them, and I have given you what I have not given to anyone up to you, and I do not have anything else to give you, because I gave you everything I had to give you.

[legatura_la_teme id_capitol="1" id_subcapitol="6"]The prophet Elijah had been spending day and night within the work for his zeal after God. He had loved Me so much, sons, that he made Me refuse him nothing of what he was asking from Me in his zeal for God. [/legatura_la_teme]But if I have given you more than I gave Him without you asking it from Me as He did, oh sons, how were you supposed to be, Israel? You have been the most protected and taken compassion on, and I have been keeping you as a heavenly ornament of nowadays. Oh, and you have not got any zeal as Elijah, My prophet, had, for he was crying because of Israel’s iniquity which was coming up to Me, and you, instead of crying as he did, you stand in the middle of lawlessness and are not afraid of the destruction tool of the lawlessness. Be careful My child, for Elijah, My prophet, goes everywhere. He is confirming on you the seal of the living God, and I will strengthen and refresh it, for I want, son, to have you and to call you My tool from the time of My appearance, from the time of My coming, as I come into appearance, son, and you should believe what I tell you. I will soon let Myself be seen, and I want that you should be with My peace on you, so that you may rejoice in the time of those who will be uncovered to be seen. There is still a little bit of time and this work will be revealed and you will see how I have done this work upon you, Romanian Israel. You will see how I spoke and speak from near you and from above you. You will see how I carried and carry you during this heavenly time, for you are within a heavenly time, and the world is within an earthly time. And you are within this time, but you are in a heavenly way in it, because this is how you were written; to be My heavenly people in time and to be the work of testimony for the day of the testimonies, for the time of the judgment of all the things, which have passed away in time. The time is hard but I have taken a step of new age with you. I wrote a beginning of a step over time and I wrote with you the beginning and the end. And when you see what mystery that day of Passover carried, when I took the step with you, you will marvel, My child, when you would find out what I did on that day through you.

At My birth of the Virgin I separated the two worlds, and the world at that time did not know and did not feel it, for few were those who carried the mystery of the fulfillment of the prophecies of God’s incarnation among the people. And after I fulfilled that work that was prophesied ahead of time, I went to My place near Father, but I did not go there until I first prophesied My return from now through the angels. Christian, look into the Scripture and you will find the voice of the angels who said that I will come back the same way I ascended to My Father. And since then I have always been coming down and ascending to Father. Since then I have always been coming down and ascending invisibly and I have always been working in an invisible way by man, but with you, My people, I have come down by word. I have always been coming down from near Father to you and I have been speaking My word from above you, and I have been working over you for forty years, as I did with Israel in the time of Moses. Then I had Moses and now I had Verginica, the trumpet that was written about in the Scriptures. It is written into the Scriptures that the Lord Himself will come down from heaven into His trumpet, to raise the dead from the grave at the sound of the trumpet. Son, do you believe in the word of this Scriptures? Do you believe that Verginica was the fulfillment of this Scripture? Do you believe that she was God’s trumpet?

Oh, Verginica, My daughter by whom I came down the second time, and by whom I will let Myself be seen in the way I came down and in the way I will come, seen by every eye! Oh, wise daughter, for you had no education, and that is why you were wise. You did not learn anything from man, nothing from the earth, and you received teaching from heaven because I was in you, and you pleased My Father and yours with the holy exhortation put on My flock and yours, for you were eating cherries and you were gathering their seeds, Verginica, and you were asking the little sheep to make the *“Our Father”* prayer for each seed. You were filling their little fists with seeds, and for each seed they were saying the *“Our Father”* prayer. When one of them were sick and you knew that his sickness and pain were coming from the things in the world that were entering him, you understood him and giving him seeds you were asking him to pray: *«Our Father Who are in heaven, hallowed be Your name, Your kingdom come, Your will be done, on earth as it is in heaven. And give us today our daily bread, Father, and forgive us our debts, as we also have forgiven our debtors. And lead us not into temptation, but deliver us from the evil one»*. Oh, Verginica, who else still wants to know what it means the name of the Father to be hallowed in man, in the Christian, in the son of this work? Who else knows to believe what he says when he speaks *«Father, Your will be done, as in heaven so on earth»*? Do you hear My Verginica what this prayer says? It says that the Father’s will be done as in heaven. I am speaking with you, for you know to understand. Who else shall I get along with? With whom? I want to get along with your people, with My people that I have from your work with Me, from your labor together with Mine. I want Israel to say the *“Our Father”* prayer and to say that the Father’s will be done, to be done as in heaven so on earth as well, so that the earth may be heaven, and so that the heaven may be earth, and the earth in heaven. And when Israel says: «Give us today our daily bread, Father», when Israel says so, let Him ask for Me, for I am the bread that comes down from heaven.
