The peace and the quietness of the Holy Spirit are above you to settle upon you, Jerusalem, My loved people. I come to comfort you with the Holy Spirit, the Comforter, as this how He is called, son; the Holy Spirit is called the Comforter. Amen.

Peace and comforting in a day of the Holy Spirit upon My manger in which I spread My word to be in the book, as any word that is proclaimed begins its fulfillment.

Oh, My loved people, a stone rain arose in the west to blow over My vineyard. You are My vineyard, and I am the vintager and I keep My vineyard and I pray to My Father for it, and I sit above it and I guide the black clouds to head for the wilderness, where no one of My people dwells. I am the One Who commands the storm and the hail to depart from over My vineyard, as My vineyard is Mine. Amen.

Sons, sons, be good and faithful, and stay before Me with a great faith and strong hope, as My book, which is the fire that got out from the midst of My people, started to burn in some places, but no one can blow out this fire, because this book is the Holy Spirit.

A stone rain arose from the west to blow over My vineyard, but I, the Lord, proclaim a word and say: In the name of the Father and of the Son and of the Holy Spirit, let it be peace, quietness, comforting and Holy Spirit upon My vineyard, and let the stone rain come back to the place where it came out from. Amen.

My people, My people, take out the book and seek well into it, as I, by My Holy Spirit, told you that a time was to come when rumors would come upon you saying that you are not good, that you are not My people. And behold, that time has come, but I tell you that you are My people and My new vineyard. The vineyard of the world is the vineyard of the world, and the vineyard of the world gave forth sour grapes instead of sweet grapes, but My vineyard is watered and tended by Me and it has new grapes and new wine. My people, you should not get out from the vineyard, as from all over a stone rain passes over the vineyard of the world and over the shepherds of the vineyard of the world. Stay under the tabernacle of the Holy Spirit, under the rain of the Holy Spirit. Oh, My vineyard, stay on My table, as I have worked hard to have you on My table. Rumors come over you from the world that you are not a choice vineyard and you are not good. Oh, close the door and stay with your ears clogged to the whispers that produce evil and sorrow. Do not be grieved, and do not take after the strangers who show enmity against you. Take after My word and do not get out of My tabernacle, as I am above you to protect you and to comfort you and to command the hail from the west to go into untraveled places, where no one of My people dwells. Your enemy will never say that you are the people of the Holy Spirit, and behold, New Israel, My and your enemy gives you a sinful name. Who gives you a sinful name? Who, My people? You should not be afraid, Israel, of this whispers, as they will be lost into the darkness, and you will stay into My light. I am the Lord, your God, new and loved Jerusalem by God, and you should not wait for love, comfort and praise on the land that is foreign to peace. You should keep the work of My peace, as that is why I always tell you *„Peace to you!”.* Peace to you in the time when the enemies of My work fight against you! But I stay before you and turn this enmity to the place where it is born from. Sons, where do the enmity and rebellion originate from? Out of unbelief, out of disobedience, out of sexual immorality, out of haughtiness and out of love! Oh, My loved people, I come down to put this work of love upon you, to love God during the bad time which surrounds you from everywhere, as I command the hail to shun My vineyard, so that it may stay for ripening and to stay on My table.

He, who is not wise, cannot stay with God and with this heavenly work.

Be wise my people; this is how you are to be, as the wise ones will overthrow the throne of emptiness, and the emptiness will cry out on its streets; it will cry out with a great shout at its downfall. But you should not be frightened, loved people by God, as I, as the Lord, proclaim: peace upon you, Israel! Oh, I am with the angels above you. All the heaven is above. Open your eyes, son. Do not be afraid with the heaven; do not doubt, and stay properly, for it is a great power to stay as it is necessary to stay.

I spoke a word upon you, my people, and I thank the Lord for the work that He gave me, and you, Jerusalem, you should not get out from under the cover of the Holy Spirit. Amen, amen, amen.

— Oh, Verginica, all the heaven startled when it heard you speaking upon the people of Israel of today. The entire heaven thanks you that you help the little ones. Do not be discouraged, Verginica. You should work and exhort your children toward work and to listen to what I say to you. He, who believes, believes, and he, who does not believe, does not believe and chooses whatever he wants, but We should work, Verginica.

Oh, Israel, Israel, it is the village of the beginning of My word and it is full of heavenly guests. This village is full of the Holy Spirit, and you will hear about the work of today, of the work of this day and of the greatness of the celebration of the Holy Spirit, Who is working upon you, Israel, and upon the village of God’s word.

Sons, sons, may the joy of the Holy Spirit seize you and stay under it! Let any wrath and any judgment which is angry with you be scattered. I, the Lord, tear apart any evil and hold back and scatter it; however, you should go on the road of the new age, which started with you, and do no longer look into this age. You have a new heaven; you should stay in it, and may My joy be blessed with you!

May the Holy Spirit rejoice within you, and from you to the multitudes! Peace to you through the Holy Spirit of hope! Do not be confirmed to this age, at the voice of this age. It is the new and holy age with you. It is the kingdom of the heavens with you on the earth. It is a new heaven and a new earth. Amen, amen, amen.
