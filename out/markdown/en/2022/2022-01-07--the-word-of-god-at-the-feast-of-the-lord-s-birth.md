I am becoming a book on earth **(Apoc: 5/1-9.)** and I am writing Myself into it with all My word during these days, for Father has sent Me to shepherd those whom He has given to Me to tend and to make them shepherds of sheep so that they may tend My sheep and to give them from Me what I give them to have and to those who do not have any life from heaven in them, the birth from above, the light over their lives, and those who find Me coming in the word on earth to walk in light.

[legatura_la_teme id_capitol="1" id_subcapitol="7"]I am the Son of the Father God and of the mother Virgin. Father had sent Me two thousand years ago to become a Man on earth by My birth of the Virgin mother and many have come to the Father through Me, to the salvation out of sin, to their salvation, to the salvation paid with My life on the cross so that the man’s guilt may be wiped out from God’s face and the man to come to Me, to the love coming from heaven to be on earth with the people and to teach the people the love against the sin in them, and here I am tending and calling to God those who are saved from the devil that separates man from God to do his will so that he might lose his eternal life with God, for your life, man, is from God; it is not from those who give you a body to be born, rather it is from God, Who gives you life when you are born and the Lord gives you the soul and then you catch life and breath from Him, and behold I am working a feast of birth on earth on this great day of the memorial of My birth among people of the Virgin mother two thousand years ago among people.

I am the One foretold by Moses and by the prophets that I would come on earth with the voice of My word and I would be the Shepherd of the people that receive Me to tend them. I am Jesus Christ, as My name was given at My birth. I came to be humble on earth and this is how I remained, humble and modest in My life, both in My appearance and work. The world of that time was not able to receive Me for the world lives its life in a worldly way and I came out of the heavenly glory, and this glory has nothing to do with the earth or humans.

I was received in a manger to be born on earth. This is how the Father worked at that time, to be like God’s holy glory in the midst of the foreign people’s glory and to be received by those who need God, by those who are poor and in want, who suffer and are not comforted, aggrieved and helpless. I am He Who heals from sufferance, sadness and estrangement and I give Myself to be their comfort and alleviation. For these and after these I came and come, for they need God and they receive Me to be with them.

I was neither known nor received by the rich ones, full of their lives and possessions that shine in a worldly way. Oh, these are not for Me! For Me it is the creation made by God, that which is beautiful without margins, without any borders, and in no way to come into narrow places and full of the gathering that is for man.

Oh, what shall I do with the barns and thrones in the houses of those who gather richness on earth? How shall I enter their palaces, on their royal seats looking like thrones? For Me it is the three-foot stools, the three little pillars, for I am the gentle One and humble in heart and appearance.

[/legatura_la_teme][legatura_la_teme id_capitol="3" id_subcapitol="3"]You, people who know that I came on earth two thousand years ago to give you the heaven, oh, flee from the luxury so tiring for the soul of a Christian who loves God, and you, too, will have the heaven and its place made of God. Do no longer fill your houses with those that are tiring for God’s dwelling with you and near you. The kingdom of God’s sons is not of this world, but their kingdom is in God.

Man needs My help, but let him look for Me not only when in trouble to be for him and with him, but always to be with him, to be able to be, to give ourselves from one another everything that is holy, for if man does not speak with Me, then it is in vain that I speak with him, and behold, man, you speak too much and too many things and all the time, oh, and you speak too little with God or even nothing and you need God and help from heaven, and whole peoples are on earth without the true God on them and they make their gods after their likeness for a man cannot live without joys, without hopes, without any longing of salvation from his troubles.

Man takes his mind from God and does whatever he needs with it. Oh, poor man, do no longer stay so foreign from God with your mind, do no longer stay like that day by day, and get up and see what it is with you and where you go with your life. Call Me to hear you and to reveal Myself to you with My great work for you, for your redemption from the enemy who took you from paradise to separate you from God, the Creator of the heaven, the earth and man. You ordain your plans, your thoughts and desires, but you do not work your sweet bond with Me; it does not come to your mind to do this holy miracle for your life. Maybe you say that you have God, that you can be with Him like that, that He can be like that with you. Oh, you cannot be for God through so many that you did and do after your mind, after your own tastes. Oh, you have so many things that you do not need so many things that you want to do and have them, but you do not need them, and you do not stop from doing this to know this. I teach you the new birth from above and I come with it to you. This is what I do when I speak these to you in a day of the feast of the birth of your Lord. I did not gather anything for Me on earth and here it is what you do! And you do this because you do not love God Who gave you life to have if you were born in the flesh on earth.

[/legatura_la_teme][legatura_la_teme id_capitol="6" id_subcapitol="3"]Oh, remember, man, remember the Lord! You are so poor of the heavenly things. I re-joiced many destitute and exhorted them to joys and I was saying to them this: *«Always re-joice!».*

Oh, it is hard without joys. The joy is the food of the soul. As long as you live on earth share joys with those around you. To refuse not to become joy for those around you is the lack of your being. When you do not feel the longing to make joys then you are cold in your soul. Oh, seek to long for joys, Christian man! Seek to feel the need of joys, to give joys, and then it means that you love, that you live and share life and hope of life.

Share joys among you, sons, and take care to live your life, for the life, which is not lived, is without the eternal fruit of the happiness of the grateful life, sons. Oh, do not lose your life, oh do not sleep, do not stop with its working mystery and live it, sons, as it is from God, and God is life. Keep away from the separation from God and be afraid of this, of everything that separates you from the Lord and from your obedience to Him! There are children at school who by no means want to learn and these pass in vain through school. The saints chose to pass through sufferance for they knew well that the body draws you to idleness and to sin, to the waste of salvation; they knew that if you are not full of God you are full of something else, full of the things, which are foreign of God.

Two thousand years ago, I was speaking to the disciples about the Father and they were perplexed, and when I left, I told them God’s whole name and I sent them on earth in the name of the Father, of the Son and of the Holy Spirit, One God in His Trinity, and behold, now, take care to live your life in this great name, sons, but learn well the work of the birth from above to know then the life with God.

Sons, sons, give one another joys freely so that they may be gathered in heaven and this richness to meet you. When someone who shares this eternal richness gives you God or from God, you shall be grateful and give thanks to God for this gathering and then you shall learn to give too so that you may have the work that will be paid to you as to some hard working man who does not live without holy work. [/legatura_la_teme]

Oh, behold, a day of the feast of birth! I, the Child coming down into the manger that received Me to be born in it, I, the wonderful Lord, I perfect Myself in the word over the earth so that for those who choose Me to be their Shepherd and to keep close to My today’s fold, of the word with which I lay Myself down into My book to shepherd those whom the Father has given Me to draw all to My Father, as I said: *«I will draw all to the Father!»*, those who want and those of whom I have mercy, as it is written.

It is a day of birth and now I am blessing all the nations to the revelation of God, the Father, the Son and the Holy Spirit, and I am sending My angels over the earth to sow the seed of faith in the hearts prepared to spring and to bring forth food.

Peace to you, peoples and nations on the earth! The angels given by God over the peoples and borders will blow their trumpets to stir the waking up, to exhort to the holy faith for the proclamation of the kingdom of the heaven on earth, for satan and the people of the antichrist try to make it wider and wider *(To fill the cup of their lawlessness before God by imposing all kinds of restrictions and sanctions against those who do not do their will, r.n.)* and they forget that God is the Victor, He and the faith in God over the nations, and all will be full of the knowledge of salvation and they will know God, as it is written.

Now, peace to you, My dear country, My Romania! We have a feast of the birth, you and I, for the Father gave birth to us once, I for you and you for Me, for the people to which I had come did not receive Me, either to be born or to be their Shepherd, but I came in a humble way and worked mysteriously, until this people gave Me over to be with the thieves to the same death, oh, and I am so merciful of this people and I want for it humility and repentance that I may be able be able to forgive it and to tend to My will, to the faith in My coming two thousand years ago and in My today’s word, with which I call the nations to the table of the love of God, the Father, the Son and the Holy Spirit, and I to give them My love when it becomes word of glory, a heavenly table on the earth.

And peace to you now, on a day of the feast of the birth, people of My word! You, the first, shall be My will on earth and that I may be able to shepherd you always son, and to grow you always and the joy with which I come to you to share with you, and to have joys from one to another.

We are sitting at the table of the feast. My mother Virgin is with you near her Son. You are the fold in which I set My word of the new birth of the world. Peace to you! Ask for My help so that you may be able to stand before Me and to be able to work and so that God may work on earth, sons.

I leave upon you the spirit of comfort to comfort you for I am gentle with you, and all those in heaven are like Me for you, gentle, sons, and on this day, come, build the joy for My day of birth, sing carols according to the tradition and peace upon you and peace over My Romania, My today’s country, Mine and yours, and of those who love it and praise it for My great glory with you into its midst, oh, sons! Amen, amen, amen.
