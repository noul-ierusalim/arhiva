Peace to you, oh peace to you, sons, My today’s disciples! Oh, peace to you, as I told My disciples two thousand years ago on the day when I came out of the tomb and, then again, the eight day after My resurrection, so that it could also see Me Thomas who was not among the disciples when I appeared before them the first time! They were also not welcomed by the rulers of the people at that time, and they were working with great care to proclaim Me resurrected and then all My history with them until My resurrection.

Oh, and the same today as at that time is unbelief, is refusal, is perplexity. I see many still wondering; they are wondering what this word is, this book, this little handful of sons who are testifying that I, the Lord, am spending time with them, leaving My word into My today’s book with them, with you, oh, sons.

Again and again I am coming in to them, as I entered at that time to My disciples through doors locked by the unbelief of the rulers of that time, I am coming in and speaking with the My word among you for them. Oh, how shall I not tell them as I am telling you, how shall I not greet them, *„Peace to you!”*? I want that they may also be partakers of the joy of those who believe, for *„blessed are those who believe”*, this is what I said among My disciples on the eight day after My resurrection two thousand years ago.

The happiness of those who believe, oh, no one can take it from them, no one, for they are those who do not fear, as their faith and its great power help them with this, as it did with so many blessed people who were walking with the Lord until their martyrdom, confessing Him, and their faith full of longing strengthened them, and behold how happy are those who believe from God when He reveals Himself before them on earth!

[legatura_la_teme id_capitol="3" id_subcapitol="4"]I am preparing now the word of My mouth to come in with it to the priests and bishops of the Romanian people, and then all over the earth where live the Christians of Jesus Christ’s church according to the living of the order from the beginning left through the saints and fathers, starting with My first disciples and to whom I have left the dowry from generation to generation of God’s sons, the faith and the confession of Christ’s kingdom with the people on earth, the resurrected Lord.

On the eighth day after My resurrection, I am coming to you, those who minister into the midst of the Romanian people and in My name and of My Gospel of two thousand years ago, and from here to all the faithful people on earth.

Oh, peace to you! Christ has risen!

I am also telling those who struggle in themselves to tell themselves that do not believe in God, I am telling them too: *„Christ has risen!”* to remember the Resurrected Lord, for I am telling them the big truth, the one that shook the whole world at that time and that after them.

Christ has risen! This holy news was the cornerstone of Christ’s church and God’s kingdom with the people on earth has grown, having Christ’s priests at its helm, first mysterious because of the lack of faith of the world’s rulers, and then faithful disciples, known for their ministry and for the truth of the faith that Christ is the Savior of those who believe in Him, is the Lord resurrected from the dead.

Oh, draw faithfully near to the Spirit of My Word, Who is coming to you to give Himself to you, for He is the Comforter, promised that would come and bring from God and would proclaim God’s works into the midst of His church, in the midst of the people. **(John: 14/26; 15/26)**

Many wondered and they still do, what is this book of God’s Word? Why does it come and when is the Holy Scriptures enough? Now I, Myself, am giving a clear answer, I Who write Myself into this book with the help of the ears and hands of the sons faithful to this word coming down from God at them on the table.

This book is prophesied through the Scriptures that would come and would be called God’s word, **(Apoc: 19/13)** My coming with the saints in tens of thousands, as it is written for Me to come **(1Thess: 3/13; Jude: 1/14; Apoc: 19/14.)**, and behold, I am coming!

This book is My today’s word, with which I grow a people to serve Me in secret, always teaching him to remain in God’s will as I was under My Father’s will all the time, receiving from the Father all My work as a Son, submissive, obedient, being taught by My Father word by word, for My Father was teaching Me; He was feeding Me with His will, and this is how I was working, not from Me but from My Father, as are doing those nowadays, those who take from Me what to work, and that they may work everything I give them to do, as I worked on earth as long as I was in the body and walked with My disciples, not alone, oh, for it is not good for man to be alone and even now I am not walking alone, for I am coming with the saints and I am the One Who is working and they are working too, and We both are growing a holy people on earth and sharing this teaching everywhere to be set on earth for those who believe and receive My teaching that is always in the present, of Jesus Christ, Who is coming to renew the world, as it is written in the Scriptures about the new birth of the world.

Come near and eat of the wine, you those who minister in My name before people, for the unnourished sprig dries without food, but the nourished and dressed one brings fourth food, oh, and this is what I do with the sons of My word through My book with them, by My dwelling in their mist as word, so that they may be able to fulfill by their faith the Scriptures that are still to fulfill and that they may remind Me by praying and fasting that the last Scriptures coming, waiting for their fulfillment, and I to fulfill them.

I am teaching you as I also taught them, and behold what I am exhorting you if you are Mine, you are Christ’s and belong to His teaching: Oh, sons, keep the holy ordinances of the church, for you have to show yourselves as an example to follow all the time before Christ’s flock. I want to mend those that have to be mended and then set back to good order, and here it is what I am asking you, and kneeling down I am asking you to make on your bodies the right sign of the holy cross when you bow before those who are led by the church, for behold, I am looking at you with the saints and see that because of your richly ornate robes, too pavonine, too stiff, you cannot lift up easily your right hand out of your tunicle and make the sign of the holy cross on its place, on your head, chest and shoulders, and with all piety for this sign, for if the cross is wryly set on you when you worship, those who look at you see this and judge, they commit the sin of judgment, poor of them, and I, the Lord, come to you and urge you, as the sons of My word, and I come and speak to you, too.

Look at those who are grown with the glory of My today’s word, for the priests among them are humbly dressed, with humble clothes, beautiful like Christ, made of woven cloth with little crosses, and their robes fit them very well and in this way they can make all their prayers correctly, oh, and I am teaching you with humility, too, for I want you to take after Me, sons, and remain in the spirit of humility under My teaching, gentle and full of love so that the flock My grow in this way too, oh, ministering sons.

Behold, the time is becoming foggier and foggier over the earth, for satan’s spirit works from the darkness against Christ’s church and wants to bring pressure to its walking with the Lord and the Lord’s will over the earth and people, and I, the Lord, with all the heaven of saints and angels want to keep further the Romanian land and the people on it under the heavenly cover, and behold, I can do this by the voice of My word, that keeps away any hostile power; however, one should watch and make right everywhere the way with Christ with fast and prayer and by the keeping with holiness of the Christian tradition, by strong faith, oh, sons, for I, the Lord, by the faith of those who wanted to be with God in them, I have fulfilled all the time the Scriptures and God’s will over the earth and again I have fulfilled in this way all the prophecies of the Scriptures.

I have set through its saints the church and its order, and I have established it to be seated on four pillars, *(Four periods of fasting during a year, r.n.)* for I have not wanted it to be shaken, and that no one may change its foundation. However, one of its pillars was shaken after that *(It is about the period of fasting before the feast of the holy apostles Peter and Paul, a period shortened by thirteen days, with the change of the feasts from the Julian calendar to the Gregorian one)* and it became very small, for without the fasting that has become very little because of the changing of the time of the Christian feasts one hundred years ago, oh, it is hard now without the whole order to keep away the enemy satan’s envy, and behold, one has to repair what was shaken at the foundation of My church, and I have taught the sons of My word what this restoration is. I am speaking with them as God spoke with man at the beginning seven thousands years ago, and you know this from the Scriptures, for I, the Lord, have the power to do this miracle, to speak over the earth, and behold, I am speaking, oh, and I am speaking with you, too, and I am teaching you the beauty of the mystery of Jesus Christ’s Christian, as there is no one to teach and tend the flock to Christ.

Behold, how beautiful, how wonderful is the Lord’s work with the children of the people of My word! I let them uncovered, to be seen. I have not worked with them as in the beginning of My church, two thousand years ago, when her priests were not known by the priests of the old law, and all of them were servants in their own families, and their patriarch was mysterious, even if the Jews called him the “the righteous one,” and he was the disciple who put before the new priesthood the holy liturgy, and the Christian history calls him Jacob, the first patriarch of Jerusalem, oh, and this is how I started the kingdom of the havens on earth, the people after Christ, the Christian people, and I was working in the midst of the people of that time and I was establishing My kingdom on earth.

I am teaching you, sons, as I taught much My disciples two thousand years ago, for man is full of himself and he is the one to be overcome and only after that the Lord has to sit within him with His face, with His will.

I was teaching the disciples looking intensively at them, over them and in them, oh, and I saw that they needed teaching and I was telling them: *«Take heed and beware of the yeast of the Pharisees, which is hypocrisy!»*. **(See Matt: 16/6)** Oh, and you too, keep away from this yeast, I am teaching you as I taught them, for I see that the exhortation I am giving you has to be learned and fulfilled.

Hypocrisy, that has always wanted to be taken as work in the church, oh, it is satanic and it comes out when you least expect it, as Judas’ sin came out with a great echo two thousand years ago, for he did not show his hidden work and he was playing the hypocrite on one side and the other, among My disciples and among the priests of the temple, who were buying his soul by the spirit of their hypocrisy, for the bishops and archbishops of that time were behaving nicely and smoothly with Judas lest he might suspect what was the deed they wanted to push My apostle to do, who was walking to and fro without any watch over him, without the knowledge of the other apostles. Oh, it was only I that I knew what the Scriptures had to be fulfilled through the restlessness of this disciple divided in his walking, with his hidden custom, hidden from the other brothers in Christ.

I had and even have on this way of My today’s people the Christians that know Me and who walk aimlessly after the custom of their nature, even if at their beginning on this way they wanted to appear that they specially valued their faithfulness to Me, for their obedience to My exhortation, for their steadfastness, but they changed afterwards, and when they changed they hid with their work with which they walked to and fro and did not understood the danger which separated them from their walking with Me, oh, and behold how their hypocrisy brings about pain and wretchedness to those who are not steadfast during the time of their life, as any deed shows its fruit in the end, good or evil, and hypocrisy does not bring anything else but only pains, as it happened with Judas and with the priests who dealt with hypocrisy in order to bring Jesus to death on the cross.

I have come with the table of word and I am with My disciples of that time at the table with you to exhort you with a sweet word like the sons who help Me with the glory of My word when I put it into the book on earth. Apostle Thomas is bringing you the greeting of My resurrection and he is speaking with you within My presence and behold, may your speaking from the presence of your Teacher Jesus Christ be blessed, loving disciple!

— Oh, Lord, out of love and longing I have wanted to see Your wounds, to worship for them before You, confessing You resurrected, my Lord and my God.

I was walking for a while, separated from the other disciples, but I understand now from heaven that all things had with a meaning, everything was set by God for the whole confession and with no partiality so that it might remain with power of faith for the followers, Lord.

Your today’s little people, these few of Yours today who help You in the coming of Your way into Your book of today, oh, they are like us, those of old, You sit with them as You sat with us and teach them as You taught us, Lord. You have taken and brought them to Your mysterious service, You have taken them from those who bears Your memory thorough the mystery of Your church, Lord, and have done with them a sweet work and kept them under Your protection, keeping them from all the evil as You protected us then, oh, and You are giving them work by the word, You are giving them the power of obedience, You are protecting them in a godly manner and they are the way of Your coming as word on earth, and they are good, they are diligent, they stay under the grace of the working faith, they help to Your progress, they serve You and pray for the salvation of those on earth and for those who left the earth, all under the new order of Your word, and their protection on Your behalf fills them with hope, it gives them steadfastness and holy patience, everything has been done by their obedience to You, their Teacher.

You, ministers in the church of the Romanian people, know that these sons, the Lord Jesus Christ uses for His word of the preparation of the glory of His day, which is coming soon, as it is written to come and to be called to come, oh, they do not have to listen but only to God first, and you should understand with your heart in you what their obedience to God is, and only after that to those that are after God’s will, who are coming to them from the earth for them to fulfill.

Be blessed, beloved brothers, be blessed by faith with my happiness, for blessed are those who believe, oh, loved brothers! If you do not have this happiness in your souls, you seek after it to receive it, to be happy, for behold, the Lord is coming among you as word because He loves you, that He wants you have the happiness that have those who love obedience to Christ, His works from all the times, for the Lord does not stay in haven, my dear, no, rather He works a lot, a lot and always, and He always fulfills mysteriously that which is written in the Scriptures to appear and to be set to be after that, and you, make your faith and love healthy, for the healing is coming to you from heaven, only that you may bow to it, only that you may believe, beloved brothers.

And now, amen, Lord, and I am bowing with longing as a disciple of the longing after You and after Your mysteries, oh, for they are many but the human mind is very small, it is little because man took without obedience to You from the tree that You had told him not to eat, and he took and ate and then he had come to know the good and evil and he had seen that he was naked and realized that he went wrong and was ashamed of the deed of his disobedience, through which he has got only shame.

I am Your disciple, I am Your messenger and I am breathing the work of repentance over all those who serve on earth in Your name, and I am asking You for those among them who humble and repent from their life sins, I am asking You to wipe them out, Lord, all of them, and I am forgiving and releasing them in the name of the Father, of the Son and of the Holy Spirit, and I am exhorting them to the gift of the holy faith, to steadfastness and mysteriously to follow You doing Your will and the will of Your word, as Your disciples today listen and bow, Lord, under Your word.

Oh, glory to You for Your coming down now! We are kneeling together with You before their doors to be opened and the Lord to be received and also His greeting of peace over them eight days after the glory of His resurrection. Amen.

— Oh, peace to you My accompanying disciples, so that I may come in with you in the midst of those who bear My name on earth during these days and I am speaking My word to them, My exhortation! I would have liked to tell them many things. However, I am looking at them to see how much they are able to carry and I exhort them to holy faith, for the Lord is working a great salvation to many.

I remain mysteriously in the midst of My today’s disciples, with whom I am going as word over the earth and who are special to Me to keep them clean and not mixed with those that are not under the obedience of God’s will, for the man’s will is great on earth, and the same is in the churches too, and it stands against God’s will with the people, oh, and that is why I am keeping apart My little people, My little hand of sons, with whom I am proclaiming and setting on earth the fulfillment of the last Scriptures, as they are written to come and be set on earth.

I am telling you now, those that are ministering in the church of the Romanian people, and by My speaking I am telling to all the faithful ones, Christians after the tradition of the first disciples two thousand years ago, I am telling you the greeting of those who were close to Me when I was resurrected and I wiped out the tears of their suffering on their cheeks, telling them after My resurrection: *„Peace to you!”* and then I told them: *«Receive Holy Spirit»*, **(John: 20/22)**, *«and as My Father has sent Me, I also send you to proclaim Christ and His victory against the ruler satan, for Christ was resurrected and overcame on the cross!».*

Peace to you, and I am also giving you the happiness of those who believe! I am He Who is. I have been perfecting Myself on the Romanian people by this word for seventy years. I am Who I am and that is why I can speak and fulfill My word. I am and I want to know all to whom My word gives power to believe, for the Father has given Me all the power in heaven and on earth, and behold, I am coming with power of word, and the Father is comforted with Me and from Me seeing My love speaking and calling the man to God’s kingdom and to the kingdom of the happiness of those who believe, for blessed are those who believe!

Love is calling out loud on the street to the people to come to it, for Christ is coming and keeps on coming, as it is written.

I am putting My name on My today’s epistle. I am Jesus Christ, the resurrected Lord from the tomb on the third day, according to the written promise in the Scriptures to be fulfilled, and it has been fulfilled.[/legatura_la_teme]

Amen, amen, amen.
