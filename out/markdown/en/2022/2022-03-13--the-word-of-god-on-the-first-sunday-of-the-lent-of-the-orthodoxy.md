[legatura_la_teme id_capitol="3" id_subcapitol="4"]On earth, in the middle of the Christian people is written on this day the Sunday of the right faith, of the Orthodoxy, as the holy fathers set this great memorial on the first Sunday of the Lent at the beginning of My church.

I, the Lord, have to come as word on the Christian flock, the flock that bears My name, to give it a great awakening to the holy watch and then to the holy work over the earth, for My flock has a great duty over all human people from the earth to keep alive the truth that came once with Me from the Father to the people to show the world where the light for it comes, and what the world needs to do for the mystery of the light, for the faith that came to save the world from sin, to tell the world that the Lord had come on earth and became a man to show the man the way, the truth and the life and that man may have the hope to put him on the way for his life on earth. And behold, My word brings this day over the church of the Romanian people and over the Orthodox people on earth the awakening to the holy work for the sheep that have not been wandered from the fold, and especially for those who have wandered away from the protection under some shepherds foreign from the truth at the beginning of My church, and great work beginning from the church is required to sound the trumpet far and wide that the truth of the love of Christ has to be kept from the wolves that steal the sheep from the fold, and it also has to be protected from the wandering spirit, for I, the Lord, soon, soon, will knock at the door of the church to ask the shepherds about the sheep, who have a great duty for the destiny and steadiness of the flock remained in the fold, and especially for the sheep that have been deceived by the work of satan with his painted face and who seems to have everywhere shepherds of sheep, oh, but these cannot hide from their deceitful work because do not take after God those who serve under the name of Christ’s apostles without they having My sending, the name of the apostle from one to another from the beginning of My church and up to this day, and behold, the truth of lie is very great and it is seen well and it can be worked over it with the holy weapons for revealing the godly truth from the ancestors as the holy fathers fought at the beginning through the apostolic spirit and cut in due time the weeds that threatened the portion of the wheat, the right faith and made the separation and Christ’s light remained in the lampstand. And now, I, the Lord, am coming to you, to those who are sitting carelessly on the shepherd chairs of the Christian flock with the order upon it as its holy fathers left it to be, for if you do not watch for the destiny of the flock, the pack of wolves come from all sides and they come more and more frequently if they see that the shepherds do not take care to surround the fold of My sheep and to watch for their destiny, oh, for too many sheep have been stolen on the way and walk in confusion with the shepherds without My sending.

The Sunday of the Orthodoxy is remembered on this day and it has to resound far and wide and Jesus Christ’s place and truth through the church and the coming back to the Lord of the sheep without shepherds and the growth through the church of the shepherding spirit, for if the sheep are not tended, are stolen from the sides and take their steps without any direction, and the Scriptures are very clear, but they must be used by the shepherds in the church so that it may be tending over the sheep, for the great wealth of a nation over the earth cannot be something more than the number of the Christian people, Christ’s people, according to the truth left through the church by its saints and through them, and not anyhow; not from any place each one may want to learn the truth left by Christ to live in the midst of the Lord’s flock.

Oh, it is a time for watching, and the time needs waking up to watch of the shepherds that stand before the flock in the name of Christ through the church, as it written. Therefore, the Shepherd of the shepherds is calling out to the shepherds to look for the sheep and to graze them according to the tradition, to turn back those that came out in the open field tended by the unsent shepherds to graze the Lord’s sheep.

Stand up to the holy work, you those who the world knows that you are in the position to tend, to keep and to feed with the heavenly things the Christian flock, over which you have been given your work, your field of work! Take the staff into your hand and the shoes for bad weather and go to the sheep that wander away, but especially go to the shepherds that steal continuously the Lord’s sheep, and tell these that they are not shepherds of My sheep, but rather they are the restive wolves that take from the Scriptures their deceiving work, which was written to appear on earth over the Christian flock in order to divide and to take sheep out of the flock to spread them in all the mountains and valleys, for the shepherds of the church have not watched for the good of the sheep.

I am coming and asking the shepherds of sheep and I am asking the shepherds with the work of ravenous wolves; I am asking them if they look well into the Scriptures that each one of them sees and understands their work well for it is written from the beginning about the coming of the deceitful shepherds and about the lack of watching of the shepherds of sheep, and behold the memorial in the church of the right faith, a day when the holy fathers of My church were gathering with the flock and speaking blessings and curses and separated to the left and to the right the lie and the truth and were reckoning deceitful the work that was piercing the Christian flock to tear it to pieces as some of the shepherds may wanted to watch in vain by their own spirit and change the Christian customs established in the beginning.

Oh, behold why the order from the beginning of My church has to be followed! In all the times there has been tried from the midst to shake the steadfastness of the faith and the walking of the flock, and the shepherds full of the love of the Holy Spirit put on the table the light to give light in the darkness and to keep the flock from the wolves which were not known to be wolves among shepherds.

Behold, the Shepherd is coming to the shepherds, the word of God is coming and it coming with a holy trumpet sounding over the Christian people on earth. I am teaching you, Christian of My church all over the earth, oh, kneel down and ask with tears the Holy Spirit the Comforter to come down for you over your shepherds through the church so that they may be able to take care of you, for the time of nowadays is harder and harder, with more fog, and there are too many wolves that lie in wait for your destiny at the crossroads, and you do not have any protectors to keep you from the spirit of lie, a spirit that seems to look like truth, but My truth is the way with the cross, by self-denial, renunciation of pleasures, richness, the spirit of the pride of this age, and all by obedience, oh, Christian sons.

Ask sons, for Me to come and give you by prayers, for I will hear you and I will rise to awakening and watch the shepherds who have the holy duty to watch and to work for you.

The trumpet from heaven is sounding; it is sounding the awakening over the Christian flock! The Lord is sounding over the sheep and over the shepherds! It is the time for the Lent over the Christian flock and a spirit of repentance and humility is needed over the Christians, and especially a spirit of holy faith, full of watch, for all things come by faith, as it is written.

I am coming down from the Father up to the feet of those who proclaim Christ over the earth through the church and I am saying in a gentle spirit: receive My calling, work through the Holy Spirit so that you may be able to know the voice of the Shepherd, Who is calling you to watch, for I will come soon, soon, and I want to find the fence standing all around the Christian flock and you under shelter and under the protecting shepherds, and in this way to be able to speak according to the truth: Peace to you, Christian flock! Peace to you, shepherds of sheep!

And now, I am saying again: Curse be upon you, those who distort the Lord’s way and the steps of Christ’s Christians!

Peace to you again and again, to those who persevere in the name of the truth through My church, and which has its order and power from its beginning, that left through the fathers! Amen, amen, amen.[/legatura_la_teme]
