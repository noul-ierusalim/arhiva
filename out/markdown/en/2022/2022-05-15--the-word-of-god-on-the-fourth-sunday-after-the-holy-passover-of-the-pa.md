I am sitting down as word in My citadel today. I am coming to you as word, sons from the citadel; I am coming to speak of My works with you.

It is a day of Sunday, it is a day of memorial of My works on earth now after two thousand years, sons. Oh, the Lord’s days and works are not for the rest of the body, but for the lifting up to heaven of the souls that want to be God’s sons in the midst of the men’s sons on earth, for God’s sons are not from this world like the men’s sons who had chosen the world and its charms and habits for body and for the scattering of the soul into those that pass away and do no longer remain. Oh, God’s sons are in the world but are not of the world, as I said to My Father when I was giving to His care those who were My disciples as long as I stayed with them on earth, and then I asked My Father to take care of them as long as I suffered and paid for their life and of those before them and those after them to take them all out of satan’s bondage, only that they may seek after God if they are still on earth in their body, after I, the Lord, paid the tribute for their redemption, My crushed body on the cross so that they may have life after that and to have it in abundance, as it is written about those who sanctify themselves for God and bring Him honor, glory and honor as the servants of this glory.

[legatura_la_teme id_capitol="6" id_subcapitol="3"]Sons, sons, the days of the Lord are not for the rest of the body, but for love, for glory, for ministry before the One Who made the heaven, the earth and the man, and for this watch is needed, sons. Therefore, I, the Lord, am coming to you and put into the book the word of healing and I am saying: Those who seek in My today’s word, those who want obey to what I am speaking today on earth for those who believe and want to commit themselves to God by My exhortation, let them not be weak and let them not grow weak in the Lord’s days, but rather get up with spirit and watch with grace and bring worship to the Lord, to meet Him, to have holy growth for them, great humility for their wrong doings, a gentle and humble spirit, all for the building of Christ’s church, for I have told those who have followed My steps by My today’s word to work and not be idle. And I told them as I did to the paralytic from today’s memorial and I said this: *«Take up your cot and walk».* **(Luke: 5/24)**

Oh, walk, dear Christian, walk son in My footsteps and do not stay to rest in your body, for I, the Lord, wait with thirst and longing after the days for God so that the Christians may stay with their Lord in the feast, and not dedicate these days to themselves like the other days of the week, oh, for it is very little to live for the Lord the one who honors the Lord’s day, for the Christian man dedicates the other days for his body.[/legatura_la_teme]

Oh, sons from the citadel of My word, I am bringing you news from heaven. I am coming into the citadel in seven days, and I am coming with the feast of the well of the meeting and with its protector, with the Samaritan woman, and we will set into the book revealing of mysteries for the life of the soul, for the holy work of God’s sons, for they have to learn many things and follow in order to be pleased to the Lord. [legatura_la_teme id_capitol="3" id_subcapitol="2"]Therefore, set between Me and you a table of holy feast of word and come out to the well to meet us in the word and to bring joy and feast of word so that God’s sons may learn, to learn what life with Christ is all the time and what do they have to work in the Lord’s days for the Lord’s day was specially sanctified once with My resurrection from among the dead, and it was paid with the great sufferance of God’s Sons through the cross two thousand years ago, oh, and one does not need rest for the Christian’s body, rather joy, gladness and glory brought to the Lord, for the Lord paid for this day of glory and joy with sufferance, and He was brought to life to give them resurrection, and resurrection has much and great work, therefore, the Christian does not have rest in his body, but great work, oh, sons.[/legatura_la_teme]

I am coming with My disciples to you at the table and I am coming with the Samaritan woman, with whom I spoke at the well of Israel two thousand years ago, sons, for it is much to learn and fulfilled through the Lord’s sons and daughters, through those who want to be My sons. Therefore, put between Me and you a table of feast to find you sat before Me and to put a table of word, of holy growth for those who have kept themselves as God’s sons by My word fulfilled over them, word that has grown up sons for the kingdom that remains, for the days for the daily body go with all their labor, for they are as passing as the body is, but life with God and God’s sons remains, oh, sons, and it goes up to heaven and wait in glory those who bring glory on earth and feast with praising before the Lord.

May My table with you be blessed in the citadel and your meeting with Me and with My companions at the well, for [legatura_la_teme id_capitol="6" id_subcapitol="3"]the man who is with God on earth never walks alone lest satan may have the occasion to blame to the Lord for the violation of holy word, of the law of holiness of Christ’s church, and behold, I fulfill and never walk alone.[/legatura_la_teme]

And now, sons, I, the Lord, am setting starting now a table of word, and you are to kindle the candles of the feast and I want to find you waiting those from heaven here, in the city with those close to you near you for those you carry before Me with ministry and glory for you will have to carry glory for the Lord, and this is the best part, that which will not be taken from you, as it is written, oh, sons. Amen, amen, amen.
