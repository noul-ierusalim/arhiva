… Peace to you, children! I have come from heaven in Spirit as I cannot come in a body. I was once in a body. I was born of the Virgin; I healed, I cured and repaired; I taught all that Father wanted; I saturated five thousand people with bread and fish, I raised the dead, and as a reward they crucified and buried Me. This is what the humankind wants; after benefactions, revenge. This is how the humankind rewards.

Oh, well children, I healed without a reward. I left the glorious throne, beautifully decorated, I left Father and My mother, I left the hosts of angels, waivodes, cherubim, seraphim and I came to the fig tree and I found no fruit in it. And what shall I do now? I was once and what did I do? Oh, here I come again now at the fig tree, distressful, and what else shall I do? Here I came Myself, I did not sent My servants anymore as those I sent did not come back; they killed them all. Oh, I came Myself in a body, and likewise I did not come back the same way I came; I changed. You change yourselves too, children.

Here, I did not come down in a glorious vessel in the body, a vessel from a great family so that you may not come to him and talk with him. I have descended in a poor manger, as then in the sheep pen. I have descended in the manger, weak and sick, in a handful of dust. I have come down to the children who keep My words, of the Father, the Son and the Holy Spirit, of the holy Church and the ordinances of the holy fathers.

Children, God wants you and the angels and the saints want you as well and wait for you and ask Me: *„When shall we meet those on earth?”* Children, do the works worthy of being able to meet the saints and the angels in heaven. You will not be asked what you cannot fulfill. You will be asked faith, humility, feast, prayer, so that you may have part on the heaven mercy. Children, if you have what to eat and clothe with, a little, humble and simple thing, be content with it. Children, the pain is coming and going but you will not pass. The heaven and earth will pass away but My words will not pass. Heaven and earth will not pass away until My words are fulfilled. Weep here in the valley of weeping to be able to rejoice in the heavenly ones, with the righteous.

Children, many will come in front of My face and say: *„Did we not heal or cure or teach in your name when we lived on earth?”* But I will say them plainly: *„I do not know you as you practiced lawlessness.”*

Oh, sons, I want to choose you as gold is chosen here on earth. The way gold is chosen and tried by fire, the same way you too, will pass through the fire and will be hardened so as good may be separated evil.

Here the devil is fighting against the Father, and the heaven is separating from the earth. The Caesar is taking what is his and the Lord is taking what is His.

Who wants to marry is free to do so. Who wants to go into marriage let him or her do so. You are not forbidden, but who wants to remain as he is, he does what is good, for it is what Father wants, for My Father’s will is that you may be holy as I am. Be wise for the wind blows harshly and strongly, from all sides, to put you down. Do you believe? Be holy? But you cannot be holy if you steal, if you lie, if you slander, if you swear, if you curse, if you judge another and do not keep what the church teaches you and the learning of the saints. Children, separate the weeds from the wheat for no saint will come to teach you. Sanctify yourselves as the saints do not come anymore, for it is the time of the Antichrist. Work and you shall be rewarded.

My children, on earth are only evil spirits. You will have no place anywhere you go; for wherever you go you will be watched.

Mothers, establish your children in faith for you are responsible for them. You are their school, for look, in school there is no more God. Mother, mother, build in them the faith, for you will be asked of everything your child is doing.

Oh, your hand will be put at your mouth so that you cannot be My witnesses, sons! *(The Security, the political police of the communist regime, r.n.)* Here that I cannot be stopped. My vessel *(Through which the Lord speaks, r.n.)* will be killed, but I will speak through the trees, stones and through all that you see with your own eyes. If you do not want to be doers, I will speak through all that is on earth to show My power.

Mothers, you who cursed, who committed fornication, who aborted, who sinned, who slept in the church, you, who talked loudly, who came to the church dressed with bare hands and feet and heads, who come into the church scented and powdered, you who drive the men mad, get rid of all these for you will have no place in heaven. Mother, mother who aborted, if you only hear your child crying and screaming! Oh, that you will take his place when you go there. Mother who committed adultery, repent and get undressed of your dirty deeds for your hour is coming to be asked of all. Mother, you who have the custom to give the devil your child or your spouse, actually, it is you that give yourself to the devil for to him you will go. Mothers! For I call out all you who are mothers, for if I speak with everyone separately I will not finish My calling.

… Adam and Eve, for food were cast out, but the world of nowadays, with all that it does, how will it appear before the judgment when it is called out as Adam? Here children, the children come from other villages to listen, to talk with the Spirit of God, Who is speaking through this vessel of Mine, who I found clean and humbled. I have come to urge you to keep God’s commandments which are written in the Holy Scriptures and to keep them as I gave them to the holy fathers, through the holy churches, not as some are reading now, a passage or a chapter, and that is all. No, children, fulfill all and you will rejoice.

Here My vessel, *(Through which the Lord speaks, r.n.)* they took her and did all kinds of tests with her. They had drawn all her blood and injected her with old serum to lose it, *(The agents from the Security, the political police of the communist regime, r.n.)* but the hand of the Father was with her; and it will not be long until I take her from the earth. Then you will see who this vessel was, and the village Maluri, I will lose out with My fire, as Sodom and Gomorrah, for none of them in this village wanted to believe, rather slandered and despised My word. *(Later, under the leadership of Ceauşescu, the village was demolished, wiped off the earth, to make place for the „socialist agriculture”, r.n.)*
