[legatura_la_teme id_capitol="7" id_subcapitol="1"]… Keep in mind that a team of tricksters will come to a grave and raise a dead man and say to you: *“Here what power we have! Pay us the honor and glory that is due to us!”* But I say to you: do not change the faith from Me, for this thing is being done by the unclean spirits, for the one who is coming before Me and is raising the dead, that one is put to cheat you so that I may not find you when I come.[/legatura_la_teme]
