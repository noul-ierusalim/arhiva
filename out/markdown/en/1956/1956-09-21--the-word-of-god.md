… Oh, My children, I have to come to an end, so that you may rest and to be able to take part at the holy Liturgy. Scrape along not to miss this mystery. Oh, Father, I sacrificed Myself for you, to stay on holy table to feed on you. As the body dies without food, the same way your soul dies without manna.
