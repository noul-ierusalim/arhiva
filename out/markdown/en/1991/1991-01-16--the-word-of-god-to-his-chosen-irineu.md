Peace to you, child loved by God! Peace from heaven and may the glory of your God work through you! Amen. Peace to you, peace from Us to you and from you to Us! Amen. Glory to those above and peace upon you, loved children by heaven! Peace to you, as in heaven so on earth! The heaven is My throne and the earth is My footstool and you, My children, are the temple of the living God. The heart of My children is God’s heaven and their body is God’s earth, and the Lord is the King of the heaven and earth. And behold, I speak to the loved one:

Peace to you, child loved by heaven! I turn to you with My word and with My good strength, for I want to always have upon you the good strength and I want you to work in it always, for your work has to remain. But the thing that you have to work comes from heaven and the whole thing that you have to work comes accompanied by the good strength. Work it with those from above and let yourself be attended by those from above and this is what you have to say at the beginning and at the end of your work: *„It is not I, but my Father is working.”* Let this faith adopt you in all your things that you have to work, for by this faith nothing is to remain not worked out in the thing that you receive and know how to do it. Do not forget even for a moment that the ages were founded through the faith in My work, and those that are seen were done by those that are not seen. My tools of old were working those that were not yet seen, and all these died in the faith without living in the time of the promises, but only seeing it from far away; and the Lord is called their God. These remained an example to the descendants, but look, My child, that they did not receive the perfection, because it is written that for the little ones something better was promised. These have been waiting for you, little ones from this time, My child, and with you they will take the perfection that they worked for.

Behold, the body of My people Israel is big, My child, it comes from them up to you. Oh, how much better must be the faith in this time, for look what a designation has the smallest one! But My smallest Israel is very small, My child! I told you too that the Israel of today is very small, for look, that he cannot raise even a dead one, and cannot say to the world to rise and to raise the world! My Israel of today does not believe that these are the days of the resurrection and of the glory of the Holy Spirit that has been working in this time to perform that resurrection the Scriptures writes about.

My child let you be of the faith which is asked for to be in this time. Be the first so that I may put you in the corner stone, to put you on the seat of My church in order to do justice between sheep and sheep. Be worthy and just. Give from you the harmful mercy to the tribe of the new Israel for the righteousness will rule over My new people. This small Israel will have to turn with his heart to his parents so that the heart of his parents may turn to him, for woe, I do not want to come and strike with plagues the small one and to put him with his face towards his parents.

I let those who sit at the helm of My church of today know and told them that I would come and set the times back to their places and I would bring that time when the priest would again sit in counsel with the prophet, as in the old time, for this is the church after the truth. And you, My child, believe in those that I came for to let you know, for you believed that I came, and sat in counsel with Me, and behold, you sit in counsel with Me and work for the good strength and for the good faith and keep My word as Abraham believed and kept it, and his faith was considered to him as righteousness. Be like Abraham and let yourself be tested by faith, as you did by now, for I wrote down justice for you by your faith.

It is a little more time and I will discover this spring so that the nations may see and be saved by faith. This spring is protected by the whole heavenly host and I will water the thirsty peoples from this spring. The evil spirit is crying and stewing in the fire that I made it burn here, in the manger I am coming down to. These are only some children, but behold, they believe and work for Me. I sent them with an open book through the towns of My people, the same way as I sent Jonah to the city of Nineveh, and they did not say as Jonah did that they would not go for Me. But the heart of My people is of stone and I will have to show My power and go through the stone, so that I may not come to strike with a curse the one who has been fed for such a long time from Me, for it will be easier for Sodom and Gomorrah than for these if they do not want to hear My voice and My words that I call out now with.

My people is caught by drowsiness; it is caught by the spirit of the world, and I have to wake it up and to put it back to the good path, so that this small son may be found on duty and that I may not undergo with him as I underwent with the people of Moses that murmured and fell in the wilderness. That is why I was well pleased with these children; that is why I put them to intercede for My people, so that I may wipe out from My face the iniquity and disbelief of this people. Woe to this people, woe to My chosen Romania, and woe to those who will come to My words if these children stop their working for Me! That is why I put them to work; that is why I was well pleased with them, but the thing of My plan is hidden from the eyes of those around, so that I may work and perfect the thing of My plan, for the good time is struggling to come, and it will come, for I will come and show Myself through the good time, by My anointed and by My servants.

All heavenly powers work for the peace of this dwelling of the heavenly coming down and for the protection of My servants from here. And behold, My child, I will have to work with power in this place; I will have to go only forward here, and I will have to lack not even a link of this chain which is making by the prayers that are coming to up Me from this place.

I will seek to find a man who I may work still another shelter with for the protection of My children here, for the time of the angels that will begin to clean My field and to wash away the iniquity of this earth of the New Canaan; this time is pressed on and wants it as well to make its thing after God’s commandment, and My children servants here, will need to be here on the duty that they have in My service, for woe to them if they keep silent! Woe, My child! Woe to My Romania if the thing of My covenant of today stops from its working!

Blessed are they who will give at least a glass of water to the smallest of Mine. I will work by My Spirit and I will make a good means for the protection of My children here; and to the one who helps to My plan, I will do to him the same way I did to the harlot Rahab, who protected and saved My spies that I sent through Joshua to search out the Jericho. And I will work with My servants from here the same way as I worked with the priests who sounded the trumpets that the walls of Jericho fell down, and the Lord gave that city to Joshua and to his chosen people.

My loved child, behold, the faith in this time has to be great, for this is the time when God is uttering an end for transgression, for emptiness and for the spirit of the world, as the world has come to its end. And as the Lord, at the sound of the priests’ trumpets and at the cry of the people, gave the Jericho into the hand of his chosen ones, so it will be in this time, for it is written: *«Rejoice, for the Lord wants it to give you the kingdom»*! Amen.

I sent a letter to the King of Romania to make him glad and strengthen him and make him faithful to the thing that I am working in this time. I let My anointed Michael know and I told him that he would have authority over the nations by My power, and I told him that he would be a holy king as is written into the Scriptures. I told him to live according to My wills and commandments and I let him know about you that I prepare you to be one with him in order to be peace between you, so that we may work in you the thing for the end of transgression and for a new beginning of heavenly kingdom for this land of the New Jerusalem. I told him that I will set My Holy Spirit into the heart of his people, so that this people may love My anointed one.

Behold how much faith has to come up to Me from you; more than from those who by their faith received kingdoms; have tamed the wild animals or closed the mouths of the lions; who put the power of fire out or cast away the hosts of the enemies.

My loved child work for My plan and everything will be for glory and victory. It is not out of My plan what I work with you; the one about you want to know and what to do with it. I cannot work in it as I work in you. I spoke when I brought you to work for Me for a time there too; I told you that I would give My children for your help, the children tested by Me and found faithful. And about the one who was for a little time beside you, behold what I tell you; I tell you a wise word which says: *„Protect me Lord from my friends, as I protect myself from my enemies.”* But I tell you once more this wise word, so that it may be to your understanding, and here is what I tell you: *„Keep away from your friends, as I keep you away from your enemies.”* Amen.

Work with the strong faith and with that which I called her an apostle of Romania, for I want to give her a share up to the end. What is impossible with men is possible with the Lord, My child, and we will be able to do it, and the power from above will be seen.

Peace to you, loved child by God! Peace and power and strong faith, for by the faith worked out in this time and by your good patience we will win multitudes of souls and many nations will be saved. Peace and sweet embrace from the Lord and from His little Mother who covers you with his honored shelter!

Peace to you, my children, my loved ones, the ones who love me! Amen, amen, amen.
