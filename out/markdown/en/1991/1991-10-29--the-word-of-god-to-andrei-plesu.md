The God of the Most Holy Trinity is God. The true One from His very beginning to all over the ages, and the beginning of God is without beginning. Amen.

In the beginning was the Word and God was the Word, and all were made by the word, from eternity, and God works by the word from everlasting to everlasting.

God is the Word and the Word is from God. God, the Word works in Romania, for Romania is the New Jerusalem, according to the Scripture that says: *«I will choose Jerusalem once more»*. Romania is the country of the brightness which is written about in the Scriptures, and the Lord is with the new beginning upon Romania.

I really speak the truth: he, who receives a prophet in the name of a prophet, receives Me, and he who receives Me, receives the Father Who sent Me. Amen. He, who receives the word, receives God, the Word, and he will have a prophet’s reward, and he, who receives a righteous one in the name of a God’s righteous one, receives a righteous one’s reward. Amen. He, who gives a clean water cup to My messengers, I truly speak, will not lose, and that one will have God’s kingdom. He, who hears the word of the truth believing in it, that one will receive the sealing of the Holy Spirit for the fulfilling of the promises, and the Father of the glory, will give him the spirit of revelation for the knowledge of the mystery of the fullness of God’s times.

The God of the Holy Spirit completes the threefold work of the godly Trinity, during this time, for the kingdom of God is the hidden dough within three measures of wheat until its complete fermentation, and behold, the kingdom of God is within this one. This is the promise land for the fullness of God’s glory. Romania is the beginning which comes for the redemption of God’s creature. Blessed is he who believes in the spirits of the heaven, for he will be a son of God’s promises. Amen.

Peace to you from the Lord, Jesus Christ, Who arose from the dead and stands at the right hand of the God of the heavens! Behold, I come for you to open to Me and to know how is My path for those who wear Me. Blessed are you if you open for Me to come in and to have dinner with you, for this heavenly table is lowered from Me upon Romania for a long time. I am. Do not be afraid that I am not. I am; I and My messengers; I and those who wear God, the Word. Behold I and My infants, I arose through them to perform sings and miracles for the new beginning, for the new Israel. I arose to put a sign over Romania and I work hard, for it is the same with them from now, as with those in the time of My body; they hardened their heart and have no way of knowing, the One Who speaks from heaven. Behold, I am, as I promised; I am with the faithful ones until the fullness of the times of the heavens, and I am through the prophets, as God’s order is.

When My Father began His work, He told Moses this: *«Behold, I send you to Israel and you shall be in My place, and Aaron shall be your prophet»*, but Israel did not want to let itself be worked and rose to rebellion instead and struck into the spirit of Moses, and thus Israel perished on the way, for it was a stiff necked people and did not know the time of its visitation, and I swore in My pain, in My wrath; I swore that they would not enter into My rest, and behold, they did not enter into Canaan because of their disobedience, unbelief and hardness of the hearts.

I sent those that spoke for Me in time and not in time, but the man denied God and killed My prophets and crushed God’s messengers, and Israel did not want to come under My wings to be a faithful people and righteous in Me. And I came in the flesh on the earth; I came to the workers of My Father’s vineyard, and they pushed Me out of the vineyard, as they did not want Me, and I went away from them, for they did not know the time of their visitation and forsook the words of the prophets that told them the mysteries of My appearance.

Then I went and performed miracles in the name of My Father to redeem from the world those that were to be with Me in the world, and I took them out of the world, for they were neither from the world, as I was also not from the world. And after I completed My work which I came for, I went to My Father so that I may come to those that I gave birth out of Me, for they were of Me. I went to My Father, for if I did not go, I could not come back to those that were Mine, and if I came back to them, that was the first church of Jesus Christ, but here is a mystery that is not perceived by the wise people of the world, for My church has been all the time a martyr and has had no place on the earth, but only under the earth and only in hiding, and it has been the living church, the true one whose head is Jesus Christ, the son of the godly Trinity, for My Father gave Me to be the head of His church and set Me above all the reigns, rules and powers, above all name which is named in this age and in the age to come.

The teachers and the scribes from the time of My body were not able to receive Me. They were in their own spirits and did not know the time of their visitation. I am the God of those that are poor in their spirit, and I find a pleasant dwelling in them, a spacious and illuminating dwelling, and for those that know My ways and do not walk in them, they who know how is to wear God and are not steadfast in Me, to those the judgment was declared for I said this at that time: *«Woe to you, those who strain out a gnat and swallow a camel! Woe to you, for you rule on the chair of the law and close the door to knowledge and take into your possession the keys of the kingdom of heavens! Woe, as neither you nor the poor can enter with God!».*

Here I am with those that did not ask about Me, and they gave Me a dwelling and let to be with Me and wear Me, as I wear them. Behold, I come to you, for they who are Mine wear Me to you and they who wear Me do My will, and if I tell them to go, they go, and if I tell them to come, they come and do the things from Me, and look, I am with those that are Mine from generation to generation, according to My promises.

I came to dine with you and you with Me. I came to be in you and you in Me. I came; I came for what belongs to Me, as your soul cries out from the earth to Me. I came to sit in counsel with you, but do not deny Me. Receive Me with My peace and let My peace be upon you. And if you do not receive me, let My peace come back to Me. And if you do not receive Me, you will be without peace, for I am a jealous God. Here I am. I am the Word and the Word is God. I am the Word of God, and again, all are worked out by the word, for what it was is still again.

Here I am! I arose to make a new thing, a new beginning, a New Jerusalem in these days. I arose upon Romania, for it is My chosen one from the time of the fullness of God; it is My blessed one and it will be the queen upon the people, for this is how Romania is written into heaven. I arose to prepare the way of My anointed that is from Me. *(The king Michael, r.n.)*. I got up with the entire heaven to work, to make a good way to bring My anointed, but no one wants to know the time of the visitation from these days.

I want to take this fountain above, to make from its spring the spring of life and resurrec-tion, which comes out from God’s throne, according to the Scripture which speaks about the river and the living water, which springs from God’s throne and of the Lamb’s. I have this hidden foun-tain and I sealed this mystery among My servants so that I may come with this spring up to this time, for God, the Word, is from generation to generation, and the Lord is carried by the bearers of God, as the godly settlement is. I was, and here, I am and will be through this heavenly thread, but I was hidden, for the Lord is persecuted and crucified from generation to generation, and great is the reward of those who carry on with the patience of Israel to the end. The Holy Spirit is the crucified God; it is the God of the martyr church, which has had no place on the earth but only under the earth and only in hiding, but look, the word of God cannot be crucified. It hurts Me the denial which comes up to heaven from those who say that serve Me, from they who speak about Me, but do not wear Me in them, for these, like the scribes and the teachers of the law of the time of my body, rule on the chair of the law, but do not take after the One Who was sacrificed for the sins of the world, and behold, Israel does not have Me anymore. The dog knows and keeps and listens to his master who feeds it, but My people do no longer know Me.

Behold, you are a soul from the living soul of this nation; you are the apostle of God’s word, and your spirit is praying speechless and longs after Me. Behold, I got into your way and blessed are you if your spirit burns afterwards, as it is written about those that had dinner with Me in Emmaus, and I will command to be written in front of your name: *«Here is one that I found no ruse in him. Here is one that did not doubt of Me»*. Well, struggle to enter with Me into the room of the mysteries of the fullness of My promises, for many will seek to enter and will not be able to, and they will be told: *«I do not know where you are from!»*, and they will cry when they see Israel’s fathers and all the prophets into the coming kingdom, and others will come and take their places and enter at this table, for it is written: *«The last will be first, and the first last».*

I came out and I took out from this spring and I went to others, but I do not use the earthly door, but rather I enter through the door of the heart. I knocked on the door of many people; I knocked on many with a great soul that are the soul of My Romania, but they did not understand it until the end the time of their visitation, the time of this nation’s visitation, in which My Spirit wants to glorify at this time. Behold, I work with those that are helpless, with those with empty hands, for I found the perfection of My miracles in those that are weak. I work from heaven and I want to show My coming upon Romania, and I want to put a sign over Romania. I work hard to raise a strong pillar, a living stone from which the living water to spring; clean and not troubled water. I work hard to raise a tower of witness to testify about this manger and to proclaim from the top of this stone the name of Romania and the name of the anointed one upon Romania. I want to put back to their places My heavenly times and My holy days, for behold, the abomination of desolation fastened its pole in My holy temple and removed from their places the heavenly days. I will work a great revival and I will pierce the hardened heart of those who do not want to know the time of their visitation, for I am a strong God and I glorify Myself in power and wrath, for it is written: *«Praise the Lord, you sun and moon. Praise the Lord, you fire and hailstone».*

Open for Me to sit in counsel with you. Receive Me to dine with you and to drink from your water, and I will give you the springs of life. Be My disciple in truth; be My relative; be My apostle; be a living soul in the soul of My Romania and love and stand with your heart near My weak ones, near My little ones. Be with your hands clean and let you be born once again, for this day is written in heaven as a day of heavenly joy on the earth. Come by Me; come to see where I pasture My sheep and follow the track of the sheep. I am the Good Shepherd and I get into the way of My sheep. Come to hear My new song, for Romania will be the queen among the nations and a new song will come out from it, and I will set on its walls a sound of a heavenly trumpet and a celebration voice. Behold, I ask you to speak for Me, and if those that are hired will be silent, then the stones will cry out and testify My mystery of this time, and the Lord will complete the work of His plan, made out from eternity.

Ask from Me and I will open the storeroom of My mystery of this time, but I want to come out from hiding, for the days of glory come. Stand by Me, for the rulers of the world do not want to open to My children for the fulfilling of My plan. Do not be afraid. I am, and you will see that I am, but let Me be carried by you so that I may be; to be with My eye, and you will be from heaven. Amen. Ask and you will receive and I will take out and give you to drink and you will know the time of your visitation which is from God in these days.

Peace to you, My child, but let you be My child! Peace to you! Peace to you through the open doors! Peace and a New Jerusalem upon your being, upon your spirit! Peace and a visible kingdom to illuminate in Me by you! And you will be the light and new resurrection upon those that will know My kingdom which is in you. Amen, amen, amen.
