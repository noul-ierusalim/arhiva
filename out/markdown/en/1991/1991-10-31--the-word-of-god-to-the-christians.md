The Holy Spirit breathes from one margin of the heavens to the other. The God of the Holy Spirit breathes over the earth and speaks from heavens and works by the word and the word turns into a deed, and again all work by the word, as in the beginning, for those that were are again and it is worked also by the word. Amen.

The God of the Most Holy Trinity arose to work in this time. He arose to work the new thing which is written in the Scriptures for this time. The Spirit of God completes in these days the threefold work of the Godly Trinity, and behold, He wakes His workers up to the work of this time.

God, the Word, got up to sit in counsel with those that He has from the world; He got up to sound the trumpet, and to bring together from all margins those that He called and to blow over them and to put back to its place the ruined kingdom of Israel, for Israel means God’s chosen people, the faithful people, the called people, chosen and faithful. Amen.

Peace to you! Peace to those that I sit in My counsel with! Peace and heavenly joy to dwell in God’s temple, for God’s temple is the one who carries Me in oneself.

I am the Lamb of God, Who washes away the sins of the world, and I let Myself be worked by those with clean hands, by those that are clean and undefiled, for the Lord is clean and holy. Amen.

I am the true life and there is no truth beside Me and there is no life without Me, and I am clean in the life of those with a true life, of those with a true heart.

When the time came to get out in the way of the Samaritan woman, I looked and saw her clean heart and said: *«Give Me to drink from your water!»*, but My speaking with her was from heaven, and she gave Me to drink from her water, for the water that I drank from her, was her faith in the Son of God, Who came out in her way, as there came the time of her visitation, for I came to search the whole body, the whole soul from which the faith in God sprang. And then the Samar-itan woman woke up and remembered the prophets and if she had a clean heart, she knew the Son of God and remained in Him, and she became an apostle for the proclamation of God’s son, the One That came in the flesh at that time, for that daughter knew the time of her visitation.

When meeting those that were fishing, they did not remain stiff-necked when I told them to throw their nets and catch fish. A night of hard work passed and they did not catch any fish, but they had a big heart, and I saw their inner being; I saw that they were to be with Me from the world and I gave them a sign to strengthen their faith, for casting out their nets at My word, they caught a lot of fish, as the fish were made at God’s word, according to their faith, and they knew the time of their visitation; and then they lay hold of the plow and did not look back and they were found worthy for the kingdom of My Father. Behold, the word is still at work and the Word is God, and the Lord knows those that are with Him from the world, and He takes them out of the world, for they are not from the world, as neither am I from the world. And behold, My Father gives Me those from the world that want to be with Me for the kingdom which is coming. Amen.

Peace to you, for I knocked and you opened for Me and you gave Me to drink and a dwelling in the temple of your hearts.

Peace to you, for I was poor and in want and you sought God! Peace and inseparable unity be among you and among the things that are lowered upon Romania, for Romania is the miracle which will be shown from God upon the peoples on the earth!

Behold the time of the new Passover, for the days of the Lord come My love, and the heart of Israel, which springs now from the earth, will leap under the coming glory and we will celebrate the new Passover and the celebration without evening of God’s creature resurrection. Rejoice and I also say: rejoice for My Father works to give you the kingdom which comes from God in this time!

You are remembered and written on the table of the Holy Trinity, that you are the heavenly witnesses the same way I was a witness at the wedding from Cana, because you are written near the sons that bear the mysteries, of whose witnesses you are. They are love, as I am love, and you should also be love, as they are love, they, whose witnesses you are towards the God of the heav-enly mysteries. Be in the Spirit of the Holy Trinity, for the Holy Spirit is the God Who completes and perfects the threefold work of Godly Trinity. Be My apostles; be My followers for this time. Be workers together with the heaven and get up well to know the time of your visitation which is from God upon Romania, which is blessed among the peoples. Love holiness; be fond of the love, but the love is only one. Be love from the love, for I am the love; I am and I arose to call out and to proclaim the good news of the Lord’s times. I am, do not be afraid. I am. I and My messengers. I and My servants. I and the little children that My Father gave Me for the signs and the miracles, unspeakable in God’s Israel, for Israel is the one who believes in the spirits of the heaven, the spirits of the prophets. Blessed are they who believe in the spirits of the prophets, for they will receive a prophet’s reward. I am, and I will soon reveal My glory. Be faithful until the end so that no one may take My glory from you. I really speak the truth that many will rush to come in at this table of heavenly mystery, but the kingdom which comes was found right only for its sons. Amen.

Peace to you, for the Holy Spirit cries out in the streets to bring together the faithful ones, all that are big in their hearts and they will be put over many things. Amen. Those who are born for Me will overcome the world and the spirit of the world, and there will be the Holy Spirit and of resurrection making upon God’s creature. Be great in your heart, but also be clean and holy in your heart, for the kingdom which is coming will establish holiness and a new earth and redemp-tion from the death, for it shall no longer be death, and we will tread with death upon death and death shall no longer be, and sin shall no longer be, for the sin is death upon My creature, and it shall be resurrection in the truth, and the dry bones shall receive life and there shall no longer be death. Amen.

Peace and heavenly joy and a New Jerusalem to be upon you, as God’s kingdom came down in your being. Let this day be written both in heaven and on earth and let it be a remem-brance day, a day of victory and of a new beginning, for the saints of the heaven stayed in groups and looked to see and to hear the heavenly mysteries of the fullness of the heavenly times. Amen, amen, amen.
