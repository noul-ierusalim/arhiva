I am coming down as word of feast into the midst of My people and from the right side of My Father Sabaoth, I am blessing this day of memorial, when on a day like this, I, the Lord, Jesus Christ, raised to heaven My trumpet after her long sufferance on My way with her, on her way with the people fed for twenty five years with the voice of the word of My mouth, shepherding it against the living with sin in it and advising it to holiness and to great watch for its reconciliation with the Father so that My people may remain on the way of its salvation and not to fall from My book with it, from the table of My teaching, but rather My teaching may yield fruit upon it, as for twenty five years I had been its Shepherd with mercy, and My trumpet Verginica, the same, *(Verginica - the diminutive to her real name: Virginia, r.n.)* and I had gone with her only through pains and fears into the midst of My people. I had dwelt in her body for twenty-five years with My work of word on earth and she had been a bearer of God. As feeble as she was, she had the spirit of gentleness between her and the people coming at the table of My word in the time of persecution for My work with her, for the devil has always persecuted My work of word by those in whom he was working so that he might not lose the victory against My faithful man on earth.

Oh, My people, the saints are looking at Me and at My speaking of today in a day of memorial of My trumpet and the saints are embracing her in the spirit of the heavenly love and of the endless comfort, but her lack of comfort from those left behind her cut the peace of her rest, for when each saint goes to heaven he takes with him the fruit of his joy, brought to Me for the souls redeemed from sin and set on the way of eternal life. When she got near Me, crying being seized with the mercy of her people who were weak in holiness, and in tears she had always asked Me not to let her people go to into the world, into the way of death, for the spirit of the world is My death in man; the world and its spirit is the man’s departure from God.

Oh, it was great My work through this trumpet by which I had been sounding for twenty years on the earth! [legatura_la_teme id_capitol="2" id_subcapitol="4"]Seventy years I had been sounding of the last trumpets in the midst of the Romanian people, where satan played his last card when he saw that I, the Lord, had the Romanian land from the Father as the country of My return and as My trumpet sounding by the last trumpets, those before My coming with the book of the judgment, the book of the Lamb and in it the word of My mouth, My word of today, with which I stood and stand against the sin in man and between man and man, for I have had this work from the Father Sabaoth even from the beginning of the world, of God’s seen and unseen things.

It is written in the Scriptures about the seven trumpets of which God sounds in the end of the time and for seventy years I have been raising on the Romanian land their voice, and I started with the holy temple, *(The Church, r.n.)*, as it is written to be, and a great voice was heard in the temple and it called the people to repentance, for the red beast marked them to be its all the time, and I, the Lord, made a heavy battle against it by messenger angels on earth, and I have always waged a great war on it, for the beast and the false prophet have always marked theirs and spoiled the holy faith and teaching and made war with My saints, blaspheming them and God, as it is written, but My river of word overflew over the earth then in the time of My trumpet Verginica and I, the Lord, stood against the lie that had a godly face and I prepared a victorious walking over the earth with My voice of word, and then the red beast poured out three demonic spirits out of its mouth over the kings of the world to make them stand up to war and to gather them for the day of their fight with Me, as it is written about satan who fights against God, to be overpowered and judged and then to be revealed with its hidden work, by which he wanted to deceive the nations on the earth. However, I will soon come to an end to be victorious and then I will speak: *«It’s done!»* for this is written about the Lamb of God, to be victorious over the red beast now, in the end of the time, the beast which has had control over the kings of the earth and over their Babylon, and about which is written to fall down with a great noise once with My trumpet sounding over the earth, the seventh angel Who will sound the judgment, the Lord, Jesus Christ, the Lamb of the Father Sabaoth. Amen.

Oh, My people of today, I had sounded the sixth trumpet before I opened My book with you on earth, and I had been sounding for twenty-five years and preparing My way by which I came out, and I came out with you, as it is written for the way of My kingdom and of its sons to be prepared, and with whom I am to fight against the kings of the earth, gathering them with one mind by their thought to make war with Me to overcome them by the voice of My mouth, released in the sky over the earth; and moreover, I am to cry out from the temple of the heaven: *«It is done!»* and then to show to the heaven the work by which I overcome satan, the old serpent.

Oh, My people, when the red beast, *(The Communism, r.n.)*, put its name on this Romanian land and started fighting with Me, I, the Lord, woke up My last trumpets by turns and I called out to the people to repent, and once with the word of the beast I also called out against it and against its lie, and I started to call out from the door of the holy temple, waking up the spirit of My servant Joseph, *(The priest Joseph Trifa, the first apocalyptic trumpet, r,n.)* and he called out and wake up many to sing the victorious song of the Lamb, and then I started fighting against the red beast. And I kept on calling out again and again by the other trumpets and then I called out by the sixth trumpet, My Verginica, and I poured out over the earth the river of My word, through which I prepared the way of My kingdom, and then I came and I opened My book, the book of the judgment of the creature, and I worked mysteriously, and I worked into your midst and I work according to the Scriptures, and not otherwise, sons.[/legatura_la_teme]

Oh, My people, with which I had sat at the table of word during all that heavenly time on earth, had always been weak, but I have prepared My way of today and behold, I go, and going I am speaking and fulfilling, and the man’s wisdom will no longer stand against Me, because I am the Word of the Father and it is written to overcome by the word.

[legatura_la_teme id_capitol="2" id_subcapitol="6"]Behold, the convulsion of the beast in agony during My days with you, My today’s people! My whole heaven has been watching for My victory against it and for your victory for you are the faithful one and I sealed you with the seal for the faithful ones, but take great care of your faith, and I have always told you this, for outside is the time of the faith-denial, My people, and behold, the outside church has clothed in red completely and has made a covenant with the beast *(With the communists and their successors to support Mircea Geoană to be president, the representative of the left politics – the communists’ successors, r.n.)* and there is no longer a church on earth now, for it has been filled by its dead, because it gave its control over to the beast *(In the time of the communist dictatorship, r.n.)* and it did not watch when I called it from the throne of My word upon it for its awakening before the Lamb Who comes.[/legatura_la_teme]

Oh, we have to overcome the spirit of the world with heavenly weapons, My people. We have to establish fast and living prayer between evil and good for we have to bring together in My Spirit those who are faithful and holy in their faith, and then to show them the way of holiness, My way with man, My walking on the earth by those who are faithful and holy and to make them the sons of My Father. My work with you is mysterious and those who are proud cannot understand it, but those who are humble in their heart and who are faithful for Me, they understand it and will understand it more and more, because it is written that the wisdom will grow and will overflow and many will understand and will stand up and, again, many will not understand and will not be able to understand My work with you on earth. However, I work it mysteriously and I fulfill it page by page and I have you as My faithful one and I work by your faith, by your power, for everything is possible with Me and with those who are faithful, and only in this way I have worked from the beginning of man on earth, and My work has always been mysterious, in order to be able to fulfill it.

Oh, I am sitting in counsel with you about My work with you, about My trumpet sounding of seventy years *(1921-1991, r.n.)*, and about the war I have been making against satan in order to be the little and victorious Lamb, and those in heaven hear and see fulfilled Scriptures for the end of the time and the saints and angels rejoice over My victory, My people. My trumpet left the great word to Me on this day and now she is speaking with you and is strengthening you to watch. [legatura_la_teme id_capitol="6" id_subcapitol="1"]Oh, watch, watch for My work with you, My people, for you are small but I am great with you on earth. Listen now to the voice of My trumpet upon you and do not grow weak in your watching, but sing instead, sing the song of victory, for the heaven sits to listen to you and to rejoice over Me and over you, and let the heaven have comfort, My people. Amen.

– I am sounding the trumpet, Lord, and I am speaking to the people to listen to Your speaking with it, to Your joy, mine and of Your saints, and I am inviting my people in my day of feast among saints to put on great holiness with every passing day more and more, for You have to overcome with the saints on the earth for the saints in heaven, who have waited with longing and sufferance for the redemption of their blood, given for his faith in You to the man on the earth and for their love of You, Lord.

Oh, people from the spring, wake up the watch and the faith in all those who were at the table of the word of God in the time of this work; wake up those who left from the earth, too, and those who are still on it and tell them to stand watch and in holiness, so that it may be a good victory for them too, for each one prepares his little garment for the wedding table of the Bridegroom, Jesus Christ, the Bridegroom of the Romanian people, Who has His table laid now on the hearth of this people so that wedding faithful and holy guests may come to it and to overcome for the Lamb of the Father and with Him, to their salvation from Him.[/legatura_la_teme]

Oh, sons, be sheep with a shepherd, be sheep and shepherd, and do not be otherwise, for I have laid my life as a sacrifice all the time on earth so that the Lord’s people may be likewise. Write and learn the work of the sheep with a shepherd, the work of the sheep and of the shepherd, because this work has to be seen with you. Where there is no shepherd there are no sheep either, and where there are no sheep there is no shepherd as well, and this is all over the earth; there are no sheep because there is no shepherd and there is no shepherd because there are no sheep, because this is a holy and great mystery, and it comes from heaven and is seen on earth, and the people do not love to be sheep and they do not love to be shepherds, and the people do something else and do not want to hear from heaven from the Lord, Who calls them to instruction from heaven, and behold, whoever does not learn does not know what he does.

[legatura_la_teme id_capitol="6" id_subcapitol="1"]The Lord has you as His marvel on earth among the people now, loved people, and the Lord does not have something else on earth now, but satan has a great kingdom in man and all over the earth, but God’s kingdom is with power and is with those who are faithful and no one can prevail against it, for it is not visible and that is why it is. Be the kingdom of God, each one of you, because only it will remain on earth, only God and everything that is not His dwelling place on earth, all things that are not with Him will burn.

[/legatura_la_teme][legatura_la_teme id_capitol="6" id_subcapitol="3"]Take care of those who want to cleave to the Lord’s mystery with you. Teach everyone that the one who has God’s kingdom in him is a great mystery. Teach them that the gold thrown into the mud does no longer have any name; it does no longer have any price. Teach them to keep this mystery, teach the one who loves to be with the Lord on earth to keep himself in God, and give them the power to believe this, for behold, I give them. The angel Rafael was a mystery on his journey with the faithful one, and this work has to be known that is between earth and heaven with those who are faithful, either the man wants it or not, for God’s mysteries on earth are angels with bodies and without bodies, and the angels are mysteries and serve the bodies of those who are faithful and their dwelling spirits in bodies.

Oh, do not forget the holy speaking and full of mystery among you, for the angels of the heavenly mysteries serve you then, and do not forget to love the work of the church with a synod, and work only in this way, only with the angels, and have nothing, nothing that cannot be confessed in you churchly, and you should punish the works of the darkness with their whole darkness, like the work of the devil which does not love the light, by hiding from it.

Be the heaven of the Lord on earth, where there is nothing but light. Learn this new kingdom, the kingdom of the light, for those who do not love to be so, they will cry because of their darkness, for those that are hidden are evil for this, and those that are kept in the light belong to those who are clean, clean in them and among them for one another, and hypocrisy proves out those who work it. This is how I am teaching my people on my day of feast, and in order that it may work the work of the light, let each one know that it becomes word and only in this way the light is. Your word is your light son, and if it is not word, then it is not light. I am speaking about this light to you, that is the word about you always in your mouth, about your light, and the Lord’s light is that which takes all things in consideration and all that are and are made.

Oh, my word is with grief in it because I see it from heaven and I urge each son to become his word for the mystery of the clean heart between brother and brother, for the spirit of faithfulness in you, sons of the holy word. Oh, the Lord becomes His word, and in this way He appears to you. Do like Him and like His work, and be like Him with your work, for the Lord is the One Who speaks His word and that is why it is, and He appears that He is and what He is. He who speaks, that one is, and he who does not speak is hidden in his own body. Oh, keep away from those who try to hide in you and among you and watch for the light of all things and for the whole of its work, for this is how God’s sons on earth are and they stay in the whole light. Amen.

Oh, be open with those that are in you, for those who have robe upon robe will be brought into the light and will no longer be able to dress. This is how you are to teach those who come to stand before the Lord with their walking on my way with you. Take care, sons, so that they may not have robe upon robe on them, but let them choose the light instead, and let it be their little garment with the Lord on earth. I exhort them to learn the work of the sheep with a shepherd, for the sheep without a staff upon them fall on the way of the wolves and they do not have another destiny, and the wolves have robe upon robe and do not reveal themselves to be known.[/legatura_la_teme]

Oh, in a little time you have to set the table of memorial for the shepherd Daniel and the people will come together at the table, sons from the spring, and the Lord will give him of the light of the teaching for the sons, and no matter how many they may be, let them learn the work of sonship, for the kingdom of the light is coming on the earth, sons.

Oh, Lord, you are word, and that is why You are Who You are. Lord, what is he who does not become his word? Oh, what is he? Teach Your kingdom with light to those who want to be sons, Lord, and they will be like You then. Amen.

– Oh, Verginica, oh, My trumpet, you have spoken a word of mystery to your people. The sufferance of those in heaven is great because they see from heaven. Oh, the people will come together at the table of word for the memorial of the shepherd Daniel and I will embrace within the word the people that came together. My word struggles to teach to the way of the light all those who hear My voice to their salvation, but those who do not become light in them do not know who their salvation has been worked.

[legatura_la_teme id_capitol="6" id_subcapitol="1"]Oh, My people, My word is My light, the light within Me. When you become your word, then you are the light within you, your inner man, who comes out to be seen with those in him. He who works in this way is the one who is seen, and he who does not work like that does not want to be seen.

Let everyone understand the mystery of the heaven with the man and let the man learn to be and to give himself over to the heaven on earth, for the mystery of the saints in their own body and outside of their body is great, and they are the visible power of the Lord in them because of their light, and blessed are those pure in heart, for they will see God and will not be afraid of those in them before Him, but rather they will be the happy ones, the happy ones, My people. Amen, amen, amen.[/legatura_la_teme]
