I stopped marriage and defilement at Christians. Through this I will distinguish them from the vain world which defiles itself to death. Who does not wash his/her little coat, will not be able to get in to the Lord. In the world neither feasting nor prayer is it used anymore, but only fornication. There shall be bodies which will lie, and the soul will not come out.
