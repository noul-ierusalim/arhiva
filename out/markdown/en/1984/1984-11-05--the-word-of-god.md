…Christians, think at the sufferance that will come on bodies. Man cannot endure tooth-ache, but what will he do when he suffers for each sin? The world that lives in fornication and drunkenness will go through torments and will call for death, but I will not send it until I wash away the furrow of sin. I look and see over all the earth. I see children of a short span with ciga-rette in their mouth. Women and men compete in drinking.
