[legatura_la_teme id_capitol="3" id_subcapitol="4"]… Look, the servant of the little church from this locality is flaunting against Me and against the sons of My work, but I will give him sufferance and I will double it until his head reaches his feet. I have sent My word to lift him up and to strengthen him but he took a satanic way, to do harm to Me.[/legatura_la_teme]

… Sons, you do not know, but I know. satan is grubbing through all hearts, but I will soon cleanse his name and of his lovers. Stand by Me and you will see great things. [legatura_la_teme id_capitol="1" id_subcapitol="4"]When I brought Noah into the ark, he did not see the flood. And I will do with you the same.[/legatura_la_teme] Even if the earthquake comes over your little houses, it will not tear them down for My strong rock is with them. Then you will see the wonder.

… All of you be careful, for the enemies want to destroy all the people of God. Behind Me, satan! What are you looking for, where it is not yours? Is it not enough for you how many you brought to your likeness?

Sons, I have always told you that all I do over you is for the smashing of the enemy.
