There is no other greater miracle than this one on earth. God has got all kinds of miracles on the entire earth, but it is not like here, where the Spirit Himself of the One Who spread out His hand on the cross comes down. [legatura_la_teme id_capitol="8" id_subcapitol="5"]I told you that a heavenly wedding is being prepared within the entire holy heaven. As in a family the bridegroom and the bride prepare long before the day of the wedding, the same way I prepare you, My people. I have set the tables and I left on earth for the guests to prepare them according to My will. And I have wedding guests in heaven as well. The heaven is full of wedding guests but no one enters to the wedding until I bring My wedding guests from the earth. [/legatura_la_teme]
