Today is a day of honor. The Lord meets Israel and Israel meets the Lord.

Peace upon Israel! Peace to you, My dear Israel!

In the name of the Father and of the Son and of the Holy Spirit, peace to those from above and to those from below, as Israel from above, the one of the grace, from above, has the Lord into its midst in coming down for a heavenly dinner into your midst, Israel that meets the Lord today. And I will come on the clouds with a great festivity and Israel will meet Me.

Romanian Israel, behold, I come into your temple, I come into your heart. What does your heart do, child of My people? I announced the children of My people to prepare the house of their hearts beautifully to receive the Lord, to meet the Lord. Never mind if it is cold outside. It is cold outside and this cold is sent by Me to speak in the cold with My people Israel. Behold, My loved people, behold what a meeting gave the Lord to you. Did you get upset, son? Behold, I breathe the words of the Holy Spirit over you, child that are frozen and chill. It is cold in the house of your heart, child of the time of My coming on the clouds. It is cold, child, and I have no place to bow down My head under your roof. I came to have mercy and compassion over you. I came to keep your warm, people numb on the way to those that are in heaven, to warm you up, son, at the bosom of the glory of My word and to guide you to the warmth, as I am the One Who warms up your heart. Behold today the voice of My word that came down for you at the manger of My word, for you, to let it upon you today, to warm you up, as it is cold son; and let us warm up one another in the time of the cold.

Here is My voice today. Let no one harden his hearing at My voice. Son, you should not do as Israel did, who has hardened his heart for forty years, and he is still the same now, as I forsook him because of this callosity, but look, I have an Israel for the redemption of the one at that time. I promised you to be, Israel; to be Israel in this time and to give My hand through you to the whole creature that was, and still is, and to redeem through you the lost one from My face. You shall not be unfaithful about this prophecy that I tell you again, as I told it to you long ago that it would be to come true. You should believe the prophecies and not denigrate them, as [legatura_la_teme id_capitol="1" id_subcapitol="6"]the old Simeon made God angry when he did not believe the prophecy, which wrote about the Holy Spirit and about the Virgin, who was to give birth to Immanuel and still was to remain Virgin; however, he wanted to wipe this prophecy out, but My angel added it into the book again, and again he hardened his heart and erased these words from the page of the book, but the words were unfolding again into their place in the book, and then I became angry and I declared upon him: *«You shall not die until this Virgin is born, so that you may see her then, and also to see the Lord that will come out of her».* And the old Simeon lived four more times over the life of a man and was no longer dying. But it came after three hundred years the time of the Virgin who was born and grew up and she gave birth to the Lord, Jesus Christ; and Simeon saw the fulfillment of the prophecy that he could not believe, and then, burdened by time, was forgiven by God.[/legatura_la_teme]

You should believe My words, Israel, as I have not lied to you with anything of those that I have been telling you for forty years, and behold My voice upon you. It is cold outside, My love; it is winter with cold. You should not flee from My warm arm. It is cold in heaven too, as the heaven is cold without you, Israel from the end. The Lord is cold without you, and you should no longer wander away from your Lord and from the Holy Spirit. Do no longer wander away, My loved Israel, as I do not want to see you crying, without comfort and without a shelter. I am your shelter, and you should stay with your Lord. Amen. In the name of the Father and of the Son and of the Holy Spirit, I tell you to stay with God, you people of God, people with a heavenly seal, taken from the table of this work of godly word.

Come near, My people. May the ear of your heart and the house of your heart be blessed today, so that the house of your heart, My temple in you, may be welcomed by the voice of My word! You should stay well awake with your heart and pray to Me, so that I may give you faith to understand with it the voice of My word. Amen.

May the entire heart of the Lord’s little Israel be blessed, so that it may eat usefully and the little Israel may grow up and breed sons for the time of the promise, for I come back on the clouds, as it is written into the Scriptures.

Peace and blessing over the supper of My word in the midst of My people! Peace to you, sons, and I let you a spirit of wisdom upon you today! Remain into My Spirit. Take and eat; this is My word. I am the Word, children of My people. Be careful with your spirit as My Father is with Me into your midst.

Verginica has come with Me, the Lord, into the midst of her people:

*— Peace to you, children! Take the exhortation of the Lord; be careful with your spirit. It is hard sons, because you did not stay well with the Lord by now, and it is hard now to understand those from heaven. But be careful with the spirit! Remember the Lord, Who told to His tempters from that time who did not understand anything from heaven and to whom He said this:* «If you do not understand those from earth, how shall you understand those from heaven?».

Behold, the Lord is speaking today with you about those from heaven, those that were from the beginning. Be careful with your spirit. Receive the word of the Lord and make the word in you be warm. Those that the Lord brings to you are from heaven and not from earth. The Lord is speaking as in heaven, not as on earth; this speaking is not as that by now.

Behold, I breathe warmth into you. Let it be a warm manger in you, that the word may come in you and that it may be warm to it. Do not be cold; do not be unfaithful. I am here with the Lord, children. Be careful with your spirit and grow into the Holy Spirit, as it comes the time of the heavenly miracles worked by the Lord upon you. See that these miracles that come may not crush you, as the miracles, which will speak from the Lord about this people who ate from heaven, come. I was the teaspoon that the Lord fed you with, and I have a prepared place on the earth where I come with the Lord for the people with a holy name. Behold, I have come with the Lord into your midst, and I have come with the faithful and obedient who watch at the manger of the heavenly word. Prepare your little coats, as soon the gates of the Lord’s glory and of the sons of the glory that come with the Lord will be lifted up; as the Lord is coming sons; He comes in a visible way, my children, and all of you will see the Lord, and will see me with the Lord at you. Let me not find you in the cold and let me find you into the house, into the faith, children, and into the obedience of those that come to you from heaven.

Mother Gigi is touching every son of this people who came to meet the Lord. This is to-day’s feast of my people. It is a day of welcome. Peace to you, my loved Israel! Be careful with your spirit and I say again: be careful with your spirit, child nourished by God.

Peace to you, to those that are gathered together at the heavenly supper! This is a place of Emmaus for the Lord, the place of the food through the word, as the Lord is the Word. Amen, amen, amen.
