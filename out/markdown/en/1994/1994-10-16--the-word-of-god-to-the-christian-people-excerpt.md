Behold My loved Israel, why the heavens have to perish and to be rolled up into their first place. That is why: so that the glory of the Lord may be revealed on you, those who are set in My love; so that your Creator may be seen by the eyes of your soul; and so that you may also see where your soul proceeds from, from what glory it is given to you as a gift, from what part it is broken and put into your body, and then your body will be only heavenly, for your soul will show to it to be able to see those that are gifted. Then you will see the bodiless nature and the indescribable glory, which is prevented now from being seen. Take heart Israel and do not be afraid, for I give you a heavenly command not to be afraid and to have a quick eye of your soul that you have from Me. I sit a little bit in counsel with you and I will not be long, but you need to be quick and sharpen your heavenly sight by My godly command, because I commanded you not to be afraid. Rejoice but do not be afraid, for I have always come and told you Israel; I told you everything beforehand, but I also told you to get up well and to strengthen yourself and sit well, and here is why I have told you this; so that you may not be afraid in the time of fulfillments.

[legatura_la_teme id_capitol="2" id_subcapitol="4"]… Open again the book of this prophecy, for as the books of Moses, Isaiah, Jeremiah, Matthew and Paul remain, likewise, Verginica’s book will also remain, Israel that is My book through her, for I was in her body as seed is in the kernel. He who wants to eat the seed in a kernel, that one has to open up the kernel to eat its seed. This is how you are to believe into My Holy Spirit, covered by Verginica’s body. The same in you, child from Israel, I find shelter in you likewise; that is to be in you and you to be in Me, but not only I in you, but you in Me as well. I do not want to stay idle in you, My people, for I was working in Verginica, and I made her into a rake, hoe, pickaxe, scythe, sickle, axe and I also made her into a drinking and eating vessel and a heavenly thread to speak My word through; I also made her into a sun but why did you not see its light, little people in the eyes of your soul? Behold sons, I have a prophetic word into your midst and I have a garden with prophets in it and I also have My word in it, and this is how you should also be, Israel; you should be one single body so that My Spirit and My Body may join with you in one single body, for My mystery is great into your midst,[/legatura_la_teme] and it hurts Me because you do not see it and because you were not worthy to know it and to be able to carry it, and behold, I come to you and I tell you: Israel get ready, for the heaven will be put aside and I will remain uncovered above you, and if you are only flesh you will melt away under the light of My glory.

… Israel son, the cover of My glory is coming undone, and the angels will sound the trumpet for the end of God’s mystery. Oh, Israel, many in you doubt and stay hunting and many of My people are not supposed to stay like that. But the few ones, who keep watch before Me, have a prophesying gift from Me, and I work out with them the fulfillments, which are to be accomplished for the appearance of those who are not seen by the flesh but only by the soul; they are seen by the inner eyes, which are called the eyes of faith in those that are not seen. The world is fleshly nature, but you, Israel, should have a spiritual nature, son, to sing together with the bodiless angels. Israel, you have seen that I became flesh as much as you are and I showed you what power the God’s soul in man can have when I told you: *«I have power to give up My soul and I have power to take it back».* This is the power of the soul, which dwells in the body, and I have to see this work in you, children from Israel. I have saints who were in the flesh on the earth, both from among children and from among young and old men, and the age of the spirit and the power of the soul were also taken into consideration in My case as well, and you will not be spared because you were a child or young or old, if you want to be vindicated, for it is written: *«The sons of this age marry and are given in marriage, but those who are worthy to be God’s sons do no longer marry or are given in marriage and they cannot even die, but rather they are like angels in heaven»*. This is the way and your standing, my loved people. It is time for you to understand who you are, and it is time to understand it willingly, for otherwise, you will soon be afraid and want to run, but then the road will turn into a scroll and you will have no way where to go. I am the way, but when I come to the end, I will stand there and go with those things from the future, and you will inherit fear, if you remain now, if you try to change your way and work from on it.

…You should be one spirit with Me, to be able to stay into you, child from Israel, to have a dwelling in you, son, and to strengthen you in your dwelling, in your body, in My temple in you. May all things perish son, but only you not perish, for you will bring everything into incorruptibility, you will bring them all to their first place, for I am in the work with you, and behold, I make the man again. This work is God’s kingdom, it is the place and the time where the man is being made, the man from heaven, worked out by My word, by the work of My word. And the day of rest comes then, and Romania will be a day of rest for Me and for you; Romania will be the day of the Lord.

Blessed be the land of Romania, and the heaven of Romania, and its heavenly body. Blessed be the day of Romania’s resurrection. Blessed be the work of My word over Romania. May you be blessed, My people Israel, by which everything is being make during this time, and those that are being made, are made invisibly, so that they may be seen worked out in the time of seeing of those that are not seen.

… I bless you with health of spirit and body. Repent of your past sins and do no longer look to them and do no longer come down from heaven, My people, as the woe is coming and you are weak and not faithful. Let Me keep you into My hand, for I do not want to lose you. And you do not want it otherwise too. Do not be afraid, and remember Me in the time of fear, and I will be with you and you will not perish, Israel. The time for the fulfilling of the prophecies is coming, and you shall not do like those of Nineveh, for I sent Jonah and the city repented, and after a while it pondered that I, the Lord, was lying, if the city did not perish, and they indulged again in revelries and pleasures, and then I gave that city over to perishing. Not so, Israel; hold on to Me, son. Do not listen to the whispers outside My work, and stay inside and pull the door behind and be perfect and clean and holy. Your holiness is the fulfillment of those prophesied by now. Your holiness, Israel, will work the path for the fulfillments of the incorruptible age, and the flesh will speak then and confess you, the one who are waited, as it is written that the nature waits for the revealing of the sons of God.

May you be blessed, My son Israel, and stay around the candle, with the cup of oil, with the clean candle and with a living heart of faith and peace! I will be with My glory above you, and the people of the sons of God will be revealed to you. Amen, amen, amen.
