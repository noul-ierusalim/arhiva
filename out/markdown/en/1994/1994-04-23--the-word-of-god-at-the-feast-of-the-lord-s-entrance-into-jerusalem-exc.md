My voice is speaking over the manger of My word. I am the Word. This is My name; the Word of God is My name for My second coming. **(Apoc: 19/13.)** Let My word come down and let it be spread all over and then let it be put into work. Amen.

You, those who watch within the meeting of My voice, work father, and give body to the voice of My word. Peace to you, and set down to give being to My word in this book, for I am this book, I, within spoken word, so that the word spoken by God may be fulfilled. Amen.

… Oh, children of the word, what a great glory would appear from this work of the word coming down from heaven, if I was received and known by those who are Mine within their con-fession, within the right faith, within the only one holy synodal and apostolic church. I have sent you with My word from city to city, and I will go with you to the day of rest and we will rest from all our toils, for I will let Myself be seen, and My glory will catch all the unbelief under it. I do not work anything without letting it be known first, as this is how the godly work has been done all the time, because it is written: «I do nothing without letting it be known», for I do not want to work without it being known.

I am the Lord of mercy and compassion and behold, I sound the trumpet in the way of the appearance of My glory, for the watchmen of the church to hear so that I may not work without making it known. May the preaching of My word be blessed from place to place, the preaching of the feast of the coming back of the glory on the clouds of glory, as it is written into the Scriptures.

… I had to come above with this heavenly spring, with this river of life, which has come back to its river bed, to the bed of the river of life, so that all the creature may get use to its coming back, to its coming back to Me, and so that there may no longer be death and iniquity, and that is why I make use of you, who carry My word, to give the news to all the crea-ture that God comes with His things, and all of His things to keep watch and set to the life without death.

… Behold, children, your rulers blame you; they blame you for pride and advise you to forsake the word and let My voice above you which is coming upon you above the manger of My word. They blame you for pride, but I speak the word saying: oh, only if every creature may fall into those that you have fallen! For you have fallen into the arms of the love of the holiness and heaven and eternity with Me. This life is a duty and it is even more than that before God. Those who truly love God cannot really blame you, but look; I do no longer see faith and light with the sons of the people. The sons of the people do not look after God’s sons to receive the testimony about Me and to believe in Me. Oh, there is no more fear of God, for the wisdom, which comes from man, has deceived even the children of faith, children, and the sons of the faith perish be-cause of lack of wisdom which comes down from heaven. But did I not promise this grace of the Spirit of Truth, Which the world does not know? And behold, not even the sons of faith know Him within His work. And Israel, after the flesh, will raise and receive the baptism of the faith from Jesus Christ, and you, Christian people, what will you do, if you do not know the One Who is professed within you, the Son of Man, Who came to redeem you from the lawlessness and also to redeem the nature fallen through sin? What will you do, Christian, if you do not get up to stand well and to stand in fear and carefully before the One coming on the clouds to you? For behold, the two ages, the Scriptures has been speaking about, have passed, and I will let My-self be seen, as it is written. Amen.

[legatura_la_teme id_capitol="4" id_subcapitol="5"]… May Romania be blessed! The country of My returning to those of Mine, the country of the trumpet sounding of the word of God over the faithful children of all the nations of the earth. And a new heaven and a new earth will come down on the earth, created out of My word, and all the nations of the earth will see a great light and be baptized, for I am the Lord, and I am faithful to My promises. And the fallen Israel will come to life, and on seeing the light, it will receive the testimony of the salvation from Jesus Christ, for My Father does not receive anyone without Me. And Israel will be grafted, and it will be a new creature and will do the work of New Jerusalem for all are being made new by the word, and they are being worked out again by the word. In the beginning God made the heaven, the earth and the man, and now it is also been worked by word, and a new heaven, a new earth and a new man are being made, in God’s image and after His likeness, likewise God’s image and likeness was in man in the beginning. But behold, much labor is needed in heaven and on earth, for the New Jerusalem is coming down from heaven and no one understands this mystery in its work.[/legatura_la_teme]
