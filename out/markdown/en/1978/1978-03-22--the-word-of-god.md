Israel, I have toiled every way possible with you, but behold, the end has come and I have not succeeded all that I wrote into the book to be fulfilled. Israel, sons, I have told you that you will be the salt of the earth and I have been seeking salt in your heart, but I have not found any. Israel, the name of Israel will not be extinguished, as it is written into the book that if one remains, it is called Israel.

… Flee from the world, for the world is dying. Untie yourselves from the thread of the world, for the world is dying! And search your head so that there may not be any thread from the world, for the world is dying together with its priests and archpriests! The world is dying! The world is dying! The world is dying! Sons, run away from the world! It will no longer be known where the world was.
