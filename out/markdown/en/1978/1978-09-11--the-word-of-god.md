… The Lord, Jesus Christ, did not despise the wood of the cross, and it has remained sanctified by the Blood of the Lord, Jesus Christ. And the spirit of Zacchaeus was sanctified by His word when Zacchaeus wanted to see Jesus.

… There is no priest, there is no prophet and there is not a prophecy that the gun might have not fired at. This moment in which you are, is the last one, when it is written that in the last times, I will come back to you and will take the one who is Mine to Me. I will separate the sheep from the goat; I will separate the lamb from the wicked sheep. Do not depart from Me, for the time is coming when the iron would be broken, the stone would be broken, the wood would be broken and you would not be broken if you are with Me.

… The entire breath will be punished. Only those who are My chosen ones will be saved. And you should know that many will knock at the door and will call out for you, as in the time of Noah.

… I have scattered severe wounds over the world, for multitudes of people who hate you and Me will perish in severe pains and three thirds will perish, and then everything will perish. And even what remains will perish if it does not repent. The Lord protects you, but as soon as you get out of My fold, you will have caught the infection already; you will get sick and die. The Lord speaks. Let he who has hearing ears hear.
