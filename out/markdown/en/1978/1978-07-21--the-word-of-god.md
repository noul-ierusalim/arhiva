… Who has left from Me into the world, will perish with the world. Even if he wanted to come back then to Me, he will no longer be received, because he was told more than once. He will be easily forgiven for his sin, but because he hated Me and did not listen to Me in order to separate himself from the sin, for this he will not be forgiven.

… There is one more step until tribulation and you will fight with your weapon in your mouth and in your heart and no one will see this weapon that it is in your heart. Christian, do not bind to the world as the devil has never been so empowered as nowadays. And I ask of you to love the manna which gives you power, at least three times a week, for it is healing. *(The Holy Communion, r.n.)*

[legatura_la_teme id_capitol="2" id_subcapitol="6"]… Do not expose, neither yourselves nor the pearls from you, for the devil has dressed both with the servant and with the beggar clothes, with the king and handler clothes, with the priest and holy clothes too.

[/legatura_la_teme][legatura_la_teme id_capitol="3" id_subcapitol="4"]… My little children, the evil spirits dress in holy garments. When the priest is gone to the unclean place, at sea, *(To the beach, r.n.)* the evil spirit is taking the garments and is doing the service to strengthen the devilish work.[/legatura_la_teme]
