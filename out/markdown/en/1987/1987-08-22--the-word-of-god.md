[legatura_la_teme id_capitol="8" id_subcapitol="5"]… I come down in all confidence that I will also pick up something for happiness. It is as you come down in a garden and look at the planted flowers and pick up one from here and one from there and make a bouquet of flowers for yourself that you like to have on your table. The same is also with Me; I would also like to pick up from the earth only the one that I like, only the one I see that I could make him worthy to sit at the table with God, for God lets him know that at the same time with the end of this age, the wedding of the heavenly Lamb is being pre-pared, and He left on the earth for the guests. He does not invite to the wedding the learned or the rich ones or those who do not want God. He calls out all those who love God.[/legatura_la_teme]

… The entire humankind on earth is like in a spider’s web of perdition. This spider of greed fills the entire hell with God’s building.

The earth is full to the brim with the fire weapons. When they break out, they will turn the earth upside down.
