… Oh, My people, the holy people will cry because of the time that is coming. There will be no longer sons and daughters that will not defile themselves. The garment and feasting in your house will be spoiled. The cross will be spoiled. Even Sunday will be spoiled. These in your house bear a sign and this is the sign of the antichrist.

… Christians, keep a check on you, children, for God decided that no child will be born. Come to your senses for the devil is fighting that in your house may be a traitor as well. The devil is fighting to destroy the holy faith. Do not rejoice, children, over ties and badges, *(Of communist pioneer, r.n.)* for these are the signs of the antichrist. Do not rejoice over the clothing of this world. Be perfect! Pray to God to change the leadership.
