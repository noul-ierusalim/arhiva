… Israel, well Israel, sons, I want to make you My disciples. I want to make you soldiers of war, but not with iron weapon, rather with invisible weapons. I want to make you My little angels. I want to make you My apostles. I want to make you My prophets. I want to make you My disciples so that no one of you may be left without holy work.

… Israel, son, the Christian of today does not resemble the Christian in the past. The nowadays Christian is like a little stalk, cut with a saw, that is God cut off the old flesh in order that a new body may spring, but instead of springing the new body, there started to spring branches of the old flesh, all that was in the past.

… Sons, find the holy little coat, find the right little coat. You find it in the Scripture not in the shops, not at the people of today. If you go to an earthly man and ask him about life, he does not know for he is blind. My little children, who sought the life at a man, he got lost. Who sought the life in the Scripture, he found it.

… Who does not want to be with Me, no one binds him and no one forces him to, but you are bound not to hold the breath of the trumpet. It will be seen later all that God has done thorough the Holy Spirit on earth, for it is a building, invisible by the earthly eye, and when you see it, you will come to look for Me, but your unbelief and crazy deeds will prevent you.

… Oh, Christian, I need you as a man needs a bricklayer at your house, or of a craftsman to work something in his work, or as a man needs a servant in his courtyard. I need you and no one will be without work.

… God requires of his people not to look where it came from, not to care about his body anymore, as this is what the devil urges him, to look on the table to see what he eats, to look that he has got no more cattle and to look that he has got no more wealth. Your fathers and forefathers sold the wealth and went into the wilderness and clothed with poverty, but because today there is not more place into the wilderness, as it got under the possession of the emperor of today *(The Communist Dictatorship, r.n.)* and the cave, and the hollow, and the wilderness, and the plain, and the hill, everything is in his possession, learn well so that you live here too.

… My little child, truly believe for there is not coming any other Christ to you, there is not coming any other mercy to you. Come to your senses, sons. Listen well to the word from Me. Do not take the bodies to the garbage pit, and unite the bodies with the spirits, for the body taken with sins, cannot rise again into the heavenly kingdom. Have you heard?

… Christians, there came from earth a big lie, *(The communism, r.n.)* which everybody listens to; the infants and the old people as well. By this lying all breath perishes. Do not believe sons the lie. By lying you killed your spirit from God. Arius lied and God punished him. Every liar is punished forever. God punished all who took out other gods for you, as the Lord told you and wrote into the book, for in heaven there was a big stubbornness at Lucifer, and God threw him down from heaven. The same is with lying today which always knocks into the gate of God so that it may lose all of God’s things. In the beginning was the Word, and now the Word is too. Daniel, in the beginning was the word. Have you heard something like this? There was the Spirit too. Do not think, man, that you will fight with Me, for God made by his word everything and you cannot rise against it, no matter how strong you believe you are. Come to your senses My believers. Learn the faith. Do not be poor and weak in faith anymore, for it is not as the world says.

… Christian, you will see the destruction of the world, and you will see the flight of the world and you will see the crying of the world. [legatura_la_teme id_capitol="6" id_subcapitol="2"]If you are with Me, Christian, and if you are with the light into the hand, to the thousandth generation will you escape your people. You will say: *„Lord, here is my mother, here is my son”*, and God will fulfill your word and will tell you: *„Chose your people and you will redeem it, but by prayer.”* [/legatura_la_teme]

… Do not covet the worldly meals. Your fathers and forefathers were separated from the world. Many did not want to look a womanly face.

… Romania, Romania, Romania! Do not give up, Romania! I have built a wall in you and it is not finished. Romania, do you really not know the commandments and the wonders? Romania, Romania, how you are trampled! *(By the communism, r.n.)* Why has this country gone weak in the spirit? Why Christians, My little children, why all wish to visit this country? Why all want to become friends with this country? Of all the countries that visited, all got frightened that in Romania is harshness for prayer. Romania was the cleanest and the most beautiful country.

… My treasures, how I will gather the little patches from the earth and the little flowers and how I will take the fruits from earth and take My fruit tree, which today God is building! After this time, the time is changing, is changing sons. If you were perfect, I would pour out all My mysteries in front of you, but as I saw the treachery in the past, I cannot son, tell anymore.

… My flowers, you are not alone, you are accompanied. And you, woman, and you, man, you are accompanied by heavenly persons. Do not commit any more sins thinking that no one sees you. You are never to be alone on earth. I willed the holy angels and My Mother and even the cherubim and seraphim to accompany you and know that if you get separated from the companions it will be hard of your head.
