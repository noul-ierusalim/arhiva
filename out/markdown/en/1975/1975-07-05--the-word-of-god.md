… My little children, I said so: when it will be darkness on earth, you will be the light. When there will be no more salt on earth, you will be the salt. My little children, there will be a king over all world. This is an earthly one but another one is coming, a spiritual one.

… Keep in mind, Christian, that it is written in God’s law about the one who is a Christian today, the age of this day is finished with him. You are the key.

… The floods and all that happen are from drinking; they are from the fornication and smoking of this world. My children, God’s wrath has spread above the world. God has decided great floods and desolation to destroy this world. This has already been seen; it is not only a sign. Do not cry over what is happening to this world, do not cry over this Babylon. Beware, for the time is coming for you to go to sleep, and when you wake up, you will no longer know. You will look to see your neighbor’s place and you will no longer know it. Have no fear.
