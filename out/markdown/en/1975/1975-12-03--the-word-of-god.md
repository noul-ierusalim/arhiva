… Israel, the end is coming! Do not get lost. Israel, the fearful world is coming, from the east, from the south and from the north. When I was calling out to you, you were laughing at Me.

Stop it, world! Stop it, world, because this is not good with Me, and it is not good for you too. Stop it, world. Come back to Me, for I will not bring you so into My kingdom. It seems that someone sprinkled salt on you, so chocked you are. Stop it, world! It was as if you were saying that I am a liar, that I am not the Lord, Jesus Christ. I will hide from your face and let the fires consume you. Stop it, world! Who scared you? Who told you that you would perish? To-morrow, I will judge you for what you have left behind. Where are your sons and daughters?

… You wanted to take a child at the priesthood school. Your child, son, while he was with you, he was a Christian, but here, at the priesthood school, he has become a pagan. [legatura_la_teme id_capitol="3" id_subcapitol="4"]The priesthood school is taught after the world today, not after Me. In the time of the grand-fathers and forefathers, the priest did not minister with a watch on his hand, nor according to the time, but according to grace. [/legatura_la_teme]

… You are obliged by the Lord Jesus to look for tears for prayer, for the time is coming, when with one gram of tears to wash a bed of laundry, for your whole people, alive and deceased. And also walk and seek to have wisdom, for the grandfathers and ancestors did not take wisdom with them, and you should look for it.
