[legatura_la_teme id_capitol="3" id_subcapitol="3"]The time is coming, you Christian, when you will search for a pit to hide but the pit will not receive you. Come to Me, for My Father has sent Me to your bed to call you. Come, you Romanian, for the damned in hell want to get out at this word, but they cannot, and you that are free, do not come. Come, for if you listen to Me as your fathers and forefathers have done, you will give freedom to your all relatives that you have in hell.

… The poor from My people who receive mercy, you should reward My mercy by praying for the one who gave to you and for the dead to whom it was given. Do not be like the gipsy from the door of the church, who ask for alms, and do not pray for the one who had mercy on him. [/legatura_la_teme]

… Israel, My people, Israel, wash yourself well so that you may not have any dirt on you, for the end is coming; there is no end that someone may remain behind Me, but all has come to an end!
