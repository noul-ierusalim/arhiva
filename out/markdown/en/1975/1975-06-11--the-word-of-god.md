… It is written into the book that the joy comes over the Christians and mourning for the pagans. There is still a little bit of time, and you will see what you have not seen since you were born. What will you say when you see seven pagan women for you to put them on your name? If you have no faith then you will join with them and go in hell with them. What will you do, male people? Will you submit or not? Woe to you, who do not want to know the law!

… Keep in mind, for someone is coming. Now he has sharpened his sword and carried it on his belt for the time that is coming. Woe to you, when you have to be put to the sword. It is better for the deaf, it is better for the mute, it is better for the blind in the days of today. But it is better for you, being wholly and being a Christian who stands on the foundation.

[legatura_la_teme id_capitol="1" id_subcapitol="6"]… The time is coming when you have to make use of faith and works as Elijah did in the wilderness, to show the true God to the world.[/legatura_la_teme] Sons, you will be in need of yourselves and of your good works, which you have been doing today. You are not bound with your hands at your back in such a way that you cannot worship, but you will be bound. However, in your time of judgment, your fasting and praying will save you.
