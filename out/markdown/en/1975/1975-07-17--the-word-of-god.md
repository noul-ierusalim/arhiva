[legatura_la_teme id_capitol="8" id_subcapitol="5"]… Our shelter will be here, on Earth. This land shall be My people’s garden and I shall make it heaven, adorned with trees, angel music and birds. This land shall be heaven, but God will clean it, as it is written. Be prepared for the end, in order not to burn with garbage, as all the sinner will perish.[/legatura_la_teme]

… Be moderate with food, drink, and rest. Sleeping from evening till morning is too much. Working from morning till evening is too much. You should know that God is determined to destroy the whole world. All the human beings and all the creatures will be destroyed. All the blocks of fats will be destroyed. Do not let yourself be destroyed together with the world. If you do a holy work, you will not perish along with the world. Be faithful, for it is written that the people from the end shall welcome God at His arrival. Be like pigeons and hide yourself from the sin. Love the sin no more. Do not strive for daughters and sons of Gomora. Do not be united with them. Do not receive them near you just because they say they believe, like you do.

… When the sun will die, do not be afraid. When the mountains will shake down, do not be afraid. Be perfect Christian for death came through sins. You will be an island. The world will not be able to pass to you. Once, in the time of our forefathers and ancestors, they wore their stuff in baskets of rushes but you have bags. Do not carry a bag with a long handle as the world does. Do not wear wrist watches and brooches on your chest. Do not wear luxuries. My little sheep, Christians have devilish things: rings on their fingers.

… Be sober while eating, drinking and resting. It is too much to sleep from evening to morning. It is too much to work from morning to evening. You should know that God has decid-ed to destroy the whole world. All the people and all the creatures will be destroyed. All the buildings will be destroyed. Do not let yourselves perish once with the world. If you do a holy work, you will not perish once with the world. Be faithful, for it is written that the people from the end will meet the Lord at His coming. Be like doves and hide from the sin. Do no longer love sin. Do no longer lust after Gomorrah’s sons and daughters. Do not be joined with them. Do not receive them near you, when they say that they worship like you.
