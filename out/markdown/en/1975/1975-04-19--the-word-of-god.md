… The fire will not be lighted from the sky, but from you Christian, from the world. Israel, all heaven is present when you sin.

… This is not 1954 year any more. Oh, you are not from that time and are from today when death is looking through the window to count on whom to take lots of them. I speak spiritually. I do not speak of the red lips or about the made eyebrows, but I speak of faith and of sufferance and you do not wait for the end as Satan has whispered in your ear that this age is no longer ending and keep this in mind, Christian.

… Be taught, for the animals got wiser than humans, for when the bird drinks, she thanks Me after every sip.

… Israel, the heaven expects to see how you come out of the school: with or without a rank and without facings? [legatura_la_teme id_capitol="6" id_subcapitol="2"]Sons, many wait for you to escape them from punishment, for to a thousandth generation it will be saved through your hands, through your exam, Christian. Oh, Christians, does a meal, or a drink, a jealousy, or a lie cheat you? These are but garbage. Throw them away from you. As Lord Jesus descended into the hell and took out all of them, the same way, you My son, will go into hell, and with God’s gift, to the thousandth generation will get it out.[/legatura_la_teme]
