I am coming down to give you comfort, sons from the spring. I am coming to bring you the Holy Spirit, the Comforter, to comfort you, for under the cross comfort from God is needed, comfort from the One Who knows how it is under the cross, well sons. When you do not have comfort in hard times and under pains, then each one of you should give comfort to the other and this will comfort you. Be comforters like Me, like the Holy Spirit, the Comforter, and you will have comfort by the comfort you give to others, for this is how it is given from God the work of those who love Him, of those who become like Him, for this means the Lord Who is loved by those who love Him.

[legatura_la_teme id_capitol="6" id_subcapitol="3"]It is a day of feast and I am coming down into the book as word and I am staying with you and giving you comfort and powers, sons. The heavenly powers want to dwell in you with their holy work and great at work, for the soul where it dwells and works anger, revenge, ambition, avarice, disobedience and then hatred, oh, in such a soul the heavenly powers cannot dwell or work.

The endearing spirit I want to multiply the in you, for it works comfort, sons. And more than that, I want it in those who turn their back to Me and to you, for the fondness from Me helps the man to God and to holiness, to sweet endurance, to hope, to holy quietness no matter how painful the way of the cross may be for those who are Mine, the way to God, for fight is needed for the coronation of life. [/legatura_la_teme]However, if someone dares to stand against Me or against you, then whoever may be will step aside, for your way with Me is assisted by the angels in groups-groups all the time because it is hard on this way, as hard as are the days of nowadays, when the devil make grimaces at Me to destroy the course and your support for Me to go; however, I am the Lord of the powers, I am the One Who overcame the death, the devil and the world, oh, sons.

[legatura_la_teme id_capitol="6" id_subcapitol="3"]Oh, My beloved brothers and sisters, oh, you should not take as a joke your obedience to God, My entire word upon you, or the watch upon you for the keeping of My word, for David was watching between him and Me so that he might not leave Me and that I might not leave him too, and My anointed one was saying to Me: *«You, Lord, have commanded Your precepts, that we should fully obey them, and oh, that my ways were steadfast to obey Your statues as I want. Do not let me wander from Your commandments, as I have hidden Your word in my heart, that I may not go wrong against You and that I may not sin against You, for You have rebuked the proud who are cursed, who wanders from Your commandments, Lord».* **(See Ps: 119/4, 5, 10, 11, and 21)**

I am comforting you with My word, sons. In time of joy and sufferance, in times of weakness or under your many burdens that you carry, when the time presses you under your duties for Me, when the trials furrow your little hearts, when you prepare for those in heaven a stopover with you and days of glory here during the feasts, then I, the Lord, bring you comfort, sons heavy with worries and heavy with pressures and sadness on My way with you. Everything you may have to carry and overcome, let it all strengthen you, for the fruit tree leans over on every side in times of storm, but it remains steadfast in its root and it deepens itself even more in the ground where it is rooted, and behold, the fruit tree can and remains steadfast. [/legatura_la_teme]And behold, we have to go through the time of preparation for the feast of My mother Virgin, for hearts with longing in them wait to head for the spring here, and let us lift up to heaven a holy feast and with a holy echo between heaven and earth, and we have to bring a sacrifice of love to prepare this day of glory, and to have only you under the cross; however, I give you the heavenly powers to carry you, and they will be able to do it in you and upon you as in heaven, and I am the One Who fulfills everything I speak by My word, sons.

[legatura_la_teme id_capitol="4" id_subcapitol="2"]On this day, we are sitting at the table of word with the group of the prophets. This group of prophets is coming together with Elijah the prophet, the one celebrated now among the saints, the one who had not eaten or drunk forty days and forty nights for the sake and for the longing of his meeting with God, and he is alive in his body and he is like fire; he has got great work to do and this saint works as much, and he is working as much as in old time, and his work will be confessed, for I, the Lord, with the saints of the heaven, came two thousand years ago, and in the same way I am coming today, as I have come from heaven down on earth then and now, and the Father gave and gives Me the saints as My companions. The devil keeps the world in the darkness, but the light from heaven puts out the darkness, and it becomes light in the darkness, and the man on earth will see that God is and that He brings His glory into view, and His saints in glory as a testimony, for great is the beauty of the invisible world, and when God’s invisible things will be revealed, then every flesh will startle and there will be only faith and then only light and glory from here to all over the earth, for the Romanian country has its destiny from the Lord to give light over the nations and to comfort all over with My glory all the nations, and this time is at hand, it is near, and it is in My word, which goes all over the earth to its revelation and Mine, and a fire will come down from heaven, the fire of the Holy Spirit, and the peoples will become wise, will receive My fulfillments and will come to Me, for I am the Savior Who came on earth two thousand years ago, and since then and up to this day I have been working for a great light over the nations of the earth, as it is written, and the prophet Elijah will appear with the glory of the turning back to God of many nations and the curse will cease, and there will be only the Lord and His righteous ones. Amen.

— Oh, Lord, You are blessed and much confessed by Your confessing saints, Lord! It is written that are cursed all those who forsake Your commandments, Your words, and about me it is written to come to turn the nations on earth with their face to You and to keep the earth and the countries on it from curse, Lord. I want all on earth to know that my work is to turn the hearts of the fathers to the sons, and the sons’ to their fathers, and the fathers are the saints of old, the saints from near and they are in heaven, and I will show on earth great signs for the people to understand God through them and to turn their hearts to the fathers, to the saints, and to call out to them to come to their help, and the sons to be protected from curse by the keeping of Your commandments, of Your words, Lord.

A river of word flows from Your mouth, and it runs everywhere, for there are people to share it, it has where to come and from where this gift of Yours to be given over the earth. Oh, and we have to strengthen everywhere inside and outside the citadel of Your word so that it may have heavenly support, Lord, for Your glory is this town, in this city with the name of fire, for Your word is a fire cleansing of sins and renewing of heaven and earth, for it is written to be made a new heaven and a new earth, and You work by the word and fulfill so.

Oh, sons of God’s word and sons of His city of today on the hearth of the Romanian people, oh, Romanian sons, the name of Romanian will be great over the nations! The devil fights with all forces of evil and tries to destroy from the earth the name of Christian of the God’s sons, but the name of Romanian will remain and this name will resound by the heavenly trumpets, and every man who will receive God’s word on this hearth, the hearth of the Romanian people, will become Romanian, for many will be born from above, from Gods’ word, from this word, for Jesus Christ speaks the Romanian language and has become Romanian, and those who will worship him in the time that comes will have a new name, and we, His saints, will fulfill the Lord’s works, as it is written.

However, you shall not forget who you are. Oh, do not forget to live up to the gift upon you and to work like the saints, sons. At my prayer there came down fire from heaven, and God fulfilled it, and behold how much power your prayer needs to have and how much victory it has to bring against those who know and think that they are big and strong, and who have to humble themselves and to come to the light from above of God’s word during this time! The light of the world is God’s word during these days, and you are the fruit. This is how you need to work, for the time is at hand. You have to work prayer with fire so that it may work like my prayer, and that is why fast and prayer is needed according to this truth so that you may be able to see and to show the Lord as I did, and also to show your sweet speaking with Him and His with you and then this glory all over, for there are many, many who wait with awe and look at a distance for it to come.

Oh, stay under the gift of the Lord, sons. Stay under the fire of the Holy Spirit so that fire may spread from this fire and all the nations to know the Lord, as it is written.[/legatura_la_teme]

And now I am bowing before You, Lord, and I am confessing with Your coming accompanied by the saints, and Moses and I are on the right and left of Your side everywhere, and we work from near You what is to be worked, and we work, Lord, closely over the citadel of Your word to keep it under care and protection, for it is Yours now. And now, You are all the blessing over it and over them for they to prepare in it the meeting and the feast for Your mother Virgin, Your mother and ours, Lord, and may they be blessed by Your saints too, to bring to You the day of glory, unspeakably beautiful, as Your blessing descends upon them working and adorning Your way and Your saints’, for Your time has come, the time of Your glory, Your glory from heaven and down to earth, Lord, Amen.

— I, the Lord, start waiting now for the day of the feast for My mother Virgin and all the beauty that will prepared to stand before Us, oh, My loved ones, with whom I travel and work.

And as for you, sons working at the spring of My word, work everything like the sons of My glory during these days, for it will be opened more and more and it will fill with glory the heaven, the earth and the hearts of those who will reveal Me within My glory, within My word, with which I announce and prepare My glory.

Oh, I am looking at you once with the start of the preparation, I am rejoicing over you, and I do not take My eyes off you. You started the work of the adornment for the day of the feast so much waited. The preparation will be great, sons, but do not doubt of your little power and of the time that still is until My feast. Again and again I work with you and I do not leave you. I am at the helm in everything; therefore, take courage, for you are little and only with Me can you do everything that is to be done.

I am comforting the little hearts, which have been waiting for the feast to come. I am sending them refreshment in their hearts and I am giving them patience too; I am comforting their longing and then I am coming to meet them, as I have always waited for them.

And now, as far as you are concerned, I am leaving all the blessing upon you for you to work for the joy of the feast that is coming, sons. Behold, you see how wonderfully everything is worked. All things come at their time and at their place, only for you to listen with great care for obedience, and do nothing by yourselves, and do everything only with Me, sons.

I am taking you to My bosom and I am keeping you like that to work, from now and until the day expected by the whole heaven and by all those who love and appreciate the city of My word and everything that is in it, sons. My mother Virgin has been all the time here with you near Me and she is giving you graces and is accompanying you mysteriously.

It will be beautiful, beautiful; everything will be beautiful. Oh, you should also be beautiful; beautiful in your love, beautiful in your conduct, in your faith, in your patience, in your word; be beautiful always, always, sons. Be like Me, beautiful, be after My will, always, always, oh, sons. Amen, amen, amen.
