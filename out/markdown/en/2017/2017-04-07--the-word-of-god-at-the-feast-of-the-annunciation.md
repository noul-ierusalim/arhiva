I am the One sent from heaven on earth, I am sent by the Father God, to Whom I listen as His Son, for I am the only One born of the Father before all eternity, as it was revealed by the Holy Spirit and as it has been believed this mystery which rests in God the Father, the Son and the Holy Spirit.

Oh, it is great the mystery and then the grace of the faith in God, in One God the Father, the Son and the Holy Spirit! That is why God has revealed through and for the faithful ones how their faith has to be and how to confess it before those in heaven and those on earth, for it is not well then when you believe God and from God, oh, it is not good not to be known above and below and all around, your faith in God and the preaching for it. Oh, what would it happen with all and everything if God, their Maker, did not watch in such a way that all things may be at their place and time and to listen to their Maker? It would happen as with the man who has not got used to listening and who spoils everything around him always against him and against his fellows, for man spoiled it even from his beginning and since then he has kept on spoiling things and then he has always grown worse by his disobedience to God.

Oh, peace to you, peace to those who receive Me and share My coming in the word, after two thousand years from My Gospel, the Gospel of the kingdom of the heavens, with which I came on earth from the Father! On this day of holy feast, a day of heavenly mystery and godly memory among the days of feast, we will stay in a heavenly work and put on the table the lesson of obedience, well sons, and then we will put its gifts from above. I am with the saints and with the angels from heaven and I am with My mother Virgin at the table with you as well, a table of word, a table to learn, and the teaching upon you is from heaven, and it is coming with God on earth to you; it is coming to the faithful ones, to whom grace has been given, the grace of the holy faith for those coming from heaven on earth to be with the people, for everything God has done, He has done for man; however, man has not listened to his Maker and to all that have been built by God’s word.

Oh, sons, be alert with the ear of your mind and heart, for we are teaching the mystery of submission and its gifts, as My mother Virgin came to know them as she was filled with the gift from haven, she was filled with God and showed to the people the Son of God and she was His mother.

Oh, sons, submission is on earth for many works and causes and all people submit, according to how their searching and lot are. There are some among people who feel great happiness and comfort when they obey, and they feel protected and loved, and they feel like that by obedience. And there are some other people still who submit themselves to the strong ones, who bring into submission their fellows for their empty glory, for their authority on earth or for Mammon, because they are greedy to have much and many things and to delight boasting themselves and bringing into submission poor and helpless people, who need to receive the necessary things from the rich and ruling people for their life. Again, there are some among people, who obey to learn what they have to learn and work after that, like the disciples who learn from their teachers to know what to do after that, and they have submission for obedience to their teacher. Oh, there are all kinds of works of submission to the teacher. This is how it is on earth, and it is as in heaven, it is obedience, but not all obedience is as in heaven and for the things from above, rather there is much obedience for gain, it is human, as requested or as man may need it. The work of My disciples was obedience, sons, for they responded to My call and to God’s teaching and mysteries came on earth with Me, after they had followed Me and listened to My words and seen My work in order to learn to work like Me after that.

Oh, you can never become a disciple if you cannot decide for submission in great faith, with great love to the one you obey. The obedience by the grace from above means love, sons. The feeling of submission and the peace of the one who obey spring out of obedience, and he who obeys loves with happiness in his heart, for love obeys.

Oh, the lack of love does not know what submission is, but only haughtiness, because man from the beginning had chosen to be proud and did not obey the One Who built him and behold, woe to the one who does not obey, for they do not have love and the gift of submission does not touch them. Submission is the gift of those who love with cleanness of love and it is heavenly work, as the work of My mother Virgin was, who stayed under the gift of the holy submission within her spirit, with her little heart and work, for a messenger from heaven was sent by God to tell her that the Lord was coming on earth among people with the kingdom of the heavens, and she was to submit to the message from above and to bring into fulfillment the Lord’s coming, My birth of her virginal body, and God appeared before people and wrote His Gospel on earth and gave it to the people so that they might believe and be baptized with faith in the name of the Father, the Son and the Holy Spirit, to become God’s people on earth and to be able to work then the mystery of God’s kingdom and its King.

Oh, mother Virgin, tell them now, tell those who have received from the heaven the gift of the faith and its gift, tell them about the work of submission, mother, tell them about the mystery of discipleship, My mother. Amen.

— Oh, my Teacher and the Child of my virginal body, oh, my God, my Maker and the Maker of my love and discipleship, oh, how much happiness my submission gave me, my dear Son! Even in pain submission gave me happiness, for he who submits for the pain that comes brings him to submission, that one feels happiness in his sufferance, he feels it through submission, for submission gives power and peace for the pains when they come. Then the message of angel has filled me with happiness when he came to let me know about Your conception in my virginal body and when he told me that the Holy Spirit would overshadow me and then I would give birth to God’s Son, You, my Son Jesus, and then I submitted myself to the mystery of discipleship and I knit the happiness with the pain for it and I got sweetened with everything that came to be able to do and bear, at the thought that You are God, my dear Son, and I could do everything for You, for my love of You, child coming from heaven for Your birth and for the new birth of the world, Lord Son.

Oh, watching children at the spring of the word of my Son Christ, give my today’s word to those who believe in the word of my Son God, Who is speaking to you, for behold, I am telling to everyone who believes in the One sent from heaven on earth as word and I am telling them this:

Pay attention, oh pay attention to my speaking as the mother of God’s Son! You cannot comfort my Son, the Word, only with your faith in His word, and you cannot be His beloved disciples if you do not fulfill the word of His teaching for those who are faithful. Oh, many come and would come to sit at the table of word here, but not all fulfill the Lord’s will for His new people in this time, and not all would fulfill if they came closer to believe. The faith without works, without the works for it is not enough for life and for the Lord, for the discipleship with Christ; however, the fulfilling of the word brought and obedience to God is needed and then discipleship with great love is needed as well, as love gives power and faithfulness and it gives birth to the Lord in the heart of the faithful one and then he can work for his Lord.

Oh, many hear but then do not fulfill in them the longing of Christ, and he, who knows from the Lord and delays day by day the fulfillment of His will on behalf of the one who believes from the Lord, is guilty, and then pains and oppression come over the spirit and body of the one who does not fulfill the longing of my Son, Jesus Christ, the holiness of the faithful man.

Oh, it is not enough for man to believe, for after he has believed, he has a great responsibility to put on his life the Lord’s will, His fulfilled word, and which comes from heaven on earth to renew the man and the world, as it is written.

The work of discipleship was my work before the Lord. I started to submit even when I was a little girl before those who carried me, or before those who taught me or gave me things to do, and in this way I submitted myself to the message of the angel after that, and I received the Son of God in my virginal womb and I gave Him a body out of my body, and then I showed Him to the people by the work of discipleship, for it was given to me to fulfill and I fulfilled like that.

Oh, love the mystery of a disciple, sons, sons of my Son, and you too, those who believe in His coming as word here, on the hearth of the Romanian people. Discipleship is love with obedience, and only faith is not enough. You cannot belong to the Lord and be His worker if you are not a disciple with love and if you are not attentive to the word of His teaching so that it may be fulfilled. The disciple has a holy and selective conduct and he also has propriety and cleanness of heart and body, garment, food, words and faithfulness by all these, and then he has a word spoken in obedience: Behold the Lord’s servant, be it to me according to the spoken word, Lord! Amen.

Oh, God’s sons are disciples of God’s works, and they love the work of the holy obedience with great faithfulness, the work of the love of God and people under the sign of the holiness and faith, which obeys working and fulfilling. Oh, this is what I have advised them on my day of feast now, my Son Christ. Oh, faith is not faith, and love is not love if those who believe and love You do not listen after that for the fulfilling of Your word, given to them by hearing and then by fulfilling it, and concerning those who hear Your will in them from You and deny accomplishing it, then let them know that it is not sufficient only to believe, lest by not hurrying to fulfill to draw sufferance suddenly upon themselves because of their disobedience to God, due to their not receiving of Your will, Son of the God the Father and my Son.

Oh, let the work of discipleship be their little pillow of rest for those who receive You and fulfill You in them, for those, who are disciples by obedience and love, Son Lord, rest in You. Oh, give them, once with the grace of the faith, give them love to fulfill Your entire word and that it may be well with them as to some disciples, to some who love You very much, oh, my Son and my God. Amen.

— Oh, My dear mother, I was your disciple and I listened to you, and you were My disciple and you listened to God, for discipleship cannot be without obedience, mother. Let those who come to be My disciples do the work of submission and its lesson and fulfillment, as without submission there is no discipleship, and the one who wants to be My disciple must learn from Me. The disciple has love of the one he obeys and then he has hundredfold fruit and has to bring forth sheaves as fruit, and by this he proves to be a confessed disciple with the wages of a disciple, for any perfect disciple is like his Teacher, as it is written, mother.

And as for you, people of My word, you have to distinguish the mystery of a disciple profoundly in order to be able to be My disciple forever, for those without faithfulness always fall, and many of them do no longer stand up after their fall, and then they try to wound the Lord’s face. Oh, set yourself to learning always, always as a disciple, and tell those who come near to believe, tell them that they need obedience with love, and only faith is not enough, lest by not doing what I, the Lord, teach the man to do for his life, their works to become worse than their works of old and in this way to lose the Lord.

Oh, sons, be My disciples with love! I loved you. I love you. I had the work of discipleship and this is what I have taught many people the love and the discipleship for it. This is how you are supposed to do and to be for Me, as God, sons, for any perfect disciple is like his Teacher, and I am your Teacher and I am your gift and grace so that you may be able to be My sons, to be My disciples, to have disciples and to have fruit through them. And if the Father glorifies Himself in Me on earth, then I also want, oh, I also want to glorify Myself in you, oh, sons. Amen, amen, amen.
