Sons, sons, get up from your bed and receive the Lord! Those in heaven have gathered together for the feast and sweet memorial, and I, the Lord, have prepared My word I am strengthening you with it to stand before Me and to put My word into the book of feast. We have a threefold feast and we put all three days in one, because on the 12th of a month like this, I set together with you and with the confessing bishop then, the sealing of the garden of the Holy of Holies, the service of dedication over the little garden that has between its borders the little white stone, the shrine of the covenant, the mystery of the new beginning, the mystery of the New Jerusalem on the hearth of the Romanian people, and that day was twenty-six years ago. It was then the Eve of the feast of the saint apostle Andrew, the disciple whom I had near Me and near you on that day, and he also put the seal on this mysterious settlement, for it has been kept under mystery. And today, on the third day of the three, is the feast of the entrance among the saints of My trumpet Verginica, *(Verginica - the diminutive to her real name: Virginia, r.n.)* the mouth that I, the Lord, had used for those twenty-five years of the beginning of My word from the end of the time, for the Lord has never worked His mysterious works without man on earth, so that man may be able to understand in time why the Lord built him on the day when He finished to make the heaven and earth by the word, and then man by His own hand.

Oh, sons, I had then with you a great day seen from heaven; We had sweet work, and great faith for it, for only the faith of those who believe in God, oh, only by it God can do His works at their time, and then it was the time when I, the Lord, set again on its first rock My church, Christ’s church, for those who say and kept calling themselves through the time servants of My church, have brought under them its beginning, for they have slept over the church, and in no way have they been shepherding the flock for the Lord to have a flock, the Lord and not they, and little by little My work and the mystery of the church has been weakened because of those without faith, without stature and without obedience to the fathers from the beginning, through whom I was able to complete the mystery of the kingdom of the heavens on earth, the serving church with the Lord in its midst among people.

I have been crying for almost two thousand years; I have been crying with the Father for mercy of the flock which has not shepherded and which has been surrounded by the strong ones, the antichrists, who have never been absent from among those who have appeared to be servants of altars, and who have been spoiling My church on its rock, and that which was torn down has never been built back and it has remained that the Romanian people was pushed down from the rock by those who have climbed up to be servants of altars, oh, and this people chosen from among the nations to be God’s mystery in the end of the time has no longer arisen from its low condition, for when the feast of the Christmas comes, Romanians do not longer have it at its place in calendar and come with it ahead of time and do no longer keep the days of feasts at their right time, oh, and it is sin, for the courage was too great, as it also was the disobedience to the fathers and the tradition well established even from the beginning, by their order from God, from the apostles and from the saints.

Sons, sons, I, the Lord, have repaired through you this damage and I have set you before Me with the mystery of the beginning of My church, and in this day you are celebrated by those from heaven who are with Me at the table with you for your new day, for My time with you, when I laid down Mu church on its stone and I sealed the little garden, which carries in it the mystery written in the Scriptures, the little white stone, a mystery entrusted to those who are faithful, to you,[/legatura_la_teme] sons, even if only few of those who were twenty-six years ago have remained here, for the others turned back from their way and went where they came from; they went into the world and this pain year by year has been putting into the shade the threefold feast of the memorial of My power upon you, and behold why I have to strengthen My power upon you always, for you are a little bit sad and I see you in tears, but your tears are My tears from those who have left their steadfastness and love, which I, the Lord gave them to have for you, those who carry My burden, the word of My mouth on earth, sons.

Oh, how blind can become the one who puts his trust in the view of his eyes and in his mind! Man forgets how much satan works, how much opposing work he does and how much courage he has to put down those who are chosen by the Lord for His many works and hard to set on earth, for the Lord works mysteriously among those that are not seen and among those that are seen!

Oh, sons, peace to you and days of feast and memorial of My works with you! Oh, peace to you with My trumpet Verginica on this day of memorial for her among those who are in heaven and among you! She works wonderfully from heaven, and wonderfully she gathers comfort and intercession for those who are My people and hers from below, and Verginica’s group is in heaven, a group working with her for My work on earth. [legatura_la_teme id_capitol="4" id_subcapitol="5"]The heaven has prepared itself to meet the king of the Romanian people, and Verginica is My trumpet of welcome together with the archangel Michael, who carries the one who is anointed over the Romanian people, bringing him before My throne, a crowned king, a Christian king, *(Michael I of Romania, r.n.)* who knew in detail the mystery of the work of My word here, the word with which I have been comforting him for a long time and preparing him in secret his coming back home, and now, after I have hardly accomplished the submission to My will of those who have opened for him to come and to come back to his people, oh, behold, I have prepared him beautifully, I have dressed him up and set him before Me, and now I have sent My angel to take him by his hand and to make his entrance among those from heaven of the Romanian people, who served Me with their life, with their faith, with their love of God and country, for the Romanian country is My love from the end of the time.

Oh, behold, My Verginica, again and again your day brings joy into heaven, and your group grows and strengthens his work to help Our work on earth. There are days of pain on the hearth of the Romanian people in many hearts who have loved My anointed one, their king, and it is joy in heaven with Us, it is comfort, and the power, which helps from heaven on earth becomes wholly.

This mystery has not been possible to be understood, and behold, neither its work, which has been revealing the Lord during this time and in this land, where I set My ruling citadel, the throne of My word on earth, and I have some gentle sons who carry Me, and whom only My power carries and strengthens them, for I have always said this that there is no one on earth to support and help them to work for the Lord and for His great and much work in this time of His coming as word on earth.[/legatura_la_teme]

I have put the three days of the memorial in one and We are writing down their mystery into the book. And then I had here with you the witness bishop, the one who fully believed in My word and its place and fruit. Oh, his little heart is heavy now, and no one knows it, and not even he knows it. I have taken the spirit of his faith at that time to keep it in heaven, for on earth he was completely divested of the gift from Me for him during this time.

The antichrist is tall, fat and wide in the midst of the church, where his place and work is. Oh, where should his work and attack be anywhere else if not near the One against Whom he stands? Behold the great blindness! Does anyone understand this work that stands against Christ and its place of operation and attack? Does the Christian flock understand this? Oh, flock without tending, what would really be, if you understood this? What would be if My mercy did not have any mercy on man, the man with no shepherd? Oh, man is ignorant, he is blind, he does not know or see; everything is covered. Those that are seen take the man’s eyes, and I, the Lord, increase My mercy for man, for there is no one to take care of man, even if he sees before him people clothed within the holy garment. Amen.

[legatura_la_teme id_capitol="4" id_subcapitol="5"]Now, Verginica, My last trumpet, put into the book near Me your word. It is your day of feast among the saints.

— Oh, who Lord, who except You would be able to mark my day of feast among the saints? If You had not asked me in marriage and if You had not been preparing me for You, would I have been able to have my day among the saints now? Oh, You are my day, my being, because You were also on earth with me, the one humiliated and ignored, for I had nothing to show me and to give me a gift and a name, because I came from a poor family. If You had not picked me from the thorns to prepare me, to renew me, to make me Your vessel and to put the trumpet in my hand and at my mouth, then I would have remained an abortion, Lord. Now I am praising You among those in heaven, I see Your glory and I am confessing You on earth and rejoicing You with my work, for Your longing after man has been great.

However, today, on my day of feast among the saints, behold, Lord, what we have to work! On the Romanian land it is laid down the preparation for a day of heavenly glory of Your anointed one Michael, the king of Romanians, who is coming now to You, to those in heaven, Lord. Let Your word speak now on my day of celebration and let it be written into Your book of today his day of victory towards the heavenly things, of Your anointed one, Lord. You are his Lord God, Whom he loved and obeyed as much as he was able to do this, for his oppression was hard and great, and it was as hard as his endurance when he had been suffering for so many hears because of his estrangement from this country and people. Bless now his rest and joy among those in heaven and his comfort after all his sufferance on earth. Amen.

— Oh, come into the joy and comfort of your Lord, My anointed one, king of Romanians! You are welcomed in a special way by those in heaven. The Romanian country is the comfort of My saints during this time, when I, the Lord, have established a ruling citadel in it and My river of word, the river of life, which flows over the earth from this citadel.

Many of the great people on earth during your time of reign and then in your time of painful wandering have seen you and called you great, (King Michael of Romania was obliged to abdicate the throne by the communists who had come with the Russian tanks, when he was threatened with the pistol at his temple, r.n.); your wandering that was nourished with the longing of your country and people. You have been an example of greatness for the rulers of the nations and this by My anointing upon you, which has lifted you up and showed you to the world, and the great world has understood mysteriously your greatness from Me, for this was My will. Now, when the Romanian people is bowing before you and it is bowing and asking you in secret to be their intercessor in heaven, oh, behold, this holy occasion is bringing now on the hearth of this nation many crowned heads, which are bowing before the one who has got a great name, the king of the Romanians on earth and in heaven. These have a special occasion during these holy moments and they are coming on the Romanian land, whose air is filled with the heavenly feast from above, and they are preparing you the garment of glory. My word on this hearth prophesied your coming back and prepared for you a paved way, and I had sent before you messengers, and the bishop witness of the mystery of My word and I have told you that I was going to work for your coming back, even if the Romanian country was not prepared for this accomplishment. However, I accomplished this, as I had announced ahead of time by the book of My word which has been written and established on the Romanian land for more than sixty years, for I have lifted up into view the little white stone and the gate that watches over the garden of My word and I had written your name on the stone to fulfill after that, and I fulfilled it. And when the years have passed, behold, the great day has come for your union with the heaven, with Romania from heaven, who is rejoicing now with her saints for your arrival, for your glory from Me, and before which the kings of the nations are bowing during these days so that they may see, and the Romanian people may also see, how much I have loved you, how much glory I have prepared for you by My anointing, for you were like Me, banished from your homeland; however, you have come back here, just as I came back then to the Father and now I am the Intercessor, and in the same way, you will be from now on the intercessor in heaven for your country for those who cry and mourn over their separation from you, oh, great king because of your anointing from Me that I have put on your head. Oh, ask Me, and it will be given to you! Ask Me, for your prayer for the Romanian people is great and gentle, the nation which is now under hard trial, for its chains bind him fast again and again by the hand of those who had taken away its freedom, which blocked its hope, *(The neocommunists, „of the left wing” who have come to power by lying promises, r.n.)*; however, now this people has got a great intercessor before Me, and We are going to work the salvation from God over the Romanian nation and land. Amen.

Oh, do not lose your hope, Romanian people! Those who dig a hole for you will fall down terribly, for woe to those who strike you for themselves, for their glory! The Romanian country is under My protection and under the protection of My mother Virgin, even if it is suffering now because of the lying people who press heavily upon it. They do not know what humanness means; they do not know what justice or mercy is either, for on earth it is as on earth, but in heaven it will not be like that, because great glory has been preparing over the Romanian land, My country of today, in which I, the Lord, have been laying down My word.[/legatura_la_teme]

This day is with great heavenly significance, the day in which are included three days of feast, days of dedication feast over My ruling citadel, over the little garden of My word and of its surroundings, built at My holy advice for My glory of today.
