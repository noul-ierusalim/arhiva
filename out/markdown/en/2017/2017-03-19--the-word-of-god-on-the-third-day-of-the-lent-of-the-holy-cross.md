Peace to you! Peace to you, sons, peace to you and to those who wait for My word to come on earth and to put it into the book and let them eat it from God, from My table of word, a table spread here, where I, the Lord, have set a dwelling place and teaching to be learned; afterwards, I go with the glory of My word that it is spread and shared, and it feeds with God the whole creature, for God becomes word, and with it He gives life and spirit of life.

Oh, sons, it is the time of the Lent at its half-way, and they have set, the synod of My saints, the cross of the rest and of the holy power on this Sunday of the halving of the fast, oh, and I do no longer have any synod of saints on earth, I do not have as I had at the beginning of My church when I was feeding the saints with My Spirit and when they made up a book of holy guidance, to be followed and well learned by those who were to follow and love God and holiness, for there is no one who loves God except the one who sanctifies himself all the time so that he may not have time for sin, for his separation from God by sin, wandering away through wild and precipitous places for the soul, and where one can go weak and be deceived by satan with the things that are pleasant temporarily and which bring remorse to man and scratches on his face and in his soul; however, he who follows the instructions in the book for the guidance of his life, that one remains with God and with the holy love, and such a soul is comfort for Me and power on earth.

Oh, I have on the Romanian land the word that has been written by this time for over sixty years of word; the word of holy teaching, always a warm table for the strengthening of the holiness of those who have received holy life from God to have on earth. I have put faith in those who have sanctified themselves for God and I have put power and love, as only love can keep the one who loves from above for God, as I, the Lord, loved on earth during the time when I lived visibly and I was seen by those around Me two thousand years ago.

Oh, sons from the spring, and you, those who take Me from here as word and comfort, oh, well sons, behold what I am telling you about love and about My coming as word and mystery on earth: Oh, if you love to be clean and holy for the Lord you will understand and love, but if you do not love to be clean you will not understand fully what love and its glory are. Therefore, be holy, for I, the Lord, am holy, and I am only love, as it is written that: «God is love», and He is not otherwise with the holy man, for his love as God is love, the One persecuted by the sinners, like His saints persecuted by the most sinful among the people and without any power, for the true power of man is his holiness, it is the cross and the life with the cross, under the sign of the holiness and the great victory, when man overcomes his self for God, for those from above put upon him.

Oh, how much darkness is on earth, how much sin and how much fight for sin and against those who are righteous, sons, and the flock of the people is led and brought into submission by those who are full of sinfulness, who always sin, using the temporary power of their thrones from which they rule upon people and bring down those who keep off the life with sin in it and who always clean their heart and body, serving the Lord with their love. The sinful ones have always, always persecuted the holy ones, and, in this way they prove that they are sinful, and I, the Lord, am speaking to those who persecuted and who still make bold to persecute and denigrate those who love God, and I am telling them this:

The Christians have to be loved. Those, who love the holy laws and the things coming from heaven on earth to be worked by them, do not have to be despised, but behold, the ministering priests at the altars even today are persecuting those who sanctify themselves. Many a priest have come before Me not reconciled to God, confirmed that they have persecuted the Christians, for they have helped the men of the law of the state to make dossiers full of calumnies and accusations against the Christians loving of God and loving of the heaven on earth and who were the carriers of holy mysteries, shared by the Holy Spirit according to the economy from above, and today it is the same; those who are zealous for the laws of the church do more harm against their brothers than the men of state who search for the guilt or innocence of those who are accused. Oh, who judged Me two thousand years ago? Was it not the priests of the temple who judged Me and gave Me over to the cross because of their temporary glory?

Oh, sons, sons, those who have endured to the end were and are blessed, for their prayers have been fruitful forever. I will send pains and crushes of soul to those who have worked maliciously and faultily, for everything turns against those who have done wrong to the innocent, and it would be well for them not to judge if they are forgiven and cleansed by sufferance, and I am saying again: life does not end once with the body, and man has to take care of the healing of his time with sin and with enmity against his fellows in his time on earth.

Oh, sons, the man that does not go into sin and who does not love sin, that one thinks that all those around him are like him, that is they are those who do not love sin, but he who loves himself and gets sweetened with sin, that one thinks about the others as he thinks about himself, that they are lovers of sin, and many have inflicted blows on you in this way, and I do not know how to mend it and to put out their malicious inclinations and to repair their heart and mind, and finally, I will have to clean them by sufferance and put out their hostile inclinations and their carelessness of soul in which they live.

Oh, if man worked only holiness on earth and between man and man, then I, the Lord, would no longer stay invisibly among people, but rather I would stay with them and I would give them the paradise and its love, the love of a child, oh, sons. Oh, the man would no longer be confused before the spirit of the woman and of her body, unbecoming with respect to the holiness required by the Lord and given to man to be worked. He who loves Me listens to Me and lets himself carried just as a baby is held by his little hand, and he who does not love Me, does not listen to Me and he does like him, not like Me, and even more, he departs from My support; the one who is not touched by the mystery of the child love that is watched so that it may be, he goes away, for I said: *«It is not good for the man to be alone».* **(Gen: 2/18)** This is what I said, for it goes well with satan to walk alone, and satan teaches man disobedience and teaches him to hide after everything he does wrong, the one who walks alone.

Oh, sons, sons, if you want to have your heart clean towards everything and everyone then do no longer walk alone, no, never, but rather walk only accompanied like the children who walk being kept by their hand by those who watch well upon them and who protect them from those that might blame them before God and people. Satan walks with the one who is alone. The sheep without a shepherd are attacked by the wolf and the same is the man who walks alone; he is attacked by the wolf, by satan.

I taught the Christian at the beginning of My work of word and I told him this: *„Christian, do not serve the devil with the cross on your neck, because the cross discharges itself in the places where sins are made”.* Then I have taught you, sons, who help Me to come down on earth as word and make Me a book with it, and I have told you to remain in your nests flying, and to fly staying in your nests, and I have also taught you not to leave without having a house on your way and again I have told you to take your house with you, for on your way there are only people foreign to God, foreign to everything, and I have beautifully taught you to be Mine, only Mine, sons. I have advised you with hope and holy laws and I have told you not to buy food from the stores which are made by the people of the world, for the hands of those in the world are full of sin, they are not like yours, and I have told you, too, to buy uncooked food and to sprinkle it with holy water and after that to wash it with water from you and to clean it by prayer and only then to prepare it to put it on the table, and let as many as possible of you know this holy work that you have been doing for the cleanness on you and around you, for the body has its own laws from the beginning of the creation, when man was with the paradise and the paradise was with man. I have taught you the life in paradise, but that is kept with obedience, sons, for those I put in paradise from the beginning did not listen, and because they did not listen, they made Me suffer all the time, and I have been full of pain since then and up to this day.

The love of a child, this is what you have to love, and this is how you are supposed to love the Lord and your brothers, sons. This is the greatest work that I want to have it set in you and it is the most burning longing that I have. Oh, be like children not like grown up people, who keep on going and getting used to sinning. All people boast about the mystery of marriage, but who really keeps this as a mystery? Oh, the mystery of the people’s marriage is no longer kept among mysteries, for it is only fornication and it is written into the Scriptures that because of the sexual immorality each one should have his woman, lest by going after women he may fall on the way. Oh, even so, man has not been able to get peace, and behold, he decides for immorality, poor of him.

The love of a child, this is the salvation, and that is why I spoke the word two thousand years ago and I said: *«If you are not like a little child, you will not be able to inherit and belong to the kingdom of God».* **(See Luke: 18/17)**

Oh, let your thought and heart belong only to God, sons, for I, the Lord, too, have My heart and longing only to you. I have spoken with you on this Sunday and I have left a book with you and those who want My teaching will take from it, for I, the Lord, when I speak with you I also take care of those who receive the word of My teaching from you, and I give of it to them to fulfill, too, and to have much comfort, sons, for I have no one to get comforted with on earth except only those who sanctify themselves for Me, as I also treated them and did good for them to the blood and then to My resurrection. Soon, soon, we will sing the song of the Passover, the song of resurrection, the song of the crucified and resurrected Lamb, and by that time you should love to fast; love it, sons, for the life with fasting from food is the life of paradise and such a living is an angelic living. When I taught the people how to follow Me I said: *«If anyone desires to come after Me, let him deny himself, and take up his cross, and follow Me. For whoever desires to save his life will lose it, and whoever will lose his life for My sake and for the Gospel will find it. For what will it profit a man, if he gains the whole world, and forfeits his life? Or what will a man give in exchange for his life?* **(Matt: 16/24-26)** *For whoever will be ashamed of Me and of My words in this adulterous and sinful generation, the Son of Man also will be ashamed of him, when he comes in glory of His Father with the holy angels».* **(Mark: 8/38)** Oh, this is what I said two thousand years ago, and I keep on saying today the same things for those who decide to be with Me and those who will be able to work with the love of a child, with the life of paradise on earth, for I, the Lord, have chosen the Romanian country to be My country in the end of the time and I am going to accomplish this mystery within its midst.

Oh, peace to you, peace to you, sons, peace to you and to those who have received My word from you to be their food! Let them embrace the love of a child, too, for I give to those who believe in Me, I give them the power to become God’s sons, as it is written, and with them I work out My victory, for I was the Lamb, and at the same time I was the Shepherd, too, and the cross was My exaltation, and it was given to Me as a rod and glory, and it has to be a holy sceptre for the sons of the kingdom from above on earth!

The love of the Lamb, the love of a child was and is My love. I give it to the people to have it and to work it in them and in their behavior, those who deny themselves taking up their cross and following Me with it, and these are My people on earth, and I am their Shepherd and Lamb, and I am their Shepherd as well. Amen, amen, amen.
