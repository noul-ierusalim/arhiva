With a speech of Holy Spirit I become word and I come into My book at the end of the time and I write Myself in it to My confession, for Father has sent Me, and I testify about My work, given to Me by the Father to fulfill it as He has given it to Me, and I do not work or speak from Myself, but I take from the Father and this is how I fulfill. I work out My coming again from the Father to man and I work by the word as I worked from the beginning when God made the heaven and the earth. I was then the word of the Father, and I am the same today, for I am His Son. The Father, the Son and the Holy Spirit, this is God’s name and in this name I come as word into My book of today. Amen.

I strengthen My entrance and the power in the gates and this is how I come in when I speak into your midst, My today’s people. Oh, get used to stay for My waiting, for I have My coming with you, that which is prophesied in the Scriptures by My own mouth two thousand years ago. Let your preparation be warm then when I come at the feasts to you with My saints, for the prayers of My saints remind Me forever of My coming of now, My coming with them, promised by God to be, as also you are promised to be and to have God into your midst and to grow it over your life for His glory on earth among people.

[legatura_la_teme id_capitol="6" id_subcapitol="2"]Oh, My people, you have to be and to live only for God’s glory. Do not forget that this is what I have meant for you. And in order for you to know how to fulfill this, become forerunner as John, My baptizer, did, for he was the one who went before My glory among people, and you have to understand like one grown by God what it means My glory that I have from the Father before the foundation of the world. I am the light of the world, I am the light and the word, and I live on earth through My church and I am in it the light of the world. Oh, become light from the light, son. Oh, turn your soul into light from light, sons, to be the forerunners of the light from the beginning, the light not created and which will comprise the world in the light with its threefold power in the last day, for it is the Father, the Son and the Holy Spirit, and it works through the saints form God among people and this is how it works them and this is how it is My forerunner. The fleshly tongue of the man cannot comprise the glory of My light, but the tongue of the spirit glorifies it from the inside of the one illuminated by it.

[/legatura_la_teme][legatura_la_teme id_capitol="6" id_subcapitol="3"]Make your soul light from light, My people, as John, My baptizer, became, for God is light and this is how He appeared. The burning bush seen by Moses on the Mount Sinai was the light from which God spoke to Moses. The pillar of fire, which had led Israel in the wilderness was the light not created by God, which became an angel of light before man. My glory on Tabor, the light from the beginning, which shined on Me when I was transfigured before My disciples, was a light like that on the Mount Sinai when God spoke to Moses. My resurrection and its light, which I showed to My disciples in an image of angel, and then the glory with which I came out before apostle Paul on the road to Damascus, this is how the light from the beginning has appeared to many people on earth, and behold, I am the light of the world as long as I am in the world, as long as the man keeps Me on earth to be light over the people, and as for you, help Me to be, My people, help Me, son, and learn from Me what God on earth with people means, learn like John, the Baptizer, who did not perform any miracle but who was the spirit of prophecy, the spirit of the testimony before Me and before the people and proclaimed Me so that his testifying proclamation may be fulfilled.

All My words spoken into your midst, My people, mean My light, God’s light. My word is prayer upon you, asking from Me to be the witness of My word and its life in you. Look at the men’s sons, for its teeth are weapons and arrows, and their tongue a sharp sword, as it is written, but you have to be My witness for My life in you on earth, for I have come from the Father to you, and I do not come from the world but I come to make you grow to be My light in the world as John, My forerunner, was, for he was the light which prepared My way to the people so that the people might know that the Son of God was coming and to receive Him.[/legatura_la_teme]

John, My baptizer, becomes My light into your midst, My people, for I have come to you with his traditional feast and I am his word when he is speaking now into your midst. Amen.

[legatura_la_teme id_capitol="6" id_subcapitol="3"]*– Oh, sons of the light which grows you to be Christ’s witnesses! Be wise for your faith in God’s word, which is growing you today, lest the deceiver may make you lose the gift of faith, lest the old devil may lead you to unbelief. Work to be worthy of this gift, which the man has to nourish in him when God gives it to him, because by this gift so many ancestors had overcome with God and for God. Take courage by your faith! God is the word among you more than in any of the times under the sky and you are nourished with the light of His word, which you have to fulfill like sons born of God’s word, a birth that has to be seen from heaven and from earth upon you. Be great in your faith and obedience, for the devil is restless to see you falling from faith, but let your fight be in Christ, Who makes you light from light on earth. If you want to learn the power of faith, learn it from me. If you want to know the power of the spirit of confession, learn it from me. If you want to be the forerunners of God’s glory, which will comprise the world in the last day, in the day of His appearance, learn it from me, for I testified about the light. Love the light and this work is not hard! Love the mystery of the light, for the sons of men love the darkness of this passing age! Love the repentance for all that you have gone wrong for and for those that you have not gone wrong as well, and be aware of everything spoken about you of the world hostile to me and to you! Love fasting and prayer and ask from God these gifts, which worked from me among people with their power, for they strengthened me in the spirit of the confession of Christ and were my power for Him. The saints condemned themselves before the Lord for everything they went wrong and did not go wrong, and this is how they taught the people to repent. Condemn yourselves for all those that you have done wrong and for those that you have not done wrong and love the spirit of repentance, for the Lord’s righteous ones are those who repent and who are alive by faith, they are those who are alive. Amen. Love Christ’s love in you, Who let Himself be condemned for the forgiveness of many, and in this way you have to be God’s sons on earth, for the fleshly tongue cannot speak of the greatness of God’s sons, but the spiritual tongue has in it God’s revelation forever, the spirit of testimony, the weapon of those who are faithful, who pass from glory to glory as God’s sons on earth, and who the world cannot prevail against, because Christ’s church is not overcome, for so it is written. Amen.*[/legatura_la_teme]

I, Lord, worked over Your people the spirit of faith, the spirit of testimony and the spirit of repentance and I pray to You for its faith; strengthen it with the glory of Your works on earth, for on earth it is the time of the faith denial and the people fall, they fall all the time under the spirit of unbelief and under the veil of the darkness of this age, which is thicker and thicker; however, always, always give Your people the gift of the holy faith, which puts away the separation between You and man, oh, Lord, and let this teach Your people on this day of the feast, and let it grow in this way as Your confessor in the way of Your coming, for You come and come all the time as word of glory on earth and You come on the way of the faith of the sons of Your people, and blessed are and will be the faithful ones of Your coming and of Your word of yesterday and today, for everything will be forgiven to those and they will be the sons of faith and will be God’s sons on earth and they will stand before You. Amen, amen, amen.

– Oh, My people, great are the gift of the holy faith and the victory that comes from it, and it comes from the heavenly heights over the sons of faith. Faith makes the man holy; it makes him divine and leads him by filling him with the spirit of the testimony for God. Be wise in your faith, sons, lest the deceiver may make you lose the gift of faith. Be alive by your faith, and be My sons, for the unfaithful are dead and work like the dead. The unfaithful are dead to the faithful ones, for the man without faith is dead, and the faithful stay near God with their spirit, deed and life, while the unbelievers go with the world, for they are as dead as the world. Oh, do not be afraid of the dead, for God is the God of the living ones. Amen.

[legatura_la_teme id_capitol="6" id_subcapitol="3"]Oh, My people, learn the work of God’s sons, learn, son, from those who have overcome by faith, by love and by light! Your birth from above, this is what you have to seek all the time before Me. My word, which gives you birth, this is what you shall fulfill when I bring you to work. This means the work of the birth from above, and those who do not understand this, let them learn, for I have come into the world to be the light of the world, to give light to the man’s way and the man to see and know the work of life upon him; let every man know it, who believes by the gift of the holy faith, let him know what God with man on earth means. Amen.[/legatura_la_teme]

Oh, My people, I have exhorted you and you shall fulfill My exhortation to be My son, for by obedience to the Father I am His Son, and this is how you should also be; you should be and listen like Me, and you will receive a great name from My Father, a name of His Son and a reward of His Son, the reward of obedience to God, My people. Amen, amen, amen.
