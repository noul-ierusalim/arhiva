I am Who I am. I am the first and the last, as it is written by Me in the Scriptures. I am The Word of God. I am speaking and these are God’s true words, for My testimony is the spirit of prophecy. Amen.

I open the heaven I am coming down as word on the earth and the armies of the heaven are coming with Me, and of My mouth a sharp sword is coming out to strike the pagans with it, and, again, to prepare and to shepherd My people, to which I may come and write Myself into its midst with My speaking, and My name is called the Word of God, which no one understands, but only I, as it is written. Amen. **(Rev. 19/13)**

I am coming in through the gates, I am coming as word in My book of today and I am writing Myself with a feast of annunciation. My mother Queen stands on My right hand and is celebrated by the angels and by the saints because of humility by which she received Me and carried Me in her womb as a baby to be born, after My angel was sent by God to her to announce her of My seeding from the Holy Spirit into her bosom and My birth, My name, My greatness and then My endless kingdom. Amen.

Peace to you, My people! Peace to you, Israel of today! My mother, the virgin, has got into your mist her coming with Me and with her feast of two thousand years ago, when My angel, who came to the bishop Zacharias to let him know about John’s conception and then his birth, he also came after that to her and announced her about My conception, about My birth and then about My kingdom.

Oh, My people, you are My forerunner, just as John was, about whom the angel Gabriel said: *«He will go before Him in the spirit and power of Elijah, ‘to turn the hearts of the fathers to the children,’ and the disobedient to the wisdom of the just; to make ready a people prepared for the Lord».* **(Luke 1:17)** My mother, the virgin, is coming down into your midst as word and she is served by the angels and gives you powers from heaven for My work with you, for your work for Me, My people.

Come, mother, to be celebrated with the angels and with the saints into the midst of My people, who is Our house of coming from God to the people again, mother. Amen, amen, amen.

*– Bless my humility by which I bow down to speak to Your people of today, Son of the Father God and my Son, to teach today Your people the power of humility, for I, when I saw the light of Your angel that was coming closer and was speaking to me, I became small, I submitted with great decency and I asked him to teach me how to understand his annunciation for me, and he taught me and told me:* «Do not be afraid, for you have found favor with God. The Holy Spirit will come on you, and the power of the Most High will overshadow you. Therefore also the Holy One Who is born from you will be called the Son of God». ***(Luke 1:30, 35)** Then I humbled and submitted myself and I said to the messenger angel:* «Behold, the handmaid of the Lord; be it to me according to your word». **(Luke 1:38)**

Oh, bless my teaching from today over the people earned by Your word, Son Lord, for this is how You have been well pleased. Amen.

– The Father, the Son and the Holy Spirit bless your word, mother. Amen.

*– Oh, people of my Son, let my humiliation before the Lord’s angel teach you the work of humility for the Lord’s works, for the Lord is coming to you with His work of today and is giving it to you to fulfill it, and by its fulfillment you may prepare for the Lord an instructed people, and that you may go before the Lord in the spirit and the power of Elijah and that the parents’ hearts may be turned to the children, and the disobedient to the wisdom of the righteous, as the Lord worked with John, His forerunner, two thousand years ago. The Lord’s work with you turns the parents’ hearts to the sons of the holy faith and softens those who are disobedient to take in them the wisdom of God’s righteous ones, and this is how the Lord chooses a people for Himself. He descend to you on the mysterious clouds and then He speaks, and the hand of His word is gathering from here and from there and making a little bunch and putting it into the vase and this is how it is presenting it, as He is presenting you today to the sons of the people so that they may leave off the spirit of their rebellion, the spirit by which they do not serve God but only themselves and the ruler of this age and many of them, hearing and seeing from you, understand from God and put away from them their evil ways and then seek after the Lord’s ways, as you have also found and sought and then come to be afterwards. No one can do this but only those that are like me and humble and bow themselves and then learn and get used to the power of the work of humility, for I was small before the Lord and this was how I fulfilled, and I fulfilled His work on the earth in obedience. Oh, put down on a piece of paper all the words that the Lord has given to you to fulfill them and fulfill them in due time and that you may be able to speak with Him always as I said to His angel:* «Behold, the handmaid of the Lord; be it to me according to your word». **(Luke 1:38) Amen.**

This is the word of my humility by which I am bowing down today into the midst of Your people to teach him this power, Lord Son. I teach him to fulfill in due time those that you bring to him for fulfillment, and I pray to You, I pray before You to give him power, to give him love to fulfill as I did, dear Son, Your will before You. Amen, amen, amen.

– Oh, My mother, there is no shepherd, there is no better teacher than the one who lay down his life before those who are shepherded and taught by him. You have shown them your obedience, mother, and this is how you have taught them today. No one more than those who are faithful to My word fulfill better and more perfect those brought by Me to be fulfilled and settled before Me and before the people, and those who are not faithful are those who do not humble, mother, are those who do not fulfill, mother, even if We humble before them teaching them and bowing down so that they may receive Us, mother. In one day I opened the mind of those who stood before Me to receive Me as word in the book and I explained to them from the Scriptures how easy they lose their fulfillment and then their faith, those who come to believe in Me without having love, humility and the humble spirit in them for their progress in My great works, mother. I showed them why they were not able to understand clearly, those who believed in Me at the news and at the sight of My wonderful works two thousand years ago, for when it came for My fulfilled word to have room in them, they could not take it in them, they could not understand it, mother, and I proved them out to be able to help them towards Me, but they did not understand My word. I will coming to My people fed from My mouth and I will tell him how the man does not allow himself to be prepared by God’s word for the wisdom of God’s mysteries in man, for when I would try to reveal My mysteries of wisdom to the man, the man does not want to understand any more, he becomes unfriendly and then he gets up and leaves God, and even more, he blames the Lord, and he even call Him a liar, not acknowledging Him as God. Oh, well, the man is not possible to remain as he is if he comes to hear and to believe and to be then with God. He has to come and to stay at his making then, for the mind of the faithful man does no longer has to remain as on earth, but it has to take in it the wisdom from heaven and that it may speak on the earth likewise, as in heaven and not as on earth, mother.

Oh, My people, I want to open your mind with every passing day, and with every passing day you are to show Me the work of your mind too and then the work of your obedience, to show Me that you are the fruit of My wisdom by which I want to work the man today. Get used to speak with your little mouth more truly and more clearly: *«Let Your will be done, as in heaven, so on earth»*, **(Matt. 6:10)**, and do not forget that only perfect disciples speak like this, and they do likewise. Word and deed, this is how you are to be, My people. I to be the word and you to be its work, for My people from the time of those fifty years of My today’s word with it has put My word upon him to its feet and it put it down and then to sleep and did not fulfill it, and the one who does not fulfill the word in which he believes, is called unfaithful.

It is a feast of annunciation, and I announce you to have humility by which you are to bow down before My word to fulfill it as My mother did, oh, My today’s people. Strengthen yourself, sons, in the spirit of the fulfilling of My word. Put down on paper all those that I have brought to you to be fulfilled into your midst and by My tool, with which I am going to prepare My glory, and I will be your Protector, your Comforter, your Healer and Victor for you, and that we may serve one another, sons: We from heaven and you from earth; you, to be the hands and the legs of My body and of My saints’ to fulfill My word on earth, and We to be your spirit and little wings and this is how we are to do the Father’s will, for the Father’s will is your holiness by obedience to God, sons. Oh, those who do not listen, they are called immoral, for those who do not obey God with their fulfillment are called servants of other gods.

Oh, My people of today, learn from My mother, the virgin, the humility with reverence and become small, son, become obedient, as My mother became small and listened when she received Me in her from the mouth of My messenger angel that came from God upon her. The Lord, your God has always taught you. Always open yourself, My people, and take the Lord your God in you when He comes to you to give Himself to you and to give you, for His work is wonderful, wonderful, My people. Amen, amen, amen.
