God, the Father, the Son and the Holy Spirit, becomes word on the earth and is coming down into His book in these days and then He is sharing Himself so that His word may be known. Amen.

I announce Myself at the gates and I come in and My word flows from My mouth and becomes river and rain and waters the fallow soil sown by Me, for My seed is My preached word and My rain of word multiplies the seed to grow and to bear fruit in man so that the man may give forth fruit in God then. Amen.

[legatura_la_teme id_capitol="4" id_subcapitol="4"]I am the first and the last. My voice resembles the roar of many waters, as it is written, and I preach the eternal Gospel to those who live on the earth and to every nation, tribe, language and people and I call out in a loud voice: *«Fear the Lord, and give Him glory; for the hour of His judgment has come. Worship Him Who made the heaven, the earth, the sea, and the springs of waters!».* **(Rev. 14:7)**

I am the first and the last, as it is written, and I become word as in the beginning when I was speaking with the man in paradise, for the man in the end to hear Me and to fulfill My word, and if he does not want to fulfill, then I fulfill it. Amen.

I speak to the Romanian nation and to its church:

On your hearth, Romanian nation, I have a manger of the birth of My word, for I have chosen from your midst the little patch of land coming first from the waters at the making of the heaven and of the earth and on it I have made a little and tiny people and I have raised from it, from place to place, a new tribe of sons of the holy faith, and then I have taught them to love and to live on your land the law of paradise, Romanian people, for it is written in the Scriptures about the revelation of God’s sons: *«They will be like Him and will see Him, for He will dwell with them, and they will be His people, and God Himself will be with them. He will wipe away from them every tear from their eyes. Death will be no more; neither will there be mourning, nor crying, nor pain, any more. The first things have passed away»*. He Who sits on the throne said, *«Behold, I am making all things new… for these words of God are faithful and true, for I am the Alpha and the Omega, the Beginning and the End. I will give freely to him who is thirsty from the spring of the water of life. He who overcomes, I will give him these things. I will be his God, and he will be My son».* Amen. **(Rev. 21:3-7)**

[/legatura_la_teme][legatura_la_teme id_capitol="3" id_subcapitol="4"]Oh, Romanian people, I speak My word to you, for I am the first and the last and that is why I become word as I was at the first beginning, for I fulfill the Scriptures. This day of feast is a day of remembrance of the right faith, a day set by the saints and by fathers over those who believe the works of God on the earth with the people, and you, Romanian people, you are the new nation chosen from the nations to be now My people, to be My new chosen Israel, to be My New Jerusalem, but the people of the church from you want to be believed for they do not believe Me that I am the first and the last, for they do not hear Me that I speak to them, because I have spoken to them very much by this river of word that is upon you and they have not wanted to speak with Me either, if I have spoken with them. They want to be believed because they do not believe Me by this word, but it is not so, and they do like the priests in the time of My passion, when, seeing the greatness of My works, thought that they could destroy Me in such a way they could not be removed from My chair, from which they ruled over the people so that I might not rule and that they might rule instead, for they loved empty glory as those of today are doing. However, at that time I told them and I have also been telling those of today that My kingdom is not of this world and that I have come to save the world and not to rule over it as do those from it.

Oh, Romanian people, I am speaking now over those who sit over you under the name of your servants for their right faith and yours in God, for today the Sunday of the right faith is written in the Christian calendar, the Sunday of the Orthodoxy.

Oh, sons, come, come and listen to the word of My mouth and I will teach you the fear of the Lord! It is written in the Scriptures: *«He who would love life, and see good days, let him keep his tongue from evil, and his lips from speaking deceit, for the face of the Lord is against those who do evil to cut off the memory of them from the earth. Come near to Me to be illuminated and let your faces not be ashamed! Taste and see that the Lord is good. The Lord’s eyes are towards the righteous. His ears listen to their cry and His face is against those who do evil to cut off the memory of them from the earth, for those who hate the righteous One go wrong»*, as it is written. **(See 1 Pet. 3:10; Ps. 34:15, 16)** Oh, take a good look into the map, for I have sent you the map through My messengers to you. Look into the map, for by this river of word, which has been springing of My mouth on the hearth of the Romanian nation for fifty years, the land of My second coming from near the Father after the man as word, by this map I have shown you My walking of this time, My word of today, the eternal Gospel, and do not forget that I am the first and the last, and it is not possible that I may not to be with My Gospel now, in the end, for I work nothing, nothing, but only what is written in the Scriptures to fulfill it. I come to you to give you the gift of the holy faith in My word of today spoken by My mouth over the earth, and do not forget that now you commit the sin of the faith denial if you deny My word now, which comes as salvation over you to teach you so that you may not perish by the sin of the faith denial, neither you nor the Romanian people, if you are denying now, for it is coming and it has already come the sin of the faith denial, about which it is written in the Scriptures that will come before the day of My greatness when I will give each one according to his deed. In the time of My first coming, two thousand years ago, those who served in God’s house put Me in the darkness and denigrated Me so that the people might not come to Me but to go to them instead, and they put Me in the grave; they put Me in the grave but the truth sprang out from the earth and the righteousness looked down from heaven, as it is written, and those who were unfaithful were proved out, those who conspired false testimony against Me with hatred so that I might not destroy their worldly living, which is soul damaging and is far away from the spirit of the light of everything. Oh, do not hide from Me as Adam did in paradise, who wanted to hide from Me for the sake of his will and then he blamed Me and hated Me after that; however, then we separated, because I was not able to rely on the traitor, on the disobedient in paradise, the one hid in his own self from My face. It is written that: *«The Lord is good and righteous, and for this He will set a law against those who go wrong in their way».* Oh, do not make to Me any place of grave out of your places which you call churches, for I am the living One and I speak and not keep silent as you say that only two thousand years ago was My speaking with the man and then it has no longer been since then. However, did I not tell that I would be to the end of the time with those who serve Me as disciples? Did I not tell that any perfect disciple was like his Teacher, Christ? Oh, the life lived in Christ, this means Orthodoxy, and this is what the church has to celebrate. However, you should not help the people in the way you work, and you will not be able to help but only by exhorting them to fulfill the Scriptures of My coming, and to fulfill them too if you want to bear the fruit of salvation in Me, for I gave My life for My church and not you. Life is beautiful without sinning, but you do not teach the people to separate from sin and to taste the fullness of the heavenly graces, the sweetest life in man; oh, do not teach the people this way, because you sin like them too. The feast of the right faith has to be the fruit of the heavenly living of the church as I grow the people of My word chosen from the Romanians to be My people to which I have come to make a house for Me with him, to dine with him, to appear to him and to share My word through him over the earth, as it is written about those who believe in Me to their advantage and with the fruit of their faith.

Oh, people of the church, it makes Me suffer when I see that you give glory to one another, that you get up and set over the others on the chairs of greatness, to show each other to the people, and you keep Me away from you, far from your living, far from My clean serving with you, and the day of the judgment comes, and the sheep will be separated from the goats, and you have not taken care of Me, you have not visited Me, for My little ones, through whom you could be with Me and visit Me, they are those who love Me with My fulfillment in them, and you have not shown your love to them, and behold, I do not have sheep to be separated from the goats when My great day will come for the sheep to be chosen from the goats as it is written in My Gospel about the day of the fearful judgment. Oh, do not forget that My church is the first one built among the saints; not that what is made from the sinners. Do not forget that the holies are given to the saints, and the saints are those who have a holy life. Do not forget that I am the Saint of the saints, the first One resurrected from the dead, and I am not of the sinners, who do no love Me, and now the sin of the faith denial is coming to you and this heavy sin is coming closer and closer, for those who have departed from My Spirit, the Only One in the church of one thousand years ago, *(The Catholic Church, r.n.)*, want to put down to the ground My church now, which has remained and which has kept in it the image of My kingdom in man by its saints, and they try to put My face down, for it is written: *«they will get together with one mind to give their kingdom to the beast, until God’s words will be fulfilled».*

I, the Lord, the slaughtered Lamb for the salvation of those who love Me fulfilling My word, call out over the servants of the church of the Romanian nations: Do not deny your faith left by your forefathers, but completely come back to the saints and to the fathers and to the feasts removed from their places for more than seventy years7, for this replacement has brought with it now the sin of the faith denial, the sin about which it is written into the Scriptures that will be shown before My coming with the saints, for the saints will judge the world and all those that are removed from their places and all those who have spoiled the holy laws from the fathers, and for which, behold, the wrath is coming over the church left from the forefathers. There has been a succession of patriarchs in the Romanian people, and the first one has changed the feasts of the saints from their places, and the one who have been appointed now is trying to bring the sin of the faith denial over the church, and behold why I had to build My church again on its first rock by My word, and I have built it, and from this rock I have spoken with you, servants who have set yourselves to sit over the nations in My name. I, the Lord Jesus Christ, urge you to wake up well and to believe in My word that is upon you so that it may not come what is worse to you, and then to wake up the flock and to advise it from Me not from you, for the sin of the faith denial is coming and it is coming with swift steps; it is coming to put you down to the ground, and I have built My church again on the first rock, and from this stone I speak over to you, and the gates of the hell will not overcome it, and it will be and it will grow from it and through it the people of God on the earth, the new people, the Christian people, as it was at its beginning through Me two thousand years ago, for it is written into the Scriptures about the new birth of the world, and I am coming to fulfill it, as it is written. Amen.

My church of New Jerusalem, by which I speak over the flock of the Romanian people and over those who sit over it in My name, this is the church by which I will not be overthrown from My throne, and that is why I have come now and set it on its first rock, and the wise people of the time do not understand what this work is, at which they look and pretend that they do not understand. By this stone, I have saved My church from destruction, for the church of the Romanian nation is now trying to sell itself on a foreign price so that it may belong to the world and not to Christ, and behold, more than seventy years ago there was great sufferance on earth and in heaven and the times of the church were changed, as it was written for them to happen because of the church’s lack of watchfulness, and now there it is again, lack of watchfulness, *(It is about the desire of the rulers over the Romanian Orthodox Church to change the Easter holidays - the Resurrection of Jesus Christ - from the old style to the new style8, Catholic r.n.)* again denial of saints and fathers, again violation of the things left by the forefathers, and the church belongs to the world and it is no longer Mine, and I make room for My grave among those who sit over My flock and who do not give Me the glory and do not give Me the resurrection and do not receive Me with those things of today by which I come and want to bring God to life in man.

Oh, church of the Romanian people, do not spoil your face! Take heed at My word over you, for I have always called you to get up, to wake up and watch, and with every passing day to give you to understand why I have been calling you. Those who sit over you in My name do not watch for Me and for you, for they mind only their own business. Oh, keep what you have left from your holy forefathers, for the people have spoiled the holy laws and they will continue spoiling them if they have set themselves to rule and have enthrone each other over you, and you are to die without a candle as they want you to die away from the faith left from your forefathers. Oh, do not deny your faith! Do not commit this sin! Open the Scripture and see into it that it is written that before My coming there comes the sin of the faith denial, and behold, that is why you have to believe that My coming is at your door and that I have come to save you, if you want. Oh, keep away from this sin, keep away, for I have come to let you know what should do, and I will make the spirit of the prophecy come down upon you, the spirit that signifies My testimony. Amen.

Behold the sin of the faith denial, the sin that comes through the man who comes before Me, as it is written! Get up and overcome this sin, Romanian people, overcome it, for I, the Lord, will prevail against it, and I want you to do to the same so that I may give you a big reward as to the one who overcomes. I have a son in to your midst, anointed by Me and by My word in the church of the Romanian people, a son by whom I want to take the death, the darkness and the lie out of the church of this nation, but he is now being kept in chains because of his faith, and he has got his hands, his legs and his little mouth bound and he cannot work, he cannot walk and he cannot speak. Pray for My great victory, Romanian people, for My victory will be yours too. Pray for the Lord to overcome for you and not the man who rules over you to have dominion over you.

Get up, for I have great compassion on you, because I suffer because of your fate, Romanian people, for I have chosen you as My people now, in the end of the time, for I am the first and the last and what I have written now upon you remains written. Amen.

Oh, do not pull yourself out of your root, do not pull yourself out of the vine, church of the Romanian people, for if you do so, then you get dry. It is written that any branch which remains in the vine the Father takes care of it and prunes it to bring forth even more fruit, and again, it is written that the branch, which brings forth fruit by itself, it does not bring forth fruit from the vine, and its fruit gets dry once with it.

Oh, servants of the church of the Romanian people, it remains written between Me and you that what I have spoken over you and over the Romanian church. I am the first and the last. Oh, how comes that you do not take My word into consideration, so great and so dear, that which has been calling upon you for such a long time? I am coming towards you with the eternal Gospel, as it is written. Here it is! I do nothing but only that which is written into the Scriptures and I work by those who are faithful to Me, for the faith does not belong to everyone, as it is written, but I, the Lord of the church, want you to belong to the holy faith, if now the Father has wanted to reveal Me to you too, in the end of the time, but if you do not want this, then you will see.

Amen, amen, I say to you, church of the Romanian people: any of the little branches, which do not remain in the vine, the Father cuts it and throws it into the fire. Amen.

I have spoken over the church of the Romanian nation on this day of memorial of the right faith, which the church calls the Sunday of the Orthodoxy.

Now I am concluding My speaking over the church of the Romanian people.

I am Who I am. I am the first and the last. I am Who I am. Amen, amen, amen.[/legatura_la_teme]
