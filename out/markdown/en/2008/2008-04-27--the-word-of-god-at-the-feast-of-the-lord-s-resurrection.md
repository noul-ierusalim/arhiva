I am the Lord of resurrection, My people, and I am the Lord your God. I am coming down upon you as word in the book on a day of Passover, for I am your Passover and I am your resurrection as well. Peace to you! My way to you is hard for Me too, let alone for those who carry it for Me, but I love you as a God that I am, and I do for you so much what I have not done for anyone else on the earth until now, and let your faith be seen, My people. Amen.

Christ has risen! I Myself embrace you with the greeting of My resurrection from the dead, Jerusalem of today, and let your faith be great and be seen, son, for after I proved out My resurrection to the disciples I told them this: *«Go into all the world, and preach the Gospel to the whole creation. These signs will accompany those who believe: in My name they will cast out demons; they will speak with new languages; they will take up serpents; if they drink any deadly thing, it will in no way hurt them; they will lay hands on the sick, and they will recover, and those who disbelieve will be condemned. **(See Mark 16:15-18)** Therefore, teach all the nations, baptizing them in the name of the Father and of the Son and of the Holy Spirit, teaching them to observe all things that I commanded you. Behold, I am with you always, even to the end of the age»*. (See Matt. 28:19-20) Amen.

Oh, My people, behold what it signifies the one who believes in Me by My death and resurrection! I am with this one in all his days, and I perform with him wonderful signs and this is how he can preach My Gospel, for without power no one is able to testify God’s truth. Oh, great is with Me in heaven and on earth the man who can separate a sinner from his sinful works, from the demons who hardly come out of man! Great is with Me he who makes the man believe in God, healing him in My name! Stand up greatly, My people, and make of your mouth the spring of My Gospel and work, for I have chosen you to make great signs and wonders from you on the earth, and from this the man to understand God with you and the man to believe and come from death to life, for no one on the earth shows the man something else than death.

Oh, what shall I do, My people, to wean the man away from lying, to make him see the emptiness of his life and to take on My way with glory on it, that with self denial, the way which makes the man true, for the man is a liar, how shall I do it? My path is hard for Me to stay with you much in this day of glory. Almost always My walking is crushed, for the evil spirit always takes care to cut off My power so that I may not come, but I squeeze My little word of today as well as I can to show My longing after the man, for the man does not love Me, My people. Why does the man not love Me? Because the man loves himself and is not used to tread on his self to receive his maker upon him, the one who can teach him to die so that he may come to life again, for without death there is no resurrection; there is not, My people, and behold, you see that it is not. Because many have come to My work of today, but if they did not want to die they have not come to resurrection either, and they have rather come and trod upon Me and then they left. Oh, only if they might come back to receive their resurrection and to learn how to receive it! Behold, My Gospel of two thousand years ago, even then the man could not take it upon himself and nor can he do it today so that it may bring him to life. Every man knows and tells it by heart, but in order that he may believe in Me, as My mouth writes, and to show himself as faithful by proving signs as I spoke that I would work with those who believed in Me, oh, there is no way for the man to preach the message of My Gospel.

Oh, My people, those who know about God and do not want to be like Him after that will pay hard! Carelessness helps the man to stand away from My glory, and the world teaches him to be ashamed of My way and to stay in its way.

Oh, My people, you are great for Me because of your belief in My work of yesterday and that of today, and be careful, for it is written into My word after My resurrection for those who believe in Me that they will cast out demons from the people, that they will speak with new languages and will heal the people. Oh, there is no man not to have a demon in him, and the demon frowns when you want to make it upset in man. Oh, the earth is full of demons and the people are the same, and the people do not know what it is, and what the demon does in man, and the people do not know what that is and what the Lord does in man there where He is able to reign in man. The demon keeps the man near him and near his, and the Lord keeps the man near Him and near His, for the darkness is great between demon and God, between man and God. My book of today is the book of judgment in which I write the man’s sins, for I look down on earth and this is how I write the book, and then I put it face to face with the man and before this I tell the man his worldly and devilish works, for the judgment of the creature is this time of My coming again as word after two thousand years from the Father to man, and he who believes in Me and in My word of today, that one gets up and understand My word of two thousand years ago either, and one like that gets up from the dead and becomes My disciples, the disciple of My holy will and a messenger of the Gospel of the resurrection. Amen.

Go, My people, and walk in the streets and sing with a spirit of resurrection over the people! Go My people and help the people to see their ugly face and teach them to wash and to come to the resurrection! Go, Jerusalem, and cast the demons out from the people, for the people do not know that they have demons in them; take them out for the people to see them, take them out and put them face to face with the man, and put Me also face to face with the man, and make the man understand what the demon means and what God means, what it means to be with the demon and what it means to be with God on earth. Go with the faith in God, My people, and pray in secret and tell Me in secret to give light to the men’s hearts so that they may see and then the people to love God. Amen.

Christ has risen, Jerusalem! Comfort My pains! You should not hurt Me, neither by your deed nor by your mouth, My people, for I am full of wounds! Be wise so that you may know how you are able to love and to follow God on the earth! Peace to you! Learn the way of the resurrection, learn it fully over you and strengthen Me over you to be able to teach you and then for you to be taught as well! Peace to you! I am Who I am, and I tell you again: I am Who I am, My people. Oh, treat Me beautifully and faithfully, because I ask this for you and not for Me. Amen.

Christ has risen! Christ has risen! Christ has risen, My people, Jerusalem! Amen, amen, amen.
