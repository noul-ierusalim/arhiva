… The Spirit of God speaks. As it is found in the Lives of the Saints, this is how this speaking will be found as well, and it will become one book.

… Oh, Christian, you Christian son. I feel sometimes to put fire on earth, to smash it, to shatter it, but my Mother will not let me. *„My son”*, this is how my mother says, *„there are many who love me but the alcohol takes them and after they fail say,* „Forgive me Mother of the Lord!” *My Son what you can do everything for sinners! From the alcohol that they make, the alcohol spoils, degrades and destroys this earth. Oh, my Son, turn back with pity to this world!”*

… Oh, My people, some people that broke up with Me and with you come back. The ones to whom the Security spread de meal when you were closed. They come back, but I feel like closing the door for them, so upset I am. Am I not The One that was then and The One that I am now? Why do they still keep coming to Me? Why are they not sitting with whom spread the table for them? The news has come to them that the wicked will perish.

… The fire will come and devour everything and there will be a new earth and do not be afraid that I will burn you too, but do not drink alcohol. Do you believe? Even if you have fasted a month and once you drank alcohol will be devoured by fire.

Come to Me, come you from all angles! The earth will change, many will change and will forgive one another, but the blasphemy against the Holy Spirit will not be forgiven, because God has baptized the earth every year, but the blasphemers against the Holy Spirit have not gone soften. Do you hear? For the dead hear too. Hear and be frightened and stop sinning! Listen, for happy is the ear which hears; you will be happy. And come to a stop as Zacchaeus did, as the one who pierced My side with a spear and today is My martyr.

… The end has started, but nobody wants to believe.

… To the womanly side I speak now. It would be better if you do not walk with your naked body, if you want to be loved, not by the world, but by Me!

My little children, come near to Me, for I speak with you and I bleed. The woman leaves her children in the house and she goes out in the street naked; and the men leave the women in the house, with the children, and they go to the naked woman. What a distraction! One does no longer know; it is a stockyard. Oh, why do you not feel this pain, Ours and yours? Sons, you should go to bed with fear, but I better tell you to watch out, for what it is today on earth, it waves to the wrath.

… The punishment comes for the evil ones who do not want to be faithful, for many of you have brothers and sisters who will perish. Come to your senses, for disbelief runs a lot. One was faithful yesterday and he is no longer faithful today; the lusts have destroyed him. My little children, stand apart, for they will burst into flames like gas; this is how the sinful man will burst into flames.

… The earth is full of naked bodies, and because of this naked body, God has planned to destroy the whole breath. Do you believe? It is better to flee, Christian, into the wilderness, so that you may not be deceived, until this time of trouble passes. And if you know that it does not deceive you, I do not send you into the wilderness, for the reward is greater here.

… Confess your sins to each other and take the Lord’s Supper to be ready, for you do not know when God takes your spirit away and you leave.
