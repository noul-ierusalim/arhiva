[legatura_la_teme id_capitol="7" id_subcapitol="1"]… Come sons, come close and keep these words for they are holy. And the pen which writes is being sanctified, but the wicked is cursed by God. Oh, Christian, come close for the dead will come out of the grave! They will come to an end the same way someone will button his coat and their flesh will embody from the earth. Oh, Christian, then you will see a miracle and will not be happy, but will cry instead. [/legatura_la_teme]All the kings will stand before Me and will cry for they have not shepherd the people to have the future.

… Oh, Christian, you have made a vow to give one fourth to the poor, a fourth to the church and the rest for you. Did you do that? Oh, people, you will bitterly cry to pass on to God, but you will not be able to do it, for there is a war among us. Wake up, Christian, for a war like this has never been since ages, a war between God and your spirit. You will ask yourself: *“Why am I on the side of the evil spirit? Because have I worked with God?”* You may ask yourself: *“Why am I on the side of the evil spirit? Because have I served God?”* That is why I told you: multiply your prayers and My things, for they give you the good power. Children without mind, come to your senses, for I command you as I commanded Nineveh. The destruction comes everywhere and no one will get away, but only those who are God’s elected ones. Christians, let it be spread from you, from Christians to Christians, that each one to prepare his spiritual life.

… Do not get out of the divine tent and do not walk on crooked roads, for I speak truly that the day which is about to come no one will believe it until is seen, but I tell you, *“Blessed is who believes and gets ready to receive it.”* Awaken to power, for no one will escape, sons. Nor the most learned king will escape you, but only God. [legatura_la_teme id_capitol="7" id_subcapitol="1"]You will see your mother and your father when they rise from the dead. You will meet someone you have not seen for a long time. Who is pagan will not have time to deny ungodliness. Let the news spread all over the corners. [/legatura_la_teme]
