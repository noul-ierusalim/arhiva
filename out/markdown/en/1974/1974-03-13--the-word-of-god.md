… It is written in the book how many times God has washed away your sins, but you have to know that it had not happened as it happened with this earth. Sons, there have been committed many sins which Lord Jesus cannot clean off but with fire.

… It is said around the world that the age has been extended, but you should not be-lieve this because it is a lie, that the age makes God. Come and stay near Me, for you will see that it is not so. Stay with Me and walk with Me, so that it may not tear you down. Oh, My peo-ple, who will be when I come? Who will remain? Oh, My people, it is very hard to be My disciple on the day when I come, because the wind blows and sweeps it away and nothing remains on it. My people, who will hold you so that the wind may not knock you down? That no stone will be left upon another? Who will hold you so that you will not fall?
