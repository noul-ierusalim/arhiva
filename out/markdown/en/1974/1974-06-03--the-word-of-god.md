… Many gods made themselves masters so that you may serve them in your life. Keep in mind son, that no one is master over himself. Do not believe the one that said that he is his master, for he is a liar. Your master is the Lord, Jesus Christ, who gave himself to be crucified, to be beaten, to be blasphemed, to be mocked for your redemption. Many emperors made themselves gods over man, but all the breath deceived itself, Christian. Look at the animals, for if they had your voice, they would speak out that God is God. You find more wisdom to an animal, for it knows which its fold is, who its God is. Well Christian, trust yourself well and be faithful to Jesus Christ, for the Lord grew tired much when He made man to show the truth. Israel, do not deceive yourself. You are asked by the father Abraham to follow the way to the end of the road.

… Israel let your name not get lost. Israel, utterly believe that even if you are buried into the ground you will be unburied, as the one who was cut into pieces and buried in the garden and the martyr Mina gave him life. Likewise, you will be unburied from where you were buried, for God does not let his children to be buried into this ground.

… Israel My people, do not change your faith. A clean and undefiled faith is the Orthodox faith and the Orthodox religion. Amen. Religion has been abolished, *(Under the communist regime, r.n.)* but there is still faith. And this they want to abolish too, but before the door of the church is closed, one minute earlier, the Lord comes.

… Many say that there is sufferance in faith and seek to leave and they go exactly to sufferance. Be careful, for God, whom He prepares for Himself, passes him through many sorrows and through many trials, the same way as My vessel passed. *(Virginia, through whom He speaks, r.n.)*

… Israel son, My building, where is your spirit? It stays away; it does not come down into the body. Here Israel, where is the spirit from Me. When I built you, I built you to be brighter than the sun.

… My children, the faith has reached to be despised, but do not leave it, for your fathers and ancestors were despised as well.

… Oh, My little children who have children killed by the world in all forms! It is said to you: pray for them, as the sin is not only theirs, for you did not keep them as the hen under its wings, for the hawk, Satan, came and did not let them live.

… Israel, holy people, for whom I wanted well to say this holy word! Be the heir of the life; do not swerve from the right line. Be worker of holy and true things, for nothing will be left on earth, but what is holy. All the unrighteousness will be shaken.

… Israel, keep in mind that today the lie is light, and know it so that you may not use the lie, for God stands against it. There will be great darkness, *(Spiritual, r.n.)*; there will be no sun, but it will not be for more than one hour or two or nine, or for a day or two or nine. There will be many years, so that the liars will not be put to shame, *(The communists with the socialism, r.n.)* for they will put a light that does not stands. My children, when the Light rises, the dead will be on earth as leaf. Many will die of horror, by itself; others will seek death. You will know the one who was with you, dead, and you will say, *„Why did he die?”* It was discovered!

Know that when you see darkness in front of you, do not look for death, for there is no one to take you to the grave and to mourn you and to raise you to life. Be faithful and fighters for righteousness. Be as the saints, as the angels, as the torches and let no one put out your life. Be perfect for many are fighting against you, but the fight will be turned back, for whoever lifts a sword, by the sword will be killed.
