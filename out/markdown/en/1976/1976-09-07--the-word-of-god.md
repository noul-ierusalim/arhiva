[legatura_la_teme id_capitol="8" id_subcapitol="5"]… My beloved people, make no mistakes any more, as Lord Jesus will come soon and He will dress His bride and leave far away, where it is written. If the trumpet stops blowing, then you should know that God is at your door, and the wedding is coming.[/legatura_la_teme] Do not say: *“Oh, My God, what am I going to do? for God took His trumpet”*, but say that God has come and took His trumpet and everything is ready for the end.

… All the devils have come out on earth and are planning to make this world sin, for these sins which they are doing today into this country, had been made in Sodom and Gomorrah and had been made in the time of Noah. If you listen, you will not see the destruction of this world, for the Lord will take you.
