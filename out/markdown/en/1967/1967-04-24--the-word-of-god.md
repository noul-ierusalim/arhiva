I said, *„In the name of the Father,”* because it is the beginning and the end. Amen. I said, *„and of the Son,”* because all will receive the Spirit, as it was in the beginning and in the end. Amen. I said, “Amen!”, so be it. Amen.

Nothing without God, for God is your Father. No one will enter My kingdom by his own will. I am the Alpha and the Omega. Whoever seeks after Me now, I will seek after him, too.

Oh, Christian, I have come to bring My joy, from above, because everything is over.
