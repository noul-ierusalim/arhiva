I announce Myself at the gates to come in as word in the book and to sit down with a table of word for those in heaven and for those on the earth who love My voice of today, taking Me as their comfort and Teacher.

I am the Lord, Jesus Christ, and the glory of My word rejoices the heaven of saints and angels. All of the heavenly armies sit down at My table of word on the earth. The holy heaven is celebrating for My holy bishops, Basil, Gregory and John. They worked My testimony and its graces on earth, and their work waited for them in glory, crowned them and made them workers from heaven on earth, and all the saints are now in My coming, and many on the earth are nourished from My mouth, when I become word in the midst of My people.

Oh, people nourished from heaven, I feed you, son, I have always fed you, comforting Myself when I speak to you. I strengthen My comfort over you to become divine, and to have you as My dwelling place, for My comfort gives the man heavenly powers if the mystery and its work can seize the man, My mystery with the man on earth, for I, the Lord, am the myste-rious One, and I am clothed in the Father, and the Father is in Me and with Me, and only the faith from heaven can help the man remain on the side of My mysterious truth, because the faith on the earth is like the man who passes away, and it is not like God, for the Lord is eter-nal, My people, and the same were and are His confessing saints of the truth above everything and which has become the eternal inheritance of My saints. Amen.

I, the Lord, strengthen My comfort into your midst, My people. It is My comforting word, which exhorts you to raise the confessing work up to Me: faith, hope and love, and these build each other in man when the man fulfills in him the commandment of life, the love for God with his entire being all the time, and the love of brothers, and the brothers are those who love God according to the commandment of the love from above, the commandment of life, about which the voice of the apostle said: *«We know that we have passed from death to life because we love our brothers»*. Amen, amen, amen.

— Lord, bless the entrance of Your saints. Amen. We come into Your book, oh, Lord, because You have taken us on Your right side, now, within Your descent, and we are confessing You the Master of love and of those who love You, for You love the Father and all those who You have from the Father, the sons of the love from above, who had and have as their work the faith, the love and the food for the love from above in man, oh, Lord of Your saints.

[legatura_la_teme id_capitol="6" id_subcapitol="3"]We, those who are confessed today among Your saints were brothers by the love from above worked on the earth among people, for we loved the mystery of brotherhood and its power from heaven upon the brothers, Lord. Oh, there is no greater power of Your fulfillment, of Your confession, than the power fed from the brotherhood full of You among brothers. There where You mark everything, there the commandment of life is fulfilled with great power, Your love among brothers, Lord. Only those who have You as their life, only they are brothers with brotherhood, which is not spoiled, and which lasts then among brothers in heaven, Lord. We always, always passed from death to life because we loved Your people and worked much for Your church, Lord, and the wolves that were lying in wait for it were afraid of us then and that is why they became worse in themselves as some who did not fulfill the commandment of love, the commandment of life, Lord.

Now, Lord, we strengthen the love from You of those whom You have made Your sons on the earth, and we tell them this: Love the commandment of life and always work it, passing from death to life, always, always by your love among you, oh, sons of the holy people, for the Lord stands into your midst and does not grow less sharing to you to have His love in you and to be like Him among brother and brother. Be wise, be more and more wise. Brothers are those who love each other, helping one another, giving themselves to one another, cleansing each other, and feeding the love from above in them and among them all the time, for great is the mystery of the heavenly mystery on earth, and its work becomes the light of the people and it gives birth from it confessors from God, as we worked and fulfilled, and then we rested in the fruits of the faith by which we hoped for the love from above and within which we worked on the earth.[/legatura_la_teme]

We exhort you, those who are anointed with the chrism of our graces, to be one always, always in the spirit of the holy prayers, by which you may be able to ask for the work of the grace upon you, and to be able to work by it the light in the midst of the holy people, light of which all the spirits of the darkness may be afraid, a restless spirit now, and to go away from the way of the work of God with you. However, you should be sons of the brotherly love, for the power of the brotherhood from above gives birth to the Lord’s blessing over brothers. Bear with abundant life in you the graces put upon you and work their work, gathering in the Lord your entire work, in order to be rich on earth and in heaven, as we also were and are. Faith, hope and love feed each other when they have as work the love among brothers, sweet work, anointed sons, work which unites the heaven with the earth, and the saints with the people, and may the spirit of obedience to God and to His entire word be your way to the Lord with all your holy requests, by which you may persevere before the Lord for your salvation and for that of the world, for the world is careless of its salvation, and you are established by the Lord as in-tercessors for the renewal of the world. Be wise and work in the spirit of humility and repent-ance, for the renewal of the world is work with heaviness and it is the Scripture, which has to be fulfilled among you, anointed sons.

Oh, Lord, we work from near you, and we work like You and we confess You, and we confess those whom You made Yours and those who have their duty to follow You, Lord, and to become love from heaven on earth and that You may be able to work in them and among them, Lord, for all of the saints wait for Your complete victory, which will be to appear soon, soon, by Your new people, the people of Your word, which has in its midst the table of the Holy Spirit, the heaven and the earth together at the table of the Holy Spirit, oh, Lord. Amen, amen, amen.

— I glorify Myself through you, My loved saints, and as I glorified Myself on the earth, when I had you as the vessels of holiness, vessels full of anointing oil, the anointing oil of the holy teaching, vessels full of holy longing, the most sweet smelling anointing oil, the most sweet sacrifice brought to Me, by those whom I love for their love by which they give themselves to God to make Him good on the earth. Bring prayers before the Lord full of longing for My people on the earth, for I want to clothe it in the love from above, and that with every passing day it may work the power, which is born from the holy brotherhood among them. [legatura_la_teme id_capitol="6" id_subcapitol="3"]The mystery of brotherhood is clean, but few are those who love and work it, for those who do not know to deny themselves for the work of the love, they do not come and do not touch the mystery and the power of the brothers from above among brothers. Those who love each other in the spirit of the heavenly brotherhood do not draw the fruits of the brotherly love to themselves, but, on the contrary, they always, always give them to each other on earth as in heaven, for love cannot work otherwise, because it is that which does not look for its own things, and behold how far the people from the holy truths are, which they have nowhere to learn from and to get used with them in their work for their life.

Oh, My people, I want you to be My fulfilled commandment, for the whole life is a commandment, and the people have violated the commandment of life and the life that loves with the love, which does not seek after its own benefits and which multiplies itself in man giving itself further, and not working otherwise.

Oh, My people, whenever I can speak to you, I get comforted. Be comforted as well with My speaking and get rich with it and always work the mystery and the power of the brotherly love, the love from above, for the place where the brothers live together has to be a place which burns giving light on the sides and which it gives itself to God then as the spring and offering of sweet fragrance, to the confirmation of My blessing over those who love themselves within Me, and loving each other, they become My kingdom, My rest, the heaven for God, and they also become the light of the world by the spirit of peace and of the holy brotherhood among them, and further, they become ways among themselves for those who are saved. Amen, amen, amen.[/legatura_la_teme]
