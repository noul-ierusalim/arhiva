*(13-09-2007 – forty days from the death of the patriarch of the Orthodox Romanian Church)*

[legatura_la_teme id_capitol="3" id_subcapitol="4"]The Father, the Son and the Holy Spirit, this is the name by which the Word of God descends over the flock of the Romanian people. Amen.

I am Who I am and I am the Word of God. I have prepared a place, over which My voice of Shepherd speaks, and this place is in the midst of the Romanian people, of which I have raised a disciple people, working together with Me in these days over My vineyard, over the soul of the Romanian people, to which I have come down again from the Father on earth to man, and I am enveloped by the word. Amen.

My godly hand holds in it the little hand, which writes My holograph for the flock of the church of the Romanian people, and My voice fills with the word the garden in which I descend for My word to be written down.

I embrace you within the word, Romanian people, chosen flock, so that I, the Lord, may come into your midst and become word, now, in the end of the time. I cannot pass over your pain, even if you do not know that you have it, for he who sleeps beside Me does not feel his pain and sickness, which makes Me suffer for his sake. You are under the pain that you do not know that you have it, but because you will soon feel it, I come closer to you to comfort you and to tell you that those who have established each other by themselves over you without Me, to do you good, as they say, but they do not care about you but rather about themselves and about their glory, because they forget that above all their human things God watches and judges everything as God, and not as man, for behold, the man, who calls himself minister and overseer over ministers in My name upon you, commits sin upon you, as though he would watch for you and over you, but those who have established themselves as overseers over you as to shepherd you, as they say, they cannot even protect themselves from those who are to come upon you and upon them from the sin of the denial of the faith from the saints, *(The year of 1924, when the church established the changing of the feasts from the Julian calendar to the Gregorian one, r.n.)* for the church, which was supposed to remain My image among those who call themselves God’s people on earth, does no longer have a clean little dress, and behold, it sold it and sells its little dress on money and on human glory, and it does this through those who are rulers on it.

I hold you tightly on My affectionate bosom of Shepherd coming down from heaven to you; flock of the Romanian people, for the abomination of desolation comprises you more and more everywhere, as the prophets of the Scriptures prophesied about the abomination of desolation, which here it is where it enlarges its place! You are almost sold out, and you do not know this, flock of the Romanian nation. There have come and established into your name and over you people clothed in word and garment of holy man, and these are not from Me, and they are rather from men, for a shepherd over a flock is not established in this way, oh, not in this way!

Behold what tears of mourning run down now before My throne for your life, flock enslaved by those who lie to you that they are set from Me over you! He who was patriarch upon you by this time is mourning now, for the thread of his life has been cut by those who are greedy of glory and of the shepherd seat of honor, *(The rulers of the Romanian Orthodox Church recommended to the former patriarch to undergo an operation, although the doctors affirmed that he will not resist and thus he died, r.n.)*, and for his sending to Me without My will, I have passed many of his sins to those who have still established themselves to reign on higher and higher seats, giving glory for glory to each other. However, open your ear, flock of the Romanian nations, to hear the spirit of the one who speaks with Me before My throne, now, when I, the Lord, am full of sighs and mourning for your life, My pain that he has seen and felt it now with his entire spirit. There have passed forty days from his arrival to Me from your midst, and I am releasing now his spirit so that you may hear his speaking with Me before you. Amen, amen, amen.

*– Great Lord in heaven and on earth, my little soul has received wings from my angel, as soon as the man separated me from my body, after I had sat on the seat of the church of the Romanian people, established within it, once by man’s will and mine too, and once again by Your will, Lord, after the Romanian nation got up from its slumber to pull out the darkness from its eyes and to see,* (At the anticommunist revolution in 1989, when the dictator Ceauşescu was banished from power and executed by shooting, r.n.), and I was then on the seat of the church, and I was threatened to leave it to save my life and the seat to remain empty for the one who was supposed to come after the changing of the time. I withdrew with humility and modesty, but aggrieved, at the same time, for the burdensome fate over the flock of the Romanian people. However, You, Who hold into Your hand Your well done entire council and looked to me to lift me up and to bring and set me back over Your flock, tested by its enemies from its midst, for the Romanian people is Your chosen people now, Lord. You descended then as prophetic word into the little garden of Your word from Pucioasa and You commanded those from the mouth of Your spring of word to look for me and to whisper Your will upon me. And they found me and spoke the word on Your behalf, which was telling me to come back, in front of the flock, for Your eye of Shepherd was watching over my life and its protection and that of the flock. When I received word on Your behalf, Lord, Your Spirit seized me within Him and I believed and fulfilled with humility that which You required of me. You are the One, Who established me then again on the seat of the church, and I sat on it and then I could listen to You and fulfilled Your plan by Your power.

There had passed a little time, and then You sent to me the one whom You chose for Your living church of the Romanian people. Then I listened to him with the entire word coming on Your behalf to me through him, the word by which You were announcing Your heavenly plan over the church, and which I believed and fulfilled. I stood up, at Your holy and right word, and I established on Your behalf the one You have loved for this time. (The bishop Ioan Irineu, r.n.) No one appointed him when it was to be set as shepherd from You for the sheep. Then I stood up and I told everyone about My appointment for him, the appointment coming from You, and then he was established, and I did as You commanded me to do, Lord. You sent him to me with Your great longing for the building of a new and holy establishment, prophesied from You to be over the face of the Romanian people. Then I fatherly listened to him and understood Your message sending to me through him. My Spirit, seized then by Your joy, was joined with You, rejoiced, believed and fulfilled, and he went with Your consent and mine and established the New Jerusalem church from Pucioasa by a heavenly commandment. Oh, the time grew short, and all those who were put to shame by the lack of the fulfillment of Your glorified face and of Your great will, Lord, arose with hostility, within a human spirit, and knocked down the one You chose, as it also happened to You from the rulers of the temple, who did not want You to be for them as the One sent by the Father. Then I could no longer protect the one You chose and sent among them, and now he has been kneeled down, and all those who call themselves great shepherds, have humiliated him and kept him in bondage, Lord.

The work of Your word from Pucioasa has filled the heaven with its mystery from You, and on the earth all are blind around it, and only those who reach to You come to see through the light and are shaken within their spirit, if they pressed down with their own hand to put this light under the bushel, with which You have come again on earth, and You have come into the midst of the Romanian people. Your gentle word is the light, and there is no man to follow it, for the man stays in the darkness, Lord, and the darkness cannot comprise the light in it, but Your light of today of Your affectionate word of Shepherd gives light in the darkness, and the darkness flees in the darkness, Lord.

I feel very sorry for the one kept in the stocks by those who sit on the thrones of church, and I am very much sorry for him, as I also felt sorry for him on earth after that he was misunderstood and humiliated by many. However, I want now, with Your consent, that all those who rule over the flock of the church to know from me and from You that they are not. He is, because You set him, Lord, but they are not. I sat twice on the seat of the church of the Romanian people, and You set Me on it the second time, and You called me the seventh patriarch, announced by You even in my time. And now You have given me free access to the earth with the word near You word, so that the church may hear Your speaking with me, and mine with You, into Your name for the church of the Romanian people, which does no longer have shepherds, for they are and will be anything else but shepherds, and all go wrong from their places because of their unbelief, by which they wrote their denial of You, their denial of their fathers and of saints, Lord. However, You had mercy on me for my work for the light of Your work, as it is written into You book of today about me, the book of Your word of fifty years over the Romanian people, and now You have given me free access to take rest, forgiving all my sins by my sacrifice on Your altar, for I want to get up and to set a limit to the darkness, which started to come over the church of the Romanian people, and which, here it is, it has been spreading all over, Lord, and the one who brought me as a sacrifice on Your altar did not do any harm to me, for I arrived to You, and I will be Your confessor from the midst of Your victorious church, by which Your saints come to rest and support Your saints on the earth. Amen, amen, amen.

– The Father, the Son and the Holy Spirit, in this name I, the Lord, have descended now as word over the flock of the church of the Romanian people, near Me with the one who was by this time the patriarch over it, and I do no longer count over it, for I have come to an end with My counting. Amen.

You, who were the patriarch over the Romanian people twice, I have written you under the name, testifying from heaven about My work of New Jerusalem on the Romanian land, and I set you to rest and in heavenly work over the flock of the Romanian people, for you were sacrificed on the altar of this nation, which you loved and for which you labored, after I, the Lord, established you by My word to sit once again on the seat of the church of this nation and to fulfill My will. The one you established on My behalf for My church, this is, and the others are not, but they are rather from the Satan’s synagogue, and they call them-selves My people, but they are not, and they lie instead, and I will make these people worship in heaven and on earth before the feet of My loved one, and they will know that I have loved him, as it is written in the Scriptures for this time. And again, I tell you this: I have set before you an open door, which no one can shut because, although you had little power you kept My word and did not deny My name. I know your works and you came and I gave you the crown of endurance, and now I am writing on you My name and the name of My citadel, of the New Jerusalem, which has come down from heaven from God into the midst of the Romanian people, and My new name, the Word of God. Amen.

He, who has an ear, let him hear what the Spirit says to the assembly of the Romanian people. Amen.

I finish My word over the flock of the church of the Romanian people.

I am Who I am. Amen, amen, amen.[/legatura_la_teme]
