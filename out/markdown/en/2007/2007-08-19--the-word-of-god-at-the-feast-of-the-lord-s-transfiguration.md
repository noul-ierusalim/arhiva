I come into the book with a spirit of heavenly feast between earth and heaven. I am the Lord, Jesus Christ, the Son of the Father Sabaoth, and I become word over the earth on His right side, and the Father is with Me in My coming down to man. Amen.

Two thousand years ago, I came down from heaven and became flesh in a virgin’s body with the help of the Holy Spirit, Who is in the Father and in Me, and I appeared as God-man, and I appeared in the image that I had from the Father born before eternity, and now My mysterious image becomes word of God’s glory on earth, and I speak with the man as I spoke with Adam in heaven and I exhort the man to come back in the homeland that I made for him after I worked him in My image and after I gave him a living soul and I called him man.

[legatura_la_teme id_capitol="7" id_subcapitol="3"]Peace to you, people of My word of life giving! I glorify Myself into your midst, I become word and mystery of word, for I refresh Myself from pressure when I speak to you and when I can tell you a mysterious word, a word not born of Me by this time. Little by little, My being gets ready for its appearance among people, and I pour Myself out little by little, and I work this by the word of this mystery, and then I let Myself be discovered. Amen. [/legatura_la_teme]

Peace to you, people who believe in My coming after two thousand years again from the Father to man, for I work it mysteriously, and you are the faithful one, and I want you to be heavenly and not earthly on earth, sons, for [legatura_la_teme id_capitol="2" id_subcapitol="1"]My disciples, when I appeared to them in the mountain in the midst of the glory that I have from the Father in heaven and on earth, they wanted to make Me a little house and to live in it in the mountain, and Moses and Elijah to remain with Me in the mountain, for I appeared with them to the disciples, so that they may see My speaking about My passion and about My resurrection.

[/legatura_la_teme][legatura_la_teme id_capitol="6" id_subcapitol="2"]Oh, My people, you should not be earthly, for the mystery of My work with you is great in your days, and I want you have great visible wisdom, for I enshroud you much in the mystery of My coming and I ask you much to work for its perfection, and to work as in heaven, and not as on earth, My people. In heaven it is not worked by hand for God, but by the spirit, sons, and that is why I have taught you that your work done by your hand should not be tangled with the one done by the spirit for Me, for I need you to be heavenly on earth, My people, and I need many disciples, for My coming to you is great, and the man cannot comprehend it, but I said that it was not good for the man to be alone, and I have taught you to gather together in little clusters and to have the Holy Spirit, the One Who shares, the Giver and the Worker of My coming to you, and then on earth, My people. I tell you all these to make you wise and to be heavenly on earth, and not earthly, for I closely taught My disciples, two thousand years ago, everything that had to be with Me on earth for My glory in their time, but they were earthly and did not understand what God meant and what man meant, because I, if I came incarnated on earth, I appeared as a man completely.

[/legatura_la_teme][legatura_la_teme id_capitol="2" id_subcapitol="1"]Oh, it is not possible to be earthly on earth, My people, for I have to do great works with you for Me, sons. Oh, get up to understand like God, for when I was with My disciples on earth and I started to prepare them and to tell them about those that were to happen to Me among people up to the glory of My resurrection, and then to My ascension again near the Father, in one day I asked them who they thought the people said that I, the Son of Man, was, and who they said that I was, and Peter, answered Me that I was the Christ, the Son of the living God, and I confirmed the testimony, which the Father put into his mouth and I told him that on that rock *(The testimony that Jesus is the Christ, r.n.)* I would build My church, and the gates of the hell would not overcome it, and then I told them not to tell anyone that I was the Christ of the Father. Then, little by little, I started to tell them that I had to suffer in Jerusalem from the rulers of the priests and from the scribes and that I would be sacrificed, and that only in the third day I was to come from death to life again, but Peter, taking Me aside, rebuked Me and advised Me to be merciful on Me, so that it might not happen something like that to Me. Then, I left him behind Me and I called him satan, and I told him that he was a stumbling block for Me if he attributed to Me those that belong to the people and not those that belong to God. Then I rose with teaching for them and I told them this: *«If anyone desires to come after Me, let him deny himself, and take up his cross and follow Me. For whoever desires to save his life will lose it, and whoever will lose his life for My sake will find it. For what will a man give in exchange for his life, when the Son of Man will come in the glory of His Father with His angels, and then He will render to everyone according to his deeds?»* **(Matt. 16:24-27)** There have passed six days after that, and taking Peter, James and John, I revealed My uncovered face before them, which shined like the sun, and My garments were as white as light, and I had Moses and Elijah on My right and left side, and I spoke with them before My disciples about all the things that were to happen with Me in Jerusalem. Then Peter, being from the earth, told Me: *«Lord, if You want, let’s make three tents here: one for You, one for Moses, and one for Elijah»*, **(Matt. 17:4)**, but a bright cloud overshadowed them, and a voice came out of the cloud, and My Father said to them: *«This is My beloved Son, in Whom I am well pleased. Listen to Him».* **(Matt. 17:5)** However, they were afraid and fell on their faces, and I touched them and I encouraged them and lifted them up, and I finished the appearance of My glory and then I commanded them not to tell anyone what they saw until I would rise again from the dead. [/legatura_la_teme]

My people, I have told you these now, to show you how earthly My disciples were, My disciples with whom I was preparing the Gospel of My crucifixion and resurrection from among the dead, and of the descent of the Holy Spirit upon the disciples afterwards, and this descent had to be prepared, sons. Oh, this is how I have prepared you too, disciple people, and I want you to understand My coming heavenly, for I want you to be heavenly on earth, so that I may fulfill My coming down, My people. Oh, I cannot do anything with the earthly man, I cannot, sons, I cannot, because that one can do it by himself, and he can as on earth, just as Peter wanted to make Me a house in the mountain and to stay there, and this is how the people has made a house for Me since then and up to this day, but I do not live in it, and I rather live with those who are faithful and heavenly and working together with Me on the earth. Amen.

[legatura_la_teme id_capitol="6" id_subcapitol="2"]I speak you a word of great mystery, My people. I want you too, to change your face before the people, as I appeared before My disciples in the mountain two thousand years ago. Be faithful, for this will be My glory and not yours, but it will have to fulfill, as it is written in the Scriptures, sons. Therefore, brother with brother, exhort yourself, for I have given you the key of the mysteries of the kingdom of the heavens, and whatever you bind on earth will be bound, and whatever you release on earth will be released, **(See also Matt. 16:19)**, as it is written about the work of My disciples. Amen.

Work according to My will, My people, work, sons, work, for little by little, My being gets ready for its appearance among people. Little by little, I pour Myself out, and by the word of My mystery with you I work this, and then I let Myself be discovered, My people, and I do no longer tell you not to tell anyone about My mysteries, but I tell you now to proclaim them instead, as then when they will be fully seen and understood, for the world to understand that the Father has sent Me to you and that I have been with you on earth and that the world has not known Me and has not believed Me that I have come to you from the Father as word, clothed within a cloud of word on the outside, and that I have come by a great name on earth and My name is called the Word of God. **(Apoc. 19:13)** Amen.

Oh, My people, this mystery is not only for you to understand, but you have to live it and to fulfill it as well, for I have come to you on earth and you are My house, son. Behold what kind of house I want the man to build Me on the earth! Behold a misunderstood mystery by the man, who, like Peter, wants to build Me a house on earth. My house is the glory, which I have from the Father from the foundation of the world, the glory, which cannot be comprised by the house made by man, and the man’s mind and sight cannot comprise it either, for I am as the heaven and the earth taken together, and I am their Creator. Amen.

[/legatura_la_teme][legatura_la_teme id_capitol="5" id_subcapitol="6"]I declare now the blessing with outpouring glory from it over those who will come together near you at the spring of My word in the day of celebration for My mother, the Virgin, My people, who get ready yourself together with Me for the days of God’s glory on earth with the people. Oh, strengthen yourself in the spirit of obedience, sons, for there is no love and there is no greater faith than obedience, My people. Prepare, therefore, the day of the glory of the feast, adorn it with glory from you for Me, and I, the Lord, will sit upon it with My glory and I will take My people of old from glory to glory, the people, which I called and call to the spring to glorify Myself upon it with the spirit of the heavenly wisdom and to exalt it from the earthly things to those from heaven, for I want to support it with great teaching if he listens to it to turn it into deed. Amen.

May the feast with the heaven of My people, which comes to the spring, be blessed. Open the gates of the meeting garden, sons who prepare the glory of the feast, and shelter the people in the garden and make it sit down, and I will give to My people, because I am full of gifts, sons.

Now, I bless upon you this day of today and its celebration and everything on its table, and then we will come back for a little while into the book to rely on you in My pains, for My pains are great, and I long for My rest after the pains, My people, and you should bear with My longing and give My house for it, for he who knows to make a house for God on earth is great with Me, oh, one like that is big, when no one knows any longer among people what God in heaven and on earth means.[/legatura_la_teme]

I set fire to My longing in you, My people, and let yourself be caught by it, sons, and come to the spirit of comfort for Me and for My pains, for I have waited for the Holy Spirit, the Comforter from the earth to comfort Me in My wound. I have given you the Holy Spirit in abundance, My people; make Him My comfort with you and then you will know yourself that you are Mine, and then you will know Me fully before you, and we will also know each other and comfort each other and will take rest, and we will work for the glory of My visible coming, and all and everything will be renewed and will be comprised in My glory and yours, so that the world may believe that the Father has sent Me to you, oh, My people. Amen, amen, amen.
