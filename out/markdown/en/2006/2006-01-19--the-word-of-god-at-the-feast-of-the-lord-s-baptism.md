I am coming down with the saints and with the angels on the Romanian land within a great feast, into whose midst I have got the dwelling of My coming and the throne of My book in the end of the time. Amen.

In the name of the Father and of the Son and of the Holy Spirit, I am coming down into the book with a feast of Epiphany, with grace from heaven over the waters of the wells of the heavenly gardens in My courtyards, in the Lord’s gardens. I have let you know, My people who take care and prepare feasts for those in heaven and for those on earth, and I have told you that many souls will come together to hear My voice, which sanctifies everything, and to receive the blessed water made holy by the word and to sprinkle the land from place to place, and the heaven to rejoice on earth at the work of the water sanctified by the word, for it will work with the power of the mystery of My place from here, from where, [legatura_la_teme id_capitol="5" id_subcapitol="5"]I, the Lord, established My church on the stone and I sealed it by My word fifteen years ago and by the word of My bishop within these days, then when I have made up the place and the ark *(altar)* of the Holy of Holies of the New Jerusalem visibly on the hearth of the Romanian people, where I have made again a people for Me in order to have him at My coming and to let the book of the judgment of the creature into its midst. Amen.[/legatura_la_teme]

I am embracing you within a spirit of Epiphany, you those who have come together at the spring of life, at the Jordan of life. And you should also embrace, with your heart in you and with the thrills of the Holy Spirit, My heaven of saints and of angels with thousands of thousands, who, round and round, up and down, to the right and to the left, stay and serve this day of feast, the sanctification with the seal of the grace of the Holy Spirit by My word and by the word of the anointed ones of My garden of today, the sanctification of the water of the gardens and of the courtyards in which, [legatura_la_teme id_capitol="3" id_subcapitol="4"]I, the Lord, have been laying down the mystery of the kingdom of God’s sons, the coming out of this mystery on earth, and which the people, who call themselves My church on earth, have not got the gift to know it and to rejoice over it. They do something else on earth, and they do not do My work; they do not work My last Scriptures. They work like people and not like God, because they have been establishing themselves, and I have not established them to be what they say that they are. However, My word is at the helm here, and this means God’s Kingdom and not man’s. Here, I, the Lord speak the word and fulfill it all over the earth, for the mystery of the fulfillments of the Scriptures of My coming with the saints now, in the end of the time, needs the speaking of the word, the people faithful to My word, and the holiness of the spirit, of the soul and of the body of those who were written to be the mystery of God’s sons on earth, a mystery that has been waited for seven ages, since the spirit of the man separated from the Spirit of God, no longer listening to his Creator.[/legatura_la_teme]

Oh, the day of feast is a great day, it is a great angelic work and there are many invisible people, and I, the Lord, together with you, My anointed ones in the little garden of My word of today, speak the word and say: the waters of the wells of the gardens and of My courts with you are blessed and sanctified with the power of the grace of the Holy Spirit, the Comforter. The steps of those who have come in time of frost and white bedding of snow are blessed by the sprinkling with these waters, so that the saints from above and from below may be able to take the water, sanctified by God through the word, by a great ministering of angels, with thousands of thousands; and let those who take water in their vessels sprinkle the land everywhere, and to sprinkle the graves of their relatives who are asleep, for the Lord waits for all and everything on the earth by the fulfilling of the new heaven and of the new earth, according to His promises and His justice. Amen.

Oh, people of My courts, you have labored with much commitment to prepare for the Lord, for His heavenly suites and for all who have come within a feast pace of Epiphany, and behold, You have prepared: the Lord with those in heaven, and you, from the earth, and you will set at the table those who are carried by the Spirit to the eternal spring. [legatura_la_teme id_capitol="6" id_subcapitol="3"]I advise you, as I have also done it by this time, to be a child, My people, and to be good and obedient, as well as I am before My Father; you should be a good child both with the Lord and with the man, so that the man may see that you work beautifully and that you do not do like Lucifer, who wanted to get up above God, as all the servants on the earth, who are called Mine, do their work and the work of the man, and these people cannot listen to God, to His Scriptures and to the Scriptures of His saints, who followed Him on the earth strengthening the foundation of the church, as I taught them, that is My saints, My people, and I am also teaching you today to be God’s son, the Scriptures that have been waited by the saints for the redemption of their righteousness, by which they have been working out My justice in every time until your time.[/legatura_la_teme]

[legatura_la_teme id_capitol="3" id_subcapitol="4"]Oh, the man is very, very heavy, and I can no longer carry him. There are many who are not light even for their own soul let alone for others’ soul, let alone for God, My people! If My love has been teaching the people and speaking to them: *«Love one another as I have loved you»*, then the whole love of those who love like Me has to be gathered within Me. However, the man has become Lucifer, and the man is very, very heavy for Me. The man is heavy for Me to carry him to the Father, and I cannot reconcile the man with the Father.

I have set a man down on the earth, coming with good news, a bishop testifying about this work of reconciliation of the man to the Father, and the bishops of the world of today have been hiding him from Me. And they, instead of embracing him as the God’s chosen one among them, as he is the wise man who has come from Me, just as Eli did when I raised Samuel for Israel, when Eli’s sons were no longer listening to God, working like Lucifer before Me, they, the bishops of the world of today, have forgotten to know that the one who does not commit sin and does not become haughty, that is the wise one, and he is also faithful within the spirit of God’s fear within him.

Oh, how shall I reconcile the man to the Father? The man is very heavy. The man in the world is heavy and I can no longer carry him. I have chosen the one who is Mine from the bishops of the world so that he may reconcile the man to the Father, for I have got My word over the earth and I receive it from the Father and I take it and I speak it, like an obedient Son to His Father. I have sent to these the one who was a bishop over the city of Târgoviște, so that he may confess from among the heavenly bishops, but they, hearing, pretend that they do not hear and believing they pretend that they do not believe, and they say that the dead are dead if they are no longer on earth. The witch from Endor called Samuel up to tell Saul a word of teaching in his great trouble, and this happened with My own accord. I have brought with Me Basil, the bishop, who held the seat of the city of Târgoviște. He had been fighting for seven years against Me and had been doing the work of Lucifer over the work of My word, and I have brought this to tell the bishops and the priests that they will fall upon each other if they do not receive the spirit of prophecy upon them, the word of the righteous One, Who is written to come in order to give birth to the world again. Amen.

I am coming down from heaven down on earth to help the man come to the Father, but the man is very, very heavy. Oh, how shall I carry him any longer? I advise you, My people, to bring up to Me a spirit of prayer for all the promises that I still have to establish on earth in order to come down with all the power before those who are not faithful, who mind only their own business, calling themselves shepherds over their church. I long, and I very much long for My bishop with whom I have worked near among you. They have been keeping him in submission to them and they have been keeping him hidden, but My glorified time is coming, and the glory of those, who made their kingdom on earth under the name of church, and they still do this, when this glory of them will depart from them.[/legatura_la_teme]

However, I, the Lord sprinkle the earth with tears and I make the waters from them drip upon the waters from you, and you should sprinkle over the earth with the sanctifying water of My word, in which I let My drops of tear fall, the tear that is under the sufferance I have been enduring, for the man is very, very heavy for Me, sons.

It is a feast of Epiphany, and the people, that has gathered together, has been waiting for you to give him a warm welcome into My houses with you, to set him at the table, for it has travelled a long and difficult way, sons. Warm up the hearts of all those who have come together at the Jordan of My word, for you sit on My behalf before them, and John, My god-father, tells them: *“This word is God’s Lamb, Who washed away the sins of the world then when he died for the man and He washed them with His own blood.”* Amen.

Oh, My people, I stay with John at the border, for his word waits once with his feast, and you warm up the one who has come a long way to Me and to you, for you are My tool, My confessor, and give to the one who comes to Me and to you; give him a spirit of life giving, for this is the fruit, which comes out of the work of the new man, born from above, in My image and after My likeness, for My love in the name of the love, which I put it over the man when, writing it on the stone, I left it over the earth for the man. Amen, amen, amen.
