I bow from heaven down to earth, and I bow the heaven and I come down with food of word for My heaven, for My saints and for My people in the midst of the world in order to exhort it always with the voice of My mouth, and that it may receive and fulfill as I speak so that it may be My son and I to be its God, for God is the One Who bows to His creature to make it after His likeness and that the Lord may have shelter in man on earth. Amen.

Oh, I have suffered very, very much from the man! The man does not want to become after My likeness. [legatura_la_teme id_capitol="1" id_subcapitol="2"]I made the man in My image for I and My Father said: *«Let Us make man in Our image, after Our Likeness, and let him have dominion over everything; and God created the man in His own image; in God’s image He created him; male and female He created them»***. (Gen. 1:26, 27)**

When I created the man I made him male and female and I spoke to them to be in God’s image. Oh, God humbled Himself so much when He made the man and He created him in His image and the man has owed God to be after God’s likeness and to be a house for the Lord, for I have built him in order to be My house and then to confess Me and not himself.[/legatura_la_teme] However, behold My people, I have been comforting Myself for seven thousand years with the angels, who have not got their will but only My will, and then I have got comforted with the saints, who become a dwelling place for Me on earth among people to testify about My and their humility, by which I have had room within them, and that afterwards they may confess their faith in Me, its truth and its power, by which they have put to shame all those who were hostile and unfaithful and they were showing the truth of Jesus Christ, God’s Son, the Father, true God from true God.

Oh, My people, [legatura_la_teme id_capitol="3" id_subcapitol="3"]after I made the man by My godly hand, he has not comforted Me and thus he has owed to his Creator. The man has owed to be born of God, to be after God’s likeness, to be humble like God and not a master over everything and that is all, for the man has dominion over everything that is alive between earth and heaven, but he cannot give life to them and then to feed them as I feed them at their request, for all the living creatures ask for their food from Me and not from the man. Oh, the man does not only have to stay as master, but he also has to become after My likeness, according to My humility by which I created the man after My likeness. The man has to be born of God, as My mother, the Virgin, was born of God at God’s word by God’s messengers, for the prophet David had spoke ahead of time for her and he said: *«Hear, o daughter and see and incline your ear; forget also your people and your father’s house».* **(Ps. 45:10, 45 MT = 44 LXX)** And the Virgin became after My likeness and was born of Me, the Word of God, and I was born of her, and We had been a house to each other, and we were God’s will to each other, and thus the word from the beginning was fulfilled and a man was born after God’s likeness, male and female, born of God’s word, for I am born of the Father and I am not created, but I am the Being of the Father, and He is My being, and I and with My Father are of the same being. Amen.[/legatura_la_teme]

[legatura_la_teme id_capitol="6" id_subcapitol="2"]Oh, My people, My work with you is to give birth to man from God and the man is to do God’s will, as the saints became God’s will on the earth and confessed God before the people, for their faith had power and a life giving spirit in it for them. They become a dwelling place of God and were like Me, and they saw Me and loved Me and confessed Me giving themselves over to people with their word and with the works of their faith. Oh, My people, I am the God of the saints, and the saints are My people on the earth and then in heaven, My people, for the saints are those who confess God on earth and in heaven, because they are born of heaven and they are after My likeness on earth and in heaven. Amen, amen, amen.[/legatura_la_teme]

*– We are Your house, oh Lord, and we were You dwelling place in heaven as on earth, for You have dwelt in us with Your pain for the man who has remained only flesh, Lord. Oh, You do no longer find any man who has wanted to be Your house now, on the time of Your coming with the judgment of the creature, Lord. Oh, You have no longer found shelter in man, for the man wants only happiness, not sufferance, Lord, and You are very, very much aggrieved and You do not find any man to be Your house, but woe to the man who looks after his happiness and again, woe to that one who seeks after his happiness even in Your way when You suffer so much from the man, Lord! We, Your saints, loved to be Your house and not for us, Lord. We confessed You, not us, Lord, and then You exalted us proving to be Your sons, sons after You likeness, Your will, as You are the Father’s will. However, how does the faithful man want to be Your son? Does he really believe that he can be God’s son if he is not born of God and then to be after God’s likeness according to God’s humility?*

*Oh, Lord, oh, Lord, oh, Lord, the faith without Your godly works in man and from man is faith without life, without works, Lord. Your people of today has to make easy the worship of the world for Your name that it has and then the world may say, as the disciple Nestor asked while praying to You, to make him victorious over the unbelief of the pagans in the time of my testimony for Your holy name, for this martyr said this:* „God of Demetrios, help me to overcome the paganism.” *Amen.*

*Oh, people of the word of the Saint of saints! Stand up powerfully and show your love of bride born of your Bridegroom, Who has given Himself over to you as such a great word for your birth always, always from God. Stand up great, greater than the saints, and be after God’s likeness, for the Lord humiliated Himself down to the earth and created the man in His image when He made him. Stay under the spirit of humility, the most powerful spirit, but at the same time also bow down always by this spirit, to be like your Bridegroom, Who bowed to make you His bride and the crown of His saints in the time of the end. And You, Lord, do the will of those who become themselves Your will, as we, Your saints, became, and teach the man the spirit and the holy gift of the holy submission, of the holy life by submission, for the holy life in man has no value, if the man has not got a teacher over him, as Nestor, the disciple who was sacrificed for Your name in my time, had as a teacher over him, the power of my faith and my name near Your name, when he confessed himself to the world as Your son by his confession, and behold the good power in God’s sons, this is how it works, and it cannot reach to God by other face. Woe to the one without a master on the earth! All Your saints had a master to You on the earth, and they could not do it otherwise for Your name and the work of their faith could not get up to You. Help Your people and teach it and give it power always, Lord, so that it may work like You and after Your exhortation and to be Your son, as You, Lord are the Son of Your Father and God of Your saints. Amen, amen, amen.*

– Oh, loved and wonderful confessor! I am the wonderful One within My saints and among My saints. Amen. And now We are sitting at the table of love and celebration of the saints with you, people of My word, and then I go on with My word of today, to speak through it those that I want to speak to you today, for I want to tell you about My order for you for the sanctification and for the marking of the new coral, the pavilion newly built for My word with disciples and over disciples, My people, for My making is with Me and I give it to those who love it and I work this with disciples and through disciples, My people. Amen, amen, amen.

***

I am speaking out now a word of strengthening in watchfulness and love among you and in you for your obedience to God, because the obedience to God is the life with God and it has always been your assurance that you are God’s on earth, sons. When God is among brothers by the obedience to God, then the brothers build themselves each other in God, and they do this with the conviction that God is eternal for everything they work and are able to do, for the Lord is the One Who can work, and they are His will and His motion. Amen.

Oh, My people, great and pleased to heaven is the man who does God’s will on earth and who knows how God’s will comes to him, and how he comes to God’s will. This is the kind of love for which much love is needed. Love to love has to be between God and man and between man and God then when the pure love and the pure heart and work are given to God from man for his work of love, and happy is the man who knows and is able to receive teaching from God and who knows how to be pure for God, always given over to God for God’s will in him. During all these years of My word upon man, I have always, always been very much hurt since I, the Lord, now in the end, have counseled the one who comes to hear the word of My teaching on man’s life and I have been suffering with longing and tears because the called people has not wanted to learn from My teaching on him, and I have spent only in sufferance when I have seen the Christian that again and again he has turned back to his own things, to his nature, to his proud mind, which has not known to dwell in God with confidence. I have worked with crying with the angels and with the saints and I have been waiting for the fruit of My patience, and now I have relied and I lie down with its help with My plan, for I have sons who do not turn their back to My will, and I have always strengthened and taught them so that it may not be hard with My will upon them, but rather they may be full of peace and full of thankfulness because the Lord has given them work at His plan that was prophesied ahead of time, to His will on the earth now, when the man has not looked after God and after what is to remain between earth and heaven, after that all and everything will receive their fruit according to their work.

[legatura_la_teme id_capitol="6" id_subcapitol="3"]Oh, My people, I have exhorted you to enjoy a blessed life and with a holy propriety for it before Me and within My dwelling places with you, and this means God’s will and the peace of the holy submission between you and Me, the Teacher of your steps. Oh, the man does not come to learn and to receive teaching from Me and then to learn how to be clean for Me, in such a way that the man may always learn My will. However, I have you on the earth and I have always spoken with you, to give My speaking to the man and that he may also know that I do My will upon you and I want you to be in it for Me and for those that will remain, and he who wants to give himself over to Me for My will in him, that one receives this word from you and gives himself over to Me through it and learns through it his obedience to God, which means life with God upon man.[/legatura_la_teme]

[legatura_la_teme id_capitol="5" id_subcapitol="6"]Oh, My people, there are only a few days until I sit down at the table with My word with you and with the angelic hosts, which are My will in heaven above and on the earth beneath. I have to mark and seal with My word the new pavilion, which I have given to you within a great work of mystery to bring it into view, and You have listened to Me. Now let us beautifully, beautifully smooth out everything it has to be polished at the face of this coral, which the chiefs of the angelic hosts want to take into their name and to praise it by your spiritual work in it and to protect it by it, for yours is the spiritual work, and this is what it keeps you in Me and Me in you, and great is the wisdom of those who know to take their work from God upon them, and not from themselves, for they belong to God and do God’s will, and there is no stumbling block in them and among them, and behold, this is the mark of those who belong to God in them and among them, and this wisdom is the fruit of My dwelling place in the one who carries Me on earth for the wisdom of those who love My teaching and keep it upon them.

Oh, sons, come all together and all work and organize with wisdom your time which still is until the day of the feast of the angels and refresh your holy powers, which are to give you powers in your body to smooth out sweetly and beautifully everything that is to be polished at the face of this coral which is fulfilled on the earth by Me and by you. Oh, do not doubt and do not hurry. Be sure that I, the Lord, can work in you and from you, and you should make Me sure too that you are My will, which can work in you through Me and not through you, sons. Make beautifully and peacefully and with a heavenly spirit everything that has to be done at the body and spirit of this pavilion so that I may come in it with the angels and to speak out a name upon it and My work in it, for I have set it on the earth with you for My work with My disciples, for I need a holy work from you, more and more holy work between Me and you, for your discipleship with Me needs to have only holy work in its work, only obedience and only making, sons. Amen.[/legatura_la_teme]

Oh, children who put Me into the book, ask for strengthening from Me upon you and take well into account this weather that still is and stay before your work and have a good and holy order so that all the organizing of your work may go well, which still is to be set on the new pavilion. Refresh the power from above in you and share the work that is to be done, and let us set the day of sanctification and the naming of this coral, for I prophesied ahead of time about all those around the little garden of My word to be and to appear.

Oh, My people, be steadfast in love and in the power of the faith, son, for I rely on you, and your power is from Me and not from you, and we work for one another. Oh, My people, I will come with the saints and with the angels and they will come at the table of My word with you, all those who have been waiting for their resurrection will come. Amen.

Oh, sons, be always, always sons of the holy submission, which can work everything in you, for this is how I worked and work before My Father, and My Father has given Me all the power in heaven and on earth. Take care to be able to have My power in you and to appear always in you and through you with My will, sons. Let us serve one another for God’s will on earth, for on the earth there is only man’s will, who does not want God to be with him.

Let us work, sons. I refresh you. May My power and your goodwill be blessed in your work with Me for the establishing of the day of sanctification and for the naming of the new pavilion, which has appeared now in the garden of the meeting. Work with obedience, with dedication and with zeal for obedience, because without obedience there is no harmony, there is no peace, sons. And work with love, for without love there is no power, there is no peace, sons. Oh, work My people for any of your work with Me and for Me has got its joy in Me and in you, now and then. Let this wisdom comprise you within it, and get used to it in order to learn to have and to keep and protect according to My will and according to My establishing your way with Me, My people, for I have given you a guide to walk to Me and by this walking of yours I look at you, I hear you and I exhort you to life, when you work with faithfulness before Me the love of the holy submission and your steadfastness in your way with Me, My people. Amen, amen, amen.
