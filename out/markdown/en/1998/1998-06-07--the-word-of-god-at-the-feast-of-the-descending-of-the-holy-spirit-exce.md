… Amen, amen I speak to you, people who do not keep My words: I renew My Scripture upon you, which spoke about My time of today, when the dead will hear God’s voice and will come to life, some to eternal life and others to eternal judgment, and as life is eternal the same is judgment. The Holy Spirit is the Lord, Who gives life to the people. He is the spring from which holiness springs up in people, in the man’s spirit, soul and body. Here it is the question that I come to you with: Did you receive the Holy Spirit? Have you heard about Him in order to receive Him and to open for Him and that He may become a spring of holiness in you? Answer Me!

When I built the man, I created him to be holy, but the man’s will was otherwise. When the man does not receive My will and My love in him, he puts on his will and remains without the Lord and thus he violates the things of the Lord in man, and he sets his will in him and is his own master. Behold, the time has come to call out the man and to bring the man into account. I hold the fan into My hand and I cleanse My threshing floor gathering My wheat into the barn and the chaff into the fire. It is written into the Scriptures that after two thousand years since My first coming, I will come again from heaven on earth to give each one according to his deed.

The Holy Spirit speaks from heaven on earth in the middle of the Romanian people, for I, the Lord, chose Jerusalem again. At the beginning I chose one Jerusalem and that one cast Me out of it. Now I have chosen another Jerusalem, but the Holy Spirit cannot be caught for it is written into My Scriptures of that time: *«A little while and the world will not see Me. However, you will see Me because I am alive and you will be alive and will understand that I am in the Father and you in Me, and I in you».* **(John: 16/16, 17)**

I have always been into the midst of those that are chosen by God to do My works with them on earth, for I have fulfilled the Scriptures with the people, because they are given from heaven for them, to be fulfilled for the people. At My first coming, I came as a Savior, but those who did not receive Me were already judged, for it was obvious that they did not love God, because they did not keep His commandments. At My second coming, I will come as a Judge for those who are alive and for the dead, but those who do not receive Me now either are judged by this, for they are seen that they do not love God.

… Oh, sons of men, take within you My speaking that is upon you. The man who helps another man in his need but he does not do God’s will in his body, soul and spirit, that one is deprived of God’s knowledge. If the man’s work does not bring to godliness the man who takes from man, that work is temporary. It is written in the Scriptures: *«The flesh profits nothing; it is the Spirit who gives life».* **(John: 6/63)** Man deceives man. Man separates himself from God, for the whole earth and every man has been filled with human works that keep the man away from God and which make the man think that he does God’s will. Nevertheless, I speak again the word of the Holy Spirit and say: if the man’s work does not make godly the man who takes from man, that work is darkness and it does not make any light and it does not produce any fruit for heaven. It is in vain that the earth has been filled up with the works of the pagans, for those who do not fulfill God’s commandments are pagans on earth. It is in vain that the people boast about their good works for the people who do not do God’s will. It is in vain, oh sons of men, if your hands help the wrongdoers, who do the same evil things like you. It is in vain, for the hands, which give and take in this way, are dirty hands and there is no good fragrance in them for heaven, for the Lord loves those who are from heaven and He is merciful to those who lack those from heaven and not those from the earth. Your good works are in vain, oh, sons of men, for you do not make them for the Lord, and you rather make them for people like you. Oh, if you made them for those who fulfill My plans on earth, then it would be for you to work them for God, Who works hard on earth to fulfill His preaching of new heaven and new earth and of new man, in God’s image and after His likeness.

It is in vain to pretend that you are God’s friends, oh, sons of men, for those who know God, they have the Holy Spirit and work out the Lord’s justice over the earth. Oh, it is in vain that you rush without Me to fulfill the Scriptures of My second coming among people, as you say. Oh, it is in vain, oh, men’s sons, for those are not My Scriptures, but they are yours, for it is written into My Scriptures: *«He comes like a thief; He comes all of a sudden. When no one expects, then the Lord comes».* **(See also Apoc: 16/15)**

Receive My speaking upon you into your mind, oh, sons of men, who do the work of false christ. Shudder with the miracle of My voice, which comes with the clouds above the garden of My word, the new earth in which God’s justice dwells. Take away the spirit of falsehood from you and join with the word of the Holy Spirit, which brings into the light the hypocrisy hidden in the man of falsehood of this age, for man upon man separates himself from God by serving the spirit of lying, and the whole earth and every man have been filled with human works which make the man believe that he does God’s will on earth.

… Oh, sons of men, he who comes before Me into My name, that one is a false prophet; he is not the Christ of the Father, for I come like a thief, suddenly. Behold, I have proved out the hypocrisy hidden in the lying man of this age, for the Holy Spirit comes from heaven and is preached over the earth, and He makes clean paths to every heart, which leaves off falsehood and the old man in man. This is what I spoke to those who were clean and faithful to Me: *«If you love Me, keep My commandments. And I will pray to the Father, and He will give you another Counselor, that He may be with you forever, until I come again with My flesh among you and give you My eternity, and you will be like Me, because you will see Me as I am».* **(See John: 14/15; 1 John: 3/2 …)** Amen.

… It is in vain that you pose as God’s friends, oh sons of the people, for those who know God have the Holy Spirit and work out the Lord’s righteousness over the earth. Oh, it is in vain that you hurry without Me to fulfill the Scriptures of My second coming among the people, as you say. It is in vain, oh sons of the people, for those are not My Scriptures, but they are rather yours, for it is written into My Scriptures: *«He comes like a thief and He comes unexpectedly. The Lord comes when no one expects».*

… Oh, My people, oh, My people, oh, My people! You should always stay enshrouded only in My word and never get out of it, for outside there are the dogs, the sorcerers, the adulterous, the idol worshippers and all the lovers and workers of falsehood, who the Christ’s coming out of their own lie, as they say; but My coming is truth not imagination. My word is the truth, which proves out the lie of the hypocrites. My word is My shield into your midst, My people, and crowds over crowds will come to get under My shield which is yours, but the righteousness is into My hand and I am clothed with it as with a garment, for My word spoken into your midst and through which I have called out to the rich and poor, to the slaves and free men, to the kings and soldiers, the word of God is the one that will stand face to face with each one. I come to do justice for My work, which had been with you for forty-five years before the coming of My glory over the earth, for the work of My word that is with you will establish by the word the visible face of the kingdom of the heavens all over the earth. Amen. One more hour and you will be like Me, for you will see Me as I am. One hour and the entire human kind will see Me in you, for you will have of My glory and My glory is the glory of the Holy Spirit, Who will change the looks of all who see and of those who do not see. One hour, My people; one hour and every word of Mine, spoken on you, comes into sight; My word is coming for you visibly, sons. One hour, My people, one more hour! Amen, amen, amen.
