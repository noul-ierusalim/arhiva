[legatura_la_teme id_capitol="8" id_subcapitol="1"]… It is written that the kingdom of the heavens is taken by force; it is taken by force, son, for it can be conquered by those who want to lay hold of it. Whoever has already tasted of its fruits, seeks to have it as his homeland.

I have been teaching you, My people, to ask for the kingdom of the heavens on earth with you. Why have I really told you to ask for this? I have told you because you are Romanian, and your country is the parable of the kingdom of the heavens. It has always been taken by force for its good fruit. I has always, always, been taken by force and conquered, and the conquerors have not known what they were conquering; they have not known what My beloved country is, the one that has been waited to be. I make it into a kingdom of the havens, and I have already done so, for My word is always in it and I have in it a country of milk and honey, I have My word as sweet food in it and whoever tastes of this food becomes sweet and holy and seeks after the kingdom of the heavens, which is with you, My people. I turn Romania into the kingdom of the heavens, and it will be conquered again by all the kings of the earth, but not to divide it, but rather to give it glory and worship it. Amen. There is still a little while and the teaching of My word will be taken by force and it will be living and incorruptible food for many nations that will come to life and will take the kingdom of the heavens by force and will conquer it and they will belong to it and will be called Romanian people. Amen. However, the offspring of vipers will be full of conceit and hardly will they bow down, but they will do it for fear of sufferance. These are the Pharisees and the scribes, which rely on themselves but their feet will be weakened and they will be in great demand of the Spirit of God in order to surround them and then to forgive them, but first they have to bring forth fruit worthy of repentance and not to rely on God by not doing fruits of repentance in their hearts. This is what John, the Baptizer, was saying to these people who came to him fleeing from the wrath of the Lord; he was saying this: *«You offspring of vipers, who warned you to flee from the wrath to come? Therefore bring forth fruit worthy of repentance and do not think to yourselves, 'We have Abraham for our father,’ for I tell you that God is able to raise up children to Abraham from these stones. Even now the axe lies at the root at the trees; therefore, every three that does not bring forth good fruit is cut down, and cast into the fire».* **(Matt. 3:7-10)** And on hearing these words many multitudes asked John saying: *«What then must we do?»* **(Luke 3:10)**, and John was teaching and baptizing them and was showing the way to Me, the Christ Who was sent by the Father to baptize the people with the Holy Spirit.

… My people, I am your Master, and I am into your midst, and you are My house on the earth; you are the heavenly kingdom in the midst of the Romania people, and you will be taken by the storm because of the sweetness that is in you. You have My sweet word in your midst, which flows upon you above you, faithful people. My word is the river of life, which flows from Me, for I am the spring of the river of life. My spring is the word that makes them all, which water them all. My word is milk and honey, which makes the man grow for the incorruptibility of the body, the soul and the spirit,[/legatura_la_teme] however, the Pharisees are offspring of vipers and they think of themselves that they are smart and that is why they do not repent, but they are not righteous and are hidden under their coat with their iniquities and bite from Me and pose themselves as righteous, poisoning Me with their deeds hidden from the face of the people.

… Sons, sons, be an example of the kingdom of the heavens, so that the man may come to My kingdom, and My kingdom to be let overcome by the man. It is still a little time and I come as a Master, and I will give each one according to his deed. Amen, amen, amen.
