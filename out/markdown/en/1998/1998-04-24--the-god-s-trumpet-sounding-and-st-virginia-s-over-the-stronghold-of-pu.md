… I come with the greeting of My resurrection: *“Christ is risen! Christ is risen! Christ is risen!”* I come in the book of Verginica. And I have already come. Amen.

Behold, Verginica, prepare your word for your stronghold, for you were My trumpet on the earth, and you are My trumpet in heaven too, for My word is written into the Scripture which says: *«I am the God of Abraham and the God of Isaac and the God of Jacob, and God is not the God of the dead but of the living, for all live in Him»*. You are alive, My Verginica, for you were My trumpet that I sounded on the earth to gather a people for Myself and to have it for My coming. I am your God, Verginica, as I spoke to Moses in the burning bush, for I am the God of Abraham, Isaac and Jacob, for they are alive and live in Me, as you also live in Me and you are My trumpet in heaven and on earth. I, Myself, make an entrance for you into your book, so that you may leave in it a word of calling over your city from where I took you into heaven. I give My Mother as your companion with a gift of soul healing, for the healthy souls heal their sick bodies. I also give you saints and angels, so that you may be helped by heavenly powers in your work, for unbelief and lawlessness, which came over your city, make Me unleash My mourning over those who forsook Us. While you were with them on the earth, they did the Christian things and feared God, but now they have tasted fully of the spirit of the world, and the spirit of the world warmed them up for the world and for their temporary life, and I, the Lord, had to find some other faithful children to replace them, because I could not stop with My work and yours from preparing My coming and the people of My coming. The first did no longer want to go on with Me and with you, and those that were behind them I made to be the first, and I go with them forward, for they are faithful and lovers of God and of eternal life, for it is written: «It is not the fleshly sons who inherit those from heaven, but the spiritual sons». I showed them all My love and I called My sons those that remained with Me and with you, but the world, which does not know Me, does not know them too, and when I appear, they will be like Me, as it is written about the sons that will see Me as I am, for they put their hope in Me and cleaned themselves, as I am clean; for I lifted the sins of those who were faithful to Me, and anyone who does not remain in Me, does not sin, because it is written: «He who is born of God, does not sin, for God’s seed remains in him and he can no longer sin, because he is born of God». Amen.

… I am in Father, and Father is in Me when I work. I become word over Verginica’s stronghold, over Verginica’s house, over the house where I, the Lord, was coming down becoming word at Verginica’s speaking, God’s trumpet for My second coming on the earth. I become a calling word over those who remained behind her under her shelter, which had to be forever holy, for My mark in this house will stand in judgment with those of this house.

… I, the Lord Jesus Christ, cry out to you: wake up! Amen. Wake up your spirits, turn the Spirit of God back into you and live in a heavenly way for My coming. I establish My people of today to keep the fasting of the Lord’s coming and live before Me as the first man in Paradise, that I come to give an eternal life to those who came before Me with fasting and watching for My coming. Pull aside from My anger, for I am coming with fire and brimstone for those who denigrate My name among the nations. Get under My mantle so that I may forgive your estrangement from Me. Come towards the joy which will remain on earth with those pleased to Me, for I come to roll the heaven up and to renew the earth and to give it as an eternal inheritance to those who live My righteousness on earth. Open your Scriptures and read them about My coming which is about to fulfill soon, soon. [legatura_la_teme id_capitol="2" id_subcapitol="6"]The antichrist and his angels are ready to deceive all the nations on Christ’s coming, a coming invented by the lie of the antichrist. The time rolled itself up and there is no more time for unbelief, for the scythe of the antichrist is coming to gather together for the fire all those who did not love God by their living.

[/legatura_la_teme][legatura_la_teme id_capitol="2" id_subcapitol="6"]… The antichrist, hidden into the mind of the people over all the earth, will do the lying work of the coming of Christ, as for a long time the antichrist has been working to thwart the truth about My coming, but this is what I spoke upon the sons of the church and told them: “The one who is coming before Me is a thief and a robber; he is a false Christ among other false Christs,” but they will be caught in their own snare and thrown out into the lake of fire and brimstone by the uttering of this word. [/legatura_la_teme]

… Israel, new Israel, live four parts out of all four parts of your time for the Lord, and make out of your sons the temples of the Lord’s coming, the sons of My coming into your midst, so that I may show Myself into your midst, you people of My coming.

You who are with Me now, and you who will come to be! Do not go mad for Me and do not go mad because of the weak and cold ones who were and are no more by the word of My coming. Look yourself up into heaven and not down on earth. Take from heaven and not from earth. Amen. Let you be a living people, for your life is in heaven, and you will take it from heaven, for the world which does not know Me does not know you as well, but when I reveal Myself, you will also be the same as I am and you will see Me as I am, beloved sons. The same as the antichrist has his own sons to his likeness and they will see how he is when his lying and perishing of people work will be revealed, the same way, you will be to My likeness when I reveal Myself and when you see Me as I am, for you will have from My glory and will see by the Holy Spirit Whom the Father Will give you to see My coming from the Father. Amen, amen, amen.
