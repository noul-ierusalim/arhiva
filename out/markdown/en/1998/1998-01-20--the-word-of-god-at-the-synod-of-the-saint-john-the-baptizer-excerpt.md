… From feast to feast I find My pleasure into your midst, My people. However son, you should not have other joy on earth, for I am your joy as you are My joy and My hope for the work of the new heaven and the new earth. Learn, son, learn My people. Always learn to be like Me and always look at you so that you may not be otherwise, son, so that you may not be like the sons of people, and to be a heavenly creature on earth instead, to be light into the midst of the darkness on the earth. Amen.

[legatura_la_teme id_capitol="2" id_subcapitol="5"]People use days of feast of the saints to spend their feast, not those of the saints, for the saints are no longer celebrated at their place, and the saints do no longer have their days of feasts on earth with the people. People have shaken from their places the times of the saints, and they do not want to come back with repentance to confess their sins and their wandering from the truth. [/legatura_la_teme]Oh, the heavenly life in man cannot start but only after the washing of the spirit, of the soul and of the body, but the people do not wake up, and I will make them shake together with their beds, to call out to Me as I called to the Father from the cross.

And you, My people, you should be a holy people on earth, for the saints are those who confess the truth over the people of the ages. You are My messenger, as through you I walk over the earth as the word of the renewal of the world, the word of the creation of the heaven, of the earth and of the world, the word of the new creation and of the new man for the new creation. Amen, amen, amen.
