[legatura_la_teme id_capitol="2" id_subcapitol="6"]… The church has not wanted to become My church, and she has remained the church of the people who set as gods in it. Sons, I said in the time of My body that I would not come before the sin of the faith denial could be seen, the sin of the hypocrite love near God’s name. Sons, God is nowhere more, because it is written: *«I do not live with those who commit lawlessness»*. I have forsaken the people more than in the time of Noah, and the people lie to each other using My name, like the blind who cannot guide one another.

[/legatura_la_teme][legatura_la_teme id_capitol="3" id_subcapitol="4"]Oh, you church! You, who do no longer have a Father! You, who have forsaken your God joining in My name, as you say, with the whole people foreign to the way of the truth! Why did you do this? Behold, you wrote with your own hand My judgment for your deed. Did I, the Lord, really tell you to do this? Oh, I came to you and I told you not to do something like that, so that I might not come to strike you suddenly. Behold, I forsook you as I did once with Israel, who denied Me by his unfaithfulness. In the days to come you will see that I have forsaken you, and you will believe then that I am the One Who have come into your way to call you and to turn you back from the sin of the faith denial before My appearance, as it is written in the Scriptures about My coming. It is written that you will sell your honor, and in fact this is what you did. I was sold on money, and behold, since then no one is able to sell or buy Me anymore but only the one who has your sign on him, the sign of the church which is selling Me on money even today. The one, who takes Me through the priest, pays Me to the priest and becomes a son of the church buying for him this adoption on money.

Oh, church, which is your Christ, really? The crucified One did not come to be served, but He came to serve everybody instead. The One Who was crucified for you speaks over you and asks you: why did you do this? How comes that you have made peace with the pagans into My name when there is no peace between you and Me; there is no holiness from you to Me? Oh, you did not want to be Mine, and you rather wanted to belong to the world, to the Babylon. You wanted to be yours and you are yours, and you are not Mine. However, I have chosen from among you for Me many who are crying now with Me because of those that you have done. You separated yourself from Me seventy years ago and now you have not come back upon My calling, and you have remained far away. Even more, you have joined with those who have no God, for I am the God of those who are holy.

Oh, you who are clean in your wisdom and are with clean hands into the midst of the church that have forsaken Me! Stand on My right hand for My word is the sword which separates the Christian from the pagan, for I did not come to bring peace but rather the sword, and behold I have already brought it, and I judge with it for the truth. Amen, amen, amen.

What does a pagan man mean? Every man who commits sin after sin after I have showed him the life and the way in My heavenly truth, that one has a pagan life, even if he has the sign of the church on his head. That one is like a viper, which bites and then runs away from the punishment of his deed. However, I will conquer the man by the Book of Truth, which is worked from My word that I speak over the man chosen by Me to hear and to write, as I commanded to John, the apostle to write the book for the end of iniquity. Behold the days of judgment, as in the days of Noah, when I was calling out as I do today, and the people were eating and drinking and they were marrying and being given in marriage and were hypocrites as in these days.

Oh, sons of men, until when? Do you not really want to wake up and see the sin of apostasy which your rulers, who sit over your soul, wrote with their own hand? My wrath is coming because of these. Wake up! The voice of God is crying upon you. Wake your hearts and stand up in front of My glory, for My word of today is an angel sent by Me to bring into the boat of salvation those who groan and cry under the darkness of estrangement from God.

You, servants of the church, who are still alive in your heart, wake up and make a sound and defend the spring of faith and your sons who choose to be on the side of eternal life! I will give you a new name and a new heart, for I will not put My new wine into old vessels. Arise and renew yourselves for the new age, built out of the saints on earth. Flee from money, flee from greed, flee from wealth, flee from the glory which comes from man, flee to My right side and sit under the grace of the holy faith until I make the antichrist a footstool under My power. Amen, amen, amen.

The antichrist is a great king into My name, says he. All the kings of the earth serve him and do not know his face. But here are the days when I, the Lord, am coming, and I will destroy him with the breath of My mouth and I will call him the son of lawlessness, the king of Babylon, with whom all the kings and subjects of the kings went into debauchery. It is written in the Scriptures that the end of all things will not come before the apostasy when the man of lawlessness will be revealed. Behold, My word is the breath of My mouth which speaks the truth about the sin of desolation, sitting in the holy place and clothed in a holy garment. I am God, the Word. Here are My days, and it will be a reward according to the deed all over where the grace of repentance does not get in, the one which I breathe upon the earth to be taken by those who humble themselves for the forgiveness of their transgressions.

I stand at the door and knock. If someone hears My voice and opens the door then I will come in to him and have dinner with him, and he with Me. Amen. This Word is the dinner which I, the Lord, lay between Me and the one who will open to My voice. Amen.

Here, the Bridegroom is coming! My word is an angel sent by Me, as it is written: *«In the days when the seventh angel speaks, then the mystery of God is accomplished as the Lord made it wellknown to His servants, the prophets.»* Amen, amen, amen.[/legatura_la_teme]
