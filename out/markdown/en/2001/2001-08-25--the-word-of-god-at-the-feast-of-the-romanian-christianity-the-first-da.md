Open to the Lord, you, watchmen from the manger of the word! There come wedding guests, sons, and I, the Lord, open up My bosom and I give them. Amen, amen, amen.

I long within My spirit after the man. I settle down with days of heavenly feast on earth and I call the man to give him of My wedding table with you, sons from the manger. I have always tried to draw the man under My mantle, as he does not know to come alone. There is no longer a way towards Me on the earth, and I make My way come down to the man and I tell him: behold the way, man! Get ready for it, for My way with the man is sweet, but no one speaks about it to you. I come to tell you and to offer you the way of life to get you used to it, for you are a wonderer and without any comfort on earth. Oh, do not be afraid! I come. Amen, amen, amen.

I settle down with days of wedding on earth, and My gardens get ready to welcome My wedding guests. You, My bride people, are tired, for it is with you that I have worked out the glory of My wedding, for the days of wedding are prepared with labor, but rejoice and cheer My wedding guests, for I, the Lord, have called and gathered them together from the four corners of the winds and I have fed them on the word and I have comforted them and made them used to know My coming and settle down for it, as you did, My bride people.

There is nowhere a table of supper on earth anymore. There is no longer a table of comfort between Me and the man. Oh, and the man is no longer used to Me at his table. No one speaks to the man about My table with him anymore. I long within My spirit after the man, for the man is a wanderer, and I come down from heaven and get into his way and call him at My table.

Man, you do no longer know of joys, but I come into your way and comfort your spirit and draw you to Me. I become a refreshing spirit of soul and I give Myself to you to drink Me for your life. Here is the spring! I spread My wedding table and I lay down abundantly and I become living water for your soul and I ask for your life to renew it and then to caress and scent you, as you see that I do with My bride people, who labors for Me for your life, wanderer man. Oh, My Father has sent Me on earth after the man, and I come and get ready coming, and I come into the man’s way and teach him to know Me when I knock at his door, but this mystery has got lost in the man’s heart.

[legatura_la_teme id_capitol="6" id_subcapitol="3"]Oh, My bride people, let us make the man get used to know what My dwelling with you is, My wedding with you before the sons of the people. This is My coming after two thousand years, a sweet coming and not with wrath. I came and called you and you heard and knew My voice. And then I embraced you and gave you birth of this word and then I adorned you with beauty from heaven and I gave you a new name and called you bride, My people, and you do the Bridegroom’s will. Now we spread the wedding table on earth and announce it into the four corners for those that are called to come, and to put those that come at the table and to give them a heavenly spirit, to give them the spirit of comfort. He, who lets himself to My comfort, is seized by it and knows My coming and comes to it and eats of it, for here is where I come, here, where I have prepared My place, that where I am, those of Mine may be here also, and to lay down My glory with them on earth, My word which calls the man to My table. [/legatura_la_teme][legatura_la_teme id_capitol="3" id_subcapitol="3"]Oh, I would no longer clear My table on earth, My table with the man, for the spirit and the bride have always said: *„Come!”*. And I have always come at the voice of the bride, but My bride is small, and I have labored hard through her on earth and I do not find any kind of support for her. However, I am He Who is and I overcome for her and for My glory before the people, so that the people may come to her and to enlarge the tent of My wedding more and more and to bring the man under My protection, for it is hard on earth. The earth moans and always tells Me that its yoke is too heavy, that it is too loaded and is as big as seven thousand years.

I want you man to get used so that you may no longer sin, for I made the heaven and the earth for blessing not for curse, and your sin is curse on earth. I want you get used to hear My voice and to come out before Me and be refreshed to welcome Me. I settle down into your way with days of love, with days of wedding, and the days of wrath are for those who do not come. But come man, come to My calling and receive comfort and you will be able to cope with it, if you receive. I am the Son of the Father Sabaoth, and My Father gave Me to give you His kingdom and to make it come down on you and to become holy for it and to say: *«Our Father in heaven, may Your name be kept holy, let Your kingdom come, and let Your will be done, as in heaven so on earth, Father»*. This is what you are to say, wandering man, for this is how I taught the disciples of My wedding to ask from the Father, and to become My disciples if they ask in this way, for the sons of the Father are those who do the Father’s will. Amen.[/legatura_la_teme]

You, those who came at My voice, which calls you to the wedding, open up your hearing and understand that the Father’s sons are those who do the Father’s will and these say: *«as in heaven, so on earth, Father».* I open up My path and come and teach you to be as in heaven so on earth, for this is the Father’s will. [legatura_la_teme id_capitol="3" id_subcapitol="3"]Soon, soon, and there will no longer be on earth but the Father’s will and its sons, for this is what Father wants. Amen.

I have come down on earth to be the man’s life. Man, you have your life and you love it. Oh, why do you love your life? Its reward is death. Why do you love your life, man? I have come to be your life, for I pay it with My death on the cross, but I was resurrected and I am alive forever, and I have life for you, man, and I want to give you life. My life is the Father’s will in man, and you, man, you love your life and have no love if you do this. How shall I make you used to the mysteries of life and then to love them? How shall I do it, man? You have no shepherds after My heart over you, and the longing for your life overwhelms you. The spirit of the eternal life cries within you and you do not know how to perceive its calling. It calls you from your inner being and you do not know to know its calling.

After the man fell down from eternity, it has remained in the man as a longing, which has always called the man to it. After the man exalted over God, then he has swerved from the way of life and indulged in sinning and then the man has made his life, his life with sin, a life which is born of sin.

Man, I have been calling you for seven thousand years! I was sorry that I made you. You have pulled yourself out of Me and made your own life and I was sorry that I made you. You have no longer wanted to be after My image and after My likeness. It has been hurting Me for seven thousand years the fact that you have no longer wanted it. Then I came after you to give you My life. And I gave it to you. However, you have not taken from it. And I have come again to give it to you. Here I am after you. I want again to make you. The making that I want now to put on you is life without death. But do you really want it? The spirit of the eternal life sighs within you and you do not stop to hear its sigh. It calls you from within you and you do not know to hear its calling. I work hard behind you, and you do not see Me, man, for the man cannot see My glory and to remain alive afterwards. I want to make you the way I am and to see Me working behind you for your life, because it is written: *«Those who will see Him will be like Him»*. I draw you to the spring of life. My word is the spring. I will increase in you the longing after the life and I will come more and more into your life with the spring of life and I will reveal its mysteries and I will fill you with the spring of life, for your life is without any desire of life if you do not have My life in it. I spread a wedding table between Me and you and how much I want you to know to come to the wedding! Oh, you need wedding garments for the Bridegroom cannot stay at His table with you. There will be no other table on earth. Soon, soon, there will remain only the wedding table, the love between the Bridegroom and the bride, the love the eternity begins with. Amen.[/legatura_la_teme]

Oh, sons of the people, I teach you what true love is. In the time of Noah, I looked down on the earth and I saw God’s sons seduced by the sons and daughters of the people, and thus love was spoiled on earth. When I saw this, I was sorry that I made the man and I told Noah to make a saving ark for him and for his sons, for the sons and daughters of the people would bring about flood on earth. And it was flood on earth and the earth was full of waters and I saved God’s sons; I saved them from among the sons of the people out of the flood. And what shall I do now? The human kind became flood for God’s sons, for the sons of the people are in abundance and those who are with Me are few. What shall I really do?

Here is what I do: I come with a table of wedding on earth. This is what I do. I call every man at My table. This is what Noah did. First, he told everybody about Me and about the news for their salvation and he called them all to salvation, but no one wanted to believe and to come. And then, their sins brought about the flood on earth, and the earth was caught under the waters with everything that was on it. However, now it will be an unequalled wonder. I, the Lord, will lift up those of Mine and I will protect and comfort them, and I will take away many, as I took away My people of today, with which I lay down the glory of My word on earth, My coming on the way of the word.

I want to gather many at My bosom and to give them life, for the people do not have life. Oh, who shall give life to the people? There is no man on earth to be able to give life. I come and give it to them. Come, so that I may give it you! Taste and be comforted. Taste of My wine. I am the Word that gives life, and the life is the light of the people. He, who has no light, let him come and receive it. Let him come! Amen, amen, amen.

Now, I give My comfort to those who are tired from the way to the spring. You, who know to come to the spring, should know that it is a great mystery to know to come. You, who come to the wedding, get used to wedding garments, for when I come, I will look at the man’s garment and I will ask him about it. Amen.

Behold My gardens, and My bride in it, My bride people, and beautiful people, adorned with the wedding garment and with the candle burning all the time. I give you My gardens from time to time to take heavenly delight in them. Seek to know the mystery of My heavenly comfort, for if I, the Lord, cannot be comforted with you, those who come into My gardens, neither will you be able to be comforted. Come with a longing full of Me when you come. This spring is the water of life. Wash your face in it for it is healing. My word is the comfort. Take it and strengthen you hearts and do not forget that I am gentle and humble in My heart, for this is what Father wants His sons to be.

The longing after the man has been tearing Me apart for seven thousand years. My spirit is crushed by the man build by My hand. I miss the new man very much. I came two thousand years ago from the Father and I became the New Man. Let every man take Me in him, for that is why I became Man. Let every son of man seek Me, for here is how I come after the man to give him life of My life.

I breathe with the word and bring together those that come and I give them of the wedding table. Comfort each other, you that come to the wedding! I am the Bridegroom of the wedding. You should spend your time with sweet songs, with sweet speaking, with a spirit of joy, for I, the Lord, am hungry after you and I miss you, for the longing after the man is all I have. However, I want the man. I want to draw everyone at My table, at the comforting table, for the life is the light of the people. Amen. Make haste to the joys of the wedding. Rejoice and be glad as in heaven, for here you are in the Lord’s gardens. I, the Lord, have spoken and made. Amen.

And tomorrow, I will embrace you again in the word. I will teach you, I will comfort you, I will feed you and I will tell you speak to you about love, for I am the love. Amen, amen, amen.
