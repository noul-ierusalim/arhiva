I breathe with the breath of My mouth over you, watchmen children from My manger of word. I assign a great value to you, because you receive Me from near My Father near you, to breathe with the breath of My mouth word on the earth. Amen.

I highly value you, children son. I am the God of everything that exists, but as big as I am, I can scarcely want to pass you before Me, because you serve to My coming as word over the earth as much as I want. Do not be afraid of the way I humbled Myself for you. Do not be afraid. I said this, I, so that the people may value your love for Me and for My people who listens from the mouth of the spring of My word.

I breathe the word with the breath of My mouth over you, so that you may give it to the holy people and to keep it holy, sons, holy and obedient in every word of Mine over you for you and for him. Amen.

My breath is the word, and its fulfillment is your obedience to My word, My people. If you do not listen to it in order to do it, it is not fulfilled. Oh, it is a sin for My word not to be entirely fulfilled. However, you should fulfill it literally and always, so that you may not have this sin, My people. Let all of your concern be the fulfillment of My word and you should have it with you everywhere, and to do it completely and in everything, for you do not need additional time to fulfill it; no, My people. If you are too small for this truth, then you should become great through those that are great in this truth and do not be hard of understanding, for you are a great people, the greatest of all peoples, for it is not the number but the gift and My grace, which has made you the greatest people between heaven and earth, only for you to have your humility full for this greatness of yours. The great love helps humility, and the full humility helps the love, and this is how you should behave between heaven and earth, My people, because I pass through the sons of the garden and I give Myself to you, so that you may fulfill it completely. Amen.

Oh, I cannot leave you too long without warm food. If I saw that you can, then I would make those in the garden have rest; those who give to you from Me. It is written into the Scriptures for the sons: *«If they receive My words and store them up within them wisely and considerately, applying their heart to them; if they call out for discernment and search highly for it as for a hidden treasure, then they will understand the fear of the Lord and then they will gain knowledge of God, for He keeps the salvation for the righteous, because He guards the ways of justice and He preserves the path of His righteous and takes them on the paths of every good»*. Amen.

Oh, you do not need special time to fulfill My word; no, My people. You need fear of God, son, and with it to love My advice with wisdom, with consideration and with discernment, My people, for these lead to the spirit of the fear of God, which makes you obey Me, son.

It is a sin for My word not to be fulfilled completely when I give it. I give Myself to you in abundance and you should also give yourself to Me, son, for I have taught you so that I might reveal Myself to you, and for you to show yourself to Me, and in every day you should show yourself so that I may see you, for I have angels in heaven and on earth to see you. When you know that I see you, you endeavor to please Me, but when you want Me that I may no longer see you, you do no longer struggle for it. Do you see why I have taught you to show yourself to Me every day? Do you really see it, son, why? I can give you advice if you do so. I can watch from near you, if you do so. I can, only for you to listen to My advice, son. I take from your heart and give to others too. I take from their hearts and give to you too, and this is how we work, and this is how we keep ourselves ready forever. And behold, My advice has to be observed, My people. I teach you to be warm with Me and not to get tired in doing so. I teach you to always be, son. You should be an open door for Me from one of My speech to another, for Me and for all that are Mine, for you are brothers from the heavenly Father and from His Son, for you are born from the Holy Spirit of the new birth and from the very beginning you are promised to be and to establish on the earth the paths of the good, the paths of the righteous, which choose from them the liars and throws them into the outer darkness, sons.

But what is the outer darkness?

Oh, children who receive the word from My mouth, from the Lord’s mouth! The Father, the Son and the Holy Spirit give you to tell this to the holy people: what is the outer darkness?

*— It is written into the Scriptures that the* «sons of the kingdom will be cast out into the outer darkness. There will be weeping and gnashing of teeth. And the foreigners will come and sit down at the table with Abraham, with Isaac, and with Jacob in the kingdom of the heavens»*. And again it is written into the Scripture:* «The kingdom of the heavens will be taken from you and given to a people that will bear forth its fruits. And he who will stumble over this stone will be broken to pieces and it will crush whomever it falls to dust». And listening to this, the bishops and the Pharisees understood that it was spoken about them.

It is written into the Scriptures: «Outside are the dogs, the sorcerers, the sexually immoral, the murderers, the idolaters and everyone who loves and practices falsehood». These are outside of the kingdom of God and are not in it even if they call themselves the sons of the kingdom, but if they do these things, they are outside of the kingdom of the heavens, for it is the holy man, out of whom the Holy Spirit works out heaven on earth.

The world is the outer darkness; the world, which does not have God in its body, soul and spirit; the world, which works, in everyone and in everything of its things, the falsehood, which passes away and does not remain and which delights the man for it in his life on the earth. There it is in the world and it will be the weeping and gnashing of teeth, and all those who call themselves sons of the kingdom of the heavens, but love falsehood and its life instead, the beautiful that passes away and the immorality of the life all the time. Those are in the outer darkness and are not in the kingdom of the heavens and do not come towards it; they do not see it and do not understand it, for they are in the darkness, in the works of the outer darkness, which is in the world, and the world is the opposite of the kingdom of the heavens.

The paths of the righteous choose those that are liars on them and cast them away into the outer darkness, for those are the sons of the world, and most of the lying sons of the world are those who call themselves sons of God. These are the outer darkness in which those that work out falsehood stay hidden; falsehood that stands against God. And from among those who are not the sons of the kingdom, those who are righteous in their hearts will be chosen by the angels, and they will come and sit down at the table with Abraham, Isaac and Jacob in the kingdom of the heavens and will walk in the paths of the righteous. Amen.

— Oh, My people, I put a high price on those who give you the word from My mouth and this is how I want you to do too, for I have drawn you out of the great tribulation, from among the sons of the falsehood, who lie to everybody saying that they are the sons of My kingdom. I have drawn you out, so that you may no longer stay with them in the outer darkness, for happier were and are the crippled, the lame, the mute, the lepers and all strangers than the sons of the kingdom, who do not do the works of the kingdom of the heavens on the earth. Those who stay with these and are under these are in the great tribulation and if I save many, this is how I will call them. I will call those who have come from the great tribulation and I will give them little white garments, as I also gave you when I chose you, when I received you and when I made you alive through My life, which I gave to you, sons.

But who else is there on the earth that knows what life is, what the kingdom of the heavens means, or what the outer darkness means, where the weeping and gnashing of teeth are and where there is, in no circumstances, the apparent happiness?

Oh, loving children, I would like to teach you more, for I am affectionate after you and I want you more and more to be the same that is affectionate after Me, affectionate after one another. Amen. I want to teach you more. But now get together at the table for the life of the body, and may the food on your table be blessed. And we will learn, and then we will put the word of today into the book.

I am affectionate after you and I spend time with you and near you: at the table and at work, in your walking and staying, in your love among you, in your speaking and in your silence, for I am affectionate after you, sons. Amen, amen, amen.

***

I am with My advice after you, little Jerusalem. Let My exhortation be your comfort, and when you are to fulfill it, I am comforted with you and I give you powers to fulfill My exhortation. Amen.

Oh, how much good I can do to you when I can walk into your inner being, oh, how many miracles I can fulfill in you, son! It is a wonderful thing for Me to be where everything is Mine, there where I can be the Creator of everything. When humility in man always comes into My way, I turn it into My rest in him and I work out the man through it wonderfully. Everything is beautiful in man when it can dwell beautifully in his inside, with a beautiful spirit, and when many are gathered together this way, there My blessing, My protection and My joy are also as well, My people.

The sons of God are those who have Him into their midst, and I have made you used to Me, My people, and I have given you power to become son of God. Take care Jerusalem of today, My people, and be one body, one will, one love, as I and My Father are one. I have always stretched out My hand above you to work you out, so that you may know afterwards to wait for Me and to know to get into My way and know to call Me and then to know to welcome Me, for you have to be careful all the time, as the one who watches over everything and in everything, son. Teach yourself to know more and more to have Me and to know to be Mine, for this is your union with Me all the time and everywhere, My people.

Oh, carry Me, son, for it is good to the one who carries Me. Let your watch never go out day or night, for it keeps Me with you and it gives Me powers for you, and I give you life in abundance and I make your rich with God and rich with heavenly protection, son, for where I am with you, the angels, the saints and the heavenly powers have work upon you and for your life, My people. I, your Lord, Comforter and Creator forever, have always commanded every heavenly power to stay near you from all sides and to carry you to My love and then to stay forever to your help on My behalf, My people.

The one, who watches with Me, has the love of the heaven forever on his side, and you should not forget about longing, My people. Come together with longing in a spirit of prayer, so that we may always be one spirit, and give Me your love, son. When your love is with Me, I am yours and I do you good and I give you from My fullness, so that you may not be poor neither in your spirit, nor in your body and nor in your soul, for I take great care of you, My people. My hand is reached out above you and you should learn to receive from it, for I have in it everything you may need and everything I need in your inner being and around you and in your country, My people, for your country is the country of My coming. My mother, the Virgin, has it as a little baby in her arms and surrounds it from all sides as the dearest treasure of her Son, as a cradle of the saints and angels.

Oh, My people, where I am there are the saints and the angels, son, and I want that we all may be one spirit, We and you, and carry one another with the spirit of prayer and of holy work, to be one spirit and one love. May My exhortation be your fulfillment, for I exhort you more and more beautifully not to live within your spirit but rather to live with your spirit within My Spirit, and to do this work beautifully, so that I may be able to take delight in it and to strengthen it on the earth, for when I have from you I am able to work over the entire earth, My people.

Oh, strengthen the spirit of your prayer, the spirit of My glory with you and the spirit of fulfillments, My people. Exhort one another to hope, peace, watchfulness, and to a holy joy, calling My power and My comfort near you. I want each one of you to be one fire, and I, like you, to be consumed in it for you, and to prepare you truly and truly for the great glory, which will rejoice those on the earth.

Stay with the little garment on you and in the spirit of the heavenly grace, and let the Holy Spirit be your food, sons, and with Him to take all your good from Me. Give yourself to the Holy Spirit every moment, with all of your hope, for you are My sanctified ones, and My exhortation is with you, and you should fulfill it. Amen.

I am affectionate after you, sons. Oh, if you saw sufficiently enough this, you would rejoice more, for I want you to have joys and I want this with the whole heaven; I want this more than you do, for I can hardly come to you when you do not have peace and joy. However, I make you strong, for I have power. Remain under My power and I will strengthen you. Believe and this is how it will be, and let your faith be great and love My exhortation greatly.

Come to joys for I can carry you in My mantle. Strengthen yourselves in all things from above, for I tell you with longing: Peace to you! Get one another together with your spirit and longing for God and give joys to the Father, the Son and the Holy Spirit, growing in grace, for the time of the heavenly fulfillments is with you, and you will be comforted and will always see that I, as by this time, fulfill My promises that I have made from the beginning of this work and until today. Amen.

Let us be one spirit, My people, and you should fulfill what you hear from Me, son. Be all in one fulfillment, all in your spirit, in My Spirit, sons, and do not forget this!

Do not wait from one another and rather let everyone fulfill this to one another, so that you may be strengthened through My promises, which you should always bring before Me, sons, so that I may fulfill everything. Amen. If you are weak, I, the Lord, tell you not to be weak. Listen to My exhortation and do not be feeble. Receive Holy Spirit and live by Him and give Him power over you, for He works to perfection within these days. May your wisdom grow in you and may your prayer to heaven crown it. Amen. Oh, do not be fearful, feeble sons. Be strong, sons, for God Almighty brings about His victory for the righteous, for He keeps the paths of righteousness, He watches in the paths of the righteous, and He carries them in the paths of the good. Amen. Sing in the Holy Spirit. Pray incessantly in the Holy Spirit, sons. Keep your spirit awake, for the Holy Spirit is being perfected from God on the earth and produces victory for the Lord. Amen.

Oh, My people, listen to My exhortation, son. Listen to My exhortation given to you every moment and receive from My peace, and take it with hope. Let all of your care be the fulfillment of My word, for the fear of God is a great help for those who love it. Help each other and teach yourselves to give life to each other, for it is the time of the sons of life on the earth, so that you may be able to fulfill with them the mystery of the new age, a godly age over the man. Amen.

I embrace you in the spirit of My peace. Be comforters and you will be comforted, for the spirit of comfort will be with you, forever with you, sons. Amen, amen, amen.
