Peace to you, those who stand with vigilance and with holy work in My name in the citadel of My word and upon the earth, sons, for I, from here, out of the citadel, go forth word upon the earth and secretly govern the course of all things, and not man governs, but only God, only He, for man cannot do what only God does, as He sees and knows what must be done and fulfilled!
