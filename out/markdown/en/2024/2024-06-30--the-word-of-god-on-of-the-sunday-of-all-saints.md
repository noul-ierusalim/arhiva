I am with the hosts of saints and angels above here, and I am in their feast, given on earth and in heaven by the holy fathers of the Christian nation on earth, oh, and there is a gathering of consolation for My saints on this day, the first Sunday after Pentecost, and now the fast is beginning to welcome the feast of the holy apostles, and everything is set beautifully and holy, for the Holy Spirit has brought and brings in time through the saints witnessing settlements and places them on earth.

Peace over My garden with you and over you, the sons who stand watch here for the things in heaven and for the things on earth who testify to God through their work, and after today's feast the white tent rises and the saints go to their homes, and you, after a short time of rest, will begin to work further for My many works with you, sons!

[legatura_la_teme id_capitol="2" id_subcapitol="4"]Among the groups of saints who are stopping here today, I have Virginia celebrated with the feast that brought her into the world, *(Saint Virginia, the sixth apocalyptic trumpet, r.n.)* even though in the book of time the days are not written in their place as they come, for those who write do not take into account how they write, and they write as they take place in the book of time. Therefore, Virginia is on a feast and is with her army that came from earth to heaven from among the people of My word, and she has remained from the feast of Pentecost until today for her celebration, and her company has remained too, oh, how great was and is the work of My word through her and the people nourished from this source of My coming again from the Father at the end of time to speak and shepherd, and My work over people has never been in any other way, it has not been with the sight of God, but in speaking from the clouds it has been all the time, for I carry Myself with the clouds, sons, and I carry Myself everywhere like lightning that goes quickly from one side to another, *(Via the internet, r.n.)* and God does His work among people through those who have from God the gift of faith and its bearing on earth, oh, sons, and woe to those who have spoken as it came to their mouths about this source of My word and people from this time, for God is mysterious, and His greatness, His work among men, and all His accomplishments, written to be settled in their time, do not come up the mind of earthly man, and behold, My time has come, and everything is like in the time of Noah, when people did not want to pay attention to what came from heaven to be announced and for people on earth to listen to God![/legatura_la_teme]

Sons, sons, pay close attention so that you may learn well, for faith needs the power of love, which My saints had, and through them I have established on earth those things in heaven to be fulfilled, and the power of love has established and fulfilled them all. Love when it is without power cannot, does not exist, and so are Christians who do not know the power of love, as for God a great and confessing love is needed, and behold, I have no power in Christians who do not know the power of love for God, and the love that cannot does not confess, does not bear fruit, does not show its deeds, does not sacrifice itself for the works of God among men so that God may be known on earth through His many works.

[legatura_la_teme id_capitol="2" id_subcapitol="4"]I had Virginia as a burning bush, from which I, the Lord, spoke My word upon the earth and it remained written among men and testifies to My work. Woe and woe again, to those to whom God revealed the work of My word through Virginia and then allowed My voice to cool them down, lest they burn under the power of the love of God, My children!

Be very careful that there is nothing that can cool you down and separate you from the work of the word of My coming in this time, as happened to many who have not watched for the power of the love of God. Oh, do not wander from place to place, sons, I have always told you this throughout the work of My word. I am telling you again: Do not go looking for something sweeter to find for yourself and let your mind, which no longer stops searching for it, lead you and which can separate you from the word with which I am now coming to people to draw them to holiness and then to Father.

Great is the wandering that leads Christians, dedicated with strong faith to My coming today, to the earth, to be torn away from God, oh, and great is satan's hatred, and this is what happened to those who have fed with dissatisfaction on the table of My word and sought other places and sources for themselves, oh, and I have always told them, I have told them about this trap hidden from their minds, and they went and found it sweeter in other places. Food is tasty and desired when you are hungry, not when you have everything and eat the chosen ones, and the abundant food that is everywhere is of no value. *(The Lord refers to the "food of the word," the discovery of heavenly mysteries, but also to the food served to guests on feast days, this food being fasting, that is, without meat, milk, cheese, eggs, as is consumed in the monastery all the time, r.n.)*

Oh, the market is full for those who seek in the market to eat the chosen ones, but for those who are hungry, it is different with these, and they avoid those from people and those who are foreign to them, and they eat clean, strained, well-cleaned and sorted food, so that they may not be harmed by those that seem good to them from everywhere, oh, and many have lost their way and did not love to remain Mine and for Me to work with them My wonderful work of today,[/legatura_la_teme] for [legatura_la_teme id_capitol="4" id_subcapitol="2"]two thousand years ago, I told Jerusalem that it would be desolate because it did not receive My coming from then to include it in My mysteries, and it was then rejected, as God was rejected by them, and behold, I have finally chosen another Jerusalem, a new Jerusalem, and I am announcing Myself over the earth with My new election over My Romanian country, the place where I have caused faith and holy work to be like never before, and neither will it be any longer, because, I now have a wonderful thing to work with this country.

Oh, you Virginia, My work with you bore fruit, it has borne a people for faith and a beautiful citadel for Us on earth, so that We may come and fulfill those prophesied during the seventy years of the word, and behold how much faith the Christians who had the part of being given this great gift, My word over the earth, over them and its fulfillment, must work, but few of them remained for the power of faith, few of those who were and did not remain for the great fulfillments of God, and which are written in the Scriptures and await faith for them, but faith, for behold, there was no lie, no error, no heresy, oh, no! Not the archpriest Irineu, My witness in the church of the Romanian people, not Marian Zidaru *(The builder, r.n.)*, who adhered to the mystery of New Jerusalem, not the man, for the mystery of this building has been coming for a long time, and was locked up by the enemies of faith. However, I have sent My servant of today of My word and it was opened to him by heavenly ordinance and he brought to light what was put in darkness and now all the enemies see this holy light lit by Me in the midst of the Romanian nation, and even those who have passed away with the body see, they now clearly see the fulfillments of My word and of the New Jerusalem with all its mysteries, oh, and what they would not give to be able to come back in the body to repair their faith and humility and sight and to embrace what they blasphemed then, for you, Virginia, are the majestic bird in the Romanian story, and I am the shepherd who made you sing from God, and you were then hidden by the enemies so that you would not sing My song over the earth, and Our little ones have unraveled the mystery and the heavenly wealth and have brought to light the whole history of My word from your time with Me, for the time of confession and all the fulfillments of My word has come, and the New Jerusalem is proclaimed from the top to the bottom, and the Romanian land will be sought after like precious gold, and kings and learned and scribes will set out for it, as it is written, and they will search and know God through My word today, and through the confirmation of the mystery of the New Jerusalem on the Romanian land, and there will be a great and holy celebration with the fulfillment of seventy years of the word descended from heaven over the Romanian land, and I, the Lord, will rule with the heavenly powers these great days between earth and heaven with all their proclamation.

You, My Virginia, have great comfort on this day. You have remained since the feast of Pentecost with all those of your company in heaven to have them at the celebration for you, and great and abundant is their comfort, and the mystery of these great accomplishments does not rise to the mind of man, heaven and earth in holy work here, in My gardens today, and soon the garden of paradise at Maluri *(Which means Margins, the bank of the flowing water, r.n.)* will be inscribed on earth, and it must be brought to view by Our little ones, with whom We work and labor, and then the book of My word with ten more years of word, and then the proclamation from the top of the work of the Word over the earth.
