I am surrounding in the spirit of peace from above the citadel of My word in the midst of the Romanian nation and My guards within it, and I am entering the word into the citadel and into the book, and above are standing the heavenly and angelic hosts accompanying Me on the path of My word across the earth.

I am the Lord Jesus Christ, I am the Son of the Father of Sabaoth and the Virgin Mother, through whom I descended to earth as a baby born two thousand years ago, and here I have come again from heaven to earth, to the Romanian land, the first to emerge from the waters at the creation of the world, and the first even now, at the end of time, to make man again, for in the beginning I created him from the soil of this land and from My breath over his creature molded from clay, and now, at the end of time, I am creating man again, and from the word I am creating him, and I call him the same as I called Myself two thousand years ago: the new man, again the creation of man.

This Sunday is bringing the Christian nation on earth the great awakening for the work of man through repentance from dead deeds and the sins of man`s life, and Christians are sitting down on the watch for the works of eternal life through fasting with the body, by stopping from much indulgence, and then the prayer through which the Christian meets with God and reveals his soul so that the Lord may cleanse him with His mercy from everything that distances him from the life for heaven, and behold, the holy time is coming, the work for the soul, the reconciliation of man with God, so that then the feast of the Lord`s resurrection, the holy remembrance of the salvation through Christ of those who believe in Him by working the commandments of life with their life on earth, may rise to heaven.

Therefore, I, the Lord Jesus Christ, am opening My arms and the word of the Shepherd and speaking this day to the Romanian Christian nation, urging it, strengthening its vigilance and Christian work before the Lord, for this country has been My country for two thousand years and since then it has had on its hearth the mystery of Christian life, a mystery left on earth by Me through My disciples *(On this land, through the Holy Apostle Andrew, r.n.)* and through the saints after them, and it has been a written history of two thousand years of Christianity, oh, and there is no other country on earth like the country of My coming now to it, now, upon My return from the Father to give birth to the world again, oh, and I work through the word.

But how ugly is the whole earth where I, the Lord, have not been wanted and accepted as Shepherd, and there the antichrist and his servants work against Me and My kingdom on earth with the people! But behold, My time has come, of the Lord Jesus Christ on earth as a word, and the breath of My mouth melts the antichrist, as it is written, **(2Thess: 2/8)** so that then heaven will settle on earth with all the adornments of the kingdom of God with men, the Lord and the Christian nation, which has been testifying to Me for two thousand years through the Christian saints through whom I have written My history and built My church, My dwelling with man, and behold, I have an inheritance on earth and I am coming to strengthen it, to care for it, to serve it and to glorify Myself in it, as it is written in the Scriptures about the blessed and full of holy fruit, but also about the unbelievers, who will be like dust scattered by the wind, for these are against My righteous ones, they are not with God and on His side, and they are adversaries, as it is written.

Oh, Romanian people, oh, Christian son, open to Me so that I may speak to you! Be careful, for you are a Christian nation and you have holy ancestors in heaven, who bring you before Me through their prayers. Oh, be careful, for behold, you have the duty to obey the ancestral law as your salvation and My heaven of saints will stand with you and will rejoice in this joy as in heaven on earth with you, for heaven is watching with love over you, Romanian nation. You have a great choice, don not forget! The saints and angels are serving you and testifying of you. They see and know that you are the people of the Lord at the end of time, working with God for the fulfillment of the last Scriptures. We have to overcome the darkness of the antichrist man, to help the people pass from death to life and many and more to be baptized into the name of the Father and of the Son and of the Holy Spirit, all through Christ, the Word of God the Father.

Come to work, come to fight with satan, come, Romanian people! The Lord raises up from you a people of apostles, who will carry My word across the earth for the resurrection of many. The Lord is asking you to work with Him. The Lord is coming to earth with tens of thousands of His saints, as it is written. **(Matt: 25/31; Mark: 8/38; 1 Thess: 3/13)** The book of My seventy-year word spoken on your hearth lies open on your table, Romanian people and land. Heaven awaits you to rise and awaken to the holy work as never before on earth, and there will be heavenly beauty on your hearth and the kings of the earth will bow down to this glory seen from all over your land.

You, servants of the church of this nation, do not set aside the word of God, but put it to work, oh, sons. Urge the Christian flock to obey God, to the holy customs of two thousand years. Everywhere on earth there is pagan life in people. Oh, not like that! The Romanian country is a Christian country. Do not allow God to be trampled on! Stay vigilant from now on, even if you have not been so before. Do your duty, for which you will be asked to answer, because behold, nation rises against another nation from place to place on earth, because paganism wants to be at home everywhere, but it should not be the same in Christian countries, where the will of God must be worked, because woe to the earth and the peoples on it if there were not a Christian nation in the world from place to place, through which the Lord may have mercy and may the earth not sink because of the many paganisms and mortal sins on earth!

Behold, satan spreads his ointments everywhere, and people, and not even Christians, know this, for everything is done in the dark so that it cannot be seen and so that it can be touched by Christians, but the one who listens is protected, is vigilant, is covered by angels, as long as he listens completely, for the disobedient man established satan with his disobedience to God in heaven, oh, that he was not satisfied after being arrogant and disobedient, and so man gave birth to satan, My adversary and that of man, and since then satan works on man, and man listens to him and takes from his mind, and does not take from God for himself.

Oh, My Romanian country, oh, Romanian son, just as it was announced about Me that I would come from the Father to people on earth, to promise them forgiveness and salvation from eternal death, so it was announced through the Scriptures about you that you would be My country of coming, **(Apoc: 21/1-3)** the country of the Lord, **(See Matt: 21/43; John: 14/21; Hebrews: 9/28)** My wedding table, the country of the New Jerusalem. **(Zechariah: 2/10-13.)** Oh, here I am with the work of My word on your hearth now!

Oh, Romanian son, oh, Christian son of this land, and especially you, the sons who have brought yourselves to Me to be My close people and to embrace holiness, behold, you have grown and are growing for God in the womb of this work, to which I want all the sons of the Romanian nation to come, and through them many from the earth to come.

Oh, man who seeks the Lord, here is what I am telling you: In this womb you are formed and grow, are born a new man and then you are. You cannot make yourself, but the womb that carries you, and you stay inside until you are formed into a man ready to be born, and then another growth, the growth of the Christian body, from a child to the size of the human body in its entirety.

This is why I told you, Christian son, to come out of the world! Come out, My son, so that you may be a son of God later! But you came out of the children of the world, but you gave yourself too little to Me afterwards, because you did not even fulfill enough with your body to come out as I advised you, so that I could protect you, and behold, I see how you do not guard yourself from the snares of satan and his anointings, for it is not enough for the soul, if you love your body more than your soul, more than God, from Whom you have the soul and the life of the body.

Oh, Romanian sons, and you, Christian sons through obedience for Christian life, nourish yourselves with the spirit of the teaching from above, not with your own spirit. Do not take from your mind the path for your steps. Take from the front, for such is the order from above for the sons of God, and you will be merciful and have humility, sons.

The time of fasting from food and sins has come. Stay with Me, stay in My love, stay always, you, Romanian children, and you, Christian children who love God, and we will make a great victory over the land of people without God with their lives, oh, how great is the iniquity, it is many and it keeps growing and it is about to cover the whole earth. But, I work without ceasing, and I work through the word to overcome through the Orthodox Christian nation, no matter how little it is on earth, for I, the Lord, can, and the Christian can also through fasting and prayer, as I said two thousand years ago for the Christians with whom I overcome the devil with the name of satan, and My adversary hidden under the name of the antichrist.

Fasting and prayer, this means the law of heaven. Oh, sons, obey the Christian law, because it is broken from heaven. Adam weeps for the entire human race, weeps with inconsolable pain and is crying out to Me on this Sunday, called in Christian terms the expulsion of Adam from paradise, and with him the entire human race then, but he is crying out now and saying: "The Lord is coming to you, Christian race. Open Him to believe in His word, and you will be the savior of the human race, from you to me, and he will thank you for your faith at the end of time, with which you bring the Lord to earth. Amen."

Oh, the Father sent Me to come to earth, to comfort the weeping of Adam and to go to the abode of the dead to release the captives and to bring resurrection to many with My resurrection. Then I left a holy ordinance through My two thousand year old church and I have enriched it through the holy fathers, so that prayers and fasting may be made through the church for those who are no longer on earth and for those who are still leaving, and that they may have salvation and eternal life through the cry from earth for them until today, and behold how great a connection there is between heaven and earth for the work of saving man! And I, the Lord, protect the work for heaven and stand in the middle for My flock, and satan cannot overcome God, and he will be overcome, he, and all his servants and his lies, for he is the father of lies, and therefore he will fall, as it is written.

And you, Christian people, have the duty to know and guard against the way of the world, the way of satan and all his anointings, and to strengthen yourself in the place kept clean for you and yours, for it is time to be wise and ever wiser in this time that is hostile to you and Me, but I, the Lord, stand at the helm and guide you with the word, only have the will to believe Me and to leave Me before you, for I am the Lord, oh Christian son.

Now I have to live the word of peace over the peoples, over the places of war between peoples, and I am saying:

Peace to you, you rulers of nations, and to you, the peoples under rulers! I, the Lord, put My angels and strengthen them over your works, for your plans are not like My plans. Therefore, I, the Lord, keep watch over those who seek peace and for those who rely on God. I, I work more than what people do, and to them I am saying from heaven: peace to you, and let every war end in peace!

Peace comes from God, not from men, and to you, those who incite to war, peace to you even more, even more powerfully, and bow to My word, for from heaven I am placing it upon you and am saying: Peace to you! Do you hear? Oh, bow and learn from Me to work love, for love is from God, it is from heaven.

The God of love is saying to those who have rebelled against war: peace to you, peace to you, peace to you, and let them hear immediately what the Lord is telling to them! Amen, amen, amen.[/legatura_la_teme]
