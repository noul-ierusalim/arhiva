I am coming with the saints in tens of thousands, as it is written about My coming. **(1 Thessalonians: 3/13)** The Scriptures send Me to come, and behold, I am coming, for the time of this Scripture has come. The heavens bow down and the Lord descends on the clouds of the cherubim and walks on the wings of the wind and releases His voice, as it is written, **(Apoc: 1/6-7)** that it is a time of unbelief and people reject God and choose another king, My adversary, the antichrist, **(1John: 2/18,19)** the one who made a name for himself after My name, as an adversary, the antichrist, and behold, I am coming and standing in battle with him, for the time has come to reveal him and, therefore, I am coming with the saints and the heavens are bowing down for the Lord to come and leave His voice on the earth.

Oh, what a name you have! You are called by My name, My adversary. Only next to Me do you have a name. Your name testifies to Me and to you, the one who is My adversary. You are a poisonous serpent; you have been against heaven and paradise since the beginning. You are dust and ashes before God. You try to show yourself strong, but you are afraid of everything and tremble with all your army, for you are afraid of Me, and it is written in the Scriptures that you are cursed for eternity. Oh, what shall I do to you? Arrogance has no cure, and all the arrogant are your army, and My meek and humble ones erase your traces everywhere through their goodness from Me. You have no strong army, oh, you have not, for My Spirit has been against you for seven centuries and more and it arouses ever greater fear in you. I will expose you and strip you of all the paint on you, and you will show yourself without wanting to, and every breath will see that you are the adversary of Christ, that you are My enemy, and that I am the Savior and Redeemer of man, I, not you, I, the One Who made heaven, earth and man, I, not you, do you hear?

Oh, you will keep hearing this word of Mine. You will keep trembling from the breath of My mouth, which reveals you and fills you with fear, and you fight with arrogance to cover your fear, and you know that you will be exposed with all your hidden work, with all its darkness against the life of man. You have known Me from paradise, since you became My adversary. You are a powerless spirit face to face with God. You have remained with the spirit of arrogance, which gives you evil power as your work. Behold, the time is coming to show you with all your evil and centuries-old labor, that I have before Me the work and the establishment on earth of the kingdom of God and of paradise, and you will no longer be able to stand by Me, for I am clothed with the power of the Father in heaven and on earth, as I left a word two thousand years ago, and it is time to prove this now, and it is My time and I am coming with it to earth little by little, and you little by little are falling and collapsing with all your chairs, with all your army, for so it is written. This word is Jesus Christ, the Word Who speaks over the earth and announces My coming, and you go away from My path, for I, the Lord, am commanding you this, while you still believe that you can be. Oh, no, the light is coming, the Lord is coming with tens of thousands of saints and with the throne of His glory, **(See Isaiah: 11/4 and 2 Thess: 2/8)** which will shine above the Romanian land, here, where I, the Lord, wage My battle of victory over satan with the name of antichrist, the adversary of Jesus Christ. Amen.

Oh, Romanian people, remain Christian, Romanian son! The adversary, antichrist, wants to separate you from God and to believe in him. You are of good lineage and you must watch all the time, so that you are not caught off guard under the heel of My enemy and yours. Oh, stay awake, because it is a time of unseen war and My enemy and yours wants you to lose My protection above you. Oh, it is impossible. My Virgin Mother has a heavenly army above you for Me and for you. The heavenly armies watch over you day and night for the work of My word in your midst. You have the heaven of saints and angels on your side, and I am with the throne of My glory on your hearth and I divide My word over the earth to chase away the enemy antichrist and protect you from his baits, with which he tries to tear your Christian shirt and make you a pagan according to his poisonous law, the poison of sin, for sin is the poison that bites from man little by little with its destructive sweetness, and to which evil spirits, who feed on man, are drawn.

Oh, Romanian people, be very careful not to be sold by those who have the work of shepherds over you, for the servants at the altars join hands with My enemy, the antichrist, and have no power to oppose them. However, you remain a Christian and do not be silent for the Lord your God and confess Him, so that you may not lose God's protection over you during such an evil time between earth and heaven. So, do not forget, do not forget that you are a Christian, do not forget to be a Christian, for the Christian is very precious on earth, as precious and great as God, because he has God as his Master.

Behold, there is big and great turmoil and confusion all around over the nations and it is a time of vigilance and I am urging you to stay awake with the Lord over this time, Romanian people, because behold, there is turmoil with you now for the one who are be to ascend the throne of power over the country, and there is no one with God among those who have registered their lots. *(In the presidential and parliamentary elections, r.n.)* Oh, what will I do? I, the Lord, stand from heaven as your Master and I rule, but you, Romanian people, how will you work? I have My coming word upon your hearth over the earth and I am sharing Myself everywhere and I am sending out a cry over the nations and telling them that it is an evil time, that one nation is raised against another nation, as it is written, but worse than this is the danger of unbelief, for satan wants to prevail everywhere and say that there is no God. Oh, who would have protected the earth and the world if it were not for God, the One Who created everything? This is why the Lord comes to earth as a word! Oh, how could I not come and deliver My beloved ones, those who love God Who comes with the saints, as it is written to come and deliver the world from unbelief and from the darkness of unbelief?

The Lord is coming! Do you hear, oh peoples? The Lord is coming on earth word; He is coming and speaking over you: Peace to you! Do you hear? You, who want to be great on earth, oh, you cannot be great unless you have God great over you. No one has greater value than the one who does the will of God among men. The one who does his wills has no value, since he always collapses face to face with his wills. The one who does the will of God and his neighbor, that one is as much as the sky and the earth and is from God one like this and has great value. Behold, love is the one that heals man from himself, love brings peace between peoples, love means God on earth with men, and that is why the Lord comes word on earth, and the earth knows its Maker and welcomes Him, even if the people on earth are cold towards God.

Oh, children of My word, I have spoken with My adversary and I have you as witnesses of My word on earth. All people are weak and waver in this time, and whoever thinks he cannot, it is because of the spirit of conceit working over people, because people do not read the Scriptures as they should be read, and in which there is so much grace, so much teaching of faith and walking in the way with God. Oh, people do not have roots in the Scriptures and that is why they are weak and they give in to the deception of the antichrist man and share their investigations, temporary slander and waste of time and mind from heaven.

Oh, sons, there is much to learn about the walking of man in God and with God. Man comes to God and does not stay to the end on the holy path, and after a while of staying, he begins to waver, for he has no roots in the Scriptures and does not know how to guard himself from the sin of separation from God, from the spirit of the world, which tempts the unstable.
