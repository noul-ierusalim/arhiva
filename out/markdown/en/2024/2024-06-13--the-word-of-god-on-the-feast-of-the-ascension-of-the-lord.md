Peace, My children, peace to you from God. Amen.

It is a royal day of feast, and we are setting a table of word, oh, sons. For two thousand years, Christians have been on earth, and they are the sons of the kingdom of God established among men with its glory, with all that I, the Lord, brought from the Father to earth for those who will deny themselves and carry their cross after Me, coming after Me and leaving the world and its temporality, for I came with eternal life and gave it as a gift to those who followed Me, and whom I took into My eternity, where I am, as I told My disciples that I was preparing a place for Me, for them and for all those who will give Me their life, so that I may carry their journey to eternity, and whom faith in Me, in the Son of God, the One sent from heaven to earth with His kingdom among men, oh, sons, has always carried.

[legatura_la_teme id_capitol="1" id_subcapitol="7"]I stayed with people on earth for thirty-three years two thousand years ago, since My time, the time of Christ, is counted. At the end of My journey then, the great men of that time crucified Me on the cross, because of the many signs and wonders that I worked and set among people for the eternal remembrance of God, and I laid at that time the foundation of the kingdom of God with people, oh, sons. I rose from the crucifixion after three days and wrote Myself on earth as true God from true God, and then another forty days passed and I left My disciples behind Me, as further builders of Christ among men with His kingdom, and I was taken from their sight by a cloud, which covered Me even as I was blessing them and explaining to them about the kingdom of God and telling them: *«You will receive power from heaven, for the Holy Spirit will descend upon you, and you will be My witnesses in Jerusalem, in all Judea, in Samaria, and to the ends of the earth»*, **(Acts: 1/8)**, and then I ascended to heaven, and a cloud hid Me from their sight and I released the two witnesses dressed in white and said to My disciples: *«This Jesus will return as you saw Him go into heaven».* **(Acts: 1/11)**

[/legatura_la_teme][legatura_la_teme id_capitol="8" id_subcapitol="5"]My coming from the Father two thousand years ago had as witnesses My disciples then, and for My coming now with the clouds I have you as witnesses, the sons who share My word from the ends to the ends of the earth, and behold, as then, even now the world does not seek after My coming and sees its own, and I stand with the sons of My kingdom in the midst of people and do My work from the end of time, and after it comes eternity, it comes in sight, it comes as it is written, it comes with its own, and I, the Lord, have opened the way to it and have given the preparation for it to those who believe in My coming as word on earth, and the preparation of heaven for those who give themselves as sons of the kingdom of God, nourished by My word and taught by Me the work of eternal life on earth, the reward of those who believe in My coming two thousand years ago and the one now, from the end of time, and which perfects the one from then, and here is the life of those who follow Me for eternity: holiness, faith, love, the heavenly work, and their joy with God forever and ever as sons of the Father, as I am.

[/legatura_la_teme][legatura_la_teme id_capitol="6" id_subcapitol="1"]So, what do those who follow Me have to work for the life of paradise? They must do what man did not listen to at the beginning and fell from paradise. I have set up the law of paradise little by little for those who have chosen Me in these days and I have told them to eat the food of paradise, fruits, greens, seeds, vegetables, and all that is for the food of paradise. I have told them to listen to God, and not to themselves. I have told those who follow Me: *«Be holy, for I am holy!»* Holiness is the law of paradise, and with it is piety, it is humility, and all through self-denial, all of this worked out with great care, because the mixing of the human, the earthly and the natural makes man fall from paradise, as it was two thousand years ago, oh, how difficult it is, sons, it is difficult for man to understand completely and then want to be for God with his life on earth. However, I, behold, stand among you, speak to you, bring you from heaven, give you My body and word and leave My power among you for you, for your protection, sons, for beyond the border is satan with his dirty and black hell among those who serve him, and I hide you in Me, I am with you, I carry you by the hand, I correct you if you incline to error, I give you from heaven, and I always add to your garment and keep you in My secret, and I stand with you in the Father, for I am in the Father, oh sons.[/legatura_la_teme]

I have a message to give to those who stand under My name as servants over people, for they stand against many things, and My flock perishes through disobedience, through insubordination, and behold, I am telling the elders, who stand so great over the flock not healed of sin, I am telling them that two thousand years ago, I worked much and many things on earth, and some remained written in the book of Scripture, and the many and more have remained in the minds and customs of Christians, from then until today, and which path to heaven has the name of the holy tradition, to which many holy written works have been gathered over time, or from one to another by word of mouth, through the connection with Me of the sons of My flock, and all are dedicated to God and taken for the work of faith with deed, oh, and I have a book for those at the helm to give it to them, and I will give it to them, sons, because there is much to clarify, to set for faith and work, and therefore, I strengthen My blessing upon you to work with you, to bring to you for sharing and clarification many works, My sons, because many of those who know My word do not fulfill the beautiful and entire counsel of My word, and their path to eternal life with Me and near the Father weakens, My sons.

[legatura_la_teme id_capitol="5" id_subcapitol="6"]Behold, the great feast of Pentecost is approaching, and you are preparing for it, and those in heaven see that the white tent is rising above and all the bedding is celebrating in My garden with you, and I, the Lord, am opening the way for those who have fallen asleep for the summer memorial before Me again for them, here with you, under the tent.

The retinues of the heavenly powers are excited and awaiting My meeting with you and with them here, in the citadel of My word, and the helping angels beside you and with you here, in the work of preparation, are full of movement and heaven beside you with all the holy haste, and which subdues time, oh, sons, for you are few and small, and My work with you is much and great, but glory to the Lord, that your faith may work mightily, for with this I have worked everything on earth, with her and her sons, all through faith, as it is written, sons.

[/legatura_la_teme][legatura_la_teme id_capitol="6" id_subcapitol="3"]Let none of the children nourished with the food of My word waver from one foot to the other for the observance of the days of Tuesday and Thursday nine times after Holy Passover, *(Not to work in the fields, not to process iron, wood, earth, etc. in workshops, r.n.)* for this sacrifice dedicated to the Lord comes from the ancestors for the protection of gardens and houses, for the protection of the fruit, for the removal of pests, for clean and nourishing rains, not destructive of the fruit, oh, that people commit many sins, and no one brings a sacrifice to God for sin anymore, and that is why the holy ancestors dedicated these nine days of Tuesday and Thursday until after Pentecost, so that through this sacrifice, good protection would come from the Lord over gardens and orchards, over people, animals and birds, for protection from thunder, lightning, destruction with hail, from waters overflowing over the rivers upon people's households.

And to the priests who urge people not to follow these wrong customs, they say, I, the Lord, ask them to take into account the holy ancestors, from whom these prayer offerings remained, these days that they promised as a sacrifice brought to the Lord, such as not washing the clothes on them and in the house, not sewing with a needle, not using tools for work in the gardens, not hitting with a hammer, not hitting stone with stone and leaving these works for the other days of the week, so that they may be protected by God for the rest of the time, and protected from diseases and punishments for sins, for man commits sins day and night throughout his life on earth, and does not bring to the Lord a sacrifice of reconciliation and purification afterwards, for man has forgotten God and looks after his own, the things without God throughout his life.

You, children of My word, do not doubt, but, on the contrary, be joyful and obedient to keep the order of these nine times on Tuesdays and Thursdays after Holy Passover, oh, I have been saying for a long time, especially for those who do not give up their work for them during these days, to bring reconciliation with the Lord through this holy sacrifice, which is considered by the Lord as the times of fasting, prayer or special days, ordained by the saints, as the saving work for the life of the Christian.[/legatura_la_teme]

Oh, many things are to be understood for those who do not yet bow down, and everything must be put on paper, so that it may be known later and brought to fulfillment for the life of paradise on earth, for I said that I would wait for paradise at the border, with My coming with the clouds, oh, sons.

I, the Lord, now am blessing your labor for preparing the feast of Pentecost and support you with help from heaven and on earth, with the saints and the angels, and I am coming to your aid in everything, and you are seeing this clearly, My children.

I am also placing a blessing for the entrance to the feast of those who have fallen asleep, of all those from Adam until now, and we will set the tables, from the earth, and up, up, to the sons, and it will be like in heaven, and only eyes and ears will be everyone and everything then, and there will be heavenly joy, to the sons.

May everything be as it is in heaven, and may everyone work and prepare for the celebration here as well. Let there be no spirit of self, murmuring or doubt in you, and only you dwelling in Me and with Me, for I am in everything, because you are little, as I like to be the sons of God, through whom the Lord can, and not them, oh sons.

Now, glory to the Father and to the Son and to the Holy Spirit, glory to the Lord through you on earth in all that you work in My name and with Me, sons! May the comfort of the Holy Spirit comfort you! May the power of heaven accompany you through the Holy Spirit. May peace and its work bear fruit among you, a new heaven, a new earth and a new Jerusalem, for My coming is with you, the Lord is with you on earth, oh sons. Amen, amen, amen.
