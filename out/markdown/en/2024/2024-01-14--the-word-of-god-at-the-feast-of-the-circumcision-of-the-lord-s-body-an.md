My coming with the saints has a citadel of reception on earth, and I have guards in it and they open it for Me and I enter as word in the book when I come, and I shepherd with it.

Peace to you, sons, watchmen in the path of My voice when I come to sit in the book and to do My work today through you on earth! Peace to you, and power from Me I am leaving upon you to carry Me and to set Me on the path where My word leads Me to do its work, sons!

It is a holy feast among those in heaven for Me and for My Virgin mother, who then watched over My growth, after I was subjected to the circumcision of the body, and then dedicated to the Lord at the temple in Jerusalem.

Oh, and [legatura_la_teme id_capitol="2" id_subcapitol="6"]I have next to Me today the great hierarch Basil, who gathered good wisdom from the earth and from heaven and served with it those of his time, and then those after him until today and tomorrow, for the bearers of God have a great dowry gathered and have holy power and have a wonderful work and the Lord is glorified through them and places His works on earth, oh, and the Lord has rest in the saints and joy from those who carry Him with His works on earth, and I give them work from heaven and then on earth, and great is before Me the work of the prayers of My saints for those on earth, as it is written, (James: 5/16, 17) oh, and therefore I am coming with them in word now, and I carry Myself with them between heaven and earth and I testify to them of eternity, and behold, the hierarch Basil is now testifying to the work of God on earth, for he now works from heaven and helps God and men, oh sons.

— Our help, of Your saints, Lord, has always been from You on earth, and now also from You we help people, for people pray to the saints for them to be their intercessors with You for their sorrows, and for Your saints to work beside You for them, and behold how beautiful is Your work through Your saints, oh Lord! Oh, so You worked from Your beginning with Your apostles two thousand years ago, and they then worked in Your name for the sorrowful on earth, and after they came to heaven near You, they work among the saints, and great is the heavenly army, the army of Your saints, Lord, and great is its work upon the earth from Your side, oh Lord!

We walk with You and soothe pains, establish comforts and overcome enemies, oh, and what would happen to so many suffering people who cry out to You and the saints, if they were not heard and supported in difficult times? Oh, how much faith and how much love for heaven must ascend from earth to us so that we can bring down help from heaven where people cry out to us, Lord!

During my work with You on earth among people, I carried much pain because of the pains caused by satan on so many helpless people brought down by him, and I rose with Your name on my lips and in my heart and stood with a strong word against the evil work of satan to snatch many from the trap set by him so that he could snatch people from his side. In the same way, now I work for those who are suffering and enslaved by satan, and who cry out to me for them in their fight with this old enemy of seven thousand years and better over the eternal life of man, and behold, Lord, in Your name I say again and again over the evil work of satan:

May the Lord rebuke you, old devil, for the evils you send upon men, and even upon the sons of God you dare to extend your wrath to test them!

I expose you with your work that is against Jesus Christ, our God, the Son of God, the Father, the Son of the Virgin Mary, the mother of all Christians, and with His great name I expose you and curse you by your new name, antichrist, and I am telling you from the Lord with a holy command: Come out of the hiding place under which you are hiding and show yourself who you are, what you do and where you are, and bow down willy-nilly before Jesus Christ and let yourself be defeated, for your reign and power with which you have been deceiving the soul in man for seven thousand years so that man does not love God and to give him indifference of soul and to darken his vigilance so that you can bring him down to your side, and man is the creature of God, and you do not yet let your-self be afraid of this and to pull back and to let yourself be defeated, for behold the light that reveals you who you are, where you are and what you are doing secretly over people. Behold the Lamb of God, against Whom you stand striking in His flock, striking from the middle.

The Lord comes as word to the earth, and the breath of His mouth contends with you **(See Isaiah: 11/4 and 2 Thess: 2/8)** and brings you to the light to make you known on earth to all believers and unbelievers and then He is going to send you to your gathering, to your punishment, do you hear, satan?

I, the bishop of Christ, I, Basil, the servant of the Lord, am calling you to appear face to face with God the Word to give an account to Him for your fight against Him and man. Be ashamed and bow down and confess yourself so that all who were, who are and who are to come may know you, and then confess God the Father, the Son and the Holy Spirit, the Lord, the Maker of heaven and earth, against Whom you have stood for seven thousand years and more, since you have been fighting over man created by God to teach him resistance and disobedience and everything that would make him forget his duty to God at all times!

Come, do not delay any longer! You are discovered by Christ, the Word of the Father Sabaoth! You sneaked into the garden of paradise to begin your wickedness against man and against God, and you began there with the work of lies, and with it you ended your work that proves you to be the father of lies, for if at first you hid in the paradise of God, here is where you has been hiding later, but especially now, at the end, when your work has been still the same as at the beginning, and you have entered under the garment of a saint and hid with it in the known house of God and you are playing your theater, but now the curtain under which you have standing does not help you anymore, oh, no. The Lord is coming, putting His finger and revealing where you have been hiding. So, it is over! The Lord is bringing you out to be known and subduing you with His power, with His heaven of angels and saints and is speaking out your name: antichrist, the adversary of Christ, hidden under the garment of a saint.

God arises and satan under the name of antichrist is going to scatter, and all his servants flee, but the Lord discovers them fleeing and does not let them. Let these perish like smoke, like wax before the fire, and let those who love the Lord mark themselves with the sign of the holy cross and rejoice at seeing Christ, Who conquers the old enemy in evil, and let them glorify the victory of their Lord over satan under the name of antichrist!

Those who love the Lord, let them flee from the hidden work of the antichrist, the one with the garment of a saint on top! This I, the archpriest Basil, am saying, and those who have a mind from above, let them understand what I am saying, for the reign of satan in a seemingly holy place is staggering and falling, for so it is written.

And now glory to Your long-patience, Lord Jesus Christ, the conqueror of the antichrist, Your enemy and of Your name, from whom he has the name of adversary! Glory to You on my feast day among the saints, when I have refreshed my speech with satan, the one from this time of his darkness, so that he could not be seen with his adversarial work, but You come from the Father with the victory over him and his false servants like him, and the sorting will be done on the right and on the left, and those on Your side will be seen, and again, those on the side of Your enemy will appear, and each will receive according to his deed, according to his work, as it is written. Amen.

— Oh, children with whom I am writing My word these days, you are protected by all the heavenly powers and by all the saints with whom I stand in battle to uncover satan with the name of antichrist. You are on My side in this work of My victory. Be stronger in faith than ever, for the victory is great, it is beautiful because it is Mine, it is for My glory and that of My saints and of all those who love Me and choose Me out of their love on earth.

[/legatura_la_teme][legatura_la_teme id_capitol="5" id_subcapitol="6"]Now sit down, sons, and prepare the feast of the Epiphany beautifully, and you will put the prayers of the devil's rebuke over the Epiphany water and you will distribute drops of this holy water over the earth, for My country today has great protection from heaven, it has My Virgin mother above it with all the protection she brings to it, and you are the bedding of My Spirit, Who speaks over the earth from My country today, over which I stand and speak My word from above, which returns from the Father to men to work My victory over My enemy, the antichrist, and to sit with this victory before the Father and then build the kingdom of God on earth with men, oh sons. Amen, amen, amen.[/legatura_la_teme]
