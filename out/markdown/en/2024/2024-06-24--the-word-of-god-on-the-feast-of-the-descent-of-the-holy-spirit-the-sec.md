[legatura_la_teme id_capitol="8" id_subcapitol="3"]We are opening the book of My word and sitting in it with the second day of the feast of Pentecost, watchful sons for My word coming to earth in My time with you. You are My witnesses from the end of time, that I have made My path through you to come again and to do a new creation again, for it is written in the Scriptures My work with you, the renewal of the world, the rebirth, when all things pass before My throne for their renewal, starting with man, for I created all things for man, and through the word I created them, and only then I had created man, after the Father, the Son and the Holy Spirit made a council to give man body, soul and spirit of life and to make him king and to subject all things to him, oh, and what pain struck Me when he, the man, rose above Me to be greater, so that the Lord would not be first. However, I was the Word of the Father, the Son of the Father God, and I immediately sat down to watch over the enemy, the evil spirit that approached man, spoiled his beauty and then took him out of obedience to God, the Creator, and it has been seven thousand years since then, since sin has ruled over man, oh sons.

[/legatura_la_teme][legatura_la_teme id_capitol="6" id_subcapitol="1"]There is no truer death than sin, than man's departure from the hand of God to be greater than God, the Maker of the world and of man, this great dishonor brought upon God by man created by the hand of God, and brought to life by the breath placed by the Lord in the body of earth created, in My image and likeness.

Oh, sons, I had received great dishonor from My creature and enmity settled between man and Me, after he wanted to be greater between Me and him, and sin was born then on earth and settled between man and Me, and God has been watching from then until today with all the angelic retinues, with all the incorporeal heavenly powers, lest one may wander in the realm of eternity from man greedy for power over earth and over heaven, as he dreamed in the beginning of his greatness over God, oh, but then man fell, he fell from heaven and I hid heaven and all the unseen creation of God from him, I placed a covering over man's eye, and only the creation seen by man has remained for his sight, from then until today, and the unseen things of God's creation have remained in secret and await their Maker for their work, and man entered under sin, under the separation between him and God through the sin in him.

Here is a mystery, unfathomable by the mind of man, who deals with sin over his life in the flesh, for man subjects his soul under the dominion of sin, under death, for there is no death, but only sin is what separates man from life all the time, oh, and man always submits to the sin he was left with, after losing God.

I have raised up the mind of man from God so that he may not tremble under fear in his life on earth. He does not know with his mind that sin is the death that separated him from God, and behold, man commits sin and feels pleasure in committing it and runs after sin, poor man, for man has given the mind from heaven to sin. For seven thousand years, I have been coming out in the way of man in every way so as not to leave him under the pain of sin, oh, and I am full of compassion for the one who has been lost from God for seven thousand years, since sin overcame man.

Sons, sons, the sin committed by man has set up the curtain of separation between the seen and the unseen of God's creation, and man no longer sees the realm of God and the heavenly beauty of eternity in which the Lord and all the heavenly powers have their abode.

[/legatura_la_teme][legatura_la_teme id_capitol="1" id_subcapitol="7"]I came to earth seventy years ago and I chose the Romanian land to come and open My tent to place among people the voice of My coming again from the Father to man, as two thousand years ago when I came so secretly, so covered My glory under the form of a man among all the people on earth. I completed My work in a wonderful way, as only the heavenly powers knew and know how divinely I covered Myself with the radiance of My glory that I have from the Father, and after I rose from the dead so that the disciples could testify to the truth of My divinity, oh, I also appeared to them in secret, but full of power and a step beyond the world, and I told them after I conquered through resurrection, I said to them: *«All power in heaven and on earth has been given to Me, and you go and testify of My divinity among you and baptize those who will believe, baptizing them in the name of the Father and of the Son and of the Holy Spirit, and the signs of God will accompany you and you will place them on earth over people, for I will be with you until the end of time and the Lord will be testified on earth».* **(See Matthew: 28/18)**

[/legatura_la_teme][legatura_la_teme id_capitol="3" id_subcapitol="3"]Oh, sons, oh, sons, the inability of man to rise from the place from which he fell, from the state of paradise in which I placed him after I had created him and breathed upon him the spirit of life, this inability borne by man makes the Lord stand covered, humbled and deprived of the glory from above in sight during the time of the works that must be brought and placed on earth over men and for men.

Oh, sons, My glory is great, it is great insofar as it hides itself because of its heavenly greatness in front of the one, so small among the works of God's creation, man, for he wanted to be the first, the great, and here he is as small as he remains, poor thing! I have struggled to make him understand the damage he has done if he wanted to be great, I struggle, but he does not help Me.

Oh, man, you have nothing to do with glory. In vain you seek to be, to be able, to have. You can do nothing if you have broken away from God. Here is what you can do! You can resist God for sin, in order to commit the sin that separated you from the Lord's grace above, and which you cast aside even after God created you to have you as His, a witness of the Lord between heaven and earth, however, you were not good and became proud and then gave birth to sin, the adversary who stands between you and God, and I am telling you all this, so that you can settle in the meanings above and break the connection with death, for death is not over people, except for sin that brings man the temporal end, through which he may choose humility, repentance and the fear of God through these, oh, that there is a long time since man's separation from God and he still does not come out from under this burden.

[/legatura_la_teme][legatura_la_teme id_capitol="7" id_subcapitol="1"]Sons, sons, the view from here, together with the feast of Pentecost in My garden with you, is enriched by the spirit of comfort now placed upon those who spend time with Me and with you here for their remembrance and calling to the table of Pentecost. The day before Pentecost Sunday, you raised to the Lord a memorial and a table of remembrance for all those who have fallen asleep from Adam until now, and they came and were seated by the heavenly and angelic powers at the table, and then they remained at the feast of the feast of Pentecost here, for this joy was given to them. My work with you at this time has the power of resurrection, it has to fulfill the resurrection of those who have fallen asleep, who are waiting, all are waiting for Me to come, for the Lord to come as he did two thousand years ago and to raise them up, for he has brought and is bringing the resurrection.

Among those who had arrived and sat at their memorial table, each in turn in his group as it is written, here is the group of Virginia, *(Saint Virginia, the sixth apocalyptic trumpet, r.n.)* the people who were part of My work with her, My sons.

Oh, My Virginia, I comforting you now. You were and are the intercessor of those who came to Us, after they knew and stood by the work of My word, descended in you seventy years ago, so that I might cry out through you over the earth that the Lord is coming, that the resurrection is coming, that the work of the New Jerusalem is coming on the Romanian land. Oh, rejoice, Our dear, at the Pentecost table with yours who have arrived from among those known at the table of My word from your time and your descendants. The adornment of your company is brilliant and it has precedence among the heavenly companies, and the dividing curtain is expected to fall and the work of My word from the end of time, My coming word on earth and its witnesses, will be confessed on sides, and those who have passed beyond among yours are full of impatience to bring their testimony and for it to work on the earth, on those who will hear and believe, and those who are seared by the evil spirit of unbelief from sin, those are to be mourned, for it is written that even if someone from the dead were to rise, they would not stoop to take, and they would give to remain strangers to salvation, and only the mercy from above would do something for them.

Therefore, My Virginia, blessed be your work in heaven, together with your people who have arrived on high, and with whom you work so much, so diligently to gather the lost ones of your people on earth. One hour more and this fulfillment will be seen, as two thousand years ago, when My righteous ones stood up with Me and testified in Jerusalem to the Son of God and His power and His resurrection. Amen. **(Matt: 27/50-53)**

On the second day of the feast of Pentecost, I have set forth in the book My and your celebration alongside those who have fallen asleep and are called to the memorial table for them, for the mystery of the resurrection unfolds petal by petal like a flower that blooms fully in all its beauty and name, and I bring this fulfillment to the earth through My word spoken over you, and which prophesies resurrection, oh, sons who make true My coming from the Father to you, and from you over the earth, and even over those who have passed away with the body, and those are the ones who believe more than those who are still clothed in the body, for those whom the body does not confuse through its narrowness, those see perfectly, see everywhere, see from God the sight and testify and wait for the moment of confession to come and bring the great and precious meeting, that everything will change and they will testify of God the Word.

[/legatura_la_teme][legatura_la_teme id_capitol="5" id_subcapitol="6"]Virginia comforts those who share in the Pentecost table and she bears great honor for her heavenly company, and who has the testimony of the work of My word, and her company has a special adornment. And now, the feast of Pentecost is lifting up and the heavenly companies are going and take their place, and I, the Lord, remain with you always and we always work on the day of My glory to show you with her and her much work of preparation, children, and we are going to work now, Me and you, and we are preparing with great grandeur the place at Maluri, *(Maluri Village, the village where Saint Virginia was born and lived, r.n.)* My testimony begun there seventy years ago through Virginiaa, My trumpet of the end of time, through which I made My word come upon the earth, and behold, I am working to come to an end, and to begin to put in their first place those things that were shaken at the beginning through the sin of man created by God, and who so hard rises alone to meet the Lord in His coming with glory.

I am blessing your rest now, and then your work with Me to establish the heavenly beauty over My garden at the Maluri, sons. We are accomplishing all of these by working them, for I have only you now, and I must be able to work through you in order to accomplish all of these and to place them before the Father and to tell Him then, oh sons, let Us tell the Father: Father, this is the work that You have given Us to accomplish and to place it before You. Amen, amen, amen.[/legatura_la_teme]
