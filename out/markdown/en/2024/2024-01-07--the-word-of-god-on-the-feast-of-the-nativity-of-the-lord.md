The word of God is born on earth, it is born from God and is filling the world with My whisper, with My love, which is becoming a word and is being shared across the land, and from here over the whole earth announcing My coming with the saints, to prepare a people for Me and to place them in My presence and to work through their faith the fulfillment of the Scriptures of this time, that My time has come and of those who choose Me on earth for their life, and I come to them and give them according to the deed, and I give them My word and body, and I dine with them, and I show Myself to them **(John: 14/21)** and I work with them the victory against satan, My adversary, about whom the Scriptures say that he pulls the stars from heaven with his tail and throws them to the earth, and here is the time and his hidden fight to take the path from under the feet of Christians and for them to fall together with My kingdom from them, and I am coming now and giving account to satan and I am going to expose him with all the darkness under which he is hidden and bring him to light so that he may lose his power and victory over Me, for I am the Son of God the Father and I have all power in heaven and on earth as the Father gave Me to have, and now I am coming to earth as word, I come, and I have a bride people, and the Spirit and the bride are saying to Me: *"Come!"*, and that is why I am coming.

Oh, glory to God in heaven, and peace on earth among men! This is sung in heaven and on earth on the holy day of remembrance of My birth, Child of a Virgin mother, two thousand years ago. I came mysteriously, for thus God works on earth, and He reveals Himself with heavenly mysteries only to those who with a pure heart love Him in their lives, **(Hebrews: 9/28)** with their faith and with their deeds. I am working just as I did two thousand years, for My Father is working and I am working too, and so I am obeying Him.

Oh, peace to you, tiny people, gathered from the Romanian nation at My word that comes with the clouds above the land of this nation chosen by the Father two thousand years ago so that I may have it as a dwelling place for the coming of My word on earth at the end of time!

Peace to you, Romanian nation, peace from the midst of this tiny people, with whom I have been celebrating the wedding for almost seventy years with the saints at their table with God on it! I have a citadel of coming in your midst, Romanian people, and I have a people and I am sitting at the table with them now on the feast day of My birth on earth.

I came two thousand years ago in a great mystery revealed by the Father to a handful of people and they carried the mystery of My coming, My incarnation and birth from the Virgin mother, oh, and how could those of that time believed this mysterious truth? But many peoples believed afterwards, for the mystery worked through itself the truth of My coming from the Father, and which was fulfilled through My death and resurrection through the cross, for I had stayed with people on earth for thirty-three years and then I returned to the Father with all the victory fulfilled according to His plan.

Oh, I have come again now, I have still come secretly, for the Father revealed Me to a handful of souls and they carried the mystery of My coming a second time from the Father, and since then I have come as word on earth, I have come with the clouds and let Myself be heard, My word is gathered and I have been walking with it over the earth and coming out from under the mystery speaking My word over the Romanians and over the peoples to the margins, and from one margin to the other they are making My coming and My words heard and proclaimed, being ful-filled by their power.

Oh, My bride country, My queen among the nations, I am preparing you, My beloved one, and I am placing you in My presence dressed in holy garments, royal, to welcome your Bridegroom, the King, and to be the first, for you were chosen by the Father two thousand years ago to be Mine at the end of time, to be the Jerusalem of My return, the New Jerusalem, the one loved by God, the Christian one, the one renewed two thousand years ago through My disciples of that time, who became Christians after Christ, after My name, and became baptizing apostles for the Christianization of the New Israel. **(Zechariah: 2/10-132.)**

Oh, My country today, sit on your knees, sit before God kneeling, you, sons and Romanian Christians! I want to tell you the pain of the Father and of Mine and that of My saints and yours, sons. Oh, My country, you are a Christian country, you are Orthodox, but you trample on the feast days of My saints and yours and you bring mockery upon those in heaven. For almost a hundred years you have trampled on the holy days left to you by your saints and Mine, oh, and they are no longer with you on earth as the feasts of My saints are in heaven.

Oh, My today’s country, My Romania, you have always stood with your forehead bowed and beaten by the evildoers who have nibbled at you and torn you to pieces, but the sweet reward for your patience has come, for I, the Lord your God, have you whole before Me and I want you to dress yourself beautifully and holy again and to be My beauty again.

You, daughters of the Romanian nation, cast off your pagan attire and clothe yourselves in the ancestral glory of this chosen nation, for it is written in the Scriptures that there is a curse upon a woman dressed in men`s clothing, and this pagan and sick custom is an abomination in this nation. I want you to come to your senses and remember your customs and return to your sources, because I have a tiny people in the midst of this nation and they live according to the law of the holy Christians as it was until the people of the antichrist knew where to strike and strip you of your glory from Me and weaken you, My country, for behold, even now the antichrist is striking where he can still strike with his hidden weapons and destroy the time and power of the feasts of My saints.

Oh, Romanian sons, it is not the people of darkness today who are your fathers, but the saints are the ones who left you holy holidays and customs, and they are in heaven intercessors for you, for the country, for the nation, but behold, you do your works on holy holidays and that is why things are not going well for you. Oh, light candles in every house and put in them a piece of paper written with a prayer for you and for the country so that the work of the antichrist may be overthrown and My servants may come to the helm everywhere on earth where My Romanians are, and the fate of every people and nation may be placed under heavenly guard, oh, that there has come the time of victory through faith for God, for Christians, and everything through the cross, just as I, the Lord, overcame two thousand years ago.

Oh, if the feasts of the saints and prayer to heaven, the watch with fasting and crying out to God had not been held in their place throughout the year among My people prepared in this land, oh, even now My path and walk would not have been set free, but it was written for Me to work through the Romanian nation My work of the end of time and to cast aside the antichrist, who keeps saying: *"There is no God."*

Oh, step aside, you who sit on the thrones of rulers of countries and peoples, for you have done so much harm to yourselves and to those governed by you according to the godless plan, men of the darkness of this age! You, antichrists, surrender your weapons at the feet of the Lord, for you say that there is no God and that you are! Oh, there is God, and He is telling you now: come out of your hiding places, for My light is coming and revealing you without you expecting it. I am the light of the world and I bring you out of the darkness and I show you to the whole world so that it may be known that you are the darkness that covers the earth as it is written in the Scriptures about the rulers of this age. Oh, it can`t be like that anymore. The Lord is coming, the light is coming, the One about Whom you say that He is not is coming. However, I have power through the word, oh, and what are you going to do now when you will be exposed as liars who deceive nations and peoples? I have asked you, I have been asking you and I have been sending you a written word that you will no longer be able to stand against God and the people you have torn from the path of Christ. You have come with your idols and stripped the Romanians of their Christian shirt in order to weaken their holy power, for the shirt of the Romanian has great power and it stands against satan, because it is sewn with little crosses on the cloth and it clothes the Romanians in invincible power like their ancestors, and woe to those who have dared or would dare to plot evil against these bearers of Mine! I am giving peoples to destruction for My work with them on earth, and I will not let any enemy gnash on those blessed and protected by God at this time.

The sign from God is coming, the great trap is coming and it will lay low those who exalt themselves to overcome God. Their seats will shake and they will fall shamefully, for they are striking God and I will rise up to overcome them and to establish with holy walk the Christian nation, the Orthodox Christian law and the establishment of the feasts of the saints. I am doing justice to My saints, as it is written, for they keep crying out to Me in heaven: *"Lord, how long until You redeem our blood, of those who were killed for Your name?"* **(Apoc: 6/10)**

You, who seek to walk and change the work of God, oh, are you not ashamed? Well, if you stay hidden so that no one knows who you are and where you work from and send out only your commands over the earth, behold, it has been a long time and it is a little longer, and you will no longer do your work, for the time has come for God to put a stop to you and not allow you anymore. I am Christ, Whom you persecute even through the servants of the church, to whom I knocked to open for Me and to be received by them with the word of My mouth and they did not open for Me, for they are unbelievers. Oh, well, if that is how you are, those at the altars, whom are you trying to persecute? Who are you afraid of, why do you keep on closing so that I may not enter and work upon you? I will meet you soon, you will meet Me soon, we will look at each other and you will know the One Whom you persecute everywhere and put to death His loved ones, and I will tell you that you have taken My little house, My little garment and have cast Me aside and you rule, but you do not shepherd the flock, and behold it is scattered by the men of the antichrist, to whom you have opened wide, and behold the flock is bitten by the pagan beasts and strangers to God like you! But I have set a new beginning for My church, so that its trace may not perish from the earth, after the scorching heat of the antichrist has passed and is still passing everywhere to ruin My work with man everywhere.

I am making a new beginning and behold, it sprouts, as it is written, for I said in My testimony two thousand years ago, the one about Me, that it is a rock on which I build My church, and the gates of hell will not prevail against it, and therefore I keep it aside with all My secret and its, and I guard it from the wolves.

On the day of the birthday celebration, I am shouting from edge to edge with the word and am saying: Wake up, peoples! Do no longer kneel and be overcome by the counsel and work of the darkness of the antichrist man! I am the light of the world, and he is darkness. Kneel, there-fore, before God, not before pagan people with life and deed, the antichrists who are in darkness and over whom I am giving a holy command, with power in it and am saying: Let all of them who want to humble themselves come out into the light and repent, if they can!

I will shake off from all the earth the work that is contrary to God with all the people of darkness, and this fulfillment will be seen. This storm is coming unexpectedly and all the kings will wither, for I, the Lord, come, the King is coming, visiting His vineyard and shaking out unbelief from everywhere and He is making way for faith and its fulfillments, as it is written.

When I have established the first king over you now, at the end of time, My country, I took from the great lineage a scion, a good son for you and I brought him and he embraced your Christian law with his entire house then through time, through descendants, for I wanted him to be a symbol of kingdom over you, so that then I would give you at the end the kingdom of heaven, over which I am King from My Father. Therefore, gather much love in heaven, Romanian sons, for the love of God draws you to it, and the Father gives you the kingdom.

I paid with My life given on the cross to give you the kingdom of the Father, O My church, and the marks of the nails with which I was nailed to the cross remained on My body as a testimony, and the wounds from the nails have not closed. Satan will see this and will be terrified for such a grave sin worked for two thousand years against the One placed on the cross and nailed to it, for this sin will humble the hearts of all My adversaries for two thousand years, even if they will not be able to bow down and receive their forgiveness.

It is a birthday celebration, for the sons of the citadel of My word, and now let us make room for the carols and the holy feast table, because we have caroling guests, and I am then going further with the word and with My Virgin mother in the work of the word, for I and she are celebrated on this day by those in heaven and by those on earth who stand as if in heaven in the celebrations, and through this obedience, blessings, grace and holy protection come to them on earth, great light in a time of such great darkness and so many people without God on earth.

Peace to you now, to those who receive carolers, and peace to you, to those who are singing carols at My manger of word! I am giving you the blessing of the festive table, sons, and may My word give you holy powers and comforts, and I am asking you, sons, be worthy of these holy gifts that have come from heaven to you, as a testimony that I am and that I leave My word upon you. Amen, amen, amen.
