[legatura_la_teme id_capitol="7" id_subcapitol="3"]Heaven is My throne and I sat down on the earth spread out on which to lean with My coming from the Father to men and to speak to them and to sit with those from heaven on earth at the table of the word, for it is written in the Scriptures about My coming with the saints, and it was impossible for My coming word not to be fulfilled for the believers and for the unbelievers.

Oh, I have not found faith on earth for My coming, but I had to come if it is written so that I may fulfill it, and I had to prepare a place and God loving soul so that I might put faith in them that the Lord is coming and that this Scripture may then be fulfilled, this miracle awaited for centuries by all the righteous and saints who lived in the body on earth alongside God's work through time among men, and behold, their joy has been fulfilled, and their expectation has ended, for I, the Lord, am coming with them to men, I am coming to earth as the word of creation of the world, and those who claim to be knowledgeable of the Scriptures say that there is still a long way to go for the coming of Christ to be fulfilled, but behold, I have been speaking over the earth for seventy years and am coming with a table of words and with My saints to the hearth of the Romanian nation, on which I have My tent of lodging now, at the end of time.

There is no faith on earth for My coming, but I am coming and I am still coming, and everything is ready to be shown to the amazement of the unbelievers who say they know everything, and so that no one may believe that the resurrected Christ is coming, and that He is the One Who shares His word over the earth far and wide, and the prophecy is fulfilled which says that the Word of God **(Apoc: 19/13)** is coming, that the Lord is coming with His saints, **(Matt: 25/31; Mark: 8/38; 1 Thess: 3/13)** all carried on white horses, on the angels of God.

Rise up, sons of the gates, *(Gates - those by whom the Lord speaks, the Lord comes as word, r.n.)* so that I may enter into you the word, and in the book, oh sons, and you may place Me on the way and so that My coming may be fulfilled like the lightning that comes out of the east and travels to the west, **(Matt: 24/25-27.)** for I, the Lord, speak to you, and My word is heard in the west in an instant (Through the internet, r.n.) and bears witness that the Lord is coming fulfilling His coming, His second coming from the Father to men. [/legatura_la_teme]

I am coming with the clouds, **(Apoc: 1/7)** oh, sons, I am coming to you so that you may sow the seed of Christ the Sower over the earth, for here and there, there is still land for My seed and faith, that I am the Word, that I am the Shepherd and that I am coming to visit My flock and its shepherds, but they are telling everyone not to come near to listen, because they know that he is not the Shepherd, that the Lord is not coming. Oh, well, how can they say otherwise, poor things, if they are made to say so, if this is what the master whom they serve on their knees asks them to do? Here they are, they were not My faithful servants, but they please the antichrist even at the cost of their lives and the flock, for this is the time, a time of great error if the shepherds are at the hands of the unbelievers and antichrists through unbelief.

I have always advised those who found Me come as a word on earth and I have always told them: *“Get out of the world, My people, so that its master does not ask you to drink of its wine! Get out, flee to the mountains”*, that is what I told them. *“Flee from the world”*, I have always told them, for in the world there is poison for those who are with God on earth, to drag them also into all kinds of debauchery and into greed for wealth and money, oh, and does man not need these, but does he really believe?

I have with Me among the heavenly ones with whom I walk with, I have in this day that daughter who came out of the world and united with the angelic host, after the world had corrupted her completely, after she had corrupted many in the world with her longing for a debauched life, and then she was called to holiness and she came out of the world and became holy, walking on top of the water from one bank to the other, and when she was speaking to the Lord during prayer, her body rose above the ground and these signs testified to her departure from the world, from the corruption on earth, from which no one escapes by remaining with the children of the world.

[legatura_la_teme id_capitol="6" id_subcapitol="3"]Oh, what great miracles God does through those who come out of the world and give Me all their love! There is no happier, shinier man than the one who loves God with his heart and with the deed of his holiness, oh, sons. Behold, there are no happy people on earth, for they do not know the secret of their happiness. Neither the rich nor the poor are happy, no one is happy if he does not live with God on earth. Behold, those from My Romania, they go to distant countries to earn money. Oh, what should you do with money, man? You do not need money, but you do not want to believe this even if God says so. Oh, do not pursue money, for you do not know what to do with it so that it does not become your sins and the loss of your soul. You cannot acquire your eternal life with God with money. Oh, you cannot cheat your life on earth with money to have it. Here's what I teach you: run from greed, run from mammon, run as fast as you can, because this devil, mammon, has a lot of glue and stays on your hands and soul no matter how much you wash.[/legatura_la_teme]

All those who sanctified themselves for their eternal happiness have renounced their wealth as satan and given themselves to heaven on earth and have come to Me cleansed of mammon, and this was also done by Mary of Egypt, the young woman who loved the lust of debauchery since she was a little child, but immediately at the sign from heaven that came her way she allowed herself to be captivated by the love of God and gave herself to the Lord with all her love, which she changed into holy powers, she left the world and the earth inhabited by sinful people and gave herself to God and His angels and the angelic life before the Lord. This being resurrected from the dead of the sinful world had nothing left of the world, and she then had the service of angels with her and love entirely for God alone, for the forgiveness of her grave sins, and she was forgiven, and I sent to her a priest of God to give her the nourishment of eternal life, and all these have testified to everyone, to this day, the miracle of God, the faith that works incomparable miracles.

[legatura_la_teme id_capitol="6" id_subcapitol="3"]I taught all those who took the word of eternal life from My table here and I always told them: *“Sons, sons, do not wear what the world wears, do not use what the world uses, do not seek to resemble the world, oh sons, so that God can know you from the children of the world, for the face of the world is ugly, it is black with sin, it is poisoned with mixture and it defiles you as much as you do not expect the ugly, it separates you from God little by little without expecting it”*, and that is why you need faith in My word and you need obedience to be happy, because otherwise the fruits of faith do not sprout, but, on the contrary, thorns sprout that smell bad and cause wounds, of sins that bring wounds, and it is not in vain that I left written in My Gospel, the parable of the rich young man who took care of his behavior and steps according to the laws of God, but had mammon as a companion, had the wealth and money under which the powerful rule the world and its people, and when I told him that he must give from himself what he confessed with mammon beside him, he became sad, left and chose to be free from his walk with God and I had to send him escape from this devil, and only then give him the right to walk on the narrow path with the steps of his life.

Oh, sons, eternal life is acquired through the forgiveness of sins and through the awareness of unworthiness, that no man is worthy of God, but through the Lord's forgiveness you then remain His son, only that forgiveness must not be stolen, you cannot pay for it with anything, and it must be acquired through the work of a pure heart, through self-denial and through remorse for disorder against the spirit of life from above over man, and behold, forgiveness is acquired only when the old man changes into the new man, and which no longer allows itself to be torn down.

I am speaking to the children who are faithful to My word in this time and I am exhorting them to repent and to realize their unworthiness, and so they may stand before Me and become partakers of Christ, for the Lord is loved by those with a cross, for He was under the cross, and He could not otherwise be before the Father as the Son of the Father. Those who have everything and still have it, do not love Him, no longer love the Lord, and it is understandable why, for it is written: *«Where your treasure is, there your heart will be also»*, **(Matth: 6/21)**, and happiness does not come on earth, for it dwells in heaven for those who desire it for themselves.

Therefore, heavenly speech is needed and too little earthly speech, and heavenly work is needed and too little earthly and bodily work, and all must be built in heaven, because otherwise nothing remains of them and they are lost together with man and all his earthly accumulation, and for which man labors far too much, losing God little by little, because the care for life with God is different, it is something else, oh, sons.

I have urged you to the love that remains, I have taught you for it, those who take from the spring the word of My mouth for those who believe in My coming. Therefore, children, prepare yourselves for your beauty in Christ and hurry towards it, so that the Lord may see that you love Him, that you want with Him, as Mary of Egypt wanted, and who, today gives Me alone to speak, and she to listen to My word for her feast.

Oh, My children, reject as much as you can the care for the body, for behold, this care takes you too far from God and secretly deceives you, as if you need much, you need money and you have work, but the work that is only for you does not unite you with God. Oh, you do not need money. You need God, for behold, you have money, but you no longer have the Lord.

Oh, children, I take care of those who give themselves to Me with love and with their deeds, because for the love of God you need only love, and I, the Lord, am waiting for you, I am still waiting for you to see that you choose the pearl of great price. I look at you a long time, I look after you a long time and I am waiting for you to understand My speech to you as Mary of Egypt understood the heavenly, angelic sign, which stopped her from the life of debauchery and gave her the holy thought to go out of the world, from among the children of the world and to choose heaven while still on earth, and she needed nothing more than the power of love and the ministering angels, who carried her and accompanied her from the Lord, Whom she loved so much afterwards.[/legatura_la_teme]

Oh, there is one more step, and I will sing the song of the resurrection with you, My children, and we will say to each other: Christ is risen! Oh, rejoice with the arrival of the feast of My resurrection and give yourselves to Me, you who take the Lord word over you. Oh, do not keep Me out of you when you hear Me knocking to enter. I am the Bread that was broken for you, you who know what My resurrection and yours is. I want you to understand My call, for I said two thousand years ago: *«Whoever wants to come after Me, let him deny himself and take up his cross and follow Me»*, **(Matth: 16/24-26)**, but following Me, for I see how much difficulty the Christian has in understanding this call of Mine for him.

We have one more holy feast, the feast of My entry into the city of Jerusalem on a donkey two thousand years ago, and then step into the week of remembrance of My passions and cross, and then we sing: Christ has risen!

Oh, sons, I watch to see your steps and to understand from you your love for Me, for I love you and caress you with tenderness, and I want you to deserve My love, so that it may be well with you, oh, sons. Therefore, remain in My love! I am your Shepherd, and I have for you the love of a Shepherd.

I have spoken My word to you and I want you to gather in it, My children. Peace to you! Self-denial, only it can help you to love God and follow Him in all truth, and always, and peace to you, peace to you, peace to you, oh children! Amen, amen, amen.
