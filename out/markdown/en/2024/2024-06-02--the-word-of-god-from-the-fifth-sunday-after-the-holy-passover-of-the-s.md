I am leaving My word over the garden and we put heavenly food on the table and I am sitting with the saints and with you at the table, the sons with whom I carry My work of coming to earth in My time with you, and on this Sunday, I am accompanied by the witness of My coming from the Father two thousand years ago, the Samaritan woman whom I met at the well of Israel, for while I was spoking to her at the well about the things above for man on earth, she recognized Me as the Christ prophesied to come, her heart was filled with longing and love, she then followed Me with the steps of her life and drew those in her house and many in the city to God, and she then proclaimed Me on earth that I came from the Father, that I shared raising and forgiveness, the gift of faith, signs and wonders, oh, and the faith in My coming from the Father grew in many, and those at the head of the people could not bend down to believe, oh, those who will be great on earth could not and cannot do this, and I, the Lord, revealed Myself to those who did not ask about Me and gave them joy, peace, life and the power to become children of God, and the children of God are those who testify of God on earth, and behold My celebration with her, with this confessing female disciple, and with you here, in the garden at the fountain, sons, and peace to you with those in heaven at the table, for this disciple has the name of the angel of the fountain of the meeting, she has a place in the garden here, she has work from heaven, and My work with you has support and saints of help from heaven for you, oh, sons!

[legatura_la_teme id_capitol="3" id_subcapitol="3"]Therefore, for all those who are among the people of My word in this time, I, the Lord, am making the holy exhortation and saying: Oh, sons, love heaven, love it and place it on earth, work on it, guard it with you and in you, and be inhabitants of heaven on earth, for man fell from heaven, he fell from heaven seven thousand years ago and even better, for he chose not to listen in heaven and fell in disobedience to God the Creator.

Oh, love heaven, sons, for the world is hell, and heaven is the paradise that must be established and worked on earth in plain sight, and let the devil fear seeing this holy fulfillment now, at the end of time, when he believes himself victorious over men and over God! However, you pass from body to spirit, from limitation to infinity, and you will comfort heaven and the Maker of all, for I told the Samaritan woman at the well then, I told her that true worshippers will work with spirit and truth and with the power of faith, not with the erection of adorned places for worship as people know how to do, but with the establishment in holiness of people, who need God with them, not houses of prayer like those of two thousand years ago, but as I was working in spirit and truth, that I was walking with My own on paths, on meadows, on the banks of waters, through gardens full of flowers and trees, so that the troubled, the disregarded, the helpless, those who believe in God working on earth, not in heaven and that is all, and behold, blessed are those who believe the word of God and put it into practice; this is what I sad, sons.

Now, I am teaching you heavenly and am telling you: awaken and expand in you the work of My angel within you, for man sleeps and does not wake up from this sleep, oh, and it becomes difficult for God, for conscience means God working in man for the life of the soul, it means the angel of the Lord in the heart and mind of man, oh, sons. Therefore, here, engage in talking much and more with God, with Me, sons, so that we may love one another, so that we may desire one another, so that we may not lose each other, sons. Oh, talk to God, talk beautifully! How beautiful it is to do this, oh, sons! As much of the kingdom of heaven lives and works in man on earth, so much will he have a share of it in the kingdom of eternity, when I, the Lord, will come with it for those who have carried it in them and with them on earth.

Let the Spirit of Christ be your spirit, My sons. Do not hold on to yourselves, so I have advised you, and you will understand by working in this way the Spirit of Christ working in you, and from you to you aside, and it will be as in heaven with you and among you. And now let us understand well this work nourished from heaven in man, for King David spoke much, much with God, and had enough time to do this more than all that he worked among the people he cared for, and his speaking with Me always had as its source the fear of God, My sons. The fear of the Lord makes man speak with God like David, like the prophets, like all the great praying people, who feared without God all the time with them, and behold, by working you rest your soul's peace, and by not working you have no peace, only this holy mystery with man on earth, with the Lord on earth, My sons, must be understood well.

The mind of man always, always errs, because of its limitations and its body, for man is for himself when he chooses for himself, oh, and that is why I, the Lord, have often advised and asked you to pass from the body to the spirit as true worshippers of Christ, to pass from limitation to infinity, sons, for the spirit is not with measure or with a border, but is infinity, and this is what man needs, infinity, sons, only that man is much too learned with his mind and with its fleeting little, and his mind is very small and keeps its own part, the poor part of it, and man loses God for it.[/legatura_la_teme]

Everything that I am telling you today in remembrance of the words that I spoke to the Samaritan woman at the well of Israel, oh, so must one understand who wants to worship the Lord in spirit and truth, not in specific places, not in Jerusalem, rather in spirit and truth, as the Father wants His true worshipers to understand and be, and I come and teach you through My source of word, oh, sons.

[legatura_la_teme id_capitol="6" id_subcapitol="1"]All who wish to become true worshipers of God, here is a lesson for this holy miracle: Separate yourselves, children, as completely as possible from worldly life, from what resembles the life and occupations of the children of the world. Oh, do not take from them to do what they do. Be afraid of such separation from God. Flee, flee in fear from the likeness of the world, from being in the world, from the customs of the children of the world, for man needs God and the things left from heaven upon him and with him, and man absolutely does not need the things brought out by the children of the world on earth, for behold, the devil of today has placed before and in the minds of Christians the spirit of the world and the wandering school of heavenly time with man.

Woe, and woe again, children, oh, that My Spirit and His heart are broken when I look and see My Christian resembling the world and the children of the world, that he takes from them, that he cannot do otherwise, and I, the Lord, send punishments and damages, because of the pain that ascends to Me through the evil angels, with what the Christians who know My coming word on earth, the food from heaven, the teachings for the Christian and angelic life do.

Oh, Christian divided in two, oh, son, the more you want not to make yourself known to whom you belong with the service with which you learn from the children of the world, the more you will lose yourself, the more you will separate yourself from the Lord. The more you try to take and resemble the world and its children, the more you will be pulled to the left and the wandering from God, and the soul will seize you, for the love of God will no longer be your work, you will no longer pull with heaven for the truth, you will no longer seek the light without evening that will carry your steps of life to the heights of victory that is soon coming on earth over those worthy of it through love from above, through the life of heaven on earth.[/legatura_la_teme]

Behold, man sleeps both day and night, he sleeps all his time on earth, his conscience, the angel of the Lord in man, sleeps. There are no longer any shepherds of Christians to wake them up, gather their conscience and see the step in vain, a step unguarded by the angel of conscience that sleeps in man.

May God arise and may His enemies, the world and its spirit, be scattered! May all Christians come to their senses, those who have known and know the word of My coming, with which I am giving to gather a people to My bosom to protect them from the spirit of the world and to set them aside for My glory, which awaits at the border!

[legatura_la_teme id_capitol="3" id_subcapitol="4"]I was sending the healed ones, I was sending them to the priests so that they too could see, understand and believe in Christ, the wonderful One in His saints. I did not send them so that their faith would be destroyed by the priests, but in such a way that the works of God would be confessed and that there would be someone to recognize them, believe in them and in God afterwards. However, now the darkness is thicker than at that time, and the priests are set to destroy faith in people and pretend to be smarter than God, and no one gives a man his hand to get up or not to fall, because the priest wants the man to listen to him and tells the man what to do or what not to do and believes himself to be so knowledgeable that God no longer has the right to work anything on earth, because the priest does not give him permission. Oh, well, if you do that, then you are something else if you are so arrogant, that the Lord is no longer allowed to bring His works down to earth among people if the priest says: no!

So, I Myself come with this word, and the priest does not know and does not believe My work. He speaks from himself and resists the man from coming to the voice of My word and says: *“Do not go, for the devil is there!”* Oh, well, you are with God, if you tell the man that the devil is there? Do you know the devil so well, if you tell the man to flee from the devil? But how can you not know the Lord, because behold, you have no idea about God, even if you stand in the priest's robe before the man, for you are something else, and behold, you deceive the man with your robe, with your standing at the altar, and you show yourself big and so strong.

I came Myself, I came from heaven two thousand years ago to speak on earth with man, because priests, like those of today, did not give the Lord to men. I spoke Myself with the Samaritan woman at the well and My speech remained with her, and here is the time prophesied to her by Me then for the true worshipers, and I have come from heaven with this time now, because there is no other way for God to reach men, as long as the priests separate men from God to bring them closer to themselves, not to God, and man no longer knows the way back to heaven, to a life of heaven on earth.[/legatura_la_teme]

I gave the Samaritan woman to drink, as I am giving it to you today, the sons who believe in My coming after man, so that I may teach him in a time of unteaching, for on earth I no longer have servants for man towards Me, and My disciple from Samaria now is leaving her holy exhortation with Me for those who love God in this time of great dryness of faith, and she is saying this:

— Drink the water that flows from heaven, and that awakens God in man! Drink this water, you who hear the coming of the Lord in word on earth here, at the fountain of heaven for you! Drink and confess, so that it may be said that you drink and believe! How to confess? By the fulfillment of the word that comes with the clouds to people on earth, and the fulfillment will be seen in all its beauty in you, many will be drawn to this paradise in man, they will also desire paradise, escape from transience while still on earth, then the paradise comes, seen and understood by more and more man, for the Father desires and expects such worshipers to appear from place to place in sight and many will then be healed, they will see the way, the life and the love from above, which comes to earth in word.

So, Lord, I am confessing now, and my story with You and for You then, this is my book of instruction for those who want me to help them with You.

And now, I am lowering my spiritual eyes and looking at my celebration as Your angel in Your citadel today, and I am comforting myself by looking here, and I thank You in this moment of comfort to me, oh, my good Lord.

— Sons, sons, I have placed great and strong exhortations in the book for the strong and for the weak, so that they may become strong and good seed to sow, and the good seed must be protected by God, for so it is written.

I call upon the heavenly powers to help My descent into the book with the word, whenever I find weakness on the way, and you, My sons, ask Me for strength so that you may be able to work, ask, and it will be given to you, for so I have promised to do and fulfill to those who ask Me.

And now, I am leaving upon you the blessing and the beginning of preparation for the feast of Pentecost, and power over you, so that you may be able to work for Me, and I am now giving you My comfort and saying to you with all My grace: peace to you, peace to you, peace to you, oh, sons!
