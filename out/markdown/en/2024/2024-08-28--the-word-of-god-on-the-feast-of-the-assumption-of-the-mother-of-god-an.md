This word is God, I am, the Word of the Father, the Word Who made heaven and earth and man, the visible and invisible things of God.

I am this word, the word that spoke to man in heaven, to Moses on the mountain, to Abraham, to Isaac, to Jacob, to David, to the prophets, to My disciples of two thousand years ago, and to all those who had the gift of hearing the word from God.

I am this word today, and until the end of time, for the work given to Me by the Father is the word and its fulfillment afterwards, without one jot of it passing away, but, on the contrary, to be fulfilled, as it is written.

And now, the book of My word from this time is opened and I am placing in the book the feast of the queen of heaven and earth, My Virgin mother, through whom I had come from the Father to earth as a baby, and I grew up a perfect man and then I fulfilled the work given to Me by the Father, My condemnation to death on the cross for the salvation of man who would know Me by the true God, Who came from God to earth, and whoever knew the Scriptures received power to believe in them and become a son of God. Since then, I, the Lord, have been shepherding and secretly building the kingdom of God on earth with people and her sons, those to whom the Father gave the gift of faith, with which they receive the Lord, their Maker.

Peace to you, oh, peace to you on this holy holiday, sons of the citadel of My word on the land of the Romanian nation! When I come to you word in the citadel, I announce Who I am. When I come to you as word, I come in the name of the Father, of the Son and of the Holy Spirit, and I place My word among you, and you write it in My book with you, for I speak so that you may hear Me and write it, sons.

Why am I setting forth this explanation of the work of My word and of those to whom I, the Lord, entrusting My speech of this time? Oh, as dangerous as it is for many reasons for you to be given special honor by those who believe My word today, it is just as dangerous for those who know you to be Mine to despise you, to defame you, to suspect you, to accuse you, to do so to you. I, the Lord, then immediately sit between you and them, so that I may receive everything they throw at you, for you are the path of My word coming to earth, as I have prepared you and placed you to stand and receive Me, and if you were to do their will, so that they would have you on their side, oh, you could no longer be My path from heaven to earth and I would be without support to come to place My word on earth, and then its fulfillment.

[legatura_la_teme id_capitol="3" id_subcapitol="4"]Let us sit and work on the royal feast day for My Virgin mother, and let us, My mother, set a table of word and remembrance of the works of God on earth, after I had come from Father, Son two thousand years ago. You have a feast with Romanian communion on the day of remembrance of your coming to Me in heaven, after you finished your walk among men, for on this same day of the year, the lord of the land, Brâncoveanu, his sons and his advisor, all martyred for the faith from ancestors, ascended to heaven, oh, and you rejoice for them, for their love of God, My mother.

Oh, do you hear, you who sit in the name of faith in God on shepherds' chairs? Do you hear the confession of faith of the Lord of the country? He and his sons laid their heads to be slaughtered and did not deny their faith from their ancestors. You did not let the sign of the denial of faith be known and beware of betraying your ancestors and God. This sign was made known through the Scriptures two thousand years ago and behold, it is written that after this sign the antichrist will work, and the adversary from darkness will be revealed. Oh, you have not watched, and you do not even try to watch. You will have no joy, but only suffering from this enemy now hidden.

This is why I, the Lord, am coming as word to earth with the hardest blow ever inflicted on My church by this still lurking enemy! I come word to earth and I keep coming, and the breath of My mouth keeps cutting off his path as it is written, and he and all his antichrists servants will be destroyed by My word, and you will then bear the heaviest shame for your denial of faith, for the submission you show before your hidden enemy, but I am still asking you, I am still asking you to rise up with the power of My cross and call the heavenly powers to battle, so that you may overcome the antichrist, not him to overcome you, not him, do you hear?

I, the Lord, am still calling you, and I have been calling you for a long time, rise up like David and cut off the power of the adversary spirit and bring My flock out of destruction and you will be written in heaven like Brâncoveanu, as the conquerors of the beast. I, with the word, make victory whenever victory is needed. Overcome you, too, through My word. Only let Me see that you listen, and I will give the hidden enemy into your hand. Dare and do not forget, I have overcome! Overcome you, too! Amen.

— Oh, Son Jesus, the frightening signs are coming, which no one can withstand, no enemy, dear Son. It is difficult, we know, but You are the Conqueror, clothed in the Father, Who gave You all power in heaven and on earth. However, You also want Christians to be victorious, and that is why You come and urge them to be vigilant, to fight, because the enemy is merciless.

You, children of faith, rely on the Lord and dare against unbelief with Christ in front! Come to the great battle! Wake up, Romanian Christians, and all Christians to see the cloud and overcome with fast and prayer, as it is written, and you will be victorious. This is what I am choosing to degree for you on my holy feast day.

Thus, Jesus, Lord, thus may the victory of true Christians on earth be accomplished. Amen.

[/legatura_la_teme][legatura_la_teme id_capitol="6" id_subcapitol="3"]— Oh, My mother, this is what I desire and expect: let the Christian be true, because the name of Christian has no power like the life of a Christian. Behold, I have told the people of My word not to stand without the garment of a Christian, without the image given by God. The Christian man must look to Me, not to the sinful world. The Christian must wear a beard and wear a Christian clothing, not of the world, and food like in paradise, not like the world that eats meat and all things on earth, which are not for the Christians of Christ, and the ordinance of fasting and prayer must be observed, so that in the difficult time the Christian may have Me on his side and I may know him and protect him as My child, because if I find him in the world or resembling the world, I leave him to the scourges that come for unbelief, because there is no sin more serious than unbelief, than the denial of faith, My mother.

I have sheltered My people, prepared by My word. I always teach them to remain obedient to My counsel, for I have set them aside to have a new seed for the new birth of the world, as in the time of Noah, a new creation, My mother.

Oh, children of My word, this is how I keep you, because today's world has chosen the loss of its children, separation from God and power on the side of the antichrist, who comes to take its fruit and then lose it with it, as it is written.

Oh, sons, My word must be desired by you as the saints and angels in heaven desire it, and you must love it and build yourselves up through it, sons. Love Me, Christian son, and do not be cold and indifferent to My voice above you, for I come from the Father to gather you as a hen gathers her chicks to warm them and teach them to eat and how to eat, because if you do not stay under My word, under My mantle, you will learn to walk and get lost from under My wings and no longer be pleased with the love with which I come to give you and learn from God to love, Christian son. Those who have loved Me and love Me in this word are those who feed on it and share it with their brothers, for otherwise there is no love for My word.

Oh, if I had not come to earth as a teaching word, could I still raise a people to faith and action? How could this have been possible if I had not given shepherding to those who came to take Me from this spring and to believe Me to come to earth as a Shepherd and fulfillment of the heavenly kingdom through the faith of those who are given the gift to believe?

Oh, sons, do not seek without a cross, do not seek comforts, positions, praises, joys, bows and worship from God and your fellow men. Seek your creation if you approach, for behold, many have not received it, have not sought it and have remained completely dwarfed by the spirit of self, the spirit of pride that makes you dissatisfied with God and your brothers. The creation of man is a work that never ends. It works on man with every step of his life, if the one who comes to God, the Maker of man, reconciles himself with it.

The Christian does not know how to stay at his making, he does not like making, he walks around and takes care of the sins of others and that is it. He who takes the sins of his neighbor upon his tongue to carry them around, does so because he is so sinful that he can no longer consider his life as deviated and lost from the path of life. He who is full of sins no longer takes care of his sins, and he has something to do with those of his neighbor, because that is what his mind, devoid of humility and truth, demands. Moses' brothers, Aaron and Miriam, incited the people against Moses and prevailed, but that people perished, because I, the Lord, was working through Moses, not through his brothers, who accused him, raising the people of Israel against him. Oh, so do those of today, who believe themselves to be part of the people of My word, but I always strengthen those through whom I work to bring the word to the earth, because from the sides they are attacked with cunning for discontent, because that is how it is on earth, the man who does not have self-denial, which makes him beautiful like God, that man is discontented, is arrogant, and behold, he is hated by God, he who is full of his own mind.

I am asking you on this day of fatherly counsel over yourselves, sons who take from My fountain of word, oh, stop working at double standards, both with those who blaspheme after the departure of the brothers, and with those who have remained faithful near the fountain. Oh, do not bring shame upon yourselves. Everything is revealed, everything, because this is how God reveals everything. Let no one pull you up or down, but be good, do not raise a word of judgment for the grievances in your minds and borrowed from each other. I have been waiting for this moment, so that I can speak to you and advise you for the things you, some of you, have done badly. Oh, why do you talk among your-selves about the work of My word? Do you have doubts? It is nothing if you choose to, but why do you share and divide your minds around to weaken helpless souls? You play hide and seek to throw poison into the parts. You are so wrong, so hated because of the lack of mind in heaven, because of murmuring, gossip, unbelief, lightness. You take it upon yourself to hurt the walk towards the Lord of the weak and helpless in nature and mind and without steadfastness for holiness. You are to be mourned. You have mixed with what is not for you. Woe to you! There is someone who has worked thus on the work of My word, and is now among the mentally ill gathered together, and I am telling you this to advise you to soundness of mind, for this word is God, oh, children.

It is a day of repentance and holy counsel. I am walking by the stature of those who taking from My source of word, for some of them are sending Me pains. I am also looking over those who have withdrawn, for behold, they are not remaining obedient so as not to be harmed, but, on the contrary, they are seeking to shake those with faith and who are seeking to be able to continue on the difficult path of My cross, for woe to those without a cross!

You who have departed from the longing for pleasures, oh, do not play with the words of your mouth. I see that you do not fear, but I, the Lord, am saying to you: fear God! You have lost your beautiful mind, because I have taken away from you the gift of faith, for this gift is possessed only by those who have self-denial, and you do not have it, because you have self-love and do not love God because of this.

You gossip about the mystery of My coming on earth and you play with fire and you do not know what you are doing. Oh, how terrible you pretend to be! You should look at yourselves as you have become after you have given Me out of your life, and how ugly you are now without My Being in you, without My word, which you are now blaspheming as a lie.

The pleasures of the flesh are falls from grace, they are separation from God, they are the shameful payment for the sin of pride, for whoever has pride in his mind is blind to self-reproach, blind to the humility punished in man for the sin of pride in his mind. Unbelief completely kills My life in the mind and in the deeds of man, and this is no longer a Christian who plays with the fall from grace. Your beauty has deformed you; your weaknesses and dissatisfactions have brought you down, with which you keep covering your concealments and cunning, which you need so much now and always. Behold, the time is coming to glorify Me greatly through the sons of My people who work for Me among men, and you are no longer there, and you are with the sons of the world and you have lost your soul’s care like the sons of the world, who, having nothing else to do, go to the gym and torture their bodies according to satan's desire, and all hell laughs at those who are lost and without holy work on earth. Oh, what will I do with these, what will become of them?

I ease My pain when I tell you that it hurts Me and what hurts Me. I tell you, those who know My pain, and where it comes from, and I tell those who hurt Me, to have mercy on them, I tell them.

It is a day of sweet celebration and I am standing with the heavenly powers in amazement beside you and holding you under My mantle, the sons of My citadel with you. I am asking you now to go forward with the holy work over the gathered people, because I would like, sons, I would like those, who want to join Me, to know that the work of creating man is what must know those who approach My mystery with you, the source of My Shepherd's word from heaven.[/legatura_la_teme]

We are setting a memorial table and our guests of the day are the martyrs for the faith, Voivode Brâncoveanu and his sons, an example that the entire Orthodox nation should follow and overcome the enemy of the holy faith through unity.

My Mother is bowing down and holding you close to give her spirit to you, those who are sitting at her feast table today.

Oh, sons, accept My shepherding over you. I would like to be able to forgive you for all that you have done wrong to My word, only it will take a complete understanding of what you have done wrong, and a humble bowing. And those who have made too many mistakes, oh, let them not speak, but let them listen to My word and My will and My decision face to face with their mistakes, oh, for it is hard for Me on earth with those who make mistakes without correction, sons.

Now, blessed be the memorial, the celebration table and the joy, even if the joy in Us and in you is hurt, for such is the time now.

I want, My sons, that My today's teachings for you may penetrate you, and I may always prepare teachings for you, because much, much work is needed for Christians to be sons of this work of the word.

And you, those who place My word in the book, beware of the blows, of the spirit in man, of the troubles that try to bring you down, and stand under My protection, for only I know your pain, and only you know Mine, only we, only I and you, oh, children. Amen, amen, amen.
