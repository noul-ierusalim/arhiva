I am He Who am. Heaven is My throne, and the earth, My footstool. In the name of the Father and of the Son and of the Holy Spirit, Father Sabaoth sends Me as word on earth, accompanied by heavenly hosts, and from My mouth comes a sharp sword for the enemies, and those who cry out to heaven, I shepherd with the word, and My name is called the Word of God **(Apoc: 19/13).**

Light and darkness fight, truth and lie, faith and unbelief, and that is why I come to earth as a word, and as long as I am in the world, I am the light of the world, and I work everywhere, and the unbelievers do not see, but those who believe in My coming see the fulfillments of My word, which makes light, once spoken to be written on earth, and then shared.

Once again, I make My voice heard over the earth in this time, as in the time of Moses in Sinai to guide from heaven those in captivity, and Moses spoke with Me, carried My word to the people and I was the Shepherd of that people.

I am above with heavenly hosts and We are carried on clouds by cherubim and seraphim. I have My twelve disciples as companions, because it is a feast day today for My first called to apostleship at that time, the apostle Andrew, and two thousand years ago, he placed the baptismal shirt within Christ over the Romanian nation, a nation that now, at the end of time, is My call to welcome Me on its hearth, so that I may come with this word on earth, and it is seventy years since I have been writing on this land the book of My coming from this time, My word on earth, so that I may shepherd with it the nations and their kings and fulfill what is written for this time.

Oh, Romanian nation, peace to you! I work from above you; I am working more than in all times before, for the resurrection of the dead is rapidly approaching, the raising of the saints, who always ask Me: *«Lord, until when?»*, **(Apoc: 6/10)** and the Christian nation has been promising this faith for two thousand years and always says: *"I am awaiting the resurrection of the dead and the life of the age to come"*, and these fulfillments are rapidly approaching, together with the power of My word, for two thousand years ago I said: *«All power in heaven and on earth has been given to Me»*, and behold, I am coming with the power of word, for so it is written. **(See Matth: 28/18-19)**

Oh, Romanian people, oh, My country of today, the cunning spirit of the enemy antichrist is storming towards you, and he does not know what awaits him, *(Reference to the political turmoil from the presidential and parliamentary elections in Romania at this time, the presidential elections being annulled by the Constitutional Court, two days before the second round due to suspicions of their influence by Russia and China through the Tic-Toc network, in favor of the "nationalist-sovereigntist" candidate Călin Georgescu, and who had a 60-70% chance over her rival, because no candidate from the major parties in government made it past the first round due to incompetence and, especially, arrogance and hypocrisy, r.n.)* but I have always spoken a word over him and said that he and all his hidden army are crumbling in My holy mountain, for I am the Lamb of the Father, and it is written that I will come to fight with this beast wrapped in lamb's fur and to overthrow it with the breath of My mouth, with the word that comes out of My mouth like a sharp sword for this enemy, who fears My name and tries to come first under this name; however, I, the Lord, go after him and frustrate his hidden plans and that is why I have come as a word on earth, and I come once with the work of the antichrist, and I come to overcome him, for he is a liar and a blasphemer, when he wants to work in My name.

I am speaking now to you, to those who still stand tall at the helm of the Romanian nation, My chosen nation at the end of time. I am looking down from above, day and night. I am looking down on you with sorrow, because you still want to be the great ones, you, the ones who have been until now. Oh, do you not want to open your eyes? Look at yourselves, and at each other. It is not seen that you do not love your country and your nation, from the seats of protection over this nation, it is not seen on your faces, in your behavior and work. It is only seen that you hold on to your seats so as not to lose them.

You have disappointed this people so much, who were ready, ready to let themselves be deceived by the enemy who appeared with the voice of a savior. Oh, you still do not want to look among you for those who are able to bring a new hope and to wisely steer My ship, the land of My word today. You keep those who can do better behind, because you stand in front as if you are the leaders. Oh, not like that! Receive My advice as a Shepherd, My sons, because I am coming to shepherd you, because I see that you are not taking into consideration the danger that has arisen ready to lay the Romanian people to the ground.

Sons, sons, be careful not to make mistakes again and again. If you do not watch, I and My angels are going to work and watch for the fate of this nation, threatened under the enemy's arrow now. This is what is now in the country because of the distrust that has fallen upon this people, led for such a long time only by those who have kept the bravest behind for the watch chairs.

Oh, sons, oh sons, not according to the eyes, not according to desires, but according to his understanding, the ruler should be placed over the people. I advised you a few days ago to gather for watchfulness and to submit yourselves to the spirit of wisdom, through humility and faith that God is your God, only do not think of yourselves more than of the good that would be to be placed according to the truth over this nation.

Who should you choose, you keep asking yourself? Oh, he must know how to speak in the languages of the earth, lest he be the one who interprets to harm and fumbles, because at the helm of a people, and especially of the Romanian people in this difficult time, there must not be a man half-prepared to face the current regulations on earth, *(Reference to the candidate in second place in the first round, poorly prepared politically and who has not known a foreign language well, r.n.)* and he must know well the things inside the people and the things outside, and then he must have the advice of the wisest among the sons of the nation, and therefore, the Lord must reveal to you the right measure for the one among you, who will be known among the peoples as wise, just, full of faithfulness for the nation and the country, watching with love at all times day and night, and through these may God be able to stand and be able to overcome for this people with His protection.

Here is why I am looking down on you with all the heavenly hosts! We are standing watch from above to defeat any enemy who would try to disturb you from the middle or from the sides.

Oh, bow down, sons! Look with Me and seek the wisest and most dedicated among you for the people, because the bad time immediately demands this, but especially humble yourselves before the people so much disoriented, lest they make a mistake again while waiting for a deliverance to come, and when it does come, let there not be a snare and slavery in its place, because nowhere is there any more watchfulness over the land, and this is why I am coming, the Lord is coming with His power, with the word of His power and with heavenly armies fighting and conquering any enemy that may appear.

Oh, how can I speak to you? I no longer urge you to believe and listen to My advice, for My word is working towards you through its being placed in My book today and is driving away the enemy, demanding an account of his trespass on God's land.

Oh, My country, oh, Romanian nation, take from God, so that you do not make a mistake! You now need a protective and well-fortified law for yourself, so that the enemy antichrist may no longer enter to ask for a place of vigilance over you for himself, because many would ask you for this high place and you to give it, but the unknown is a trap for you, he is an enemy, he is cunning, and you do not watch with watchfulness and you rather take enemy advice instead, and you do not know to whom you open.

I will hold your hand, Romanian people, I will shepherd you, I will watch over you, so that you do not fall into error again, so that you do not fall into the enemy's hands, but seek My word with which I breathe upon you to revive you and shepherd you, and over the enemy I breathe with power to drive him away, to lay him down and overcome him, for it is written in the Scriptures that I, the Lord, Jesus Christ, will overcome him with the breath of My mouth, with My word, with My rebuke, **(2 Thess: 2/8)** and I always say to him: Go away, enemy spirit, go away from the land and the Romanian nation, for the Lord is dwelling with the throne of His word on this land, and you fear the breath of My mouth for it will drive you away, for the kingdom of heaven is coming towards men, and this glory and the life of the age to come are being prepared in heaven and on earth, for the Spirit and the bride of the Bridegroom are saying: Come, come, Lord! Amen, amen, amen.

And you, the watchmen for My coming on earth as word over My citadel with you, sit before Me and open the book of My word to write in it and to set it on the path, whenever My voice comes to you to share it and for My word to work, and now, I am going sit with you in council and we are going to set a holy feast table in the citadel, for they are days of celebration (patron saint), days of Christ's victory through My work with you, when thirty-three years ago, I wrote Myself on earth with the creation and sealing of the little garden of My word, and I came forth above with the mystery of God from the midst of the Romanian nation, and I am going to sit in council with you and with the people of My word, with the one who was, with the one who still is and with the one who is born and seeks under the glory of My word and its fulfillments; and therefore, I am giving you a new power and I am giving you, so that I may be able to work through you, to write Myself with you on earth for the glory, that My glory from the end of time is coming, and you are My witnesses, oh sons. Amen, amen, amen.
