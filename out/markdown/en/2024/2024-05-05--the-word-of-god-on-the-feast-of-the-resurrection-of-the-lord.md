A spirit of holy and heavenly joy, a spirit of comfort, is coming from heaven to earth. All nature is rejoicing and taking part in the joy of the feast of My resurrection, for I, the Lord, through the word of My mouth speaking from above in the clouds and distributing the comforting shiver over the earth and am saying to everyone Myself: Christ has risen!

Pay attention, heavens, and give heed, earth, Christ is risen! In heaven and on earth there is a feast of resurrection. Christ has risen! People and birds are singing the song of Christ's resurrection, and all nature trembles as it did two thousand years ago, when I, the Lord, suddenly rose from the grave and showed Myself alive, after the leaders of Jerusalem condemned Me to death on the cross, to stay in the grave for three days, so that I might then appear Myself resurrected and prove Myself God, the Son, true God from true God, and then raise up a people for Me, a herald of My resurrection and disciples from generation to generation to this day, witnesses for Me from one to another, so that the truth may be preserved with strength and faith that God came from heaven to earth, stayed with people for thirty-three years, appeared resurrected from the dead, spent forty days showing Himself to the disciples, and then He returned to His Father, and He remained on earth with people, the Paschal Lamb, the new Paschal, the Passover Lord.

I am embracing in a chorus of saints and angels all around the people of My word, the people of New Jerusalem, My new creation in the midst of which I have set a new Passover table, the renewed Passover, which I promised two thousand years ago to My disciples, and for this I had to raise up for Me a good people to resemble Me in faith, in love, in holiness, so that I could with them establish again on earth a life of paradise, for man fell from paradise along with the life of paradise given to him by Me to live and obey it exactly in the garden of paradise, and I, the Lord, am happy today that I have a people on earth with whom I have a Passover table and a heavenly feast in the midst of the human race, and behold, this is how the world and its deeds are judged, for the whole world has forgotten paradise, has forgotten everything, and I am working through My people today, I am working to establish again paradise on earth and to set a holy table in the garden of heaven, to sit with the saints and angels at the table with the people of My word, to be the Lamb on the table, the new food, the Lord's Passover.

I am embracing the nations and peoples of the earth in the word of resurrection to make everyone feel the holy taste of heaven and the breath of the Holy Spirit of the feast of My resurrection, and behold, the time of Passover is now, not when man would like it to be, but when it appears and when it proves that My resurrection was then, for the sign of the descent of the heavenly light that testifies to My resurrection, behold, this sign is with people, so that the truth and its place among people and His righteous, faithful people, the Orthodox Christian flock, is strengthened, it has been enmity in all times by the band of the antichrist, the man of lawlessness with his war against Christ, but the heavenly signs put to shame the adversary and keep the truth in its place and the time of the Lord's Passover, of the Passover Lamb on the Christian table to this day.

I am sending the Passover greeting over the earth and over the peoples and I am saying Myself everywhere: Peace to you, people of all tongues and nations of the earth! Christ has risen, I Myself am telling you! Oh, examine yourselves if you are in faith, if you belong to Christ by faith, by life, by dress, by food, by love for God. Oh, do not forget, Christ was dead and rose again and left on earth the Passover table, the Lamb of the Father for the food of Christians, a holy ordinance on the table and at the table.

The people on earth do not want to learn up close this holy wisdom that came from heaven and was placed on earth with a new Passover table. I am the Lamb of the Father, the Passover little Lamb. But all the people slaughter the lambs of the sheep and eat them on Passover day, and I, the Lord, am despised and disregarded as the Passover Lamb.

It hurts Me the foolishness and ignorance of God among men, and that is why I am coming to earth with a word at this time, I am coming to speak to men from the midst of a people taken from the world and prepared by Me beautifully, with a life of paradise, so that I can sit with them at the table of the new Passover and share on earth My news that I am coming and that I keep on coming to prepare the earth, the man and heaven, for those whom I will gather in order to glorify Myself over them and to testify to My coming and its work, when no one expects Me to come. [legatura_la_teme id_capitol="8" id_subcapitol="3"]The renewal of the world is coming, it is coming, for the world has completely forgotten God and spends time on earth in all kinds of tastes and pleasures that perish due to ignorance and unwisdom, and behold, all men, all are wandering, and no one knows where they are going.

I speak to the peoples of the earth and I tell them again and again Myself: Christ has risen! I cannot work according to the will of man, for man does not have Me as his God. But it is coming with hasty steps, the renewal of the world is coming as it is written, the new heaven is coming and the new earth is coming, heaven is coming and settling on earth for a life of heaven over people.

What does paradise life mean? It means for man to eat what I gave him in heaven as his food, **(Gen: 1/29.)** and it means to obey God and the laws of life, to leave death and its deeds and to walk according to My word, just as in these days I have made a heavenly settlement over the people of My word with which I am bringing My coming, testimony, proclamation and My work with them over the earth, and especially a new Passover table in their midst, the Lamb of the Father on the table for the food of My people.

[/legatura_la_teme][legatura_la_teme id_capitol="3" id_subcapitol="3"]The return of man to paradise, this is My work with the people of My word from among the Romanian nation.

Man, oh man, return to God! Look to the beginning, look better and longer, for the end is coming and will finish you off completely. Turn your face to Me and seek the wisdom of heavenly life on earth and no longer deform your life, face, and dress, for a man who shaves his male adornment from his face is an abomination and contempt towards God, the Maker of man, and so is a woman who forgets her calling by leaving her female clothing for her male one, but it is written in the Scriptures that a woman who dresses as a man is an abomination before God, for she also cuts off her head adornment and walks uncovered to the mockery of God and the angels of God, and the spirit of fornication draws man to sin, to weaknesses, to separation from God and from the holiness that keeps the Lord dwelling within man, and the Lord has rest in the man who is sanctified for the Lord, for the wisdom from heaven on earth.

Oh, the fall of man was not so great before. The fall from faith due to the abandonment of the fear of God, this brings the scythe over the earth and over people and the devil has piled up his gatherings, for all people are pagans in life and appearance, and no longer pull towards the Lord, no longer pull towards the heaven, no longer pull towards the paradise, oh, no longer do they do this.

You, children of God, but also you, children of men, listen to the word of the Lord: The most important work of day or night among all the works of man is the work of prayer, spending time with the Lord, oh, sons. When this work is small and smaller and drier, you little by little lose the Lord, the Lord's guard over you, your dwelling with God, and with those in heaven, you lose love, for it falls to the point of separation from God, the gravest sin on the love between man and God. When love falls, this happens because of the mistakes against the life of God in man. The deeds of love make life sweet and precious in man, and man is happy through God, Who dwells within man, but when man is mute and indifferent to the soul within him, oh, how much the Lord is missing from such a man with a soul so sick and shaken by the spirit of eternal perdition, and that is why I come from heaven as a word to earth to awaken man and for him to choose the spirit of life, and My speech in the days of the feast of resurrection is My love for man, oh, but I must descend from heaven the spirit of love for God upon men, for man does not have love, because he has the sin of his work. However, I come and breathe upon men the spirit of heavenly wisdom so that men may take it into them and find Me with My source of word, for behold, I am standing above on the clouds of My glory and speaking to men and am saying:

Come back to life, you people and nations! Christ has risen! Come and follow Him! Come to heaven, for heaven is coming to you, come![/legatura_la_teme]

On the second day of Passover, I am going to speak again, again I will share from heaven, and again we will sing Christ is risen and we will share the spirit of resurrection over the earth, oh, sons.

And now, strengthen your staying before My word, sons who put My words in the book on earth, and peace to you, and Christ is risen, oh sons!

In the length and breadth of the earth, I, the Lord, am saying on the day of the resurrection feast:

Christ has risen, Christ has risen, Christ had risen! Amen, amen, amen.
