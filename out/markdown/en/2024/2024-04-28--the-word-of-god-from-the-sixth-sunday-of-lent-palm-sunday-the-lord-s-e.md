I am who I am. Blessed is he who believes that this word is the word of God!

I am Who I am, I am this word, I, the Lord, speak it, so that it may be written on earth, and then distributed, and so that it may work its fulfillment among men.

[legatura_la_teme id_capitol="7" id_subcapitol="3"]Let the book of My word be opened for the feast day of Palm Sunday, so that the memory of My entry into Jerusalem on the colt of a donkey two thousand years ago may be celebrated with glory of word, for if I raised Lazarus from the grave, the time had come for Me to enter the citadel of Jerusalem with songs and hosannas and for the Scripture about the King Who would come in the name of the Lord to be fulfilled and for this fulfillment to be found, only all those who do not want God on earth are always lying in wait like those two thousand years ago so that the truth from God may not be caught by people, so that My written coming may be fulfilled then and now. However, I am coming and fulfilling if this is written, for I am coming for the faithful, for those who are pure in heart and love, for where the love of God is, God is there as well, is and will be, as it is written.

I am placing peace from heaven over the citadel of My word in the midst of the Romanian nation and I am entering the book of the word on a holy feast day and I am speaking My word to the peoples of the earth and I am saying to them:

Peace to you, peoples! The Lord's word is coming to the earth and saying: peace to you! Do you hear? I am entering My Jerusalem, the one from the Romanians, I am entering on a white horse, and blessed is and will be the one who believes that this word is the word of God!

I stand on the clouds above and release My voice so that My word may be written and go everywhere. I come with the clouds, for so it is written that I come, and on this day the memory of the day when I was carried on a donkey to Jerusalem is made in heaven and on earth and the Scripture was fulfilled which says: *«Rejoice, daughter of Zion, for your King is coming!».* **(Zacharia: 9/9)**

Oh, no one expects to see God's accomplishments announced until they are fulfilled. The spirit of the world has been standing against God on earth for seven thousand years through people, and people always choose unbelief and get carried away by the spirit of the world and get completely dizzy with everything, everything, and when I am to come to fulfill the Scriptures, I find no faith on earth; however, I cannot stumble in the unbelief of people, oh, I cannot, and behold, I am coming and fulfilling, I am coming and writing My word on earth and sharing Myself with it and the faithful find Me, for I am coming for them, and I am speaking to them, giving them comfort and consolation, I am giving them strength and hope, and I am giving them from heaven, for on earth they work only with lies as in all times, and the Lord looks down from heaven upon the sons of men to see if there is one who understands or one who seeks God, but all have turned aside, all have become worthless, and there is none who does goodness, not even one, as it is written.

[/legatura_la_teme][legatura_la_teme id_capitol="3" id_subcapitol="3"]You, the peoples, and you, the leaders of the peoples, hear the word of God! The spirit of the world is the enemy of God on earth and in men. I brought the people of Israel out from among the sons of men when they were slaves in Egypt, and I told them through the spirit of prophecy, I told them to kill the nations on the way and to walk their pure path to Canaan, to be free from the corruption of the spirit of the world and its idols. However, he did not listen to go out of the world. I had also told them not to deal with the sins of the world and with the sons of the world.

I had boundless pain when I lost Israel on its way to Canaan because of the spirit of the world and the peoples without God in their way of life. Oh, man cannot obey God if he does not come out of the world, for the spirit of the world attracts man, clings to the mind of man, and the Lord has been losing man for seven thousand years, only that in all times I have had a remnant preserved by grace, that is by obedience to God remaining on My side, I have fulfilled with it those sent by God to settle and be on earth, to go with My work through time as two thousand years ago, when I did not stop working if the people of Israel cut Me off so that the rulers over them would not lose their dominion and power, which the arrogant and haughty need, and who cannot work otherwise, because they have the spirit of the world as food for their minds.

But here I am again, the word of the Holy Spirit over the earth and over the peoples, after two thousand years since then, for I am Who I am, I am the Word of the Father Sabaoth, and My Father works, and I also work everything that the Father works, and I am calling the peoples to the knowledge of God, for the kingdom from above is coming to earth, the Lord is coming with tens of thousands of His saints. I am coming, because it is written that I am coming, and you, the peoples, receive the Holy Spirit so that you may know with Him God, Who speaks over you so that you may receive from heaven, because on earth lies reign and everyone feeds on it, some feed each other only with lies and its fruits, but truth comes from heaven, the Lord comes more than ever on earth, the word of the rebirth of the world is coming, the Lord is coming riding on a white horse, as it is written to come, and on earth let the Scriptures be opened and searched in detail, for in them are written the fulfillments that are coming and are hurrying to come and settle with their fulfillment on earth.

Therefore, you peoples, come to meet the Lord, come to the truth from heaven, for the work of truth is coming to earth and lawlessness will perish from everywhere, and heaven will be established from place to place and the sun will shine seven times more, as it is written, and everything will be fulfilled through the work of the church of Christ, for in it is the truth about God, and against which the spirit of the world has stood for two thousand years!

The staying with the world is for Christians as with Israel who united with the nations on the road on its way to Canaan, and the Lord's people fell prey to the spirit of the world. I have always advised the Christian to come out of the world, not to stay with the world, because with a life of a true Christian for God you cannot stay with the world, because the world that calls itself Christian does not have a Christian life, and you know that you are with God and the world does not like it, it does not like it that way, and that is why I have advised the Christian to stay in his little nest and be satisfied with his God, because when the Christian goes out into the world the Lord weeps, because the world is very, very sinful and defiles the mind and life and body of the Christian man, of the one who walks on the path to heaven. Oh, the road to heaven does not pass through the tavern, it does not pass through those places full of sin and the spirit of the world that serves sin and lusts and all kinds of appearances, as sin gives man punishment, because disobedience to God is the gravest sin, from which all sin then comes.

[/legatura_la_teme][legatura_la_teme id_capitol="6" id_subcapitol="3"]The love of God is the enemy of the spirit of the world, and there is no happier man than he who has as work of the mind and heart the love of God and its deeds. Let all the peoples of the earth gather to this greatness, for the renewal of the world is coming, heavenly beauty is coming on earth, the fulfillment of this Scripture is coming, which I Myself declared that it would come and settle down, and therefore I am calling the people of the earth to this fulfillment, which is coming.

Here, man, is the hour of the truth about you, and if you cannot tell it and let others around you know it, let the Lord, receive Him when He knocks and do not leave Him outside to wait at your door, but do not do like Adam, who spoke confusedly during the hour of the truth about him, and who should not have let himself be led by Eve, but should have taken the truth from God, and if he sought to hide from God when I spoke to him, behold, he gave in to lies, poor thing, and I, the Lord, had to atone for the cross and contempt because of his sin.

Oh, so did Judas, he did not stoop to come and forgive him, and he worked on his own and went and put an end to his own life. Oh, if he had bowed down to the forgiveness from Me, when I was still on the cross waiting for him to appear and forgive him, I would have taken him with Me to heaven on that day like the thief who confessed God, and who asked Me from My right hand, while standing on the cross, to remember him in My kingdom, which will come with Me on earth, and which Father showed him then that he would come with Me on earth, and I waited for him beyond My cross and his and took him with Me to heaven on that very day and fulfilled his prayer. I would have gone to the Father with two confessors full of repentance and love for Me, for My pain from them, and I would not have been happier than that happiness at that moment. But Judas despaired for himself after, confessing Me to the high priests and Pharisees as innocent, and himself as My betrayer to them. God was grieved for him, but he despaired, and he did not return to be reconciled with Me and after that to go to the Father reconciled with him, and he would then testify like the centurion who crucified Me and who knew Me then, that I am the Son of God, and he proclaimed this from margins to edges afterwards.

Oh, it is not nature that is to blame, but man's self-mind, for nature is nature, but the mind is the one through which you repair yourself or ruin yourself worse, and that is why it is good to let the Lord have a word for you, you unsubmitted man and confused by your mind.

[/legatura_la_teme][legatura_la_teme id_capitol="3" id_subcapitol="4"]You, all of you who are fighting with all your power to erase the name of Jesus Christ from the earth, oh, what will you do when you see that you have lost, that you have fought in vain? My name cannot be erased, it is written in blood on the earth, and the blood testifies for Me, as it is written, and not only My blood, but also of all those who were cut off for My testimony.

Two thousand years ago, I wrote My great name on earth, the name of the One Who rose from the dead on the third day, and when darkness fell for three hours on earth, the sun and all the elements were frightened, and the moon and the stars watched over My rest in the tomb, but the angels were filled with hope, and the righteous saints rose up **(Matt: 27/50-53)**, who rose with Me, while Annas and Caiaphas, Pilate and Herod, the Pharisees and the scribes, and all those around them, were afraid of death, trying to seek shelter behind the lie they had invented, oh, but this did not work out for them, for I then stayed forty days resurrected in Jerusalem, until the day I rose up at the right hand of the Father, from where I descended and stayed thirty-three years on earth among men.

[/legatura_la_teme][legatura_la_teme id_capitol="6" id_subcapitol="3"]I had taught My people not to stand apart from one another, for behold, Judas was lost if he walked and worked separately, on his own among the children of the world, for he who separates himself from his brothers loses the blessing and power, first loses them through his own work, and then separates himself from his brothers, since he no longer has the power from above on his side. Judas was lost among the brothers, for if the brothers do not know about each other, if the brothers do not know your thoughts and work, it means that the devil has overcome you with his enemy darkness on the spirit of brotherhood, and behold, one must always shine upon those who are the Lord's on earth with their steps, for the soul needs light and work in much light, much food from above, and the body must be very small compared to the wealth that is required for the soul before God from every man who loves the Lord.[/legatura_la_teme]

So, behold, I have entered with My word everywhere on earth on this holy holiday day, and it, once spoken, works and is fulfilled, and the kingdom of God's love comes and is established among those who choose this happiness, for only God is man's happiness, and let people hear this truth!

[legatura_la_teme id_capitol="3" id_subcapitol="4"]I will still say to those stubborn on the heights on which they stand and who believe themselves to be the masters of the world and even masters over My church, which has in its womb the truth which is one, and here is what I am saying to those who allow them to walk during the celebration of the feast of My resurrection, again and again I tell them, and I am telling them this way:

The church, that has preserved within itself the two-thousand-year-old truth of Christ, is the Orthodox Christian Church, and whoever wants to have the celebration of the Lord's Passover once for all, behold, let them come to the church untouched by the spirit of the world for the truth of Christ through the church. Those who have gone out for two thousand years and have kept going out and have kept dividing themselves into all kinds of groups, let them come, and by no means those who have not come out from under the truth of the church of the fathers left until today on its rock.

Anathema be to all those who cause to wander again and again and to shake again and again the truth of God and the place where He stands and remains! This is My word, the word of God upon those who presume to believe that they will shake the throne of God between earth and heaven, for they will shake and they will fall, and I, the Lord, remain and reign over My true ones, and they mean the church of Christ on earth.

[/legatura_la_teme][legatura_la_teme id_capitol="6" id_subcapitol="3"]And now, behold, the Christian nation is preparing to enter the week of remembrance of My passions of two thousand years ago, and I, the Lord, am saying to all:

Sons, sons, work on tearful repentance, work on awareness of your unworthiness and go examining yourselves and purifying yourselves through repentance, go like this until the feast of My resurrection, and I will give Myself to you then as sons resurrected through repentance, through holiness and through love for God, and we will be joy to each other and songs of resurrection to each other, and those who keep Me outside are those who do not love Me and do not renew themselves for My reception and leave Me outside as a stranger.

Therefore, I examine you, I know you, I cleanse you, I forgive you, I renew you and teach you the mystery of the resurrection, sons, and I long for you more than the saints in heaven, for with you I am now fulfilling on earth My kingdom and its work of establishing it among men, and especially I am fulfilling Myself through those who bear My word upon earth, for I stand on the clouds, speaking from above and awaiting, sons, I awaiting My great and ever-greater kingdom among men, and heaven and earth are working together now, working this great fulfillment, oh, sons. Amen, amen, amen.[/legatura_la_teme]
