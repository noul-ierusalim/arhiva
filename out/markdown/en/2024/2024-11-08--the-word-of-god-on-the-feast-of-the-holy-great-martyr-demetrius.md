Open to the Lord, sons, watchmen on the way of Christ's coming! Open to My word so that it may enter you into the citadel and into the book, sons!

Peace to you, oh, peace to you, again and again peace to you, children working with God and for God, oh, that I have no others, I have no one to lean on between heaven and earth, and therefore, peace to you, and that I may take from it, take from you what I give you, so that I may give you again and again and be a worker over you and among you and be able to penetrate with My word over the earth and to loosen with it the hardness of hearts and the stubbornness of the mind, oh, that those on earth no longer need God, and they want My place as ruler over them, over everything, and they do not know that only I, the Lord, am the One Who I am, and no one can take Me aside, and only I rule over what they say and do and that they can, too, and this because only I am the One Who I am, and only I have this name!

I am the One Who am, only I, sons, and behold, again and again I write My word through you in the citadel upon the earth and I am saying this:

This word, placed by Me on earth in My time with you, is the work and coming of the Holy Spirit of the divine Trinity, and blessed is he who does not commit resistance, disbelief or strike at the work of the Holy Spirit, for that one will be of the Lord forever and ever with God, the glorified in the Trinity of One Being, sons, oh, and woe to him who sins with his heart or tongue against the Holy Spirit, for two thousand years ago, I said that such like this will not be forgiven, for time has been throughout the work of the Father and the work of the Son, and there is no more God than this working measure over men, and behold, the Holy Spirit completes the work of the Trinity of One Being, and all those, who have had the fate given from above from heaven to meet on earth with God, the Holy Spirit, must have their heads on their shoulders, and there must be faith without turning back, so that no one may sin without forgiveness then, children, for two thousand years ago, I spoke and said: *«Whoever sins against the Son will be forgiven, but if he does this against the Holy Spirit, he will no longer be able to be forgiven»*, **(Matthew: 12/31-32, Mark: 3/28-29)**, for the Lord is this much: the Father, the Son and the Holy Spirit, and when I said, two thousand years ago, that the people on earth should be baptized in this name, I told the disciples to whom I left work after My ascension to the Father and I told them: *«In My name you will work signs and wonders for the faith of the people and you will go in the name of the Father and of the Son and of the Holy Spirit as My messengers».* **(Mark: 16/17-20).**

Oh, sons of the citadel of My word, behold, you are My messengers upon the earth with the work of the Holy Spirit coming from heaven upon you, that you may share it everywhere in the name of the Father and of the Son and of the Holy Spirit. Amen.

Oh, what should I do with those who do not know this sign and do not want it upon them as a sign that they are Christians, sons of God among the sons of men? [legatura_la_teme id_capitol="6" id_subcapitol="3"]I established with the voice of My tongue this sign of the Christian nation and I called it the cross and I gave it as the banner of those who confess Me as their God and Shepherd. The God Who is triune in name and in work has placed His sign upon the earth to seal and designate with it those who are His among men as sons of God, and I spoke through the holy ancestors of the Holy Spirit and showed the God-loving sons the sign of the Holy Trinity, so that they might place it upon them, and I told them to bend the two little fingers of their right hand toward the palm of their hand, and to hold the other three tightly together and to show with them the name of God upon them and to place them on the forehead for the Father, then on the chest for the Son, and then on the right and left on the shoulders for the Holy Spirit, and to then seal the name of the divine Trinity upon them by saying the word amen.

Oh, I have said this now, so that the ignorant may hear the word from heaven through which God tells those without a cross on their bodies that only the people with the cross are the witnesses who work in the name of the Father and the Son and the Holy Spirit, and the others go astray and lead many astray, and they wage war with God, the cross and those who bear the cross, for the cross is the shield and sign of the Christian people on earth and is the banner of victory left from heaven for the children of God. Amen.[/legatura_la_teme]

It is a feast day, My sons, and I am standing above you with the company of martyrs who came from earth to heaven victorious through the cross against unbelievers and idolaters, against pagan kings, oh, and today's feast day is the martyr Demetrius and I am coming to you with a word on a feast day of saints and we are establishing a necessary advice for My journey with you in the name of the Holy Spirit, with Whom God completes His threefold work towards His great end on earth, and towards the new beginning about which it is written in the Scriptures that it will be, and you may have nothing else to work on, except this glory of Mine with you, My sons, and that is why I always come and always strengthen your body and spirit with heavenly powers, for you are very few under this task, oh, that I have had so many people for seventy years of word, but the Christians have not been able to give themselves to Me as workers, for they have had their own to carry, and I have waited with the spirit pained by longing, to come to Me and to help you renounce everything for My name, even yourselves, so that you may have nothing of your own except the triune God as worker and give Me your esteem and love, so that I may work with them for those things that are yet to be accomplished, while on earth everyone is minding their own business, but we are working and going towards My victory with you, and that is why I said that those, who have known My coming today, should beware, that they should beware of entering into you with doubt or a spirit of judgment. I have taught everyone to beware of the sin of the tongue, oh, because it is no longer the time for the forgiveness of this sin against the Holy Spirit, with Whom I, the Lord, am working through you at this time, and rather let everyone hurry towards a spirit of humility and regret for their lack of love and unwisdom, for denying the preparation for the saints at the end of time, for their great delay through their disregard for God, oh, sons.

[legatura_la_teme id_capitol="6" id_subcapitol="3"]Seventy years of the word of the Holy Spirit, seventy years of indiffer-ence of those who have not hurried to be My hard workers, Christian families who have not stood before the Lord all at once at the hours of prayer under the same roof, without holy union at the fraternal table, separated from each other by many things, hiding many things from each other, separated at the table of food, where they have called Me to bless if they still called Me together as brothers through holy ordinances as in heaven. Even worse and worse than this, they had more purses, each with their own secrets, the love of money being called this soul-killing passion, oh, and here is separation between brothers together under the same roof and fence, and what could I, the Lord, have done in all this time from this lack of love and separation between brothers with secrets between them, with all kinds of habits and desires? Oh, how could I be one with them, when they were not one in everything and in Christ then? Oh, what pain, how long, sons! How little is God understood by man! They worked, poor people, until the last penny to carry their children through schools for an earthly life, without love for God, neither the parents nor the children, without pity for My pain from their coldness towards Me, oh, and what use was their belief that I am this word if they did not prove their faith through deeds? The woman had one purse, the man another, the children the same, and here is the sin of the love of money in the step of the Christians who knew My word up close, but without a saving step towards the Lord![/legatura_la_teme]

Oh, sons, a Christian heart that loved My brothers and sisters have been close to My people lately, a warm heart that I, the Lord, always looked at, while he was gathering his savings from his work, grain by grain, for his son to live without any shortages on earth, and he went on like this without respite until he left everything one day, for I took him from among his people to stop him from gathering, and I put the servants of My people to work for his little soul, so that they would cry out to Me for his forgiveness and the release of his little soul, for the love of money was stopping his steps towards heaven and I could not find a way to help him.

Behold, sons, this is how many have violated the saving ordinance among those who have stayed close to My word in these seventy years of heavenly word, for their desires were greater than My will on their part, and they did not seek a guide for themselves, and so they went according to their own will day after day, wandering among the sons of the world, working only for the flesh, heading nowhere, and on My great day they will say to Me: "Lord, we were also with You!"

Again and again, I place word in the book and say again: This work of the word is the work of the Holy Spirit of the divine Trinity, oh, and what are you doing, who dare to strike from the sides towards it, thinking that you are striking people, when you are striking in the Holy Spirit? If those who bear My word on earth seem wrong to you, oh, who are you to hide under what you say against them? Am I not the Lord, the One Who takes care of them, I, and not you? Behold, if you hide under words with which you strike back, so that you may come out well for your wavering, oh, look carefully at those wavering through time, for all those who have acted like you by striking back, have fallen from the walking path of My guiding word through hard times, and all those who have judged like you are none other than those who struck back for their own unsteadiness, which they could have corrected if they had sought healing.

Behold, doubt halves victory and walking on the path with the Lord. He who shakes his mind, and then his faith, then becomes dizzy, for disobedience to My word brings the Christian's failure for Me and another falls through the one who falls and one follows the other, as Eve followed the serpent in paradise and she transgressed My word, through which Adam also fell later.

Oh, sons, oh, sons who stand under My Spirit, your faith must be virginal, sons. No matter how much the Christian errs, he must be careful not to defile his faith, its power and its heavenly purity, and if otherwise happens in the heart of the Christian who knows God in this word, behold, as the man who became an enemy of God in heaven fell, so it happened to those who were separated from My paternal arm in all these seventy years of word and they have lost their minds with the borrowed one from another to their separation from Me, becoming each other's serpent that urges disobedience from Christian to Christian, and behold, a great miracle would have been now, if I had had many sons in the fulfillment of My word spoken from time to time, for this time of My glory with the obedient people, and we are going through hard times and the Lord sends angels to help you, for you have much work to do, as I give you, just as the Father gave Me when He sent Me to work on earth for the salvation of many, oh sons.

And now, as for the turmoil on earth among the people who think themselves great and greater in this time, read from the psalms which say that the Lord shatters the counsels of the nations, rejects the thoughts of the peoples and despises the counsel of the princes, and only the counsel of the Lord remains forever from generation to generation, through the nation whose God the Lord is, chosen for His inheritance.

The martyr Demetrius comforts himself with the love of My word and takes love from it and gives it to you and bestows holy strength upon you, and he timidly thanks Me for My great word on his feast day among the saints and gives Me all precedence, and he remains praying to Me for those among you who listen to God on earth, My children.

Now I, the Lord, bless your care for My works with you on the Maluri *(Maluri Village, the village and the house where Saint Virginia was born and lived, r.n.)*, and you put heart to heart and strength to strength through My grace upon you, and work, sons, with great grace, for behold, the time is with us, but let us ask the Father for this, and you dress well and work, warming yourselves with Me and the angels, oh, for this is beautiful, so that God may work, sons.

And now, you are to make a little bit of time for the celebration of the holy archangels in the citadel, to which table those who have fallen asleep from Adam to you are coming, My sons, with a spirit full of longing, they are coming, warming themselves and waiting for Me to come with the last breath of My mouth and which will sound the great meeting and the great consolation, My sons.

I am sending a word among the nations and am saying to them: Peace to you, nations!

Peace to you, those who are among the peoples under the banner of the cross of Christ, and who testifies through its sign to the Father, the Son, and the Holy Spirit!

Peace and holy victory over the peoples bearing the sign of the holy cross!

Peace also to the peoples, who are not opposed to those who are with God in their fight against unbelief on earth!

The feast of the Birth of Jesus Christ is coming; it is coming and it is crying out everywhere, the Lord is crying out: Peace to you, peoples! Lay down your weapons of war and take up the cross, the sign of the victory of the Christian nation on earth far and wide, for if there were no Christians of the cross on earth, oh, there would be no more earth or anything on it, for Christians are the soul of the ground on earth.

Oh, peace to you, My people through whom I, the Lord Jesus Christ, trumpet far and wide the voice of My word over the earth! Amen, amen, amen.
