I accompany you on holy days and I am setting a table of word with you, sons, and then I am sharing Myself from My table with you, and the feast of the word of God multiplies over the earth.

Peace to you, oh, peace to you on the day of the royal feast, according to the custom of the ancestors, that of the fathers, sons! I am teaching you this day as a Shepherd Who I am to you, and to feed you from heaven and to learn more and more to remain children, for he who does not have parents is not a child, he does not grow spiritually to be a child with parents and to have a share in God always, always, and then forever, for I said: *«Whoever does not receive the kingdom of God as a child cannot have My kingdom in him».*

[legatura_la_teme id_capitol="1" id_subcapitol="7"]Sons, sons, many are Christians on earth, but few among them are spiritual and children through this, and the rest are earthly Christians, they do not listen to anyone, but only to themselves from within, and from without they take as they please, for they are earthly. I, the Lord, had always had a child on earth, and I had had a spiritual Child, for I had parents, I listened to them and I did not take from within Me, but from My parents. So also My Virgin mother, she was always a child, for she did not work from within herself her work, but everything that was given to her to do by those who carried her, and again, of what was written in the law that required to be fulfilled, and she took her Child in her arms and they went to worship in the temple, they went according to the law, they went according to the parents, and she worshipped her Child to the Lord and submitted to the law of purification after birth, and in the temple she received words over her and over Me through the spirit of prophecy, all with obedience like a child, all torn from the kingdom of heaven within her.

[/legatura_la_teme][legatura_la_teme id_capitol="6" id_subcapitol="3"]Man likes to grow up because he does not like to be a child, to remain a child, to have parents, to have a maker and guide who would guide his steps and thoughts and deeds and feelings and faith and everything within him so that the kingdom of children could be in him, the kingdom of God, the one above, and imperishable like God. David was appointed king by God over his people, but he had God as his father and he said: *«Lord, cleanse me from my hidden things and protect me from the evil from them, from the sins that are born from the hidden things in me»*. Oh, he gave the Lord a place inside him, he did not remain silent in him without knowing, and when he made a mistake, he revealed himself for healing and cried like a child for help.

The mystery and work of a child I am putting as a teaching on My table today, because it is difficult for man to get used to the kingdom of God within him, sons! If man looked better and more at Me, he could so easily understand this mystery of eternal life, the mystery of the kingdom of God in man. What does it benefit man if he did not will the kingdom of God within him? What did it benefit Adam if he did not remain a child of the One Who created him from the earth and placed heaven in him, which Adam then destroyed?

To be a child means to be great through obedience. To be obedient means to be a child, always small. Behold, man cannot do this, he cannot do it since his beginning on earth.

[/legatura_la_teme][legatura_la_teme id_capitol="1" id_subcapitol="3"]The Father gave Me work to listen to Him, and the Father has been in Me from eternity. After the creation of heaven and earth and all that was created by the word, the Father sent Me to earth the first time, He sent Me to work on earth, and the earth was the footstool before My feet **(See Isaiah 66:1)**; it was the dough from which I made man. And when I had finished the work given to Me by the Father, I placed the spirit of life in the man created from the earth in My image and likeness. Then the Father placed His wisdom in man and placed him before Him so that he might work the work given to him with obedience, and Adam named all of God's creation and remained obedient as a child, until he wanted to be great, higher than his Father, and then the kingdom of the Father in him was shaken, and then he also was shaken and saw the reward of his disobedience.

[/legatura_la_teme][legatura_la_teme id_capitol="3" id_subcapitol="3"]Then I came a second time to earth, and I obeyed the Father to come after man and to build again in God the lost man, and to redeem for the Father all the seed that came out of man. Thus, I worked two thousand years ago this work given by Father, for I am a child and I listen while working, oh, and since then I work and I keep working. And now the Father sends Me a third time so that man may hear Me working and speaking of the new creation of the world, of the creation of God, and the hardest obstacle is placed by man.

Behold, man, I come to your aid on earth and I find you working cunning and hypocrisy, when you have to work the eternity that you have shaken even since your beginning from God. I call you to Me to come and work together, but you walk, walk, and suddenly you change and take up your old walk again and sit down and no longer walk as you have walked since you came to Me to walk and learn to walk. Oh, you are only doing well next to Me, but behold, you are kicking your good always, always, and I am shouting everywhere for your return to God.

Sons, sons, man has corrupted the state of God within himself and has stood in the place of God. Oh, do not stand within yourselves, this is not from God. Man takes the state of God and stands within himself. It is not good. This is what is on earth because of this! Where there is no God, there is sin, only sin. That is why it is good for God to dwell within man with His kingdom, which is waiting to awaken and reign, if only man would want to, who must understand his separation from God, for even from heaven the fox climbed over man's head and dragged him out of the holy garden, and behold, the woman is not afraid, she is not ashamed to walk with the fox around her neck and head to show better, because the woman is a fox, and the men walk on the road with the fox on their arm and are unaware of them, oh, and how could the Lord not cry out and not speak of this wandering pain of man and mind, starting from children to parents, and again, from parents to children!

It hurts Me that man stays closed inside himself, and that he does not open to be cleansed of sin through the work of the light from above. When will My resurrection come inside man? I am waiting for man to answer Me and not forget to answer Me, for man owes Me, after I had come and paid for his life so that he might then be resurrected.

The earth must be renewed, it must be pulled out of the swamp of sins on it, children. I cry out over the earth for man to stop sinning and to be renewed for the kingdom of God within him. The man in whom God lives and reigns is so beautiful, and I want this miracle to be seen all over the earth and for people to see it and desire their renewal, too!

Oh, man, if you do not bring your fellow men to the resurrection and to God, you will not find out how great this happiness is and what it brings you. Hurry to open and let the Lord enter your life and confess Him through your holiness, and with your tongue give Him glory and thanksgiving for His mercy, and let not your tongue be silent.

Sons, sons, I have set the word of lifting man from sin and I am waiting to enjoy the fruit. I always come on holidays and speak to you and go to people with the voice of My word, which cries out in the streets. I am granting you holy powers and giving you peace so that you can receive Me and set Me on the path to people so that My comfort from you may increase and I may give some of it to you, sons.[/legatura_la_teme]

Peace to you, that you may work with Me and for Me! Peace, children, peace to your little hearts, and now share My word throughout the earth! Amen, amen, amen.
