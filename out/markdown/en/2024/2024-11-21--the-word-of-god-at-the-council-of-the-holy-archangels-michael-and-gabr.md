The Lord comes to earth in word, accompanied by the angelic hosts and the heavenly powers and by His saints in tens of thousands.

The Lord is coming accompanied by His angel, Michael the archangel.

Let the gates of the citadel of My word be opened *(Gates - those by whom the Lord speaks, the Lord comes as word, r.n.)* on the land of the Romanian nation, My country from the end of time, the country of My return from the Father to establish My kingdom on it, and let the news be spread to the ends that I, the Lord, have a country and a people raised by My word, by the nurse from heaven, for without a nurse, man no longer knows Me as Father from heaven and does his will like any child spoiled by parents, and from whom I cannot make a disciple for Me, however, if he has a nurse, she holds his hand, puts his heart in her hand and he grows vigorously and has someone to watch over him and guide him for his beautiful steps on the path of glory on it.

Peace to you, citadel of My word, and peace to you, who watch for the Lord in the citadel! My angel, Michael, the archangel, is calling and going before Me to enter the citadel and the book, children. Oh, if every being has a shared guardian angel among the angels of God, I, the Lord, how would it have been if I had no angel? I teach the Christian not to forget his angel, who will write before and after him all his steps, all his deeds, his thoughts and his words, and which are not lost anywhere and go to God through the guardian angel from Me.

[legatura_la_teme id_capitol="7" id_subcapitol="1"]Oh, peace to you, to those who have gathered on the holy feast day announced by Me to sit here in the citadel for the angels, and for those who have passed away in the body for seven thousand years and more, and who are called here now, to their remembrance at this table for them, oh, what a great expectation there is for them for Me to come to prepare their eternal salvation and sweet eternity with Me! Here they are, looking after you, after their loved ones on earth, for a salvation, because those who are after the flesh on earth, some love heaven and the work for heaven and for those who have departed from their family, and others love the earth with the things of the earth, and behold, some become prayerful in heaven for their loved ones on earth if they have relatives united with those in heaven, with the saints and with obedience to God, and others of the departed expect prayers from the earth and have no comforts and joys, if their loved ones on earth are earthly and separated from heaven, from the love of God and from holiness, because behold, man loves himself and stays far from the love from above.[/legatura_la_teme]

Here, in the citadel, heaven is approaching the earth and a great deliverance is being worked from above and below, and therefore, do not forget, to the sons here and everywhere who love My coming, a word to you, oh, do not forget to be with Me always, always. Keep Me close to you all the time, keep the Lord with you, because if you do not stay close to Him, you do much in His name, you do from your mind, from the desires of your heart, and which can bring you heavy damage and pain. When you start to trample on My word over yourselves and do as you please, oh, you no longer take from Me, you no longer like to be and work according to My pleasure, and you feel embarrassed by Me when you start to do as you please, and not as the Lord wants, and rather you choose to trample on My will for your own will, and you no longer want Me with you, next to you and you hide from Me like Adam.

[legatura_la_teme id_capitol="6" id_subcapitol="3"]Oh, Christian who knows Me in this word, pay attention to what I am telling you, that many have come, heard and believed, but they have not fully understood what this work of the word means and have played in their minds between faith and unbelief and have lost the power from above for them. Bitter tears will flow to you for all your cold time near My source of the word, because only My word will be able to comfort your tear. My mercy is great in this time of My glory on earth, and you will stand before many who will taste from this spring and your cold time will hurt you when you see the grace and gift of the Lord upon those so hot, who will come from the four corners of the earth, taste from My coming in the spring of the word and find out how much I have called the Christian, how much I have bent down to teach him all the time, how much I have waited to make him a disciple worthy of My glory, of the love of God, and he has been cold and have not wanted to have a nurse for his love, which means life, because only love will remain in life, only it.[/legatura_la_teme]

I am speaking face to face with the angelic hosts. Oh, how shall I do to build in a Christian the spirit of tenderness and make this spirit visible through sweet and tender words, through mercy and help, through warmth of heart, through brotherly and paternal feelings among My fellow men, so that I would be able dwell among them with My way of living together with the brothers?

[legatura_la_teme id_capitol="6" id_subcapitol="3"]Oh, obedient sons, let all learn to take care of Me as if I were their own child. This is what I want. Just as a mother cannot take her mind off her little child, and only keeps and carries her thoughts to him when she is not with him because of the many worries she carries, so too you should take care of Me, sons, so that you may always be with Me, so that I may always be with you. I teach you to be like Me, to be as I am for you and with you. Just as a child does not know about his mother and her thoughts, but calms down only in her arms, so you also, you do not know how much I have you in My thoughts and care, you do not know, like a child who does not know when his mother carries him in her thoughts, through her great tenderness for him, sons.

Oh, I teach you to be tender, to be like God, to be beautiful through the spirit of tenderness as the angels are, sons. Man has cruelty in his heart many times, and has harshness and a spirit of judgment towards his fellow men; however, I am tender to share comfort, to save, to forgive, to comfort, to heal, sons, only let man help Me, wait for Me, call Me, want Me close to him.[/legatura_la_teme]

I am embracing My country today in word. Peace to you, My Romania! I am strengthening the angelic hosts and the heavenly powers at your borders. I love you from the Father, Who chose you to be My path over the earth with the glory of My word from your midst, and with which I proclaim Myself from the east to the west.

[legatura_la_teme id_capitol="5" id_subcapitol="6"]I am now descending the blessing of the Father, the Son and the Holy Spirit upon those whom I have called the bearers of this word upon the earth, and I am strengthening them in their work from Me to carry on their work from God, for behold, I am going before them through hearts full of longing for God and I am opening a wide way for them to proclaim Me everywhere, that great glory is approaching and the east and the west will meet, faith and unbelief, light and darkness, white and black, Christian and pagan, and the choice will be made and many will take the path on which I am coming and going, and the village of Maluri, from where I began My coming and its work, will receive on its small land the love of many, a new love, secretly preserved by the Lord, Who descended upon this little village in the year 1955, and for whom I, the Lord, am now preparing the prophesied glory to appear and it will be everything Romanian, oh, because nothing is sweeter than everything that is Romanian, and it will be beautiful as at the beginning of the world and there will be a sweet celebration at Maluri village and a great consolation that will share peace over the country and over the earth, and everything that will be on that day for seventy years of word, will be called the Word of God, written everywhere, over nature, over people, over their hearts, over flowers, over birds, over waters, and everything will rejoice and the glory of My word and its celebration on the Romanian land will be proclaimed over the earth. The sunrise and the sunset will meet on that glorious day. Heaven and earth will embrace then. Everything will be holy and all love, for holiness is born of love, and all those who will truly believe and love will be holy, they will be through the love of God, and many will say happily: *"Turn Your face, Lord, from our sins, and blot out all our iniquities, for we love You."*[/legatura_la_teme]

Oh, I have cried out for years and kept saying: *“Flee from the world, flee from the world, flee, My people!”* Let the world seek you, but let them seek you because you are Mine, My witness and herald, not so that you may stay in the world and with the world, but so that the world may see in you your love for God, the joy of life, for the love of God is joy, it is glory.

[legatura_la_teme id_capitol="6" id_subcapitol="3"]Sons, sons, beware of foxes, beware of women, beware of talking to women, for they do not protect you, for they cut off the heads of prophets and Christians! Beware of death through women, as for seventy years Christians have been dying through women, and women are like hell on earth! Oh, beware of walking alone, especially through My citadel alone, beware of these cursed hiding places, for the disorder that suits the devil is blatant, and I, the Lord, have not established a great citadel on earth for the devil, but for the heavenly and angelic hosts and for the dutiful and obedient. Oh, do not forget that Eve took advice from the devil, from the serpent, and took Adam away from God, and they fell from God, taking a foreign word for their steps, for the devil lied to her that they would not die if they broke My word, and the lie shook the faith and faithfulness of the two, oh, and so the woman works until the end, to the fall of many from God, and I am telling you: be wise, sons, be wise by My word over you!

[/legatura_la_teme][legatura_la_teme id_capitol="7" id_subcapitol="1"]Oh, here is what I am telling you: those who have fallen asleep and are called to the table of the angels for their commemoration rejoice with great joy. The entire generation of those who have fallen asleep from the beginning of the world until today, all have come to their commemoration, together with the heavenly and angelic hosts. I, the Lord, am sharing comfort and resurrection with them, and the most beautiful host is the host of Virginia, My trumpet, and many are now looking here at the table and have great joy for those who are theirs according to the body, and who have kept the path and have not fallen from it.

The dead from the wars of these days are joining the ranks of the sleeping, for I, the Lord, am receiving from the citadel here requests for them, and their spirits are comforted by My beauty and the heavenly ones, with which I dwell with celebration and with glory of word upon the earth.[/legatura_la_teme]

Comfort, comfort, sweet and powerful word, I am placing this word over pains, over sorrows, over the love between brothers, so that all may learn from Me the spirit of tenderness and its holy fruit, and peace to you at the fraternal table, My sons! Oh, take comfort and be like the angels of God, My sons!

[legatura_la_teme id_capitol="5" id_subcapitol="6"]And now, again and again I give you blessing, power and support from heaven and earth for the new work at Maluri village. Be of one heart, all the few whom I, the Lord, rely on for the holy work for the manifestation of the garden of paradise over the place where I, the Lord, descended seventy years ago and stood on the stone of the house and began through My trumpet Virginia the glory of My word over the earth for years in a row, after I had prepared her for a long time to hold Me and to pass through her with the word of My mouth, and which have come little by little to the manifestation and will gather together the souls of the love of God, the souls who are waiting, sons, and you ask the Father to support your labor with favorable weather for the work there, oh, but do not fear the cold, do not fear anything, for everything comes and settles beautifully and with great glory, and I am at the helm, I am next to you and I have taught you this day to keep Me next to you and not to forget this throughout the time of your work with Me for My glory with you sons.[/legatura_la_teme]

Oh, I have work with My angels to quiet the tongues of those who are shaking the faith of some of those who have joined and have not been careful not to take advice from the serpent for their faith and obedience. We will seek to pluck from the fire those who will accept Me to advise them, so that they may learn to have a nurse for themselves, for those who are born according to the flesh are taking down each other like Adam and Eve, losing the Lord, the paradise and faith; however, those born according to the spirit have a nurse and grow for Me, remain Mine and will be counted among those who will share the great joy when rest comes, after My work with you will end in glory and we will offer it to the Father, from Whom we will receive the prize of running, the consolation, the great and much consolation, oh, sons. Amen, amen, amen.
