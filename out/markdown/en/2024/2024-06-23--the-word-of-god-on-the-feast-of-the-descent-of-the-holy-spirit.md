It is the heavenly glory in the celebration of the Holy Spirit above the Garden of My Meeting with you, sons of the citadel of My word in the middle of the Romanian land. The feast of the descent of the Holy Spirit is bringing all heaven here to you today, and I, the Lord, am sitting on the clouds of cherubim as on the throne of the glory of the Father and the Son and the Holy Spirit, and great is the work with which I am working on earth supported by you, those who gather in My book the voice of My word over you and share My coming from the Father and announce on earth the word with which I feed you so that you may be and remain Mine, and with which, behold, I divide and shepherd with the power of word kings and subjects, righteous and sinners, believers and unbelievers, for the time has come for My return from the Father to earth as word under My new name, the Word of God, as it is written, **(Apoc: 19/13)** as the Father gave Me to work in the beginning and I made then heaven and earth and man and all creation, and even My coming now, I had made it all at the beginning, and all are waiting in line for their work.

Now, we place in the book the greeting of the meeting of heaven and earth here, We who are in heaven, and you, from earth, preparing My bed, celebrate, and behold, peace to you, My sons, and I am finding you watching, working and preparing the bed, so that We may sit down!

The dawn of the Pentecost holiday has appeared in the sky, and you have not retired to rest with your body, My sons, oh, nor have My servant angels with you, and therefore, I am now clothing you in freshness of spirit and body and we are proceeding to receive the guests: the heavenly retinues in groups-groups, and then those close to Me and you on earth, those who fulfill My word of preparation for the life of paradise on earth, and we will sit down and sound the gong of the holiday, oh, My sons.

How beautiful this day will be with all the preparation for it and for Us, for you and sanctification of the fountain of the Mother of God, the new fountain of the Garden of Meeting, and which will receive power from My Virgin mother and distribute comforts and victory over evil spirits, so that they do not come near here, for here the Lord is Master over the citadel and distributes His glory over the earth, whenever He leaves His word of glory over the garden here, oh, sons.

And now, peace to you, male and female disciples with whom I spent two thousand years ago, until I clothed you in the Holy Spirit, the Comforter, so that you would then be My witnesses from edge to edge! Oh, peace to you together with My disciples here today, for the service of My word over the earth, and which goes like lightning from one side to the other, *(Via the internet, r.n.)* as it is written about My coming today!

Here we are with a festive spirit! I have no more now than then to accompany Me on great days to holy gatherings. We and they are meeting now, under the grace of the feast of Pentecost this year. It is unfathomable joy to the human mind, it is great joy for Us, Who have come from the unseen of God, and We now give some of it to those gathered at the table of the Holy Spirit here, where the Lord with His people from heaven is drawing near for a resting place.

Oh, peace to you and the Holy Spirit, peace and comfort to Us and to you from one to another, sons of the citadel of My word, and behold, We are bowing down and receiving from you, what you have prepared, so that you may go before Us and comfort each other, to the sons with whom I am speaking on earth today.

Oh, Son Jesus, glorify Yourself in them and through them, for Your glory with them is great, and it is not seen, and therefore it is great. Everything that is seen pales before Your invisible glory, before Your word's walking on earth, and we, those in heaven, have eyes from above and see everything that is to be kept secret, and that is why those who believe Your word and all that is under its secret are so great, and that is why are so unhappy, those who believe only what they see, only what is seen, who enslave people and keep them so small, so weak, so unfulfilled, and are to be pitied those who believe so hard in the things that are not seen, and wait at the border to show themselves and give themselves to those who deserve them and believe without a doubt in Your word, heard and proclaimed, and they will share in the comfort of the Holy Spirit, the gift for the faithful, the gift that secretly clothes the sons of God on earth, my dear Son Jesus.

— Oh, My mother, from among the disciples celebrated by the Holy Spirit given to them then, you left your loving word over today's feast. Now, they are also written in the book next to Us on this day and they are giving their gifts to Our little ones today, their speech with them, My mother.

— We are blessing You, Lord, and confessing You with Your work on earth through Your disciples today, with whom You share Your power through the word and its fulfillment afterwards, oh, and how beautiful is Your work today with them, and with what power Your word goes from edge to edge like lightning, as it is written, and only those in heaven see the mysteries of Your word from then and today, for they have the whole vision, not partly, as those on earth have, Lord.

Oh, You were taking us with You everywhere and we were Your witnesses, even though at the time we did not understand what You were doing with us. There were twelve of us as witnesses, and sometimes You took only three of us for Your mysteries. At the supper table of Your word, You have here now those who are under obedience, who no longer have their wills and their works, and who do not set aside Your teachings for them, and they are under obedience of paradise, and You set them aside, and the time of Your waiting was very long and You kept asking everyone to stay aside and on Your side, oh, and Your desire to have them as Yours and only Yours did not take hold of everyone.

Therefore, Your word today, so full of power, protects under it Your bearers, those to whom You come and share Your word throughout the earth, Lord.

Oh, glory to You! Your coming is great and many and very glorious. Glory to You, Who shepherds Your faithful people to the Father, O Lord!

— I am coming and I keep coming, as word upon the earth, I am coming, for I promised through the prophets that I would come and take away the sheep from the hands of the shepherds who do not shepherd them, and I would shepherd them Myself, and this I am fulfilling and doing now.

I am comforting Myself with all those in heaven now under this feast-prepared bed by you, My disciples today, and be faithful and very faithful, sons, for the ornaments that are not seen, and that are now placed by Me over this bed at the feast of the Holy Spirit, these ornaments are part of those that do not rise to the human mind, and prepared by Me for those who love Me and desire Me with them and for them, oh, and it is so beautiful here with you, so beautiful, sons, and you should believe the word that I am telling you, and rejoice that many unseen things are being prepared and that await their discovery, oh, sons![/legatura_la_teme]

Oh, peace to you now, and peace to the brothers gathered with you in the garden, sons!
