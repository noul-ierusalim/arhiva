… Virginia, Virginia, make yourself a roll of paper and write all My work for Me on it, the work I have done so far in the ten years of prophecy, and what is going to be done from now on. Write down all the visions seen with your own eyes, write down all My words heard with your own ears, and write down the journey you made with Me on the earth, in the air and up in heavens. Write on the roll of paper about the way I, God, come down in your body and speak. Write about all I have prophesied from the beginning until today, because they have for-gotten what God told them ten years ago. They have also forgotten what God told them a year ago. Virginia, make a roll, bind it well and keep it hidden until the day of fast, because hungry and thirsty nations will come to your door and they will knock like beggars to give them food. And then, you will wake up like a rich man, and, being urged by the Holy Spirit, you will feed them.
