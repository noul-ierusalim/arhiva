[legatura_la_teme id_capitol="7" id_subcapitol="3"]… I am the Son of God. Amen. I have come from the holy heaven on earth. I am the Alpha and the Omega, Who wrote the Holy Scripture. I am invisible; this is written. Oh, son, you will be blessed in your spiritual life, if you believe in the Lord, Jesus Christ, Who has been speaking to you today, for the Lord, Jesus Christ is in this body. What a wonder! Do you see, Christian, that the Lord is speaking with you? Do you really see it? There is still a little while and He will be seen before you and you will marvel at this wonder. You will see how a light has kept this body.[/legatura_la_teme]
