Sons, what does this work call itself? It is called the house of God’s mercy. I brought it to you and it is a God’s heavenly meal. Sons, at the beginning I worked many miracles but today I am in the Spirit and keep in My hand this end. Keep well, Christian, into your hands the life without the death, for I will ask your life from you. This work is called the house of the God’s royal feast. Behold, when you come, Christian, at this meal, you eat God’s feast, and this is what you are to say: *„Thank you Father for you made me, the unworthy, worthy to come into your house and eat this spiritual meal.”* Oh, sons, you leave this place for your home and forgive everything. You come the second time, the third time, and it is not known that you have eaten from My feast. My children, if you do this way, you will be taken out of this place, and the blind, that do not see, will come and I will make My feast with them. This work is called the God’s altar, which is put between two stones. This work will shine as the sun. This will be a ship on the sea and it will last until the end, and God will say: *„Come, you and your house, into the ark, but take off your shoes.”*

Sons, all from the monasteries do not bow down to this heavenly work. It is hard to make small someone who is great.

Sons, it will be no more death, and many of you will pass on foot this life, but keep away from this kind of sleep, as it is not for garbage that I build you; I did not built you, so that you put on weight, on this earth.

… Christian who is to pray for this world? When the world sees that are your mocked by the world, no one will give you a share too.

Christian, come back to God, for the day of Passover is coming, so that we may have the Lord’s Passover. This work is called the immortal flower, and if it is listened to, it will grow into a big flower.

Son, I dressed again in the Spirit to tell those who are left, on the last day, these big words. You, Christian, will be blessed, that at the end age, it will be put on you the seal of the Holy Spirit.

Here, what My mother is teaching you: *„Son, when you get up from your bed, get up on your knees! Bow down to the earth three times and then say this way:* «I thank you, Lord, that you woke me up and gave me to see the light of the day. I thank you that you sent your angel to protect me this night». *Son, make after this the morning prayer!”*
