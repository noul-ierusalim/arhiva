Oh, Israel, people that believes fulfilling My word, which is coming on the clouds with Me to you! Oh, son, My resurrected body, the one without death will appear soon, soon as the Scriptures say. [legatura_la_teme id_capitol="3" id_subcapitol="4"]Sons, sons, the man would have lived to take out many words from the Scriptures, but God has not allowed him to do everything he wants. Sons, you should know that the man of today is upset because he did not take the book of John out of the Scripture, the last one. The man is upset that he did not hide this book. This is what he wanted to do but he was afraid of the word, which binds the man not to touch and spoil this book, for this is what the prophet says: *«If anyone adds something to this book, the Lord will add to him the plagues which are written in this book, and if someone takes away from the words of it, the Lord will take away his part from the tree of life, and out of the holy city, which are written in this book».*

The man is upset that he did not hide this book but the word, which binds, has remained strong over it.[/legatura_la_teme] Sons, sons, this is what is written into this book: *«Amen, amen, I come soon. Amen, amen, come Lord Jesus!»* Do you see, sons, the spirit of the bride, which responds: *«Come Lord Jesus!»* and this word of the Holy Spirit has to be fulfilled and spoken by the bride who will meet My resurrected body, which soon, soon will come and have a bride, [legatura_la_teme id_capitol="4" id_subcapitol="1"]for My Father sent Me on earth to prepare My bride, because at My birth He also made a bride for Me, He gave birth to a bride for Me, and behold, I take My bride out of the one born on earth once with My birth among the people, out of Romania. I have been wooing her for two thousand years and no one among the people has known this mystery of the Father and of the Son and of the Holy Spirit. The Romania’s heaven is full of holy people. Each country has its own heaven of saints, but Romania has saints named by God by election, by the birth once with My birth. Romania has the name of a girl, the name of a bride.[/legatura_la_teme]
