… I am the Word of God. Amen. I am the Son of God, the Father, and I am in the word. Amen. I am in the Father and the Father is in Me. I am the Father, the Son and the Holy Spirit. Amen, amen, amen.

It is written into the Scripture about the mystery of the ages, which has elapsed from My visible coming among people, from My birth among men. It is written into the Scriptures that I will come and I will be called the Word of God. **(Apoc: 19/13)** Amen. It is written into the Scriptures that I will have a people that will stand before Me at My second coming.

Peace to you, My people called at My preparation for My coming of nowadays! Peace to you, son Israel, for My people is called Israel, and he has been called like that since Abraham and forever and ever. Peace to you, My people, and stay under the tent of preparation, son Israel; and stay well, dear child, for the little crowns of bride are not different from one another. Peace to you, My bride, My people bride! The bride has got white garment and that is why I gave you birth from heaven and word, My bride people, in order to be a clean bride before My coming.

I am coming down as word. I am becoming word in the garden. I am becoming feast of celebration and food on My table with Israel, I am at the wedding in a day of feast, and I am with the heavenly guests at the feast. It is written that I will send Elijah, the former prophet before My second coming to turn the sons to their parents, and the parents to the sons, to spare the man who turns his face to God. Prophet Elijah is looking with Me at you, My today’s people, and he is shaking himself upon you with the spirit of his zeal for God. He had nothing on earth but great love and zeal for Me and for My kingdom in man, in Israel, who was My people. His sacrifice of heavenly priest on earth had great power, for great is the mystery of God’s priest. What was his sacrifice that he brought to Me, well Israel? His sacrifice, which burnt before Me, was his prayer, which made fire from heaven come down as a burnt offering. His prayer burnt on his altar, a burnt offering, and his prayer became the fire that burnt on his altar, which consumed everything that was put before Me as a sign of the fulfillment of his prayer and his faith in the true God.

Oh, My people, you shall not be afraid of the today’s false prophets; do not be afraid of them. [legatura_la_teme id_capitol="1" id_subcapitol="6"]Prophet Elijah is upon you and he works zeal in you; he works more than he worked then over Israel. His zeal had great power and his faith overcame the lying prophets. Elijah was God’s only prophet. There were many prophets around him; there were four hundred, over four hundred and still other four hundred; he was the only one prophet before Me and in My name in Israel, and he prevailed against kings and prophets, and My work within him was great, and the power of his prayer was also great. It was not the calf that was put on his altar but his prayer counted as a burnt offering before Me. The altar of the false prophets also had a calf, but I do not want the burnt offerings, rather, I want a spirit of faith, a spirit of prayer, a spirit of man’s work with Me, the only true God among gods. That is why I told Israel: *«Israel, you shall not have other gods beside Me».* **(Exodus: 20/3)** [/legatura_la_teme]And in the same way I am speaking today, and I am feeding you, My people, with truth from Me, to be My truth among people, for people, son, have many gods, but you are to work, sons, within the spirit and power of Elijah; you shall work and keep working so that every man may know that there is no other God besides Me. I have always urged you to a spirit of prayer. I have always come to you and exhorted you not to have other gods besides Me so that your prayer may be fruitful, son from Israel, in order to bring forth fruit as Elijah’s prayer did, who brought down fire from heaven, as a sign of the true God.

Oh, My people, I am in a celebration with you and we honor the prophet Elijah and his prayer. The people were extremely hated before Me, and the prophet Elijah wanted to turn them with their face to Me, so that I may give beauty to the face of the people. I often sneak among you, My sons, and I come with the mysteries of My word, with the parables of the life, as I have a way through the middle of Israel. The man is greatly detested before Me, and no one takes a look into his mirror to see how ugly he is. Every man looks into the mirror, but he looks only to see how beautiful is, to see himself the most beautiful possible, but no one looks to see how ugly he is before Me. The man looks into the mirror to see how good he looks like with his gods, with his pleasures, with his kingdom, and the man does no longer serve the true God. Only false gods everywhere; only lying upon the man, and [legatura_la_teme id_capitol="1" id_subcapitol="6"]the prophet Elijah goes down on the earth and always demolishes the Baal’s altars, and Baal with his sons raises other altars and the smoke on their altars choke those from heaven and those from earth who are from heaven, and no one looks into the mirror any more to see how ugly he is before Me. [/legatura_la_teme]

Israel, beautiful child, you should be beautiful, as that is why I call you beautiful child, to be beautiful before Me. I am the example of the New Man, Who came down from heaven, so that every man, who wants to be from heaven, may look at Me; the man, who wants to be as beautiful and clean as the heaven. I am your mirror, Israel, beautiful child, child adorned with the gifts of the Holy Spirit that came to you with Me. You should not look, beautiful child; you should look neither with your thought nor with your eyes to those dyed gods. You should not look for you spoil your beauty if you look; you spoil your looking and its sight if you do look; you spoil your godly nature that I have always have on you; you spoil it, if you look at the dyed time of today. You should be a clean child, a child refreshed by the Holy Spirit, and you should stay within the Holy Spirit, as no one stays any longer within the Holy Spirit before Me today.

… The whole world is under the burden of sin and from heaven I also give help, calling and path for every man who wants to find the way. The dead sin catches life everywhere. The living sin keeps many souls in its life and there is no one to teach the man. The man of the church hid himself from My word that flows from My throne over to the earth. The man of the church rakes up; he rakes up with its torches to see if someone from his church rises to stand in today’s mystery of the living church, which comes with My word over to the world. Like the Jews of old, who were searching out those who believed in Christ secretly, the same is today; the man of the church is searching out in his house to throw out those who are going towards the work of the New Jerusalem over the church. The man of the church does not want to go for the work of new man, of Edenic man. The man of the church from the world worships all kinds of gods and he does not rise to holiness in the church. He has left away the true way, and the man of the church has become a Pharisee and has not risen to repentance. I have always called him out but he does not get up, because he cannot do it anymore; he cannot, because he is afraid of holiness; he cannot, because he has no fear of God. The man of the church is careless and he lives like the world and even worse than the world, because he also hides under a holy garment and the man does no longer know where the way is.

My coming in the word, My work of preparation has caught them all off guard, as in the time of Noah, and even today, no one believes Noah, as in that time. However, I have come and left room and time for grace, as at that time, as in the time of Noah. It is the time of grace but the man of the church from the world makes smoke so that God may not be seen in the work of the New Jerusalem, coming out of the midst of the church, coming out as fire to consume everything that is dry and spoiled, and that the living waters, and the waters forever living may make way before Me. The man with a sinful life does not love My word and the life that comes from it, and that is why the man of today’s church cannot stand up to be pleased to Me, to bring repentance before Me.

… The man wants meat and he does not want the heaven. The man says that Elijah the prophet ate meat and went into heaven. How about you, man, who say so? You do not know where he ate meat from and what kind of meat Elijah ate through My ravens. The man wanders away with the Scriptures in his hands and he does not look for salvation in them, he does not seek to take a good look into the mirror. I want to give his life back to the man and the man does not seek after Me, but I have come out to meet him, and you should stay, My people, you should stay well in your work with Me, for I have to fulfill, son, with you, the last of the Scriptures. Israel, Israel, you shall believe son, into the Scriptures, for I have added to you nothing foreign by My fresh teaching, nothing except those that are written in the Scriptures.
