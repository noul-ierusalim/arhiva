I embrace you with My word, people of My coming. And you should also embrace Me in the same way, for you have to learn from Me everything I do before you. Amen, amen, amen.

Oh, My people of today, you should learn well My signs that are upon you and you should not desire for another sign to stay with Me. In the time of Israel in Egypt, I came to him so that he might do what I did, but he did not do what I did. I had come to him, but he did not come to Me, and after My great labor for him we separated.

Oh, My people, I embrace you, son, by My word. It is My sign upon you that you may not forget Me and that you may not forget God, My people. Amen. The mind of the man is being given to those that are seen, and I have always taken care of the man in order to turn him to Me, as I have taken care of Israel when he has been My people. I told him through the prophets: *«The Lord will give you a sign: behold, the virgin will conceive, and bear a son, and He will be God and will be with the people».* Amen.

Oh, My people, I have a feast for Me into your midst, for you are My church, and I am your head to God. I embrace you with My word and you are to learn to do what I do. You should take after Me for everything you do, for I stay as a sign before you to teach you. Amen.

When I came as a child on the earth, My mother took Me in the church and by taking Me there she confessed Me, for the old Simeon stood up because of the waiting of the heavenly sign, which he found written into the Scriptures, and he received Me and embraced Me with the word, saying: *«Now You are releasing Your servant, Master, according to Your word, in peace, for my eyes have seen Your salvation, which You have prepared before the face of all peoples; a light for revelation to the Gentiles, and the glory of Your people of Your people Israel»*. Then I released the one who confessed Me on the earth and then he went and confessed to those who were in the dwelling of the dead, that he saw My salvation for the peoples and that it came near, because the Lord had come down on the earth. Amen.

[legatura_la_teme id_capitol="8" id_subcapitol="5"]My salvation has never been as close as now, My people. I have never come on the earth more than now. Son, your salvation is close. I come back soon, soon with everything on the earth, and I want to find you waiting for Me, My people, and I want that we may prepare a great salvation over the people and I want to bring the heaven down to the earth, as heir forever, the same as I put the Eden for the man on the earth in the beginning. [/legatura_la_teme]But the man did not listen to My sign; the man did not do as I told him, and the Paradise remained without man. If I did not create the man, I would have also not created the Paradise at the man’s sight, and because the man did not listen to My sign, I took the Paradise and put under the Cherubim, since the man did not keep its law from eternity.

It is easy for the man to forgive God, to keep the Lord aside, but I have taken care to make the man remember Me in his wandering way. When I saw Saul that he was departing from his way, I came into his way and made a sign in his body so that he might remember Me then and that he might never forget Me afterwards. He was praying to Me to take away his sign from him, but I told him that it was enough what I gave him and that I did not have to give him or to ask from Me something else. I gave him grace. And how did I give him grace? I made him know Me sufficiently and that he might never forget Me then and this means grace upon man. Amen.

A great many of saints who loved Me with their soul and who could not bear the pain to leave Me or to forget Me, made themselves signs on them, rough skin, invisible chains and links, ascetic life and tears of humiliation, and in this way they stood in bondage with their mind and their hearts from Me, and they made signs for themselves so that they might not forgive Me. Moses had a sign in his way with Israel through the wilderness: the pillar of fire, the cloud and the manna. Oh, and with all these Israel forgot Me! He forgot Me for his sins and for his occupations. I put Moses as a sign before him and then the pillar of fire, the cloud and the manna, and however, that people still gave Me away from him.

The man is too much accustomed to forgetting God. The man is too learned to forgive Me. That is why the saints were helping each other with what they could so that they might not forgive Me, for the man’s thought is conforming to those that are seen, to those that serve the man, to those that the man serve for him.

Oh, My people, which is the sign that I have put before you, so that you may not forgive Me? This word is the sign. I put this sign again and again before you and I ask you to do what I do. If I embrace you with My word, you should do the same to Me, so that we may be right to each other, so that it may be seen that we look at each other and so that it may be seen that we want each other, My people. You know it well that [legatura_la_teme id_capitol="1" id_subcapitol="3"]the man built by My hand did not want Me and then he forgot Me and he remained only within himself, and I have been hurt by him since then and until today. I made him with longing when I created him, to be comforted with him, I and with My Father and all the angels. I made him and then I brought the Paradise from eternity and put it before him on the earth, already full, already adorned in the day when I made the man. Then I brought the man into Paradise and I gave him the entire Paradise, from which I took for Me what belonged to Me and to My Father. However, he did not take this sign into consideration and violated it instead, and then I had no rest with the man, and if I had not rest, I took him out of the Paradise.

I made everything in six days, and then I rested in one day; I rested with the man, for I rested in him. Then the man violated the law of the Paradise and I did no longer come to the day of rest, because of the man’s fall from Paradise, from the law of the Paradise. The man fell by not taking into consideration My sign, for in his sixth day he trespassed My word and got out of the Paradise and was set on the earth to work the land from which he was taken, and I, the Lord, the man’s Creator, had no longer reached the day of rest with the man, but rather it was the day of sorrow in which I still stay; I stay and I always put signs into the man’s way to turn him back to Me in his heart, so that the man may remember Me, for it is easy for the man to forget Me, My people. [/legatura_la_teme]Oh, he who has love with longing in it for Me does not need any sign in his way so that he may not forgive Me, for that one has as a sign the love, which burns in flames in him after Me, and its fire keeps a man like that one with Me.

My love for you always becomes a sign into your way, My people. It becomes word from Me, from My love, which burns after you, for My heart has been sorrowed for seven thousand years after the man. Oh, My people, you should not be in need for another sign to stay with Me, so that you may do like Me. Behold, I always embrace you with the word, and you should do the same with Me, in such a way that we may be right to each other, My people, so that it may be seen that we do not forget each other and that we want each other, My people. Oh, you know better than anyone on the earth that the man created by My hand did not want Me as I told him, and then he forgot Me and remained only in his own self. However, you are the man built by My Spirit, and you should be able to do what I have asked from you, and you should be, because I built you and I build you of My Spirit, My people, and He is My sign before you. Those, who have seen My signs from beginning to end, have forgotten Me and have withdrawn within themselves and have forgotten Me, but you have My Spirit as your sign, the Spirit of your Lord, Jesus Christ, Who stays within you in a mysterious manner and shepherds you, so that you may not forget Him and so that you may not forget God, My people.

[legatura_la_teme id_capitol="6" id_subcapitol="2"]Oh, son from the end, behold what a sign I give you: when your love will burn fully in your inside and outside, moment by moment, then you will see Me that I have come, and I will let Myself be revealed for the whole peoples, and then we will shine on this mountain more than any other brightness of Mine which I have ever released on the earth among the people, and then, son, I will take My day of rest back, I and with My Father will take it from your hand, for you are the man who is built by My Spirit, by My Spirit in the end, by the Spirit of My coming at the end of the time. Amen. The heaven and the earth have been waiting for this end of the time for seven thousand years to take back again their first brightness, their eternity, My people.

I have put you to work near Me and near My Father. I have put you near Me to work out the spirit of the new creation of the world, of the new heaven and of the new earth. I have put you a sign among the nations by My sign upon you, for this is how I have been well pleased, My people. And I ask you with the whole longing and with My whole Spirit to get well through My speaking upon you and stand well before this sign in such a way that it might pierce you and to be enough for you, so that we may not forget each other, because I have always, always remind you about Me and about My day of rest from the beginning, so that I may join it to the one in the end, and that I have desired with tears and sigh in Me and in My Father, My people, the people of the Spirit of the Lord. The day of My coming is with you. This is what this sign means, and the sign is the word, which flows over you of My mouth. Amen. All of the peoples will fully hear, soon, soon, that My Spirit is resting upon you working and that I have come in order to come. Amen. And as for you, My people, you should become My little boat so that I may be able to pass in it together with those that are not seen into those that are seen and to pass everyone from those that are seen into those that are not seen now, for all these wait to pass into view, so that they may be afterwards. Amen.

I wish you a burning longing, My people, so that I may come into the little boat and to reveal Myself perfectly with you and you with Me. And I tell you now with a burning desire: The sooner I come, the sooner I want to finish everything and to come to give you the reward of your faith and of your waiting, and especially to give you love for your love, My people. Amen, amen, amen.[/legatura_la_teme]
