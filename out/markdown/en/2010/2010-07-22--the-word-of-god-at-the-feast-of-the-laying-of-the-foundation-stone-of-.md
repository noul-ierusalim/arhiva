[legatura_la_teme id_capitol="5" id_subcapitol="3"]I am writing Myself into My book of today of the memorial of My victory, when I, the Lord, had power to lay down, with witnesses in heaven and on earth, the stone of foundation of the renewal of those what have been spoiled by the people for almost two thousand years, the laying on its stone of My first church, as it was founded by Me through My apostles and then, through them time after time, until the man from the outside has interfered and shaken My foundation, and he has done it as he wanted and not after God’s will, and for such a long time, I have been suffering until now when I have laid on earth what it was written to be, before the man of lawlessness to wipe out completely My traces from among people, My face, through the church according to My will before Me.[/legatura_la_teme]

I am writing Myself into the book with the day of the new beginning, on which I have got now the work of My word and My new people that listens to My advice, for to [legatura_la_teme id_capitol="6" id_subcapitol="3"]those who have to be Mine and with Me, I said: *«Come out of the world, My people, that you may have no participation in her wine, and that you don’t receive of her plagues».* **(See Apoc: 18/4)** And then I have said again in the time of My word of today that My people in the end will be separated and distinguished from the whole world on earth and they will stay with Me and will serve Me, and I will take care of them and through them I will preach My Gospel by the river of My word over the earth and I will go with it, running, and I will feed from place to place those that will believe and be baptized in this river, to their adoption by God, to be God’s sons into the midst of a deceptive people, which does not want to know from Me how one lives with the Lord on earth, for those who learn the Lord from the people, behold their face, which does not take after Me on earth, after the church which I established two thousand years ago through My disciples, and which only within a great mystery has been able to live with My face among people!

Oh, many tried and still try to come together around My miracles, that even their heart beats for one moment or two or three, but there is another miracle, for I have counted the people that I remain with, not those who seek after My miracles. This is how I worked two thousand years ago too, when thousands were following Me, but by every attempt I had made to draw them to the Father, I only remained with two or three of them.[/legatura_la_teme]

[legatura_la_teme id_capitol="5" id_subcapitol="3"]I am comforted by that day when I had the power to lay down the foundation of the mystery of My new beginning of holy church, in which, I, the Lord, to be able to work wholly, body and word, and from the midst of this mystery to come out as word over the earth, but this memory also hurts Me, and the pain is My comfort. The pain has been My comfort during all the time of the heaven and the earth, and the pain has also been the comfort of those who have joined their life in with Mine either. There has not been a more beautiful face on earth, a purer smile and a clearer look than that of those who bear in them the pain after God, the pain, in all the time of deception, for the life is that which is in heaven, not that on earth as the man may believe, the way man is enchanted for the life he makes on earth; moreover, I have also spoken into My word about the most beautiful people by My pain in them. The earth is the way on which the Lord appears to man, who does not know to come back to his house, but whoever does not have eyes to see, then he has got them for something else, if he has still got them, and that is why a man like that gets enchanted by his life on earth.[/legatura_la_teme]

[legatura_la_teme id_capitol="8" id_subcapitol="5"]Oh, I, the Lord, am comforting you, those who have remained with Me in all your trials, in all My pains and yours for Me, and I give you My kingdom on earth with you and help you in everything and in all things, to be Mine and that I may be your God the way I have to be for those who are with Me, and not as man believes, who comes and knows Me how I am here with you and then he leaves and says to himself that God is with him in every way he may choose to walk on earth then,[/legatura_la_teme] after I have always advised all, lest, by their not obeying Me, to become My enemies and then to turn their back to Me jumping out of My little boat. Oh, this is how they think, those who do not have My pain and have theirs instead, but those who love Me, do not longer think about themselves, but rather they are good and serve Me according to My will, and day by day they grow in the wisdom of My mysteries with man, and their faith gives forth fruit to God, saved souls from the great sea, in which the whole world drowns and it does no longer reach out its hand to come out at the shore, so that it may also see the light.

[legatura_la_teme id_capitol="5" id_subcapitol="3"]I am receiving you into My Spirit on this day and I am comforting you with My pain, those who have remained faithful to My coming of today; those, with whom, nineteen years ago, I started My mysterious work of the church, according to My will on earth, being body and word on its table, as I wanted and I have not been able to have by this time, because I have no longer found faith on earth for My holy walking with man, and not as he has been trying to walk and to call himself Mine. I am full of comfort in My pain because I have you, those who have remained with Me in all My trials. Be, therefore, My protected ones, and protect My people from unbelief and disobedience and from the lack of humility and steadfastness, and then from joys that lose the man![/legatura_la_teme] Be watchful at the mast and keep the relation between you and Me for those that are in My little boat and for those on earth, who still try to give ear to the news that I breathe upon you to guide them too, for the waves are raging and those in the midst of the waves lose their courage. However, I call out to them My exhortation of life and I give them to know how to walk, and blessed are and will be those who come to listen from God in this time of darkness as there has never been on earth!

[legatura_la_teme id_capitol="5" id_subcapitol="6"]Behold, I have into the mist of the people My bed, on which I, the Lord, am sitting and speaking into the midst of a people of disciples and I am dwelling with them, and there is a prophecy into the Scriptures about My mystery now on earth. *«Behold God’s dwelling is with people, and He will dwell with them, and they will be His people, and God Himself will be with them as their God».* **(Apoc: 21/3)** This is the revelation from the Scriptures about the mysterious citadel of the New Jerusalem, which is coming with the Lord on earth in a great mystery and which does not come to the mind of the man with a short view. However, I am the mysterious One, and I work with power and I stand in the way of temptation and watch with those in heaven on earth to keep those who become God’s sons, to whom I have given the power to believe and not to perish as those who are unfaithful.[/legatura_la_teme]

I set My love with a flame in you in a great measure, watching sons in the gates. Oh, stand before Me for My coming, for I have great work to do! I am pouring Myself out in you with the fire of My love and with its burning flame, and from you I also want to pour Myself out like that in all the sons who are My people at the spring so that they may also have fire in their little hearts for Me and that I may work through them for you and for My works, well, well, children sons. All those that have broken you down and made you grow weak in your watch, in you power, in your work, in your endurance, in your hope, and in all those things that I have put in you, let them deeply and wholly repent and let them become My helpers, so that I may wipe out their things by which they have pressed upon My heavy yoke upon you, and which were supposed to be really easy. I am also giving them My love, which is to put out entirely from them the love for themselves and to become a flame of the Holy Spirit, with which to welcome those who come to appear before the Lord and to be resurrected from their sins, for the man’s body is of no use but only to sin.

[legatura_la_teme id_capitol="5" id_subcapitol="6"]We are at the holy work for the building and adorning of the mound in the hill, which, I, the Lord, by your hands and the hands of the least of My people, put the pearls prophesied to be for the adorning of the necklace of the little garden of My word, whose spirit has been sighing for a long time, as My Spirit, but My patience will give it the joy meant for it, for I am busy working and the angels and the saints are its crown.

I am ordaining a fresh blessing for the whole work of the mound in the hill and of its establishments and over the little sons, whom I want to be obedient, humble and full of hope in Me, for I am the One Who can, and I can work through those who are obedient and gentle, who prove out their faith, its works and the purity of their heart.[/legatura_la_teme]

[legatura_la_teme id_capitol="6" id_subcapitol="3"]Oh, people from the spring, read in the Scripture your life and its work between Me and you, between brother and brother, and fulfill in this way, son, as it is not good if you do not fulfill in this way. I, the Lord, fill with blessing the power of your body and soul, but you should be one; this is how My people is always supposed to be, and it should be careful too, so that I may look at it and be pleased with it. Amen.

Peace to you, in a day of holy remembrance, My people! I give you heavenly progress in your entire work, son, so that We may establish My Father’s entire work on earth, you and I. Be one, sons! Be one in everything and always, and all of you keep in step with everything, all to the left and all to the right, and be always like that for the Lord can do it, sons, and He can do it in those who are little, who let themselves into His hand and under His power.

I can do sons; I can work in those who can. Be faithful! I can! Amen, amen, amen.[/legatura_la_teme]
