The Holy Spirit, the Comforter, is the comfort, the power and the protection from God over those who are faithful to Him and who work for Him between heaven and earth. I let the comfort of the Holy Spirit come over the people of My word of today and I am putting on My table with it the word of feast with saints. [legatura_la_teme id_capitol="2" id_subcapitol="5"]Because the Romanian people has been left behind concerning the correction of those shaken from their place by the enemies of the faith of their forefathers and saints, then I, the Lord, wait for it to stand up strongly and to put back at their place the feasts of My saints, and they are not supposed to tell to themselves that it is not a sin to remain so as God’s enemies did upon it, oh, it is not supposed to say so, but they need to bring back those that were shaken, as it is not good to spoil those left by the saints and fathers, and the houses that have not been reinforced by the parents do not help the sons for blessing to long for them on earth, and behold, when the world celebrates into the name of My birth of the Virgin mother a day of holy feast, I, with My saints and with those who are obedient and faithful on earth, set the feast of saints, and we celebrate at our table the bishop Spyridon, the one full of the spirit of humility and love, a man of forgiveness and of miracle making, and when the day of feast of the celebration of My birth is coming, then We are going to write Ourselves into the book with its memorial in heaven and on earth. Amen.[/legatura_la_teme]

[legatura_la_teme id_capitol="6" id_subcapitol="3"]Oh, My people, I bring you comfort, as you need much comfort now. May the Holy Spirit, the Comforter, strengthen you on My way with you, My people! May He always pave My way to you from heaven to earth, and may He strengthen you on the way with Me, for the time is with endurance in it, and it is with faith-denial as well, because for a while I keep telling you, and it is with all kinds of trials, but you should stay in the Holy Spirit, son, so that He may be able to stand into your midst as the word from God and that it may lead you and teach you to speak in His language in all your speaking, for there is no other speaking from God, but from man, My people. Oh, remember My apostles how they spoke before My ascension to the Father and asked Him to send them the Holy Spirit, how they spoke after the One I promised them came upon them, the Holy Spirit the comforter, to teach them from Me, the Spirit Who has spoken through the prophets and showed them and to the seers the things that were, those that were that those that would come and appear on earth. Oh, remember this, My people, and put on your spirit within the word of the Holy Spirit and enrich your speaking with the One Who proceeds from the Father, with the Holy Spirit, the Comforter, so that the Father may bless you on earth and that He may strengthen you as His house, for the blessing of the fathers strengthens the house of the sons, as it is written, and you should obey God, and you should not do anything without His blessing, for only His blessing strengthens your life and its work with every passing day. Remember those who were disobedient in time and be obedient every day, for each day has its work, as one day I spoke to Abraham in one way and the next day I spoke with him otherwise, *(Promising him a great people as his offspring, and then asking him for the sacrifice of his successor, r.n.)*, and he passed the test of his faith well because he listened, because he steadfastly believed in God being afraid of his omnipotence, being afraid of the punishment for his disobedience, and behold, he who obeys is praised on earth and in heaven for the work of obedience, son. Oh, you should do nothing without My word, without listening to it, and you should work only according to it and in this way you will always be blessed and you will have the reward of a disciple, as it is written from the Holy Spirit that: *«all the paths of the Lord, loving kindness and truth, mercy and faithfulness for those who seek His place and His testimonies, to such as keep His covenant and his testimonies».* **(See Ps. 25/10)** Oh, son, how beautifully the Holy Spirit, the Comforter, reveals to You the Lord’s paths when you seek Him, His place and His testimonies! He says: *«I will be gracious to whom I will be gracious, and I will harden or soften the man’s heart»*, **(Ex. 33/19)**, and the Lord is compassionate and full of faithfulness with the one who seeks after His paths and blesses the sons’ house and the sons are those who listen to Him and do nothing without the Father. I teach you, My people, the speaking of the Holy Spirit, the spirit of prophecy, son, the Spirit who prays sighingly in those who seek after the Lord’s paths, the Spirit Who prayed in David when he said: *«Remember Me, Lord, with favor that You show to Your people. Visit me with Your salvation, that I may see the prosperity of Your chosen, that I may rejoice in the gladness of Your nation, that I may glory with Your inheritance. Our fathers did not understand Your wonders and did not remember the multitude of Your loving kindness, but were rebellious, but blessed are those who keep justice and blessed is one who does what is right at all times».* **(Ps. 106/3-7)**

I teach you, My people, the work of hope and the work of love and that of forgiveness by love, so that your prayer may reach before Me, and I to fulfill. Call the saints and the prophets from Me to you, oh, call them in time of trial, and they will come on My behalf and will strengthen you. They will protect you and will bring you comfort, son.[/legatura_la_teme] I, the Lord, tell them to have eyes and ears at your calling, for you have help and comfort only from heaven for the pains that pierce you to take after Me on earth. All the trials, all the pains and all the blows which come upon My work with you because of the devil’s envy, as he does not love My walking with you, all prove that My work with you is spirit and truth, for against whom the devil and the man deceived by him has always thrown? Oh, only against Me and against all My places of rest and work among people, only there his target has been, for he is suffering more than anytime now when he sees that the fall of his pride is coming near. Oh, only pride makes the man fall, as it also made Adam fall, and then it made Lucifer fall through him, and then man retaliates, as Lucifer does, and draws the people downwards if he fell from his glory through man, and he does not want the people to be saved to take the place of his horde fallen by disobedience, for he has no longer listened to God and listened to man and thus he lost his glory from God.

Oh, My people, oh, son tested by the spirits opposing Me and you, let us stay well and let us stay with fear of God and with much attention at the word of the Holy Spirit, the Comforter, for the Lord watches for His citadel and work, son! [legatura_la_teme id_capitol="6" id_subcapitol="3"]May every son of this people be content with God, and let him stay as the Lord puts him to stay and not work, desire or choose for himself something by his own choice, for it is one thing that I ordain you how to stay and how to work and it is another thing to assume this position yourself, to take also as you see that the Lord gives, or to draw to yourself those given from the Lord to work for Him. Your heart’s desire, which does not take after the Lord, and those, which the proud one, the one who is discontent and disobedient assumes himself, those make him hide and do evil work, oh, and My blessing leaves him and then he walks in the darkness after that.

Let us be careful in the way we serve, sons, and how we receive those that put us to test for our rising or falling! Remain in the Holy Spirit, the Comforter, Who takes from what is Mine and teaches you! I have always advised you and I still advise you not to talk with the one who slanders his neighbor, because this work does not come from Me, but he does the work of the devil instead. I have always, always advised you, according to what I see that each time, which is or comes, asks for it, for the Holy Spirit sees and gives you news for the good work for the good protection and for obedience, sons.[/legatura_la_teme] This is how My bishop Spyridon was tested in the time of his work with Me on earth. He was tested and slandered every way possible, but he did great work and he made miracles before the unbelievers for the strengthening of My truth in him, for the shame of those who were doubtful and full of the black spirit of the envy of the brothers, and My faithful servant endured full of peace and serenity. And now he is speaking from Me to you, on his day of feast, My people, for I am with the saints within My coming and they are giving you their joy because they are coming with Me to you and because they are able to speak upon My people to give them of the heaven full of the reward of the faith; to give them of those gathered in heaven for those who are faithful, for those who are holy in their faith and in the work of their faith, and to give Me comfort on My hard way of coming now from the Father to man again. Amen.

*‒ And I want to give You comfort too, oh, Lord, for You suffer from heaven to earth, and You suffer, You suffer! I also give comfort to those who suffer for You and with You on earth too, here in Your garden, blessed for this time into the midst of the Romanian people. Oh, how mysteriously You work! Everything that tests Your work from the side of the new Jerusalem and this citadel, are those that cover protectively Your mystery and the work of today, the people of Your word, Lord. At all times Your wisdom has worked great salvation over the sons of faith, and those who are not faithful are not able to understand the economy of Your mysteries, those who are not steadfast in their faith, as those in my time were who put to test my endurance for You, my endurance with them, for they were full of desire for honor, full of desire for human progress on earth. This is how my bishops brothers were at that time, and they put before me all kinds of obstacles and they took all kinds of revenges against me, but my love forgave them, for this is the work of love and no other work is like it. I am speaking to the sons of Your people and telling them this:*

*Oh, sons, forgive and you will be forgiven! This is what the Lord was telling His disciples and this is how I am telling you too. Forgive those who blaspheme you and are hostile to you! Forgive those who bring pains and only pains to you! They will suffer; soon, soon, they will suffer because they have not been yours in order to remain with you, because you have not been theirs either, that they may have believed you, that they may have loved and received you with faith for their good, for the Lord wanted to do them great good through you, but they did not agree, and they rather worked doubt and a spirit of doubt, becoming spies to each other and in that way they did the work to damage the souls, their souls and the souls of those who were in one consent with them. The time is coming and has already come so that they may suffer after you, for you loved and forgave them, and they did exactly the opposite, as it happened between me and those who served at the altars in my time with them on earth, and they despised me because of my humility, for I loved and forgave them and this was how I was working with the work of love, and I was working with the Lord, forgiving and loving, loving and forgiving by love and, again and again, loving by forgiveness. Oh, be at peace in time of trial, for the Lord saves you always, just as He saved the bishop with disciples in his work, and whose disciples tried to dishonor him secretly, putting a naughty woman to blame him of the sin pleasant to the devil against the Lord’s saints, but the Lord saved the faithful one and brought into view the wickedness of the hidden disciples and their open work, and the righteous one gave them his forgiveness by his love, for those with a loving spirit of people have in their holy nature the work of forgiveness and forgetting to speak evil, sons. Oh, be good sons and ask from the Lord for His saints to protect you from heaven and from earth, for they set great work on earth for those who call them in spirit and truth in time of sufferance! Oh, get used to understanding well the mystery of those who are resurrected, for the saints do not remain in the tombs, but they are at great work for all those who are faithful and who mourn after their salvation from sin and from hardships. Be faithful! Do not come out of the blessing even for a moment, because this is dangerous! Leave all your care to the Lord! He will release you from your oppressions, for His walking with you is up to the day of His victory.*

*Oh, do not leave them under pains, Lord, for they are small! Make a sign with them for good, so that those who hate them may see and be ashamed because of their wickedness, seeing how You, Lord, comfort and help those who are faithful, those who have nothing from You but help and salvation from their hardships! Make the sons of the people understand that Your work with them is so much denigrated, so much blasphemed, so much slandered from all the sides, it is the Spirit of Truth, it is You, the One Who are not loved by those who do not love You because of their self-love! Oh, gather those who are faithful into one power of love and forgiveness and become a high mountain with them and from it, feed, Lord, the nations with the Holy Spirit, the Comforter, Who takes from what is Yours and gives to those who are faithful, as You said that You will work and fulfill, and behold, the time has come, oh, Lord! Amen.*

— Oh, bishop of humility, how great was the deed of your faith, and those who are haughty, instead of trembling before your miracles, they hardened more and more, for they were serving the devil of envy and hatred that came from him. However, you were before them the work of love and forgiveness by love, and you were full of peace, full of goodness, and full of healing for all. You went before the king, and you went clothed in the robe of your humility when he asked you to heal him of his sickness, and those, who opened for you to come in to the king and to heal him, did not know to see the bishop of the Lord in you. You went and laid your hands on him and healed him in an instant, after the gatekeepers of the kingdom denigrated you and hit you as though you were a foreigner, and then you came out humbly and gently and went to your humility, to your work for your Lord, Jesus Christ. I ordain you as a great protector for My work with My people of today and for My citadel of new Jerusalem on the Romanian land. This is how I know to reward My saints. I prepare for them a praised place in My kingdom and I put them to keep it so that it may be, and they to be its pillars and My pillars as well. Amen.

Oh, My people, those who left Me and you were half-hearted and that is why I am sighing now, and I share My sigh with you, and those who knew to believe and wanted to understand by the fear of God, like that of Abraham, in their heart, they are even today and they are the people of this work, they and not few of them who have been striking from the margins into My work starting with My trumpet Verginica3 and up to this day.

[legatura_la_teme id_capitol="6" id_subcapitol="3"]Oh, sons, be like Abraham the father of those who are faithful! Open the Scriptures and read in them about the power of his faith, about his work and about his fear of God in him for all that the Lord was asking from him, and he did as he was told lest he might be punished for disobedience, for his lack of faith, sons.[/legatura_la_teme] All My care is with you and after you. I set the saints as watchmen of My kingdom with you. Ask from the Father, from the Son and from the Holy Spirit, the victory by His work with you over the earth to the salvation of the nations which were and which are! The Holy Spirit, the Comforter, Who proceeds from the Father and holds your little hand on the way, He is the One Who counsels you in His kingdom with you. Give power to the people to be able to believe, and work with your prayer and testimony and work much with holy fast and work, for the dispelling of the work of the devil that is your enemy! Put your written prayer into a burning candle! Love forgiving and forgive loving, and do not be afraid of the blows that come from those who cannot believe! I, the Lord, am He Who works among you and speaks by the voice of the Holy Spirit, the Comforter, Who has spoken by the prophets, by those who were faithful. Amen.

Remain in My love, sons! Be My people, for My people is My love, and those who are faithful are happy and will be happy, those who remain in My love, in My beloved people, with whom I, the Lord, was and am well pleased, and by their faith I can and I work, and I will work as I did through Abraham. Amen, amen, amen.
