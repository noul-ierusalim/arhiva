Oh, sons, oh, sons, peace to you, and upon the citadel of My word, peace!

We are setting up a table of word in the citadel and those who accompany Me from among those in heaven are gathering on this middle day between the one before it and the one after it, a bundle of feast days in the middle of the citadel and over all the people of My word, the people before and now, and we are singing songs of holy victory celebration, oh sons.

[legatura_la_teme id_capitol="5" id_subcapitol="3"]On the day that passed, we have marked the day when I, the Lord, I and those from heaven who came with Me, I and they and you then marked the sealing and sanctification of the garden of My word and of the shrine placed by you in it, the white stone, written in the Scriptures, to emerge and testify over the earth that the time has come to reveal the history of My word from this time on the hearth of the Romanian nation starting from the year 1955, and the mystery of the New Jerusalem, the country with this holy name in the midst of the peoples of the earth, oh, and only you had this holy news through My revelation, only its recipient, as it is written in the Scrip-tures about this great mystery, **(Apoc: 2/172)** which will fill the earth and the nations on it with the great news, and then My walk with you will be understood through word and deed.

Oh, and I had with you on that day of December 12, thirty-two years ago, I had as a witness from the church of the Romanian people My chosen bishop, *(Irineu of Bistriţa, r.n.)*, the one an-nounced by My word that he would be My witness and servant with you for the sealing of the garden of My word and of the shrine built in it as a testimony that I, the Lord, have come by the word on earth and am working on it the kingdom of heaven in the midst of a faithful people for this fulfillment awaited for centuries by My servants from all times of heaven and earth, a heavenly mystery announced through the prophets, and all its work hidden from those who are strangers to God on earth, from those without God in them with His will in man. And here, sons, I have the photograph of that great and victorious day for God and I am looking at the time of that day and I am sighing, sons, and I am sighing with you, and I am sighing with then, with those whom I still have only in the photograph, but I am comforting Myself with you, oh, that you bore My cross patiently and did not come out from under it in times of threat upon you from the sides and from the middle, and I have reached with you great fulfillments, announced by Me throughout the time of this word through My trumpet Virginia and through her little sister afterwards, and with you I have fulfilled and I fulfill, sons, and I am going on with the fulfillments, I am going with the few with whom I am left, because I have remained with you and with those who remained with you and with those who were born on the way, oh, and no matter how few you are under the weight of God's fulfillments on earth now I can work with you because I am the One Who can and fulfill, and you are My witnesses and everything is possible through faith as in all times, the Lord is fulfilling His heavenly works and placing them on earth for the joy and comfort of the saints in heaven and on earth, for I am the Son of the Father God and I can through you, sons.[/legatura_la_teme]

Now, on this day of Saint Andrew, the second after yesterday's mentioned among the three days bundled in the feast, I, the Lord, am working the word of testimony together with the witnesses of that time with whom I came from above for the sealing of the garden of My word, that I had from those in heaven the apostle Andrew, My first called to the work of My Gospel, and I had on My trumpet Virginia, and behold a feast of patronage over the citadel of My word, for tomorrow is the day when I came forty-three years ago and took Virginia among those in heaven after years of suffering and pain under My cross with her, and hers with Me, starting from her birth on earth and up to heaven, until I took her next to Me so that we could work from heaven the work further until the end, oh, and I have you now under My cross and we are going, sons, and I am bowing down and thank you for staying and for staying with faith at the helm through great waves, for Me to go further with you, for behold, three days in one with a feast over the citadel of My word and I am sitting at the table of the word with those with whom I came thirty-two years ago to then testify from heaven the work of God on earth among the people then.

Oh, and it was on that day, sons, the hierarch of the church of the Romanian people chosen by Me for this time was with us, and together with him and you stood in holy service the three hierarchs: Basil, Gregory and John, alongside the apostle Andrew, and a heavenly and witnessing work was done through you and through Us, and every word and every deed that was placed on earth on that day was recorded in the book.

I thank you for remaining with Me as witnesses of that holy time, sons, and I also thank those who were with you from that day, and who did not remain until the end of My walk with you, for they chose their own wills afterwards. And I thank the witness bishop from then until today, I always thank him and remember him with his work next to My work from this time, for the day of crowning was the day when I, the Lord, placed the heralding sign on the earth in sight so that those on earth might find out and know the work of My word, its progress and its fulfillments afterwards, for I then built with you a citadel around the garden of My word and I have My throne with you and I rule over the earth by the word and there is no other ruler, but I alone am, but does anyone understand this besides you?

Oh, come with a table of words, My beloved ones from heaven, on days of holy remembrance, come, for the spirit of comfort is what we are working and placing in the book on this day!

[legatura_la_teme id_capitol="5" id_subcapitol="6"]Come, Virginia! You are the comfort of those in heaven, now, at the end of the age, working with Me according to the will of Our Heavenly Father. Come, all of you, with food of the word upon the earth, for the living and the dead await the word from heaven in this time of resurrection of many, for here in the citadel the work of resurrection is being done through the word of God, as it is written that resurrection and many resurrections may be fulfilled, many risings to faith, the work of a new man through faith and through holiness afterwards, for, man, you must sanctify yourself with your life in order to be with God afterwards, for behold, the Lord comes to earth with His saints. Do you hear?

*— Now, O Lord, we, those from heaven who have come with You to today's meal in the citadel here, gather in one word all, not one by one, and we pass from one to another with the word of confession and we speak alongside You, that You had us as witnesses from heaven then in the garden of Your word for its sealing and its setting aside for You and for those in heaven, for on this little patch of earth You stood on the day when You created the first man from the clay of this earth at the creation of the world. You took the clay in Your hands and worked the body of Adam and then put the spirit of life into him by Your breath upon him, and he was beautiful like You, for after You, in Your image You created him. Now you have come and chosen this garden chosen from the beginning and placed in it the sign written in the Scriptures to appear, to announce and testify to Your coming with the saints if this time has come, and blessed are You, Lord, and blessed are we that there was someone to believe that You could fulfill, and we now have joy in the last workers with You on earth and over people, that the last fulfillments are coming and there will be holy joys with people on earth, and with us in Your coming word to those who have remained faithful.*[/legatura_la_teme]

We are a bundle of confessors alongside Your bishop Irineu for that great day on earth, and the gift of faith has come and is coming upon people, and all the ministers in the churches will believe, and the name of Your bishop Irineu remains written in heaven and cannot be erased, for it is a name of confession for all Your work from that day over the citadel of Your word today.

And, Lord, now, I, Your trumpet, speak to You about my difficult journey during my time with You on earth, and I am saying now that the journey of those who bear Your word on earth today was just as difficult, oh, how great was their suffering, great, from Your beginning with them and later, for they were sold to evil mouths and their sighs and fears were many, because from the middle emerged all the pains they bore after my departure to You, and then again in Your time with my little sister, with my Lelica, until we reached those who carry us now, Your little people, with whom You and we now support ourselves.

The journey with us was painful for our little ones, who still carry us with faith today against their many weaknesses, but You are with Your power as in my time with You, oh, and they still long for joys, for those in heaven with them for the end of the hardships upon them, for time is pressing, Lord, and they have to bear those that come from You and settle on earth, and they are small with their human strength, oh, that this is also necessary, and let us be with more support, with closer comfort, with strength of soul and body for them, for time is pressing, Lord, and our little ones are small.

You, heavenly powers, strengthen their power, for the Lord Jesus Christ is working with them over this time of unbelief and wandering among people, oh, and only here, only in this Jerusalem is there an oasis of light, of greenery, of holy faith.

I have written myself among the holy confessors this day, I have written myself, Lord, in Your book for our little ones today, and who have also suffered blows from the middle like us so that You would no longer walk, Lord, and so that they would lose their strength for You, but You supported them through great pains, through fear and turmoil, and they asked You through hardship so that they would walk and not leave You and not fall under the pressure of the difficult walk.

Oh, bless, Lord, this citadel and them too, that they may be able to do all that we can do through them, good Lord! All of us in heaven are watching with You here, where You have Your work to carry and to fulfill, and glorying to You and Your power, Lord, and behold, this is a day of testimony and of watching and of work with watching and of proclamation of Your word upon the earth, for Your glory is great and it becomes word and works through the word until the day of visible glory when all things are made new, a new heaven and a new earth, for the old things are passing and going away and You are coming, O Lord.

[legatura_la_teme id_capitol="7" id_subcapitol="3"]— Oh, beloved heavenly confessors, oh, Virginia, great may Our watchfulness be over Our work with them, with those of today with whom We are working the walk of God over the earth from margin to margin as it is written, and then the celebration of the age that will be, and there will be sighing over those who have stood and stand against God.

Woe to those who sit against God, for behold, the time is coming when there will be no one on earth who does not believe, and all will believe, and the Lord will be glorified by all the peoples of the earth, as it is written, and they will give thanks to the Lord, for My glory will be great and it will only be everywhere! Amen.

You, who are on earth, do not speak ill of the work of My word and its bearers, for they are protected by God. You harm yourselves if you are not obedient. I am with My good through them for the salvation of many, for the time of trial is coming and I am giving shelter from the bad time to many who will rejoice that I am coming to work through the word the work of the Holy Spirit, the Comforter Who governs the world, even if the world does not want to know this truth, for no one except God governs, nor do this, but the much conceited and haughty man oh, he thinks himself great, he thinks he can, only that everything is in My hand, and man does not want to become small as much as he is small.

Oh, sons, I have placed a word of testimony in My book with you on this day of remembrance of My works in the last days. You are destined by God to work with you on earth great fulfillments of the Scriptures in the last days, and you must have faith and more faith, and help heaven to be perfected upon earth, for My time has come, has come again and is coming, as it is written.

Stand with faith in those things that are ready to come and be brought to light, oh sons, and we will then rest with joy, we will celebrate and sing songs of victory and we will give thanks to the Father, for the Father awaits us with sweet reward, and you ask with holy prayer so that the Father may bring about the great deliverance of many, repentance over the nations and the return to God of those who will be saved, and your cry will bear a hundredfold fruit, O sons, as it is written. Amen, amen, amen.[/legatura_la_teme]
