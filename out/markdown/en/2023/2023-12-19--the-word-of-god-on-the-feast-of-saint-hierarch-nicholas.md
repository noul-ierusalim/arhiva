Peace to you, sons, oh, peace to you, My guards between earth and heaven for My coming word upon earth!

I am standing above on clouds of glory and speaking words of celebration and I am with My saints that have come on this day written in the Christian calendar with its celebration. The saints in heaven are celebrating the hierarch Nicholas, My confessor, and I am coming to you with word, sons. I have given you ears to hear so that you may hear My voice when I speak from above and so that you may put My word in the book, and on the way so that it may go and be fulfilled.

[legatura_la_teme id_capitol="5" id_subcapitol="6"]We are writing in the book with all that I have to share with you now. First, I am pronouncing a blessing that you may go forth as heralds and sharers of holy joy among those in My city of residence at this time and to proclaim to them the remembrance of My birth and the joy of this saving feast.

Oh, sons, prepare yourselves for the feast, adorn yourselves with grandeur and sit before those who are going to gather to receive from you the carols of the birth of Christ and to spend in the middle of the city the eve of the celebration of My birth on earth two thousand years ago, and My Virgin mother and the manger that received Me came from the Father through birth, oh, that no place was found for My mother to be received and to give birth to her Son promised by the prophets and by the herald angel, but the cattle received Us if people did not do this, and you now remember through carols and announcing that time now with a Christian feast and standing among people singing carols. All hearts will be comforted, all will rejoice, and they will then speak about this joy and you will be received with love, with longing and with hope, sons.

Go now and prepare the meeting place and the way there and your walk with celebration through the midst of the citadel of the Lord, for the city of Pucioasa2 is My Bethlehem of today in which I, the Lord, have the city of My word and I guard you between earth and heaven for the coming of My word, My children.

Oh, blessed be all the work you are preparing and all that moment of meeting accompanied by the carols of the birth of the Lord, all the sweet feast that you will give to those who are going gather and taste with their hearts the holy feeling of the Christmas holiday! I am Christmas, I am Christ, I am the One Who comes word on earth to you and I am preparing the glory of My visible coming for I am coming soon, I am coming because it is written, and that is why I am coming.

Dear and holy, the holy vigil evening is rising to heaven when you are standing among those who are gather together in the city to wait for you to come to carol and make them happy, and the angels are writing and testifing among those in heaven, just as on earth the celebration of Christmas in the city of Pucioasa will be testified far and wide, and about you, sharers of joy in the spirit of a holy celebration, on Christmas evening.[/legatura_la_teme]

So, sons, and peace to you and to the celebration with you of those who live in this city, and who will then carry within them for a long time this moment of joy, this holy gift! Amen.

And now, here is the holy feast day for Hierarch Nicholas, just as all the saints who confessed on earth have received a great name in heaven and on earth, and they are celebrated on the days of their ascension to heaven among the saints, and are asked by Christians to confess to God for them on earth, and then for their life in heaven. So, how beautiful it is on earth when Christians celebrate those in heaven, how beautiful!

It is written in the history of the saints that this great father of the Christian flock watched over them with mercy and shepherding love, and he placed his help where he saw that help from God was needed. He taught both parents and their children the love and gratitude for God and the holy watch-fulness for the steps of those he shepherded.

Behold, I, the Lord, have nourished many Christians from My spring of word and have strengthened their lives with God, but if they have given birth to children, and have not strengthened them day and night with the word of My mouth, with heavenly teachings, with holy vigilance for their path with Christ until the end, oh, they have lost them to the sons of the world to which their children were delighted to go from God, and not only have they brought great pain and shame to the parents, regret and tears here, and afterwards when they will have no fruit to bring to God from their children lost to the sin that separates them from God, but some of the parents have also been lost along with their children, for the parents have also abandoned the path according to the guiding word from heaven, and behold, I once said that parents die through children and children die through parents, oh, that they did not watch over the path of their children in time, and when the children weakened, they also weakened and fell from My bosom and them, and I have always kept watch with the word, but no one paid attention, no one took it.

Oh, Christian son, I am weeping for you now, because I have nourished you from heaven for a long time and kept you close to give you eternal happiness, but you have weakened and have not understood the holy vigilance so that you may not fall from God. Oh, you have not feared God. You have wasted your life time as on earth. You have gone to the sons of the world, you have followed them, according to their sick tastes and habits. You have served only for the body, you have loved rest, goodwill, wealth, name, but all these have ruined you, they have ruined your time for your preparation to enter eternal life and have there a hold to which you can draw, to wait for you to inherit it, and not to lose it like that on earth, which perishes and you do not remain with it. Oh, at least gather in your heart now regrets and repentance for disobeying God. At least cry out in prayer for yourself and for your children, who have loved this passing age more, and have not gathered for the eternal one.

[legatura_la_teme id_capitol="6" id_subcapitol="3"]Oh, sons, oh, Christians, gather to Me love for God and a spirit of humility and repentance, because the soul needs clothing, your man has got what he is to be, he needs it there. Man is condemned by the sin he has committed. Seek that God may forgive you, that He may leave your sins, that He may wipe them out, but gather yourselves from now on so that you may have a great experience where the payment for what you have done here starts.

I was speaking with the Father on earth, I was speaking from the earth, and the Father was speaking with Me from heaven and He made Himself heard at My baptism in the Jordan, and on the mountain afterwards when the Father glorified Me in great light before My disciples, oh, and I was comforted speaking with Him, and the disciples heard Me when I was speaking with the Father for Me and for them.

Oh, sons, oh, Christians, you also speak to the Father, you also speak with Me, with the saints, with the angels, with My Virgin mother, speak with those in heaven, sons. Nourish your souls, nourish your man who will be after the life of the body. Take rest carefully in time, oh, for you do not know when the Lord is going to come, and what will you do if the soul does not have the strength to stand before the Lord with wealth, with clothing, and not naked?[/legatura_la_teme]

I created man so that through him the works of God's creation, heaven and earth, would be connected in work, and behold, when a saint is celebrated on earth, there is also a celebration in heaven, and the celebration extends from heaven to earth, and again, from earth to heaven, for the glory of My saints is great and I am coming with them in My coming as it is written that I will come. **(Matt: 25/313; Mark: 8/38; 1 Thess: 3/13)**

And [legatura_la_teme id_capitol="3" id_subcapitol="3"]on this day, hierarch Nicholas is sitting down in My book with you, sons, and is exhorting the hierarch as well as I work, I in him, and he in Me, and this is the reward of those who sanctify themselves for the Lord on earth, and I glorify Myself from among them in this time of My coming, and I am saying this so that the people on earth may know the reward of My saints, of those who love God on earth.

— I loved You, Lord, and I chose for my life to be Your love on earth and to give it to those who do not have it and do not know it.

I watched like a gentle shepherd and tamed people with a heart of beasts and bow them down to repentance, stopping them from doing evil to the poor and helpless and innocent. I saved from a life of sin those who were ready to fall into this mud, sin, and I loved people very much, for the feeling of mercy taught me so.

People fall, they fall from God and lose their way and go on the path of destruction through sin. The Lord teaches you the sinless path, man, but who can speak to you about eternity and teach you to believe and be able to work with God?

Miracles are needed, Lord, for the weakness of the human mind is great, and love is needed to be placed in people, because the human mind must be heavenly love. I have worked like this. Love has preserved me for You and for people, but miracles are needed if otherwise man does not come to the path of life. Make people know You and seek You and love You, and You give them forgiveness of sins, and then Your light will surround them in it and they will walk in the light, oh, Lord! Amen.

— I am the light of the world, I and those who stand in the light so that they may overcome the darkness on earth and in man and that God may be confessed throughout the earth.

I go out with you to the people, to the children of the citadel of My word, and many will see My light with you and from you. I show Myself to people, loving them through you and teaching them love and heaven with them on earth.

Let sin perish from the earth and let people remain and let people become the sons of God, let them become love as I am![/legatura_la_teme]

I walk with you among men, My sons. The glory of the Father is with us, and the saints are mysterious and are with us, and peace to you, the sons who confess God!

Grace, grace upon you, sons!

Keep yourselves in humility, in great humility, o sons! Amen, amen, amen.
