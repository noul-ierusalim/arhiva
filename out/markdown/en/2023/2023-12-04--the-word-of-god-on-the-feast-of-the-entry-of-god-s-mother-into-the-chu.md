The glory of God descends to earth and becomes a word and a book and travels across the earth from place to place, and this book is called the Word of God, the throne of God, from which His word flows upon people.

I glorify Myself through the word on earth and I have a citadel on the hearth of the Romanian nation and I have watchmen in the citadel to place My word in the book when I speak from above so that I may be heard and so that the word may be written and then placed on the path to go and feed the human race from heaven and for people to know that God the Word is coming on earth.

Peace over My today country and over the citadel of My word, and from here over the earth!

Peace from God on earth, on the believers and on the unbelievers on earth, for soon there will be no more unbelief on earth and all people will know and understand that there is God, amen!

It is a feast for My Virgin mother, the mother of the Son of God, the empress who sits at My right side in the glory of God above. Oh, My mother was little when she was brought by her parents and given to God for service. She remained obedient from a young age and grew up serving, and when she was about to pass the age of childhood, the archangel came from heaven and told her of My birth and she obediently received the news from God and then those announced by the prophets were fulfilled and I came to earth by birth from the Virgin and then this news went over all the nations of the earth until today, and it was so that all the nations of the earth would be filled with the faith that God came to earth as Savior, and this feast day has remained in remembrance for two thousand years and more, and behold, heaven works on earth so that people would believe, have hope and follow the heavenly path just like My Virgin mother, with faith and obedience like her, according to the will of God, and so that they would have a Father in heaven, so that He would take care of them on earth.

Oh, My mother, you were announced by the prophets that I would be your Son, mother. [legatura_la_teme id_capitol="6" id_subcapitol="3"]You were then born on earth and you believed when the angel announced to you that you would give birth to Me. Faith brings to fulfillment the will of God on earth, My mother, oh, and this is not easy for the heart and mind of man, for man is forgetful, and is busy with many things on earth, but God is the One Who can fulfill His will among men, oh, and how easy it would be for man to believe and to bow down with wisdom, with humility, and to take from heaven for him his work on earth, as you worked, listening and believing, My mother!

Oh, let Us speak in heaven before those on earth and let Us make Ourselves heard to help people for faith, My mother!

*— Oh, Son Jesus, all those with a corrupt and sick mind and who say to themselves:* "There is no God!", all of them will not have You as God even after they leave earthly life, and I say to them:

Oh, if there were no Maker of all that is seen and unseen, you would no longer be the one who says: "There is no God!" and there would be nothing of all that is, but behold, on earth and under the earth, in the waters and in the air there is life, it is from God and it confesses and proves that God exists, and you, mad with your mind, would have to see your illness and speak of nothing but your illness, which only God can cure you, and Whom you defame because He is not and that you are instead, but the truest truth that there is God is you, and if you are, there is also God and from Him you have life.

Oh, and if many people on earth believe that there is a God, why don't they seek Him and listen to Him with gratitude for His constant care for those between earth and heaven for people to take care of them?

Man, you need suffering so that you don't forget the Lord your God and then make a mistake like Adam, who allowed himself to be without God and do his own will, but his will completely destroyed him, oh, and this is what happens to all those who love to do their own will on earth.

Oh, people, oh, sons, when the Lord knocks at your door, when He now speaks to you through His word, oh, do not take lightly these moments that test your faith and faithfulness on the spot and that examine your inner and outer behavior. Be vigilant for the way my Son works for you and over you, oh, and do not be like those who did not let Him examine them and correct them in time, and if they did not want to, it is because they chose to remain in their sick wills and without a healer over them, and they lost the path of life and fell into sin.

Oh, man, oh, disobedient son, if you do not receive the Lord for your steps, it is because you make yourself great, you believe yourself great and able to direct the steps of your life, only the great are those who bend down, they are those who receive in order to have and to be able and to know how to walk. When man made a mistake in heaven because of his lack of watch over him, the Lord said to him: «Earth you are and into the earth you will go, and until then the earth will bear thorns and thistles for you». Oh, sons, thorns and thistles are the sins of man and these are his fruit on earth and they sting the Lord so that He cannot come near to examine him, to do him good, to direct his life.

Oh, sons, be heaven, not earth, for in heaven God dwells with His heaven of angels and saints, and you will be with His heaven if you love to have watch over you, for watchfulness guards you from sinning, from separating yourself from God because of sin as it happened with Adam, and Adam went to earth if he did not love heaven and heaven's watch over him on earth.

[/legatura_la_teme][legatura_la_teme id_capitol="7" id_subcapitol="1"]Those who have fallen asleep will rise from their graves and take their bodies as my Son Jesus Christ took His body when He rose from the grave, and as those whose graves were opened and rose with Christ, took up their bodies and stood on their feet testifying to God, **(Matthew: 27/52,533)**, and so it will be fulfilled again, according to the word of the Lord, and all will stand before Him and testify to this word, which you hear now, and they will no longer be unbelievers, and faith will increase throughout the earth, and behold, let yourselves now be worked by the word of God and be with great faith before Him, for this is the time, oh, and no one is waiting anymore, no one is watching for it, no one.[/legatura_la_teme]

O, Son Jesus, I have spoken to You before believing and unbelieving people, and I have spoken to people and I have worked on Your word, and so let them listen too, for I have now set myself an example of obedience to God so that they too may learn obedience to You and work out their salvation through obedience, o, my dear Son.

— You are also precious to Me, My mother. I bow like a child before you, and I bow to those who bow, mother, for such is the work of one who is a child, humility is the work of a child of God on earth.

[legatura_la_teme id_capitol="5" id_subcapitol="6"]Now, to the sons of My citadel of the word, I am letting you know to make time for the feast of My Birth when carols are sung on earth, because I am looking down from heaven and see that you are awaited by many, and if I see this, I am going to announce to you to prepare yourselves with My glory and to sit before those who will seek you for their joy and comfort, oh, because people no longer have comfort, sons. I comfort them, you comfort them too, we share comfort, sons.

Now, gather yourselves together and prepare yourselves to appear with My glory, and be careful of this obedience that I am giving you, for you are from Me on earth and I have made known to you that you are Mine and that I am with you and to you now, at the end of time. Neither lions nor wolves ate you in My time with you, but, on the contrary, I have brought good time for you, a time announced throughout the time of My word that it would come, that I will appear with My people and that we will nourish the faith and hope of many, and then their love for God so that I can extend My protection over them in the time of the awakening with fear of the unbelievers and opponents of God on earth.[/legatura_la_teme]

And now, peace to you, sons, on the day of the holy feast and the table of word between Me and you!

Peace to My faithful people through obedience, and peace to those who will learn the humility for obedience to God and God's will on earth! Amen, amen, amen.
