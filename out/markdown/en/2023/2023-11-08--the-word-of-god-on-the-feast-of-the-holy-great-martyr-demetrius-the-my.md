The haven above here is full of the glory of the heavenly and angelic hosts, and there is a glorious preparation for the coming feast, and therefore I am setting out word of news that the feast of the angels is coming and preparation must be made in the citadel of My word, so that I, the Lord, may come and speak with glory, may come and share My word over the earth, for I am the Word of the Father and I can through the word to work the new heaven and the new earth and may all the wonders of this prophesied fulfillment come and appear, only let there be faith and grace through faith, to the children of the citadel of My word.

And now we are here, sons, I and My saints, I and the company of martyr-confessors to celebrate the great martyr, Demetrius, the confessor who stood before the kings for My name, and behold a feast of words, and peace to you, oh, sons! And I am putting power from heaven upon you, for I am finding you weak, and I am not going to dwell much on the word this day, but I must proclaim a blessing for the feast of the angels and its preparation and so that you may always be able to work through My blessing, through God, sons.

At My beginning through My trumpet Virginia, after 1955, I spoke a lot and announced many fulfillments that they would come, but for this, faith is needed as in all times when I, the Lord, fulfilled and worked on what was written in the prophets and in the Scriptures, and now I have been fulfilling with you what was announced at the beginning of My work of word, because I have had to prepare Romania and its people, the land with treasure in it, My coming in word over the earth for great fulfillments, and I am working with you, and that is why I must always awaken you, to always work with Me, My children.

[legatura_la_teme id_capitol="6" id_subcapitol="3"]But I must urge some of those who believe with even more words if they do not watch enough to listen to My advice, for I, Christian son, when you show yourself to be stubborn and unyielding, I come near to tell you your condition, and I come through the face of those around you as I show Myself to work and to announce to you, and if you do not receive, if you say that it is the man, that it is the brother, then you are acting now like the people of Israel who did not receive Me and rejected Me and gave Me over to destruction, for this people was stubborn in obedience, oh, and do not do so either, for I have come from heaven to you, not from where it may be, and I must have sons with the fear of God in them, because otherwise no one listens, no one, except those who tremble at My word, and in whom I can find My pleasure and fulfillment, as it is written.

I am speaking in the citadel of My word and giving counsel to all who call themselves My people and am saying: He who does not fear God all the time is not with God. He who fears the Lord, such a one recognizes Him as the Almighty Master, and thus his God confesses Him, and otherwise man is not of God.

I am teaching you, children, through My descent, a word to you, and I am teaching you also from your side, I am teaching you both from heaven and from earth, lest you believe in vain the word of My coming, for many seek My word when it comes, but do not place it upon them fulfilled, and I, the Lord, want to fulfill those who have taken Me as their guide, oh, that it is not just any way that you are called to be worked by this word, but only when you fear God and stand small in His working hand of a new man.[/legatura_la_teme]

Oh, but there are not many with this wisdom in them, and that is why, as two thousand years ago, I always teach those who approach to walk the path with Me, oh, and I have and still have much to put to work, only that there be fear of God and faith and bowing down with modesty and full obedience to those who come from heaven for those who sit as sons of God before Me for their creation.

Oh, sons, stand at the work of creation if you have chosen to walk the path of My word in this time for the preparation of a new people, through whom I fulfill My Scriptures that are yet to be fulfilled, sons. Therefore, here is what I am going to tell you so that you may know from Me:

[legatura_la_teme id_capitol="6" id_subcapitol="1"]The righteous are not those who seek and do justice for themselves. Oh, those are not the righteous ones, but the humble are righteous, and who can always say before a word of rebuke: *„I have done these and greater things than these.”* And again, I am saying, Christian son, for under the guise of those near you, I am coming and giving you advice, rebuke or work to do, and this says a lot, only to have your mind from God, not from yourself, for you are accustomed to seeking justice for yourself and they drive the Lord away from you when He comes near to work on your making.[/legatura_la_teme]

I cry and I keep on crying over the earth and over the people as if I were always saying: Behold the Bridegroom, go out to meet Him! I always cry over Romania to prepare it for My meeting and I am working with you, with the faithful so that I may be able to work through their faith, oh, sons. I am saying to all of you: Learn the faith and its work, learn from the saints and be faithful, sons nourished from heaven with the manna of My word. Work like the saints who overcame and shamed the proud and godless kings over them, and behold, the one celebrated today has an entry in the book from Me and speaks thus on his day among the saints above:

<i— I was a Christian in secret, Lord, and I am telling Your least ones today that the grace of faith in Christ banishes even the fear of death and makes way for life from above for those with strong faith through their love for You, Lord Jesus.

I was my father's successor according to the flesh at the helm of the city since my youth, but by not denying You I remained Your successor, and the emperor tormented me if I shamed him with Your great name and I overcame him through faith in You, for I loved You, Lord, hating this passing age, which kills the soul in man, Your wealth in man, Lord.

Therefore, faith with submission, for otherwise faith can do nothing, o children of Christ. Learn the fear of God, which brings you to obedience and submission for the work of creation, o, and so help them, O Lord, that they may be, and peace be with You in their midst, and teach them Your peace!

— So, peace to you, sons! Learn to have this when I give it to you. And now, I am saying again a word of blessing over all your work with Me, and may the feast of the angels rise to heaven from you together with the remembrance of those who have fallen asleep, whom you will place at the feast table for their comfort, sons.

[legatura_la_teme id_capitol="6" id_subcapitol="3"]I have given notice here to teach comfort to work, because otherwise restless states and lack of submissions take place, sons. Submission brings peace and comfort, just take an example from a kitten that shares its comfort with those around it, because the news goes that the cat heals the pain of the body of the sick or tired, but this healing does not come from anything else of hers, but only from the spirit that lives in her, the spirit of comfort, because the cat comforts, comforts always, oh, sons. But man is lower with the soul in him. Man is tense, he is full of himself, he is troubled within himself, he is arrogant, because he lacks the spirit of comfort, he is troubled, because he has the spirit of pride in him, which reveals the master with whom he works, the arrogant, the conceited satan. And he who has the Lord as his Master, he bows down, does not seek his own righteousness, does not desire to have it, for he is gentle and humble of heart like God.

I am always watching and listening over you, sons. This is how I worked with My disciples of two thousand years ago, whom I always taught and corrected. I collect your words, your behavior, and if you are harsh, I come to you and rebuke you and teach you love through everything, oh, sons, only you know how to receive God, for man is not right, since he is wrong in many things, not in few, and the sons of God learn from God, for I am gentle and humble of heart, sons.[/legatura_la_teme]

Behold, now I am urging you to the work of consolation, I am strengthening you and supporting you for this, so that you can prepare the well at Maluri *(Maluri Village, the place of the house where Saint Virginia, the trumpet of God – the sixth, lived while she was in the flesh, r.n.)* and that it be given to those in the village who do not have water and are waiting to have it, sons. We will make it to be a favorable time and so that you may work this gift for those who are deprived of water, for the Lord is urging those in the village of My trumpet Virginia to repent, because in My time with her, the wicked in this village rose up with malice and sold her to those who tormented her *(To the servants of the communist red beast dictatorship of that time, r.n.)* because she belonged to God, and we now are urging them to listen and repent on behalf of those who persecuted her then and are no longer on earth and they are waiting for a consolation, love for them. So, let us hurry to give them this joy and then we will go further step by step for the glorious preparation of this patch of land testifying to My coming as a word on earth, starting from there, from the Maluri, oh sons.

Do not forget what I told you: I am in front and we are going step by step and writing in the book everything that is done, and we will write more on the day of the feast of the angels and it remains to name the dug wells, to give them angels, starting with the one for the villagers, and you seek My blessing with all your heart always, and so work everything that I do with you on earth, and let everything gather in heaven near the Father, the Son and the Holy Spirit, and only for God may you be on earth and only to Him may you give place for the justice of all, and you, all of you, stay humble, and do not forget that I, the Lord, under the image of those next to you come to you and stay with you, with the creation above, to give you a beautiful face and that you may be My image, the image of the sons of God on earth among men, oh sons. Amen, amen, amen.
