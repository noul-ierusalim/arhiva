The groups of saints and angels are singing to Me; the whole heaven is singing the song for My resurrection of two thousand years ago, for then I died in torments on the cross and I resurrected on the third day, fulfilling these Scriptures, oh, and it is a day of the feast of resurrection among those in heaven, and it is only a song of victory for the Son of God, and the hell and its powers are shaken from its foundation and the song, “Christ has risen!” penetrates to those in the hell.

I am the Lord, Jesus Christ, I am God the Son, and I am sharing on earth among people the spirit of the resurrection and its comfort, its benefic fragrance for those with days of holy feast over them, for the Christians who honor the Lord’s Passover, for those who know that at the Holy Passover one sings, „Christ has risen!”

Oh, it is otherwise now on earth, at the feast of the resurrection; it is not boiling the spirit of sin so much, for the breeze of the pain of the soul, which has passed over the earth from one side to the other, *(Pain provoked by the corona-virus pandemic, r.n.)* has made, in the time of the fasting for the coming of the feast of the Lord’s Resurrection, calmness as in a monastery, and still greater peace in the air, and even deeper, in comparison with the big bustle which all people have used to lift up day by day and night by night by their habits, more or less guilty before God, Who has been waiting for two thousand years with the kingdom of the heavens in His arms to set it on earth with the people, to be on earth as in heaven, and that those on earth to see this; nevertheless, God is not desired by the people on earth with His things, oh, and the sighing is catching those who stand against the heavenly good with them on earth, and I, the Lord, am with mercifulness, oh, but I am also with the holy exhortation and I am telling man this:

Oh, man, who spend your life on God’s earth, as you choose your will for it, do not forget to keep watching, for when it goes well with you one day, and you may think that this is how it will always go with you, then tomorrow it will be otherwise; maybe you will sigh, maybe fear will catch you, maybe you will be hurt from what you have been working in your life without God’s helm in it, and you will see that are weakened by unexpected trials, oh, and that is why you have to watch once with God for the peace of your life, for you always have to ask for the peace from Me, to allow its way towards you; you have to stay connected to Me and to be strong in the joys and trials of your life and faith and in those of the victory in the struggle against sin, which draws pains and death, the reward that is not pleased to the soul, oh, and that is why, I, the Lord, I am advising you to come all the time under the shelter of the Lord’s help on earth and to cover yourself with the protecting power of His cross, of the sufferance that Your Lord endured under the cross for your redemption from under the payment of your great sin, and behold, how beautifully I am advising you and how much I am waiting for you to get along with you for your life, for your peace, for your protection from God all the time, day and night, oh, for if the people on earth loved God to be with them and the laws of life to be eternally with them, then there would not come on earth pains, tears, sighing and heartbreaking worries, and all the evil things would be removed from those who stay under My protecting shield for man and there would come no end, too, for I, the Lord, created the man so that through him the great honor for God’s name among people may be glorified on earth. However, the man does not take God into consideration, the One Who keeps the heaven and the earth by His power, the One Who died on the cross for the salvation of the sinful ones, and who draw death on themselves by sin. Still, I died for your sin, man, and I am telling this with compassion and longing, and I am urging you to have mercy of God and of you, and I am also exhorting you to come and stay under the shelter of My cross always, for he who denies the cross even for a moment, he who flees from the cross is always in danger, he comes to stand before the sighing when he does not expect it, but the cross always destroys the enemy power, the wait and the envious devil against man. Oh, and let no one say that there is no devil and that there is only God, for if the one who says so were right, then a man like that would love God only and would never give any reason to the devil to rejoice. However, behold, only few love God, very few on earth love Him truly and longingly and with humility under the cross, for the cross saves, the cross brings resurrection, it protects and it has power.

Oh, man, I am speaking to you on a day of the feast of resurrection and I am telling you; do no longer flee from holiness. Oh, do no longer flee from the love of God, and rather seek to establish your steadfastness, which always protects you. You saw what those in Jerusalem did two thousand years ago, for on a day like this, they were singing Hosanna to Me, and then in three or four days, they were giving Me over to death calling out to Pilate: *„Crucify Him, crucify Him, and let Barnabas with us!”.*

Oh, it is not possible with God without man having steadfastness on His way with man. Because Israel did his will and not God’s will with it, and because it had always gone wrong and always rebelled, their fathers and followers, oh, this people has always suffered, and then it has been scattered all over the earth, the people who has always kept boasting from that time and up to this day that they have been the Lord’s people, when God cannot be pleased with them.

Oh, behold, steadfastness is needed, man. I am the Way, but all the time steadfastness is needed for the laws of the walking with God, Who protects those on the way with Him, oh, and I am giving you advice of steadfastness on your way with Me, and I am giving you power for a spirit of obedience, for I, the Lord, want protection for you, only for you to want this.

Now am I am embracing the Romanian people, My Orthodox people, into whose midst I have a dwelling place for My coming and a voice over the earth, and I have also got a little and tiny people, prepared by Me, for Me, to be My help and to send My word over the earth, sent under My name. The Word of God is My name **(Apoc: 19/13)** with which I am coming on earth as word, and I have armies from heaven with which I walk between heaven and earth, as it is written to come with the saints, and all of Us are dressed in white little shirts and We are riding on heavenly horses, as the beloved John saw and wrote in the book of Apocalypse the glory of the Lord’s coming after two thousand years, and no one can change anything in this book, which announces that the Lord is coming, for its fulfillment is at due time, even if the Scriptures have been changed, even if some of the people, who were especially hired for this, changed them so that they may no longer show that the Lord is coming. However, I am going to come and fulfill all that were written about Me in the books of Moses, in the prophets and in the psalms, and I am full of word and I am coming in this way, and I am going from margins to margins with the voice of My word over those who believe Me and receive Me with it, and especially over those who have chosen to put Christ aside, to stay without faith in God and to show their antichrist work, and to these I am telling this:

You are here below, and I am above you, and I am coming in heavenly chariots with the victory of the Father, of the Son and of the Holy Spirit and I am fulfilling the Scriptures exactly as it is written, and they are not going to be delayed but only for those that I have chosen, who are long enduring, waiting for Me to come and to comfort them, to protect and to guide them through the midst of the human darkness and to raise them to be victorious near Me, and I to stay all the time on earth until the end of the time, as it is written, and by their faith, by this faith I am victorious, and they are also victorious and are called victorious.

I am with those from the end, who believe in My coming and who live on earth among people in My will and who do not throw aside the word of God to be free from My word, for they know that obedience to God is that which protects them and it carries them to the glory of the paradise on earth and in heaven, to the paradise from which Adam fell, the first man created by God, and who wanted to be free from God and then lost the paradise, for God has not created man to be separated from God, but that man may make the will of the One Who has built him.

Now, I am reminding with pain of the pain that gets through the heaven, when it is spoken, and one keeps speaking about the human rights. Oh, but does someone speak anymore about God’s rights, the One Who has made the heaven, the earth and the man?

Oh, God’s sons, and you, sons of men, there are only and only lies all around that are spread through the people from the seats of the earth and through their hired people. There have been spreading only lies as two thousand years ago, when the rulers of the Jews paid with money those who spread the lies, believing that they may succeed and bring the truth in the darkness, and God as well, so that they may rule over the deceived mobs, lied as today, for the truth is not told, and no one is in the habit of telling the truth, but the lie only, and this to the interest of those who rule over the mobs, and who rotate one after the other. Only that I, and those who walk in My way on earth, have nothing in this world, and neither the world has anything in us, for My way has its way covered from the look of those who lie to themselves day by day and then those who spread lies. Only that it is written with a heavenly and writing pen about the lie on earth and about those who serve it and who are in the service of the devil, the father of the lie, the ruler of this age lacking the light of the faith and the love of God, and it is written clearly that the Lord scatters away the plans of the people and thwarts the thoughts of the nations and denigrates the plans of the chiefs, and only the Lord’s advise remains forever, and only the Lord’s thought remains from generation to generation; oh, and it is also written that it is blessed the nation whose Lord is its God, and happy is the people whom the Lord has chosen to be His inheritance.

Oh, humble yourself, Romanian people, before the Lord, your God! You have a special election among the nations, and behold, I have the way of My coming into your midst and level the ways for the nations that all may come to the Lord, for this is written, and I do this wakening into your midst, for I am coming as word on your hearth, and I am going with the today’s chariots *(The Internet, r.n.)* for the proclamation of My word over the earth and I put it on the way to go; I put it into an envelope or in the air and it goes and revives God in man, and the kings of the earth will boast Me when will hear the speaking of My mouth and they will sing the ways of the Lord and will receive the Lord, the One Who dwells in the midst of Romanian Jerusalem, the One on high, and Who looks at the humble things, and Who knows from far away those that are high, as it is written.

Oh, people of Romanians, oh, people of My word, call out to Me with fire and longing and day and night tell to God: Fulfill, Lord, the kingdom of the heavens on earth with the people, for on earth it has to be as in heaven, for this is what You told us to ask from the Father to be on earth, God’s will to be with the people on earth.

Oh, little sons and obedient to the holy wills, oh, do not forget that at the prayer of Elijah, the prophet, in the time of the pagan kings on earth, at his prayer, fire from heaven came down, and this heavenly sign, required by prayer, confessed the true God. Oh, sons, I have to do what to do with those who have their own minds and plans, and who want to do evil against those who want to submit them to their will, because they are only people and that is all.

There is the heavenly army arrived here with you for service on a day of the feast of resurrection, sons, guided by My word. Those in heaven gather together near you in the feasts and take care of you all the time, as though you were My little babies, for you are My little people, My little chicks, **(Matt: 23/37)** and the saints take care of you on My behalf, for you take care of Me to reach from margins to margins with the word of My mouth, sons. The heaven is singing above you the song for My resurrection of two thousand years ago: *„Christ has risen!”* and My angels are singing once with you, sons.

Oh, Romanian people, receive My comfort through My little ones, through My messengers to you, for I am comforting you with the word and with My love for you, because I have you from the Father, to be the hearth and the people for My coming after two thousand years.

Oh, I have looked on earth during this night of Passover, and especially I have look over you, Romanian people, for you are My house of coming now, in the end of the time. The event that has come out over the nations *(Coronavirus pandemic, r.n.)* has made you behave and stay well in your little house as one stays in the time of the fasting for the preparation of the Lord’s Passover. You do no longer know of the holy ordinances, in which lived your ancestors who loved God. You entered the mixture of those without your faith, and this mixture wants to make you forget your origin and faith, and your God, Jesus Christ. However, I do not allow those who have no God over them to separate you from God, and I will always wake you up to look up to Me, and not to forget what a great God you have among the nations of the earth and what a holy root you have.

Oh, do not forget, do not forget about God. Forget and leave out sin, but do not forget about God, oh, Romanian people, for it is the time to make you into a great light, which will shine over all the nations on earth, and you will be the country of the brightness, **(Dan: 11/41)** you will be through My word spoken by My mouth in your midst, and which shines in the darkness and lights the world, and the nations of the earth will come to take light from this mountain, from My citadel in you, Romanian people. I am the One Who paid with My death on the cross for your name of Christian and Romanian, for My Father made you then at once, to be for today the land and the people to which Jesus Christ is coming, God’s Son, the second coming from near the Father and as word over the earth.

Oh, peace to you, My Romanian people! You cannot have another destiny than which is meant for you from the Father, that is, to be My today’s house, My wedding hall, the Lord’s table and His Supper on it, God as body and word into your midst.

Oh, peace to you! Christ has risen! Peace to you, Romanian people!

You, nations on earth, seek Me with a humble heart. Every evil that come over the nations is only to do good, and the power of the devil grows less little by little; it is being weakened to nothing. Look into My word that clarifies the times, for it is the time for the Lord to work because the people have gone very much immoral, oh, so immoral that the spirit of haughtiness completely blinds those who are enchanted by it.

Oh, peace to you, nations who live and walk in the Orthodox faith! You are My comfort, the sign of the faith that awaits Me to come, for by the faith of My church I am coming and saving it.

Oh, peace to you, peace to you, church of the Romanian people! I am giving the spirit of the holy revival to the Orthodox flock on a day of the feast of resurrection, oh, and do not be afraid, little flock; the Father is with His kingdom in your midst, as it is written, but keep yourself in the clean and pure faith, that which has been brought from the ancestors until today through all the trials of the time, for the Lord had to find faith on earth before His coming.

Christ has risen, church of the Romanian people! Oh, take care of the spirit of your resurrection, take care always!

Christ has risen! This is how the heavenly armies are singing over the citadel of My word in the midst of the Romanian people!

Christ has risen! This is how is supposed to sing the Romanian people, and let it sing with the heavenly armies in one voice, and may the song meet to the glory of the Father, of the Son and of the Holy Spirit.

I am embracing you within My arms. I am embracing you within the spirit of the resurrection, Romanian sons! You, too, should embrace the Lord, Who is coming to you as word, and be only an embrace with Him.

Christ has risen! Christ has risen! Christ has risen! Amen, amen, amen.
