Again and again I am coming down as Shepherd over the Christian flock on earth, but I am also tending with My word those without God.

Again and again I bring down word for faith, for the day of My glory is coming and I have to level its way to come. I have the shovel in My hand and I am cleansing My threshing floor and gathering the harvest and I am feeding those who believe in My coming to the people, but also those who have hardened their hearts against faith, oh, and I am also giving them from the food of the teaching, because the time has come for the people to learn and to know, willing or not, that the Lord is coming, and then they have to believe this; they have to believe when they hear the word of My coming and for the preparation of My day of glory.

I am the Lord of resurrection, the Lord Jesus Christ, I am He Who is coming, but as two thousand years ago, I am finding resistance against faith. However, I am also finding hearts that have the gift of faith, and faith can work, it can do so by the humility of the man’s heart, faith can work in the one who humbles himself in his heart, either for the beauty of the heart, or for the sins that humble the man, because sin is not joy, as man may believe about the sin that he likes to do, but it is rather the humility of the man thrown down by sin; it is the falling down from the way and then it is the shame of life, and I, the Lord, am calling out and gathering for the waking up from sin every man who has fallen under the burden of his many sins, and I am showing him the beautiful life, that which is not enslaved by the power of sin, that full of humility, full of prayer and forgiveness, of the seeking after the resurrection, of praised walking under the cross, for woe to those who are taken from glory to glory when this glory comes from the people, woe to them, woe to this wretched mortals, who are not worthy of glory, of the glory which is as fleeting as man! And again, happy are those under the glory of the cross, their glory with God and like God under the cross, for they are the sons of king; that is those who overcome for the eternal life of the victors through the carried cross, the great light of the man carrying of God, carrying of cross, of the payment hardly earned on earth and then their rest, for this is right for those who overcome, to those who overcome themselves looking at the promises eternally comforting of those who are faithful and long after God on earth, not after the sin which all love so much and look for it, and then they cry forever for the things that they have earned on earth by their sin.

On this day of Sunday, it is ordained in the Christian calendar the memorial of My stopover at Jacob’s well, when, while resting from My travelling, I met a Samaritan woman and I spoke with her at the well, and I did the work of My Father Who had sent Me.

I passed with My disciples through Samaria and I was waiting for them to come back while they went into the citadel to buy some food, but meanwhile I did the work for which I had come on earth, oh, and I put the faith in Me in the woman who came to draw water from the well while I was resting there. To everyone on whom I have mercy, I open his eyes to see and I open his mind to understand, and in the same way, I harden the heart of anyone on whom I want to do this, and I do this in order to work the proof about the godly truth among people, just as I hardened the heart of the Jews, and I did this so that they might raise their hand against God and put Him on the cross of the glory of the resurrection after that, and I opened the mind and heart of the Samaritan woman to know Christ while He was speaking with her at the well. She had been brought low because of her sin, but also by the humility in which sin was keeping her. However, I took mercy on her and reveled Myself to her as Jesus Christ and spoke face to face with her, oh, and how easily she believed that God was speaking with her, how easily?

[legatura_la_teme id_capitol="2" id_subcapitol="6"]Oh, man enslaved by sin, how easy it would be for you to want to be guided by God on earth on the way of your life! Oh, how easy it would be for you, how happy you would feel because of that! Oh, behold, behold what happens to you if you let yourself led by the people with a hardened heart for God, who lie to you all the time and who fill you with fear, with despair, with sadness all the time! *(Everything that has come especially after the corona virus pandemic, r.n.)*

Oh, I have great mercy on all people, as all are guided by lie and by its work through the people, who are highly placed by the spirit that is foreign to God.

I am calling out to the people and I am telling them the word from heaven, the true word.

Oh, listen to Me, you men! I am speaking from heaven upon you. I am calling out to you and saying: everything is a lie on earth. Do no longer receive anything from on earth! Oh, do no longer seek to believe the news which are given to you, but have wisdom from God and for God instead, for God is at the helm of all things, and He knows all things, for all the people receive what they have worked for themselves, and God is proving Himself more and more, and everything is sorted out to the right and to the left, either for glory or for fire, and man will listen to God’s will on earth, willing or not, poor of him.

Oh, arise, you those with a weak spirit and fed by the spirit of falsehood, which is shared with you as plain truth! Oh, flee from the deceit, which is given to you by television, by the news, which makes your mind sick, your life and your good faith, and which fills you with disorder, with despair and with fear. Oh, do not stay without God, because it is hard without God on earth. It is destitute of power and grace the man who does not have God as light in him always, with which to see the spirit of lie, which swarms everywhere through the people who give over the people. Oh, do not sit at the table with them, do not sit before the television with them, and do not eat what they eat with their soul in them! I am calling out to you to hear Me that I am calling you out, and I do this so that you may eat from God, from the Spirit above, Who sees from above the whole work of the lie, for it will be completely brought into the light.[/legatura_la_teme]

Oh, this time is great! There are the days of the Lord on earth among people, and it is written about them in the Scriptures. Do not depart from God, you, those who are fed by the lie of the proud people who are over you, and who think that they rule the world! Come to the water of life; come to the Lord to tell you the truth and only the truth! My mouth is opening to give you from God. This time is as in the day of the descending of the Holy Spirit over My disciples two thousand years ago. The Holy Spirit is coming on earth to give to the people and they have to receive from God; they do not have to keep on taking from the people.

[legatura_la_teme id_capitol="2" id_subcapitol="6"]To those who are hardened in their heart I am telling as I told those who were hardened two thousand years ago, and I am telling them this: You do not want to receive Me, however, I have come in the name of My Father, and you give yourselves away that you do not have the love of God in you to love His glory, not the glory from the people, and if you do not have the love of God, behold, you give what you have; you give lies and with them you intoxicate all people with it, everyone. Oh, you do not receive Me because of the hardening of your hearts; still, if someone is coming in his name, you receive one like that. And I am also telling you this, as I told those two thousand years ago, who did not receive Me. Yes, you receive the one whom no one in heaven sends to come to you, but comes on his own and does on his own everything he does, and the one who puts over the people the work of those who want and try to rule the world on their own.

Oh, behold, even the shepherds of the church of the Romanian people are among them, for they have let themselves lied, bought, led by those who want now to take from their hands the work, the flock and My house. And if even now they do not wake up and do not unite for their life and for the life of their flock, they will remain with empty hands, and the antichrist man will put on airs in the churchly dwelling places, where the Christians will no longer have any place. However, the Lord is coming down on earth as word, and He will give to those who are with Him, and behold, He is giving them from His mouth and He is hurrying with the day of His glory, because it is written about the Lord’s faithful ones, that they will not be ashamed during the bad time, and in the days of hunger they will be satisfied because the Lord will take care of them when no one will be on earth to take care of them.

[/legatura_la_teme][legatura_la_teme id_capitol="3" id_subcapitol="3"]The Samaritan woman wants to call out from near Me too, in her day of remembrance of My works on earth at that time. Oh, and come, tell them about your calling over the earth, oh, daughter of the faith and of God’s confession over the people, you, who spoke with Christ at Israel’s well two thousand years ago!

— I am calling out, Lord, I am calling out with restlessness, and I am calling out and telling those on earth, to whom You are coming now as word:

I am calling out over you, people on earth, I am calling out and tell you that the Lord is calling out to wake you up, to set you to watching and to faith with work, and do wake up, as I woke up at the moment of my speaking with the Lord at the well, and then I lived for Him, I lived the Lord with my life on earth, and now I am among the witnesses in heaven.

Oh, do no longer live, do not longer live your days, people on earth, people all over the earth! Live the days of the Lord, for they have come on earth, and the Lord is working with His word for you from the midst of the Romanian land, and these days of the Lord are written to come; it is written about them in the Scriptures.

Man, the Lord is coming on earth, man! Oh, do no longer seek not to hear, not to know! Come closer to hear what He is going to say to you, what He is going to teach you, what He is telling you to fulfill for the day of His glory, if you want to have part of the joy and the rest of those who had mercy at that time, as I also had mercy during those days when He came into the midst of Israel, stubborn in his heart and faith.

Oh, the stubbornness of the unfaithful helps more the Lord’s appearance and His victory, and it will help even more as in the time of Pharaoh, when the Lord saved His people from bondage, and more than two thousand years ago, when the Lord proved His divinity before those who hardened their heart and faith and was resurrected from His crucifixion. And the today’s stubbornness of the rulers of the world, and even that of the servants of the Romanian Church that hardened their hearts not to believe and bow before the word of the Lord’s glory, this will bring even more glory to the Lord of glory, the One Who is speaking now His word over the earth against the lie in which all men stay, and who do no longer wake up, poor of them.

Behold the days of the Lord with you on earth! Oh, do no longer be blind! The blindness over the people is great, for this is what they want and this is what all the people, who have been set great over the people, seek and try to do. Only that the time is coming for them to see the Lord overcoming them and all their sick order, foreign to the Lord’s will over the earth, for no one seeks after the Lord for the things worked on earth, for people want to be gods, poor of them.

And you, people big and small, righteous or sinful, kings or servants, free or slaves, wake up and do no longer be afraid of the things that come over the earth, incurred by your sins, for there is no more dangerous virus or more murderous pestilence than sin. Oh, do no longer be afraid of viruses, of microbes, of bites, or of bad swellings, but be afraid of that which is really the true enemy of your life: sin. It is the enemy and you do not get rid of it with a mask on your mouth and with gloves on your hands. You are saved from this enemy only by faith, and you need fast and prayer, and these have to be sweet for you, as the love of your hearth, for the faith the Lord is going to overcome all the servants of the lie on earth and it will rule all the time and He will stop the hand of those who mock the holy things.[/legatura_la_teme]

And let the servants of the church stay under God’s will and let them bring the people to the obedience of God; they do not have to submit to the rulers who do not do God’s will but their own will over the people of their nation, and they do the will of the rulers who are upon them, the will of other rulers, without God and without them in their plans over the people and nations for they are not gods and they are without God in all their works, because they do not do the Lord’s will in their life.

Oh, Lord, oh, Lord, oh, Lord, let the people live Your days on earth, and let them no longer live their days, if Your days have come to do their work on earth!

Oh, stay at the helm, Lord! Take the helm from the hand of those who want to destroy more and more Your possession, that is the man, Your most valuable jewel, Lord, and which, they put no price on.

And again, let the angels of the nations stand their watch for which they have been ordained by God, for behold, the borders have been uncovered, they have been torn apart, and the countries can no longer defend their people and borders.

Oh, glory to You for my voice now near You over the people, Lord! I have missed so much, I have longed so much to tell them all what I have just said now, for I am with Your witnesses, and I am in the work that I had to work on earth for Your confession now, in the days of Your coming, Lord, as word over the earth.

— Oh, daughter of humility; it, humility, helped you to bring your steps under your cross and Mine at that time. I will soon come with strength over the blindness on earth, because the blindness put over all people is great, and all have their eyes bound, but I will come with healing, and it will be working over those who have humility, and they will be raised through it.

Let My coming as word over the earth be watched, for I am coming with great healing in the word with power in it, and happy will be those who will seek the healing from above, because on earth satan and the antichrist man are the enemies with a hidden face under the mask of the good for the people, but the wisdom from above is coming; there is coming the heavenly knowledge, the Lord is coming and He is tending His flock to the spring, and the living water has life in it, it has power of resurrection and faith as well, and it is given as a gift to those who have a big heart, to those who are humble, and they will receive faith, for it is written: *«The Lord resists the proud, and gives grace to the humble, to those who are modest, and He gives them in abundance. He gives them grace after grace»*, as it is written. **(See 1 Peter: 5/5-6)** Amen, amen, amen.
