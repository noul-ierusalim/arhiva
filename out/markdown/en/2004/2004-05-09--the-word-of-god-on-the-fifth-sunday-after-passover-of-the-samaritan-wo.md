I am above you on the clouds of heavenly glory and with the feast of the memorial of My meeting with the Samaritan woman at the well in Sychar, at Jacob’s well. Open for Me, children from the gates, to come into My book on My days with you as word. God’s love responds to the love of God, and in this way I made a well of Holy Spirit with you, a spring of word, a river that flows from heaven down to earth for all those who love Me to drink of it, because this is what I spoke to the Samaritan woman: *«…whoever drinks of this water that I give him, will become in him a well of water springing up to eternal life».* Amen, amen, amen.

Oh, My people, God’s love responds to the love of God. I put you to a difficult work for the preparation of My days of feast with you and with those who, by their love of God, will gather together at the well of the Holy Spirit, at a feast of Pentecost *(Whitsuntide)*, and will meet them with the spirit and the word of My mouth, and I longingly desire that the one who drinks of My water, to become in him a spring to water in him the eternal life, the love of God, the living with the saints and with the angels in godly spirit, because the one who worships God, has to worship Him in spirit and truth. Amen. I give you, My people, power of spirit and body to work out My feast with the man in the meeting garden, in the meeting house, at the meeting well, for the spirit of the feast of Holy Spirit and word will hover and will be glorified with sweetness and comfort in all the sky of My courts with you, children who labor for the way of My coming and for the way to Me and of the man who is always thirsty, always, because he drinks of his water and not of My water, which becomes in the one who drinks of it a spring of flowing water for eternal life, because this is how the one who loves Me is and the one whom I love because of his love of God. Amen.

I will give you help because you are little on earth. I will help you, sons, through those who love God. Do not be afraid. I watch in due time and you will have everything I need for My work with you on earth for the feast of Holy Spirit, which we will establish with all its glory before those who, with longing and love and with a thrill of Holy Spirit, come together for its joy and who will love Me, and they will love you too, for the one who is beautiful is loved, and the one who is loved is the one who is being transformed into My image and after My likeness, in the spirit of gentleness and humbleness of the heart, for I have taught them all to be beautiful and I said: *«Learn from Me, for I am gentle and lowly in heart».*

[legatura_la_teme id_capitol="1" id_subcapitol="7"]I stopped by the well in Sychar, by Jacob’s well in My time with My disciples from among the Jews, while I was preparing them for the kingdom of God, with which I came on earth once with My birth from the Virgin, a Man among men and God from God, for the Father had sent Me to become the Son of Man on earth, and then to give Myself with love as a sacrifice of reconciliation of the man to the Father, by the crucifixion that My people did to Me, the people to whom I came to make him a partaker of the grace of My resurrection from the dead, and of My ascension into the Father, so that I may be able to draw all to the Father, all those who will drink of My water with love, of the spring of life, for I am its spring. Amen.

I spoke with the Samaritan woman, who came to take out water: *«Give Me to drink!».* I was tired because of My travelling when I stopped by the well. However, knowing Me that I was of the Jews, she asked Me: *«How is it that you, being a Jew, ask for a drink from me, a Samaritan woman, for Jews have no dealings with the Samaritans».* Then I, Who came from the Father into the midst of Israel, spoke to her: *«If you knew the gift of God, and Who it is Who says to you:* „Give Me a drink,” *you would have asked Him, and He would have given you living water… for everyone who drinks of this water will thirst again, but whoever drinks of the water that I will give him will become in him a well of water springing up to eternal life».*

I spoke with her in the spirit of prophecy, in the spirit of revelation, and she knew this and said to Me: *«I see that You are a prophet»*. And, after I told her everything about her, she went on saying to Me: *«I know that Messiah comes, Who is called Christ, and when He has come, He will declare to us all things».* Then I revealed Myself saying to her: *«I am He, the One Who speaks to you».* And then she went away into the city and confessed Me, and My disciples, who came with food and saw Me speaking with her, spoke to Me: *«Rabbi, eat!»* but I told them: *«I have food to eat you do not know about. My food is to do the will of Him Who sent Me, and to accomplish His work. Behold, I tell you, lift up your eyes, and look at the fields, that they are white for harvest already, and he who reaps receives wages, and gathers fruit to eternal life; that both he who sows and he who reaps may rejoice together».*[/legatura_la_teme]

Oh, My people, My food is to do the will of Him Who sent Me and to do His work. The same is with you, even if you understand little your mystery with Me, My mystery with you, sons. However, you should be wise for your love for Me before the people whenever I am glorified through you, for the one who is beautiful is loved, and the one who is loved is the one who loves Me. Those who see, see you that you are Mine, and those who do not see, do not see who you belong to and who you serve between earth and heaven.

I told you by those who work through My Spirit that the man is hungry because he has always eaten, but who is to tell him on earth except you that the health, the power and the beauty do not come from the flesh? I was indeed the most beautiful among people, and I had been eating food from now and then, to be a true Man and a true God. I had not always eaten and I had not eaten anyhow, sons. I do not know how to tell the man that the food makes him grow old and that the man does not know this and he has been struggling, poor of him, so that he may have and to eat. He cannot learn for not eat, and the man cannot be beautiful within his spirit and his body, and he is sick within his whole body, and that is why, I, the merciful One, call the man to teach him, to give him wisdom, and to be able to perceive with it and not without it, because there is no man to be able see better than the man who has wisdom, the one in him and over his life. Amen.

Oh, My people, behold on earth the word by which I said: *«He who is of the flesh is flesh, and he who is of the spirit is spirit».* He who is born of Me is the one who is born of the Holy Spirit from above, and that one worships God in spirit and truth, not in the flesh and not in the holy mountain or in Jerusalem, but everywhere and all the time in spirit and truth, and two thousand years ago I said to My disciples: *«My food is to do the will of the One Who sent Me and to accomplish His work!»*. Amen.

I am above you, New Jerusalem. I am with the clouds of My glory. The saints and the angels celebrate the feast of today of the well of the meeting within My courts with you, for this well has the Samaritan woman as its angel, the one I met at the well of Israel with and I declared the gospel of the kingdom of the heavens and of the eternal life upon her and upon the city of Samaria. May the mystery of the Samaritan’s will, the mystery of the well of the meeting be blessed into your midst and within My courts with you, because I meet with the man at this well, at this spring of word into your midst and which springs upon you out of My mouth, and I speak with him about the life of the age that is to be, about My kingdom in the man and between man and man, and little by little over the whole earth starting with you, people of My word, because you have the river of life upon you and you grow through it and stay little to be of My kingdom, for it belongs to those who are little. Amen.

Put small cups at the well of the meeting, sons, for the saints have come in thousands to drink, and open your eyes to each other to see My glory and of My saints’ in My gardens with you. Be the way to each other for My glory with you, to your glory with Me and set yourselves as an example of the kingdom of the heavens in you before those who will call out the heavenly trumpet and whom I will gather at the feast of Holy Spirit. Be hungry for the work of Holy Spirit over the earth and over the man who comes at the spring, for the man lives not only on bread alone, and woe to the one who lives only on bread alone, and blessed is the one who lives on every word that comes out of My mouth, for this is what I said: *«Whoever drinks of the water that I will give him, will become in him a well of water springing up to eternal life».* Amen.

Oh, My people, the man gives his eternal life for his temporary life, for the one that he has from Me, My people. However, you shall speak to the man that he lives not only on bread alone, but one lives especially with the word of God, which has in it the life and its mystery and its eternal life. You shall be a teacher on earth in all your life in which you have learned from Me, for the man is slow for My fulfillment in him, for his fulfillment in Me. I have always, always, given you the water of life. Become a spring of flowing water to the eternal life of many people, Jerusalem of My graces. I give you power and help enough to be able to prepare for Me the man who will come to the spring of the Holy Spirit and for His sweet glory that comes from Me, your God, the God of patience and comfort, My people.

May the well of the angels, which you work out now and prepare it beautifully, be blessed! Oh, do not worry! I am watching and I give you powers and angels and help to prepare it beautifully for the feast and to be able to put on it My blessing in a day of the Pentecost feast, and the blessing of My angel and that of all the angelic hosts, sons. We will make it a shelter as well, because I will be able to help you by those who love God, and this garden of My meeting with the man at the spring will be more and more beautiful with every passing day, more and more heavenly, and more and more filled with the heaven and with the spirit from heaven, sons, as I prophesied about all My glory with you for the last days, and as I have spoken about it during all the time of this work, which has been speaking on earth for fifty years, My people. And soon, soon, we will celebrate the feast of its Pentecost and we will glorify ourselves with it on earth and up into the clouds, and from the clouds down on to the earth, and the clouds of My glory will carry you, those who love Me with longing and with Holy Spirit, in My chariot of victory, sons. Amen, amen, amen.

Oh, children who put the voice of My word into My book! Be only eyes and ears for Me after we will have spent the day of feast, because I still want to speak into the book and I want you to know that all who are asleep and who hear their names from your little mouths will gather together in a great number at My feast of Holy Spirit with you, and I tell you that they have already gathered together and are waiting for you with My joy into the midst of My gardens with you. Amen.
