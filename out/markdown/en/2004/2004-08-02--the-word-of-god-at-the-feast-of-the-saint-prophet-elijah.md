My glory is upon you, Jerusalem of My coming. I have built you for My glory, for it is written that *«The Lord has built up Zion and He has appeared in His glory and has responded to the prayer of the destitute, and He has not despised their prayer».*Amen, amen, amen.

[legatura_la_teme id_capitol="1" id_subcapitol="6"]I am a great God and I sit on the cherubim. Clouds and haze are around Me. Righteousness and justice are the strong foundations of the throne of My glory. A fire goes before Me and burns up My adversaries on every side, and My lightning lights up crosses and lights up the world and the earth sees and trembles before the face of the Master of the earth. Amen. My glory is like fire, and I let Myself be seen with it by the faithful disciple of My prophet Elijah. I became a chariot of fire and with My glory I covered his teacher and I took him up into those that are not seen; I took him within the chariot of fire of My glory, for a fire comes out of Me and goes before Me and it cleanses My going from all My enemies in the sky and on earth and from the deep then when the lightning of My glory crosses the sky and lights the world, and the earth trembles before My face, for I am the Master of everything on it. Amen.

The fire of My glory is not the fire which burns like the fire on earth, and which is consumed while burning. My glory is a fire which illuminates and gives wisdom and brings the world and its kings into submission, for *«God is a consuming fire»*, as it is written. For two thousand years, the fire of My glory has become the grace of Holy Spirit over the faithful man who has been following Me, who has been living in Me and I am happy with the man I live in and from whom I shine with My glory and with the word of the glory of My word, for the Word became flesh and dwelt in those who saw My greatness as that of the One born of the Father, full of grace and truth. Amen. Oh, for two thousand years I have become grace and truth in the one who I have been dwelling in and glorifying Myself among the people to make My saints on earth by the work of My glory upon the man. The fire of My glory is not consumed, but rather it is glorified before the man by the work of the grace, as in the days of Moses the burning bush, which I was hiding with My voice in, was not consuming because of the fire in it when I was speaking to Moses, and the word of My mouth was written by fire on the stone, to remain on earth as the commandment of life and full of grace. Amen.[/legatura_la_teme]

I overflow over you with a river of word and great is My word, My people, and let it become rivers in you, and let the rivers clap their hands, as it is written, and let them serve the Lord with joy, for the Spirit says: *«Come to the gates of the Lord with songs of praise; enter into His gates with thanksgiving, into His courts with praise. Give thanks to Him and bless His name. For the Lord is good. His loving kindness endures forever, His faithfulness to all generations».* Amen.

Oh, My people, I glorify Myself into your midst with the saints and I exhort you to the zeal for God, to the grace from grace for I want to be rivers of grace from you on earth, so that your work of grace may be enough for Me and for My Father and for the heaven of saints and angels, because a wonderful work for heaven comes out of grace, My people. I have taught you, sons, and I have also taught you through the saints to love the life which makes you suffer, for the man does not feel his life but only when it suffers in man, and then its pain is the dearest, because My kingdom with the man nestles in it. When the apostle Paul prayed to Me for the healing of his sufferance, I told him this: *«My grace is sufficient for you, for My power is made perfect in the sufferance in man, in the weakness in man».*

Oh, how much grace and how much work of grace have been flowing over the people from the heart of My prophet Elijah! My grace in man makes the man love Me and then it makes him to serve Me with great zeal, with great longing, with great mercy on My wound caused by the loss of man. I cry in heaven and on earth after the man with grace, after the man with pain in it for God, for My wound from man, and that is why I have always fed you with the word of My mouth, My people, and I want to see you sharing it with the man who seeks Me and does not know to seek after Me and to find Me, for the man who seeks after Me from his outside, that one cannot become a dwelling of My kingdom and a spring of grace by his pain after God. Elijah the prophet was consumed with longing after God and he was praying to see God’s face and he saw the face of the Lord’s glory, and the grace was working with power from him for the sons of Israel to turn them back to God’s love.

[legatura_la_teme id_capitol="3" id_subcapitol="2"]Oh, My people, the work of the grace is great and much and the man who tries to work over the man with his love for Me cannot work well without a teacher, without the Holy Spirit, by Whom I make prophets over the people. The man who tries to work in My name over the man does not know that it is not enough only to help the man believe that God is and to come from his worldly way after God. The man has to learn then the way and its life and the work on it and the fruit of this work, which is learned from God and not from man, and I have been wounded, since I made the man and to this very day, for the man has no longer been learning from God so that he may know after that.

Oh, the man does no longer use the life that suffers in him, the grace and the spring from Me in man, in order that I may become counselor from Me on man, the one who makes signs and miracles over the unfaithful ones from which every man is caching the spirit of world. I can no longer exhort the man by the word, for it does not come to Me if I do not make signs and miracles on earth to make the man believe that God is and that the Lord is on the way after the man to turn the man back to life and to hate death, for the life that does not suffer in man it does not suffer because the man lives in death.[/legatura_la_teme]

I prayed to the Father to make Me sower and harvester over the earth. I pray to the Father for you to be My strong help in My hard work over the man. Stand up in this day of memorial of the prophet Elijah and pray insistently and with grace with all the words of praying, which I, the Lord, have given you to bring them for you and for every man before Me. Let My grace go up into flames in you, for the life full of grace is a sweet life and I glorify Myself from the man, sons. The great prophet Elijah is consuming his spirit among those in heaven as on earth when he was in his body, and his zeal is asking Me for My salvation from the unfaithful man on earth.

However, I, My people, have been waiting for the resurrection of the dead and, again, I have been waiting for every man who was and is, and for the life of the age that is to be for those who have sanctified themselves before Me. I cry with the Father and with the saints, and We cry after rest, and the man lives carelessly on earth. I prayed to the Father to make Me sower on earth. I pray to the Father to give Me workers, to have praying people, to have intercessors for man, but the Father has nowhere to give Me from, for the world and its teachers are only water and dust, and there is no man to desire it, and there is no one to have zeal after Me, the man has nowhere to know and to believe that I am the way, the truth and the life and that it is not the man.

Oh, sons anointed from the little garden of My word, be earnest praying people for My victory over the earth and ask so that in your work with Me I may always give you the zeal of the prophet Elijah, who was crying for My mercy before Me. Ask from Me, and work out everything you ask for, and ask together when you ask, and be one spirit, for it is written: *«For where two or three are gathered together in My name, to ask from Me with the faith that they will receive, they will receive the accomplishment of their requests».* Amen.

My glory is like fire. My word upon you is fire, sons. Eat it more than bread, and you will feel My burning in you, My mourning, and My pain will suffer in you and you will multiply My grace in you, the life, which suffers in man, and you will seek after My healing. Amen. I have come to throw fire on earth and I wish it were already kindled. I have made you My anointed ones in these days that I may have work from heaven on earth. Ask with passion, with hearts full of passion and ask for My comfort in man. Work with the spirit and with the power of Elijah, the prophet, and may your faith be blessed, and may it be in you from Me, that I may fulfill through it everything I have to accomplish for the kingdom of the heaven on earth. You should work everything I have told you to do and do not let it unaccomplished, and also you should not work what I have told you not to do, for on earth it is as on earth, and in heaven it is as on heaven, and you should not do the man’s will on earth, but you should rather do My will. Amen. If you look on the earth to a big house when it is questioned whether it has worked according to the law on earth, then if the owners of the house do not have any good answers for its entire accomplishment, then they give gifts to those who inquire them about the law requirements, and they grant them a delay for this is how it is on earth. Behold, on earth it is as on earth, and in heaven it is as in heaven, where righteousness and justice are the strong foundations of My throne, and out of My mouth comes fire, which burns everything up on My way before Me, to be in heaven as on earth. Amen.

Elijah, My prophet, is feeding on My glory into your midst, My people. I have built you for My glory, for it is written: *«For the Lord has built up Zion. He has appeared in His glory. He has responded to the prayer of the destitute, and has not despised their prayer».* Amen, amen, amen.

*— Oh, Lord, take my prayer into consideration, for I join with the prayer of those who pray to You for Your people, to make Yourself known as the God of the ages, Lord. Exalt Yourself with Your people and with Your hand above the nations, so that they may see Your strength. Renew Your miracles and confirm through them Your power and Your right arm, so that no one may say:* „There is no one besides us”. Be merciful on Your people, who You spoke about as Your firstborn, and be merciful on Your citadel, on Your house, on the place of Your glory, and fill it with Your brightness. Strengthen on earth the truth of Your work in these days and fulfill all Your word, rewarding those who patiently and faithfully wait: and Lord, give credence to Your prophets and listen to the prayers of those who cry out to You for Your people, to become known over the earth as the God of the ages, Lord. Amen.

Oh, I give you of my zeal for the Lord my God, Whom I loved more than any people on earth, oh, children from the end! Do not forget about your ongoing union with the Lord. Strengthen yourselves in Him whenever you feel your absence from Him, and if you do not feel it, receive one another just as Christ has received you, for you have the work that has been waited for seven thousand years by all saints. Stay together in Him. Seek after the Lord’s rest in you. The Lord has no one to have rest in from His pains on earth. Be the Lord’s comfort and be the fire of His pain and ask from the Father for the comfort of His Son, Whom I have always prepared His way to you and your way with Him on earth and your union with Him, for He has built you for His glory. Amen. Live into Him and for Him, for He has built you now for Him and not for you. The grace of the Lord is with you, for it is written: «He gives grace to the humble». Amen.

Oh, Lord, give them always Your love from the Father so that they may carry it as You did, and let them be loving and merciful sons to the Father just like You, and give them help in their needs and give them the wisdom that You have been giving to all those who love You, to all those who rely on You, fulfilling You in them with Your grace. Amen.

— Oh, only them I have on earth, and I have you in heaven, oh, My saints who have been praying to Me for them! I listen from the earth to your praying in unity and I fulfill it by grace. Amen.

Oh, My people, let My wisdom grow in you, and you should give from it so that it may grow and that it may open up like the springs, which spring up when they flow. I have built you for My glory and I take your prayer into consideration, but embrace Me with all the pain in Me and become My throne and My footstool, so that I may shepherd the earth from the place of My glory, for I, Jerusalem, have built you for My glory so that I may be the Shepherd into your midst according to the Father’s heart, for the Father has been waiting for you with Me and He has been waiting for Me with you, and the Father has been waiting more and more to appear over all the earth with My glory in your midst, and that I may become known with you on earth as the God of the ages and of the life of the age that is to be. Amen, amen, amen.
