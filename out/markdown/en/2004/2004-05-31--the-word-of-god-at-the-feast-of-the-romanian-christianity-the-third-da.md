There is still a little word and then We may leave the peace of the Holy Spirit upon Us and upon you. On the third day, let Us, We and you, please the saints and the angels and those who are asleep. Amen.

Oh, children from the gates, I, the Lord, still put a little strength into your small bodies, and where I put it, it is done according to My word. The Father, the Son and the Holy Spirit sit in the Trinity and bless now the rest of those who have labored, and blessed are those who have taken and tasted of My table of word of the heavenly Trinity, Which is called God. Amen.

The saints and the angels were your heavenly guests and together with you they served the Father, the Son and the Holy Spirit. The invisible heaven has become a border against any evil, which would have come near to trouble you and put to unrest the spirit of the feast of Holy Spirit, because I, the Lord, told you sons, to pray together with the entire people for comfort and for heavenly help and for harmony over the feast, and the saints and the angels watched in this way, and now they give glory to the Father, to the Son and to the Holy Spirit and honor God, for the Three are One. Amen.

Oh, how comfortingly I have been teaching those who have travelled to come and receive from heaven! It hurts Me that the man on earth does no longer read the Scriptures and that the people do not come together to look into them and to understand them, and then to receive their fulfilling work upon the man. Oh, the man does no longer know to have a Father as I have and to do what his Father tells him to do. Behold, I cannot do anything from Me if I do not see the Father doing as well, and those that the Father does, these I, the Son, also do exactly. I told the Jews who were fighting to find fault with Me because of My word by which I called Myself the Son of the Father, and I was telling them this: *«For the Father has affection for the Son, and shows Him all things that He Himself does. He will show Him greater works than these that you may marvel. For as the Father raises the dead and gives them life, even so the Son also gives life to whom He desires. For the Father judges no one, but He has given all judgment to the Son, that all may honor the Son, even as they honor the Father. Most assuredly, I tell you, he who hears My word, and believes Him Who sent Me has eternal life and does not come into judgment, but has passed out of death into life».* Amen.

[legatura_la_teme id_capitol="7" id_subcapitol="1"]Oh, sons, whoever believes in Me and in the Father, that one does not come to judgment because he has life. The Son has life in Himself like the Father, and behold the hour when those in the graves hear the voice of the Son, and those who hear it come to life. And again, the hour is coming when all those in the dwelling of the dead will come up at My voice for the judgment of their works, and My judgment is right, because I do not do My will but My Father’s Who has sent Me to do it, and the Father confesses Me. Amen.[/legatura_la_teme]

Oh, blessed is the one who believes in Me and in the One Who has sent Me as word on earth! The man does no longer know to have a Father as I know. [legatura_la_teme id_capitol="3" id_subcapitol="4"]Here is what I tell those who pose as shepherds with people: you who boast about God, search the Scriptures, because you think that in them you have eternal life; and these are which testify about Me, yet you will not come to Me, that you may have life, because My word in them and the one spoken by My mouth today does not live in you, because you do not believe in the One Whom the Father has sent, and that One testifies about Me, and you have never heard His voice, nor seen His face. You do not receive Me, and if another comes into My name, that one you receive him, because you receive glory from one another and you do not seek the glory that comes from only God. And if you do not believe those written in the Scriptures about Me, how can you believe My word of today, which is coming with the clouds with a noise like the sound of the rushing of a mighty wind, which comes quickly and becomes a teacher on earth?

Oh, My people, the man does not know to have God as his Father. I speak to you as in the Scriptures of two thousand years ago and I tell you that if someone comes into My name, the one the world receives and its teachers who receive glory from one another, and this is the judgment of the world. He who has lived a false life will receive his reward, for he will receive the false Christ and will perish together with him, and he who receive Me, the Son of the Father Sabaoth, that one searches the Scriptures and finds eternal life in them and they are those that testify about Me. Amen.[/legatura_la_teme]

Sons, I have given instruction of eternal life to all who have come to the spring, for that is why I call them when I call. I do not call them for the food and for the rest of their body, but I call them to give them the word of eternal life, the word which testifies about Me, for I am the One Who become word, according to My Father’s will. Amen.

The saints and the angels sit down in the rest of the fulfillment of My word, which has made a feast of Holy Spirit in My gardens with you, and those who are asleep, who have stayed invisibly at the feast receiving of its joy and of the fulfillment of the Scriptures that are about those in the dwelling of the dead who hear My word of their resurrection, also stay ready to come to rest after My word releases them once with your calming down after your labor, by which you prepared for Me a time of fellowship with you for those who have come to the spring. I have given them to have and to drink, and happy will be those who will have drunk to their resurrection. Amen.

The Holy Spirit remains in you, sons, and let yourselves be always seized by Him, as also My disciples were seized two thousand years ago when I gave them and when they confessed Me to all those who had come together in Jerusalem, and to all those who had heard the voice of My apostles, for wherever the Holy Spirit is, He is imparted to each one of them in his own language and His power and work are so great when He is poured out from the Father and from Me for the resurrection of the man, for He has eternal life in Him. Amen.

We rest now, My people. Let Our rest remain in the Holy Spirit. And let Us rest in one another: We in you and you in Us, for the eternal life and confessing all over the earth the Word of the Father, Which calls the man from death to life. Amen.

From time to time I put you to hard work and to unrest for My coming in word with those on earth, for it is the time of My coming, and you are My way. Peace to you now! Give thanks for everything. Keep away from those who separate the man from God and watch over those who were born from above, for the birth from above is expensive, sons, and it has to be valued in man and protected in order to be. No enemy can remain over the one in whom My love remains and is wrapped within it. Peace to you! Remain in My love! Amen.

Oh, children who are the gates for My word so that it may be able to come and set down into the book! Take care of My life from the sons of Jerusalem. Life is eternal, and I have to remain the life of My people. This is what I wanted to tell you, and this is how you are to work over My people, and let him remain like Me, for it is written about those who love and live Me: *«They will be like Him»*. Amen, amen, amen.
