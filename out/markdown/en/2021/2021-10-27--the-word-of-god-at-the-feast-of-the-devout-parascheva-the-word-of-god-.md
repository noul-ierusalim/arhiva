My book with you stays open and I am writing Myself down with you in it, for you help Me in the work of My word and its book in this time to be set on earth, sons in the cita-del of My word. In the feasts of saints I am coming near with My voice and remaining with you in the word for I have great care of you, of My work through you, of all that I have to carry with you and for you to carry with Me, sons, for you are small and helpless, and you bear burdens, oh, and I have not got any strong people with its love for God and for you, and I have no one to help Me with, and even more than this you help the people that has My word as exhortation of life, with which I come to you for them and for the whole world, sons.

[legatura_la_teme id_capitol="2" id_subcapitol="6"]Oh, who else knows, who else would like to know that God reigns everything between heaven and earth, and that are not the people who do this? People are against God with all the inclinations of their nature and of their desires without God and they do nothing else. How-ever, I watch at the helm to keep the heaven and the earth so that God’s plan may remain above, the work that I make with those in heaven and especially the holy watch against the folly of those without God, oh, for the man’s separation from God is too much and too great, and satan’s spirit, the work of the antichrist man struggles with all his might to control the people every way possible so that he may have people to work through; however, it is to be known from heaven and down to earth that the great victory belongs to God, to the Son of God Who came on earth against satan to take the man out of destruction as it is written, and that is why I, the Lord, come on earth as word and the word once spoken and written, has power, for God is the Word, oh, sons. [/legatura_la_teme]

Now, We are sitting down with you at the table of word, for the host of the devout females from heaven have gathered together in a Christian feast because of the devout Para-scheva, the disciple who shares heavenly mercy to those who seek to receive mercy from God, for the people on earth are grieved and oppressed from the fruit of the sin and they seek with prayer after My mercy and after that of My saints.

We are sitting at the table of feast with the retinue that carries this merciful and giving saint, and We give comfort and exhortation of life over the people of My word and over those who take and put over them the fulfillment of My exhortation for those who love their life with God.

Therefore, peace to you, sons in the citadel! And peace to you, people nourished with the glory of My word! You should know that without My exhortation, always, always brought on this table, you could no longer stay awake, dear Christian son, for the sleepiness on earth is too great, it is so great that hardly anyone of you is able to wake up from it, and I am telling you this: take care of your soul, and that care of it more than of your body, take care Christian, who keep on the way of My word to have it on the table, and then take it and fulfill it in such a way that you may not be counted among those who don’t watch while wait-ing for the Bridegroom to come from above.

[legatura_la_teme id_capitol="6" id_subcapitol="3"]Oh, sons of My word, stay awake, stay awake so that you may not loose what you have received from God through the faith given by Him to follow Him on earth! You shall not gather things on earth, no sons, no! Keep the way with the Lord, for all the people will lose their possessions on earth, and the cost of their possessions is too expensive, too painful, oh, and those who gather things on earth have no treasure in heaven to go for it and to rejoice over it.

Oh, take care of your soul, sons nourished from heaven. I look from heaven over the sons of men to see whether there is someone to understand and seek after God, but all have turned away, all have become wicked and there is no one to do good; there is none, as it is written. Oh, God’s image has disappeared from men’s likeness. The adornment of the man’s face and of his head has gone long ago from the earth.

When I built man, I built him with the manly adornment; I did not built him gla-brous, rather I adorned him beautifully. Oh, where is the men’s beautiful image? I do not speak a word of judgment, but that of pain and longing after the man built by God with a beautiful image. Man takes after man, and no one looks back to his ancestors and saints, to those who were pleased by God, and in heaven is pain because of the man’s carelessness, who does not have Me as his God. People without prayer to God are easily caught in satan’s hook. Man does no longer receive Me; he does no longer call Me but only in time of trial and then he forgets Me again and again. [/legatura_la_teme]

Oh, sons who seek after Me, who wait for Me in this word, oh, if you have found Me, if you have known Me, then keep with all your power to this light of word and turn it into works over you, for it is hard the forgiveness of the one who hits, despises or doubts of this word from heaven descended for you, those who eat of it the goodness of life, the faith in the kingdom of the heavens, which I, the Lord, gave you to built in you and for you by the fulfillment of My word, which brings the heaven on earth, the paradise, forgiveness, love, oh, for the Lord is coming on earth as word, and this mystery and its work is great glory, sons.

[legatura_la_teme id_capitol="6" id_subcapitol="1"]Oh, learn and learn again how to keep away from sin, how to keep away from the sin in you, those around you by keeping away from sin. Oh, learn this, but not all from within you or from you, but from Me, sons, for you do not belong to anyone if you receive instruction only from you, for I am the Teacher, sons. Make way to your protecting angels to Me, for be-hold there is great pain in heaven and on earth, because the men’s protecting angels are weep-ing, they are weeping because they do not have any way to come before God to rejoice over his face, for people do the works of their separation from God and they are taken by the evil angels into satan’s book, because this are fed with joy by those who cause sufferance to God. [/legatura_la_teme]

God’s angels weep, they weep and have no power over people, for people have turned their back and face away from God; they turned to the sins that are pleased to satan and to all the perishing things on earth.

Oh, keep away from pains, sons! When God lets you take your own decisions, or on behalf of your people or over the people, then you do this, and you can do this because God lets you do as you like it. However, when it comes to those that you can no longer decide when they come to seize you or to test you, then willing or not you give up for the things that come upon you without asking you, and you allow them to come, as they have been gathered by the things worked by you. Therefore, let man learn how to keep away from pains, how to do good and follow it and it will go well with him.

[legatura_la_teme id_capitol="6" id_subcapitol="3"]All those who seek after help from God, let all learn to do good to get away from pains and to rejoice over the Lord and over His protection, for I want comfort over man and that is why I say: Blessed is the one who has God as his love!

Therefore, sons, carry Me in your body, in your conduct, in your thought, in your speaking and look, in your visible image, in the love of the spirit in you. And when you want to speak with your brother, then speak with him, son, as you would speak to God, not as to one who works evil, for otherwise you do not know how to be with God as with man, and with man as with God. However, I am the Teacher and I teach you so beautifully, My people, and I want you to have a beautiful conduct to which all God’s angels may be draw, look and gather around you to their joy, for I, the Lord, want to be comfort in heaven and on earth, and it has to be worked and then shared, oh, and there is nothing to do when My face sets on the face and on the conduct of those who hear My voice and set down to work Me, but they also have to make for Me a bedding among them, among those who stay on earth with the angels and saints. [/legatura_la_teme]

And behold, the devout Parascheva greets My table of word with you and she exhorts you to the love of God, sons.

— Lord and Teacher, I am reminding them of Your commandment, and to all those on earth, and I am saying: Love God! Love Him with all your being, with all you are and love Him to be with Him from everlasting to everlasting then! Make your life from the love for the Lord, the love of your heart, your work, and especially your conduct for God’s sons. He has always given you His love and you should always, always give yours to Him. Do not forget that happy are and will be those who love the Lord, that these promises are written and they will be shared as no one can understand with his mind.

And You, Lord, stay in them with Your love moment by moment so that they may work through You and not by themselves, for those who want to do something by themselves are piti-ful, forgetting how small the man is and how great God is.

Oh, glory to You with them, glory to You over them, and make it visible, Lord, and make the nations go to it and prepare the great salvation, oh, Lord!

Oh, glory to You from us, those from above, and from them here, where we bow and kiss when You take us with You, when You come with Your saints!

— Oh sons, My saints wish you as God’s sons and the sons of His glory. I want to set on earth My love as man’s happiness who seeks, but I am the One Who have what man seeks, and he does not find as he seeks. I reveal to you with My love over the earth. My Word is My long cry after man. Like a trumpet let on the earth sound God’s word all over and let it be heard over the nations!

Oh, men all over the earth, the Lord is coming! You will believe that He comes when you see Him, and I want to come with the joy that is given to all, but let all wait for it, for I call after man to wait and not to come unexpectedly.

Oh, sons in the citadel of My word, peace to you! Do the work of My coming as I come, and set Me in everybody’s way to hear how I call them.

Peace to you! Ask, sons, what keeps you from My work with you and I will give you. Amen, amen, amen.
