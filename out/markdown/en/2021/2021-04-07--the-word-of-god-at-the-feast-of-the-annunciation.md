I am finding wakefulness at the gates *(Gates - those by whom the Lord speaks, the Lord comes as word, r.n.)* and I am coming into the citadel of My word on the hearth of the Romanian people; I am coming with a feast of word and I am proclaiming Myself with a day of royal feast, the feast of the Annunciation, the angelic proclamation brought to the Virgin who would give Me birth in the flesh on earth; she was to receive God, the Son, by the proclamation of the angel sent by God to her to prepare her to be the Mother of God, My mother, oh, and how beautiful is on earth when a heavenly messenger comes and proclaims from heaven joy and glory of the fulfilling word!

Oh, every word from God is powerful; it does not pass without being fulfilled, as the angel Gabriel, God’s messenger, told to the Virgin Mother; he told her a word full of power, and he told her that the word that comes from God is full of the power of fulfillment, and then she believed the angel and submitted to the Lord for the fulfillment of the word spoken over her.

Oh, behold, I am the fruit. I am the word full of power, the fulfilled one, the word proclaimed by the angel. I am the word, for the Virgin carried Me in her womb after the angel’s proclamation, and the Holy Spirit descended over her and then I was born and I received the name of Jesus, given by the angel, and the Son of the Most High, and then I was called the Son of the Father in heaven and on earth.

Oh, peace over the country of My coming as word on earth! Peace to you, My today’s country! On your hearth, on the hearth of the Romanian people, I have been writing My today’s book, the book of the Word of God, **(Apoc: 14/6)** the word from heaven, and which carries in it the power of fulfillment, for an angelic word is written: *«For no word from God shall be void of power!».* **(Luke: 1/37)**

The word sent from God, and which is fulfilled on earth, would be to the power of the faith of the people, that people may not receive rebuke or punishment for their unbelief, as is now happening on earth, and I am especially saying this for the word of God that flows like a river **(Apoc: 22/1, 2)** on the hearth of the Romanian people, and which was set aside by the house of God, by the church of the Lord, by the hierarchs and especially by the priests, oh, for I have not come to knock at another door with the glory of My word, with the heavenly proclamation that I share, but I have come to there has been no one to revive them to faith, to the receiving of the heavenly love over the house of God, and the flock is now suffering humiliation from the antichrist man, and it is suffering because God is not welcomed in His house on earth, and behold, behold, His house is now devastated by the antichrist enemy, by his orders coming out from hiding, *(Orders not to let Christians go to worthy of worship, oh, and he also wants the Christians to worship him, who is unworthy, who here he is, by his satanic power of the lawless lies, gives himself out for God, he shows off as strong so that he may sit in God’s temple, as it is written that he wants to do this. **(2Thess:2/4)** However, I am coming soon on earth and going out into his way to destroy him with the breath of My mouth, **(2Thess: 2/8)** with the word of My mouth, a word written on earth in its book, the book of the Word of God, with which I come into his way to frustrate his deceitful and lawless lies, and which are for those who are on the verge of perdition, who did not receive and do not receive the love of the truth to be saved and freed from satan’s mouth, oh, and behold the deceptive work, sent through Oh, do you see what happens to you if you keep on denying the word of God, the faith in this word, which watches for you?

Oh, behold, the time has come for you to need protection, and you are not one with Me, with the glory of My word, with which I also breathe over satan, as it is written to be destroyed by the breath of My mouth, by My word spoken upon him.

I want to fulfill the Scriptures of new heaven and of new earth, and you do not watch with Me, you have not received Me in My house so that I may have power for you and through you during this time of trial. Oh, I said that I still have great souls in the Romanian people, and I want to wake them up to watching and praying with power in it, so that satan may be overcome and be brought to shame by the Lamb of God, of Whom, satan does no longer want to hear on earth, only that his destiny is sealed, for My word breathes with power and destruction over satan and over his lies, oh, and My word is powerful and wants to wake up those who are great in their soul and that they may watch in prayer near Me, and I to fulfill, and these great souls are from among the servants at the altars, are those full of grace among them, and near whom My angels stay close on My behalf, and the prayers of My saints before Me, as it is written, **(Apoc: 6/7-10)** oh, and let them cry out and say like king David in times of trials and let them pray like this: *„Come, Lord, do not depart from us, for the great trial is close, it is here, and there is no one to help us! Come, Lord, for the dogs, the lions and the mad bulls have surrounded us, but You save us from their mouth to proclaim Your name and word to Your church and that the nations of the earth may be afraid of You and come to worship You and to proclaim their Lord to those who will believe through them and to the people that is born to preach Your miracles, Your word and salvation!”.*

Oh, My mother, this is how are to pray to God those among the servants full of grace of the Lord’s house, and who, not by their power and strength but by My Spirit, will call out the salvation of My house and of My flock from under the yoke of the antichrist man, who, here he is, how he wants to touch the order of the Lord’s house and of its holy laws! However, We are coming with heavenly armies and working with the angels, mother, for the angels are Our servants and they are watching on Our behalf for those who are receiving from Us the love of the truth to be saved, mother.

Oh, but today you are being confessed by the angels that you are My mother, as then when the archangel Gabriel told you that you would be the mother of God, and now the angels are singing to you, mother, to the memorial of that wonderful day, when you received Me in your womb from the Holy Spirit, and when you listened, too, and when I also listened, and by My obedience I fulfilled Our Father’s will, oh, My mother; We are the fruit of obedience, and now We are together in heaven.

Oh, and We are coming down today with the Spirit, We are coming to those who want Us to not be together, *(Christian cults that do not honor the Mother of God, r.n.)* who sing to God and keep on singing to Him, rejoicing them greatly; for I suffer bitterly from them, because they do not give glory to the grace the Father has given to you, that they do not want to bless you for your choice done by the Father, oh, and what a pain on Me from them, for you are My mother and you are with Me near the Father!

What are We going to do with these, who have flaws in their wisdom, and who have made their own way to God, mother? Oh, how shall We do to draw them back to the root from where they have pulled themselves out, or their ancestors, who broke off and did not remain in the vine to bring forth fruit in the vine, and not just at random, as they are doing now?

Oh, only that those in My house would become wise to stand up to the faith in My word and to its fulfillment then, to go after the lost sheep and to bring them back, mother, and to tell them the truth for the faith and for the walking in the faith then! They say that they stepped aside because of the errancy in the church, oh, but what a great sin do they commit with this judgment of their minds! They forget that they too are no better or truer, and they forget this because of the spirit of pride, for without knowing this, this is what they work for, they work pride and they work a spirit of judgment. And even worse than this is something else: they have fallen into the sin of wandering of soul and faith, because they do not know from where the way comes to the people, on which, behold, these have started to walk and go, and as they may say, to the love of God. Oh, no, let them not believe that but let them rather repent and ask with humility that God may give them a broken and humble heart so that they may be able to look back, to the Christian order that I, My disciples and their followers left then, and let them also look at themselves and have wisdom to see that they have left the way from which I have worked over the earth for faith and for the fulfillment of the works of faith in men.[/legatura_la_teme]
