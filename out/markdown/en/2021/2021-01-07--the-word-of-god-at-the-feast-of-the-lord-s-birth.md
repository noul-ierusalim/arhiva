On this day of holy feast for Me, sons, and for you as well, those who believe what I am giving you to believe, I am coming down as word of birth in My manger with you, in My manger with you in the midst of the world on earth, and I am coming into the world as I came two thousand years ago, when My mother Virgin, helped Me to come, after the angel had come to her and told her that I would come to be born on earth from the Father and from her virginal body, and she obeyed believing. And now, I am being helped by you, after I have put faith in you for My coming as word into the world, that I am born as Word in My manger with you and you help Me to be able to come into the world, and the world, sons, has nothing in Me, and I have nothing and no one in this world but you alone, oh, sons, and I have in it all those who believe like you that the Lord is coming on earth as word, and if I have you, behold, I am bringing to you from haven and I am making you to be the distributers of God and I am also making you messengers before Me, to announce My way and the day of My full glory and that of God’s kingdom over all those on earth, as I am also King over all now, because I am the Son of the Father, King of the kingdom of the heavens, sons, and to have all those with power of faith and love of faith like that of My mother Virgin, who easily believed and received God in His coming into the world two thousand years ago, oh, I had lived mysteriously in the world for thirty years, and then I appeared as the Son of the Father, and those who heard and understood Who I was, were afraid and fulfilled the Scripture of My crucifixion, and then I also fulfilled the Scriptures of My resurrection and the great victory against satan, the ruler of this age, and I overcame the death with My great victory. And here I am, after two thousand years, with the memory of the day of My birth from the mother Virgin, for this is how My Father has been well pleased with Me and for Me and for her, and on this day I am spending with you the feast of My birth, the feast of the Child Jesus Christ, and We are sitting at the table of holy feast and writing a book from heaven with you on earth, sons, and I am saying, in My great love: Peace on earth, and much glory to God in the highest, glory to the Savior Jesus!

[legatura_la_teme id_capitol="1" id_subcapitol="7"]I am the Savior. I am the Messiah of the Father, just as I was two thousand years ago. I am the Christmas of My Christians, sons, and I belong to those who fulfill the mystery of faith and My will with them and over them on earth. Oh, the coming of Father Christmas is not a mere story and that is all, as the people have used to call the feast of My birth. This holy memory is true and it has remained from that time and up to this day, for on the day of My birth I received gifts at the manger from the three Magi coming from far away to found Me, to see Me and to announce My birth, and they were prompted by My Father to start travelling to the manger of My birth, for My Father wanted Me to receive gifts, as the little children receive from their parents, and the mystery of the Father Christmas started at that moment and has continued, and the mystery begins immediately at sight and the little ones receive gifts in the name of the Father Christmas, in My name, for Christmas is My name, the name of the One Who created the heaven and the earth, the visible and invisible things of God’s creation, and woe and woe again to those who lie under the name of science people to overcome the One Who was and is the Maker of all thing! Woe to them, woe to those who make bold to lie again and again and to tell that they know, that they have revelations, that they are intelligent, that they know the things that are under the control of the Lord God!

[/legatura_la_teme][legatura_la_teme id_capitol="3" id_subcapitol="1"]Oh, how much fraudulence in the world, oh, how much of it, sons! God has made the visible and the invisible things and He has set a boundary between them to be known what it is for the view of the men’s eyes and what is not to be seen by them among those that are under God’s control? And then, who are you, the one who are called by the name of a science man? You are a thief, the one who has stolen from those that are under God’s control and lie to the world that you know. Oh, you do not know anything but only what God has allowed you to know, and you cannot be more than that, no matter how many devices you may make to help you see with them, and with which you go higher than God because of your conceit and desire to be more than God; however, you are the inquiring one, and all those who are like you are the offspring of the one who stole from the tree of knowledge, but with all these, he was not able to know more than he was naked before God; naked, ashamed and hidden in himself, and he was revealed as a thief who reached out his hand to take from there where was nothing for him.

The man who calls himself by the name of a science man does not believe in Father Christ-mas, that the Father Christmas comes with gifts, for only children believe in these things, only those who have the power to believe and to belong to the kingdom of the heavens; however, I am throwing down, because I am the Maker, I am throwing down those who are sharing the wonders of their blind mind only for the hallucinations, concocted out of the spirit of their inquisitiveness, satan’s spirit, who works to destroy God from the man’s mind and from the earth, for satan is angry with God; he is deadly angry with Him, because death is on his side and he is able to create and share only death. However, his blind fight is as weak as he is, like the lie, which is born of him, oh, and this fight will come to its end because the light is coming, My coming is appearing, and there it comes and how greatly it is coming! Moreover, once with Me it is coming the mourning and the gnashing of the teeth of those who have pretended to be gods of all kinds, of every hand, and who deceive the people on the earth to stand against God, the Maker. Oh, no, because behold, the Father Christmas is coming, he is coming with the gifts from above, he is coming with the angels and saints in tens of thousands, as it is written **(1Thess: 3/13; Jude: 1/14; Apoc: 19/14.)** to come, and My coming is great and full of gifts and joy from above for those who mourn on earth with longing after the truth, and I am the truth, oh, and the mystery of the truth is great, and all its mysteries will be brought into the light and there will be mourning for those who are in the dark at the sight of the light, and blessed are and will be those who will appear with faith and repentance on behalf of the light and on behalf of the new birth of the world, and I am only happiness that I am able to speak and to set on earth the word of truth, the Holy Spirit, Who is rebuilding the world again.

[/legatura_la_teme][legatura_la_teme id_capitol="7" id_subcapitol="3"]Oh, how great was and remained the day of My birth on earth two thousand years ago! Oh, and how great is and will be the day of My coming with the tens of thousands of My saints, with those in whom I have glorified Myself on earth over time with their life, with their love, with their faith in Me! Oh, and they have kept on asking Me, generation by generation, for two thousand years: *«Lord, until when? We have loved and followed You with faith and longing. How long it is before we see the price of this love and its expectation?».* **(Apoc: 6/10)**

Oh, until now, beloved, for behold, I am with you, and I am within My love of word on earth with you in tens of thousands, as it is written that the Lord is coming, and here I am, and I am not stumbling in My coming because there is no faith in the world for Me to come, because it is written: *«Let him who is holy be holier still, and let him, who stays in the iniquities of this age, let him continue to do so, for My day is coming when no one expects Me to come and to give each one according to their work.».* **(Apoc: 22/11)**

Oh, sons from My manger of word, I am making Myself known to the world on earth because I am born as word and I am coming from the Father, that I am not speaking from Myself but the Father is giving Me work to do, and He is giving Me as He did two thousand years ago. Oh, how many knew Me at that time? Very few, and the others, so many of them, were with their heads in their clouds, like many, many in this time.

[/legatura_la_teme][legatura_la_teme id_capitol="6" id_subcapitol="3"]You, sons, you, and all those who believe in Me and in My word like you, oh, seek to know Jesus Christ well so that the philosophies of this passing age may not steal your good mind and understanding. If the heifer breaks loose and goes to eat in the lucerne field, it swells and it may die. The same are with those who take from those who call themselves people of science and philosophers or whatever they may like to be called, oh, and the same is with those who set on the stone their bait for those who are baptized or not baptized in a churchly way according to the holy tradition which has been left down from the beginning for those who want to follow Christ on earth, because there is an order for the Holy Spirit to come over those who are baptized, but the one who is baptized does not work then the Holy Spirit of the baptism, of the Christianization when he became a Christian through baptism, and he is inclined to sources, foreign to those coming on earth for the man to be a Christian through them.

Sons, sons, take great and much care of you, take care of the shirt of your baptism lest it may be torn apart and in this way God not to recognize you that you belong to Him, for when you want to believe something that does not come from God and want to believe and shake off from the rock of steadfastness in Christ, you lose your grace, sons, and you keep on going downwards and no longer know about God, because if you want to be with the Lord, then you have to be as good as some little children that He protects from all the enemies, and you have to remain, to confess and to keep yourselves in Him, sons. For I keep looking over those who take My word as their helm, and I see how they take from the conceited man of this dark age because of the lack of the light from God in the midst of the world, oh, and I want to take care of those who come to My protection, because it is dangerous for the heart to have any kind of food to chew, for you know how Adam died because of the foreign and evil whisper against man’s dwelling in God.

It would be good for man to cry so that he may be happy in eternity, because I said: *«Blessed are those who mourn, for they shall be comforted!».* **(Matt: 5/4)** And they will be comforted for the tears that they have on earth, not wet tears, because there are stronger tears, too, and they are because of the great pains, of the longing that awaits with hope, and these are not squeezed but remain and hurt inside mysteriously, sons.

Then I also said: *«Blessed are the poor in spirit, for theirs is the Kingdom of Heaven».* **(Matt: 5/3)** They do not defile their minds with the knowledge from the people who do not know about God, for I cannot give them anything from Me to have, to those who spend their life in sin because they are all conceited, all of them, oh, and it would be good for them to humble themselves because of their unworthiness. God’s sons relate everything to God, not to the people, for there is no knowledge in the people who reason out those that are set under their mystery, because the people fabricate and give to each other from themselves as they do in a pub with their glasses and then they completely get intoxicated and lead other people to intoxication and after that they fall over the others as those who are drunken with wine, but the poor in spirit do not take from the people because they have God, Who gives them according to His will, what and how much He knows that is needed, and theirs is God’s kingdom, not satan’s kingdom on earth, the ruler of this age, who deceives every man with hallucinations, with revelations, as he may say. However, he cannot build anything because he is not the Builder, and he cannot do what God does; however, I am coming soon, soon, and all things will be separated to the right and to the left, and he who is holy will be holier still, and he who is filthy will become filthier still, for this is what the Lord says: each one will receive according to his works.

Oh, the people do not have any fear of God, and the Christians do not have either. If the Christians had this fear of God, then I would give them gifts to have and to work with them from Me My plan over the earth and I to find them dressed in this way and not naked, not without work and not a hidden talent, **(See Matt: 25/18)** for this means to be found dressed, to have gifts given from Me and to wear them in order to have a reward so that I may have what to pay you for on the day of visitation.

I ask you, Christian, who take from Me the word of instruction; I ask you, people, with whom I have been speaking from heaven for so many years, oh, learn how it is called that you are with God on the way, for He has not asked for your legs on the way with Him, but for your heart and the love from it and for your obedience in faith and according to the work of the faith, not your walking in the body and with the food of the body and then to stay with your spirit in the world, that is within its mind and eye, for if you take from the world, you are no longer with Me on the way, but you are with the world, and at the same time, you are the world, too.

Oh, sons, take from the Holy Spirit, and always be born of Him to be My Father’s and Mine. The Holy Spirit was the seed of which I was born as a Child and as a man, and I am came through the womb of a Virgin, passing to appear as born on earth. The Holy Spirit foretold Me through the prophets that I would come being born of a Virgin and that I would be the Son of the heavenly Father, for the Holy Spirit proceeds from the Father, and the Father speaks through the Holy Spirit like the Son, and the Holy Spirit speaks through the prophets, because the Father wants to fulfill and to show His will, and you should be nourished like this, Christian son, and do not seek to know from the man of science, because you hear the things spread by the man of science, but you do not see him but only believe and then you say that you know. Oh, do this with the things from God too, shared and heard, and you will see what is good and what is bad for to take and to know.

[/legatura_la_teme][legatura_la_teme id_capitol="3" id_subcapitol="4"]And now I am confessing the work of My word during this time and I am saying: As two thousand years ago, My apostles came out of Israel to follow Me and that I, Myself, born of heaven on earth in the body of a Virgin, to shepherd them, oh, in the same way I am shepherding these, with whom I am walking now over the earth for the new birth of the world. Moreover, as the priests of that time rejected My teaching over My disciples, the same is with you, priests of today; you do not believe, and even more, you teach the Christians to look at Us, that is Me and them too, far from you and far from the obedience of you. Oh, but they listen to Me, like My disciples two thousand years ago, and with whom I kept on going further with God’s history on earth, and the same is with these people today, because you do not receive Me in your houses with My well-being for you, and because you do as those at that time did, and you do not want to believe My word in the midst of these today’s disciples of Mine, who are from among you, and are not from among those who are foreign to the churchly tradition, because many have stepped aside.

Oh, I feel hurt because of you for tomorrow, not for today, and that is why I am speaking from time to time with you too, and I am asking you not to stand against Me, as the priests of that time did, do not stand against My coming to those who are little from among you, because you are not going to rejoice; on the contrary, you all take after one another and choose not to believe, for this is what you have been considering that is right to work with Me, with My word nowadays. However, I am advising you within a warm spirit: do not keep being unfaithful to the end, but rather be faithful, sons! Oh, if only you stayed under the spirit of My obedience as they stay!

Oh, if I, the Lord, were not this holy word, this living spring would have long ago disappeared; that is, the spring through which I have been speaking with this written history on this land for almost seventy years. However, I am coming from heaven, I am coming on the clouds for the new birth of the world, oh, you stiff-necked sons. Oh, why is it so hard with you, really?

Two thousand years ago I said: *«When the Son of Man comes, will He find faith on the earth?».* **(Luke: 18/8)** Behold, I put faith in these, who open to Me when I knock, and I lead a people and give it instruction and food of word from heaven, and I, Myself, shepherd it. Oh, for such a long time, I have been standing at the door and knocking at you, too, for you to bow and search Me in the word, to believe Me in it and to receive Me in your house and then to tell Me: welcome! If only you did like the priest who grew Samuel up, because that one taught Samuel how to receive Me, how to answer Me when I called him, and he also taught him how to listen to Me, and Samuel grew up and was a great prophet over Israel and he knew the things from Me for the people, oh, and God’s history with Samuel was written in the Scriptures, just as today is being written My history with these disciples, faithful to Me and hired for Me, and who are from among you. If only the rulers of the church and of the country listened to Me through them, as the kings of the earth were listening to Daniel, My prophet. Behold, this history is being written on earth during your time and you are written as unfaithful in it, like the priests who lived two thousand years ago, like the kings of that time, for it is written in the Scriptures that I, Myself, will come to shepherd My sheep.

Oh, do not be upset with Me and with them, with those who minster My word over them, for I am coming with the clouds above and I am speaking My word from above so that it may be set into its book when it comes.

These sons are not separated from you. Oh, do not be upset with them because they obey Me, for here I am, into their midst as word and their protection, and I am greater than you, ministering sons. Those of two thousand years ago were worried not to lose their thrones and because of this they took Me for their enemy, but they were fully wrong, and they saw only great pain after that. Oh, do not be like them, rather be faithful, as I have taken nothing, nothing from you, but I have only given you, I have loved you, even if you are so cold as you keep Me outside.

Oh, what harm have I done to you? What have I taken from what you have since you have punished Me and do not open to Me? Those of old sent Me to be crucified; however, the pagans received Me and believed that I came from the Father on earth, and the same is today, because faith, behold, does not belong to anyone, rather it belongs to those who humble them-selves before God.[/legatura_la_teme]

I have written a heavenly book on a day of the feast of My birth on earth and I have wanted to touch the hearts of those who are stiff-necked, those who are stubborn.

However, you, sons who believe My word, fight against the losing spirit and know its face and deceiving work against those who believe from God. If someone goes out from among you take him for My enemy and take care that you may remain in the root, sons, because the wind which has been standing against Me with the man for two thousand years is as fierce as in all the times against those who have come to be with God, with their salvation from God.

Seek that your prayer may be well received and seek that you have a beautiful soul for your stature before Me, sons. Those who were very sinful knew how to pray and their prayer was granted; they forgave and were forgiven in their turn. And because Israel persecuted his brother, he fell into harsh slavery after that, and in the times of pain, Israel’s sons repented and called out to Me to forgive them, and they were heard and then forgiven and freed, for otherwise their prayer is weak, their calling out to heaven is powerless.

The day of My birth was a great mystery, because I came to become Myself known and to give help to the one who would come to help Me. This is how I have always worked, because I have always had mercy, and I have had My mother as My help and We comfort each other in pains.

Oh, My mother, My Virgin mother, it is Our feast, mother. We are here in the citadel with the saints, We are with the heaven here, My mother.

It is sweet, and the pain is sweet, it is sweet the memory of my joy and the pain of two thousand years ago, when the Father brought You as a little Child on earth, and I received You, protected You and fed You as a mother does for her own son.

Everything that is with God on earth is sweet! It is sweet the memory of Your birth, dear Son! It was beautiful the victory for You in the times of Our persecution, like that after Your cru-cifixion and resurrection, when my spirit received its peace, for You were victorious.

Glory to those in the highest to the Father, to the Son and to the Holy Spirit, and peace on earth for the day of the feast of Your birth two thousand years, Jesus, Son! Oh, take care of them; take care of those who keep Us to be on earth for the great fulfillment of the renewal, Lord! You were wholly God, and You were a child at the same time so that, we, the people, may know how to be children and to be part of the kingdom of the heavens, Son and child God.

Your love deserves glory and worship, and that expectation still hurts Us, it still does, my beloved Son.

Oh, Father, may Your will be done on earth as in heaven, Father! Let Us and the whole heaven protect this land and what We have on it, Father, for the victory is coming as a reward for those who have and will have patience forever, My Father.

Oh, sons, peace to you! I am with the Father, I am with My mother Virgin, I am with those from heaven here with you. Peace to you, sons! Let it be on earth as in heaven! This has been our waiting, always, always.

Peace to you, peace to you, peace to you, sons! Amen, amen, amen.
