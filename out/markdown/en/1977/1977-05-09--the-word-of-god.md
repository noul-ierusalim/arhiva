[legatura_la_teme id_capitol="3" id_subcapitol="2"]… Israel, you shall not take the name of God in vain, or one of His things. My little children, some say that this cross is not true and it is not sanctified, and they consider it as being cursed. You should keep away from this spoken thing. This holy cross had also been before the Lord Jesus, but it was not blessed, for the dead people were crucified on it by the law-givers. But by this cross, the Lord, Jesus Christ, forgave any sin, and a tree with sweet fruits for everybody was built by it. This holy cross has power to take the man out of the pit, as it took Daniel out of the pit of lions, as it took out our and your fathers and forefathers.[/legatura_la_teme]

[legatura_la_teme id_capitol="6" id_subcapitol="2"]… You, who have the gift to speak with Me, through prayer you will bring forth even to the thousandth generation. God gave power to His Son, as well as to His least servant, sons. Know that you will not be greater and another less, but all alike will be measured. That you will say to the mountain: “Become a door that I may pass through you!” and it will be done, Christian. And you will say to the mountain: *“Move away!”* and it will move, Christian. And you will say to the mountain: *“Crumble!”* and it will crumble, for I tell the truth: By your faith God will work the completion of this age. Do not become guilty, for it is a sin.[/legatura_la_teme] Christian, why am I calling you to come to Me? I called you long ago and I said: *“Come!”* And why do you come and turn back? Why do you not stay near Me? What do you say when you see Me? What do you say, sons, when you come to stand before Me? Will you have the courage to speak, or will you remain silent? Will you have the courage, or you will turn yellow and grieve? My people, God does not want your sorrow, but He wants you to stand before Him as His most precious child. Why do you enchant yourself, Christian? Why do you deceive yourself? You know very well that all and everything will come to an end but know that the end of the sin will come, too. However, if you do this, you put an end to the holy thing and you always prolong your evil things. An apple falls from the tree. Why does not a rotten apple stay in the tree? The same is with you if you always sin. You will fall and no longer stay. [legatura_la_teme id_capitol="6" id_subcapitol="2"]I have come from the holy heaven to make this word known to you. Israel, I want you to be salt. With you I will salt the past and the end. With you I will salt this age.[/legatura_la_teme]
