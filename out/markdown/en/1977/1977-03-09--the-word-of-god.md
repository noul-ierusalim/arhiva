… I gave permission to have radio appliances and I find Christians at matches. *(Soccer and other “sports”, r.n.)* Forbidden! It is forbidden, Christian, to watch matches! I do not go to the matches and no one is allowed to. Who dared went wrong and on passing away from this life, those black *(devils)* who made matches, take them and put them into the hell. What is that a *“match”*? This is something that was not in the time of your forefathers and ancestors. Beware of this word, *“match”*. Beware of trick. This is the greatest idol in your times.
