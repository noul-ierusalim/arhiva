*(The Lord’s message to the bishops of the church)*

In this time of holy feast, in heaven for the saints and on earth for the good and faithful Christian people, I, the Lord Jesus Christ, come in as word into the book with the glory of the teaching and with the spirit of blessing for the feast of the Pentecost, when My glory will settle and will rest here, in the Garden of the Meeting under the White Tent and I will spend time with the people gathered together at My spring and it will be preached on earth, and My mystery of this time will go from place to place, rejoicing and comforting the hearts that are thirsty with longing after the Lord, as I come to give comfort, because this is what I promised in the day of My farewell between Me and My disciples two thousand years ago, after that forty days had passed from My resurrection, being with them from time to time; and then I took leave of them and ascended near the Father, on high, promising them that I would not forsake them until the end of the time and that I would be with them under the rose, and the Holy Spirit, the Comforter, would also give them from Me and He would guide and comfort them, as it is His mysterious work, the comfort.

With a feast of My ascension to the Father I come down into the book and in this day I remember My word of two thousand years, by which I said that if I went to the Father I would draw everyone to the Father and to Me, and I promised My disciples the Holy Spirit, the Comforter.

Oh, the man needs comfort upon his life on earth, and he does not know where it really comes from. Oh, there is so much lack of love on earth, but no one knows the way to comforting anymore, even if the man has been looking only for the comfort, but he has no comfort if he does not know who the comfort is, and whoever does not know it, does not have it and he is poor of life and of power of life. Oh, I would comfort everywhere and every time all those who are deprived of comfort, for the man is destitute on earth, even if he were the richest man within those that are on earth or the most adorned in his mind, or the greatest on earth.

I ascended to the Father from My disciples, whom I gave and let the spirit of the comfort, but those who are comforted are those who share comfort, and this is how I exhorted them to work out the comfort for each other and to give light with it to the world of those who are not comforted. I was and I am the most wounded One, and I have been like that from the beginning of the world and of the man and I have been this way since I lost the man, but I have been comforting Myself offering comfort, and this is how I have been carrying My pain, and this is how I have been exhorting the man to work out his joy and his comfort, and the one who will listen will see that I have been true in whatever I have said. Oh, it is only the devil who does not rejoice over the man’s comfort and that is why he has been paving the way for lying instead of comforting, and the man remains, poor of him, deprived of the comfort from above, from God, and he has no comfort.

There came above My feasting with My disciples, and there came a white and beautiful cloud, and while I was speaking them a word of comfort, the little cloud came down between Me and them and in an instant it made My way to the Father, and they were filled with comfort at once and remained within My Spirit and I in their spirit, and I worked together with them, I with them and they with Me, in everything after that, and only the little cloud separated us by its mystery, and I and My Father were near them, hidden in the cloud.

Oh, behold what a sweet speaking I come down between Me and man with, and I promised that we will spend time in word with those who set themselves as shepherds of the church and I will try to wake them up from the ignorance of God, and I will do this because of the mercy of the Christian flock.

Therefore, take heed, oh, take heed of the One Who is speaking to you from heavens, to you, the rulers of the church, to you, those who have forgotten what the life of the shepherd is like, the life of Christ, the parable for the shepherds. I am the Shepherd with the cross, and you do not have a cross, but you do have great glory gathered around you and you are not afraid of the evil spirit of the glory that comes from the people. My glory was the sufferance for the flock and the cross, and I have been shepherding the flock then and on over the ages. There has been no church dwelling where I have not been presented before the ministering priest at the altar, crucified with nails and crowned with thorns on My forehead. Oh, why really, why all those who become ministers of altars set Me before them crucified on the cross? Do they cry for My mercy? Oh, no.

Oh, here it is what I tell you, I, the Lord, resurrected from crucifixion. The Christian flock has days of holy feasts and glory for the Lord, ordained by the saints and fathers, and the day of My crucifixion is not a day of feast, but the day of My resurrection is a day of victory instead and it is a feast of victory and rest after victory and of the joy for it. Oh, here it is why the Christians should not reveal Me crucified on the cross, because this appearance is the judgment of those who crucified Me and of those for whom I was crucified, and the Christians should rather preach Me resurrected on the cross and not crucified, as I have exhorted the people of My word in this time, and this is how it has obeyed and it has My day of resurrection as the day of rest, and My Christian flock should reveal Me, and this is how the shepherds over the flock should confess Me, that is resurrected and not crucified.

I am Jesus Christ, the Lord of resurrection, and I speak to you I in a day of holy feast and I want to shepherd you if you receive Me as Shepherd over you, and if you do not receive Me, I am not your Shepherd, and you are not shepherds over the Christian flock, which is My flock. I ask you, those who do not receive Me to teach you, to wake you up, to shepherd you: who are you? You should ask yourselves who you are and each one of you should say: *„Who are we? Who we look like with and who do we serve?”* Oh, ask and give yourselves an answer, for I have given one concerning you and I have this account on the table.

Behold, behold, I come and I tell you that I am the Shepherd of the Christian flock and I look at you to see you how you go and what you do. Behold, you exalt yourselves too high with your greatness in which you have established yourselves and by which, without any fear of God, you want to spoil the things set by the saints and by the fathers, although you do not have this right, and I tell you on the day of My teaching upon you that you will not be able to change anything, and that everything you change you will do it against you, and the right worshipper Christians will remain on the side of the saints as during all the times of trial upon My church and that is why they will be Christians as well, and you, and the others like you, will be within the blaze of the amalgamation and agitation of the satan-antichrist, which tries to strike in the stone, in the Cornerstone, Which is called Jesus Christ.

I come from the Father as word on the hearth of the Romanian people and I have written into My book of today over sixty years of word and I have not stumbled against the carelessness of the shepherds of the church, oh, no, and neither have I stumbled against your carelessness to come and to shepherd. Therefore, listen:

You have interfered in the lawlessness, which is to be signed in a few days into the name of the Orthodox church and into the name of the church leaders, *(Refer to the assembly of patriarchs of Orthodox churches in the so-called* „pan-Orthodox Holy and Great Council” *of Crete, r.n.)* and the church in the Romanian people has also be drawn into this lawlessness through the heads of the church.

I do My duty and I say: Do not go to this amalgamation! Flee from this lawlessness, which brings on wrath over the earth, for behold a land beaten everywhere by all kinds of plagues! Those who go there in order to say *„yes”* to the lawlessness and to the overthrow of the Christian traditions and of their place in the calendar, you will be those to break off from this flock and you will make a break into the flock and it will be war. Oh, why, really, do you think so highly of yourselves to speak into the name of the flock, which you do not ask if it wants this amalgamation according to your will that violates the holy traditions? Oh, what does this also mean? Where does this unknown law come from? Behold, this is the hidden work of the antichrist, who has been dreaming all the time to destroy My church. Oh, why cannot you be intelligent to see the hostile work? Oh, the flock does not know what you want to prepare for it, you, who pose as great and that you have the right to decide for all, but woe to you!

I, the Lord, will overcome, I, the One full of mercy for the flock, and you will separate by yourselves and there will be war, because there has still been fear of God in many of the shepherds of the church and among those who love God with fear. The spirit of prophecy is that which works and that which stands against those who slip down towards this iniquity, for Moses was crying before Me because of the people’s trespasses and because of its dignitaries’, and the priest Aaron was making a golden calf and the people was dancing around it with joy, and destruction was coming upon them and they did not see it.

You, those among the shepherds of the church, you, who are seized by anxiety and cry within yourselves and outside of you because of the lawlessness that is approaching in the name of the church, oh, step on My right side and get out of this amalgamation and put on you the love of God in a great measure, and faithfulness for those things left by the saints and by the fathers once and for all, as the Scriptures has been teaching you this, and the flock will be split in two, one part on My right side and the other one on My left, but you keep away from the sin of unbelief, for this sinful lawlessness strikes in the work of the Holy Spirit, which no one on earth can set limits against it. I exhort you as a father to live in the Christian way, and for this I call you to look into My book of today and into the charter of the establishment of My rulership citadel in the midst of the Romanian people, where I have the throne of My spring of word, and by whose holy law all My church on earth should live on earth during these hard times so that it may be truly Mine, to be like Me and not like the world, for behold, the face of the world hour by hour plays to please satan, and no one is able to stop this slaughter upon people. Behold, the lawlessness has been prepared to be accomplished, and messengers of the church of the Romanian people go to it, who are going to sign in the name of the church and of the flock in the church, and this injustice will be great upon the flock and many will wake up and fight for the faith received from the fathers and there will be pain and many will cry, and those who love God within the truth will mourn. Oh, the fear of God would have been able to stand against this attack, but where is this fear to stop those who rise to do this lawlessness?

Now, I, the Lord, speak and announce the guilt of those on the side of the church of the Romanian people, who will write their names for the strengthening of the covered lawlessness with a great name made up by those who have worked thievishly and who have announced a lawless meeting, calling it *„the great and holy synod”*. This name is written for the fool, for this is what the flock treacherous shepherds believe, this is what they believe that are the things in the flock of the church of the Romanian people, and of other nations and churches with a holy name of the Orthodox church. Oh, there is no need of this synod, but the rulers of the world need to strike in God and into the Christian flock, and in order to do this they buy people in order to play their game, and the work of those who lead the people astray and of those who sell out the holy cause of the Lord and of His saints is in this way and no otherwise.

I, the Lord, Jesus Christ, the Shepherd with the cross, separate from those who separate from Me and from the saints, when they do this by their own power, and I exhort My flock and the right faithful Christians to stand up well and with fear and to be careful when the walls shake, for the time is shaken once with the lawlessness on earth. Let the fasting and praying go up into the heaven. Let the holy Christians work out watch and faith, for the Holy Spirit comes with the comfort and shepherds the faithful ones on My behalf. Let those who are faithful carry Me in secret and let them strengthen their holy life, for I am with them to the end, as it is written. Amen.

In this day of holy feast I have My trumpet Verginica near Me, and the saints celebrate her birthday on earth between she and them, in the year when the enemies of the church decided by treason to replace the feasts of the saints[1], and they replaced them taking days out of the Christian calendar in 1923, when Verginica was born, whom I made My trumpet after that, and since then the Romanian people has no longer been celebrating the days of feast of the saints on their time, on their place in the ancient calendar. Now I am going to establish Myself in the book with My trumpet, and we will cry again and we will speak again from heaven, for the Lord’s crying and of His saints is a great watch over the earth; it is a great atonement and only the Lord’s sufferance brings down mercy on the earth, and it brings down graciousness and patience, and patience has fruit, for it sprouts and it brings forth a new fruit; it brings forth a new hope. Amen, amen, amen.
