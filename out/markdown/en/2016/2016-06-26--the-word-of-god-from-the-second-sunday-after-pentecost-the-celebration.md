*The Word of God for the pastors of the Orthodox Church at the end of Synod in Creta*

.................

You, that should pastor Christ’s flock, as you are seated on church chairs, what do you do, and what is you work? Out of a great pain we do put this question to you and we let you know that you’re not working any good for yourself by what you say and do, and look what you do! You do mixing, and this have a nasty smell fore our Lord and for the Saints. O, who hath said unto you, to gather in the name of the church you’ve racked up now? The sky from heaven looked at this workmanship as sky looked at the Tower of Babel, and the ragtag mixed mess happened between you, you who have assembled in the name of great and Holy Synod, because you’ve mixed in there with heretics and you have tried to speak to the sky in heaven all together, even if your God left teachings through your Saints that you should not mix with those heretics and not to sit down with them at the same table, as well as the Scriptures teach you. Oh, how much pain and how much shame went up from you into heaven! And you, from the Romanian country taken out there, you’ve made a wrong step by going there, and you have held with all diligence not to listen to the Lord who sent you the news for you not to go in there and you had your way with the word, and you have turned your back to His Word and you left off and remained big and strong, but under which God? Oh, you do neither know what you’re doing and where you walk and who you serve. Oh, you’ve said for the sons of My Word that they are heretics, and, look, you do the service and the ministry together with heretics as at the Tower of Babel, and who knows how many languages are struggling there so that this mess can be seen, but whatever you do, you do for your glory, not for the Lord, not for the flock, Oh no, no way. True Apostles of Christ, they make people of God from people, and build in them the Holy Spirit, and that’s how they build Christ’s church on Earth, and you build yourselves up cathedrals, forcing the people whose money you take, but it’s not for them what you hath build yourselves, because God does not live with you in these buildings, which you fill with people and glory for you from them, but to learn people about God, oh, this is not seen on the faces of those whom you force to give all to you, same as the shepherds taking wool and sheep cheese, and then their flesh, as written in the Scriptures about the shepherds, unbelievers for the flock and for Lord’s flock.

Oh, you will not be able to change any of those placed through the Saints, and you’ll harm yourselves as long as you are working through so much hodgepodge *(any jumbled mixture; mess, r.n.)* of Orthodox churches, and in our day of praise we tell you that you are lost and that it would be the time for you to wake up at the crying of those from the Earth that shows at you with their finger, saying that you are not gathering for the herd in the name of the Church, or in the name of God, but you let yourself being torn away from God, toward separation, as the work of antichrist has to be fulfilled in this time, as it is written.

O Lord, when thou hast sent Thy disciples in the world You told them to build yourself in people and sign them with thy name, thou hast not told them to do from place to place cathedrals, but armies of Christians to do, as You have done when you did Your work.

And now we bow ourselves to You, our Lord, that we could talk. And look, from the Romanian people we have in heaven You trumpet Verginica, born among men in the All Saint’s day, and great was Your work with her in its time on Earth, and great consolation you worked with her for those in heaven, so eager to come the day of your victorious and righteousness of Your Saints.

Oh, how much teaching should be, on Earth, Lord, always, always! Oh, make a wide path for your Word Lord and make it a honorable path and fulfillment for it! Oh, bring this comfort on Earth and do not let it be collapsed your flock and her Christian work, by those who are not write with You and with your Christian flock, and rise from the middle of Your church those who are strong with the spirit, that they should take care of the remnant by grace preserved, because your true flock are the Christians who fulfill your will on earth like in heaven, Amen.
