It is a feast on earth for the Christians when it is a feast in heaven, and the feast in heaven is majestic among the saints, and tears of love flow on earth upon those who love the heaven and its saints, for the saints are the comfort of those that are on earth in times of joy or trouble, and only the saints can leave the true book of history to be written on earth, and they are those who push all things forward, since all those who cling to the ruling chairs draw everything down, everything back, only for them, boasting to each other and to the lowly people that they are someone and that they have got up into the world, oh, but they get up into the world only to get rich and only to spend their life haughtily, for on the chairs of a certain city there are no high people to be pure in their heart and with their hands and life clean, but rather there are only covetous people of wealth and ranks and glory, only those buy ruling chairs, and the masters who are good, merciful and affectionate to the people of the nation, oh, they hardly come to rule in order to do good and only good to their neighbors, who are in great need of leaders with big hearts, being occupied with the protection and with the good of life of those on whom they rule and serve as to some loved brothers, by whom they build and take care of the country together.

Oh, it is a feast of saints in this day, and the Christian people brings inclination and reverence to the saint Parascheva, the protector from heaven on the hearth of the Romanian people, who is a great giver of good to those who are pure within their heart and desire, but the joy for the saints and for those who honor the saints on the earth would be greater if the people were worthy of the holy life and of God’s commandments always on their lips and in the deeds of their lives, and as for Me, the Lord, it would be to have a great kingdom with people and with much work with the heaven among the people and then the people would not be in need of God, and God would not be so much in need of heaven loving people either, and many miracles would be seen rejoicing the faithful ones and strengthening the faith and love for God over so many people, who do not have the Lord in their thought, in their life and in their creed; however, if it does not happen that way, behold, the kingdom of the sin has got a great stature on the earth with the people, and the wickedness grows within hearts because of the lack of God in people, for the people without God in them are not good people even if their nature might help them to be good, because those who are good, if they are not good for the Lord, the fruit of their lives is not called the rewarded and eternal fruit, and the fruit is lost once with the place from which it appears to be.

I, the Lord, am coming to you as word and I embrace you within My word, Romanian people, because you have on your hearth the body of the saint Parascheva, a lover of God in all her time on the earth. Oh, I embrace you, people loved by God for your choice, because the Father has chosen you for Me to come in this time as word on the earth and to have a house where to go to, to be waited when I come and to be welcomed and loved at My coming as word on the earth.

Oh, peace to you, Romanian people! Moreover, peace to you, cities and countries with the name of Orthodox-Christian, as the Lord and His saints like it to be! Oh, increase your faith and watchfulness for it, because the wind, which blows to wipe out the Orthodox-Christian faith from your face and hearts, is too strong, that is the church that has been pleased to Me even from the time of My body on earth and up to this day and until tomorrow, oh, loved children of My Father Sabaoth! I speak My word to you, the word of God, oh, Christian cities according to the tradition from the beginning, for it is a feast in the name of this faith in which the devout Parascheva lived and loved, a martyr who was seen keeping watch between heaven and earth in the Romanian city of Moldavia, one of the three Romanian countries, *(”Moldova”, ”Ardealul” and ”Țara Românească”, r.n.)* in the name of the Trinity of God, the Father, the Son and the Holy Spirit, the three in one single country, My country of today, the country of My word in the end of the time, for I, the Lord, and My saints stand above it in its sky, and I have been speaking for sixty years on the hearth of this people, in order to make up My book and to prepare by it a faithful and holy people like Me, for there is nowhere holiness and a holy place on the earth, and I, the Lord, cry with the saints after holy halting places to have a rest from mourning, because We mourn without a holy flock in order to be able to shepherd it with those sweet things from heaven and eternal like God, and which are not passing away like man, for the man passes away like grass and he has not got any prepared place if he have not loved the Lord on earth to have Him as his Father in heaven as well.

Oh, My country of today, My Christian country, I, the Lord, have you within great heavenly watch, because all the saints watch upon you and scent you with the fragrances of the heaven when the Father has chosen You for Me as a feast of wedding table now, in the end of the time. Oh, I have the word of My mouth as food on the table and with it I give you life and exhortation and we share it with all the countries, and especially with all those who have a church into their midst and Orthodox Christian faith.

I am covering you in this day with My mantle and speaking to you, countries and churches of the right faith and I am exhorting you to strengthen yourselves day and night in watch, faith and love of God, because everywhere it is dark where there is not the light of the Orthodoxy and of its Lord, Jesus Christ, the One Who left His kingdom on earth two thousand years ago to establish it from place to place with its seed on earth. Oh, then all the kinds of countries and people have made up all sort of beliefs with My name on them and they have claimed their right to be in line with those who have called themselves Christians on earth, but one is My church, not many, and it has been called Orthodox Christian, and the others are false; they are made after the people and not after God’s will, and these forms of beliefs are not pleased to My, and the mixture is dangerous, oh, sons of the holy faith, for all are dyed and they are not light upon people, and only I, the Lord, I together with those who take after Me in their walking and clothing and conduct, only those were light as I am the light of the world. And behold, the Orthodox Christian churches and their flocks, as long as they are light in the world, they are like Me, only that the people like to be with their darkness considered as light, and the Lord has no room within them.

You, Orthodox Christian countries, oh, how beautiful it would be on earth for you to be inseparable within the love of Christ, and to have the power to work out always only light over the nations on the earth! However, you did not make My love grow from one another and My Romanian country, My country of today, behold, it has not been steadfast and it has bowed for help to other countries that are far from God with their life and faith, and it had to be otherwise; however, you, Christian countries according to My will, you have built weapons of war and you have wanted to let it be known about them and to display them so that you may spoil the love among you and to turn it into threatening, and threatening does not build love, but it rather builds anxiety, oh, sons of the faith, and what is more pathetic is that the countries to which My Romanian country bows, like other countries with a dyed faith, tend to spoil the holy traditions in Romania and the churchly tradition, as it is received from the fathers and from the saints, and I have been suffering from this anxiety, and the time will come for Me when I will not spare the rod if the Orthodox church bows to play the games of the pagans and to spoil completely its little wedding garment for My table of word into its midst.

Oh, you, little and tiny city, Romanian child separated from your fatherly house, My Moldavia over the river, *(The Republic of Moldavia, r.n.)* I cry out to you: do not stagger so that you may not be separated from the vine, for it is written that the branch that does not remain in the vine gets dried and is thrown into the fire, and the one which remains in the vine, My Father prunes it away to bring forth even more fruit, and to bring forth fruit from the vine and that it may not remain asunder. Oh, you are so small alone, and what have you been doing as little as you are, when those who covet you to be theirs are great and threatening and are not your good friends as you might think of them now, in the time of hardships for your freedom and for your native name? I have wanted to annex you in peace and with My power to the whole body of My country where you belong to, *(Romania)* and when I wanted it you did not want it by those who were opposing in you and who were alien to your inheritance, and now you have your fate into your hand *(For Presidential elections, r.n.)* and I advise you to kneel down before being brought down to your knees by those who are stronger in you, who want you on their side, and you had better kneel down before Me and ask for wisdom so that I may give it to you and to strengthen your faith and watchfulness, oh, because you are as hardened within your spirit as little as you are, after you had been broken and separated from your house, from your mother country, oh, child wandering away among nations and within other lands now, and behold, the key for your fate of wrath is into your hand. That is why kneel down and be wise, oh, My Moldavia beyond water, and do not stagger from one foot to another because I want to help you if you set Me close to you for your help, for this is for your good and not to be foreign as you are now.

And you, great people within your expansion and nation, oh, peace to you, Russian people, and hold on to the side of the holy faith and holy church, for you have a great heaven of big saints in their faith and with heavenly miracles above you, and do not let drowsiness seize you as it has happened to the church servants in Romania, country of My word of today, because these have not wanted to listen to Me as you have listened to God and you have not gone to mix with those who have wanted to spoil their faith by a mixture of sin and inclination for denial of the holy fathers. Oh, peace to you, Christian church and flock, and be united within the spirit of those from heaven of your great nation, because the great saints had been coming up into heaven from your midst in the time of those one thousand years since your holy ancestors had received Me on your hearth, who had become the people of Jesus Christ between earth and heaven, and you should fight and remain likewise, oh, Christian people, but take a good look into the books, for I, the Lord, love holiness, I love peace and I love brotherhood, mercy and faithfulness and I love the sacrifice that is pleased to Me, and I tell to all the nations to know from God that the strongest weapon of defense is the love of God and of brothers, and then from it the right faith, the Orthodox Christian faith into the midst of the great darkness of the nations without God on earth.

Oh, Russian people, do not worry! No harm arises from the midst of the Romanian people against you, because it is not in the nature of this people to do any harm against a Christian brother and against his neighbor, because the Romanian people is an affectionate people within his heart and love, even if the Christian nations are not inclined for peace towards all the nations, for this is written: that all may live at peace with the entire world as much as is possible. But I exhort you more and more, church of the Russian people, not to bow down together with those who try to diminish the obedience to God through the church as it has been left through the saints and through the parents, because the servants of the church of the Romanian nation have bowed and made Me suffer deeply and have caused those in heaven to sigh, and I, the Lord, have always wanted to cry out to them to stand up for repentance before they have put down the candlestick on the table, as it is written for those who do not repent while there has still been time for repentance. *(Refer to the assembly of patriarchs of Orthodox churches in the so-called "pan-Orthodox Holy and Great Council" of Crete, r.n.)*

Oh, sit down as exhortation into your midst and as an example for the Orthodox Christian countries and exhort them to steadfastness with watchfulness and obedience to the fathers for those who sit on the chairs of shepherds, because there is a great danger to put down from their places the traditions for My church on earth, and mixture to get in over it.

The saints in heaven cry and are full of mourning and watching over the Christ’s churches, and behold, the saint Parascheva in the Moldavia country, always a protector over the entire Romanian land and over all the sisterly countries within their faith and church, behold its word of mourning over the whole churches of Christ in the Orthodox Christian church. Oh, behold the voice of the saints, who have made up a churchly flock on earth, behold a voice from heaven:

– Lord, I cry from near You and with You, and I cry with all the holy brothers, and we call out all Your churches to cry together with us, lest Your seed may disappear from the earth, Your holy church, Lord, for You fight to be its Savior, oh, Lord, while its enemies want to lose it and to mix her within their darkness without God and full of works of sin.

Oh, the Christians in the churches should not forget what the saints expect from them and therefore I tell them this:

Oh, Christ’s children, you ask from us all the good for you and you have always asked us to be your intercessors with the Lord within your sufferings and joys and we bring you mercy and help from Him, but we also ask you to do what we did on earth: holy faith, love of God and life with holiness in it, which you can take within you by the obedience to those ten commandments, which give life from God to those who follow them on their way to heaven. I, from the midst of the Romanian people, where I watch over this hearth of God Word from, am bringing now, on my day of feast among the saints, I am bringing you my brotherly crying: Do not trample down God’s commandments, but rather fulfill them with great love, and let them be your whole joy of life, as it is proper for the Christians and for the Christian countries, which do not have the life of Christ as their freedom, the way on which the man condemns sin and becomes free from sin, as I had fulfilled in the time of my life on earth, and thus I had received a place with the heavenly hosts in heaven and we serve the Lord and together with the Lord between heaven and earth for you, and we want to meet together serving the Same our Lord, Jesus Christ, and to be one in His love, for the Lord has meant the eternal happiness for all His servants.

I, Lord, from near You and with You cry for love and happiness, because I have been speaking now to the Christian people on the earth from everywhere, and I give you my entire joy, as I served only You on earth, as I worshipped only You and as I wanted and loved only You.

– I, the Lord, seal with My name your word of love, loved daughter. Jesus Christ is your inheritance in heaven, and I want to give this inheritance to the whole Orthodox-Christian people on earth, after each one has brought Me his life in order to make My kingdom out of it.

Oh, if the saints in heaven and if the saints on earth were not all the time, how would it be possible for the time to go on with so many unfaithful people and with so many servants of sin everywhere on earth?

Behold, I have been giving the word over the Christian people everywhere from My manger into the midst of the Romanian people. I can no longer tell the Christian people that many want to be My people when they do not take after Me and after the faith that has been left from the fathers, and I say: the Orthodox-Christian people, for only in this flock I find My scope and My settlement, that from the beginning of My church two thousand years ago.

Oh, children of the right faith, children from everywhere, behold My bedding of coming as word on earth and from where I share Myself! I have sixty years of written word on the hearth of the Romanian people. I have written a book of history for Me into the midst of this nation. Oh, love the Lord and bow to the footstool of His feet, for holy is the Lord and His bedding in His citadel of word, and then the worship song will be churchly sung in such a way that all the nations of the earth will see the salvation coming from God and not from the people, and the mountains will rejoice over the face of the Lord, for behold, the Lord is coming, and He is coming to judge the earth, as it is written! Amen, amen, amen.

Now, My people from the spring, oh, sons, again and again I strengthen you at My work with you, and may your work increase, for the working days pass one after the other as many as they are until the arrival of the feast of the angels, when I, the Lord, I together with you will stand in the threshold and we will open wide to the guests of everywhere and we will give them great comfort and sweet exhortation for steadfastness until the end with the Lord, Whom they have chosen.

Oh, be confident of My power, sons, and then be confident of it in you, and only then we will have rest from our burden, and the rest will be with comfort after each work done, which I make up with you. Restlessly give your hand to each other, and then let the angels come into you and let them be able to work, and to work from heaven, sons, for the work is holy and it is a lot.

Oh, take care of the full peace among you, and serve one another with reverence, redeeming any split of a second, because it is closer and closer the need to be ready to set the table of the autumn feast, the feast of the angels, oh, sons.

I, the Lord, clothe you within the spirit of the heavenly powers and I will treat you with comfort and then you will be able to do everything it has to be done, and you will marvel at how great it is the one who is with God, and God with him, for I, the Lord, want that you may be like Me, sons, and that I may see Myself within you, and that you may see you within Me, and this is how My measure with the man is reached out, the measure of love, oh, sons, Amen, amen, amen.
