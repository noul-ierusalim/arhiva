I am coming closer to the manger before dawn. I am coming at the boundary between day and night, for I proclaimed Myself that I would come, and I have come, and this is how I am teaching My people that are fed from heaven during these days of My coming. Behold, I find watching at the manger and I am met by My watchmen. I am working during the night My today’s word, watchmen sons, and then We are going to travel with it into the midst of Israel, My today’s people, the people from My coming. Amen, amen, amen.

… The proud man with the right faith is hiding, he is hiding from My today’s word and he is calling himself right in his faith. The man does not take this word as coming from Me, and he says that he does not believe it. Oh, the man could also say that the prophets also spoke from themselves and not from God, but the word of the prophets was and is being fulfilled, as also My today’s word is being fulfilled because it is My word. Amen. The today’s Christian man is beating his chest and he is saying that he is right, but for the way with righteousness in it the man is not struggling. Therefore, I want to shelter My today’s people to keep over it My word and My coming with the word on earth. Amen. I want to keep My people under a tent, for it is written into the Scriptures this: *«Behold! God’s dwelling place is with people, and He will dwell with them, and they will be His people, and God Himself will be with them as their God. He will wipe away from them every tear from their eyes. Death will be no more; neither will there be mourning, nor crying, nor pain, any more. The first things have passed away».* **(Apoc: 21/ 3, 4)**. Amen, amen, amen.

… In the old times, when My work was in bud, I was relying on those who received the word of My coming. I was speaking sweetly to them, to be able to rely on their faith in My coming down to them and to be able to write My book of today from its beginning and until today, the book with seven seals, which *«no one in heaven or on earth or under the earth, was able to open it or look into it»*, as it is written into the Scriptures about it when it says: *«I saw in the right hand of Him Who sat on the throne, a book written inside and outside, sealed shut with seven seals, and a mighty angel proclaiming: “Who is worthy to open the book, and to break its seals?»* **(Apoc: 5/1-2)** I, the Lamb of the Father, opened its seals and bring it down on the earth, and blessed are those who are written in this book, in the Lamb’s Book of Life. Amen, amen, amen.

[legatura_la_teme id_capitol="3" id_subcapitol="3"]… I cross the heaven and the earth with the work of the man’s creation. The angels from heaven work invisibly for Me the work of My word, which comes with Me on earth with its fulfillment, for the angels are spirits with power. Le the man not forget that I am God and not him, and if he forgets, then I remind him that he forgets to become small before Me to be able to protect the one who acknowledges My being and power.[/legatura_la_teme] Let the man not forget that the life of faith is the way with justice, the work of justice between man and God and between man and man, because there is no faith in God otherwise. However, the man beats his chest and calls himself righteous and he wants to be believed that he is righteous, but he is unjust in his inner being, both with God and with man. And I said: *«When the Son of Man comes, and the angels of power together with Him, will He find faith on earth any longer?»* **(Luke 18:8)** Who is able to answer God? Who among people? Let him come before and say: *“Here I am, Lord, take my faith in You for righteousness!”* But behold, I come as a river of word over the people, and the man does now bow to receive from God and to set Me over him, as Abraham worked at My word over him, when I counted his faith in My coming to him as justice and righteousness. I want to have in the people of Jerusalem, the heavenly gift of the faith and the righteousness, which come from faith. Amen. I want to put My people of today under shelter but this gift of faith in My coming with the angels of My power and with the saints, for the confirmation of the Scriptures, which spoke by My mouth and they said: *«They will see the Son of Man coming on the clouds of the heaven with much power and with much glory, and He will send His angels at the sound of trumpet and they will gather His elected from the four corners of the winds, from the margins of the heavens to all the other margins, and He will throw the chaff into an unquenchable fire»*. This Scripture is the work of My word of today, which brings all those that are mine by their faith with justice sown between heaven and earth within My coming, at the man’s faith, for the fulfilling of My Scriptures by the righteous man’s faith. Amen.

Let all the nations of the people on earth take heed and let them tremble at the voice of My coming, which cries from the margins of the heavens to the other margins, speaking again, after two thousand years: *«When the Son of Man comes, will He find faith on earth?»* Amen, amen, amen.
