Behold sons, who listen to My word, Lazarus was dead in the grave and he heard Me and came to life and stood before Me, but all the living, who were seeing and hearing Me, did not want to hear Me and did not want to know who I was and where I came from and that I was the Savior who came. However, you, be the sons of My coming, and set yourselves to labor for My day. Set My day for you and be the day of My coming; be light from My light over the earth and give My day to the people everywhere, so that I may appear right and victorious. Do not take after the scribes and Pharisees and after the teachers who want Me and you to be silent, for this is what they said to Me as well. When I was preparing to enter victoriously in the city riding on the cold of the donkey, My disciples were rejoicing and praising God for the glory with which I was glorified over the multitudes, saying: *«Blessed is the King Who comes into the name of the Lord!»* However, the Pharisees were pressing Me on to rebuke them so that it might not appear that I exalt Myself. But I answered them: *«If they do not speak, the stones will speak out»*, and then I cried over that city which did not know My day and those for its peace. The same way I have been crying today and here is how much I have been crying. I have been crying for the entire earth; I cry and call out to the man on the earth and I cry for those who have become teachers in My name over the people, for those have become too great and do not know the time of My visitation. Oh, only if they knew those that are for their peace! But they did not know it and that is why they hid from their eyes.

Oh, sons who heard My word, behold, now it happens as then with those who did not know Me. I will not let one stone on another, for they did not receive those that were for their peace; they did not know them and that is why they hid from their eyes, for it is written: *«They have eyes and do not see, they have ears and do not hear, they have mind and do not understand»*. And as for you, bring before Me a little donkey for loading, for the Lord, sons, for Me, so that I may stay over you and to come in the house of the man, to rebuke him and to put out of My house the one who sells and buys, on My name, the souls of those who are oppressed and enslaved by those who rule over them. Behold My day, the day of salvation for those who wait for Me with it. Give yourselves to Me as branches and flowers and be a crown to Me for My coming, sons. Amen, amen, amen.
